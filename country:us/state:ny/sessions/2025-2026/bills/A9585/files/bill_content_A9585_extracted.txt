Except as otherwise 17 provided in this article, the program shall be implemented, and enroll- 18 ment of employees shall begin, within twelve months after the effective 19 date of this article. The provisions of this section shall be in force 20 at such time as the program administrators open the program for enroll- 21 ment. 22 1. Duty and liability of the state. The state shall have no duty 11 or liability to any party for the payment of any child and dependent 12 care savings benefits accrued by any enrollee under the program.
