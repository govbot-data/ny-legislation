BILL NOS06253A

Amd §7-118, El L
 
Requires state and county boards of elections to post sample ballots online at least twelve days prior to the first day of early voting; requires such ballots to be posted in a searchable database.

        AN  ACT  to  amend  the election law, in relation to requiring state and
          county boards of elections to post sample ballots
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.    Subdivision  3 of section 7-118 of the election law, as
     2  separately amended by chapters 410 and 411  of  the  laws  of  2019,  is
     3  amended to read as follows:
     4    3.  Sample  ballots  may  be  mailed by the board of elections to each
     5  eligible voter at least three days before  the  [election,  or  in  lieu
     6  thereof,  a  copy  of  such sample ballot may be published at least once
     7  within one week preceding the election in newspapers  or  on  a  website
     8  maintained  by  the board of elections] first day of early voting.  Each
     9  county board of elections shall transmit electronically in a  format  to
    10  be  determined  by  the  state board of elections, as it is available, a
    11  copy [of the candidate and contest information contained on each of  its
    12  ballot styles] of a sample ballot for all election districts within that
    13  county  to the state board of elections. [The state board shall create a
    14  system to allow such information to be made publicly available  via  its
    15  website, as well as the website of any county board of elections, and in
    16  such a way as to be accessible with a computer screen-reading program.]
    17    §  2.  Section  7-118 of the election law is amended by adding two new
    18  subdivisions 3-a and 3-b to read as follows:
    19    3-a. The state board of elections shall post a sample ballot for  each
    20  election  district  in  the state on the board's website at least twelve
    21  days prior to the first day of early voting. The board shall  post  such
    22  sample  ballots  in  a searchable database which allows voters to obtain
    23  polling site information, voter district information and a sample ballot

     1  that such voter shall be eligible to cast at the next  election.  Sample
     2  ballots  shall  have  an embedded security tool to prevent tampering and

     4    3-b.  Each county board of elections may post a sample ballot for each
