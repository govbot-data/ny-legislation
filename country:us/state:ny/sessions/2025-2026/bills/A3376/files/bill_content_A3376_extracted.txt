DINOWITZ, BURDICK, KELLES, WEPRIN, FORREST -- read once and referred to the Committee on Insurance AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to requiring owners of firearms, rifles and shotguns to obtain liability insurance The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 400.04 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 400.04 Liability insurance for owners of firearms, rifles and shot- 4 guns. 5 1. Any person in this state who shall own any firearm, rifle or shot- 6 gun as defined in section 265.00 of this chapter shall, prior to such 7 ownership and prior to the issuance of a license under this article, 8 obtain and continuously maintain a policy of liability insurance in such 9 amount or amounts as determined by the superintendent of financial 10 services specifically covering any damages resulting from any negligent 11 acts involving the use of such firearm, rifle or shotgun while it is 12 owned by such person. Failure to maintain such insurance shall be a 13 violation punishable by a fine of at least one hundred fifty dollars and 14 not more than one thousand five hundred dollars and shall also result in 15 the immediate revocation of such owner's registration, license and any 16 other privilege to own or possess a firearm, rifle or shotgun. 17 2. For purposes of this section, a person shall be deemed to be the 18 owner of a firearm, rifle or shotgun if such firearm, rifle or shotgun 19 is lost or stolen until such loss or theft is reported to the police 20 department or sheriff which has jurisdiction in the county, town, city 21 or village in which such owner resides. 22 3. Any person who owns a firearm, rifle or shotgun on the effective 23 date of this section shall obtain the insurance required by this section 24 within ninety days of the issuance of regulations by the superintendent EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
