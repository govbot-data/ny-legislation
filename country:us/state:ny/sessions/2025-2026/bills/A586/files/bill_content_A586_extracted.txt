Paragraph (b) of subdivision 3 of section 14-120 of the 2 election law, as added by chapter 4 of the laws of 2019, is amended to 3 read as follows: 4 (b) If, by application of paragraph (a) of this subdivision, a 5 campaign contribution is attributed to a limited liability company, the 6 contributions shall be further attributed to each member of the limited 7 liability company in proportion to the member's ownership interest in 8 the limited liability company for contributions that exceed twenty-five 9 hundred dollars. 10 § 2.
