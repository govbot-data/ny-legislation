BROWN, MAHER, LEMONDES, ANGELINO, TAGUE, McDONOUGH, DeSTEFANO, GANDOLFO, WALSH, GIGLIO, PALMESANO -- read once and referred to the Committee on Election Law AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to verification of voter qualifications The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The election law is amended by adding a new section 5-108 2 to read as follows: 3 § 5-108. Verification of voter qualifications; citizenship. The state 4 board of elections shall participate in the systematic alien verifica- 5 tion for entitlements (SAVE) program, administered by the United States 6 citizenship and immigration services agency, to verify that a person who 7 is either registered or registering to vote is a United States citizen. 8 Notwithstanding any law, rule, or regulation to the contrary, the state 9 board of elections is authorized to share and transmit all relevant 10 information provided by a person, who is either registered or register- 11 ing to vote, to the state board of elections with the United States 12 citizenship and immigration services agency for the purpose of verifying 13 such person's citizenship status. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
