and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Consulate General of the Co-operative Republic of Guyana in New York upon the occasion of hosting Guyana's 56th Republic Anniversary Interfaith Service, to be held on Sunday, February 22, 2026; and WHEREAS, The Co-operative Republic of Guyana, located on the northern coast of South America, is a nation rich in cultural diversity, natural beauty, and historic significance, having achieved independence from Great Britain on May 26, 1966, and proudly becoming a Republic on February 23, 1970, forging a sovereign identity rooted in democratic governance and cooperative development; and WHEREAS, Guyana's national motto, "One People, One Nation, One Destiny," reflects the spirit of harmony among its citizens of African, Indian, Indigenous, European, Chinese, and Portuguese descent, a multicultural legacy that continues to flourish both in the homeland and throughout the global diaspora; and WHEREAS, The Interfaith Service stands as a sacred and unifying observance, bringing together religious leaders and faithful of many traditions in thanksgiving, reflection, and prayer for the continued prosperity, peace, and advancement of the Guyanese people; and WHEREAS, The Guyanese diaspora has made profound and lasting contributions to the State of New York, enriching the social, cultural, economic, and civic fabric of communities through leadership in public service, healthcare, education, entrepreneurship, labor, faith institutions, and the arts; and WHEREAS, Through cultural celebrations, philanthropic outreach, and steadfast community engagement, Guyanese-Americans have strengthened neighborhood institutions, uplifted immigrant families, and preserved the proud traditions of their ancestral homeland for future generations; and WHEREAS, Providing distinguished leadership for this commemorative observance is Ambassador Michael E. Brotherson, Consul General of the Co-operative Republic of Guyana in New York, who has faithfully served in this role since 2022, working diligently to advance diplomatic relations, support Guyanese nationals abroad, and promote the cultural and economic interests of Guyana throughout the region; and WHEREAS, Under Ambassador Brotherson's stewardship, the Consulate General continues to serve as a vital bridge between nations, fostering unity within the diaspora while strengthening the enduring partnership between Guyana and the United States; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor this meaningful observance, celebrating the history of the Guyanese Republic and the vibrant legacy of its people; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Consulate General of the Co-operative Republic of Guyana in New York upon the occasion of hosting Guyana's 56th Republic Anniversary Interfaith Service on February 22, 2026, and to extend sincere appreciation for its continued commitment to cultural preservation, spiritual unity, and international fellowship; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Ambassador Michael E. Brotherson, Consul General of the Co-operative Republic of Guyana in New York, in recognition of his distinguished leadership and service. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
