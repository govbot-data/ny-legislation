SANTABARBARA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the transportation law and the state finance law, in relation to establishing incentives for attracting and retaining air traffic controllers in New York state The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The transportation law is amended by adding a new section 2 14-o to read as follows: 3 § 14-o. Air traffic controller retention and incentive program. 1. 4 There is hereby established an air traffic controller retention and 5 incentive program which shall be administered by the department of 6 transportation. Such program will provide the following financial 7 incentives to federally employed air traffic controllers working at 8 airports in New York state, in amounts to be determined by the commis- 9 sioner of transportation: 10 (a) Signing bonuses for newly hired air traffic controllers. 11 (b) Retention bonuses for air traffic controllers who remain employed 12 in New York state for a specified time period, to be determined by the 13 commissioner of transportation. 14 (c) Relocation assistance for newly hired air traffic controllers 15 employed at airports with staffing shortages who move to New York state 16 for such employment. 17 2.
