JENSEN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Judiciary -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the domestic relations law, in relation to the distrib- ution of New York state pensions at the time of divorce The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subparagraph 4 of paragraph d of subdivision 5 of part B of 2 section 236 of the domestic relations law, as amended by chapter 281 of 3 the laws of 1980, is amended to read as follows: 4 (4) the loss of inheritance and pension rights upon dissolution of the 5 marriage as of the date of dissolution, provided, however, when calcu- 6 lating such pension rights, the court shall consider the date of dissol- 7 ution for persons who work for the state of New York the same as if such 8 person retired from the state of New York; 9 § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth day after it shall 10 have become a law and shall apply to those who retire on or after such 11 effective date.
