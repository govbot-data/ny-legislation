SAME ASNo Same As
 
SPONSORWilliams
 
COSPNSRDeStefano, McDonough, Novakhov, Simpson

Amd §532, RPT L
 
Provides for the taxation of state owned land for all purposes, exclusive of improvements and in accordance with any payment in lieu of taxes agreement.
