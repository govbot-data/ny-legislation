PALUMBO, WEIK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the criminal procedure law, in relation to qualifying offenses for pre-trial detention The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (d) of subdivision 4 of section 510.10 of the 2 criminal procedure law, as amended by section 2 of part UU of chapter 56 3 of the laws of 2020, is amended to read as follows: 4 (d) a class A felony defined in the penal law, [provided that for 5 class A felonies under article two hundred twenty of the penal law, only 6 class A-I felonies] or a class B felony defined in article two hundred 7 twenty of the penal law shall be a qualifying offense; 8 § 2. This act shall take effect on the thirtieth day after it shall 9 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
