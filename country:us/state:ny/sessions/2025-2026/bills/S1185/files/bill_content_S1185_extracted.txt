The tax law is amended by adding a new section 630-m to 2 read as follows: 3 § 630-m. Gift to the congressional chartered veterans service organ- 4 izations fund. Effective for any tax year commencing on or after January 5 first, two thousand twenty-six, an individual may elect to contribute to 6 the congressional chartered veterans service organizations fund created 7 pursuant to section ninety-nine-ss of the state finance law for the 8 support of congressional chartered veterans service organizations, who 9 help assist veterans in gaining access to the United States department 10 of veterans affairs benefits and the state department of veterans' 11 services benefits they are entitled to. Such contribution shall be in 12 any whole dollar amount and shall not reduce the amount of tax owed by 13 such individual.
