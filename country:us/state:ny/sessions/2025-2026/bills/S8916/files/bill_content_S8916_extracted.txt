BORRELLO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Local Government -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT in relation to permitting the village of Mayville board of trus- tees to transfer the funds of the Mayville ambulance reserve fund and the Mayville fire and rescue equipment reserve fund to the newly formed North Lake Fire District reserve accounts The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Legislative findings. Whereas the village of Mayville board 2 of trustees established a fire and rescue equipment reserve fund and 3 ambulance reserve fund to replace and repair equipment for the Mayville 4 fire department. The fire and rescue equipment reserve fund was estab- 5 lished on February 13, 2018, and was funded entirely by tax revenue from 6 the village of Mayville for the purpose of purchasing firefighting and 7 rescue apparatus and the ambulance reserve fund was established on 8 February 6, 2024, and was funded entirely by billing for emergency 9 medical services (EMS) calls performed by the village of Mayville fire 10 department. Upon the dissolution of the Mayville fire department and 11 merger with the Hartfield fire district and Dewittville fire district to 12 create the North Lake fire district effective September 3, 2024, it was 13 the intention of the Mayville fire department to transfer such funds to 14 another capital reserve fund operated by the newly created North Lake 15 fire district which services the taxpayers of the village of Mayville as 16 well as other taxpayers outside of the village. 17 § 2. Transfer authorized.
