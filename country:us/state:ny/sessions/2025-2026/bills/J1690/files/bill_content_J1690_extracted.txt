and WHEREAS, Daniella and Jonathan Muller has given not only their time and energies, but also their competence, intelligence, and leadership, and consequently have been designated for this special honor; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Daniella and Jonathan Muller upon the occasion of their designation as Guests of Honor by the North Shore Hebrew Academy at its 71st Annual Dinner Celebration at the Old Westbury Hebrew Congregation on Sunday, March 22, 2026; and WHEREAS, Daniella and Jonathan Muller have been called upon to contribute their time and talents to countless civic and charitable endeavors and have always given of themselves unstintingly; and WHEREAS, Throughout the entire period of their service, a period of constructive involvement, Daniella and Jonathan Muller have stood constant in dignity, good grace, and humor; and WHEREAS, Daniella Muller, involved extensively with North Shore Hebrew Academy (NSHA) since her childhood, has served faithfully in various capacities of the academy's Board of Directors; she has held the esteemed roles of executive vice president, president, and vice chairman of the board; and WHEREAS, Under Daniella Muller's steadfast leadership, NSHA has grown its student base, raised critical funds, completed major campus renovations, and built a team of distinguished administrators and faculty by making critical hires; she has dedicated countless hours to the school and broader community, giving generously of her leadership, legal expertise, and fundraising acumen; and WHEREAS, Jonathan Muller has distinguished himself as co-chair of the NSHA Summer Outing, a critical fundraising event for the academy, and in his involvement in recruiting parents to assume active roles in the revered outing; and WHEREAS, Active in the athletic community of North Shore Hebrew Academy, Jonathan Muller proudly mans the scoreboard and offers livestream commentary for hockey games; and WHEREAS, Daniella and Jonathan Muller are well known for their deep community involvement despite their demanding professional lives; their unwavering commitment to community, leadership, and selflessness are reflected in the character, or middot, of their four beloved children: Benjamin, Jack, David, and Philip; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by individuals for the benefit of others which Daniella and Jonathan Muller have displayed throughout their life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Daniella and Jonathan Muller upon the occasion of their designation as Guests of Honor by the North Shore Hebrew Academy at its 71st Annual Dinner Celebration; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Daniella and Jonathan Muller.
