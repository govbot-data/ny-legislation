and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to commend the ANS Association upon the occasion of hosting its 10th Anniversary "I Rise" Conference, to be held on Saturday, July 26, 2025 in Brooklyn, New York; and WHEREAS, Since its founding, the ANS Association has been a beacon of hope and resilience, uplifting individuals and communities through its dedication to providing support, encouragement, and meaningful opportunities locally and globally; and WHEREAS, The theme of this milestone celebration, "I Rise," exemplifies the strength, unity, and determination of those who have triumphed over adversity, and it reflects the organization's ongoing commitment to fostering personal and collective growth; and WHEREAS, Over the past decade, the ANS Association has empowered countless individuals by addressing critical needs, building connections, and inspiring hope for a brighter future, thereby cementing its legacy as a transformative force for good; and WHEREAS, Under the visionary leadership of Apostle Dr. Chloe Natacha and a team of dedicated advocates, the ANS Association continues to expand its impact, embracing the challenges of today while preparing to shape a better tomorrow; and WHEREAS, The 10th Anniversary "I Rise" Conference not only honors individuals who have made significant contributions to their communities but also serves as a call to action for ongoing service and support for the next decade and beyond; and WHEREAS, It is fitting that this Legislative Body recognize and commend the ANS Association for its decade of unwavering commitment to excellence, its empowerment of those in need, and its contributions to building a stronger, more compassionate world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the ANS Association upon the occasion of hosting its 10th Anniversary "I Rise" Conference, and to extend its warmest congratulations and best wishes for continued success in its vital mission; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Apostle Dr. Chloe Natacha and the ANS Association.
