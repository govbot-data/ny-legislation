Amd §3306, Pub Health L; amd §§220.00, 220.43, 220.41, 220.39, 220.21, 220.18, 220.16 & 220.09, Pen L
 
Relates to the classification of the drug xylazine as a schedule I controlled substance, and to the criminal sale and possession of xylazine or preparations, compounds, mixtures or substances containing xylazine.

        AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to the classification
          of the drug xylazine as a controlled substance; and to amend the penal
          law, in relation to the criminal sale and possession  of  xylazine  or
          preparations, compounds, mixtures or substances containing xylazine
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision (e) of schedule I of section 3306 of the public
     2  health law is amended by adding a new paragraph 7 to read as follows:
     3    (7) Xylazine.

     5  chapter  664  of the laws of 1985, is amended and a new subdivision 12-a
     6  is added to read as follows:
