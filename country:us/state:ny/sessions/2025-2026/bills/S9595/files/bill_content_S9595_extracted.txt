COONEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Higher Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to designating the state university of New York college at Geneseo as New York's public honors college The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Legislative findings and declaration. The legislature here- 2 by finds and declares that the state university college at Geneseo has 3 earned national distinction as a premier public liberal arts institu- 4 tion. For decades, it has served an award-winning liberal arts educa- 5 tion with proved outcomes of academic rigor and development of critical 6 thinking and leadership skills. The legislature further finds that the 7 state's member of the Council of Public Liberal Arts Colleges, has 8 formally adopted a mission to be "New York's Public Honors College." It 9 is therefore the intent of this act to codify this mission in statute, 10 ensuring that the college remains a dedicated center for high-ability 11 students and maintains its commitment to academic rigor and an equity- 12 centered honors experience. 13 § 2. The education law is amended by adding new section 352-b to read 14 as follows: 15 § 352-b. New York's public honors college. The state university of New 16 York college at Geneseo shall be designated and known as "New York's 17 public honors college." The primary mission of such public honors 18 college shall be to provide a distinctive and academically rigorous 19 public liberal arts honors education. As New York's public honors 20 college, the institution shall prioritize practices that advance excel- 21 lence, success, and access. 22 § 3.
