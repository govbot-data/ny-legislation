Senate Resolution No. 1954
 
BY: Senator RHOADS
 
        HONORING  Ryan  Tullo  upon  the  occasion  of his
        designation as  recipient  of  the  New  York  State
        Citizenship Award
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor the young
adults  of today, and leaders of tomorrow, whose character, conduct, and
accomplishments best exemplify the ideals of responsibility,  integrity,
and  service that are foundational to civic life in this great State and
Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Ryan  Tullo upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the New
York State Citizenship Award; he will be recognized at a  Senior  Awards
Night event on Thursday, May 28, 2026; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  New  York State Citizenship Award recognizes students
who demonstrate outstanding citizenship through excellence in academics,
meaningful   community   involvement,   leadership   in    school    and
extracurricular  activities,  and  a  consistent  commitment  to serving
others with responsibility and respect; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ryan  Tullo,  a  senior  at  Wantagh  High   School,   has
distinguished  himself  as  a  young  adult  of  exceptional  character,
demonstrating dependability, initiative, and a  strong  sense  of  civic
responsibility in both his school and broader community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  the  area  of  academic  citizenship,  Ryan  Tullo has
consistently pursued academic excellence while  contributing  positively
to  the  school  environment  as a member of the National Business Honor
Society, National Honor Society, and World Language Honor Society,  each
of  which  reflects  his  dedication to scholarship, responsibility, and
intellectual growth; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ryan Tullo has further demonstrated academic integrity  and
leadership  by maintaining high standards of personal accountability and
serving as a role model to his peers, exemplifying what it means to be a
responsible student citizen within the school community; and
 
  WHEREAS, In  the  spirit  of  school  citizenship,  Ryan  Tullo  has
actively   contributed   to  the  culture  of  his  school  through  his
involvement in  organizations  that  promote  leadership,  service,  and
collaboration among students, helping to strengthen a sense of unity and
shared purpose within Wantagh High School; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletically,  Ryan  Tullo  has exemplified sportsmanship,
teamwork, and respect for others through his  participation  in  varsity
baseball  and  golf,  earning recognition from Long Island Baseball, All
League (2024 and 2025), Preseason All Long Island, and Defensive MVP  in
the  Leiderman Cup, all of which reflect his dedication, discipline, and
commitment to excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Through athletics, Ryan Tullo has further demonstrated  the
principles  of  citizenship  by  supporting his teammates, demonstrating
perseverance in competition, and representing  his  school  with  pride,
humility, and integrity both on and off the field; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ryan  Tullo has exhibited outstanding leadership and civic
responsibility through his roles as Founder and President of the  Health
and Wellness Club, Treasurer of the National Business Honor Society, and
Secretary of the Entrepreneurship Club, where he has helped guide peers,
organize  initiatives,  and  encourage  positive  engagement  within the
student body; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  each  of  these  leadership  roles,  Ryan  Tullo   has
demonstrated   a  strong  commitment  to  service,  accountability,  and
collaboration, fostering environments in which students  are  encouraged
to contribute meaningfully to their school and community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ryan Tullo has further strengthened his citizenship profile
through  extensive  community  involvement,  including work at the front
desk of the Long Island Baseball facility, coaching winter workouts  for
Long  Island Baseball, and assisting younger athletes in developing both
skills and character through structured athletic programs; and
 
  WHEREAS, In service to his community, Ryan Tullo has contributed his
time  and  effort  to  the  Helping  Hands  Mission  by  assisting  with
Thanksgiving  boxes,  participated  in  the Wantagh baseball summer camp
alongside his peers, and coached at the 11U  baseball  clinic,  each  of
which  reflects  a  genuine commitment to helping others and giving back
without expectation of recognition; and
 
  WHEREAS, Through these experiences, Ryan Tullo has demonstrated that
citizenship is not only expressed through formal achievement,  but  also
through  consistent  acts  of  service,  mentorship,  and responsibility
toward others in both structured and informal settings; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ryan Tullo's record reflects a young  man  who  understands
the  importance  of  contributing  to  the  well-being of his community,
supporting younger students and athletes, and using his skills and  time
to positively impact those around him; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  impactful  leadership,  steadfast responsibility, and
unwavering drive which Ryan Tullo has continually displayed have  earned
him   this   distinguished  recognition,  reflecting  his  strong  moral
character and dedication to civic engagement; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ryan Tullo plans to attend Lynn University  where  he  will
continue  his  academic  and  athletic  career  by  playing baseball and
studying criminal justice, with aspirations of becoming a police officer
or serving within the Federal Bureau  of  Investigation  (FBI),  thereby
continuing  his  commitment  to public service and community protection;
and
 
  WHEREAS, As Ryan Tullo looks forward to  the  next  chapter  of  his
life,  he  is poised to further develop his leadership abilities, expand
his academic and professional  goals,  and  continue  making  meaningful
contributions to society through service and public responsibility; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Poised  with a great sense of enthusiasm and purpose, this
scholar now stands honorably with just pride in his accomplishments  and
in   eager   anticipation   of   future  challenges,  understanding  and
appreciating the guidance, encouragement, and support  provided  by  his
teachers, mentors, coaches, family, and friends; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Ryan Tullo upon the occasion of his designation  as  recipient  of
the New York State Citizenship Award; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Ryan Tullo.
