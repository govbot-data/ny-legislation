Kennedy High School in Bellmore, New York; and WHEREAS, Citizenship, at its highest expression, is demonstrated through responsibility, integrity, and a consistent commitment to the well-being of one's community; Sophie Shiner has exemplified these qualities throughout her high school career, earning the respect of peers, educators, and community members alike; and WHEREAS, Sophie Shiner has distinguished herself as a young leader who understands that citizenship is not defined solely by participation, but by active contribution; through her service, leadership roles, and daily conduct, she has consistently modeled what it means to be a conscientious and engaged member of society; and WHEREAS, As Track and Field Captain, Sophie Shiner has demonstrated citizenship through leadership, encouraging teamwork, perseverance, and accountability among her peers while fostering a culture of mutual respect and shared achievement within her athletic community; and WHEREAS, In her role as Key Club Vice President, Sophie Shiner has further embodied civic responsibility by helping to organize and support service initiatives that strengthen her school and local community, reinforcing the importance of volunteerism and collective action in building a stronger society; and WHEREAS, As a founding member of the Psychology Club, Sophie Shiner has contributed to the development of a thoughtful academic environment that promotes awareness, dialogue, and understanding of human behavior, reflecting her commitment to intellectual citizenship and the pursuit of knowledge in service of others; and WHEREAS, Sophie Shiner has extended her sense of citizenship beyond school walls through meaningful community service, including her work supporting children with special needs, where she has demonstrated patience, empathy, and a steadfast dedication to ensuring that all individuals feel supported and valued; and WHEREAS, Through her engagement in social media advocacy, Sophie Shiner has responsibly utilized modern platforms to create awareness and provide encouragement to others, showing that civic engagement today includes thoughtful and respectful communication in both physical and digital spaces; and WHEREAS, Sophie Shiner's actions are deeply informed by her personal family experiences, which have shaped her understanding of resilience, responsibility, and the importance of lifting others during times of difficulty, further strengthening her commitment to compassionate citizenship; and WHEREAS, In every endeavor, Sophie Shiner has demonstrated reliability and initiative, consistently stepping forward to serve as a positive example for her peers and contributing meaningfully to the betterment of her school environment and broader community; and WHEREAS, Sophie Shiner's aspiration to become a psychologist reflects her enduring commitment to serving others, as she seeks to dedicate her future career to helping individuals navigate challenges and improve their quality of life through care, understanding, and professional support; and WHEREAS, Sophie Shiner's academic diligence, coupled with her strong moral compass, has positioned her as a role model of student citizenship, one who recognizes that education and service are deeply interconnected in the development of responsible and engaged adults; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body recognizes Sophie Shiner as a young citizen whose character, service, and leadership reflect the very best ideals of community responsibility, and whose ongoing contributions promise a future marked by continued dedication to the public good; and WHEREAS, Poised with a strong sense of purpose and responsibility, this scholar now stands honorably, with just pride in her accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the guidance and support provided by her teachers, mentors, family, and community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Sophie Shiner upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the New York State Citizenship Award; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sophie Shiner.
