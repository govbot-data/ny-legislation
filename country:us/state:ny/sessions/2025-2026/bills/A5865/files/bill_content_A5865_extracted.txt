STECK -- read once and referred to the Committee on Consumer Affairs and Protection AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to cash out requirements for prepaid debit cards The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 5-b of section 396-i of the general business 2 law, as added by chapter 668 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 5-b. A gift certificate, other than [an open loop gift certificate or] 5 a promotional gift certificate, with a remaining value of less than 6 [five] ten dollars may be redeemed upon request for its cash value. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
