As used in this section, the following terms shall have 2 the following meanings: 3 a. Within sixty days after the effective date of this act, each 9 covered provider shall prepare and submit to the department of public 10 service and the legislature a written report that shall make findings 11 and recommendations concerning the quality and availability of line-pow- 12 ered voice services.
