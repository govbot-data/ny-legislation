Zarb School of Business at Hofstra University, and Juris Doctor (J.D.) from Jacob D. Fuchsberg Law Center at Touro University; and WHEREAS, Deeply committed to her community, Jothy Narendran is involved with several charitable, educational, and business-related organizations; besides serving as President of Spectrum Designs Foundation, she is Chair of the Touro Law Center Board of Governors and is a former Chair of the Go Red for Women LI Campaign Event for the American Heart Association Long Island Chapter in 2021; she has also been a contributor to Big Brothers and Big Sisters of Long Island for the past 25 years; and WHEREAS, Throughout her career, Jothy Narendran has received numerous awards, honors, and distinctions, including the Long Island Business Influencer in Law (2024), Long Island Business News Hall of Fame (2023), Blank State Media Women of Distinction (2023), Herald Media Publications Top Lawyers of Long Island (2023), Touro University's Paul S. Miller Award (2021), and the Big Brothers and Big Sisters Presidential Award (2007); and WHEREAS, Jothy Narendran has exemplified commitment to her community, and her hard work and impressive accomplishments are a testament to her success; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by individuals for the benefit of others which Jothy Narendran has displayed throughout her life; and WHEREAS, It has always been the objective of this Legislative Body to honor and support those individuals who have displayed their commitment to the betterment of their communities; and WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records this tribute to Jothy Narendran, that future generations may know and appreciate her admirable character and the respect and esteem in which she is held by her peers; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Jothy Narendran, President of Spectrum Designs Foundation, upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition as honoree at the Summer Soiree; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Jothy Narendran.
