A10156 Summary:

BILL NOA10156
 
SAME ASSAME AS S09402
 
SPONSORGiglio
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add §1244, amd §1242, V & T L
 
Establishes bicycle with electric assist safety courses; requires purchasers of bicycles with electric assist to provide a bicycle with electric assist safety certificate at the time of purchase; requires operators of bicycles with electric assist to carry identification when operating such bicycles.

                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                    February 12, 2026

        Introduced by M. of A. GIGLIO -- read once and referred to the Committee
          on Transportation
 
        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to establishing
          bicycle  with  electric assist safety courses, requiring purchasers of
          bicycles with electric assist  to  provide  a  bicycle  with  electric
          assist  safety  certificate  and  requiring operators of bicycles with
          electric assist to carry identification when operating such bicycles
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The  vehicle  and  traffic law is amended by adding a new
     2  section 1244 to read as follows:
     3    § 1244. Bicycle with electric assist safety course. 1. The commission-
     4  er shall initiate  and  put  into  effect  a  comprehensive  educational
     5  program  designed  to advance bicycle with electric assist safety, which
     6  provides for the training of all purchasers of  bicycles  with  electric
     7  assist in the safe operation of such bicycles.
     8    2.  "Bicycle  with  electric  assist  safety certificate" shall mean a
     9  certificate issued by the commissioner evidencing that the holder there-
    10  of has successfully completed a course of instruction  in  bicycle  with
    11  electric  assist  safety  as  provided in this section. The commissioner
    12  shall put into effect a plan for the training of all purchasers, and  in
    13  the  case  of  purchases by minors, the parent or guardian of such minor
    14  purchasers, of bicycles with electric assist and the issuance of bicycle
    15  with electric assist safety  certificates.  For  the  purposes  of  this
    16  section,  the  term  "bicycle with electric assist safety" shall include
    17  instruction in the laws relating to and safe operation of bicycles  with
    18  electric assist.
    19    3. "Bicycle with electric assist safety certificate" shall also mean a
    20  certificate issued by the commissioner evidencing that the holder there-
    21  of has successfully completed a course of instruction in the laws relat-
    22  ing  to and safe operation of bicycles with electric assist. The commis-
    23  sioner is authorized to collect a fee not to exceed ten dollars for  the
    24  issuance of such certificate.

     1    4.  The  commissioner is authorized and directed to establish a system
     2  for tracking and replacing bicycle with electric assist  safety  certif-
     3  icates  issued  by the commissioner. The commissioner is further author-
     4  ized to require that any other entity approved by  the  commissioner  to
     5  provide  courses  of  instruction and award bicycle with electric assist
     6  safety certificates establish a system for tracking and replacing  bicy-
     7  cle  with  electric  assist  safety  certificates  so  as  to enable the
     8  replacement of lost or stolen certificates to those who have  previously
     9  been awarded such certificates.
    10    5.  For the purpose of giving such courses of instruction and awarding
    11  bicycle with electric assist safety certificates, the  commissioner  may
    12  designate  as the commissioner's agent any person the commissioner deems
    13  qualified to act in such capacity. No charge whatsoever shall be made to
    14  any person less than eighteen years of age for the issuance of a bicycle
    15  with electric assist safety  certificate.  Persons  less  than  eighteen
    16  years  of age may be charged a fee for instruction in bicycle with elec-
    17  tric assist safety, except that the office may not  charge  a  fee  when
    18  providing such instruction to persons less than eighteen years of age.
    19    6.  The  commissioner is authorized, at the commissioner's discretion,
    20  to develop a method for approving internet-based bicycle  with  electric
    21  assist  safety  courses  as  an appropriate and effective method for the
    22  administration and completion of training in the laws  relating  to  and
    23  safe  operation  of  bicycles  with  electric assist for the purposes of
    24  awarding a bicycle with electric assist safety certificate  pursuant  to
    25  this  section.  All approved internet-based bicycle with electric assist
    26  safety courses shall employ methods to ensure attendance of the  student
    27  throughout the course.
    28    §  2. Section 1242 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding
    29  two new subdivisions 14 and 15 to read as follows:
    30    14. No person, firm, association or corporation engaged in  the  busi-
    31  ness  of  selling or leasing bicycles with electric assist shall sell or
    32  lease any bicycle with electric assist  unless  the  purchaser  of  such
    33  bicycle  provides  a  bicycle  with  electric  assist safety certificate
    34  issued pursuant to section twelve hundred forty-four of this article. If
    35  the purchaser of a bicycle with electric assist is  a  minor,  or  if  a
    36  bicycle  with  electric assist is being purchased for a minor, then both
    37  the minor and the parent or guardian of such minor shall each provide  a
    38  bicycle with electric assist safety certificate certifying that both the
    39  minor  and the parent or guardian of such minor have completed a bicycle
    40  with electric assist safety  course  as  required  pursuant  to  section
    41  twelve hundred forty-four of this article.
    42    15. Every operator of a bicycle with electric assist shall carry iden-
    43  tification  with  them when operating such bicycle with electric assist.
    44  For the purposes of this  subdivision,  "identification"  shall  mean  a
    45  drivers' license, state-issued identification card, or, where the opera-
    46  tor is a minor, a school-issued identification card.
    47    § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
