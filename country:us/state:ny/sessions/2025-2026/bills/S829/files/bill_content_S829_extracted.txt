GALLIVAN -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law and the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to safety in active work zones The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding four new sections 2 120.65, 120.66, 125.28 and 125.29 to read as follows: 3 § 120.65 Vehicular assault in an active work zone in the second degree. 4 1. A person is guilty of vehicular assault in an active work zone in 5 the second degree when, with criminal negligence, such person: 6 a. enters or intrudes into an active work zone while operating a motor 7 vehicle; and 8 b. such entry or intrusion causes serious physical injury to another 9 person. 10 2. For purposes of this section the term "active work zone" shall mean 11 the physical area on a public highway, street or private road where 12 construction, maintenance or utility work is being conducted, which area 13 is properly designated to be an active work zone by signs, cones and/or 14 other traffic control devices and where workers are physically present. 15 Vehicular assault in an active work zone in the second degree is a 16 class E felony. 17 § 120.66 Vehicular assault in an active work zone in the first degree. 18 1. A person is guilty of vehicular assault in an active work zone in 19 the first degree when such person recklessly: 20 a. enters or intrudes into an active work zone while operating a motor 21 vehicle; and 22 b. such entry or intrusion causes serious physical injury to another 23 person. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new section 37 1221-a to read as follows: 38 § 1221-a. Intrusion into an active work zone. 1. No driver of a vehi- 39 cle, operator of a bicycle or pedestrian shall enter or intrude into an 40 active work zone, except upon direction from a flag person, police offi- 41 cer or other designated person in charge of traffic control or direction 42 from a traffic control device regulating entry therein. For purposes of 43 this section, the term "active work zone" shall mean the physical area 44 on a public highway, street or private road where construction, mainte- 45 nance or utility work is being conducted, which area is properly desig- 46 nated to be an active work zone by signs, cones and/or other traffic 47 control devices and where workers are physically present. 48 2.
