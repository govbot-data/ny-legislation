Paragraph a of subdivision 1 and subdivision 8 of section 2 11-0913 of the environmental conservation law, paragraph a of subdivi- 3 sion 1 as amended by chapter 183 of the laws of 2024 and subdivision 8 4 as amended by chapter 267 of the laws of 1978, are amended to read as 5 follows: 6 a.
