Election inspectors and poll clerks; emergency provisions for 10 filling vacancies or absences. 1. If, at the time of a meeting of the 11 inspectors, there shall be a vacancy, or if any inspector shall be 12 absent, the inspector present who is the designee of the same party as 13 the absent inspector shall appoint a qualified inspector from the addi- 14 tional inspector list established pursuant to section 3-406 of this 15 title until such additional inspector list is exhausted.
