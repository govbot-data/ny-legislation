Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J02227 Summary:BILL NOJ02227  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORPERSAUD COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ02227 Text: Senate Resolution No. 2227 BY: Senator PERSAUD COMMENDING Class of 2026 Valedictorian Aleekah Ault and Salutatorian Deon Archer of Brooklyn Democracy Academy, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord with its long-standing traditions, to honor the youth of today, the leaders of tomorrow, whose character and achievements exemplify the ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of 2026 Valedictorian Aleekah Ault and Salutatorian Deon Archer of Brooklyn Democracy Academy, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and WHEREAS, Valedictorian Aleekah Ault and Salutatorian Deon Archer represent the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious resource, our youth; their achievements have brought enduring honor to their families and communities and should be recognized and saluted; and WHEREAS, As Valedictorian Aleekah Ault and Salutatorian Deon Archer take leave of Brooklyn Democracy Academy, they bestow a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement, and community service to which others may aspire; and WHEREAS, Valedictorian Aleekah Ault and Salutatorian Deon Archer may now stand with pride as they assess their achievements, experience the satisfaction of their labors, and feel the joy of their accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Class of 2026 Valedictorian Aleekah Ault and Salutatorian Deon Archer of Brooklyn Democracy Academy, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Valedictorian Aleekah Ault and Salutatorian Deon Archer.
