AN ACT to amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, in
relation to permitting requests for self-exclusion from gambling to be
made electronically
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 1344 of the racing, pari-mutuel
2 wagering and breeding law, as added by chapter 174 of the laws of 2013,
3 is amended to read as follows:
4 2. The regulations of the commission shall establish procedures for
5 placements on, and removals from, the list of self-excluded persons,
6 including procedures that allow a person to submit a request pursuant to
7 subdivision one of this section electronically. Such regulations shall
8 establish procedures for the transmittal to licensed gaming facilities
9 of identifying information concerning self-excluded persons, and shall
10 require licensed gaming facilities to establish procedures designed, at
11 a minimum, to remove self-excluded persons from targeted mailings or
12 other forms of advertising or promotions and deny self-excluded persons
13 access to credit, complimentaries, check cashing privileges, club
14 programs, and other similar benefits.
15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
