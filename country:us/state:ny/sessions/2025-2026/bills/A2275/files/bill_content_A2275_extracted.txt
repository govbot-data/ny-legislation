ROSENTHAL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, the railroad law and the public authorities law, in relation to the use and availability of opioid antagonists in train stations and on trains operated by public transit authorities The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1.
