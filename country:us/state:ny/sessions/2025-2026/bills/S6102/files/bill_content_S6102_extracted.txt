PARKER, BAILEY, MYRIE, RIVERA, SEPULVEDA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Consumer Protection AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to the suspension of a health club contract The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section 2 624-a to read as follows: 3 § 624-a. Rights of suspension of contracts for services. Every 4 contract for services at a health club, which is premised upon the use 5 of such health club's facility or premises, and where the use of such 6 facility or premises (1) has ceased, partially or fully, (2) has been 7 rendered impossible or illegal, or (3) where the non-use of the facility 8 or premises, or the non-rendering of service, has frustrated the purpose 9 of the contract, shall provide the buyer, at the option of the health 10 club, the right to suspend payment for all services until access to the 11 facility or premises is restored, or the right to a credit for dues and 12 fees paid. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
