Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01839 Summary:BILL NOJ01839  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORSTEC COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01839 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1839 BY: Senator STEC CONGRATULATING Liam Foy upon the occasion of reaching 1,000 points in his career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team WHEREAS, Excellence and success in competitive sports can be achieved only through strenuous practice, team play, and team spirit, nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition enhances the moral and physical development of the young people of this State, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the value of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, imparting a desire for success, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Liam Foy upon the occasion of reaching 1,000 points in his career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team; and WHEREAS, Liam Foy is an outstanding scholar-athlete who excels in many aspects in his community; from the court to the classroom, he brings joy and hard work to all he does; and WHEREAS, As a student at Bolton Central High School, Liam Foy has made a profound impact on many of his peers, teammates, coaches, teachers, and community; this exceptional athlete has been a major contributing factor to the success of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team and continues to bring his leadership skills and his positivity to the program; and WHEREAS, During the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team's championship game, Liam Foy scored his 1000th career point; his team captured the championship with an impressive 65-49 win, ending the season with an overall record of 26-1; and WHEREAS, Thanks to the commitment and effort of Liam Foy, his fellow players, and his coaches, this Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball state championship title is the first state championship that any Bolton Central High School athletic team has won in the school's history; and WHEREAS, Liam Foy has been rightfully praised for his many achievements, and it is his strong work ethic, kind heart, and willingness to always help and encourage others that makes him an inspiration and significant part of his community; and WHEREAS, Poised eagerly with enthusiasm and determined purpose, this exemplary athlete now stands honorably, with just pride in his accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the caring counsel and support provided by his coaches, teachers, and parents; and WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to commend and pay tribute to those young people who, by achieving outstanding success in athletic competition, have inspired and brought pride to our great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Liam Foy upon the occasion of reaching 1,000 points in his career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Liam Foy.
