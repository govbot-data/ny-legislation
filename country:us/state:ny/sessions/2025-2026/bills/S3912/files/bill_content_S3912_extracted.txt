HARCKHAM -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Procurement and Contracts AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation to prohibiting the purchase of single-use plastic products by state agencies The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 165 of the state finance law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 10 to read as follows: 3 10. Single-use plastic products. (a) For the purposes of this subdivi- 4 sion: 5 (i) "Single use plastic product" shall mean plastic items intended to 6 be disposed of immediately after use, including plastic and polystyrene 7 food and beverage containers, bottles, straws, cups, cutlery, and 8 disposable plastic bags. 9 (ii) "State agency" shall mean any agency or department over which the 10 governor has executive authority, including all offices and divisions 11 thereof, as well as all public authorities for which the governor 12 appoints the chair, the chief executive, or the majority of board 13 members, including all offices and divisions thereof, except for the 14 Port Authority of New York and New Jersey. State agency shall include 15 the state university of New York and the city university of New York. 16 (b) (i) Beginning January first, two thousand thirty, a state agency 17 shall not purchase any single-use plastic products to be used at any 18 event, briefing, and meetings hosted or sponsored by such state agency. 19 (ii) Beginning January first, two thousand thirty-five, a state agency 20 shall not purchase any single-use plastic products. 21 (c) Nothing in this subdivision shall be construed to prevent the 22 purchase of single-use plastic products by a state agency under the 23 following circumstances: 24 (i) when no alternative is available or practicable; EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
