Subsection (c) of section 612 of the tax law is amended by 2 adding a new paragraph 48 to read as follows: 3 (48) an amount equal to the interest paid by the taxpayer during the 4 taxable year on any qualified education loan to the extent and as 5 provided in 26 USC § 221. 6 § 2.
