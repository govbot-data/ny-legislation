Section 355 of the education law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 22 to read as follows: 3 22. The state university trustees shall adopt a policy requiring that 4 each institution of the state university of New York vet all information 5 displayed on their websites and marketing materials to ensure the infor- 6 mation is accurate and impartial. 7 § 2. The board of trustees shall adopt a policy requiring that each 10 institution of the city university of New York vet all information 11 displayed on their websites and marketing materials to ensure the infor- 12 mation is accurate and impartial. 13 § 3. This act shall take effect on the first of July next succeeding 14 the date on which it shall have become a law.
