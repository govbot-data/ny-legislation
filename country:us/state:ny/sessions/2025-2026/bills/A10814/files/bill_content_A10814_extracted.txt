Section 305 of the education law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 63 to read as follows: 3 63. The commissioner shall require the public schools athletic league, 4 the New York state public high school athletic association, and other 5 high school interscholastic athletic associations hold separate state 6 interscholastic athletic championships for public and non-public 7 schools.
