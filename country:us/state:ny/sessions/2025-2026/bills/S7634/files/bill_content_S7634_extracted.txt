The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a new 6 section 19-0332 to read as follows: 7 § 19-0332. Certificates of compliance for purposes of the vehicle and 8 traffic law. 9 1. For the purposes of sections three hundred ninety-eight-c and 10 section three hundred ninety-eight-d of the vehicle and traffic law, the 11 commissioner or the commissioner's designated representative is hereby 12 authorized to issue certificates of compliance for motor vehicle repair 13 shops immediately adjacent to the real property boundary line of a 14 building occupied exclusively as a school. No such certificate shall be 15 issued unless the facility to which it is applicable is in compliance 16 with section three hundred ninety-eight-d of the vehicle and traffic 17 law. The certificate of compliance shall affirm that the motor vehicle 18 repair shop does not contain a coating line. 19 2. For the purposes of this section, "coating line" shall mean the 20 application of one or more surface coatings, using one or more applica- 21 tors, together with any associated drying or curing areas. "Surface 22 coating" shall mean a material applied onto or impregnated into a subs- 23 trate for protective, decorative, or functional purposes. Such materials 24 include, but are not limited to: paints, varnishes, primers, sealers, 25 adhesives, inks and maskants. 26 3. Upon a determination by the department that a motor vehicle repair 27 shop that is required to apply for a certificate of compliance under the 28 vehicle and traffic law has not done so, the department shall issue a 29 notice of non-compliance to the motor vehicle repair shop. The notice of 30 non-compliance shall clearly state the deadline by which the motor vehi- 31 cle repair shop must apply for a certificate of compliance in order to 32 not be found in violation of this section. 33 4. Upon determination of a violation of this section, in addition to 34 taking any enforcement action authorized under this chapter, the depart- 35 ment shall notify the department of motor vehicles of the violation. 36 5. Upon a denial of an application for a certificate of compliance by 37 the department, the department shall notify the department of motor 38 vehicles of such denial. 39 § 4.
