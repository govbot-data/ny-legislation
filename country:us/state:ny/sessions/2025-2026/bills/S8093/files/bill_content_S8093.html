Allows members of the New York city fire department pension fund to obtain service credit for retirement eligibility and retirement allowance from any of the public retirement systems of New York state.

        AN ACT to amend the retirement and social security law, in  relation  to
          allowing  members of the New York city fire department pension fund to
          obtain service credit for retirement eligibility and retirement allow-
          ance from any of the public retirement systems of New York state
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subparagraph  (iii)  of  paragraph  2 of subdivision c of
     2  section 513 of the retirement and  social  security  law  is  renumbered
     3  subparagraph  (iv)  and  a  new  subparagraph  (iii) is added to read as
     4  follows:
     5    (iii) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to  the  contrary,  a
     6  member of the New York city fire department pension fund subject to this
     7  article  shall  be eligible to obtain credit for any period of allowable
     8  prior service, in any of the public retirement systems of the state,  as
     9  defined  in subdivision twenty-three of section five hundred one of this
    10  article, including the New York state and  local  employees'  retirement
    11  system,  New York state and local police and fire retirement system, New
    12  York state teachers' retirement system, New York city employees' retire-
    13  ment system, New York city teachers' retirement system,  New  York  city
    14  police  pension fund, New York city fire department pension fund and the
    15  New York city board of education retirement  system,  and  such  service
    16  shall  be deemed to be in service of the uniformed force of the New York
    17  city fire department for purposes of eligibility  for  benefits  and  to
    18  determine the amount of benefits under the New York city fire department
    19  pension  fund,  provided that such member pays or transfers into the New
    20  York city fire department pension  fund  all  member  contributions  set
    21  forth  in  section five hundred seventeen of this article plus interest,
    22  at a rate of five percent per annum. For a  member  who  transfers  such
    23  contributions from any of the public retirement systems of the state, as
