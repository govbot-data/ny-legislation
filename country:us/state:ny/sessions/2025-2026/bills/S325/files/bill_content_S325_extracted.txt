BAILEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT providing for accelerated payments to the city school district of the city of Mount Vernon The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. In order to avoid endangering the fiscal stability of the 2 Mount Vernon city school district, certain apportionments payable to the 3 city school district of the city of Mount Vernon shall be paid on an 4 accelerated schedule as follows: 5 a. (1) Notwithstanding any other provisions of law, for aid payable in 6 the school years 2025-2026 through 2052-2053 upon application to the 7 commissioner of education submitted not sooner than the second Monday in 8 June of the school year in which such aid is payable and not later than 9 the Friday following the third Monday in June of the school year in 10 which such aid is payable, or ten days after the effective date of this 11 act, whichever shall be later, the Mount Vernon city school district 12 shall be eligible to receive an apportionment pursuant to this act in an 13 amount equal to the product of five million dollars ($5,000,000) and the 14 quotient of the positive difference of thirty minus the number of school 15 years elapsed since the 2025-2026 school year divided by thirty. 16 (2) Funds apportioned pursuant to this subdivision shall be used for 17 services and expenses of the city school district of the city of Mount 18 Vernon and shall be applied to support of its educational programs and 19 any liability incurred by such city school district in carrying out its 20 functions and responsibilities under the education law. 21 b. (1) On or before the first day of each month beginning in July 2026 36 and ending in June 2053, the chief fiscal officer and the superintendent 37 of schools of the Mount Vernon city school district shall prepare and 38 submit to the board of education of the city school district a report of 39 the fiscal condition of the school district, including but not limited 40 to the most current available data on fund balances on funds maintained 41 by the school district and the district's use of the apportionments 42 provided pursuant to subdivisions a and b of this section. 43 (2) Such monthly report shall be in a format prescribed by the commis- 44 sioner of education.
