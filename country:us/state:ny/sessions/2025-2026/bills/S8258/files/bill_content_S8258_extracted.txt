Legislative findings. In order to increase the authority of 2 the superintendent of financial services to respond to the harm posed by 3 impaired insurers in this state, the legislature finds that it is in the 4 best interest of the people of this state to enact an administrative 5 supervision statute. The superintendent of financial services has the 6 right and responsibility to enforce the insurance law and the authority 7 to seek redress against any person responsible for the impairment or 8 insolvency of the insurer, and nothing in this act is intended to 9 restrict or limit such right, responsibility, or authority. 10 § 2.
