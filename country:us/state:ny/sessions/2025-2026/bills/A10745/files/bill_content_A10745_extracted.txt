BRONSON -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Real Property Taxation AN ACT to amend the real property tax law, in relation to freezing the assessed value of real property owned by persons aged sixty-five or over, for the purposes of determining taxes owed on such property The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 467 of the real property tax law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 1-a to read as follows: 3 1-a. Notwithstanding any provision of state or local law, rule, regu- 4 lation or ordinance to the contrary, for the purposes of determining 5 taxation under this chapter, the assessed value of real property owned 6 by one or more persons, each of whom is sixty-five years of age or over, 7 shall not be increased beyond the assessed value determined on the valu- 8 ation date occurring most recently before the date all such persons 9 reached the age of sixty-five, regardless of the actual assessed value 10 of such real property at the time of taxation. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately, and shall apply to taxa- 12 ble years beginning on or after such date.
