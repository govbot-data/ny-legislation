Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01687 Summary:BILL NOJ01687  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORRHOADS COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01687 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1687 BY: Senator RHOADS HONORING Danny and Lisa Rosen upon the occasion of their designation as recipients of the Young Israel of Plainview Community Leadership Award for Outstanding Service to the Community WHEREAS, Individuals who give of their time and energies and serve the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and WHEREAS, Danny and Lisa Rosen have given not only their time and energies, but also their competence, intelligence, and leadership, and consequently have been designated for this special honor; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Danny and Lisa Rosen upon the occasion of their designation as recipients of the Young Israel of Plainview Community Leadership Award for Outstanding Service to the Community to be celebrated on Sunday, March 22, 2026; and WHEREAS, Danny and Lisa Rosen have been called upon to contribute their time and talents to countless civic and charitable endeavors and have always given of themselves unstintingly; and WHEREAS, Throughout the entire period of their service, a period of constructive involvement, Danny and Lisa Rosen have stood constant in dignity, good grace, and humor; and WHEREAS, After moving to Plainview, New York in 2013, Danny and Lisa Rosen were first introduced to the Young Isreal community in 2017 when their son, Jared, enrolled in Nursery Aleph at HANC Plainview; they were so impressed by the community's warm, vibrant, and welcoming nature, they decided to move closer and officially join YIP in 2019; and WHEREAS, Together, Danny and Lisa Rosen raise their two children Jared and Zachary in Plainview and are proud to be part of the Young Isreal community; and WHEREAS, In his professional life, Danny Rosen is a Senior Technical Program Manager at Google where he founded the Google Jewish Customs project and was responsible for launching the "What Time is Shabbat?" feature; and WHEREAS, Danny Rosen is an active participant on the YIP Board, where he heads the Marketing Committee and led the creation of YIP's backyard project; and WHEREAS, Lisa Rosen serves as the Senior Associate Provost for Institutional Research and Strategic Analysis at Hofstra University, where she oversees the university's reporting and data analytics hub; and WHEREAS, Together, Danny and Lisa Rosen prioritize giving back to the community by supporting shul initiatives, hosting Shabbos meals and participating in meal trains; by leading through example, they hope to model for Jared and Zachary the vital importance of Chesed and Tikkun Olam; and WHEREAS, Both avid readers, Danny and Lisa Rosen enjoy hiking, playing pinball, bowling, and traveling with their children; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by individuals for the benefit of others which Danny and Lisa Rosen have displayed throughout their life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Danny and Lisa Rosen upon the occasion of their designation as recipients of the Young Israel of Plainview Community Leadership Award for Outstanding Service to the Community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Danny and Lisa Rosen.
