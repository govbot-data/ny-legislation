The wholesale value of the part shall be the greater of: (a) 15 the amount the dealer paid for the part or a substantially identical 16 part; (b) the cost of the part in a current or prior established price 17 schedule issued by the franchisor or issued by a third party that has 18 previously supplied the part to the motor vehicle dealer; (c) the cost 19 of a substantially identical part in a current or prior established 20 price schedule issued by the franchisor or by a third party; or (d) the 21 reasonable wholesale price for the part. 22 § 2.
