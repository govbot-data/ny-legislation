PEOPLES-STOKES -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to apportionment for charter school tuition payments The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 3602 of the education law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 42 to read as follows: 3 42. Aid for charter tuition payments. (a) Any school district that is 4 required to pay tuition to charter schools pursuant to section twenty- 5 eight hundred fifty-six of this chapter in the two thousand twenty-five- 6 -two thousand twenty-six school year and thereafter shall be eligible 7 for an apportionment under this subdivision. 8 (b) Each school year, any such eligible school district shall submit 9 the total amount of charter school tuition paid by the district in the 10 base year on a form prescribed by the commissioner. 11 (c) The apportionment for charter school payments shall equal the 12 positive difference, if any, of the amount of tuition paid in the base 13 year less the amount of tuition paid in the two thousand twenty-five-- 14 two thousand twenty-six school year. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
