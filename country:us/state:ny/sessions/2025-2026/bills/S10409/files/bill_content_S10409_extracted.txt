SEPULVEDA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Housing, Construction and Community Development AN ACT to amend the real property law, in relation to evictions in manufactured home parks The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph 4 of subdivision d of section 233 of the real 2 property law, as amended by chapter 566 of the laws of 1996, is amended 3 to read as follows: 4 4. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraphs one and two of this 5 subdivision, nor of any other general, special or local law, rule or 6 regulation to the contrary, the officer to whom the warrant is directed 7 and delivered shall give [seventy-two hours] fourteen days written 8 notice to the person or persons to be evicted or dispossessed, if such 9 person or persons rents a manufactured home in a manufactured home park 10 from a manufactured home park owner or operator and such officer shall 11 execute such warrant between the hours of sunrise and sunset. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
