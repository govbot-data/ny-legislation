        AN  ACT authorizing the town of Brookhaven to alienate certain parklands
          for use as a recharge basin and to dedicate other lands as replacement
          parklands
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of Brookha-
     2  ven, located in the county of Suffolk, acting by and through its govern-
     3  ing  body and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such body,
     4  is hereby authorized to discontinue as parklands the lands more  partic-
     5  ularly  described  in  section  three  of  this act, for the purposes of
     6  constructing a stormwater runoff and recharge basin.

     9  an amount equal to or greater than the fair market value  of  the  park-

    11  capital improvements to existing park and recreational facilities.
