Subdivisions 3 and 5 of section 11-0541 of the environ- 2 mental conservation law, as added by a chapter of the laws of 2025 3 amending the environmental conservation law relating to enacting the 4 monarch preservation plan, as proposed in legislative bills numbers S. 5 3163 and A. 1819, are amended to read as follows: 6 3.
