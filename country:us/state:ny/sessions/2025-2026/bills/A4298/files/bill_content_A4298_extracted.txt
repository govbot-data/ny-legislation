The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 223-b to read as follows: 3 § 223-b. Camera technology required for purchases or upgrades of park- 4 ing violation devices. 1. On and after the effective date of this 5 section, the commissioner shall require that all purchases, upgrades or 6 replacements of devices used to identify parking violations at the site 7 of the violation shall contain a camera capable of conspicuously photo- 8 graphing the vehicle, producing a time/date stamp, and any other factors 9 relevant to the enforcement of parking violations pursuant to article 10 two-B of this title. The commissioner shall also require any state or 11 local entity that currently possesses such camera technology to imme- 12 diately implement the technology and comply with the provisions of this 13 section. 14 2. Nothing in this section shall interfere with any rights, benefits, 15 including the terms and conditions of employment, or the civil service 16 and collective bargaining status of any existing or new employees. 17 § 2. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new section 18 223-c to read as follows: 19 § 223-c. Photographs required for parking violations. 1. The commis- 20 sioner shall require that all parking violations issued pursuant to 21 article two-B of this title be accompanied with a photograph of the 22 alleged violation. Such photograph shall be time/date stamped and 23 conspicuously display the vehicle and relevant parking sign, if applica- 24 ble.
