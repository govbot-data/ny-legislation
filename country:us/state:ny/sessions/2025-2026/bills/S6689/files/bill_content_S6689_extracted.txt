GALLIVAN -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to extending the time where certain entities can provide fiscal intermediary services The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 4-a-1 of section 365-f of the social services 2 law, as added by section 3 of part HH of chapter 57 of the laws of 2024, 3 is amended to read as follows: 4 4-a-1. Fiscal intermediary registration. (a) [Fiscal intermediary 5 registration.] Except for the statewide fiscal intermediary and its 6 subcontractors, as of [April] July first, two thousand twenty-five, no 7 entity shall provide, directly or through contract, fiscal intermediary 8 services. All subcontractors of the statewide fiscal intermediary, shall 9 register with the department within thirty days of being selected as a 10 subcontractor. 11 (b) In selecting its subcontractors, the statewide fiscal intermediary 12 shall consider demonstrated compliance with all applicable federal and 13 state laws and regulations, including but not limited to, marketing and 14 labor practices, cost reporting, and electronic visit verification 15 requirements. 16 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall be deemed to 17 have been in full force and effect on and after April 1, 2025.
