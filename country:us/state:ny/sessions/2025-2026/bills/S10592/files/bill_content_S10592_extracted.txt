RAMOS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the transportation law and the executive law, in relation to the development of a scannable code that links to trans- lations of work zone and truss type construction signs The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 22 of the transportation law, as added by chapter 2 223 of the laws of 2005, is amended to read as follows: 3 § 22. Work zone safety and enforcement. 1. The department shall, in 4 cooperation with the superintendent of state police, the commissioner of 5 motor vehicles, the [chairman] chair of the New York state thruway 6 authority, local law enforcement agencies and representatives for 7 contractors and laborers, develop and implement rules and regulations 8 for the increased safety of work zones. Such rules and regulations shall 9 include, but shall not be limited to, a police presence at all major 10 active work zones as defined by rules and regulations set forth by the 11 commissioner, the use of radar speed display signs at all major active 12 work zones as defined by rules and regulations set forth by the commis- 13 sioner, and a system for reviewing work zone safety and design for all 14 work zones under the jurisdiction of the department. 15 2. (a) The department shall, in cooperation with the secretary of 16 state and any other state or local agencies the commissioner shall deem 17 necessary, develop a scannable code to be displayed on work zone signs, 18 and signs notifying of truss type construction in compliance with 19 section three hundred eighty-two-a of the executive law. Such code 20 shall, when scanned with a smartphone, tablet, or other similar device, 21 open an internet link on such device that (i) determines the user's 22 location; and (ii) displays the contents of such sign in the most 23 commonly spoken language other than English and Spanish within a ten- 24 mile radius of such user. 25 (b) Beginning one year from the effective date of this subdivision, 26 all work zone signs used in an area where there is residential housing 27 and/or a reasonable expectation of pedestrians shall be printed in EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
