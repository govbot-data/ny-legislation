KAVANAGH -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to preventing the harassment of a rent regulated tenant The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The first undesignated paragraph of section 241.05 of the 2 penal law, as amended by chapter 573 of the laws of 2019, is amended to 3 read as follows: 4 [The] There shall be a presumption that a course of conduct as 5 described in subdivision two of this section was intended to cause such 6 persons to vacate such dwelling units or to surrender or waive any 7 rights in relation to such occupancy. However, the good faith commence- 8 ment and pursuit of a lawful eviction action by an owner against a rent 9 regulated tenant in a court of competent jurisdiction shall not, by 10 itself, constitute a "systematic ongoing course of conduct" in violation 11 of paragraph (c) of subdivision two of this section. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
