BORRELLO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring at least two photo speed violation monitoring systems in highway construction or maintenance work areas located on a controlled-access highway or on the thruway The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraphs 1 and 2 of subdivision (a) of section 1180-e of 2 the vehicle and traffic law, as added by chapter 421 of the laws of 3 2021, are amended to read as follows: 4 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the commissioner of 5 transportation is hereby authorized to establish a demonstration program 6 imposing monetary liability on the owner of a vehicle for failure of an 7 operator thereof to comply with posted maximum speed limits in a highway 8 construction or maintenance work area located on a controlled-access 9 highway (i) when highway construction or maintenance work is occurring 10 and a work area speed limit is in effect as provided in paragraph two of 11 subdivision (d) or subdivision (f) of section eleven hundred eighty of 12 this article or (ii) when highway construction or maintenance work is 13 occurring and other speed limits are in effect as provided in subdivi- 14 sion (b) or (g) or paragraph one of subdivision (d) of section eleven 15 hundred eighty of this article.
