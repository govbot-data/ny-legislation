Section 79-e of the civil rights law, as added by chapter 2 98 of the laws of 1994, is amended to read as follows: 3 § 79-e. Right to breast feed. Notwithstanding any other provision of 4 law, a [mother] person may breast feed [her] their baby in any location, 5 public or private, where the [mother] person is otherwise authorized to 6 be, irrespective of whether or not the nipple of the [mother's] person's 7 breast is covered during or incidental to the breast feeding. 8 § 2.
