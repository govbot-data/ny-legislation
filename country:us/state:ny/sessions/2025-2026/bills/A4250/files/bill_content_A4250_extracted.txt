The closing paragraph of section 366 of the agriculture 2 and markets law, as amended by chapter 185 of the laws of 2014, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 Any person violating any of the provisions of this section, upon 5 conviction thereof, shall be punished by a fine not exceeding [one] five 6 thousand dollars, or by imprisonment not to exceed [six months] one 7 year, or by both. 8 § 2.
