Paragraph (b) of subdivision 1 of section 399-aaaa of the 2 general business law, as added by chapter 362 of the laws of 2019, is 3 amended and three new paragraphs (c), (d) and (e) are added to read as 4 follows: 5 (b) "menstrual product" shall mean products used for the purpose of 6 catching menstruation and vaginal discharge, including but not limited 7 to tampons, pads, and menstrual cups.
