and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to commemorate the Carlos Lezama Archives and Caribbean Cultural Center's "Lunch by the Bay," a signature celebration of heritage, family, and excellence, to be held on Sunday, June 29, 2025; and WHEREAS, Held annually in honor of Father's Day, this cherished event uplifts community values, celebrates legacy, and recognizes the vital role of fatherhood within the Caribbean cultural experience; and WHEREAS, In 2025, the Carlos Lezama Archives and Caribbean Cultural Center proudly honors Paul Romeo as Father of the Year, in recognition of his unwavering dedication to family, his exemplary character, and his deep commitment to community service and cultural preservation; and WHEREAS, Paul Romeo's selection as Father of the Year reflects not only his personal integrity and achievements, but also the importance of honoring men who serve as role models, nurturers, and leaders across generations; and WHEREAS, The Carlos Lezama Archives and Caribbean Cultural Center, founded in memory of the late cultural icon Carlos Lezama, continues to preserve and celebrate the contributions of Caribbean people to the fabric of New York life through education, performance, and community engagement; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proud to join in this joyous occasion, and to recognize both the celebration and its distinguished honoree for their contributions to cultural preservation and community uplift; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the Carlos Lezama Archives and Caribbean Cultural Center's 2025 "Lunch by the Bay," to be held on June 29, 2025, and to honor Paul Romeo as Father of the Year, with gratitude for his dedication and leadership; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Carlos Lezama Archives and Caribbean Cultural Center and to Paul Romeo.
