WALCZYK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Energy and Telecommuni- cations AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to enacting the "geothermal education and outreach (GEO) act" The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "geothermal 2 education and outreach (GEO) act". 3 § 2. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new section 4 1872-b to read as follows: 5 § 1872-b. Geothermal education and outreach program. 1. Definitions. 6 As used in this section, the following terms shall have the following 7 meanings: 8 (a) "Geothermal energy system" means a renewable energy system, as 9 defined by section sixty-six-p of the public service law, that generates 10 electricity through the use of geothermal electric or geothermal ground 11 source heat technology. 12 (b) "Program" means the geothermal education and outreach program 13 established pursuant to subdivision two of this section. 14 (c) "Qualified school district" means a public school district that 15 the authority determines can feasibly convert to geothermal energy as 16 its primary source of energy based on location, resources, and other 17 relevant factors as determined by the authority. 18 2. Geothermal education and outreach program. (a) The authority is 19 hereby directed to establish and administer, in consultation with the 20 commissioner of education, a geothermal education and outreach program 21 to provide guidelines, planning, and financial support for qualified 22 school districts that wish to convert to a geothermal energy system as 23 their primary source of energy.
