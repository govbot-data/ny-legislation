Conrad CONGRATULATING Paramedic David Rice upon the occasion of his retirement after 23 years of distinguished service to the Paramedic Unit of the Town of Tonawanda WHEREAS, This Legislative Body seeks to acknowledge those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to enhance the stature of the State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Paramedic David Rice upon the occasion of his retirement after 23 years of distinguished service to the Paramedic Unit of the Town of Tonawanda; and WHEREAS, The Town of Tonawanda's Paramedic Unit is a public service organization within the Town of Tonawanda Police Department and a critical link in the emergency medical service (EMS) system for the communities of both the Town of Tonawanda and the Village of Kenmore, providing high quality and advanced pre-hospital medical care; and WHEREAS, David Rice is known for his extraordinary patient care, empathy, and compassion; and WHEREAS, Throughout his career, David Rice has answered more than 16,000 calls for service, providing the highest standards of pre-hospital care for the citizens and visitors of his community; and WHEREAS, To the many paramedic personnel who have served alongside him and those he has mentored throughout his career, the dedicated service of David Rice reflects the very best of what it means to be a public servant; he has earned the admiration, esteem, and affection of his colleagues; and WHEREAS, While a proud member of the Town of Tonawanda's Paramedic Unit, David Rice served his community with loyalty, honor, and distinction; in his official acts, he was governed by a keen sense of duty, assisting others in need and providing essential services to members of his community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Paramedic David Rice upon the occasion of his retirement after 23 years of distinguished service to the Paramedic Unit of the Town of Tonawanda; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Paramedic David Rice.
