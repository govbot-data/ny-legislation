Section 215-a of the labor law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 3 to read as follows: 3 3. For the purpose of this section an employer or such employer's duly 4 authorized agent who denies a promotion to an employee solely because 5 such employee has failed to meet an established quota shall be deemed to 6 have penalized such employee as to such employee's employment. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately; provided however that the 8 amendment to section 215-a of the labor law made by section one of this 9 act shall survive the expiration and reversion of such section as 10 provided in subdivision (p) of section 406 of chapter 166 of the laws of 11 1991, as amended.
