Section 1266 of the public authorities law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 12-b to read as follows: 3 12-b. For the purposes of this subdivision, the term "required work" 4 shall mean any necessary removal, relocation, protection, or replacement 5 of any pipes, mains, conduits or other infrastructure of any public 6 service corporation and any fixtures and appliances connected therewith 7 or attached thereto, either temporarily or permanently. Whenever the 8 authority determines that any required work exists in connection with 9 the improvement, construction, reconstruction, or rehabilitation of a 10 transportation facility, all of the following provisions shall apply: 11 (a) The design for such required work shall be prepared by the author- 12 ity or the authority's contractor. Such design shall be subject to the 13 review and approval of the public service corporation, which shall not 14 be unreasonably withheld.
