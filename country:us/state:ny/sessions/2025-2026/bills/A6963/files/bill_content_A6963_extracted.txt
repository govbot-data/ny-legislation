Legislative intent. The legislature hereby finds that the 2 state of New York and the city of New York have a migrant crisis and 3 that as the city of New York distributes funding for the relief of this 4 migrant crisis, such funds should have oversight from the comptroller of 5 the state of New York on how such funding is spent. 6 § 2.
