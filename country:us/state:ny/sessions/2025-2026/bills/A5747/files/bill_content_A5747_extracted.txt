DILAN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Health AN ACT to create a temporary state commission to study and make recom- mendations concerning the incidence of asthma in the borough of Brook- lyn in New York city The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. A temporary state commission is hereby created to study and 2 make recommendations relating to the incidence of asthma in the borough 3 of Brooklyn in New York city. The commission will place particular 4 emphasis on youth in communities with an unemployment rate exceeding one 5 hundred twenty-five percent of the statewide level. 6 § 2. The commission shall consist of eleven members to be appointed as 7 follows: five shall be appointed by the governor; two shall be appointed 8 by the temporary president of the senate and one by the minority leader 9 of the senate; and two shall be appointed by the speaker of the assembly 10 and one by the minority leader of the assembly. The members of the 11 commission shall serve at the pleasure of the official making the 12 appointment of such member. Of the five members appointed by the gover- 13 nor, one shall be from the department of health and one shall be from 14 the department of environmental conservation. The remaining nine members 15 of the commission must reside in a city with a population of one million 16 or more. Vacancies in the membership of the commission shall be filled 17 in the manner provided for original appointments. Membership on the 18 commission shall not constitute a public office. A chairperson and vice- 19 chairperson of the commission shall be elected by a majority of its 20 members, all members being present. 21 § 3. The members of the commission shall receive no compensation for 22 their services, but shall be allowed their actual and necessary expenses 23 incurred in the performance of their duties pursuant to this act. The commission may meet within and without the state, shall hold 2 at least one public hearing each quarter, and shall have all the powers 3 of a legislative committee pursuant to the legislative law. 4 § 5. The commission shall make a report of its findings and make any 5 recommendations it may deem necessary and appropriate to the governor, 6 the temporary president of the senate and the speaker of the assembly no 7 later than one year after the effective date of this act. 8 § 6.
