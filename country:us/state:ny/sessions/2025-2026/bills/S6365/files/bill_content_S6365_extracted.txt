The correction law is amended by adding a new section 125-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 125-a. Notice of fees and charges for debit cards provided upon 4 release. 1. For the purposes of this section, a "release debit card" 5 shall mean the payment of funds required by section one hundred twenty- 6 five of this article in the form of an electronic benefit transfer card. 7 2. Any financial institution that enters into a contract with New York 8 state to issue release debit cards shall provide a clear, understand- 9 able, and detailed schedule of fees and charges with each such release 10 debit card. 11 3. Such schedule of fees shall: 12 (a) be presented in a clear and conspicuous manner, be easily accessi- 13 ble, and written in plain language; 14 (b) include, but shall not be limited to, fees for ATM use, balance 15 inquiries, fund transfers, card replacement, account maintenance, and 16 customer service interactions; 17 (c) be provided in physical forms as a separate document and, if 18 feasible, electronic forms as a separate attachment upon the card's 19 issuance; 20 (d) be available on the official website of the card provider; and 21 (e) be provided in the six most common non-English languages spoken by 22 individuals with limited-English proficiency in New York state; EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
