SIMONE -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation to requiring state agencies to prepare an active intelligence speed assistance system plan The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 2 163-e to read as follows: 3 § 163-e. Active intelligent speed assistance system for state-owned 4 vehicles. 1. For purposes of this section, the term "active intelligent 5 speed assistance system" means a vehicle system that uses, at minimum, 6 the GPS location of the vehicle compared with a database of posted speed 7 limits, to determine the speed limit, and prevents throttle input once a 8 vehicle exceeds a specified speed threshold related to the speed limit. 9 An active intelligent speed assistance system shall be capable of being 10 temporarily disabled by the driver of the vehicle. 11 2. Each state agency shall prepare an active intelligent speed assist- 12 ance system plan for the equipment of all state agency vehicles with 13 active intelligent speed assistance systems. The state fleet active 14 intelligent speed assistance system plan for light-duty non-emergency 15 vehicles shall be prepared on or before December thirty-first, two thou- 16 sand twenty-seven, and the state fleet active intelligent speed assist- 17 ance system plan for medium- and heavy-duty vehicles shall be prepared 18 on or before December two thousand twenty-eight. Such plans shall 19 include interim targets for achieving the equipment of all state agency 20 vehicles with active intelligent speed assistance systems. 21 3. All state agency light-duty non-emergency vehicles shall be 22 equipped with active intelligent speed assistance systems by no later 23 than December thirty-first, two thousand thirty, and all state agency 24 medium- and heavy-duty vehicles shall be equipped with active intelli- 25 gent speed assistance systems by no later than December thirty-first, 26 two thousand thirty-one. 27 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
