MAY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Environmental Conservation -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to limiting the department of environmental conservation's ability to fix special open seasons for taking wild deer The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph c of subdivision 9 of section 11-0903 of the 2 environmental conservation law, as amended by chapter 911 of the laws of 3 1990, is amended to read as follows: 4 c. Such seasons may be fixed for any number of days during the period 5 beginning on the eleventh day after the close of a regular open season 6 for deer established by law and ending on [February 10] the third Sunday 7 of December immediately following; provided, however that the department 8 may, upon consultation with local governments and stakeholders, issue 9 regulations for special seasons for particular counties or designated 10 wildlife management units. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
