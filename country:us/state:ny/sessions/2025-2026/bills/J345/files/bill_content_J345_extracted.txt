and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Esmerlin Santiago Matias Garcia upon the occasion of his nomination as YouTuber of the Year at the 2024 Soberano Awards; and WHEREAS, Esmerlin Santiago Matias Garcia, popularly known as Alofoke, was born on December 6, 1981, in Ensanche Capotillo, Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic; he is the son of Santiago Matias and Ada Garcia; and WHEREAS, A young entrepreneur, radio personality, and music producer, his program, "Alofoke Radio Show," has gained great popularity, where he discusses entertainment news, music, and urban culture; and WHEREAS, Additionally, Santiago Matias has been a key figure in the careers of many urban genre artists, and his record label, "Alofoke Music," has worked with several well-known artists; his irreverent style and ability to connect with a young audience has allowed him to position himself as one of the most influential figures in the industry; and WHEREAS, Santiago Matias was nominated as YouTuber of the Year at the 2024 Soberano Awards for the impact he has had on the digital entertainment industry; he has established himself as a key figure in content creation in the Dominican Republic, especially with his channel, "Alofoke Radio Show"; and WHEREAS, Furthermore, his charisma and positioning have certainly proven his influence and power in the urban music scene; in 2024, Santiago Matias organized a successful show at the iconic Prudential Center in New Jersey; he also broadcasted his program from Carnegie Hall in New York; and WHEREAS, Santiago Matias is a strong advocate for urban artists, promoting their talent and contributing to the visibility of this genre; and WHEREAS, With him throughout have been his wife, Charylie Alvarado de Jesus, and his two children, Byroon and Prince, all of whom feel privileged to be a part of his life and rejoice in his many achievements; and WHEREAS, Santiago Matias' nomination as YouTuber of the Year is a testament to his incredible talent, dedication, and the authentic connection he has built with his audience; through his engaging content, creativity, and passion, he has not only entertained but also inspired countless viewers; his commitment to producing content that resonates with people from all walks of life has set him apart as a true innovator in the digital space; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Esmerlin Santiago Matias Garc�a upon the occasion of his nomination as YouTuber of the Year at the 2024 Soberano Awards; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Esmerlin Santiago Matias Garcia.
