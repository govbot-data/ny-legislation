The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1885 to read as follows: 3 § 1885. Natural carbon sequestration research program. 1. There is 4 hereby established the natural carbon sequestration research program. 5 The purpose of the program is to develop innovative science-based 6 solutions to facilitate sequestration of carbon in New York's natural 7 and working lands in ways that decrease total greenhouse gas emissions. 8 In developing the program, the New York state college of agriculture and 9 life sciences as named pursuant to section fifty-seven hundred twelve of 10 the education law shall conduct, support, and develop technologies and 11 management strategies, along with verification systems to facilitate the 12 natural drawdown of carbon from the atmosphere and storage underground. 13 2.
