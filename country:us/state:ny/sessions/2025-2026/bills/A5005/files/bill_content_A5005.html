A05005 Summary:

BILL NOA05005
 
SAME ASSAME AS S06184
 
SPONSORBichotte Hermelyn (MS)
 
COSPNSRGonzalez-Rojas, Lucas, Conrad, Colton, Bendett, Brabenec, Brown K, DeStefano, Sayegh, Hyndman, Zaccaro
 
MLTSPNSRSimon
 
Amd §13-105, NYC Ad Cd; amd §§519 & 614, R & SS L
 
Provides that members of a retirement system who retire from such system and later rejoin the retirement system are reinstated with the same rights, benefits and privileges they had prior to retirement.

        AN  ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York and the
          retirement and social security law,  in  relation  to  providing  that
          members  of  a retirement system who retire from such system and later
          rejoin the retirement system are entitled to the same rights, benefits
          and privileges such member had prior to retirement
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Section  13-105 of the administrative code of the city of
     2  New York is amended to read as follows:
     3    § 13-105 Membership; eligibility in certain cases. Notwithstanding any
     4  inconsistent provision of this chapter, the code, or any other  law,  an
     5  officer  or  employee  in  city-service  who  is  otherwise eligible for
     6  membership in this retirement system but  who  is  entitled  to  receive
     7  benefits  from any other pension fund or retirement system maintained by
     8  the city may become a member of this  retirement  system  provided  such
     9  benefits  are  suspended  during [his or her] such officer or employee's
    10  active membership in this retirement system. Such member shall  contrib-
    11  ute  to this retirement system as a new entrant, provided, however, that
    12  in the case of a member who has retired or retires from  public  service
    13  on  or  after  July first, nineteen hundred seventy-six and suspended or
    14  suspends such member's retirement allowance upon entrance into city-ser-
    15  vice, such member shall be entitled to the same rights and privileges as
    16  those to which such member was entitled upon such member's last date  of
    17  entry  into  a  public retirement system or pension fund of the state or
    18  the city of New York.
    19    § 2. Subdivision 2 of section 519 of the retirement and social securi-
    20  ty law is amended by adding a new paragraph (vi) to read as follows:
