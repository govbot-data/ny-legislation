Add §19-159.9, NYC Ad Cd
 
Relates to notification and hearings for proposed construction or removal of bicycle lanes or racks; requires a presentation at a public hearing when the department of transportation proposes to construct or remove a bicycle lane or rack; defines terms.

        AN  ACT  to  amend  the  administrative code of the city of New York, in
          relation to notification and hearings  for  proposed  construction  or
          removal of bicycle lanes or racks

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The administrative code of the city of New York is  amended
     2  by adding a new section 19-159.9 to read as follows:
     3    §  19-159.9  Notification  of  bicycle  lane  or  rack construction or
     4  removal.  a. Definitions. As used in this section, the  following  terms
     5  shall have the following meanings:
     6    1. "Affected council member" means the council member or members whose
     7  districts  would contain a proposed bicycle lane or bicycle rack that is
     8  to be constructed or removed.
     9    2. "Affected community board" means a community board or boards  whose
    10  districts  would contain a proposed bicycle lane or bicycle rack that is
    11  to be constructed or removed.
    12    3. "Bicycle lane" means a portion of the roadway that has been  marked
    13  off or separated for the preferential or exclusive use of bicycles.
    14    4.  "Bicycle  rack"  means a fixed-in-place stand, solidly anchored to
    15  the ground or other fixed object, which allows a bicycle to lean against
    16  it in an upright position with both wheels on a level surface.
    17    b. When the department intends to construct or remove a  bicycle  lane
    18  or  bicycle rack, the department shall provide notice through electronic
    19  and written means to the affected council member of the affected council
    20  district, the state senator of the affected senate district, the  member
    21  of  the  assembly  of  the  affected  assembly district and the district
    22  manager of the affected community board prior  to  the  construction  or
    23  removal  of  such  lane  or rack. Such notice shall be provided at least

     1  ninety days before the department constructs or  removes  such  lane  or
     2  rack.
     3    c.  The  department shall present its plan at a public hearing held by
     4  an affected community board within forty-five  days  of  the  department
     5  sending  the  notice  required  under  subdivision b of this section. If
     6  changes are made to such plan, it shall present  the  plan  again  at  a
     7  public hearing within forty-five days. The community shall be allowed to
     8  provide  input  on  such plans and the department shall not construct or
     9  remove a bicycle lane or bicycle rack until forty-five  days  after  the
    10  final public hearing.
    11    d. The department shall consider comments from any public hearings and
    12  may  incorporate  changes,  where  appropriate, into its bicycle lane or
    13  bicycle rack plan or cancel plans for construction or  removal  of  such
    14  lane  or  rack if it determines that such lane or rack would be inappro-
    15  priate.
    16    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
