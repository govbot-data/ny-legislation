Resolved (if the Assembly concur), That article 5 of the 2 constitution be amended by adding a new section 8 to read as follows: 3 § 8. There shall be a state board of parole, responsible for determin- 4 ing which incarcerated individuals may be released from incarceration to 5 parole and establishing any conditions of release for such individuals. 6 The legislature shall determine the qualifications for such members and 7 the functions, powers, and duties of such board, other than those set 8 forth in this section.
