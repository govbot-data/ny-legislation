S10645 Summary:

BILL NOS10645
 
SAME ASSAME AS A10952
 
SPONSORMARTINS
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §§622.0, 622.4 & 622.5, Chap 273/1939
 
Relates to the Volunteer and Exempt Firefighter's Benevolent Association of Albertson; changes "firemen" to "firefighters"; relates to the management and control as well as the usage of moneys received from foreign fire insurance companies by such association.

        AN ACT to amend the Nassau county civil divisions act,  in  relation  to
          the  Volunteer  and  Exempt  Firefighter's  Benevolent  Association of
          Albertson
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section  1. Sections 622.0, 622.4 and 622.5 of the Nassau county civil
     2  divisions act, as added by chapter 65 of the laws of 1940,  are  amended
     3  to read as follows:
     4    §  622.0  Incorporation;  membership.  a. A corporation by the name of
     5  "Volunteer and Exempt [Firemen's] Firefighter's  Benevolent  Association
     6  of Albertson," is hereby created.
     7    b. It shall be composed of all persons who now are, or hereafter shall
     8  be  volunteer  members  of  the  Albertson Hook, Ladder, Engine and Hose
     9  Company No. 1, and such  exempt  volunteer  [firemen]  firefighters,  as
    10  defined  in  section  two hundred of the general municipal law, who have
    11  acquired such status by virtue of having rendered service in the Albert-
    12  son Hook, Ladder, Engine and Hose Company No. 1 of  Albertson,  town  of
    13  North Hempstead, as shall:
    14    1. Notify the secretary of the Albertson Hook, Ladder, Engine and Hose
    15  Company  No. 1, Albertson, Nassau county, New York, in writing, prior to
    16  the organization meeting of the corporation  created  by  this  article,
    17  which  meeting  is  hereinafter  provided for, of their desire to become
    18  members, or
    19    2. Attend such organization meeting or an adjournment thereof, or
    20    3. Notify the secretary of such corporation in writing,  within  sixty
    21  days  after  [his]  such  secretary  election, of their desire to become
    22  members thereof, and also such other of the exempt  volunteer  [firemen]
    23  firefighters  aforesaid,  or  of  those  who may hereafter become exempt
    24  volunteer [firemen] firefighters of the Albertson Hook,  Ladder,  Engine
    25  and Hose Company No. 1, as may become members of such corporation pursu-
    26  ant to the provisions of its by-laws.  Any [fireman] firefighter, howev-
    27  er,  who  has  been or shall hereafter be removed for cause, expelled or

    36    d. Such taxes shall only be used [for the care and relief of  disabled
    37  or  indigent  volunteer  and  exempt  firemen and their families] by the
    38  corporation in furtherance of its purposes set forth in section 622.1 of
