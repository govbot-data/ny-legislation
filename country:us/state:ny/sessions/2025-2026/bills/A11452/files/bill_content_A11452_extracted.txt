Tapia) -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT in relation to authorizing the commissioner of general services provide compensation to Catholic Guardian Services for certain expenses in connection with holding property for the purposes of providing housing and shelter for homeless, destitute, refugee, delin- quent and/or disabled persons in New York city The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The commissioner of general services in the executive 2 department is hereby authorized to compensate Catholic Guardian Services 3 for fair and equitable holding expenses, as determined by said commis- 4 sioner, associated with property located at 2322 Valentine Avenue, coun- 5 ty of the Bronx and further described in section 2 of chapter 243 of the 6 laws of 2004 which was conveyed to Catholic Home Bureau for the purposes 7 of providing housing and shelter for homeless, destitute, refugee, 8 delinquent and/or disabled persons in New York city, and which shall 9 revert to the state of New York pursuant to section 5 of such chapter 10 upon the termination of such use and the property shall be conveyed from 11 Catholic Guardian Services to the state of New York. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
