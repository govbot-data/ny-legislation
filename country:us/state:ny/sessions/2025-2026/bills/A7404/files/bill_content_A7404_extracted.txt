JENSEN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring student athletes participating in public school sponsored athletics complete anti-bullying, anti-hazing and anti-harassment training The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 305 of the education law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 63 to read as follows: 3 63. The commissioner shall promulgate rules and regulations to estab- 4 lish uniform statewide protocols to make participation in all public 5 school sponsored athletics, in grades seven through twelve, subject to 6 mandatory anti-bullying, anti-hazing and anti-harassment training for 7 student athletes and their guardians each season. The department shall 8 develop parameters for such a class, and each district's athletics 9 director shall be tasked with formulating the manner in which student 10 athletes and their guardians complete such training. Failure to complete 11 the training would bar the student athlete from participating in such 12 athletics until the training is completed. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of July next succeeding 14 the date on which it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the 15 addition, amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary 16 for the implementation of this act on its effective date are authorized 17 to be made and completed on or before such effective date.
