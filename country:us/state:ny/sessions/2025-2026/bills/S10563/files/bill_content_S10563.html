S10563 Summary:

BILL NOS10563
 
SAME ASSAME AS A11486
 
SPONSORSKOUFIS
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add §7-127, El L
 
Provides notice regarding the timeliness of the receipt by the board of elections of ballots capable of return by mail to advise voters that such ballot should be returned to the board of elections as soon as possible by any approved method, and further recommend the use of alternative methods of return besides domestic mail services if the voter will be returning their ballot within several days of the election.

        AN ACT to amend the  election  law,  in  relation  to  providing  notice
          regarding  the  timeliness of the receipt by the board of elections of
          ballots capable of return by mail
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The election law is amended by adding a new section 7-127
     2  to read as follows:
     3    § 7-127. Notification to  accompany  all  early  mail,  absentee,  and
     4  special  ballots capable of return by mail. 1. Notwithstanding any other
     5  provision of law to the contrary, any ballot  issued  pursuant  to  this
     6  chapter that is permitted to be returned by mail shall be accompanied by
     7  a conspicuous written notice, in a form prescribed by the state board of
     8  elections,  informing  the voter of all the methods by which such ballot
     9  may be timely returned under applicable law, including,  as  applicable,
    10  return  by  mail,  express  delivery  services, delivery by the voter or
    11  agent to the appropriate board of elections, or appropriate early voting
    12  site or poll site on election day, and deposit  in  a  ballot  drop  box
    13  established pursuant to section 8-416 of this chapter.
    14    2.  Such notice shall advise the voter that, to ensure timely receipt,
    15  such ballot should be returned to the board  of  elections  as  soon  as
    16  possible  by  any  approved  methods,  and  further recommend the use of
    17  alternative methods of return besides  domestic  mail  services  if  the
    18  voter  will  be  returning  their  ballot  within  several  days  of the
    19  election.  The notice shall also include a reminder of the importance of
    20  seeking a hand cancellation date for any  ballot  returned  by  domestic
    21  postal  mail to ensure the date of mailing can always be determined. For
    22  overseas voters, such notice shall advise voters to consult  the  antic-
    23  ipated delivery times of their chosen delivery service to ensure ballots
    24  will be returned timely. Such notice shall include the website and tele-
    25  phone  number of the appropriate board of elections for the location and
    26  hours of operation of ballot drop-off locations  and  information  about
    27  other return options. The state board of elections, each county board of
