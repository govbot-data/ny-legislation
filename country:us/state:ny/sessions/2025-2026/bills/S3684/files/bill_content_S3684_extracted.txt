COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to requiring the metropolitan transportation authority to create ten-year capital program plans The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraphs (b) and (c) of subdivision 1 of section 1269-b 2 of the public authorities law, as added by chapter 637 of the laws of 3 1996, are amended and two new paragraphs (d) and (e) are added to read 4 as follows: 5 (b) [not] Not later than ten days after the effective date of this 6 paragraph the authority shall submit to the metropolitan transportation 7 authority capital program review board two capital program plans for the 8 five-year period commencing January first, nineteen hundred ninety-five; 9 [and] 10 (c) [on] On or before October first, nineteen hundred ninety-nine and 11 every fifth year thereafter through and including October first, two 12 thousand sixteen, the authority shall submit to the metropolitan trans- 13 portation authority capital program review board two capital program 14 plans for the five-year period commencing January first of the following 15 year[.]; 16 (d) On or before October first, two thousand twenty-five, the authori- 17 ty shall submit to the metropolitan transportation capital program 18 review board two capital program plans for the ten-year period commenc- 19 ing January first of the following year; and 20 (e) On or before January thirty-first, two thousand thirty-five, and 21 every tenth year thereafter, the authority shall submit to the metropol- 22 itan transportation capital program review board two capital program 23 plans for the ten-year period commencing January first of the following 24 year.
