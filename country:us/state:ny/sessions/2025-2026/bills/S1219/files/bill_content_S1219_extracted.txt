BAILEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Cities 1 AN ACT to require that the siting, construction and establishment of supportive housing facilities in cities having a population of one million or more be disbursed in the boroughs of such cities in propor- tion to the population of each such borough The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, with- 2 in cities having a population of one million or more, supportive housing 3 facilities within each such city shall be sited, constructed and/or 4 established throughout the boroughs of the city in proportion to each 5 borough's population to the city's total population. Provided, however, 6 that the provisions of this section shall not apply to the siting, 7 construction or establishment of any supportive housing facility for 8 which the appropriate community board or, if there be no community 9 board, the appropriate neighborhood board shall have granted an 10 exemption from such provisions. 11 § 2.
