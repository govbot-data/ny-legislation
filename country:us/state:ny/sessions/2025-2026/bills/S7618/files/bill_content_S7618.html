Add §214-p, Ag & Mkts L
 
Requires certain labeling to be used to indicate the safety dates and quality dates of food products; requires the department of agriculture and markets to educate consumers about such provisions.

        AN  ACT  to  amend  the agriculture and markets law, in relation to food
          safety and quality date labelling requirements
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1. The agriculture and markets law is amended by adding a new
     2  section 214-p to read as follows:
     3    § 214-p. Food safety and quality date labeling  requirements.  1.  (a)
     4  Beginning  one  year  after  the  effective date of this section, a food
     5  manufacturer, processor, or retailer engaging in the  labeling  of  food
     6  products for human consumption that chooses, or is otherwise required by
     7  law,  to display a date label to communicate a quality or safety date on
     8  a food product shall use the following uniform terms on such date label:
     9    (i) "USE by" or "USE by or Freeze by" to indicate the safety  date  of
    10  the food product;
    11    (ii)  "BEST if Used by" or "BEST if Used or Frozen by" to indicate the
    12  quality date of the food product;
    13    (iii) "UB" to indicate the safety date of the  food  product  if  such
    14  food  product  is too small to include a uniform term under subparagraph
    15  (i) of this paragraph; or

     1    (iv) "BB" to indicate the quality date of the  food  product  if  such
     2  food product is too small to include the uniform term under subparagraph
     3  (ii) of this paragraph.
     4    (b)  No  person  or  other  entity shall sell or offer for sale in the
     5  state any food product for human consumption manufactured  on  or  after
     6  July  first,  two thousand twenty-eight, that displays a food date label
     7  that is not labeled in accordance with paragraph (a)  of  this  subdivi-
     8  sion.
     9    (c)  No  person  or  other  entity shall sell or offer for sale in the
    10  state a food product for human consumption manufactured on or after July
    11  first, two thousand twenty-eight, that is labeled with the phrase  "sell
    12  by".  This  paragraph shall not prohibit the use of "sell by" dates that
    13  are presented in a coded format, that are not easily readable by consum-
    14  ers, and that do not use the phrase "sell by".
    15    2. Notwithstanding subdivision one of this section, a person or entity
    16  may sell or offer for sale  in  the  state  a  food  product  for  human
    17  consumption  that was acquired by said person or other entity before the
    18  effective date of this section until the expiration of the  food  safety
    19  or quality date on such food product.
    20    3. (a) A person or other entity that is responsible for placing a food
    21  safety  or  quality date on a food product shall estimate the shelf life
    22  of such food product, using a  scientifically  valid  method  as  deemed
    23  acceptable  by  the  department  in  consultation with the department of
    24  health, taking into consideration the quality,  characteristics,  formu-
    25  lation,  processing  impact, packaging or container and other protective
    26  wrapping or coating, and typical transportation,  storage,  and  display
    27  conditions of such food product.
    28    (b)  Considerations under paragraph (a) of this subdivision shall also
    29  include those of the retail store and consumer. For purposes of estimat-
    30  ing shelf life, home storage conditions shall be considered  similar  to
    31  the  usual retail store, except that refrigerated food may be calculated
    32  using a home temperature storage standard  at  or  below  forty  degrees
    33  Fahrenheit.  Such  factors  shall  be  measured  or otherwise determined
    34  utilizing testing and sampling procedures customarily  utilized  by  the
    35  food industry for such purposes.
    36    4.  (a)  The  department shall update its website to educate consumers
    37  about the meaning of safety dates and quality dates under this section.
    38    (b) The department shall conduct specific outreach activities:
    39    (i) targeting, but not limited to, the following:
    40    (1) wholesalers, retailers, grocery stores, and corner stores;
    41    (2) food donation groups such as food banks and food  recovery  organ-
    42  izations; and
    43    (3) the general population;
    44    (ii) regarding:
    45    (1) the meaning of safety dates and quality dates under this section;
    46    (2)  the  acceptance  of  food  with  labels  not  complying with this
    47  section; and
    48    (3) the acceptance of food with date labels past the quality date.
    49    (c) The department shall promulgate rules  and  regulations  requiring
    50  all  wholesalers,  retailers,  grocery  stores,  and  corner  stores  to
    51  conspicuously post signage informing customers and employees  about  the
    52  meaning of safety dates and quality dates under this section.
    53    5. (a) This section shall not apply to infant formula or any alcoholic
    54  beverage,  as defined by section three of the alcoholic beverage control
    55  law.

     1    (b) This section shall not prohibit a store from displaying on wine, a
     2  distilled spirit, or a wine- or distilled spirit-based  product  bottled
     3  or  packaged  on or after July first, two thousand twenty-eight, a label
     4  with a  statement  that  communicates  the  date  on  which  such  wine,
     5  distilled  spirit,  or  wine-  or  distilled  spirit-based  product  was
     6  produced, manufactured, bottled, or packaged, or from selling or  offer-
     7  ing  for  sale  in  the  state  such wine, distilled spirit, or wine- or
     8  distilled spirit-based product with such label. Such statement  may  use
     9  date  formats,  including,  but  not  limited  to, dates in a time, day,
    10  month, and year format and Julian dates.
    11    (c) To the extent that any other law or a national  shellfish  sanita-
    12  tion program provision governing the labeling of shellfish authorizes or
    13  requires  the use of terms that conflict, or are inconsistent, with this
    14  section, this section shall not apply.
    15    (d) This section shall not prohibit, and shall  not  be  construed  to
    16  discourage,  the sale, donation to, or use of consumable food after such
    17  food's quality date pursuant to subparagraph (ii) of  paragraph  (a)  of
    18  subdivision  one of this section has passed. A wholesaler or retail food
    19  facility may donate a food product for human  consumption  that  is  not
    20  labeled in accordance with this section or is past the quality date.
    21    (e) This section shall not prohibit the use or display of a label that
    22  allows  consumers  to  view  online information about a food product for
    23  human consumption.
    24    (f) Unless otherwise required  by  law,  this  section  shall  not  be
    25  construed  to require the use or display of a date label on a food prod-
    26  uct for human consumption that did not require a  food  label  prior  to
    27  enactment of this section.
    28    (g)  Nothing  in this section shall be interpreted or applied so as to
    29  create a conflict with  federal  law  or  regulations  pertaining  to  a
    30  specific  industry,  food,  or food product covered by the provisions of
    31  this section.
    32    6. The commissioner, in consultation with the commissioner of  health,
    33  shall  promulgate  any rules and regulations necessary to effectuate the
    34  provisions of this section.
    35    § 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after
    36  it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition,  amend-
    37  ment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implemen-
    38  tation  of  this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and
    39  completed on or before such date.
