Add Art 28-EE §§490-aa - 490-ee, Gen Bus L
 
Requires commercial dealers of products made available or placed for sale in this state to inform the secretary of state of any recalls on such products; directs the secretary of state to inform people of such recalls.

        AN ACT to amend the general  business  law,  in  relation  to  notifying
          people of recalled products
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new article
     2  28-EE to read as follows:
     3                                ARTICLE 28-EE
     4                             RECALL NOTIFICATION
     5  Section 490-aa. Definitions.
     6          490-bb. Product recall requirements; commercial dealer.
     7          490-cc. Product recall requirements; secretary of state.
     8          490-dd. Enforcement.
     9          490-ee. Promulgation of rules and regulations.
    10    § 490-aa. Definitions. For the purposes of this article, the following
    11  terms shall have the following meanings:
    12    1. "Department" means the department of state.
    13    2. "Commercial dealer" means any person who  is  in  the  business  of
    14  manufacturing,  remanufacturing,  retrofitting, distributing, importing,
    15  or selling at wholesale any product in New York state.  This  definition
    16  shall  not  be  construed  to  include  retailers, initial consumers, or
    17  secondhand dealers.
    18    3. "Secretary" means the secretary of state.
    19    4. "Initial consumer" means a person who purchases a product  for  any
    20  purpose other than resale.
    21    5. "Secondhand dealer" means a person who sells as a primary source of
    22  income  reconditioned, remanufactured, refurbished, previously owned, or
    23  consignment items. Such term shall not include the initial  consumer  or
    24  someone  who  purchases  a  product  primarily  for personal use but who
    25  subsequently sells the product.
    26    6. "Person" means a natural person and any entity, including  but  not
    27  limited to a sole proprietorship, partnership, firm, corporation, limit-
    28  ed liability company, or association, and any employee or agent thereof.

     1    7.  "Recall"  means  a request to return a product to the manufacturer
     2  due to a defect in the product.
     3    8. "Retailer" means any person who as a business or for-profit venture
     4  sells  or  leases  products for-profit in New York to initial consumers.
     5  Such term shall not include: (a) someone who  purchased  or  acquired  a
     6  product  primarily  for  personal  use  and who subsequently resells the
     7  product; or (b) any secondhand dealer.
     8    9. "Knowledge" means: (a) the receipt of notice or having actual know-
     9  ledge; or (b) the presumed having of knowledge deemed to be possessed by
    10  a reasonable person who acts in the circumstances,  including  knowledge
    11  obtainable upon the exercise of due care.
    12    §  490-bb.  Product  recall requirements; commercial dealer. 1. When a
    13  commercial dealer has made available or placed for sale in this state  a
    14  product for which a recall has been issued, the commercial dealer shall,
    15  within  twenty-four hours of issuing the recall, provide notification to
    16  the department of such recall.
    17    2. All notices under this  section  shall  include,  in  a  clear  and
    18  conspicuous fashion:
    19    (a) a description of the product.
    20    (b) the reason for the recall.
    21    (c) a picture of the product, if available.
    22    (d) instructions on how to return or exchange the recalled product.
    23    (e) any other information the secretary shall require.
    24    3.  No  notice  under  this  section  shall include sales or marketing
    25  information on that product or any other product other than  return  and
    26  exchange policies.
    27    §  490-cc.  Product  recall  requirements;  secretary of state. 1. The
    28  secretary shall:
    29    (a) display on the homepage of the department's website a link to  all
    30  products  made  available  or  placed for sale in this state for which a
    31  recall has been issued and for which the secretary has knowledge of such
    32  recall.
    33    (b) provide a method for commercial dealers  to  give  notice  to  the
    34  secretary,  as  required  under  section  four hundred ninety-bb of this
    35  article, in a form prescribed and made available by the secretary.
    36    2. Within seven days of receiving a notice from a commercial dealer of
    37  a recall, the secretary shall:
    38    (a) update the department's website to include all information in  the
    39  notice from the commercial dealer related to such recall.
    40    (b) issue public service announcements to be distributed to television
    41  and  radio  stations  and other media throughout the state informing the
    42  public of such  recall.  Such  public  service  announcements  shall  be
    43  distributed  in  English and such other languages as the secretary deems
    44  appropriate.
    45    § 490-dd. Enforcement. 1. Where it is determined after a hearing  that
    46  any  person  has  violated  one  or more provisions of this article, the
    47  secretary may assess a civil penalty for each violation. Any  proceeding
    48  conducted  pursuant to this section shall be subject to the state admin-
    49  istrative procedure act.
    50    2. The department shall provide the attorney general  any  information
    51  on  violations  of  this  section that are necessary for the purposes of
    52  enforcement by the attorney general pursuant to section  sixty-three  of
    53  the executive law.
    54    3. The secretary or their designee may administer oaths and take affi-
    55  davits  in  relation  to any matter or proceeding in the exercise of the
    56  powers and duties under this article. The secretary  or  their  designee
