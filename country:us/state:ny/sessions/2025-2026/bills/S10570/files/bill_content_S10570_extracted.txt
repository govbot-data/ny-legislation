Definitions. For the purposes of this article, the following 11 terms shall have the following meanings: 12 1. "Department" means the department of state. 13 2. "Commercial dealer" means any person who is in the business of 14 manufacturing, remanufacturing, retrofitting, distributing, importing, 15 or selling at wholesale any product in New York state. This definition 16 shall not be construed to include retailers, initial consumers, or 17 secondhand dealers. 18 3. "Secretary" means the secretary of state. 19 4. "Initial consumer" means a person who purchases a product for any 20 purpose other than resale. 21 5. "Secondhand dealer" means a person who sells as a primary source of 22 income reconditioned, remanufactured, refurbished, previously owned, or 23 consignment items. Such term shall not include the initial consumer or 24 someone who purchases a product primarily for personal use but who 25 subsequently sells the product. 26 6. "Person" means a natural person and any entity, including but not 27 limited to a sole proprietorship, partnership, firm, corporation, limit- 28 ed liability company, or association, and any employee or agent thereof. Product recall requirements; commercial dealer. 1. When a 13 commercial dealer has made available or placed for sale in this state a 14 product for which a recall has been issued, the commercial dealer shall, 15 within twenty-four hours of issuing the recall, provide notification to 16 the department of such recall. 17 2. All notices under this section shall include, in a clear and 18 conspicuous fashion: 19 (a) a description of the product. 20 (b) the reason for the recall. 21 (c) a picture of the product, if available. 22 (d) instructions on how to return or exchange the recalled product. 23 (e) any other information the secretary shall require. 24 3. No notice under this section shall include sales or marketing 25 information on that product or any other product other than return and 26 exchange policies. 27 § 490-cc. Product recall requirements; secretary of state. 1. The 28 secretary shall: 29 (a) display on the homepage of the department's website a link to all 30 products made available or placed for sale in this state for which a 31 recall has been issued and for which the secretary has knowledge of such 32 recall. 33 (b) provide a method for commercial dealers to give notice to the 34 secretary, as required under section four hundred ninety-bb of this 35 article, in a form prescribed and made available by the secretary. 36 2. Within seven days of receiving a notice from a commercial dealer of 37 a recall, the secretary shall: 38 (a) update the department's website to include all information in the 39 notice from the commercial dealer related to such recall. 40 (b) issue public service announcements to be distributed to television 41 and radio stations and other media throughout the state informing the 42 public of such recall. Such public service announcements shall be 43 distributed in English and such other languages as the secretary deems 44 appropriate. 45 § 490-dd. Enforcement. 1.
