AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to including
stormwater in the definition of sewage for purposes of certain water,
sewer, and water and sewer authorities
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivisions 12, 13 and 14 of section 1045-b of the public
2 authorities law, as added by chapter 513 of the laws of 1984, are
3 amended to read as follows:
4 12. "Sewage" shall mean the water-carried human or animal wastes from
5 residences, buildings, industrial establishments or other places,
6 together with such groundwater infiltration, stormwater, and surface
7 water as may be present. The admixture with sewage of industrial or
8 other waste also shall be considered "sewage" within the meaning of this
9 title.
