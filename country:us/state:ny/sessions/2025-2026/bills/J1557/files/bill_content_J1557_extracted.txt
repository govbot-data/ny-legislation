and WHEREAS, Parker Family Maple was designated 2026 Business of the Year by the New York State Agricultural Society during its 194th Annual Forum, held on Thursday, January 8, 2026, in Rochester, New York; and WHEREAS, This prestigious recognition is bestowed upon a single New York State agribusiness that exemplifies outstanding quality, leadership, and innovation while advancing the integrity of agriculture statewide; and WHEREAS, Parker Family Maple is a multigenerational, family-owned maple enterprise located in the hamlet of West Chazy, Clinton County, whose agricultural roots date back to the early nineteenth century, when Henry Parker immigrated from Quebec prior to 1840; and WHEREAS, In 1889, Adolphus and Amelia Parker established the family's dairy and maple operation, laying the foundation for a legacy of agricultural excellence that has continued through successive generations; and WHEREAS, By 1930, their son, Pearlie Parker, significantly expanded maple production, increasing the number of taps and constructing a sugarhouse that served the family for decades; and WHEREAS, Pearlie's son, Earl Parker, further advanced the enterprise by expanding the farm's acreage, modernizing operations, developing retail and dairy facilities, and becoming a respected maple equipment dealer; and WHEREAS, Patricia Parker played a vital role in the growth of the business by introducing a gift shop, allowing Parker Family Maple products to reach customers across the nation; and WHEREAS, Earl Parker was a pioneering leader within the maple industry, co-founding the Northeastern New York Maple Producers Association and earning induction into the Maple Hall of Fame in 2011, an international honor recognizing exceptional contributions to the maple sector; and WHEREAS, Today, the Parker family legacy is carried forward by Michael Parker and his wife, Laura Parker, along with Kristina Parker, who together oversee an advanced maple operation tapping approximately 100,000 trees and producing more than 60,000 gallons of maple syrup each season; and WHEREAS, Parker Family Maple operates a state-of-the-art sugarhouse featuring automated systems and organic certification, establishing a benchmark for quality, efficiency, and sustainability within the maple industry; and WHEREAS, The farm serves as a vibrant community hub, hosting educational field trips, seasonal breakfasts, and celebratory events that bring together residents and visitors while promoting agricultural awareness and appreciation; and WHEREAS, The New York State Agricultural Society has commended the Parker family for successfully preparing future generations to assume leadership roles, ensuring continuity, integrity, and innovation within the business; and WHEREAS, Parker Family Maple's achievements reflect not only the dedication of the Parker family, but also the vital support of their employees, customers, extended family, and the surrounding community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Parker Family Maple upon the occasion of its designation as 2026 Business of the Year by the New York State Agricultural Society; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Parker Family Maple.
