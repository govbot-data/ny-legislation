AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to the
calculation of statewide greenhouse gas emission limits; and to repeal
section 75-0109 of the environmental conservation law relating thereto
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 75-0109 of the environmental conservation law is
2 REPEALED.
3 § 2. Subdivision 2 of section 75-0101 of the environmental conserva-
4 tion law, as added by chapter 106 of the laws of 2019, is amended to
5 read as follows:
6 2. "Carbon dioxide equivalent" means the amount of carbon dioxide by
7 mass that would produce the same global warming impact as a given mass
8 of another greenhouse gas over an integrated [twenty-year] one hundred-
9 year time frame after emission.
10 § 3. Subdivision 1 of section 75-0107 of the environmental conserva-
11 tion law, as added by chapter 106 of the laws of 2019, is amended to
12 read as follows:
13 1. No later than one year after the effective date of this article,
14 the department shall, pursuant to rules and regulations promulgated
15 after at least one public hearing, establish a statewide greenhouse gas
16 emissions limit as a percentage of 1990 emissions, as estimated pursuant
17 to section 75-0105 of this article[, as follows:
18 a. 2030: 60% of 1990 emissions.
19 b. 2050: 15% of 1990 emissions].
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
