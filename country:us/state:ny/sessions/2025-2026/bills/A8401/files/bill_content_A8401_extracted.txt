The section heading and subdivision 1 of section 534 of the 2 real property tax law, the section heading as amended by chapter 736 of 3 the laws of 1962 and subdivision 1 as amended by chapter 351 of the laws 4 of 1977, are amended to read as follows: 5 Certain state lands subject to taxation for all purposes except 6 certain county purposes. 1.
