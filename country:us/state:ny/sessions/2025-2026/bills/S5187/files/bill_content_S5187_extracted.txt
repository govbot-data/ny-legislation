The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a 2 new section 27-0111 to read as follows: 3 § 27-0111. Illegal disposal cost recovery. 4 A person who is responsible for the illegal disposal of waste as 5 defined in section 27-1301 of this article, or causes a release or a 6 substantial threat of release of a contaminant which presents a substan- 7 tial danger to the public health or safety or the environment, shall be 8 liable for the response costs and for damages to natural resources.
