AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law and the parks, recreation
and historic preservation law, in relation to vintage snowmobile
registrations
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 4-a of section 2222 of the vehicle and traffic
2 law, as amended by section 1 of part MM of chapter 58 of the laws of
3 2023, is amended to read as follows:
4 4-a. Additional fee. (a) In addition to the other fees provided for in
5 paragraphs (a), (b) and (c) of subdivision four of this section the
6 commissioner shall, upon application in such cases for the registration
7 of a snowmobile or the renewal thereof, collect the annual one hundred
8 twenty-five dollar fee for residents and one hundred twenty-five dollar
9 fee for nonresidents or a fifty-five dollar fee for residents and
10 fifty-five dollar fee for nonresidents who provide proof, at the time of
11 registration, that such individual is a member of an organized New York
12 state snowmobile club that is a member of the New York state snowmobile
13 association or is a member of an organized New York state snowmobile
14 club that is a trail maintenance entity and a member of the New York
15 state snowmobile association which are imposed by section 21.07 of the
16 parks, recreation and historic preservation law. In the event that an
17 individual seeking snowmobile club membership is unable, for any reason,
18 to secure such club membership, [he or she] they may contact the New
19 York state snowmobile association, who shall secure such membership for
20 such person. This fee shall also be collected from dealers at the time
21 of original registration and at the time of each renewal. The commis-
22 sioner shall effectuate regulations regarding what is required as proof

21 § 2. Subdivision 4 of section 2222 of the vehicle and traffic law is
22 amended by adding a new paragraph (f) to read as follows:
23 (f) A fee of five dollars for each individual resident and non-resi-
24 dent vintage registration.
25 § 3. Section 21.07 of the parks, recreation and historic preservation
26 law is amended by adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows:
27 3. Notwithstanding the fee as established in subdivisions one and two
28 of this section, an individual resident or nonresident registering a
29 vintage snowmobile who provides proof at the time of registration, that
30 such individual is a member of an organized New York state snowmobile
31 club that is a member of the New York state snowmobile association or is
32 a member of an organized New York state snowmobile club that is a trail
33 maintenance entity and a member of the New York state snowmobile associ-
34 ation, shall pay an additional fee of twenty dollars for each vintage
35 snowmobile for the snowmobile trail development and maintenance fund in
36 addition to the registration required by the vehicle and traffic law. In
37 the event that an individual seeking snowmobile club membership is
38 unable, for any reason, to secure such club membership, they may contact
39 the New York state snowmobile association, who shall secure such member-
40 ship for such person.
41 § 4. This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, a
42 special vintage registration for a snowmobile shall not be issued until
43 the commissioner of motor vehicles certifies that the department of
44 motor vehicles is prepared to issue special vintage registrations for
45 snowmobiles; and provided that the commissioner of motor vehicles shall
46 notify the legislative bill drafting commission upon such certification
47 in order that the commission may maintain an accurate and timely effec-
48 tive data base of the official text of the laws of the state of New York
49 in furtherance of effectuating the provisions of section 44 of the
