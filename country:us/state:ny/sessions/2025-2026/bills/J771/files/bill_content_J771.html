J00771 Text:

 
Senate Resolution No. 771
 
BY: Senator O'MARA
 
        COMMENDING  New  York  State  Trooper  Jonathon R.
        Butcher upon the  occasion  of  his  designation  as
        recipient of the Superintendent's Commendation Award
        on May 7, 2025
 
  WHEREAS,  Law  enforcement  officers  from the State of New York put
their lives on the line every day for  citizens  of  this  great  Empire
State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  quality  and sanctity of community life are cherished
values, worthy of safeguarding, and this Legislative Body  is  moved  to
honor  those  individuals who serve to preserve and protect such values,
lauding particularly their unwavering  courage  and  dedication  in  the
daily performance of their duties; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
New York State Trooper Jonathon R. Butcher  upon  the  occasion  of  his
designation  as  recipient of the Superintendent's Commendation Award to
be celebrated at the New York State Police 49th Annual  Superintendent's
Commendation  and  Awards Day Ceremony on Wednesday, May 7, 2025, at the
State Police Academy in Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, This prestigious award was established to honor outstanding
individuals, dedicated  to  their  service  to  law  enforcement,  whose
valiant efforts protect the people of New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Trooper  Jonathon  R. Butcher of Horseheads, New York, has
been a member of the New York State Police since September 13, 2021; and
 
  WHEREAS, On November 18, 2024, Trooper Jonathon R. Butcher  received
a report of an individual lying on train tracks in Chemung County; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Upon  arrival  at  the  scene, Trooper Jonathon R. Butcher
observed the subject lying on the train tracks while a train was rapidly
approaching; and
 
  WHEREAS, Filled with bravery, courage, and a strong sense  of  duty,
Trooper  Jonathon  R.  Butcher swiftly ran to the individual in need and
pulled him off the train tracks, saving the  individual's  life  seconds
before the train would have impacted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Trooper  Jonathon  R.  Butcher's  quick  thinking and fast
response saved the life of the man on  the  train  tracks;  without  his
noble  actions,  the scene on November 18, 2024, would have been tragic;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The quick thinking, bravery, and exemplary  performance  of
duty  of  Trooper  Jonathon  R. Butcher is in the highest traditions and
standards of the New York State Police and is worthy of both praise  and
honor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body that those
dedicated public servants who unselfishly  devote  their  lives  to  the
preservation  of  order  and the protection of others are worthy and due
full praise for their commitment and noble endeavors; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor New York State Trooper Jonathon R. Butcher upon  the  occasion  of
his designation as recipient of the Superintendent's Commendation Award;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Trooper Jonathon R. Butcher.
