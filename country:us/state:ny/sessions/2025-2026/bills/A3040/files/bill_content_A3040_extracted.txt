ROSENTHAL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Housing AN ACT to amend the multiple dwelling law, in relation to temporary certificates of compliance or occupancy The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 4 of section 301 of the multiple dwelling law, 2 as amended by chapter 131 of the laws of 1979, is amended and a new 3 subdivision 8 is added to read as follows: 4 4. The head of the department may, on the request of the owner or 5 [his] such owner's certified agent, issue a temporary certificate of 6 compliance or occupancy for a multiple dwelling or a section or a part 7 thereof for a period of ninety days or less, provided that such certif- 8 icate shall bear the endorsement that the dwelling has been inspected by 9 the department and complies with all the requirements of this chapter, 10 and that such temporary occupancy will not jeopardize life, health or 11 property. Such temporary certificate may be renewed once at the 12 discretion of the head of the department for similar periods but shall 13 not extend[, together with such renewals,] beyond a total period of [two 14 years] one hundred eighty days from the date of its original issuance. 15 8. Any person found to have violated the provisions of this section 16 shall be liable for a civil penalty of not more than twenty thousand 17 dollars. 18 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
