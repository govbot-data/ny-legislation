and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of 2025 Valedictorian Soriah Johnson and Salutatorian Sihon Brown of Urban Action Academy, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and WHEREAS, Valedictorian Soriah Johnson and Salutatorian Sihon Brown represent the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious resource, our youth; their achievements have brought enduring honor to their families and communities and should be recognized and saluted; and WHEREAS, As Valedictorian Soriah Johnson and Salutatorian Sihon Brown take leave of Urban Action Academy, they bestow a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement and community service to which others may aspire; and WHEREAS, Valedictorian Soriah Johnson and Salutatorian Sihon Brown may now stand with pride as they assess their achievements, experience the satisfaction of their labors and the joy of their accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Class of 2025 Valedictorian Soriah Johnson and Salutatorian Sihon Brown of Urban Action Academy, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Valedictorian Soriah Johnson and Salutatorian Sihon Brown.
