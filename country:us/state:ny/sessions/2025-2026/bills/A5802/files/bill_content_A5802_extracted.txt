Subdivisions 2, 3 and 7 of section 81 of the cannabis law 2 are amended to read as follows: 3 2. Sec 1471 et seq.; 7 (b) prior to delivery or sale at a retailer, cannabis and cannabis 8 products shall be labeled according to regulations and placed in a 9 resealable, child-resistant package accompanied by conspicuous warnings 10 in large, bold-face, legible, unobscured, and visible font contained 11 either on the retail packaging, or if a marketing layer is used, the 12 marketing layer; and the majority of said package shall depict that it 13 is a cannabis product, including unconventional methods of adminis- 14 tration or ingestion or edible consumption of cannabis products; and 15 (c) packages, labels, shapes and products shall not be made to be 16 attractive to or target persons under the age of twenty-one. 17 3.
