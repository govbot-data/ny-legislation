Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J00388 Summary:BILL NOJ00388  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORSTEC COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ00388 Text: Senate Resolution No. 388 BY: Senator STEC HONORING Crossborder Development Corporation upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2025 Small Business of the Year Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize the achievements of those individuals and businesses that contribute substantially to the economic vitality of their communities, thereby enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Crossborder Development upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2025 Small Business of the Year Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce at its 113th Annual Awards Dinner on Friday, January 31, 2025, at the Warren Ballroom at SUNY Plattsburgh; and WHEREAS, Specializing in helping international companies expand into the U.S., particularly businesses from USMCA countries, Crossborder Development provides expert support to streamline market entry and ensure long-term success; their comprehensive services, including immigration assistance, relocation support, and human resources setup have made a meaningful impact on businesses seeking to establish a foothold in the North Country and beyond; and WHEREAS, Crossborder Development is invested in the community, and works to foster economic partnerships as well as supporting workforce development initiatives; and WHEREAS, Crossborder Development understands the complexities and challenges associated with cross-border business growth; their team of professionals provide tailored services to meet the unique needs of their clientele, ensuring the smoothest possible transition and growth into the United States and New York markets; and WHEREAS, The business' staffing and recruiting expertise not only helps companies build dynamic teams, but also strengthens the regional labor market; its dedication to business excellence and community support truly makes Crossborder Development worthy of this prestigious recognition; and WHEREAS, Businesses that give of their time and energies to serve the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business and its contributions to the local and State economies, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the sparkling successes he has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Crossborder Development Corporation upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2025 Small Business of the Year Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Crossborder Development Corporation.
