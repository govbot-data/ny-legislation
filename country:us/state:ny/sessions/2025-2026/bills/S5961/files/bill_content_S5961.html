Amd §92, add §111, Exec L; amd §355, Ed L; add §226, Ec Dev L
 
Establishes an international division within the department of state to establish and maintain international sister state relationships to facilitate and maintain the exchange of students and faculty.

        AN ACT to amend the executive law, the education law  and  the  economic
          development law, in relation to establishing an international division
          within the department of state

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 92 of the  executive  law  is  amended  and  a  new
     2  section 111 is added to read as follows:
     3    § 92. Deputies. The secretary of state shall appoint a deputy, who may
     4  perform  all the duties of the secretary of state. Any such deputy shall
     5  receive an annual salary to be fixed by the secretary  of  state  within
     6  the  amount  appropriated  therefor.  The  secretary  of  state may also
     7  appoint and fix the compensation of such other deputies, assistants  and
     8  employees  as  [he]  such  secretary  of state shall deem necessary, and
     9  including a director of the international division,  as  established  by
    10  section  one hundred eleven of this article, within the amount appropri-
    11  ated therefor and prescribe their powers and duties.
    12    § 111. International division. 1. There  is  established  an  interna-
    13  tional  division  within the department of state. The secretary of state
    14  shall provide for the establishment and operation of such division,  and
    15  shall appoint a director to supervise the operation of such division and
    16  its employees.
    17    2.  The  international  division shall solicit, establish and maintain
    18  sister state relationships between the state of New York and  states  in
    19  other  countries.  For each of this state's international sister states,
    20  the international division shall act as the conduit between the governor
    21  and the chief executive officer of each sister state.
    22    3. The international sister state program shall, in  cooperation  with
    23  the  department of economic development for the purposes of implementing
    24  article ten of the economic development law, establish an active network

     1  to operate a clearinghouse for information resources between this  state

     4    4.  The  international  division  shall assist and work in cooperation

    17  institutions  with  colleges  and  universities  of  the  state's sister

    20  226 to read as follows:
    21    § 226. Cooperation with international division. The provisions of this

    23  consultation and cooperation with  the  international  division  of  the
    24  department of state.
