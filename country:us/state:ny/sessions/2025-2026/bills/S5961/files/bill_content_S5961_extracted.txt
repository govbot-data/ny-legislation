Section 92 of the executive law is amended and a new 2 section 111 is added to read as follows: 3 § 92. Deputies. The secretary of state shall appoint a deputy, who may 4 perform all the duties of the secretary of state. Any such deputy shall 5 receive an annual salary to be fixed by the secretary of state within 6 the amount appropriated therefor. The secretary of state may also 7 appoint and fix the compensation of such other deputies, assistants and 8 employees as [he] such secretary of state shall deem necessary, and 9 including a director of the international division, as established by 10 section one hundred eleven of this article, within the amount appropri- 11 ated therefor and prescribe their powers and duties. 12 § 111. The international division shall solicit, establish and maintain 18 sister state relationships between the state of New York and states in 19 other countries.
