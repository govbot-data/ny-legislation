Amd §16-w, UDC Act
 
Changes the eligibility and criteria for awards of grants from the beginning farmers NY fund to enable more farmers to participate; requires that tiers be established for grants less than $15,000.

        AN  ACT  to  amend the urban development corporation act, in relation to
          the beginning farmers NY fund

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivisions  2,  3 and 5 of section 16-w of section 1 of
     2  chapter 174 of the laws  of  1968  constituting  the  urban  development
     3  corporation  act,  subdivision  2  as  amended by section 1 of part W of
     4  chapter 58 of the laws of 2016 and subdivisions 3 and 5  as  amended  by
     5  section  1  of part FF of chapter 58 of the laws of 2015, are amended to
     6  read as follows:
     7    2. The corporation shall consult with the  department  of  agriculture
     8  and  markets  in order to establish such criteria governing the award of
     9  grants as authorized herein, as the corporation and such department deem
    10  necessary. Such criteria shall include, but not be limited  to,  farmers
    11  who  have  not  produced an "agricultural product" as defined by section
    12  three hundred twenty-eight of the agriculture and markets law, for  more
    13  than  ten  consecutive  years, and who will materially and substantially
    14  participate in the production of an agricultural product within a region
    15  of the state. Such criteria shall also consider  farm  income  and  farm
    16  investments as necessary to accommodate beginning farmers reporting less
    17  than ten thousand dollars of annual farm income.
    18    3. Appropriations to the beginning farmers NY fund may be used for the
    19  following purposes:
    20    (a)  to  assist farmers in demonstrating innovative agricultural tech-
    21  niques including, but not limited  to,  organic  farming  and  specialty
    22  crops.
    23    (b)  capital grants in accordance with a business plan to improve farm
    24  profitability. Upon completion of such business plan,  recipients  shall
