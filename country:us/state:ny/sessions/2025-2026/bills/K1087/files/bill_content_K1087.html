K01087 Text:

 
Assembly Resolution No. 1087
 
BY: M. of A. Weprin
 
        COMMEMORATING the 124th Anniversary celebration of
        the Birthday of the revered Lubavitcher Rebbe, Rabbi
        Menachem M.  Schneerson
 
  WHEREAS,  The  true  architects  of  society and community are those
individuals whose faith, leadership, and commitment  serve  to  sustain,
uplift,  and  strengthen  the  spiritual and civilized values for all of
humanity; the Lubavitcher Rebbe, Rabbi Menachem  M.  Schneerson  is  one
such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS,  World  Jewry  is  celebrating  the  124th  birthday of the
renowned and revered leader of World Jewry, the Lubavitcher Rebbe, Rabbi
Menachem M. Schneerson; and
 
  WHEREAS, The  Rebbe's  leadership  continues  to  inspire  humanity,
including his establishment of over 6000 Chabad Lubavitch Centers around
the  globe,  helping  people  of  all walks of life, regardless of race,
color, or creed, throughout the world-from  Australia  to  Africa,  from
Alaska to Argentina, from Israel to India, from Moscow to Mumbai; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Rebbe  Rabbi  Menachem  M.  Schneerson's  educational
activities throughout the  globe  have  enriched  and  strengthened  the
religious,  educational,  cultural and ethical fibers of all citizens of
the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Rebbe  Rabbi  Menachem  M.  Schneerson,  personifies  a
remarkable  and  holy  man  who  inspired countless Jews and non-Jews to
greater dedication, action and commitment in all  matters  "between  man
and G-d" and "between man and man"; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Rebbe, Rabbi Menachem M. Schneerson proclaimed that the
time  of  the  Redemption  has  arrived and "Moshiach Is On The Way" and
called upon all citizens  of  the  world  to  prepare  with  a  personal
commitment to increase all activities of goodness and kindness; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today,  in  the  United  States  of  America,  the  Chabad
Lubavitch movement has centers and institutions in every  State  of  the
Union; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Globally,  the  Chabad  Lubavitch  movement  has over 6000
centers in over 1,000 cities in 102 countries all over the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, This year, June 13, 2026, corresponding to 28  Sivan  5786,
we will celebrate 85 years since the Rebbe and his wife, Rebbetzin Chaya
Mushka  Schneerson,  miraculously escaped Nazi Europe and arrived to New
York in 1941; 770 Eastern  Parkway,  Brooklyn  New  York  is  the  World
Headquarters of the Chabad Lubavitch movement; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This year, beginning Sunday, March 29, 2026, corresponding
to 11 Nissan, 5786, the  Rebbe's  124th  birthday,  to  July  30,  2026,
corresponding  to  16  Menachem  Av, 5786, is celebrated as "124 Days of
Education,"  in  honor  of  the  Rebbe  and   his   global   educational
contributions and visionary leadership; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Rebbe  personified  leadership,  dignity,  and grace,
focusing on noble global causes and humanitarian efforts for the benefit
of all of humanity, justly earning the devotion of his followers and the
admiration and respect of governments and people around the world;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate  the  traditional  observance  of  the  Anniversary  of  the
Birthday  of the Lubavitcher Rebbe, Rabbi Menachem M. Schneerson, and to
recognize his birthday on Sunday March 29,  2026,  corresponding  to  11
Nissan 5786, to July 30, 2026, corresponding to 16 Menachem Av, 5786, as
"124  Days  of  Education,"  in honor of the Rebbe and in tribute to the
leadership and educational accomplishments of the beloved Rebbe; and  be
it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the son of Rabbi Shmuel M. Butman, the legendary Director
of the Lubavitch Youth Organization.
