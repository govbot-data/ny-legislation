Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript K00123 Summary:BILL NOK00123  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORSchiavoni COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topK00123 Text: Senate Resolution No. 369 BY: Senator PALUMBO CONGRATULATING Peconic Landing upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2024 Pinnacle Award for Best in Wellness in Senior Living Communities in North America by the International Council on Active Aging WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body that the quality and character of life in this great Empire State is greatly enriched by the faithful and diligent work of those institutions which serve to meet the needs of the senior community and its citizenry; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Peconic Landing upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2024 Pinnacle Award for Best in Wellness in Senior Living Communities in North America by the International Council on Active Aging; and WHEREAS, The Pinnacle Award from the International Council on Active Aging honors senior living communities, wellness directors, and executives whose achievements, innovations, directions, or commitments in wellness stand out as extraordinary guiding lights for all organizations and individuals in the active-aging industry; and WHEREAS, Peconic Landing is a community that provides a vibrant and fulfilling lifestyle through choice and opportunity; members are encouraged to pursue their interests and passions to the fullest for continued growth toward successful living; and WHEREAS, The curation of meaningful relationships among Peconic Landing members, its team, and the greater North Fork community at large, nurtures a welcoming spirit and a strong sense of unity which is evident in the joy and comfort reflected in the residents who reside there; and WHEREAS, The efforts of Peconic Landing to become a leader in its field and example of top tier wellness in senior living has justly paid off with this esteemed recognition by the International Council on Active Aging; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those institutions which unselfishly devote their energy, resources, and thoughtful care, with the purpose of improving the quality and dignity of life for our revered seniors, are worthy and due full praise and recognition for their noble and dedicated efforts; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Peconic Landing upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2024 Pinnacle Award for Best in Wellness in Senior Living Communities in North America by the International Council on Active Aging; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Peconic Landing.
