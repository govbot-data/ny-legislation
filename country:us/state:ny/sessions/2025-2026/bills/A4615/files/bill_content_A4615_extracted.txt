Declaration of purpose. 21 The purpose of this article is to regulate outdoor night lighting to 22 preserve and enhance the state's dark sky while promoting safety for 23 people, birds, and other wildlife, conserving energy and reducing our 24 carbon footprint, and preserving the aesthetic qualities of the night 25 sky.
