Paragraph B of subdivision 9 of section 201 of the work- 2 ers' compensation law, as amended by chapter 352 of the laws of 1981, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 B. "Disability" also includes disability caused by or in connection 5 with a pregnancy, including, but not limited to, pregnancy loss. 6 § 2. Paragraph (b) of subdivision 2 of section 204 of the workers' 7 compensation law, as amended by section 5 of part SS of chapter 54 of 8 the laws of 2016, is amended to read as follows: 9 (b) The weekly benefit which the disabled employee is entitled to 10 receive for disability commencing on or after May first, nineteen 11 hundred eighty-nine shall be one-half of the employee's weekly wage, but 12 in no case shall such benefit exceed one hundred seventy dollars; except 13 that if the employee's average weekly wage is less than twenty dollars, 14 the benefit shall be such average weekly wage; provided, however, the 15 weekly benefit which the disabled employee is entitled to receive for 16 disability related to pregnancy loss shall be sixty-seven percent of the 17 employee's average weekly wage but shall not exceed sixty-seven percent 18 of the state average weekly wage in effect.
