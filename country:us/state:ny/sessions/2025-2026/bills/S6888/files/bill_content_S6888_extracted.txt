It is the intent of the legislature to hereby implement the amend- 13 ment to section one of article fourteen, of the New York state constitu- 14 tion pertaining to the Mount Van Hoevenberg Olympic Sports Complex 15 located on forest preserve lands in the town of North Elba in Essex 16 County. 17 2. The legislature intends that, as offset for the use of forest 23 preserve lands for the sports complex, the state shall acquire as an 24 addition to the forest preserve at least two thousand five hundred acres EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. Definitions. 10 For purposes of this title, the following terms shall have the follow- 11 ing meanings: 12 1. "Appurtenances" shall mean those facilities that are directly 13 related to and necessary for the construction, operation, maintenance, 14 and public use of modern Nordic skiing and biathlon trails, such as 15 access roads, parking lots, sanitary facilities, offices, day lodges and 16 amenities, snowmaking, five kilometer of paved training trails, and an 17 open-air Nordic skiing and biathlon stadium. This term shall not 18 include: 19 (a) facilities such as zip lines, hotels, condominiums, swimming 20 pools, all-terrain vehicles or off-road vehicles for public use, tennis 21 courts and other structures and improvements which are not directly 22 related to and necessary for operation, maintenance, and public use of 23 the sports complex; or 24 (b) any structure located at or above an elevation of two thousand two 25 hundred feet above sea level used for the sale of any goods, services, 26 merchandise, food, or beverage. 27 2. "Mount Van Hoevenberg Olympic Sports Complex" means a one thousand 28 thirty-nine and two-tenths acre parcel of forest preserve land located 29 in the town of North Elba in Essex County on which the Mount Van Hoeven- 30 berg Olympic Sports Complex is currently located. For purposes of this 31 title, land owned by the town of North Elba which is located adjacent to 32 forest preserve land and over which the state has an easement, including 33 land on which the Olympic bobsled run is located, shall not be consid- 34 ered part of such sports complex. 35 § 9-2303. Construction, operation, and maintenance of the Mount Van 36 Hoevenberg Olympic Sports Complex. 37 Not more than three hundred twenty-two and twenty-six hundredths acres 38 of forest preserve land at the Mount Van Hoevenberg Olympic Sports 39 Complex may be used for the construction, operation, and maintenance of 40 Nordic skiing and biathlon trails, and appurtenances thereto. Any and 41 all development at the sports complex shall conform to guidelines set 42 forth in the Adirondack park state land master plan and shall be author- 43 ized by a unit management plan, as required by section eight hundred 44 sixteen of the executive law. 45 § 9-2304. Land to be acquired for inclusion in the forest preserve in 46 the Adirondack park. 47 As an offset for the use of forest preserve lands for the sports 48 complex, the state shall acquire no less than two thousand five hundred 49 acres of land for inclusion in the forest preserve in the Adirondack 50 park, subject to legislative approval. Such approval shall include a 51 metes and bounds description and an accurate survey of the lands 52 proposed for inclusion in the forest preserve. 53 § 2.
