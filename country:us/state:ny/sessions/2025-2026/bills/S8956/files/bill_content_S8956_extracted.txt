STEC -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Housing, Construction and Community Development AN ACT to amend the public housing law, in relation to transferring the village of Potsdam housing authority to the village of Canton housing authority; and to repeal title 102 of article 13 of such law relating to the village of Potsdam housing authority The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Title 102 of article 13 of the public housing law, as added 2 by chapter 533 of the laws of 1968, is REPEALED. 3 § 2. Section 520 of the public housing law, as added by chapter 42 of 4 the laws of 1969, is amended to read as follows: 5 § 520. Village of Canton housing authority. 1. A municipal housing 6 authority to be known as the village of Canton housing authority is 7 hereby created and established for the village of Canton in the county 8 of St. Lawrence for the accomplishment of any or all of the purposes 9 specified in article eighteen of the constitution of the state of New 10 York. It shall constitute a body corporate and politic, be perpetual in 11 duration and consist of five members. It shall have the powers and 12 duties now or hereafter conferred by this chapter upon municipal housing 13 authorities. It shall be organized in the manner prescribed by and 14 subject to the provisions of this chapter, and the authority, its 15 members, officers and employees and its operations and activities shall 16 in all respects be governed by the provisions of this chapter. 17 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, rule or regulation to 18 the contrary, the village of Potsdam housing authority, in its entirety, 19 shall be transferred to the village of Canton housing authority. All 20 powers and duties of the village of Potsdam housing authority pursuant 21 to section thirty-seven of this chapter or any other provision relating 22 to municipal housing authorities under article three of this chapter 23 shall be transferred to and assumed by the village of Canton housing 24 authority.
