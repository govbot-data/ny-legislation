The general municipal law is amended by adding a new section 37 99-aa to read as follows: 38 § 99-aa. Use of employment verification system. It shall be unlawful 39 for the governing board of a city, town or village or any municipal 40 corporation to adopt a resolution, ordinance or local law requiring 41 employers to use the federal electronic employment verification system 42 known as E-Verify; provided, however, that nothing in this section shall 43 be construed to prohibit an employer from using the federal electronic 44 employment verification system when required by federal law or as a 45 condition of receiving federal funds or to check the employment authori- 46 zation status of a person who has been offered employment. 47 § 4.
