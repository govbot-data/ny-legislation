and WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to acknowledge those individuals who have rendered extraordinary service to the public in pursuit of the common good, exemplifying the highest standards of civic engagement and responsibility; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Willard Streiff upon the occasion of his retirement, effective December 31, 2024, after an extraordinary 52 years of dedicated public service to the Town of Floyd; and WHEREAS, A lifelong resident of the Town of Floyd, Willard Streiff began his career in public service in the early 1970s during the Nixon administration, starting as a sanitation laborer; over the ensuing decades, he served in numerous essential roles including highway worker, water maintenance technician, school transportation provider, and ultimately, Town Supervisor, a position he held with distinction for the past 10 years; and WHEREAS, Willard Streiff's knowledge of his hometown's infrastructure, landscape, and people is unmatched, having been a reliable presence during emergencies, including the historic blizzard of 1993, when he helped clear the way for emergency responders amid dangerously deep snow; and WHEREAS, Willard Streiff has witnessed and supported the evolution of his community, including the growth of Floyd's Amish population, whose contributions in trades such as carpentry and cabinet-making have enriched the local economy and culture; and WHEREAS, Known for his steady hand, tireless work ethic, and a leadership style rooted in patience, empathy, and fairness, Willard Streiff has provided guidance to his successor with characteristic humility and wisdom, advising, "Get to know your people. Be patient. And don't make snap decisions."; and WHEREAS, Despite stepping away from his official duties, Willard Streiff remains a valued member of the community, often called upon for his expertise, and enjoys spending time with his family, including visits to his daughter stationed with the U.S. Air Force in Las Vegas, as well as camping trips to Sylvan Beach; and WHEREAS, Throughout his remarkable career, Willard Streiff has demonstrated a deep and abiding love for his community, earning the respect, admiration, and gratitude of colleagues, residents, and local leaders alike; and WHEREAS, A public celebration honoring Willard Streiff's decades of service will be held on May 22, 2025, at the Town of Floyd Office Building, where residents will have the opportunity to express their appreciation and congratulations to a man whose work has quietly but profoundly touched countless lives; and WHEREAS, Willard Streiff's legacy of quiet leadership, unwavering dedication, and humble service stands as an enduring example of what it means to serve one's community with integrity and heart; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Willard Streiff upon the occasion of his well-earned retirement after 52 years of dedicated public service to the Town of Floyd, and to extend to him its best wishes for continued health, happiness, and fulfillment in all his future endeavors; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Willard Streiff.
