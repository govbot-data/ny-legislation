Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the amounts appropriated herein may be suballocated or transferred between other agencies, including the office of temporary and disability assistance and the department of veterans' services, with the approval of the temporary president of the senate and the director of the budget. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, funds from this appropriation shall be allocated only pursuant to a plan (i) approved by the temporary president of the senate and the director of the budget which sets forth either an itemized list of grantees with the amount to be received by each, or the methodology for allocating such appropriation, and (ii) which is thereafter included in a senate resolution calling for the expenditure of such funds, which resolution must be approved by a majority vote of all members elected to the senate upon a roll call vote.
