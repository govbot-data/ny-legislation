The education department of the state of New York shall establish 21 an information campaign to be distributed to all youth sports programs, 22 school sports programs, amateur sports programs, and intercollegiate 23 sports programs, requiring such programs to disseminate information to 24 parents and other spectators of such programs on the protections 25 afforded sports officials.
