STIRPE -- read once and referred to the Committee on Science and Technology AN ACT to amend the economic development law, in relation to creating the mobile high-technology training facilities program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The economic development law is amended by adding a new 2 section 362 to read as follows: 3 § 362. Mobile high-technology training facilities program. 1. The 4 division is hereby authorized to create a mobile high-technology train- 5 ing facilities program. The purpose of the program is to award one to 6 one matching grants on a competitive basis to qualified applicants for 7 the design, development and/or outfit of mobile high-technology training 8 facilities that complement certificate, degree or other recognized 9 credentialing programs. Such program shall be established by the divi- 10 sion within ninety days from the effective date of this section. 11 2. For purposes of this section, the following terms shall have the 12 following meanings: 13 (a) "Qualified applicant" means any educational or not-for-profit 14 institution providing certificate, degree or other credentialing 15 programs in high-technology fields including, but not limited to, nano- 16 technology, biotechnology, advanced manufacturing processes and informa- 17 tion technology or any other field defined as an emerging technology by 18 paragraph (b) of subdivision one of section thirty-one hundred two-e of 19 the public authorities law. 20 (b) "Division" means the division of science, technology and inno- 21 vation. 22 3. Applicants shall submit an application to the division comprising a 23 plan including, but not limited to: EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
