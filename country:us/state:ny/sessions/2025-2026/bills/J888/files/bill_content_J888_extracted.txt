Veterans Service Organization, a nonprofit which is dedicated to supporting veterans through housing, employment, and community services and is part of their broader Veterans Everyday Campaign; and WHEREAS, This annual event serves as a tribute to the sacrifices made by veterans, bringing together individuals of all ages and backgrounds to participate in a 5K run or walk, thereby promoting physical wellness and community solidarity; and WHEREAS, Proceeds from the event support the ongoing efforts of the B.V.S.J. Veterans Service Organization to provide essential services to veterans, including housing assistance, employment programs, and community outreach; and WHEREAS, The 2025 Veterans Everyday 5K Run/Walk exemplifies the values of service, community, and health and stands as a testament to the enduring respect and gratitude owed to our veterans; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 2025 Veterans Everyday 5K Run/Walk and to recognize the efforts of the B.V.S.J. Veterans Service Organization in honoring and supporting veterans through this meaningful event; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the organizers of the 2025 Veterans Everyday 5K Run/Walk.
