                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________

                               2025-2026 Regular Sessions
 
                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                    February 4, 2025
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced by M. of A. GALLAHAN -- read once and referred to the Commit-
          tee on Transportation
 
        AN  ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to authorizing
          distinctive license plates for supporters of the conservation  of  the
          Seneca  white deer; and to amend the state finance law, in relation to
          establishing the New York state "preserve the Seneca white deer fund"
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The  vehicle  and  traffic law is amended by adding a new
     2  section 404-ff to read as follows:
     3    § 404-ff. Distinctive  "I  support  the  Seneca  white  deer"  license
     4  plates.  1.  Any  person  residing in this state shall, upon request, be
     5  issued a distinctive license plate bearing the  phrase  "I  support  the
     6  Seneca  white  deer".  Application for such license plate shall be filed
     7  with the commissioner in such form and detail as the commissioner  shall
     8  prescribe.
     9    2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued
    10  in  the  same  manner as other number plates upon payment of the regular
    11  registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article,
    12  provided, however, that an additional annual service charge  of  twenty-
    13  five dollars shall be charged for such plate.
    14    §  2. 1. A distinctive plate established pursuant to section 404-ff of
    15  the vehicle and traffic law, as added by section one of this act,  shall
    16  only  be  designed, produced and issued upon the delivery to the depart-
    17  ment of motor vehicles of a surety bond in the amount  of  six  thousand
    18  dollars,  which  shall be executed by a surety company authorized by the
    19  department of financial services to transact  business  in  this  state.
    20  Provided, however, that if the commissioner of motor vehicles shall have
    21  received  prior  to  plate  design, production and issuance at least two
    22  hundred orders for such distinctive plate together with  the  additional
    23  annual  service  charge  applicable  to  each such order, which shall be

     1  non-refundable, no such surety  bond  shall  be  required.  All  service
     2  charges  collected  pursuant to this section shall be deposited pursuant
     3  to the provisions of section 404-oo of the vehicle and  traffic  law  to
     4  the  credit of the department of motor vehicles distinctive plate devel-

     6  shall  be  used for the design, production, advertising and distribution

     8    2. If, upon the expiration of two years following the date upon  which
     9  distinctive  plates  in  the  series  are  first  available for sale two
    10  hundred or more sets of such plates are sold, a bond delivered  pursuant
    11  to this section shall be discontinued. If fewer than two hundred sets of
    12  such  plates  are  sold  by  such time, the department of motor vehicles
    13  shall be entitled to recover against the bond in an amount proportionate
    14  to such shortfall.
    15    § 3. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section  97-aaaa
    16  to read as follows:
    17    §  97-aaaa.  The New York state "preserve the Seneca white deer fund".
    18  1. There is hereby established in the joint custody of the  commissioner
    19  of  environmental conservation and the comptroller, a special fund to be
    20  known as the "preserve the Seneca white deer fund".
    21    2. Such fund shall consist of all moneys collected pursuant to section
    22  four hundred four-ff of the vehicle  and  traffic  law,  and  all  other
    23  moneys appropriated, credited or transferred thereto from any other fund
    24  or source pursuant to law.
    25    3. The preserve the Seneca white deer fund shall consist of the reven-
    26  ues  required  to  be  deposited  therein  pursuant to the provisions of
    27  section four hundred four-ff of the vehicle and  traffic  law,  and  all
    28  other  moneys  credited  or  transferred  thereto from any other fund or
    29  source pursuant to law.
    30    § 4. This act shall take effect immediately;  provided  however  that,
    31  section  one  of this act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth
    32  day after it shall have become a law. Effective immediately,  the  addi-
    33  tion,  amendment  and/or  repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for
    34  the implementation of this act on its effective date are  authorized  to
    35  be made and completed on or before such effective date.
