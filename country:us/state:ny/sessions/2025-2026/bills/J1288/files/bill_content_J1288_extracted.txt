and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Brody Kaufman upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of a Liberty Medal, the highest honor bestowed upon an individual by the New York State Senate; and WHEREAS, The New York State Senate Liberty Medal was established by Resolution and is awarded to individuals who have merited special commendation for exceptional, heroic, or humanitarian acts on behalf of their fellow New Yorkers; and WHEREAS, On Tuesday, April 22, 2025, 17-year-old resident of New York State, Brody Kaufman, was in the Florida Keys on vacation when he was notified of a kayaker who had flipped over in the ocean and was being swept away in a strong current; and WHEREAS, Brody Kaufman, a junior volunteer firefighter with the Harris Hill Volunteer Fire Company, immediately sprang into action to assist in rescuing the victim from potential drowning; he helped source three different ropes and aided in securing the at-risk man, using the rope to pull him nearly 300-400 yards down a bridge toward safety; and WHEREAS, Emergency vehicles, including the Coast Guard, arrived at the scene shortly after the man was safely brought to shore; witnesses described the scene as a powerful example of ordinary citizens stepping up in a moment of crisis; and WHEREAS, A truly brave and courageous man, Brody Kaufman played a vital role in this extraordinary rescue effort, and should be praised by the people of this great Empire State for his astute actions in working to preserve the life of another; and WHEREAS, Brody Kaufman, in his spontaneous and heroic act, has so aptly demonstrated his character and compassion for the welfare of others, personifying, by virtue of his action, the collective concern of ordinary citizens across the community of New York State who voluntarily respond without thought of danger or reward when others are in need of help; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Brody Kaufman upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of a Liberty Medal, the highest honor bestowed upon an individual by the New York State Senate; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Brody Kaufman.
