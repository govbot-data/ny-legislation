The executive law is amended by adding a new section 382-c to 10 read as follows: 11 § 382-c. Search of digital device. No police agency, prosecutorial 12 agency or other law enforcement agency shall conduct a search of an 13 electronic device used to display a mobile or digital alternative to a 14 driver's license or non-driver identification card to a law enforcement 15 officer for purposes of identity verification in violation of the 16 provisions of subdivision seven of section five hundred four-a of the 17 vehicle and traffic law. No police agency, prosecutorial agency or other 18 law enforcement agency shall seek the consent of the owner or user of 19 the electronic device to conduct a search of the device. 20 § 3.
