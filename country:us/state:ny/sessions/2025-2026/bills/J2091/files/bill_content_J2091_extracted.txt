Calhoun High School in Merrick, New York, has proven herself to be an intelligent, compassionate, and driven student leader; and WHEREAS, Anna Fitzpatrick is actively involved in a variety of extracurricular pursuits, including her extensive work at Camp ANCHOR, showcasing her mentor skills and teamwork abilities; and WHEREAS, By mentoring younger students and contributing to educational enrichment, Anna Fitzpatrick has embodied the principle that citizenship includes a duty to inspire and support the next generation; and WHEREAS, Through her leadership as captain of the cross-country team, Anna Fitzpatrick has demonstrated not only her aptitude, but also her cooperative spirit and responsibility that are hallmarks of exemplary citizenship; and WHEREAS, Academically, Anna Fitzpatrick has consistently pursued rigorous courses at Sanford H. Calhoun High School; she has earned the distinction of AP Scholar with Distinction, as well as a perfect score in AP Biology; her intellectual curiosity, combined with her collaborative spirit, makes her a valued peer in the classroom; and WHEREAS, True citizenship is demonstrated not only through academic success, but through a steadfast commitment to service, ethical leadership, and an unwavering sense of responsibility toward others; and WHEREAS, The essence of citizenship lies in the willingness to act with integrity, to serve selflessly, and to contribute positively to society, qualities which Anna Fitzpatrick has demonstrated through both word and deed; and WHEREAS, Inspired by her family's experience and guided by her deep empathy and concern for others, Anna Fitzpatrick founded "Lizzie's Army," which has raised over $15,000 for breast cancer research; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the selfless dedication shown by individuals for the benefit of others which Anna Fitzpatrick has displayed throughout her life; and WHEREAS, As Anna Fitzpatrick looks forward to her next chapter, she is sure to continue fostering her leadership skills, advancing her academic achievements, and making a meaningful difference in her community; and WHEREAS, Her example affirms that responsible citizenship is a lifelong endeavor, one that extends beyond the classroom and into every aspect of personal and professional life; and WHEREAS, Poised with a great sense of enthusiasm and purpose, this scholar now stands honorably, with just pride in her accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the caring counsel and support provided by her teachers, mentors, and parents; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Anna Fitzpatrick upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the New York State Citizenship Award; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Anna Fitzpatrick.
