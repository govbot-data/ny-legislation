The state administrative procedure act is amended by adding 2 a new section 403 to read as follows: 3 § 403. Compliance with the New York state climate leadership and 4 community protection act. 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the 5 contrary, the department of environmental conservation, the department 6 of public service, the New York power authority, the Long Island power 7 authority, the office of renewable energy siting, and the New York state 8 energy research and development authority shall, by promulgation of 9 regulations, require an application for a license to include information 10 regarding compliance with the goals set forth in the New York state 11 climate leadership and community protection act.
