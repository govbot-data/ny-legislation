The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 2 4-b to read as follows: 3 § 4-b. Cryptocurrency as a form of payment. 1. The following terms, 4 when used or referred to in this section, shall have the following mean- 5 ings: 6 a. "Cryptocurrency issuer" means an issuer of any form of cryptocur- 12 rency, including but not limited to, bitcoin, ethereum, litecoin and 13 bitcoin cash. 14 c. "Person" means an individual, partnership, corporation or any other 15 legal or commercial entity. 16 2.
