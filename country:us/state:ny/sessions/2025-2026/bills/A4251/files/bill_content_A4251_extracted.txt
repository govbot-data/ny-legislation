The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1640-t to read as follows: 3 § 1640-t. Scramble crosswalks in cities with a population of one 4 million or more. 1. There shall be scramble crosswalks in cities with a 5 population of one million or more leading to and from school buildings 6 during times of student arrival and dismissal. Such scramble crosswalks 7 shall include, but not be limited to, the following requirements: 8 (a) scramble crosswalks shall operate on weekdays between 8:00 A.M. 9 and 4:00 P.M.; 10 (b) pedestrians shall wait until a pedestrian-control signal indicates 11 a sign to walk; 12 (c) vehicles shall not turn right at the intersection while the traf- 13 fic signal indicates a red light; 14 (d) bicyclists may proceed with pedestrians when a pedestrian-control 15 signal indicates a sign to walk, provided however, such bicyclists shall 16 yield the right of way to all pedestrians in the intersection; 17 (e) bicyclists may proceed with vehicular traffic while the traffic 18 signal indicates a green light; and 19 (f) signs shall be erected at such intersections with a scramble 20 crosswalk indicating that no person shall enter the intersection unless 21 a pedestrian-control signal indicates that all pedestrians may walk. 22 2.
