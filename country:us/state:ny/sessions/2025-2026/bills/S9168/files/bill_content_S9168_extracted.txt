WEIK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to providing distinctive license plates for family members of police officers killed in the line of duty and eliminating the registration fee for family member distinctive license plates The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive plates for families of police officers killed in 4 the line of duty. 1. Any parent of a police officer killed in the line 5 of duty or the spouse of a parent of a police officer killed in the line 6 of duty, or the surviving spouse of a fallen police officer, or any 7 child, step-child, sibling, step-sibling, or grandparent of a fallen 8 police officer residing in this state shall, upon request, be issued a 9 license plate bearing the words "police officer killed in the line of 10 duty family". If a distinctive plate is issued to a family member of a 11 police officer killed in the line of duty pursuant to this section, 12 additional distinctive plates may be issued for every vehicle registered 13 in the name of the family member of a police officer killed in the line 14 of duty recipient residing in this state or the spouse of a police offi- 15 cer killed in the line of duty family member. Application for said 16 license plate shall be filed with the commissioner in such form and 17 detail as the commissioner shall prescribe. 18 2. The distinctive plate authorized herein shall be issued upon proof, 19 satisfactory to the commissioner, that the applicant or the spouse of 20 the applicant is a parent or the spouse of a fallen police officer 21 parent, or the surviving spouse of a fallen police officer, or any 22 child, step-child, sibling, step-sibling, or grandparent of a fallen 23 police officer. 24 3. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 25 in the same manner as other number plates provided, however, no regis- 26 tration fee or service charge shall be charged for such plate.
