WEIK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to renaming the bridges of the state highway system after New York state's medal of honor recipi- ents The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 10 of the highway law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 47 to read as follows: 3 47. Create and implement a plan to rename the bridges of the state 4 highway system after New York state's medal of honor recipients. Such 5 plan shall (a) require that the bridges to be renamed be as close to the 6 recipients' homes as feasible and (b) provide for the installation and 7 maintenance of adequate signage pursuant to a schedule for the placement 8 of signs to be established considering existing signs that need replac- 9 ing. However, to avoid confusion and to limit any possible disruption of 10 commerce, the designation called for pursuant to this subdivision shall 11 be one of ceremonial nature and the official name of such bridge shall 12 not be changed as a result of this act. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
