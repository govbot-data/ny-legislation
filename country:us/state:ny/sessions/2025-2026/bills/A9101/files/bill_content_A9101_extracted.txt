Section 504 of the public health law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 6 to read as follows: 3 6. (a) For the purposes of this subdivision, the following terms shall 4 have the following meanings: 5 (1) "Certified sanctuary" means any facility that provides temporary 6 or permanent safe haven to nonhuman primates that is fully accredited by 7 the Global Federation of Animal Sanctuaries. 8 (2) "Nonhuman primate" means all nonhuman members of the order 9 Primates. 10 (b) Any animal testing facility that no longer has need for a nonhuman 11 primate in its possession shall, in consultation with such animal test- 12 ing facility's attending veterinarian, consider options for and deter- 13 mine the appropriate care and treatment of such nonhuman primate, which 14 shall include offering for release such nonhuman primate to a certified 15 sanctuary.
