        AN  ACT  to  amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to providing
          distinctive license plates for gold star family members and  eliminat-
          ing  the  registration  fee  for gold star parent and gold star family
          member distinctive license plates

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 404-i of the vehicle and traffic law, as amended by
     2  chapter  277  of  the  laws of 1991 and renumbered by chapter 108 of the
     3  laws of 1994, subdivision 1 as amended by chapter 732  of  the  laws  of
     4  2004 and subdivision 3 as amended by chapter 486 of the laws of 1996, is
     5  amended to read as follows:
     6    §  404-i.  Distinctive plates for gold star [mothers] parents and gold
     7  star families.  1. Any gold star [mother] parent or the spouse of a gold
     8  star [mother]  parent, or the widow  or  widower  of  a  fallen  service
     9  member,  or  any child, step-child, brother, sister, step-brother, step-
    10  sister, or grandparent of a fallen service member residing in this state
    11  shall, upon request, be issued a license plate bearing the  words  "gold
    12  star  [mother]  parent"  or  "gold  star  family",  respectively.   If a
    13  distinctive plate is issued to a gold star [mother] parent or gold  star
    14  family  member  pursuant  to this section, additional distinctive plates
    15  may be issued for every vehicle registered in the name of the gold  star
    16  [mother]  parent  recipient residing in this state or the spouse of such
    17  gold star [mother] parent, or the gold star family  member.  Application
    18  for said license plate shall be filed with the commissioner in such form
    19  and detail as the commissioner shall prescribe.
    20    2. The distinctive plate authorized herein shall be issued upon proof,
    21  satisfactory  to  the  commissioner, that the applicant or the spouse of
    22  the applicant is a gold star [mother] parent or the  spouse  of  a  gold
    23  star  parent, or the widow or widower of a fallen service member, or any
