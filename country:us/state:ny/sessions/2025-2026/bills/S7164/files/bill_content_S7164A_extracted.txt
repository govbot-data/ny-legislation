AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to authorizing
certain persons to use an ATV to enter and exit certain lands for ice
fishing
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 2403 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 2-a to read as follows:
3 2-a. Public lands permitting ice fishing. (a) During times when ice
4 conditions are reasonably safe for the operation of ATVs, as determined
5 by guidelines provided by the department of environmental conservation,
6 and only on lands where snowmobile use is permitted by the department of
7 environmental conservation, including wildlife management areas, state
8 forests, forest preserves, and state parks, a person may operate an ATV
9 to enter and exit such lands when such person is sixty-five years of age
10 or older or such person is severely disabled. Such persons may utilize
11 an ATV as the operator or passenger and shall restrict such usage to
12 traveling to and from ice fishing locations.
13 (b) For the purposes of this subdivision, "severely disabled" shall
14 mean any person having any one or more of the following impairments,
15 disabilities or conditions which are permanent in nature and have been
16 certified by a physician:
17 (i) limited or no use of one or both lower limbs;
18 (ii) neuro-muscular dysfunction which severely limits mobility;
19 (iii) a physical or mental impairment or condition other than those
