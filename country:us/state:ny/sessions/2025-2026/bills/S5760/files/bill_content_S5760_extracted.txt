JACKSON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Civil Service and Pensions AN ACT to amend the civil service law, in relation to requiring appoint- ing authorities to provide appointment and promotion letters The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 61 of the civil service law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 4 to read as follows: 3 4. Appointment and promotion letter. (a) An appointing authority who 4 extends an offer of appointment or promotion to a position in the clas- 5 sified service to any person shall provide such person with an appoint- 6 ment letter within fourteen days of the effective date of such appoint- 7 ment.
