        AN ACT to amend chapter 260 of the laws of 1969, relating to incorporat-
          ing the Pound Ridge Volunteer Exempt Firemen's Benevolent  Association
          and  providing for its powers and duties, in relation to updating such
          association's charter

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Sections  1, 2, 3, 4, and 7 of chapter 260 of the laws of
     2  1969, relating to incorporating the Pound Ridge Volunteer  Exempt  Fire-
     3  men's Benevolent Association and providing for its powers and duties, is
     4  amended to read as follows:
     5    Section  1. Incorporation: membership. A membership corporation by the
     6  name of "Pound Ridge Volunteer Exempt [Firemen's] Firefighters'  Benevo-
     7  lent  Association"  is  hereby  created.  It  shall  be composed of such
     8  persons eligible to membership therein as hereinafter provided as  shall
     9  (a)  notify  the  secretary  of the Pound Ridge Fire Department, Inc., a
    10  volunteer fire department, in writing prior to the organization  meeting
    11  of  such  corporation hereinafter provided for of their desire to become
    12  members, or (b) shall attend such meeting or an adjournment thereof, and
    13  also such persons so eligible as may thereafter become members  of  such
    14  corporation pursuant to its by-laws.
    15    §  2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now, or have
    16  been, or hereafter shall be active volunteer members of the fire depart-
    17  ment of the Pound Ridge Fire District, in the Town of Pound Ridge, Coun-
    18  ty of Westchester, or of any fire corporation in such Fire District, and
    19  who have acquired or shall hereafter acquire the status of exempt volun-
    20  teer [firemen] firefighter as defined in  section  two  hundred  of  the
    21  general  municipal law by virtue of having rendered service in such fire
    22  department or fire corporation, and all persons who are now or hereafter
    23  shall be active volunteer members of  such  fire  department,  shall  be

     1  eligible  for  membership in the corporation created by this act, except
     2  that any [fireman] firefighter who has been removed for cause,  expelled
     3  or  dropped  from  the rolls of any fire company or fire department, and
     4  who  has  not been reinstated therein, shall not be eligible for member-

     7  teer member of the fire department of  the  Pound  Ridge  Fire  District
     8  shall  terminate  before  such  person shall have acquired the status of
     9  exempt volunteer [fireman] firefighter as defined in section two hundred
    10  of the general municipal law by virtue of  having  rendered  service  in
    11  such fire department.
    12    § 3. Purposes and duty of the association. The purposes of such corpo-
    13  ration  shall  be [the maintenance of]: (a) to maintain a suitable head-
    14  quarters for[, and the promotion of  fraternal  intercourse  among]  the
    15  members  of  such  corporation[,  the];  (b)  to provide relief, aid and
    16  assistance [of] to such members [and their families] who are disabled or
    17  indigent, or injured in the performance of  such  member's  duties,  and
    18  such  members'  families;  (c)  to promote and safeguard the welfare  of
    19  the volunteer members of such corporation, and such  members'  families;
    20  (d) to provide for the health and safety of the volunteer members of the
    21  corporation; (e) to enhance the morale  of the volunteer members of such
    22  corporation  by  providing social and recreational  activities  for  the
    23  volunteer members of such corporation; and (f)  the  promotion  of  [the
    24  welfare  of]  the  volunteer  fire  service  within the Pound Ridge Fire
    25  District and territory provided with fire protection  by  contract  with
    26  such Fire District.
    27    §  4. Powers and duties. Such corporation shall have all of the powers
    28  of a membership corporation, and  the  provisions  of  law  relating  to
    29  membership  corporations  shall  apply  to such corporation except where
    30  they conflict with the provisions of this act. [However, unless  written
    31  approval  of  the  state  board  of social welfare shall have first been
    32  obtained this corporation shall not establish and maintain any  home  or
    33  institution   which,  if  separately  incorporated,  would  require  the
    34  approval of such board.]
    35    § 7. Precept for payment of foreign fire insurance premium taxes. Such
    36  corporation shall collect and there  shall  be  paid  to  it  all  taxes
    37  imposed  by section [five hundred fifty-three] nine thousand one hundred
    38  four of the insurance law for fire  department  use  and  benefit,  upon
    39  premiums  for insurance against loss or damage by fire covering property
    40  situated in the Pound Ridge Fire District, in the town of  Pound  Ridge,
    41  county  of  Westchester, or within any duly organized territory in which
    42  the Pound Ridge Fire Department, Inc. is or may be obligated  to  render
    43  fire  protection.  The  officer  of  said  corporation designated by its
    44  by-laws to collect and receive the aforesaid  tax  shall  have  all  the
    45  powers and be subject to all the provisions of the insurance law, relat-
    46  ing  to  treasurers  of fire departments. Such corporation shall also be
    47  entitled to receive a share of the tax imposed by section [five  hundred
    48  fifty-four]  nine  thousand one hundred five of the insurance law, based
    49  upon the business written in the territory with respect to which  it  is
    50  entitled  to  collect  and  receive  the tax under section [five hundred
    51  fifty-three] nine thousand one hundred four of the insurance  law.  Such
    52  taxes  shall  only be used [for the care and relief of disabled or indi-
    53  gent volunteer and exempt volunteer firemen and their families]  by  the
    54  corporation in furtherance of its purposes as set forth in section three
    55  of this act.
