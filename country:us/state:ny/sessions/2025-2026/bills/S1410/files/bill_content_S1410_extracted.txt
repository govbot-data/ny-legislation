Whenever the authorities budget 5 office issues a letter or report regarding the activities and operations 6 of any public authority, the head of the public authority which the 7 letter or report was about shall submit a written response to the letter 8 or report within thirty days of the receipt of the letter or report. If 9 the letter or report makes recommendations for corrective action, such 10 head shall report within one hundred eighty days after receipt thereof 11 to the authorities budget office what steps were taken to implement such 12 recommendations, and, where recommendations were not implemented, the 13 reasons therefor.
