the Eagle Court of Honor ceremony will be held on Saturday, June 14, 2025, at the Wheatley School Auditorium in Old Westbury, New York; and WHEREAS, Emily Solomon of Old Westbury, New York is a member of Scouts BSA Troop 8 G; and WHEREAS, America has achieved its greatest heights of glory through the dedication, determination and tireless efforts of the outstanding citizens of this Nation; and WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain its glory; and WHEREAS, Scouts BSA and its members are dedicated to the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and WHEREAS, Recognizing the important value of an active Scouts BSA Troop in the community, Emily Solomon became a founding member of Scouts BSA Troop 8; and WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in Scouting, earned by approximately five percent of all Scouts, is the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout; and WHEREAS, This outstanding young woman has been involved in Scouting for many years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all the necessary achievements, including completion of a community service project, which was successfully planned, developed, and executed; and WHEREAS, For her project, Emily Solomon led a project named Lasagna Love; she coordinated a food and fund drive to raise awareness regarding food insecurity within her community and prepared and delivered meals to families in need across Long Island; and WHEREAS, Her dedication to the safety and well-being of the citizens of East Williston, New York and the surrounding area is represented through her project and demonstrates her devotion to both community and commitment to others; and WHEREAS, As she goes forward in her academic career, Emily Solomon plans on continuing her education at the University of Wisconsin-Madison while continuing the ideals and principles of Scouting America; and WHEREAS, Emily Solomon's perseverance in rising through the ranks to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of her ability to master difficult tasks; her accomplishment will enable her to emerge as a leader, fully equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to her family, her school, her community, her State and her Nation, and no doubt her future will be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Emily Solomon on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Emily Solomon.
