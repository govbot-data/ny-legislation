Calhoun High School in Merrick, New York, has proven herself to be an intelligent, compassionate, and driven student leader; and WHEREAS, Ruby Cilibrasi is actively involved in a variety of extracurricular pursuits, including as captain of both the varsity tennis team and badminton team; and WHEREAS, Through these activities, Ruby Cilibrasi has demonstrated not only her aptitude, but also the cooperative spirit and shared responsibility that are hallmarks of exemplary citizenship; and WHEREAS, A shining example for her peers and our youth, Ruby Cilibrasi is heavily involved in clubs, community service, and volunteer activities; she can be found helping organize events, mentoring younger students, and sharing her enthusiasm for sports and school activities with others; and WHEREAS, Academically, Ruby Cilibrasi has consistently pursued rigorous courses available at Sanford H. Calhoun High School, including six AP courses this year; her intellectual curiosity, combined with her collaborative spirit, makes her a valued peer in the classroom; and WHEREAS, The essence of citizenship lies in the willingness to act with integrity, to serve selflessly, and to contribute positively to society, qualities which Ruby Cilibrasi has demonstrated through both word and deed; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the selfless dedication shown by individuals for the benefit of others which Ruby Cilibrasi has displayed throughout her life; and WHEREAS, As Ruby Cilibrasi looks forward to her next chapter, she is sure to continue fostering her leadership skills, advancing her academic achievements, and making a meaningful difference in her community; and WHEREAS, Her example affirms that responsible citizenship is a lifelong endeavor, one that extends beyond the classroom and into every aspect of personal and professional life; and WHEREAS, Poised with a great sense of enthusiasm and purpose, this scholar now stands honorably, with just pride in her accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the caring counsel and support provided by her teachers, mentors, and parents; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Ruby Cilibrasi upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the New York State Citizenship Award; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Ruby Cilibrasi.
