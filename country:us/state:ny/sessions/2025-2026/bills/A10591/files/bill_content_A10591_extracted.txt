BEEPHAN -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Local Governments AN ACT to amend the general municipal law and the volunteer firefight- ers' benefit law, in relation to authorizing municipalities to estab- lish a stipend program for volunteer firefighters The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The general municipal law is amended by adding a new 2 section 200-aaa to read as follows: 3 § 200-aaa. Stipend program for volunteer firefighters. 1. For purposes 4 of this section: 5 (a) "fire company" shall have the same meaning as defined in section 6 three of the volunteer firefighters' benefit law. 7 (b) "stipend program" means the payment of a nominal fee to a volun- 8 teer firefighter for the completion of scheduled duty coverage as deter- 9 mined by the authority having jurisdiction. 10 (c) "volunteer firefighter" shall have the same meaning as defined in 11 section three of the volunteer firefighters' benefit law. 12 2. The governing board of a city, town, village or fire district, fire 13 protection district, or other authority having jurisdiction may, by 14 local law, ordinance or resolution, establish a stipend program for 15 volunteer firefighters and determine the structure, scheduling, eligi- 16 bility requirements, and compensation parameters of such program. 17 3. Any stipend provided pursuant to a stipend program establishment 18 under this section shall not be deemed remuneration or gratuity under 19 the volunteer firefighters' benefit law or any other provision of law 20 and shall not interfere with or impact the volunteer status of volunteer 21 firefighters under the volunteer firefighters' benefit law or any other 22 provision of law. 23 4. The office of fire prevention and control may issue non-binding 24 guidelines to assist authorities having jurisdiction in the development 25 and administration of stipend programs established pursuant to this 26 section.
