AN ACT to amend the insurance law, in relation to including image-guided
biopsies under certain insurance coverage for breast cancer screening
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Item (iv) of subparagraph (A) of paragraph 11 of subsection
2 (i) of section 3216 of the insurance law, as amended by chapter 424 of
3 the laws of 2024, is amended to read as follows:
4 (iv) upon the recommendation of a physician, screening and diagnostic
