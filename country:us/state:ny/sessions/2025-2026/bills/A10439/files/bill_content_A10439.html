Add Title K Art 191 §§191.00 - 191.55, amd §§460.10, 490.05 & 60.27, rpld §§190.77, 190.78, 190.79, 190.80, 190.80-a, 190.81, 190.82, 190.83, 190.84, 190.85 & 190.86, Pen L; rpld §700.05 sub 8 ¶(s), amd §700.05, CP L; amd §899-aa, Gen Bus L; amd §208, St Tech L; amd §646, Exec L
 
Relates to offenses involving theft of identity; creates five tiers of crimes, based on the financial loss to the victim(s) and the number of people victimized.
