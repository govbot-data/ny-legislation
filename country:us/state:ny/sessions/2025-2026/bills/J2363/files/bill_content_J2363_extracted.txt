Kennedy Elementary School - Richard immediately ran to the nearest phone and called the fire department; and WHEREAS, The West Babylon Fire Department found not only the house on fire, but that a mother and young daughter were still inside and unaware that the house was on fire; and WHEREAS, For his heroic actions, the Chiefs of the West Babylon Fire Department will be presenting Richard Duncan with an award in a small ceremony at the Fire Headquarters in West Babylon, on Saturday, June 27, 2026; and WHEREAS, In his spontaneous and heroic actions, Richard Duncan demonstrated his character and compassion for the welfare of others, exemplifying the best qualities inherent in our citizens; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Richard Duncan upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of a Liberty Medal, the highest honor bestowed upon an individual by the New York State Senate; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Richard Duncan.
