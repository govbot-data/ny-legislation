Any method used to receive electronic 43 bids and offers shall comply with article three of the state technology 44 law, and any rules and regulations promulgated and guidelines developed 45 thereunder and, at a minimum, must (a) document the time and date of 46 receipt of each bid and offer received electronically; (b) authenticate 47 the identity of the sender; (c) ensure the security of the information 48 transmitted; and (d) ensure the confidentiality of the bid or offer 49 until the time and date established for the opening of bids or offers. 50 The timely submission of an electronic bid or offer in compliance with 51 instructions provided for such submission in the advertisement for bids 52 or offers and/or the specifications shall be the responsibility solely 53 of each bidder or offerer or prospective bidder or offerer. No political 54 subdivision or district therein shall incur any liability from delays of 55 or interruptions in the receiving device designated for the submission 56 and receipt of electronic bids and offers.
