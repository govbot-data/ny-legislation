WEIK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to aggravated assault in the third degree The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 120.80 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 120.80 Aggravated assault in the third degree. 4 A person is guilty of aggravated assault in the third degree when such 5 person commits the crime of assault in the third degree as defined in 6 section 120.00 of this article and is aided by another person who is 7 physically present. 8 Aggravated assault in the third degree is a class E felony. 9 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of November next succeed- 10 ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
