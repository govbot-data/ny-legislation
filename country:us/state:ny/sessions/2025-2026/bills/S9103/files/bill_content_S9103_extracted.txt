SEPULVEDA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Racing, Gaming and Wager- ing AN ACT to amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, in relation to prohibiting gambling on elections The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law is 2 amended by adding a new section 913 to read as follows: 3 § 913. Gambling on elections prohibited. 1. For purposes of this 4 section, the term "gambling" shall have the same meaning as such term is 5 defined by section 225.00 of the penal law. 6 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, gambling upon the 7 results of any political election shall be prohibited. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
