and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Michael Donner of Donner's Landscaping & Property Services upon the occasion of his designation as Emerging Entrepreneur by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated at its 53rd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 19, 2026, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, Donner's Landscaping & Property Services, LLC, was officially founded in November of 2022, although Michael Donner began assisting clients with driveway and sidewalk shoveling in 2021; and WHEREAS, Determined to succeed, Michael Donner used his personal sedan to complete contracts timely and efficiently despite not having a business vehicle during his first season; and WHEREAS, Eventually, Michael Donner and Donner's Landscaping & Property Services began expanding their services by doing small landscape leveled tree work and snowplowing with a newly acquired truck; and WHEREAS, Following their official founding in 2022, Donner's Landscaping & Property Services has continually expanded their services and revenue each season; the company is now staffed by three crews with nine total employees; and WHEREAS, Serving the Clarence, Williamsville, Lancaster, and Cheektowaga communities, Donner's Landscaping & Property Services proudly offers a wide variety of top-quality services, including lawn maintenance, landscape renovations, spring clean-ups, leaf clean-ups, gutter cleanings and repairs, tree and stump removal, tree trimming, and snow removal; and WHEREAS, Founded and operated upon Michael Donner's vision, Donner's Landscaping & Property Services ensures they can assist with all aspects of property maintenance, and employees approach all clients with a helpful and professional mindset; and WHEREAS, The Clarence Chamber of Commerce's recognition of Michael Donner as Emerging Entrepreneur is a fitting tribute to an individual whose organization's innovation and service has strengthened the Clarence community and enriched the lives of countless individuals and families; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Michael Donner of Donner's Landscaping & Property Services upon the occasion of his designation as Emerging Entrepreneur by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 19, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Michael Donner of Donner's Landscaping & Property Services.
