The New York State Department of Labor, in consultation 2 with the Office of Employee Relations, the Office for People with Devel- 3 opmental Disabilities, the Office of Mental Health, the Justice Center 4 for the Protection of People with Special Needs, the Council on Develop- 5 mental Disabilities, Adult Career and Continuing Education Services-Vo- 6 cational Rehabilitation (ACCESS-VR), the Office of the Chief Disability 7 Officer, and any other relevant groups as deemed necessary, shall estab- 8 lish a voluntary training program and certification for employers, enti- 9 tled the Neurodiversity Training Pledge, to support an inclusive work- 10 place and the advantages of a neurodiverse workforce. Such certification 11 shall be awarded upon completion of the training program. The training 12 program shall promote techniques and strategies employers can implement 13 to increase neurodiversity in the workplace. 14 b.
