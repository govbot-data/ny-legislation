BILL NOA07318
 
SAME ASNo Same As

Add Art 14 §§310 - 312, Transp L
 
Provides for the reestablishment of the Westchester Parkway Commission, to advise the commissioner of transportation regarding the operation and maintenance of the special parkways within the county of Westchester.

        AN ACT to amend the transportation law, in  relation  to  reestablishing
          the Westchester parkway commission
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. The transportation law is amended by adding a  new  article
     2  14 to read as follows:
     3                                 ARTICLE 14
     4                       WESTCHESTER PARKWAY COMMISSION
     5  Section 310. Westchester parkway commission.
     6          311. General powers and duties of the commission.
     7          312. Meetings of the commission.
     8    §  310. Westchester parkway commission. 1. There is hereby created the
     9  Westchester parkway commission. The  commission  shall  be  an  advisory
    10  council  to  the  commissioner with respect to the operation and mainte-
    11  nance of the special parkways within the county  of  Westchester  desig-
    12  nated  in  section  seventy  of this chapter and with respect to capital
    13  projects to be undertaken.
    14    2. The commission shall be composed of five members. The members shall
    15  be residents  of  the  county  of  Westchester.  The  members  shall  be
    16  appointed by the governor with the advice and consent of the senate and,
    17  except for the initial appointment terms, shall serve for a term of four
    18  years. The governor shall make such appointments, one upon the recommen-
    19  dation  of the temporary president of the senate and one upon the recom-
    20  mendation of the speaker of the  assembly,  each  initial  appointee  to
    21  serve  for a term of one year; one from a list of names submitted by the
    22  county executive, which initial appointee shall serve a  term  of  three
    23  years;  one  from  a  list  of names submitted by the chairperson of the
    24  county legislative body, which initial appointee shall serve for a  term
    25  of  two years; and one who shall be the chairperson as designated by the

     1  governor, which initial appointee shall serve for a term of four  years.
     2  In  the  event  of  a  vacancy,  the vacancy shall be filled in the same
     3  manner as the original appointment. The commissioner,  or  such  commis-
     4  sioner's  designee,  shall  be  an  ex officio member of the commission.
     5  Members shall not receive a salary or other compensation, but  shall  be
     6  reimbursed  their actual and necessary expenses incurred in the perform-
     7  ance of their official duties.
     8    § 311. General powers and duties of the  commission.  The  Westchester
     9  parkway commission shall have the following powers and duties:
    10    1.  To  assist  in  the development of standards for the operation and
    11  maintenance of the special parkways in Westchester county and to  advise
    12  the commissioner with regard to such standards for operation and mainte-
    13  nance on such parkways.
    14    2.  To  monitor  the  parkway  operation  and  maintenance  activities
    15  performed on the special parkways in Westchester county by  the  depart-
    16  ment  and  to  advise and report to the commissioner whether or not such
    17  standards as may have been adopted are being met.
    18    3. To act in conjunction with the commissioner with respect to assess-
    19  ment of parkway capital needs and evaluation  of  project  alternatives,
    20  and priorities relating thereto.
    21    §  312. Meetings of the commission. The Westchester parkway commission
    22  shall meet at least four times a year at the call  of  the  commissioner
    23  and  shall  keep a record of all of its proceedings and recommendations.
    24  Staff services for the commission shall be performed by personnel of the
    25  department. The commissioner shall designate an executive secretary from
    26  among the staff of the department, who shall act as  the  administrative
    27  agent  of  the  commission  and  assist the commission in fulfilling its
    28  duties and functions.
    29    § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth  day  after  it  shall
    30  have become a law.
