Subdivisions 2 and 6 of section 70-b of the executive law, 2 as added by chapter 95 of the laws of 2020, are amended to read as 3 follows: 4 2. (a) For any incident under this section, the office of special 16 investigation shall issue a public report and post the report on its 17 website whenever the office of special investigation initiates an inves- 18 tigation and (i) the office of special investigation declines to present 19 evidence to a grand jury or (ii) the office of special investigation 20 does present evidence to a grand jury but the grand jury declines to 21 return indictment on any charges. The report will include, to the extent 22 possible and lawful, the results of the investigation of the incident. 23 Such report shall be issued within sixty days of the initiation of the 24 investigation; provided that such period may, for good cause shown, be 25 extended for thirty day intervals.
