Conrad HONORING Peter Ananiadis upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of owning and operating Nick's Place in Buffalo, New York WHEREAS, This Legislative Body seeks to acknowledge those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to enhance the local communities which make up this great State of New York; and WHEREAS, For the past 30 years, Peter Ananiadis rendered faithful, conscientious, and valuable service to his community and to his loyal patrons of Nick's Place, a Greek diner in the City of Buffalo, New York; and WHEREAS, In his capacity as owner and operator, Peter Ananiadis always led his staff with passion, kindness, and dedication; and WHEREAS, Peter Ananiadis moved from Greece to Buffalo as a child and worked his way up in family restaurants until he became the owner of Nick's Place; and WHEREAS, Despite Nick's Place closing on Saturday, February 22, 2025, Peter Ananiadis will keep busy in his leisure time by helping at Nick's Place on Elmwood, also known as Nick's Place Express, the restaurant owned by his beloved son; and WHEREAS, In his official acts, Peter Ananiadis was governed by an unwavering sense of duty and always demonstrated keen attention to detail, ultimately growing Nick's Place into a thriving, successful business and cherished community staple; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business, its contributions to the local and state economies, and its vibrant role in improving the quality of life in its community; now, therefore, be it WHEREAS, During his time operating Nick's Place, Peter Ananiadis earned the admiration, esteem, and affection of his colleagues; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Peter Ananiadis upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of distinguished service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Peter Ananiadis.
