LEVENBERG -- read once and referred to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to penalties for assault and menacing of child protective services workers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 120.08-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 120.08-a Assault on a child protective services worker. 4 A person is guilty of assault on a child protective services worker 5 when, with intent to prevent an employee of a local social services 6 district directly involved in investigation of or response to alleged 7 abuse or neglect of a child from performing such investigation or 8 response, the actor causes serious physical injury to such employee 9 including by means of releasing or failing to control an animal under 10 circumstances evincing the actor's intent that the animal obstruct the 11 lawful activities of such employee. 12 Assault on a child protective services worker is a class C felony. 13 § 2. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 120.08-b to read 14 as follows: 15 § 120.08-b Aggravated assault on a child protective services worker. 16 A person is guilty of aggravated assault on a child protective 17 services worker when, with intent to prevent an employee of a local 18 social services district directly involved in investigation of or 19 response to alleged abuse or neglect of a child from performing such 20 investigation or response, the actor causes serious physical injury to 21 such employee by means of a deadly weapon or dangerous instrument. 22 Aggravated assault on a child protective services worker is a class B 23 felony. 24 § 3. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 120.18-a to read 25 as follows: EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
