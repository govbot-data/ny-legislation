S07873 Summary:

BILL NOS07873A
 
SAME ASSAME AS A08526-A
 
SPONSORPALUMBO
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
 
Authorizes alienation of certain parkland in the town of Brookhaven, Suffolk county and the lease of such land for a wireless communication tower, and an easement for access and utilities.

        AN ACT in relation to authorizing the  town  of  Brookhaven,  county  of
          Suffolk, to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. (a) Subject to the provisions of  this  act,  the  town  of
     2  Brookhaven,  in the county of Suffolk, acting by and through its govern-
     3  ing body, and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such body,
     4  is hereby authorized to discontinue as parklands and to  lease  at  fair
     5  market  value  to LI Smart Solutions LLC for a term not to exceed thirty
     6  years, the lands more particularly described in section two of this  act
     7  for  the  placement and operation of a wireless communication tower.  In
     8  addition, the town of Brookhaven, in the county of  Suffolk,  acting  by
     9  and through its town board is hereby authorized to convey an easement to
    10  LI  Smart  Solutions  LLC  over  certain  municipally  owned  park lands
    11  described in section three of this act, to maintain an access  road  for
    12  the  purposes  described in section three of this act, provided that the
    13  thoroughfare shall be publicly accessible and able to be used  for  park
    14  purposes.
    15    (b)  All  proceeds  from such lease shall be used for capital improve-
    16  ments to existing park and recreational facilities and/or for the acqui-
    17  sition of additional park and recreational facilities, and the  town  of
    18  Brookhaven  shall  dedicate  an amount equal to or greater than the fair
    19  market value of the easement interests being conveyed  by  this  section
    20  for  capital  improvements  to existing park and recreational facilities
    21  and/or for the acquisition of additional park and  recreational  facili-
    22  ties.

     3    §  4.  Should  the  leased  lands described in section two of this act
     4  cease to be used for the purposes described in section one of this  act,
     5  the  lease  shall  terminate and those lands shall revert to the town of
     6  Brookhaven for public park and recreational purposes  and  the  easement
     7  described  in section three of this act for access shall also revert and
     8  be extinguished. At the time of such  reversion,  the  removal  of  such
     9  wireless  communication tower shall take place and the property shall be
    10  returned to its previous state, consistent with  park  and  recreational
    11  purposes.
    12    §  5.  In  the  event that the town of Brookhaven received any funding
    13  support or assistance from the  federal  government  for  the  purchase,
    14  maintenance  or  improvement  of the parklands set forth in sections two
    15  and three of this act, the discontinuance and alienation of  such  park-
    16  land  authorized by the provisions of this act shall not occur until the
    17  town of Brookhaven has complied with any federal requirements pertaining
    18  to the alienation or conversion of parkland,  including  satisfying  the
    19  secretary  of  the  interior  that the alienation or conversion complies
    20  with all conditions which the secretary of the interior deems  necessary
    21  to  assure  the  substitution of other lands shall be equivalent in fair
    22  market value and usefulness to the lands being alienated or converted.
    23    § 6. This act shall take effect immediately.
