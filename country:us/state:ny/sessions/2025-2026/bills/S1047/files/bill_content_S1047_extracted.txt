The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1885 to read as follows: 3 § 1885. Ride clean rebate program. 1. For purposes of this section 4 "eligible purchase" shall mean the purchase of a bicycle with electric 5 assist, as defined in section one hundred two-c of the vehicle and traf- 6 fic law, or the purchase of an electric scooter, as defined in section 7 one hundred fourteen-e of the vehicle and traffic law, that: (a) has not 8 been modified from the original manufacturer's specifications; (b) uses 9 a battery that has been certified by an accredited testing laboratory 10 for compliance with a battery standard; and (c) such certification or 11 the logo, wordmark, or name of such accredited testing laboratory shall 12 be displayed on packaging or documentation at the time of sale for the 13 product or directly on the product itself. 14 2. The authority shall develop a program, entitled the ride clean 15 rebate program, to encourage the deployment of bicycles with an electric 16 assist and electric scooters. 17 3. The program created pursuant to this section shall include a fifty 18 percent rebate, for eligible purchases, provided that a rebate for an 19 eligible purchase shall not exceed one thousand one hundred dollars 20 ($1,100).
