Nimey began the business known today as Nimey Auto Repair in faithful service to his community; and WHEREAS, In 1975, Joseph G. Nimey officially purchased the property where the business is located today: at the corner of Oneida Street and Chapman Road in New Hartford, New York; and WHEREAS, Joseph G. Nimey retired in 2002 and sold the business to his dedicated son, Joseph M. Nimey, who had been working at the business since 1983 when he graduated high school; and WHEREAS, Joseph M. Nimey, alongside two to three employees, has since ran the business with professionalism and passion for the past 42 years; and WHEREAS, A true family legacy, Joseph M. Nimey's two children, Greyden and Leighton, have enjoyed working at Nimey Auto Repair for the past seven and four years, respectively; and WHEREAS, A testament to love, teamwork, and a shared commitment to excellence, Joseph M. Nimey's wife Lisa also joined the business as the office manager in 2019, taking care of customer relations with grace; and WHEREAS, The success of Nimey Auto Repair is due in part to the commitment and contributions of the dedicated leadership and staff over the past six decades, including that of the current office manager, Jared Hartman; and WHEREAS, The Nimey family has, over the course of three generations, developed a consistent clientele of valued community members-all of whom bring joy and purpose to the faithful staff and management of Nimey Auto Repair; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business, its contributions to the local and state economies, and its vibrant role in improving the quality of life in its community, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the sparkling successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 60th Anniversary of Nimey Auto Repair; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Nimey Auto Repair.
