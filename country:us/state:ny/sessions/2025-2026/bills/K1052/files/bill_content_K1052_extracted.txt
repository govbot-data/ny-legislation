and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim March 15-21, 2026, as Agriculture Week in the State of New York, in conjunction with the observance of National Agriculture Week; and WHEREAS, Agriculture Week recognizes and celebrates the abundance provided by agriculture; annually, producers, agricultural associations, corporations, universities, governmental agencies, and countless other organizations across America join to recognize the contributions of agriculture; and WHEREAS, The agricultural industry is a leading driver of our local economy, contributing to dairy, grain, and livestock production and supporting countless jobs in transportation, logistics, and agribusiness; and WHEREAS, It is essential for all citizens to understand how food and fiber products are produced and to appreciate the role that agriculture plays in our daily lives; and WHEREAS, By building awareness, the Agriculture Council of America encourages young people to consider career opportunities in agriculture; and WHEREAS, The celebration of National Agriculture Week is in recognition of all the farmers who feed us and our families every day and the importance of agriculture as a vital industry and economic driver in New York State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim March 15-21, 2026, as Agriculture Week in the State of New York, in conjunction with the observance of National Agriculture Week, honoring and recognizing the important contributions of all branches of farming to the health, economic well-being, environment, beauty, and future of New York State; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to The Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of New York.
