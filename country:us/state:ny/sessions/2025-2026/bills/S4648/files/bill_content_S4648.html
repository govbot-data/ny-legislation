        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation  to  increasing
          the  number  of  photo speed violation monitoring systems in a highway
          construction or maintenance work area; and to amend chapter 421 of the
          laws of 2021 amending the vehicle and  traffic  law  and  the  general

          making such provisions permanent
