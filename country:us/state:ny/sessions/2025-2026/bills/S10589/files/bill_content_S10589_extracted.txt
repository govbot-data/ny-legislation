PERSAUD -- (at request of the Office of Temporary and Disability Assistance) -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Social Services AN ACT to amend chapter 436 of the laws of 1997 amending the social services law and other such law relating to enacting The Welfare Reform Act of 1997, in relation to changing the name of the office of temporary and disability assistance to the office of economic security The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph 2 of subdivision (a) of section 122 of part B of 2 chapter 436 of the laws of 1997 amending the social services law and 3 other such law relating to enacting The Welfare Reform Act of 1997, is 4 amended to read as follows: 5 (2) the office of [temporary and disability assistance] economic secu- 6 rity. 7 § 2. Notwithstanding any other provisions of law to the contrary, 8 wherever the term "office of temporary and disability assistance" 9 appears in the consolidated or unconsolidated laws or regulations of 10 this state, such term shall be deemed to mean "office of economic secu- 11 rity". 12 § 3. Notwithstanding any other provisions of law to the contrary, 13 wherever the term "commissioner of temporary and disability assistance" 14 appears in the consolidated or unconsolidated laws or regulations of 15 this state, such term shall be deemed to mean "commissioner of economic 16 security". 17 § 4. Terms occurring in laws, contracts, publications, guidance and 18 other documents. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the 19 contrary, whenever the former department of social services or office of 20 temporary and disability assistance is referred to or designated in any 21 law, contract, publication, guidance, or document pertaining to the 22 functions, powers, obligations and duties of that department transferred 23 by chapter 436 of the laws of 1997 to the office of temporary and disa- 24 bility assistance, such reference or designation shall be deemed to 25 refer to the office of economic security or the commissioner thereof.
