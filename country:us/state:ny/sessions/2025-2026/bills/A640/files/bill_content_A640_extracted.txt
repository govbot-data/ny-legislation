Special advocate in proceedings regarding animal welfare. 1. 7 In any civil or criminal proceeding regarding the welfare of an animal, 8 the court may order, upon its own initiative or upon request of a party 9 or counsel for a party, that a separate advocate be appointed to repre- 10 sent the interests of such animal and to help ensure the well-being of 11 any living animal victim. If a court orders that an advocate be 12 appointed to represent the interests of an animal, the court shall 13 appoint such advocate from a list provided to the court by the chief 14 judge of the state of New York pursuant to subdivision five of this 15 section. 16 2.
