Section 92 of chapter 617 of the laws of 1937, relating to 2 an additional alternative form of government for the county of Westches- 3 ter, is amended to read as follows: 4 § 92. Civil service law to apply; powers and duties of the personnel 5 officer. Except as otherwise provided by this act, all positions in the 6 county service shall be subject to the civil service law of the state. 7 The personnel officer shall have with respect to the county service the 8 powers and duties of a municipal civil service commission [except that 9 the state civil service commission shall continue to have those powers 10 and duties specified in the civil service law relating to (a) the 11 assignment of positions in the classified service to exempt, labor, 12 non-competitive and competitive classes, or to such other classes as may 13 be established generally by rules of the commission, (b) the determi- 14 nation of the merit and fitness of applicants and (c) the certification 15 of lists of eligibles for appointment to positions in the county 16 service. The state civil service commission, in certifying lists of 17 eligibles for appointment to positions in the county service shall 18 include in such lists only the names of residents of Westchester county 19 unless specifically instructed otherwise in writing by the personnel 20 officer after approval by the county executive]. 21 § 2.
