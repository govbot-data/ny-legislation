Subparagraph (ii) of paragraph (a) of subdivision 10 of 2 section 230 of the public health law, as amended by chapter 558 of the 3 laws of 1994, is amended to read as follows: 4 (ii) If the investigation of cases referred to an investigation 5 committee involves issues of clinical practice, medical experts, shall 6 be consulted.
