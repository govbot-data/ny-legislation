Egger, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to citizens of the State of New York whose lifework and civic endeavors served to enhance the quality of life in their communities and the great State of New York; and WHEREAS, Xavier M. Egger, of Stony Point, New York, died on Thursday, April 3, 2025, at the age of 19; and WHEREAS, Xavier M. Egger was born on May 31, 2005; he was a United States Army veteran and volunteer firefighter; and WHEREAS, This caring and kind young man will be immensely missed by all who were fortunate to know him and have him in their lives, albeit for such a short time; for the upstanding person he was, Xavier M. Egger is due respect and appreciation from his fellow New Yorkers; and WHEREAS, Armed with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of compassion, Xavier M. Egger leaves behind a legacy which will long endure the passage of time and will remain as a comforting memory to all he served and befriended; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to mourn the death of Xavier M. Egger; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the family of Xavier M. Egger. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
