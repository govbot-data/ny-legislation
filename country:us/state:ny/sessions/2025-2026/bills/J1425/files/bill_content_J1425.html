J01425 Text:

 
Senate Resolution No. 1425
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
        COMMEMORATING  Jubilee Day to be celebrated by the
        Desert of New York, Ancient  Egyptian  Arabic  Order
        Nobles Mystic Shrine on June 7, 2026
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commemorate  occasions  of  deep  historical,  cultural,  and  spiritual
significance  that  honor  freedom,  resilience,  faith,  and collective
progress and reaffirm the enduring values of service, unity, and  uplift
within our communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  Jubilee Day, to be observed on Sunday, June 7, 2026, by the
Desert of New York, Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles  Mystic  Shrine
(A.E.A.O.N.M.S.); and
 
  WHEREAS,  Jubilee  Day is a sacred and celebratory observance within
the Prince Hall Shriner tradition,  commemorating  liberation,  renewal,
and thanksgiving; it draws its name and spirit from the Biblical concept
of  Jubilee,  which  is a time of restoration, freedom, forgiveness, and
communal  rejoicing,  and  serves  as  a  reminder  of  the  triumph  of
perseverance over bondage and adversity; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Within  the  Prince  Hall  Masonic  and Shriner tradition,
Jubilee Day holds profound historical and cultural meaning, honoring the
emancipation legacy of African-descended people, the victories  achieved
through  faith  and  fortitude,  and  the ongoing commitment to justice,
brotherhood, and collective responsibility; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine,  an
appendant body of Prince Hall Freemasonry, has long stood as a pillar of
philanthropy,   civic   leadership,   and   fraternal  unity,  advancing
charitable  works,  community  empowerment,   youth   development,   and
humanitarian service throughout the State of New York and beyond; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Desert  of  New  York continues to uphold these noble
traditions  through  observances,  such  as  Jubilee  Day,  which  bring
together   Nobles,   Daughters,   families,  and  community  members  in
fellowship, reflection, worship, and celebration of shared heritage  and
purpose; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  2026 observance of Jubilee Day is conducted under the
esteemed leadership of HPIP Jimmie Chaple, Imperial Deputy of the Desert
of New York, and HPIC Cassandra Duff, Imperial Deputy for the Desert  of
New  York,  whose  guidance,  stewardship,  and  devotion  exemplify the
highest ideals of the Shrine  and  reinforce  its  mission  of  service,
unity, and enlightened leadership; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Jubilee Day serves not only as a commemoration of the past
but also  as  a  reaffirmation  of  hope  for  the  future,  encouraging
continued  service,  moral  fortitude, and collective advancement in the
spirit of brotherly love, relief, and truth; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  Jubilee  Day, to be observed on June 7, 2026, by the Desert
of New York, Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine, and  to
recognize its profound historical, cultural, and spiritual significance;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body extend its sincere commendation
to  HPIP  Jimmie Chaple, Imperial Deputy of the Desert of New York, HPIC
Cassandra Duff, Imperial Deputy for the Desert  of  New  York,  and  the
Nobles  and  Daughters  of  the  Desert  of New York for their continued
dedication to faith, freedom, fraternity, and service to the  community;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to HPIP Jimmie Chaple, HPIC Cassandra Duff, and  the  Desert
of New York, A.E.A.O.N.M.S.
