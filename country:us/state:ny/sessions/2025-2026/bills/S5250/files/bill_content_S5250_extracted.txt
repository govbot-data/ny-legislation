CANZONERI-FITZPATRICK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Energy and Telecommunications AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to limits on surcharge increases; directing a study of the costs associated with recent environmental and energy related laws and the value of stranded utility assets resulting in discontinuance and/or abandonment of util- ity gas infrastructure; and imposing a moratorium on new energy taxes, fees and regulations The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1.
