K00394 Text:

 
Assembly Resolution No. 394
 
BY: M. of A. McMahon
 
        CONGRATULATING  the  University at Buffalo Women's
        Basketball Team upon the  occasion  of  winning  the
        Women's  National Invitation Tournament Championship
        on April 5, 2025
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in   college  sports;  this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
University  at  Buffalo  Women's  Basketball  Team  upon the occasion of
winning the Women's National Invitation Tournament (WNIT)  Championship;
to  the  praise  and  applause of their excited fans, the Bulls defeated
Troy in an 88-84  victory  in  front  of  a  record  crowd  to  win  the
Championship  on  Saturday,  April  5,  2025, at University at Buffalo's
Alumni Arena; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's  colors  of  white  and  blue,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported  the Bulls throughout their journey as they ended their season
with an impressive 30-7 overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS, A truly historic occasion, this  momentous  win  marks  the
first  ever  WNIT  Championship  win for the University at Buffalo; this
season's impressive record of 30 wins is also a program record; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport  such  as  basketball,  which  demands  athletic
prowess,  speed  and  agility,  Head  Coach  Becky Burke and her skilled
coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team,
teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove  invaluable
both on and off the court; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Head Coach Becky Burke and all the outstanding athletes on
the University at Buffalo Women's Basketball Team have clearly  utilized
dedication,   determination   and   teamwork   in  providing  a  lasting
contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition  of  their
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of the University at Buffalo Women's Basketball
Team:  Mallory  Daly,   Noelani   Cornfield,   Sitota   Gines,   Kirsten
Lewis-Williams,  Paula  Lopez,  Makenna  Shaffer, Ariana Seawell, Caylen
Spano, Terah Harness, Jordyn Beaty, Chellia  Watson;  Head  Coach  Becky
Burke;  Associate Head Coach Jacey Brooks; Assistant Coaches James Ewing
and Erin Sinnott; and dedicated support staff  members  Sarah  Tranelli,
 
Jake Shaver, Kayleah Mcguire, and Ella Unger on their outstanding season
and overall team record; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted  to  the  members  of  the  University  at  Buffalo  Women's
Basketball  Team,  Head  Coach  Becky  Burke, Associate Head Coach Jacey
Brooks; Assistant Coaches James Ewing and Erin Sinnott, and  the  team's
support staff.
