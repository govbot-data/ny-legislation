The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive Adirondack park plates. 1. Any person residing 4 in this state shall, upon request, be issued a distinctive plate in 5 support of the Adirondack park of a design approved by the commissioner 6 in consultation with the commissioner of environmental conservation. 7 2. An application for such plate shall be filed with the commissioner 8 in such form and detail as the commissioner shall prescribe. 9 3. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 10 in the same manner as other number plates upon payment of the regular 11 registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article 12 and an initial one-time service charge of twenty-five dollars shall be 13 charged for such plates. 14 § 2. 1.
