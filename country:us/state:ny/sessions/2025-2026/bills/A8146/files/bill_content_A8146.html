SPONSORShrestha

Authorizes the town of Hurley to alienate certain lands used as parklands for the purpose of constructing a new highway garage for the town and to dedicate certain other lands as parklands.

        AN  ACT authorizing the town of Hurley to alienate certain parklands for
          use as a highway garage for the town and to dedicate  other  lands  as
          replacement parklands
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of  Hurley,
     2  located  in  the  county  of Ulster, acting by and through its governing
     3  body and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such  body,  is
     4  hereby  authorized to discontinue as parklands and to alienate the lands
     5  more particularly described in  section  three  of  this  act,  for  the
     6  purpose of constructing a new highway garage for the town.
     7    §  2.  The  authorization provided in section one of this act shall be
     8  effective only upon the condition that the town of Hurley  dedicate  the
     9  lands  described in section four of this act as parklands, provided that
    10  the town of Hurley has never used such lands for public  open  space  or
    11  park purposes.
    12    §  3. The parklands authorized by section one of this act to be alien-
    13  ated are described as follows:
    14    All that certain piece or parcel of land, situate, lying and being  in

     1    §  5.  In  the event that the fair market value of the parklands to be
     2  dedicated by the town of Hurley described in section four  of  this  act
     3  are  not equal to or greater than the fair market value of the parklands
     4  to be discontinued in section three of this  act,  the  town  of  Hurley
     5  shall  dedicate  the difference of the fair market value of the lands to
     6  be alienated and the lands to be dedicated for the acquisition of  addi-
     7  tional parklands and/or capital improvements to existing park and recre-
     8  ational facilities.
     9    § 6. In the event that the town of Hurley received any funding support
    10  or assistance from the federal government for the purchase, maintenance,
    11  or  improvement of the parklands set forth in section three of this act,
    12  the discontinuance and alienation of such parklands  authorized  by  the
    13  provisions  of  this  act  shall  not occur until the town of Hurley has
    14  complied with any federal requirements pertaining to the  alienation  or
    15  conversion of parklands, including satisfying the secretary of the inte-
    16  rior  that  the  alienation  or  conversion complies with all conditions
    17  which the secretary of  the  interior  deems  necessary  to  assure  the
    18  substitution of other lands shall be equivalent in fair market value and
    19  usefulness to the lands being alienated or converted.
    20    § 7. This act shall take effect immediately.
