RAMOS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Investigations and Govern- ment Operations AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to defining employees of the state The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 292 of the executive law, as 2 amended by chapter 830 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 6. The term "employee" in this article does not include any individual 5 employed by [his or her] their parents, spouse, or child; provided, 6 however, that for the purposes of this article, the following shall be 7 deemed employees of the state: 8 (a) statewide elected officials, including the governor, lieutenant 9 governor, comptroller, and attorney general; 10 (b) state officers and employees, including: 11 (i) heads of state departments and their deputies and assistants who 12 serve at the discretion of a statewide elected official; and 13 (ii) officers and employees of statewide elected officials who serve 14 at the discretion of the statewide elected official; 15 (c) legislative employees, including officers and employees of the 16 legislature; and 17 (d) legislative members, including senators and members of the assem- 18 bly. 19 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
