CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Housing, Construction and Community Development AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to requiring contact be made with the complainant to verify restoration of service before concluding an investigation The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The administrative code of the city of New York is amended 2 by adding a new section 27-2033.2 to read as follows: 3 § 27-2033.2 Resolution of complaints. When investigating a complaint 4 regarding a violation of the requirements of this article, the depart- 5 ment shall not consider the matter resolved until the department has 6 made contact with the complainant to verify the applicable service has 7 been restored. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
