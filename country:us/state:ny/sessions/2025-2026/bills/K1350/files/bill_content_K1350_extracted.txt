Steck RECOGNIZING May 15, 2026, as Go Purple for Kids in Care Day, in conjunction with the observance of National Foster Care Awareness Month WHEREAS, Together For Youth has served children and families in our communities with compassion, commitment, and excellence for more than 140 years, providing a wide range of services, including foster care; and WHEREAS, Every child deserves to grow up in a safe, nurturing, and loving environment, yet too many children face circumstances that prevent this ideal from being reality; and WHEREAS, Foster parents play a vital role in ensuring the safety, stability, and well-being of children who have experienced abuse, neglect, or hardship, by caring for them with nurturance and support; and WHEREAS, May is nationally recognized as Foster Care Awareness Month, and Together For Youth is leading a statewide effort to raise awareness through its "Go Purple for Kids in Care" campaign, calling on individuals, organizations, and communities to show their support by wearing or displaying purple throughout the month of May and sharing the message for the need for foster parents; and RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize May 15, 2026, as Go Purple for Kids in Care Day, in conjunction with the observance of National Foster Care Awareness Month; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Together For Youth.
