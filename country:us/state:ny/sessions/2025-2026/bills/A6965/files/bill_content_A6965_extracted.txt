CHLUDZINSKI -- read once and referred to the Committee on Environmental Conservation AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to removing requirements that a parent, guardian or person and the minor remain at ground level at all times while deer hunting The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (b) of subdivision 1 of section 11-0935 of the 2 environmental conservation law, as added by section 1 of part ZZ of 3 chapter 55 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as follows: 4 (b) such parent, guardian or person has had at least three [year's] 5 years' experience in hunting deer; and 6 (i) such parent, guardian or person holds a hunting license; and 7 (ii) such parent, guardian or person maintains physical control over 8 the minor at all times while hunting. For the purposes of this paragraph 9 "physical control" shall mean that the physical proximity of such minor 10 to the parent, guardian or person is such that the parent, guardian or 11 person is reasonably able to issue verbal directions and instructions, 12 maintain constant visual contact, and otherwise provide guidance and 13 supervision to the minor; and 14 [(iii) such parent, guardian or person and the minor remain at ground 15 level at all times while hunting; and] 16 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that 17 the amendments made to subdivision 1 of section 11-0935 of the environ- 18 mental conservation law made by section one of this act shall not affect 19 the repeal of such section and shall be deemed repealed therewith.
