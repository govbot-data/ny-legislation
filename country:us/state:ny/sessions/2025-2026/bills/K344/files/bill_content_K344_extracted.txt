Cyphers, and Ralf Beck; Neil Hesson, Jr., was selected by the Board as the first Board President; and WHEREAS, Shenendehowa's athletic teams are called the Plainsmen, and their mascot is the stallion; they participate in 34 varsity sports through all seasons and currently boast alumni playing in both Major League Baseball and the National Basketball Association; and WHEREAS, With an enrollment of more than 9,500 students, Shenendehowa is the largest School District in Saratoga County and the largest student population in the Suburban Council, an interscholastic athletic consortium of 11 area schools within the region; and WHEREAS, Under the able leadership of its current School Superintendent, Dr. L. Oliver Robinson, the Shenendehowa Central School District's commitment to excellence for all students is unapologetic and unwavering; and WHEREAS, As an impactful way of honoring the legacy of the Shenendehowa Central School District and all that is to come, a grand celebration of its 75th Anniversary of educational excellence will take place on Saturday, May 17, 2025; and WHEREAS, The Shenendehowa Central School District has remained steadfast in its mission to work continuously and in partnership with the community to ensure that all students develop and demonstrate the knowledge, skills, abilities, and character needed to live useful, productive, and rewarding lives; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 75th Anniversary of the Shenendehowa Central School District, noting the propriety of its goals, the constancy of its commitment, and the significance of its accomplishments in preparing the youth of today and leaders of tomorrow; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to School Superintendent, Dr. L.
