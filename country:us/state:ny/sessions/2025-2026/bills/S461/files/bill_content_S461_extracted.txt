HOYLMAN-SIGAL -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Environmental Conser- vation AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to prohibiting industrial uses of products or substances containing perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a 2 new section 37-0122 to read as follows: 3 § 37-0122. Prohibition against industrial uses of perfluoroalkyl and 4 polyfluoroalkyl substances. 5 1. No person shall use in an industrial plant any product or substance 6 that contains perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl, including but not 7 limited to vats, conveyor belts, molds, ovens, pans, pots, chemicals 8 used as mold releasers, lubricants, seals, and coatings. 9 2. For purposes of this section, "industrial plant" shall have the 10 same meaning set forth in section eighteen hundred one of the public 11 authorities law. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect three years after it shall have become 13 a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
