Subdivision (a) of section 5201 of the civil practice law 2 and rules is amended to read as follows: 3 (a) Debt against which a money judgment may be enforced. A money judg- 4 ment may be enforced against any debt, which is past due or which is yet 5 to become due, certainly or upon demand of the judgment debtor, whether 6 it was incurred within or without the state, to or from a resident or 7 non-resident, unless it is exempt from application to the satisfaction 8 of the judgment.
