The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a 2 new section 19-0322 to read as follows: 3 § 19-0322. Air quality monitoring required for major mass transportation 4 projects. 5 1. For purposes of this section, the following terms shall have the 6 following meanings: 7 (a) "Major mass transportation project" means the construction or 8 reconstruction of any rapid transit, railroad, omnibus, marine transpor- 9 tation or other mass transportation facility with a cost of seventy-five 10 million or more dollars. 11 (b) "Excavation" means the movement or removal of earth, rock or other 12 materials in or on the ground by use of mechanized equipment or by 13 blasting, and includes, but is not limited to, auguring, backfilling, 14 boring, drilling, grading, plowing in, pulling in, trenching and tunnel- 15 ing; provided, however, that the movement of earth by tools manipulated 16 only by human or animal power and the tilling of soil for agricultural 17 purposes shall not be deemed excavation. 18 2.
