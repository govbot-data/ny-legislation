GOUNARDES, HOYLMAN-SIGAL, KRUEGER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT directing the department of transportation to conduct a study to evaluate the standard approach of setting or adjusting speed limits The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The department of transportation shall conduct a study to 2 evaluate the standard approach to setting or adjusting speed limits in 3 New York state, including: 4 (a) assessing all factors for setting or adjusting the speed limit; 5 (b) assessing the usage of 85th percentile rule in setting or adjust- 6 ing the speed limit; 7 (c) evaluating the application of 85th percentile rule in setting or 8 adjusting the speed limit and its correlation with traffic crashes; and 9 (d) evaluating alternative methods of 85th percentile rule to set or 10 adjust speed limits with better impact (correlation) on preventing traf- 11 fic crashes and provide recommendations on those alternative methods. 12 § 2. For purposes of this act, "85th percentile rule" shall mean the 13 speed at or below which 85 percent of all vehicles are traveling under 14 free-flowing conditions. 15 § 3. Within one year of the effective date of this act the department 16 of transportation shall publish a report with the findings and recommen- 17 dations of the study in a clear and conspicuous location on its website 18 and shall deliver a copy to the legislature. 19 § 4. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
