GLICK -- read once and referred to the Committee on Consumer Affairs and Protection AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to requiring hotels, motels and innkeepers to offer condoms for sale to registered guests The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section 2 204-b to read as follows: 3 § 204-b. Sale of condoms. Every keeper of a hotel or motel or inn 4 shall, in the registration office, sell or offer for sale to registered 5 guests of such hotel or motel or inn condoms or similar prophylactic 6 devices designed to prevent the spread of sexually transmitted diseases. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 8 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
