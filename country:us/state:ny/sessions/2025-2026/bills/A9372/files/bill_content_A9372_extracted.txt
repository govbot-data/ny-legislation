BERGER -- read once and referred to the Committee on Higher Education -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring the disclo- sure of certain members of student clubs and organizations to the college at which the club or organization is located The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 6438-e 2 to read as follows: 3 § 6438-e. Disclosure requirements for student clubs and organizations. 4 1. Any club or organization approved, recognized, or funded by a college 5 in the state shall disclose to the administration of the respective 6 institution the name and contact information of at least one: 7 (a) president, or individual acting in the capacity or function of a 8 president; 9 (b) treasurer, or individual responsible for managing, overseeing, or 10 controlling funds or financial accounts; and 11 (c) individual responsible for creating, managing, overseeing, or 12 administering communications on social media applications. 13 2. The individuals disclosed under subdivision one of this section may 14 be a student, faculty advisor, or other designated representative. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
