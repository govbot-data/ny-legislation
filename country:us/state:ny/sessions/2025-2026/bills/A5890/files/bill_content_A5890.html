BILL NOA05890

SPONSORChludzinski
 
COSPNSRBologna, Morinello, Brown E, Smullen, Beephan, Hawley, Manktelow, DeStefano, Giglio, Brown K, Lemondes, Molitor, DiPietro
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §162-a, St Fin L; amd §104-d, Gen Muni L; amd §2877-a, Pub Auth L; amd §12-a, Hway L
 
Permits contracts for the purchase of rock salt or sodium chloride mined or hand harvested in Canada to be awarded as if such rock salt or sodium chloride was mined or hand harvested in the United States.

        Introduced  by  M.  of  A.  CHLUDZINSKI -- read once and referred to the

        AN ACT to amend the state finance law, the general  municipal  law,  the
          public  authorities  law  and the highway law, in relation to awarding
          contracts for the purchase of rock salt or sodium  chloride  mined  or
          hand harvested in Canada
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 162-a of the state finance law, as amended by chap-
     2  ter 72 of the laws of 2023, is amended to read as follows:
     3    § 162-a. The New York state buy North American salt  act.  1.  Use  of
     4  North  American  materials.  (a)  Notwithstanding any other provision of
     5  law, when soliciting a contract for the purchase of rock salt or  sodium
     6  chloride,  any department or agency of the state may award such contract
     7  to the responsible and reliable bidder offering to supply rock  salt  or
     8  sodium  chloride that is mined or hand harvested in the United States or
     9  Canada, and which offer is within ten percent of  the  lowest  price  or
    10  best  value  offer,  rather  than to the lowest responsible and reliable
    11  bidder.
    12    (b) If it has been determined by a court or federal  or  state  agency
    13  that any person intentionally:
    14    (i)  affixed  a  label bearing a "Made in America" inscription, or any
    15  inscription with the same meaning, to any rock salt, or sodium  chloride
    16  product  used  in  projects  to  which  this section applies, sold in or
    17  shipped to the United States that was not mined or hand harvested in the
    18  United States[; or].
    19    (ii) affixed a label bearing a "Made in Canada"  inscription,  or  any
    20  inscription  with the same meaning, to any rock salt, or sodium chloride
    21  product used in projects to which  this  section  applies,  sold  in  or
    22  shipped  to  the  United  States that was not mined or hand harvested in
    23  Canada.
