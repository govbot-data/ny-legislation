                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          7889
 
                               2025-2026 Regular Sessions
 
                    IN SENATE
 
                                      May 13, 2025
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by  Sen. PALUMBO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when
          printed to be committed to the Committee on Local Government
 
        AN ACT relating to permitting the Setauket fire district and Stony Brook
          fire district to enter into contracts with the state university of New
          York at Stony Brook for property, services, and other consideration

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Notwithstanding any provision of the law to the contrary,
     2  the Setauket fire district and the Stony Brook fire  district  are  each
     3  hereby  authorized,  acting  by and through the governing bodies of such
     4  fire districts, to enter into agreements or  contracts  with  the  state
     5  university  of  New York at Stony Brook, pursuant to and consistent with
     6  sections 355 and 6301  of the education law, and in the manner  provided
     7  in  section  99-r  of  the  general  municipal  law, for the purposes of
     8  providing or receiving any property or service described in section 99-r
     9  of the general municipal law related to the provision of fire protection
    10  or emergency medical services within the  territory  protected  by  such
    11  fire  districts. Notwithstanding any provision of the law to the contra-
    12  ry, the state university of New York at Stony Brook, within  the  limits
    13  of  any  specific  statutory appropriation authorized and made available
    14  therefor by the legislature or by the governing body responsible for the
    15  operation of the state university  of  New  York  at  Stony  Brook,  may
    16  contract  with  the  Setauket  fire  district  or  the  Stony Brook fire
    17  district, in the manner provided in section 99-r of the general  munici-
    18  pal  law,  for  such  services  as  herein  provided and may provide, in
    19  agreements and/or contracts entered  into  pursuant  to   this act,  for
    20  the  reciprocal provision of services or other consideration of approxi-
    21  mately equivalent value, including, but not limited to,  routine  and/or
    22  emergency services, monies, equipment, buildings and facilities, materi-
    23  als  or    a  commitment  to  provide  future  routine  and/or emergency
    24  services, monies, equipment, buildings and facilities or materials.
    25    § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of January next  succeed-
    26  ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
