WEBB -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Higher Education -- recom- mitted to the Committee on Higher Education in accordance with Senate Rule 6, sec. 8 -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to enacting the college student suicide prevention act The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "college student suicide prevention act". 3 § 2. The education law is amended by adding a new section 6438-e to 4 read as follows: 5 § 6438-e. College student suicide prevention act. The governing board 6 or body of every higher education institution shall, before the first 7 day of August, two thousand twenty-seven, adopt policies, procedures, 8 and guidelines on student suicide prevention, intervention, and postven- 9 tion. Such policies, procedures, and guidelines shall be developed in 10 consultation with collegiate and community stakeholders, campus-employed 11 mental health professionals, and suicide prevention experts. 12 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
