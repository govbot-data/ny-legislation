and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 24th Anniversary of the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program, to be celebrated at its Annual Awards Banquet on Saturday, February 8, 2025; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program has remained steadfast in its mission to combat hunger, provide essential resources, and uplift underserved communities in New York City; and WHEREAS, For over two decades, this esteemed organization has served as a beacon of hope and sustenance, distributing thousands of meals annually to individuals and families in need, with particular focus on supporting vulnerable populations, including seniors, children, and those facing economic hardship; and WHEREAS, The success of the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program is a direct result of the unwavering dedication of its leadership, volunteers, and supporters, who have tirelessly worked to meet the ever-growing demand for food security and community support; and WHEREAS, The 24th Anniversary Awards Banquet serves as a momentous occasion to honor the program's remarkable achievements and recognize the exceptional contributions of individuals and organizations who have partnered in its noble mission; and WHEREAS, The International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program has continually demonstrated its commitment to creating a more compassionate and equitable society, addressing not only the physical needs of the community but also fostering a spirit of unity, kindness, and service; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is moved to acknowledge the profound impact of the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program and to applaud its enduring efforts to improve the lives of so many; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 24th Anniversary of the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program on February 8, 2025, and to honor its extraordinary legacy of service to the residents of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program.
