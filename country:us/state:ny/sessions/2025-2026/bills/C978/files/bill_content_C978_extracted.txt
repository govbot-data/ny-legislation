Benedetto CONCURRENT RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE AND ASSEMBLY providing for the election of two Regents of the University of the State of New York WHEREAS, The term of one Regent of the University of the State of New York elected At Large and the term of the Regent of the University of the State of New York from the fifth Judicial District expire on the thirty-first day of March in the year two thousand twenty-six; and RESOLVED, (if the concur), That pursuant to the provisions of section 202 of the education law, the vacancies created by the expiration of these terms and for other cause are hereby filled by concurrent resolution; and RESOLVED, (if the concur), That Patrick A. Mannion of the county of Onondaga be re-elected Regent of the University of the State of New York from the fifth Judicial District for a term of five years commencing on the first day of April in the year two thousand twenty-six; and RESOLVED, (if the concur), That Dr. Janice W. Shorenstein of the county of New York be elected At Large a Regent of the University of the State of New York for a term of five years commencing on the first day of April in the year two thousand twenty-six.
