ROSENTHAL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to distinctive license plates for society for the prevention of cruelty to animals officers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive plates for society for the prevention of cruelty 4 to animals officers. 1. Society for the prevention of cruelty to animals 5 officers in this state or the spouse of any such member shall, upon 6 request, be issued a license plate bearing the letters "S.P.C.A". If a 7 distinctive plate is issued to any such member pursuant to this section, 8 such a distinctive plate shall not be issued to the spouse of such 9 member. Application for such license plate shall be filed with the 10 commissioner in such form and detail as the commissioner shall 11 prescribe, and shall be verified annually by the applicant. 12 2. The distinctive plate authorized in subdivision one of this section 13 shall be issued upon proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that the 14 applicant or the spouse of the applicant is presently a society for the 15 prevention of cruelty to animals officers. 16 3. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 17 in the same manner as other number plates upon payment of the regular 18 registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article 19 and an initial one time service charge of fifteen dollars. 20 § 2. 1.
