Amd §176, Town L
 
Provides that the election of chief and assistant chief by a fire department may be on the same day as the day of election of the fire department's election of administration officers.

        AN ACT to amend the town law, in relation to the election of fire chiefs
          and assistant fire chiefs

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 11-a of section 176 of the town law, as amended
     2  by chapter 351 of the laws of 2000, is amended to read as follows:
     3    11-a. The members of the fire department of the  fire  district  shall
     4  meet  at  a time and place designated by the board of fire commissioners
     5  on the Thursday following the first Tuesday in April of  each  year  and
     6  nominate  persons  for the offices of chief and such assistant chiefs as
     7  may be provided for in the rules and regulations adopted by the board of
     8  fire commissioners; provided, however, that the board  of  fire  commis-
     9  sioners  may  by  resolution, adopt a policy allowing fire districts the
    10  option to set the day of election of chief and assistant chiefs  on  the
    11  same  day  as the fire department's election of administration officers.
    12  [If the Thursday following the first Tuesday in April falls upon  a  day
    13  of  special  religious  observance,  the board of fire commissioners may
    14  designate any other weekday in April after such Thursday as the day upon
    15  which to hold such meeting.] Each nominee shall be a member of the  fire
    16  department.  In  addition, the board of fire commissioners may by resol-
    17  ution require that any or all of such fire department officers shall  be
    18  residents of the fire district. All nominations shall be made by ballot.
    19  The person acting as secretary of such meeting shall promptly notify the
    20  fire  district  secretary  in  writing of such nominations. The board of
    21  fire commissioners at its meeting next succeeding  the  making  of  such
    22  nominations  shall  consider  the same and shall appoint such persons to
    23  the offices to which they have been  respectively  nominated  or,  if  a
    24  nomination  is  not  approved the board of fire commissioners shall call
