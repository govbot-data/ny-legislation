Section 3 of the cannabis law is amended by adding two new 2 subdivisions 25-a and 25-b to read as follows: 3 25-a. "Exempt fire agency" means a fire department of a city, munici- 4 pality, village, town or other governmental subdivision that provides 5 fire and emergency services or other safety sensitive tasks to provide 6 public safety to a community. 7 25-b. "Exempt employee" means a volunteer firefighter or an emergency 8 medical services employee of an exempt fire agency whose work responsi- 9 bilities are tasks that impact the safety of a mitigation effort or the 10 safety of other employees. Activities may include, but shall not be 11 limited to, driving and operating fire apparatus, operating in imme- 12 diately dangerous to life atmospheres, establishing and operating hose 13 lines, performing all manner of rescue activities, and delivering emer- 14 gency medical care to the sick or injured. 15 § 2. Section 127 of the cannabis law is amended by adding a new subdi- 16 vision 7 to read as follows: 17 7. (a) For the purpose of assuring the safety of other volunteer fire- 18 fighters and emergency medical services employees, the public and the 19 protection of their greater community, this section shall not apply to 20 an exempt employee of an exempted fire agency.
