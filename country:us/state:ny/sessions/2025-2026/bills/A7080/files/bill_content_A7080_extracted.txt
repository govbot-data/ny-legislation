PRETLOW -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the public officers law, in relation to the disposal of certain information or data maintained by agencies of government The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 86 of the public officers law is amended by adding 2 three new subdivisions 12, 13 and 14 to read as follows: 3 12. "Data base" means a set of data, consisting of more than one data 4 file, that is collected and stored for a given purpose. 5 13. "Data file" means an electronic record consisting of related 6 numeric, textual or graphic information that is organized in a strictly 7 prescribed form and format. 8 14. "Electronic record" means any record that is stored, processed and 9 used by means of a computer. 10 § 2. Subparagraph iii of paragraph (b) of subdivision 1 of section 87 11 of the public officers law is amended by adding a new clause 3 to read 12 as follows: 13 (3) With respect to an electronic record, the actual cost of reprod- 14 uction shall include the cost of the staff time and computer time 15 required for reproduction and the cost of the storage device or medium 16 by which such record is transmitted to the person who has requested it, 17 where such storage device or medium is provided by the agency, provided 18 however that such actual cost of reproduction with respect to an elec- 19 tronic record shall not be greater than the actual cost would be if the 20 records were transmitted by the agency as photocopies, plus the cost of 21 such storage device or medium if provided by the agency. 22 § 3. Subdivision 1 of section 87 of the public officers law is amended 23 by adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows: 24 (d) Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph (b) of this subdivi- 25 sion, any agency may, pursuant to rule duly promulgated by such agency EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
