JONES -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to creating an exemption to the snowmobile registration requirements by creating a seasonal trail pass pilot program for Canadian citizens; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 5 of section 2222 of the vehicle and traffic 2 law, as added by chapter 839 of the laws of 1973, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 5. Application. The owner of each snowmobile requiring registration 5 under this section shall present an application for registration to the 6 commissioner, on a blank to be prepared and furnished by the commission- 7 er for that purpose. Such application shall contain or be accompanied by 8 such evidence of the ownership of the snowmobile described in the appli- 9 cation as may be required by the commissioner. Where the snowmobile 10 owner is a citizen of Canada, no registration shall be required, but a 11 trail pass as described in subdivision five-a of this section shall be 12 required. 13 § 2. Section 2222 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding 14 a new subdivision 5-a to read as follows: 15 5-a. Canadian citizen trail pass. All applicable fees shall be paid to 16 receive a trail pass. Trail pass fees shall be equal to those described 17 in subdivisions four and four-a of this section, in addition to any 18 other applicable fees prescribed by law. Passes shall not authorize the 19 use of a snowmobile other than for pleasure, and shall require compli- 20 ance with all laws and regulations applicable to the use of snowmobiles 21 in New York state. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
