AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring school
districts to provide information regarding water safety and swimming
certification to certain parents or students upon enrollment
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 926 to
2 read as follows:
3 § 926. Water safety and swimming certification. 1. Beginning with the
4 two thousand twenty-six--two thousand twenty-seven school year, each
5 school district shall provide, to a parent or guardian who initially
6 enrolls their child in the school, information on the important role
7 water safety education courses and swimming lessons play in saving
8 lives. The information must be provided electronically or in hard copy
9 and must include local options for age-appropriate water safety courses
10 and swimming lessons that result in a certificate indicating successful
11 completion, including courses and lessons offered for free or at a
12 reduced price. If the student is eighteen years of age or older, or is
13 under the age of twenty-one and is enrolling in adult education classes,
14 the information must be provided to the student.
15 2. For the purposes of this subdivision, the term "water safety" shall
16 mean age-appropriate education intended to promote safety in, on, and
17 around bodies of water and reduce the risk of injury, including fatal or
18 non-fatal drowning.
19 § 2. This act shall take effect July 1, 2026.
