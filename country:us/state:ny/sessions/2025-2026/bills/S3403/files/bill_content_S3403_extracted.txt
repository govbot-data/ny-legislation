COMRIE, HARCKHAM, MAYER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Trans- portation AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to liability of the state for damages caused by reported defects and potholes; and to amend the transportation law, in relation to establishing an interactive website for reporting defects and potholes on the state highway system and thruway The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 58 of the highway law, as amended by chapter 1110 2 of the laws of 1971, is amended to read as follows: 3 § 58. Liability of state for damages. The state shall not be liable 4 for damages suffered by any person from defects in state highways 5 between the sixteenth day of November and the last day of April except 6 when the defect found to cause the damages was reported by telephone or 7 by an online report submitted to the telephone number or website desig- 8 nated by the department to receive such reports and fourteen days have 9 elapsed from the date the initial report was submitted and the defect 10 was not repaired and, except between the first day of May and the 11 fifteenth day of November on such highways as are maintained by the 12 state under such system as the commissioner of transportation may adopt 13 pursuant to section twelve, but the liability for such damages shall 14 otherwise remain as now provided by law, notwithstanding the 15 construction or improvement and maintenance of such highways by the 16 state under this chapter; but nothing herein contained shall be 17 construed to impose on the state any liability for defects in bridges 18 over which the state has no control.
