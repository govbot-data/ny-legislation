The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive "Honor Flight" license plates. 1. Any person 4 residing in this state shall, upon request, be issued a distinctive 5 "Honor Flight" license plate. Application for such license plate shall 6 be filed with the commissioner in such form and detail as the commis- 7 sioner shall prescribe. 8 2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 9 in the same manner as other number plates upon the payment of the regu- 10 lar registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this 11 article; provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of 12 twenty-five dollars shall be charged for such plate. Such annual service 13 charge shall be deposited pursuant to the provisions of section four 14 hundred four-oo of this article to the credit of the department of motor 15 vehicles distinctive plate development fund established by section nine- 16 ty-five-g of the state finance law, and shall be used for the 17 production, design, advertising and marketing of distinctive license 18 plates pursuant to such section ninety-five-g; provided, however, that 19 additional annual service charges collected in excess of six thousand 20 dollars shall be deposited to the credit of the Honor Flight fund estab- 21 lished pursuant to section ninety-seven-ss of the state finance law and 22 shall be used to support Honor Flight. 23 § 2.
