        Introduced  by M. of A. CASHMAN -- read once and referred to the Commit-

          reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee
 
        AN  ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to authorizing
          a distinctive "Honor Flight" license plate; and  to  amend  the  state
          finance law, in relation to establishing the Honor Flight fund

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is  amended  by  adding  a  new
     2  section 404-ff to read as follows:
     3    §  404-ff.  Distinctive  "Honor Flight" license plates. 1.  Any person
     4  residing in this state shall, upon  request,  be  issued  a  distinctive
     5  "Honor  Flight"  license plate. Application for such license plate shall
     6  be filed with the commissioner in such form and detail  as  the  commis-
     7  sioner shall prescribe.
     8    2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued
     9  in  the same manner as other number plates upon the payment of the regu-
    10  lar registration fee prescribed by section  four  hundred  one  of  this
    11  article;  provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of
    12  twenty-five dollars shall be charged for such plate. Such annual service
    13  charge shall be deposited pursuant to the  provisions  of  section  four
    14  hundred four-oo of this article to the credit of the department of motor
    15  vehicles distinctive plate development fund established by section nine-
    16  ty-five-g  of  the  state  finance  law,  and  shall  be  used  for  the
    17  production, design, advertising and  marketing  of  distinctive  license
    18  plates  pursuant  to such section ninety-five-g; provided, however, that
    19  additional annual service charges collected in excess  of  six  thousand
    20  dollars shall be deposited to the credit of the Honor Flight fund estab-
    21  lished  pursuant to section ninety-seven-ss of the state finance law and
    22  shall be used to support Honor Flight.
    23    § 2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 97-ss to
    24  read as follows:

     1    § 97-ss. Honor Flight fund. 1. There  is  hereby  established  in  the
     2  joint  custody of the commissioner of taxation and finance and the comp-
     3  troller, a special fund to be known as the "Honor Flight fund".
     4    2.  Such  fund  shall consist of all revenues received pursuant to the
     5  provisions of section four hundred four-ff of the  vehicle  and  traffic
     6  law  and all other monies appropriated, credited, or transferred thereto
     7  from any other fund or source pursuant to law. Nothing contained in this
     8  section shall prevent the state from receiving grants, gifts or bequests
     9  for the purposes of the fund as defined in this section  and  depositing
    10  them into the fund according to law.
    11    3. Monies of the fund shall be expended to support Honor Flight.
    12    4.  Monies  shall be payable from the fund on the audit and warrant of
    13  the comptroller on vouchers approved and certified by the state director
    14  of veterans' services.
    15    § 3. 1. A distinctive plate established pursuant to section 404-ff  of
    16  the  vehicle and traffic law shall only be designed, produced and issued
    17  upon the delivery to the department of motor vehicles of a  surety  bond
    18  in  the  amount  of  six  thousand dollars, which shall be executed by a
    19  surety company authorized by the department  of  financial  services  to
    20  transact  business in this state. Provided, however, that if the commis-
    21  sioner of motor vehicles shall have  received  prior  to  plate  design,
    22  production and issuance at least two hundred orders for such distinctive
    23  plate  together  with the additional annual service charge applicable to
    24  each such order, which shall be  non-refundable,  no  such  surety  bond
    25  shall  be  required.    All  service  charges collected pursuant to this
    26  section shall be deposited pursuant to the provisions of section  404-oo
    27  of  the vehicle and traffic law to the credit of the department of motor
    28  vehicles distinctive plate development fund established by section  95-g
    29  of  the  state finance law and shall be used for the design, production,
    30  advertising and distribution of distinctive license plates in accordance
    31  with such section 95-g.
    32    2. If, upon the expiration of two years following the date upon  which
    33  distinctive  plates  in  the  series  are  first  available for sale two
    34  hundred or more sets of such plates are sold, a bond delivered  pursuant
    35  to this section shall be discontinued. If fewer than two hundred sets of
    36  such  plates  are  sold  by  such time, the department of motor vehicles
    37  shall be entitled to recover against the bond in an amount proportionate
    38  to such shortfall.
    39    § 4. This act shall take effect immediately; provided,  however,  that
    40  sections  one  and  two of this act shall take effect on the one hundred
    41  eightieth day after it shall have become a law.  Effective  immediately,
    42  the  addition,  amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation neces-
    43  sary for the implementation of  this  act  on  its  effective  date  are
    44  authorized to be made and completed on or before such effective date.
