This act shall be known and may be cited as the "court 2 order protection act". 3 § 2. Rule 5016 of the civil practice law and rules, subdivision (d) as 4 amended by chapter 93 of the laws of 1970, is amended to read as 5 follows: 6 Rule 5016. Entry of judgment. Judgment upon the general verdict of a 10 jury after a trial by jury as of right shall be entered by the clerk 11 unless the court otherwise directs; if there is a special verdict, the 12 court shall direct entry of an appropriate judgment. 13 (c) Judgment upon decision.
