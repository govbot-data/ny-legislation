(a) Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of 2 Union Vale, in the county of Dutchess, acting by and through its govern- 3 ing body, and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such body, 4 is hereby authorized to discontinue as parklands and to lease at fair 5 market value to Homeland Towers, LLC for a term not to exceed thirty 6 years the lands more particularly described in section two of this act 7 for the placement and operation of a wireless communication tower.
