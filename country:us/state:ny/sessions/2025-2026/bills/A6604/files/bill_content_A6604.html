        Introduced by M. of A. K. BROWN -- read once and referred to the Commit-
          tee on Local Governments
 
        AN ACT in relation to authorizing the town of Huntington to alienate and
          discontinue  the  use  of  certain parklands to the Huntington African
          American Museum
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of Hunting-
     2  ton,  county of Suffolk, is hereby authorized, acting by and through its
     3  governing body, and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such
     4  governing body, to discontinue the use  as  parkland  and  alienate  the
     5  lands  described in section three of this act, to the Huntington African
     6  American Museum for the  purpose  of  displaying  exhibits  and  holding
     7  events.
     8    § 2. The authorization contained in section one of this act shall only
     9  be  effective  on  the condition that the town of Huntington dedicate an
    10  amount equal to or greater than the fair market value of  the  parklands
    11  being alienated pursuant to section one of this act towards the acquisi-
    12  tion  of  new parklands and/or capital improvements to existing park and
    13  recreational facilities.
    14    § 3. The parkland authorized by section one of this act to be  discon-
    15  tinued as parkland is described as follows:
    16  ALL that certain plot, piece or parcel of land, situate, lying and being
    17  in  Halesite,  Town  of  Huntington,  County of Suffolk and State of New
    18  York, as shown on a map prepared  by  H2M  architects  +  engineers,  as
    19  project  number  HAAM-2401,  last  dated  August  3,  2024,  bounded and
    20  described as follows:
    21  Beginning at the northeasterly corner of  said  parcel  also  being  the
    22  intersection  of  the  southerly side of Mill Dam Road with the westerly
    23  side of New York Avenue (N.Y.S. Route 110).
