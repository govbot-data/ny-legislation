AN ACT to amend the workers' compensation law, in relation to requiring
the workers' compensation board to submit a supplemental report every
year thereafter
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The workers' compensation law is amended by adding a new
2 section 153-a to read as follows:
3 § 153-a. Supplemental report. The board shall on or before the first
4 day of February, two thousand twenty-seven, and every year thereafter,
5 make in writing to the governor, the speaker of the assembly and the
6 temporary president of the senate a supplemental report on the status of
7 the workers' compensation board and methods to modernize such board,
8 which shall be delivered in addition to the annual report required under
9 section one hundred fifty-three of this article. Such supplemental
10 report shall include, but not be limited to, data and information sets
11 and recommendations concerning:
12 1. the status of membership of the workers' compensation board;
13 2. recommendations to modernize the workers' compensation board and
14 the status of any ongoing modernization efforts, if applicable;
15 3. the total number of claimants who have filed a claim before the
16 workers' compensation board;
17 4. the total number of claims in which an award or decision was made,
18 and the total number of claims in which a court order was made;
19 5. the average length of time between the time a claimant files a
20 claim to the workers' compensation board and the time an award or deci-
21 sion, or court order, is made in regards to such claim;
