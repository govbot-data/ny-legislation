AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to the creation of an
online standardized medical physical examination form
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The public health law is amended by adding a new section 25
2 to read as follows:
3 § 25. Standardized medical physical examination form. The commissioner
4 shall establish an online standardized medical physical examination
5 form, which may be utilized by, but not limited to, schools, colleges,
6 universities, camps for children and doctors' offices.
7 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
8 have become a law.
