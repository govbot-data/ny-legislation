Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J00481 Summary:BILL NOJ00481  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORPARKER COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ00481 Text: Senate Resolution No. 481 BY: Senator PARKER HONORING the Long Island Consistory No. 61 upon the occasion of its Ceremony of Remembrance and Renewal on March 27, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and honor organizations and traditions that preserve history, uphold principles of unity and service, and inspire the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment; and WHEREAS, The Long Island Consistory No. 61 will hold its esteemed Ceremony of Remembrance and Renewal on March 27, 2025, a solemn and reflective observance that reaffirms the values of integrity, fraternity, and commitment to service that are the foundation of Prince Hall Freemasonry; and WHEREAS, This sacred ceremony serves as an opportunity to honor those who have come before, reflecting on their wisdom, sacrifices, and unwavering dedication to the principles of truth, justice, and brotherhood; and WHEREAS, The Ceremony of Remembrance and Renewal not only pays tribute to the past but also strengthens the resolve of its members to continue their work in the community, embodying the highest ideals of faith, perseverance, and enlightenment; and WHEREAS, The Long Island Consistory No. 61 has long been a pillar of leadership and service, dedicated to uplifting individuals and communities through education, charitable works, and mentorship, ensuring that future generations carry forward its noble mission; and WHEREAS, The observance of this ceremony reinforces the bonds of unity among brethren, reminding all in attendance of the importance of moral character, self-improvement, and a steadfast commitment to the greater good; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body recognize the Long Island Consistory No. 61 for its enduring contributions to society and commend its members for their dedication to preserving the sacred traditions of Prince Hall Freemasonry; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor the Long Island Consistory No. 61 upon the occasion of hosting its Ceremony of Remembrance and Renewal on March 27, 2025, recognizing its invaluable role in fostering unity, reflection, and continued service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the leadership of the Long Island Consistory No. 61.
