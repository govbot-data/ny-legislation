and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Abu Bekr Court No. 74, Imperial Court Daughters of Isis, and Illustrious Commandress Allison Pope-Hillman upon the occasion of its Annual Thanksgiving Service on Sunday, May 17, 2026; and WHEREAS, The Imperial Court Daughters of Isis, the esteemed women's auxiliary of the Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine, has long stood as a pillar of charity, sisterhood, and devotion, uniting its members in a shared mission of service and community uplift; and WHEREAS, Abu Bekr Court No. 74, based in Brooklyn, New York, has faithfully carried forward that mission, serving as a beacon of benevolence and purpose and enriching the lives of all those touched by its work; and WHEREAS, The Annual Thanksgiving Service is a cherished tradition in which the members of Abu Bekr Court gather to give thanks, renew their bonds of fellowship, and rededicate themselves to the principles of service and charity that define their organization; and WHEREAS, Illustrious Commandress Allison Pope-Hillman has led Abu Bekr Court No. 74 with grace, vision, and steadfast dedication, inspiring those around her to serve their community with purpose and pride; and WHEREAS, The Annual Thanksgiving Service, to be held on May 17, 2026, at the Brooklyn Masonic Temple, stands as a testament to the enduring unity, faith, and commitment of Abu Bekr Court No. 74 and all its members; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Abu Bekr Court No. 74, Imperial Court Daughters of Isis, upon the occasion of its Annual Thanksgiving Service, and to commend Illustrious Commandress Allison Pope-Hillman for her distinguished leadership and her tireless dedication to service, charity, and community; and be it further RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body extends its warmest congratulations and best wishes to Illustrious Commandress Pope-Hillman, to the officers and members of Abu Bekr Court No. 74, and to all those gathered in fellowship on this meaningful occasion.
