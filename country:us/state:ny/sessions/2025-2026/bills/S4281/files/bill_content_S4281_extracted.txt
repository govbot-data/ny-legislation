The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 2808 to read as follows: 3 § 2808. Database and reporting requirements for contracts with minori- 4 ty and women-owned businesses. Every state authority continued or 5 created by this chapter or any other chapter of the laws of the state of 6 New York shall create and maintain a database to track the issuance of 7 contracts to minority and women-owned enterprises.
