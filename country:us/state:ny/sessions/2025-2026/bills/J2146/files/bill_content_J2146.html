J02146 Text:

 
Senate Resolution No. 2146
 
BY: Senator RHOADS
 
        HONORING  Michael  Antonellis upon the occasion of
        his designation as recipient of the New  York  State
        Citizenship Award
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor the young
adults   of   today,  and  leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and
achievements best exemplify the ideals  and  values  cherished  by  this
great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Michael Antonellis upon the occasion of his designation as recipient  of
the  New York State Citizenship Award, to be celebrated at a ceremony on
Tuesday, June 2, 2026, at Farmingdale High School; and
 
  WHEREAS, The New York  State  Citizenship  Award  is  bestowed  upon
students  who  embody  the  highest  principles of civic responsibility,
integrity, and dedication to the common good, serving as role models who
strengthen the fabric of their communities  through  their  actions  and
example; and
 
  WHEREAS,  True citizenship is demonstrated not only through academic
success,  but  through  a  steadfast  commitment  to  service,   ethical
leadership, and an unwavering sense of responsibility toward others; and
 
  WHEREAS, Michael Antonellis has exemplified these ideals through his
consistent  demonstration  of  respect,  accountability, and compassion,
reflecting the core values of engaged and responsible citizenship; and
 
  WHEREAS, Michael Antonellis is an intelligent and motivated scholar,
student athlete, musician, and leader in his senior year at  Farmingdale
High School in Farmingdale, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  At  Farmingdale High School, Michael Antonellis has pushed
himself academically with his coursework, taking both Advanced Placement
courses and college-level courses, including Farmingdale's hardest  math
course,  AP  Calculus  BC; despite his heavy courseload, this remarkable
scholar has maintained a weighted GPA of 102.51; and
 
  WHEREAS, Michael Antonellis' impressive academic record  has  earned
him  membership  in  several  honor  societies, including National Honor
Society, Science National Honor Society, Tri-M Honor Society, and French
Honor Society, where he serves as treasurer; and
 
  WHEREAS, Outside of the classroom, Michael Antonellis  has  been  an
athlete on Farmingdale High School's cross country and winter and spring
track  teams  since his freshman year; his love for running inspired him
to complete his first half marathon in 2025; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to being a student athlete, Michael  Antonellis
plays  the  trumpet  and  is a proud member of Farmingdale High School's
marching band, jazz band, wind ensemble,  and  pep  band;  as  a  strong
 
musician  that  his peers look up to, Michael Antonellis was selected to
serve as section leader for the trumpets in his marching band; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  proven  leader and role model, this extraordinary young
man is being recognized not only for his  academic  and  extracurricular
achievement,  but also for the profound impact he has had on Farmingdale
High School and his local community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Michael Antonellis has a passion for  giving  back  to  his
community,  and  he  volunteers through programs with the National Honor
Society, the Varsity Leaders' Club, the Farmingdale Public Library,  and
the New York State Retriever Rescue, where he fosters puppies for future
adoption; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Volunteering  is  a  central  part  of Michael Antonellis'
story, and he has recorded over 100 hours of community service; and
 
  WHEREAS, To  gain  real-world  experience,  Michael  Antonellis  has
worked at Bounce Family Entertainment Center in Syosset, New York, since
2025,  where  he ensures safety, manages events, and provides leadership
to party groups; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  experience  that  Michael  Antonellis  has   gathered
throughout  his  time  at Farmingdale High School has shaped him to be a
well-rounded and accomplished individual; his commitments and desire  to
involve  himself  throughout his community has rightfully earned him the
New York State Citizenship Award; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Michael Antonellis looks forward to his next chapter, he
hopes to study accounting; no matter where his path takes  him,  Michael
Antonellis   is  sure  to  continue  fostering  his  leadership  skills,
advancing his achievements, and making a difference  in  his  community;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  His  example  affirms  that  responsible  citizenship is a
lifelong endeavor, one that extends beyond the classroom and into  every
aspect of personal and professional life; and
 
  WHEREAS,    Poised    eagerly   with   enthusiasm   and   purposeful
determination, this scholar now stands honorably, with just pride in his
accomplishments  and  in  eager  anticipation  of   future   challenges,
understanding  and  appreciating the caring counsel and support provided
by his teachers, mentors, and parents; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Michael  Antonellis  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation as
recipient of the New York State Citizenship Award; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Michael Antonellis.
