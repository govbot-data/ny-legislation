Buttenschon COMMEMORATING the 25th Anniversary of Quanterion Solutions Incorporated of Utica, New York LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION commemorating the 25th Anniversary of Quanterion Solutions Incorporated of Utica, New York WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize those small businesses within the State of New York which contribute to the economic health and vitality of the communities they serve; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 25th Anniversary of Quanterion Solutions Incorporated of Utica, New York; and WHEREAS, In 2000, Quanterion Solutions Incorporated was formed in West Utica, New York with a mission to provide high quality quantitative engineering services to be used as criterion for critical decision making, thus the name Quanterion was adopted; and WHEREAS, In 2005, Quanterion relocated to the newly completed SUNY campus; since May of 2017, the company headquarters has been in its own building at 266 Genesee Street, Utica, across the street from the historic Stanley Theatre; and WHEREAS, Over the past two and a half decades, Quanterion has expanded its technical expertise to include knowledge management, software development and engineering, and information technology services while still embodying reliability, maintainability and quality; and WHEREAS, Quanterion is very successful in its long and short term collaborations with large and small industrial and commercial entities as well as with educational institutions; and WHEREAS, The range and depth of Quanterion's technical expertise has also been clearly demonstrated through its breadth of government technical work such as its long time leadership of the Department of Defense Information analysis Centers, each provide a centralized knowledge center for the user community to obtain scientific and technical information related to specific technologies; and WHEREAS, Quanterion's staff, consisting of primarily IT/Software developers, engineers, and scientists, supports numerous advanced technology programs and technical information centers for the U.S. government and commercial customers across the country, ensuring that they deliver solutions to solve even the most challenging technical problems; and WHEREAS, Since starting as a three-person company in an old mill in West Utica, Quanterion has experienced exponential growth boasting clients not only in New York State but also in New Mexico, Washington, D.C., North Carolina, and Colorado; and WHEREAS, As Quanterion reflects on 25 years of trailblazing data analysis solutions to organizations both locally and around the world, they are grateful to their customers, partners, supporters, and employees for supporting the work they've done in developing, integrating, and validating data-driven technological solutions across each of their technical focus areas; now, therefore be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberation to commemorate the 25th Anniversary of Quanterion Solutions Incorporated, recognizing its exceptional analytical services and its unwavering commitment to high quality and hard work; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Quanterion Solutions Incorporated.
