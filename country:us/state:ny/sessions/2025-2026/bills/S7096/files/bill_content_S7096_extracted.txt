LANZA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Consumer Protection AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to warning labels on certain sports and muscle creams The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 "Arielle's law". 3 § 2. The general business law is amended by adding a new section 391-x 4 to read as follows: 5 § 391-x. Sale or promotional distribution of sports or muscle cream 6 containing methyl salicylate. 1. No person, firm, corporation or any 7 other entity shall sell or offer to sell, or give away as part of a 8 promotional distribution, any form of sports or muscle cream containing 9 methyl salicylate unless a warning label is displayed thereon stating 10 that the product contains methyl salicylate, should be used only as 11 directed, and that a physician should be consulted prior to such use. 12 2. Violation of the provisions of subdivision one of this section is 13 punishable, as a civil penalty, by a fine not to exceed five hundred 14 dollars for each violation thereof. 15 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
