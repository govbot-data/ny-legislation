and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Go To Girls, Inc. upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Organization of the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated at its 52nd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 20, 2025, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, Go To Girls (GTG) is a nonprofit organization made up of caring, concerned, hardworking women, who believe no family should ever feel alone, especially when in need; and WHEREAS, During the Global Pandemic, in a time of uncertainty and stress, ten mothers with unique personalities, strengths, careers and stressors discovered the true meaning of friendship and established this vital organization to bring brighter days to the school-aged children of the Clarence Central School District; and WHEREAS, In partnership with the district Family Support Center, school counselors and other community outreach programs, Go To Girls provides support to identified children and families within the community with careful consideration to doing so in a safe, confidential, equitable, compassionate, and judgment-free way; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, Go To Girls has provided 177 Bundles of Love, 120 Birthday Bags, and two Kindness Awards: Bundles of Love ensure that Clarence School children have what they need to keep them warm and safe from the elements during a cold Buffalo winter; Birthday Bags provide a family with everything to celebrate their child's big day; and Kindness Awards have awarded $2,500 to two graduating Clarence seniors who have displayed acts of kindness; and WHEREAS, The success of Go To Girls is in direct correlation to the efforts of its dedicated founders and support staff, whose involvement is, and always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those who enhance the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown a long and sustained commitment to excellence certainly have earned the recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Go To Girls, Inc. upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Organization of the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Go To Girls, Inc.
