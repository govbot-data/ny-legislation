Senate Resolution No. 375
 
BY: Senator GALLIVAN
 
        HONORING  Scott  Medole  upon  the occasion of his
        designation as recipient of  the  Volunteer  of  the
        Year  Award  by  the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on
        March 21, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
commend  those distinguished individuals and businesses which contribute
substantially to the economic vitality  of  their  communities,  thereby
enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Scott Medole upon the occasion of his designation as  recipient  of  the
Volunteer  of  the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be
celebrated at its 52nd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March  20,  2024;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Service  to community and country is the foundation of our
society, and the contributions  and  efforts  of  those  individuals  in
service to them are a vital part of, and invaluable asset to, the fabric
of our State and Nation; Scott Medole is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Scott  Medole has been a citizen of Clarence, New York for
nearly 50 years; he graduated from Clarence High  School  in  1980,  and
later,  SUNY  Fredonia  in 1984, where he received a Bachelor of Science
degree in Business Administration before  serving  his  country  in  the
United States Air Force as an emergency room medic; and
 
  WHEREAS,  After  his  honorable  discharge  in  1989,  Scott  Medole
returned to Clarence and  became  involved  in  his  family's  insurance
business, working alongside his parents Norman and Nancy Emerling; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Chosen  for such a prestigious recognition by the Clarence
Chamber of Commerce for his unwavering commitment to community  service,
Scott  Medole  has  been a dedicated member of the Harris Hill Volunteer
Fire Company for the past 40 years; he serves  in  this  capacity  as  a
second-generation fireman and is Co-Chaplain of the fire company; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Further,  Scott Medole is an active member of the Clarence
Chamber of Commerce, past  original  member  of  Eastern  Hills  Sunrise
Rotary  Club, supporter of local credit unions North Towns and Niagara's
Choice, and is a tireless promotor of community events  and  businesses;
he also gives his time serving as a ride medic for various local bicycle
groups in the area; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Scott  Medole  contributes  much of his passion for giving
back to his faith, serving as a hospitality minister at Nativity of  the
Blessed Virgin Mary Church; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  his  leisure  time,  Scott Medole enjoys spending time
outdoors;  he  stays  active  by  biking,  playing  pickleball,  skiing,
golfing,  fishing,  hunting,  and  hiking  the Adirondack High Peaks; he
 
additionally loves playing euchre and spending  quality  time  with  his
family; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body that those who
enhance the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown  a
long  and  sustained  commitment to excellence certainly have earned the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Scott Medole upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of
the Volunteer of the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Scott Medole.
