This act shall be known and may be cited as the "small 2 water utility transparency act". 3 § 2. The public service law is amended by adding a new section 89-q 4 to read as follows: 5 § 89-q. Such audits shall 10 focus on capital investment in the private water company, compliance 11 with state and federal water safety regulations and laws, financial 12 capacity of the private water company, management ability and function 13 of the private water company, water adequacy and sufficiency of the 14 private water system and affordability. 15 2.
