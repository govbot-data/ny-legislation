FERNANDEZ -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Alcoholism and Substance Use Disorders AN ACT to amend the mental hygiene law, in relation to recovery communi- ty centers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The mental hygiene law is amended by adding a new section 2 32.05-b to read as follows: 3 § 32.05-b Certified recovery community centers. 4 1. The commissioner shall promulgate regulations consistent with this 5 section for the voluntary certification of certified recovery community 6 centers. 7 2. Such regulations shall be evidence-based, utilizing information 8 from sources with expertise in recovery. Such regulations shall, at a 9 minimum, provide guidance for: 10 (a) staffing; 11 (b) referrals to and coordination with community and peer-based 12 supports including support related to co-occurring disorders; 13 (c) participant safety; 14 (d) participant rights; 15 (e) confidentiality; 16 (f) re-occurrence support; and 17 (g) administrative and operational policies and procedures. 18 3. Once the commissioner has certified a location as a certified 19 recovery community center, such certified recovery community center 20 shall be included on the office's website as an available option for 21 individuals seeking such an environment. 22 4. The commissioner shall regulate and ensure that such centers which 23 are certified to be certified recovery community centers are continuing 24 to meet the requirements of this section.
