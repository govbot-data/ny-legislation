and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor the Syracuse Central School District's first ever Girls Wrestling Team for being trailblazers in the sport of wrestling, and congratulating them upon the occasion of winning their inaugural match; and WHEREAS, The Syracuse Central School District's creation of its first ever Girls Wrestling Team is a monumental step toward ensuring equity regarding accessibility to sports for all students; and WHEREAS, In a traditionally male-dominated sport, the creation and successes of the Syracuse Girls Wrestling Team sets an example about the importance of inclusivity and fairness in sports; and WHEREAS, This new dedicated team gives girls a platform to build self-confidence, teamwork skills, and resilience through athletic competition; such an opportunity can empower young women to take pride in their abilities and strengths and pursue goals that were traditionally unavailable to them due to gender-based restrictions; and WHEREAS, The Girls Wrestling Team was started in October of 2024, with the athletes practicing in socks rather than in standard wrestling shoes, proving their unrelenting dedication to the sport regardless of any obstacles; thanks to generous donors, the athletes were able to secure proper equipment, ensuring their readiness for competition; and WHEREAS, A sport such as wrestling, which requires skill, dedication, strength, and athletic prowess, is also a healthy way for young individuals to channel and release negative emotions such as anger, while building important interpersonal relationships with their peers and coach; and WHEREAS, Coach Heather Niver and all the outstanding athletes on the Syracuse Central School District Girls Wrestling Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor the Syracuse Central School District's first ever Girls Wrestling Team for being trailblazers in the sport of wrestling; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Coach Heather Niver and the members of the Syracuse Central School District's Girls Wrestling Team.
