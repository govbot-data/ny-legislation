Add §400.04, Pen L
 
Establishes the ammunition purchase certification act which requires purchasers of ammunition to have an ammunition purchase permit number.

        Introduced  by M. of A. GRAY, DeSTEFANO, SMULLEN, BLANKENBUSH, McDONOUGH
          -- read once and referred to the Committee on Codes
 
        AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to establishing  the  ammuni-
          tion purchase certification act
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. This act shall be known as the "ammunition purchase certif-
     2  ication act".
     3    § 2. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 400.04  to  read
     4  as follows:
     5  § 400.04 Ammunition purchase permit number.
     6    1.  No  person shall purchase ammunition in this state without a valid
     7  ammunition purchase permit  number  issued  by  the  division  of  state
     8  police.    The  division  of  state  police  shall serve as the point of
     9  contact for the processing of ammunition purchasing  permits  and  shall
    10  establish criteria and procedures for the issuance, renewal, suspension,
    11  and revocation of such permits.
    12    2.  An  ammunition  purchase  permit  shall be issued subsequent to an
    13  individual passing the required background check  for  the  purchase  of
    14  ammunition or after approval via application, as determined by the divi-
    15  sion  of  state police, and shall be valid for a period of one year from
    16  such initial purchase or date of issue. There shall be no additional fee
    17  for such issuance. The current fee of two dollars and fifty cents  shall
    18  be charged at the time of the initial purchase or filing of the applica-
    19  tion and any subsequent renewal.
    20    3. Each permit shall be a physical permit card with a unique identifi-
    21  cation  number.  From  the date of issuance, purchasers shall use either
    22  the permit card or its  unique  identification  number  when  purchasing
    23  ammunition.
    24    4.  The  permit  number  may be renewed for a subsequent period of one
    25  year upon a valid purchase after the expiration of such permit number.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
                                                                   LBD08225-01-5

        A. 3825                             2
 
     1    5. An ammunition purchase permit number may be revoked at any time  by
     2  the division of state police.
     3    6.  The division of state police shall notify the holder of an ammuni-
     4  tion purchase permit of the suspension or revocation of their permit  in
     5  writing  by  certified  mail  and such suspension or revocation shall be
     6  noted in the statewide license and record  database  for  licensees  and
     7  sellers.
     8    7.  A  person  who  purchases  ammunition  without  a valid ammunition
     9  purchase permit number is guilty of a class A misdemeanor.
    10    § 3. This act shall take effect on the first day of the twelfth  month
    11  after it shall have become a law.
