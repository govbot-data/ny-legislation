The executive law is amended by adding a new section 294-b 2 to read as follows: 3 § 294-b. Participation in the United Nations universal periodic 4 review. 1. The state, through the division, shall participate in the 5 United Nations human rights council's universal periodic review by 6 establishing an interagency task force for the purposes of drafting and 7 publishing a report.
