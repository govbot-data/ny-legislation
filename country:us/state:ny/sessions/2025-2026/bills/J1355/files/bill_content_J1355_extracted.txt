Fiorini-Parsons upon the occasion of her retirement after years of exemplary service to her community and State WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to honor and extol those individuals who perform exemplary service on behalf of their communities and State, dedicating their energy and efforts to bettering the lives of others; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Sharie L. Fiorini-Parsons upon the occasion of her retirement from the Oneida County Board of Elections after five years of service; and WHEREAS, Sharie L. Fiorini-Parsons began her service with the Oneida County Board of Elections in 2020; despite her first year as Deputy Commissioner of the Board being darkened by a global pandemic, as well as occurring during a presidential election, she endured and agreed to continue with the role; and WHEREAS, Throughout her tenure, Sharie L. Fiorini-Parsons worked closely with both Board leadership and staff to ensure compliance with all State and federal laws and introduce the voter registration and elections; and WHEREAS, A loving wife, mother, grandmother, and daughter, Sharie L. Fiorini-Parsons's well-deserved retirement will allow her to spend more time with her husband, Scott; her children, Danielle and Michael, Chelsea and Michael, Kari and Frank, and Ariana; her grandchildren, Giuliana, Francesca, Michelina, Giavanna, Nico, and Michael; her parents, Jerry and Frannie; and her mother-in-law, Sheila; and WHEREAS, In 2025, Sharie L. Fiorini-Parsons retired from the Oneida County Board of Elections after five years of dedicated service; this auspicious occasion was celebrated on Friday, December 19, 2025, at It's a Utica Thing! Kitchen & Bar; and WHEREAS, Such service, which is truly the lifeblood of our communities and State, too often goes unrecognized and unheralded; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Sharie L. Fiorini-Parsons upon the occasion of her retirement; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sharie L. Fiorini-Parsons.
