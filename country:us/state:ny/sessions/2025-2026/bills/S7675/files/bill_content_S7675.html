        AN ACT to amend chapter 220 of the laws   of   1976   incorporating  the
          Brockport  Exempt Fireman's Benevolent Association, Monroe county, New
          York, and providing for its powers and duties, in  relation  to    its
          purpose and the use of foreign fire insurance premiums
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Sections 1, 2, 3 and 7 of chapter 220 of the laws  of  1976
     2  incorporating  the  Brockport  Exempt  Firemen's Benevolent Association,
     3  Monroe county, New York, and providing for its powers  and  duties,  are
     4  amended to read as follows:
     5    Section  1. Incorporation; membership. A membership corporation by the
     6  name of "Brockport Exempt Volunteer [Firemen's] Firefighters' Benevolent
     7  Association, Inc." is hereby created.  It  shall  be  composed  of  such
     8  persons  eligible to membership therein as hereinafter provided as shall
     9  (a) notify the secretary of the Brockport Fire [Department] District,  a
    10  volunteer  fire [department] district, in writing prior to the organiza-
    11  tion meeting hereinafter provided for of the corporation created by this
    12  act of their desire to become members, or (b) shall attend such  meeting
    13  or  an  adjournment  thereof,  and  also such persons so eligible as may
    14  thereafter become members of such corporation pursuant to its by-laws.
    15    § 2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now, or  have
    16  been, or hereafter shall be active volunteer members of the fire depart-
    17  ment  of  the  village  of  Brockport,  in the town of Sweden, county of
    18  Monroe, or of any fire company in  [such  village]  the  Brockport  Fire
    19  District  and who have acquired or shall hereafter acquire the status of
    20  exempt volunteer  [firemen]  firefighter  as  defined  in  section  [two
    21  hundred]  200  of the general municipal law by virtue of having rendered

     1  service in such fire department, fire district or fire company, and  all
     2  persons  who  are  now or hereafter shall be active volunteer members of
     3  such fire [department] district, shall be eligible for membership in the
     4  corporation  created  by this act, except that any [fireman] firefighter
     5  who has been removed for cause, expelled or dropped from  the  rolls  of
     6  any  fire  company  or  fire department, and who has not been reinstated
     7  therein, shall not be eligible for membership in such  corporation.  The
     8  membership  of any person in such corporation shall terminate when [his]
     9  their membership as an active volunteer member of the  fire  [department
    10  of the village] district of Brockport shall terminate before such person
    11  shall  have  acquired  the  status  of  exempt  [fireman] firefighter as
    12  defined in section [two hundred] 200 of the  general  municipal  law  by
    13  virtue of having rendered service in such fire [department] district.
    14    §  3.  Purposes. The purposes of such corporation shall be the mainte-
    15  nance of suitable headquarters  for,  and  the  promotion  of  fraternal
    16  intercourse  among  the members of such corporation, the relief, aid and
    17  assistance of such members and their families who are injured,  disabled
    18  or  indigent,  and  the  promotion  of the welfare of the volunteer fire
    19  service within the [village of Brockport and]  territory  provided  with
    20  fire  protection  by  [contract  with  such  village] the Brockport Fire
    21  District, to promote and safeguard the welfare of the volunteer  members
    22  of  the  corporation  and  their families; to provide for the health and
    23  safety of the volunteer members of such corporation; and to enhance  the
    24  morale  of  volunteer members of the corporation by providing social and
    25  recreational activities for the volunteer members of the corporation.
    26    § 7. Precept for payment of foreign fire insurance premium taxes. Such
    27  corporation shall collect and there  shall  be  paid  to  it  all  taxes
    28  imposed  by section [five hundred fifty-three] 9104 of the insurance law
    29  for fire department use and benefit, upon premiums for insurance against
    30  loss or damage by fire covering property  situated  in  the  village  of
    31  Brockport, [in the] town of Sweden, county of Monroe, or within any duly
    32  organized territory in which the Brockport Fire [Department] District is
    33  or  may  be  obligated  to  render  fire protection. The officer of said
    34  corporation designated by its by-laws to collect and receive the  afore-
    35  said  tax shall have all the powers and be subject to all the provisions
    36  of the insurance law, relating to treasurers of fire  departments.  Such
    37  corporation shall also be entitled to receive a share of the tax imposed
    38  by  section  [five  hundred fifty-four] 9105 of the insurance law, based
    39  upon the business written in the territory with respect to which  it  is
    40  entitled  to  collect  and  receive  the tax under section [five hundred
    41  fifty-three] 9104 of the insurance law. Such taxes shall  only  be  used
    42  [for  the  care  and relief of disabled or indigent volunteer and exempt
    43  volunteer firemen and their families] by the corporation in  furtherance
    44  of its purposes as set forth in section three of this act.
    45    §  2.  This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that
    46  the amendments to section 7 of chapter 220 of the laws of 1976  made  by
    47  section one of this act shall apply to taxes due on premiums received on
    48  and after January 1, 2026.
