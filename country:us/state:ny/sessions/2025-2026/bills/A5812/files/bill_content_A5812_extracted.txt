The public housing law is amended by adding a new section 2 223-c to read as follows: 3 § 223-c. Braille labeling requirement. All dwellings in projects 4 located within the state shall include braille labeling on all room 5 numbers, elevator buttons, and directional signage in common areas in 6 such dwellings. 7 § 2.
