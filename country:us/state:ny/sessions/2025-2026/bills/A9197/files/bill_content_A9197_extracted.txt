CONRAD -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring license plates for certain bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 1242 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 14 to read as follows: 3 14. (a) Any bicycle with electric assist that can exceed twenty-eight 4 miles per hour with battery assistance up to and exceeding seven hundred 5 fifty watts shall be registered and issued a license plate by the 6 department. 7 (b) Such license plates shall be of such material, form, design and 8 dimensions and contain or set forth such distinguishing number or other 9 identification marks as the commissioner shall prescribe provided, 10 however, that there shall be at all times a marked contrast between the 11 color of the number plates and that of the numerals or letters thereon, 12 and provided further that no bicycle with electric assist shall display 13 the number plates of more than one state at a time, nor shall any plate 14 be used other than those issued by the commissioner. 15 (c) Each such license plate shall be conspicuously displayed on the 16 rear of the bicycle with electric assist. 17 (d) The fee for such license plates shall be a nominal fee determined 18 by the commissioner. 19 § 2. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new section 20 1290 to read as follows: 21 § 1290. Registration of electric scooters. 1. Any electric scooter 22 that can exceed twenty-eight miles per hour with battery assistance up 23 to and exceeding seven hundred fifty watts shall be registered and 24 issued a license plate by the department.
