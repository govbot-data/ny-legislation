AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to library cards issued
to minors
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 816 of the education law, as added by chapter 247
2 of the laws of 2013, is amended to read as follows:
3 § 816. Local library and school partnerships. 1. Legislative intent.
4 In order to promote and encourage children to read and learn outside the
5 classroom, this law seeks to encourage local libraries and schools to
6 work together to promote the availability of library services and
7 resources.
8 2. Definitions. For the purposes of this section, the following terms
9 shall be defined as follows:
10 a. "School district" shall mean any public school district in the
11 state of New York that contains one or more schools with any combination
12 of grades from and including kindergarten through grade twelve.
13 b. "Public library" shall mean any public or association library as
14 defined in section two hundred fifty-three of this title.
15 3. Upon the registration of a minor pursuant to section thirty-two
16 hundred five of this chapter to attend full time kindergarten offered by
17 a school district, parents or persons in parental relation to such minor
18 shall have the option to check a space on such registration form author-
19 izing such minor to receive a local library card from the respective
20 local library.
21 4. Upon the request of a public library that is located within the
22 boundaries of a school district, the district shall disseminate informa-
