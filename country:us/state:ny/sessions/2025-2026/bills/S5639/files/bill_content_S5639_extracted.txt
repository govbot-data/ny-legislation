BAILEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Elections AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to requiring any student graduating from a high school in New York state be provided with a voter registration form The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The election law is amended by adding a new section 5-232 2 to read as follows: 3 § 5-232. Graduating high school students. 1. In addition to any other 4 method of voter registration provided for in this article, every quali- 5 fied person shall be provided with an application for registration and 6 enrollment upon graduation from any public or private high school within 7 the state. Such application shall be in the same form as prescribed by 8 section 5-210 of this title. 9 2. A school district in which qualified persons are graduating shall 10 transmit any completed application for registration form to the appro- 11 priate board of elections not later than ten days after receipt except 12 that all such completed applications and forms received by the school 13 districts or department of education after the twentieth day prior to an 14 election and by the twelfth day before such election shall be transmit- 15 ted in such manner and at such time as to assure their receipt by such 16 board of elections as soon as practicable and not later than the tenth 17 day before such election. All transmittals shall include original signa- 18 tures. 19 3. Completed application forms received by a school district not later 20 than the twelfth day before the next ensuing primary, general or special 21 election and transmitted by such school district to the appropriate 22 board of elections so that they are received not later than the tenth 23 day before such election shall entitle the applicant to vote in such EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
