        AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to electric  vehicle
          charging station fee transparency
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The public service law is amended by adding a  new  section
     2  66-x to read as follows:
     3    §  66-x. Electric vehicle charging station fee transparency. 1. "Elec-
     4  tric vehicle charging station" shall have the same meaning as in  subdi-
     5  vision  one of section three hundred thirty-nine-ll of the real property
     6  law.
     7    2. The department is hereby authorized and directed to, no later  than
     8  June  first, two thousand twenty-seven, promulgate rules and regulations
     9  for any electric vehicle charging station for which payment is required:
    10    (a) establishing  fee  transparency  standards  for  electric  vehicle
    11  charging stations, provided such standards shall:
    12    (i)  require  electric  vehicle  charging stations to clearly post and
    13  update the total price for the use  of  the  electric  vehicle  charging
    14  station at the point of sale;
    15    (ii)  prohibit electric vehicle charging stations from requiring users
    16  to pay a subscription fee in order to use such station; provided, howev-
    17  er, that owners  and  operators  of  public  electric  vehicle  charging
    18  stations may have separate price schedules conditional on a subscription
    19  or membership; and
    20    (iii)  prohibit  requiring  payment  via  mobile  device  as  the only
    21  accepted form of payment; and
    22    (b) defining "significant upgrade",  which  shall  be  the  basis  for
    23  determining   what   changes   to  electric  vehicle  charging  stations

     6    (c) defining "subscription fee" as  a  recurring  payment  made  to  a
