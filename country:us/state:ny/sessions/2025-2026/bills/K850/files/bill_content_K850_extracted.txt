Buttenschon COMMEMORATING the 10th Anniversary of the Woodland Farm Brewery WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the achievements of those individuals and businesses that contribute substantially to the economic vitality of their communities, thereby enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the Woodland Farm Brewery to be celebrated during its Woodland 10-Year Anniversary Weekend on Friday, January 9-Sunday, January 11, 2026; and WHEREAS, In 2015, the Woodland Farm Brewery was established in Utica, New York; the Brewery has a focus on lagers, barrel-aged beers, and everything in between, resulting in a wide range of beers, hard ciders, wines, and mixed drinks; and WHEREAS, With a 10-barrel brewhouse, the Woodland Farm Brewery produces fresh, new batches regularly; patience and time are extra ingredients in making beer; the barrel-aged beers spend from one to three years developing before they are ready to be shared, and many of the barrels are sourced from New York State distilleries, wineries, and cooperages, several of which have won awards across New York State and the United States; and WHEREAS, The members of the Woodland Farm Brewery are characterized by an impressive commitment, unbridled enthusiasm, and excellence in their craft; and WHEREAS, It is with immense pride that the hard-working and dedicated members, including owner, Keith Redhead, of the Woodland Farm Brewery celebrate this milestone 10th Anniversary and anticipate continued success; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the Woodland Farm Brewery; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Woodland Farm Brewery.
