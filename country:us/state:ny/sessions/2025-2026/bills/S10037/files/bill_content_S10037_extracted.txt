ASHBY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT in relation to directing the department of transportation to construct and install safety gates at a certain railroad crossing in the village of Castleton-on-Hudson, county of Rensselaer The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no later than 2 two years after the effective date of this act, the department of trans- 3 portation shall construct and install safety gates at the railroad 4 crossing that crosses over Scott avenue, to the west of Main street, in 5 the village of Castleton-on-Hudson, county of Rensselaer. Such safety 6 gates shall operate via positive train control. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
