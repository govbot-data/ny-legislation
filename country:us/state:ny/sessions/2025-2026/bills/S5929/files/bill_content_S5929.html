        AN  ACT authorizing the commissioner of general services to transfer and
          convey certain state land to the Syosset central school district;  and
          providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act  but  notwithstanding
     2  any  other provision of law to the contrary, the commissioner of general
     3  services is hereby authorized to transfer  and  convey  to  the  Syosset
     4  Central  School  District,  located  in  the  hamlet of Syosset, town of
     5  Oyster Bay, county of Nassau, the state land described in section two of
     6  this act, owned by the state of New York and  being  in  the  hamlet  of
     7  Syosset,  town of Oyster Bay, county of Nassau for the purpose of build-
     8  ing new and  expanding  current  facilities  at  Berry  Hill  Elementary
     9  School. The transfer and conveyance of the land described in section two
    10  of  this act shall be made in consideration of the sum of one dollar and
    11  upon such conditions as the commissioner of general  services  may  deem
    12  proper.
    13    §  2. The land authorized by section one of this act to be transferred
    14  and conveyed shall be the parcel as follows:
    15    ALL that certain plot, piece or parcel of  land,  situate,  lying  and
    16  being  in Syosset, Town of Oyster Bay, County of Nassau and State of New
    17  York, as shown on a map prepared  by  H2M  architects  +  engineers,  as
    18  project  number  SYSD-2501, lasted revised January 15, 2025, bounded and
    19  described as follows:
    20    Beginning at a point on the northwesterly side of Syosset-Cold  Spring
    21  Road  the  following  two  courses and distances along the northwesterly
    22  side of Syosset-Cold Spring Road from the northeasterly most end  of  an
    23  arc  connecting  the northwesterly side of Syosset-Cold Spring Road with
    24  the northeasterly side of Renee Road:

    33    Containing within said bounds 149,892.19 S.F. or 3.44 acres.
    34    §  3.  As  a  condition  of  the  transfer, the Syosset Central School
    35  District may submit to the commissioner of general  services,  for  such
    36  commissioner's approval, an accurate survey of the lands to be conveyed,
    37  which may be used in the conveyance thereof.
    38    §  4.  The office of general services shall not transfer or convey the
    39  aforesaid land unless the application is made to the office  of  general
    40  services  by  the  Syosset Central School District within one year after
    41  the effective date of this act. Said application shall be in the form of
    42  a resolution setting forth a comprehensive  statement  approved  by  the
    43  Syosset  Central  School  District  board of education acknowledging and
    44  accepting the use restrictions set forth in section five of this act.
    45    § 5. The transfer and conveyance made pursuant to this  act  shall  be
    46  subject to the following use restrictions and reverter:
    47    (a)  The  land described in this act shall be used exclusively for the
    48  purposes of building new and expanding current facilities at Berry  Hill
    49  Elementary School.
    50    (b)  The  conveyance of the land described in this act shall contain a
    51  covenant and restriction  requiring  that  the  land  be  used  for  the
    52  purposes  set forth in subdivision (a) of this section. In the event the
    53  premises are not accepted and/or used for such  purposes,  ownership  of
    54  the land shall revert to the state of New York.
    55    §  6.  This  act shall take effect immediately and shall expire and be
    56  deemed repealed two years after such date.
