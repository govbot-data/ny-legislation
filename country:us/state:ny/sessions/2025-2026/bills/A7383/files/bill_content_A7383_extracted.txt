BARRETT -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Governmental Employees AN ACT to allow Andriel Santana to be eligible to take the civil service exam for the position of deputy sheriff for the Columbia County Sher- iff's Office The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding the provisions of section 58 of the civil 2 service law or any other provision of law to the contrary, Andriel 3 Santana, who has been continuously employed by the Columbia County Sher- 4 iff's office, in the county of Columbia, as a full-time correctional 5 officer, shall be eligible to take the competitive civil service exam- 6 ination for the position of deputy sheriff and shall, contingent upon 7 his score on such examination, be eligible to be placed on the eligible 8 list for appointment as a full-time deputy sheriff for the Columbia 9 county sheriff's office. 10 § 2. Andriel Santana shall otherwise be subject to the merit and 11 fitness provisions of the civil service law applicable to full-time 12 deputy sheriffs. 13 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
