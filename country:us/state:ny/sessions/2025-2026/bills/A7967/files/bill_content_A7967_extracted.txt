The division of human 27 rights, in consultation with the office of the attorney general and the 28 office for people with developmental disabilities, shall develop and 29 implement a public awareness and education campaign to inform the 30 public, businesses, housing providers, transportation services, and 31 government entities about: 32 (a) the rights of individuals with disabilities to be accompanied by 33 guide dogs and service dogs as provided under this article; 34 (b) penalties associated with violations of this article; and 35 (c) appropriate conduct and reasonable accommodations for individuals 36 accompanied by guide dogs and service dogs. 37 2.
