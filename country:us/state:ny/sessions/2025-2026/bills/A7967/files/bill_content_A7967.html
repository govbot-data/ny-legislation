BILL NOA07967
 
SAME ASNo Same As
 
SPONSORTapia
 
COSPNSRSteck, Eachus, Shimsky, Benedetto, Hevesi, Angelino
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §§47, 47-b & 47-c, add §47-d, Civ Rts L
 
Protects the rights of persons with disabilities to be accompanied by a guide dog or service dog in a place of public accommodation or housing accommodation; imposes a misdemeanor penalty for violations; requires the division of human rights to conduct a public awareness and education campaign.

    26    §  47-d.  Public  awareness  and  education.  1. The division of human
    27  rights, in consultation with the office of the attorney general and  the
    28  office  for  people  with  developmental disabilities, shall develop and
    29  implement a public  awareness  and  education  campaign  to  inform  the
    30  public,  businesses,  housing  providers,  transportation  services, and
    31  government entities about:
    32    (a) the rights of individuals with disabilities to be  accompanied  by
    33  guide dogs and service dogs as provided under this article;
    34    (b) penalties associated with violations of this article; and
    35    (c)  appropriate conduct and reasonable accommodations for individuals
    36  accompanied by guide dogs and service dogs.
    37    2. The public awareness and education campaign under  subdivision  one
    38  of this section shall include, but not be limited to:
    39    (a) online resources, including informational materials on state agen-

    41    (b)  distribution of printed educational materials to places of public

    43    (c)  outreach  and  training  programs  targeting  businesses,  public

    48  groups to promote awareness.
