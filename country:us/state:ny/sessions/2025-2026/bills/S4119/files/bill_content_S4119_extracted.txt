SCARCELLA-SPANTON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Insurance AN ACT to amend the insurance law, in relation to coverage for electro- lysis procedures to stop the growth of hair, when deemed medically necessary The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subsection (i) of section 3216 of the insurance law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph 40 to read as follows: 3 (40) (A) Every policy which provides medical, major medical, or simi- 4 lar comprehensive-type coverage shall provide coverage for electrolysis 5 procedures to stop the growth of hair, when determined medically neces- 6 sary, including but not limited to instances necessary for gender-af- 7 firming treatment. 8 (B) Coverage provided under this paragraph may be subject to annual 9 deductibles and co-insurance as deemed appropriate by the superinten- 10 dent. 11 § 2. Subsection (k) of section 3221 of the insurance law is amended by 12 adding a new paragraph 24 to read as follows: 13 (24) (A) Every group or blanket policy delivered or issued for deliv- 14 ery in this state which provides medical, major medical, or similar 15 comprehensive-type coverage shall provide coverage for electrolysis 16 procedures to stop the growth of hair, when determined medically neces- 17 sary, including but not limited to instances necessary for gender-af- 18 firming treatment. 19 (B) Coverage provided under this paragraph may be subject to annual 20 deductibles and co-insurance as deemed appropriate by the superinten- 21 dent. 22 § 3. Section 4303 of the insurance law is amended by adding a new 23 subsection (ww) to read as follows: EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
