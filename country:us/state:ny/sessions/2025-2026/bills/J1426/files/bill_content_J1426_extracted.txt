and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend HPIP Jimmie Chaple, Imperial Deputy of the Desert of New York, Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine (A.E.A.O.N.M.S.), upon the occasion of the 2026 Desert Conference, to be held Thursday, May 14-Sunday, May 17, 2026; and WHEREAS, As Imperial Deputy of the Desert, HPIP Jimmie Chaple serves in a position of high trust and responsibility, charged with advancing the mission, vision, and operational strength of the Desert of New York while upholding the ancient and honorable traditions of the Shrine rooted in fraternity, faith, charity, and disciplined leadership; and WHEREAS, The Desert of New York has long stood as a beacon of charitable service, civic engagement, and fraternal unity, fostering brotherhood, mentorship, and leadership among its Nobles while extending meaningful service to communities throughout the State; and WHEREAS, Under the distinguished leadership of HPIP Jimmie Chaple, the 2026 Desert Conference serves as a vital convocation for Nobles and leaders from across New York, providing opportunities for strategic dialogue, leadership development, ceremonial observance, and renewed commitment to the philanthropic and civic objectives of the A.E.A.O.N.M.S.; and WHEREAS, Through steadfast dedication to scholarship initiatives, youth mentorship, health awareness efforts, and charitable outreach, HPIP Jimmie Chaple exemplifies the highest ideals of the Shrine, reinforcing its enduring legacy of service, compassion, and collective advancement; and WHEREAS, The 2026 Desert Conference represents a significant moment of reflection, fellowship, and forward vision for the Desert of New York, celebrating both its storied legacy and its continued pursuit of excellence under enlightened leadership; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend HPIP Jimmie Chaple, Imperial Deputy of the Desert of New York, A.E.A.O.N.M.S., upon the occasion of the 2026 Desert Conference, and to extend best wishes for continued success in leadership, fellowship, and service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to HPIP Jimmie Chaple, Imperial Deputy of the Desert of New York, A.E.A.O.N.M.S.
