and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Tim Ryan, upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to Chanatry's Hometown Market in Utica, New York, on Friday, April 18, 2025; and WHEREAS, Tim Ryan is a trained and skilled Meat Cutter and has worked in the Meat Department his entire 50-year employment; and WHEREAS, While he is not working, providing exceptional service to the patrons of Chanatry's Hometown Market, Tim Ryan enjoys spending time with his wife, Julie, and his daughter, Kelsey; he is a fan of the New York Yankees and the Minnesota Vikings; traveling and skiing are two of his favorite activities; and WHEREAS, Tim Ryan offers 50 years of experience, serving his customers and providing loyalty and dependable longevity while employed by Chanatry's Hometown Market, Mohawk Valley's award-winning supermarket and a cornerstone of the Utica community since 1912; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by an individual for the benefit of others which Tim Ryan has displayed throughout his life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Tim Ryan, upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to Chanatry's Hometown Market, on April 18, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Tim Ryan.
