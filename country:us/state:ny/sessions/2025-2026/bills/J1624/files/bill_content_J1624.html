Senate Resolution No. 1624
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
        CONGRATULATING  Thomas  and Alice Connors upon the
        occasion   of   celebrating   their   68th   Wedding
        Anniversary
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  takes  great  pleasure  in paying
tribute to flourishing marriages that have stood the test  of  time  and
serve as a paradigm for present and future generations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the sense of this Legislative Body to congratulate
Thomas and Alice Connors upon the occasion  of  celebrating  their  68th
Wedding Anniversary on Sunday, April 12, 2026; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Residents  of  Deerfield, New York, since 2005, Thomas and
Alice (Havey) Connors met more than 70 years  ago  when  they  were  ice
skating  near  their  homes  in  Massachusetts;  after their wedding and
honeymoon, the couple resided in Massachusetts where they spent 41 years
raising their five children, Debbie, Thomas, Michael, Alyssa,  and  Mary
Ellen; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  1989,  Thomas  Connors  and  other  concerned citizens
established  a  soup  kitchen  named  Open  Table  where  he  and  Alice
volunteered  as  servers  for  a  decade; this well-established facility
still operates today; they also volunteered their  services  at  another
soup kitchen, Hope House, for ten years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  32  years,  Thomas and Alice Connors conducted weekly
group sessions for 25 men at a prison where participants were encouraged
to talk about their deepest emotions with the  couple  who  would  share
their own coping skills; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Thomas and Alice Connors' devotion to their Catholic faith
also inspired their involvement in various church ministries,  including
their  service  as facilitators for the Rite of Christian Initiation for
Adults (RCIA) which prepares individuals wishing to  join  the  Catholic
Church; they served in this role for 20 years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Through  the  years,  Thomas and Alice Connors enjoyed the
growth of their family, welcoming eight grandchildren; their  unwavering
commitment  to their faith and each other has been a profound influence;
the couple's dedication, caring, and respect for  each  other  has  been
passed down through subsequent generations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  love,  devotion,  and caring Thomas and Alice Connors
have shown to their family, friends, and all who  know  them  are  their
hallmark  and  tradition;  this meaningful occasion is the result of the
love, hard work, and spirit they have invested in their happy  marriage;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In September 2025, a surprise 90th birthday celebration for
Thomas  Connors  was  a testament to the steadfast support of the entire
family who organized and attended  the  event  in  Utica,  some  members
traveling thousands of miles from California; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Thomas  and  Alice Connors have touched the lives of their
family and many friends through the shining example  of  their  marriage
which  is  a  testament to their devotion and admiration for each other;
the longevity of this enduring  marriage  truly  marks  a  milestone  in
life's journey, an event of personal triumph and joy; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of this Legislative Body that, when an
occasion of such importance is brought to our attention, the same should
be memorialized by us for the edification and emulation of others;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Thomas and Alice Connors upon the occasion  of  celebrating
their 68th Wedding Anniversary; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Thomas and Alice Connors.
