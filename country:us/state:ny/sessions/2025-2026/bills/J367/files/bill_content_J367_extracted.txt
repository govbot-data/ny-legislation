Valentine, Grand Worthy Matron Marsha L. Mack and other elected officers, Eureka Grand Chapter continues to guide its members with grace, dignity, and a resolute commitment to excellence in service to God, family, and community; and WHEREAS, The 128th Annual Grand Session will further uplift the Order's rich heritage and provide a space for spiritual renewal, fraternal fellowship, and the celebration of extraordinary accomplishments across the state and nation; and WHEREAS, The enduring legacy of the Prince Hall Eastern Star is woven into the very fabric of civic life throughout New York, and this Grand Session serves as a vibrant reminder of the Order's profound influence on generations past, present, and future; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 128th Annual Grand Session of Eureka Grand Chapter, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, and to extend its highest commendation to the leadership and members for their continued devotion to the spiritual, charitable, and social uplift of communities across New York State; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Grand Worthy Patron Grant M.
