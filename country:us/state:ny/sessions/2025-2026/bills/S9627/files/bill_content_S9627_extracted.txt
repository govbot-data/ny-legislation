The public health law is amended by adding a new section 2 2833 to read as follows: 3 § 2833. Medical cannabis access in health care facilities. 1. For the 4 purposes of this section, the following terms shall have the following 5 meanings: 6 (a) "Health care facility" shall mean a general hospital or nursing 7 home as such terms are defined by section twenty-eight hundred one of 8 this article. 9 (b) "Medical cannabis" shall have the same meaning as defined by 10 subdivision thirty-three of section three of the cannabis law. 11 (c) "Patient" shall mean an individual who is terminally ill. 12 (d) "Terminally ill" shall have the same meaning as defined by subdi- 13 vision fifty-one of section three of the cannabis law. 14 2. A health care facility shall permit patient use of medical cannabis 15 while at such health care facility and shall: 16 (a) prohibit smoking or vaping as methods to use such medical canna- 17 bis; 18 (b) include the use of medical cannabis in such patient's medical 19 records; 20 (c) reasonably regulate the way in which such patient stores and uses 21 medical cannabis, including requiring the medical cannabis be stored in 22 a locked container; and 23 (d) develop and disseminate written guidelines for the use of medical 24 cannabis within such health care facility pursuant to article three of 25 the cannabis law.
