this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Bloomfield High School Varsity Softball Team upon the occasion of winning the 2025 Class C NYSPHSAA Softball Championship at the Greenlight Networks Grand Slam Park, in Binghamton, New York, on Saturday, June 14, 2025; to the praise and applause of their excited fans, the Lady Bombers defeated the Tuckahoe Tigers with a score of 11-0 to win the championship; and WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's colors of royal blue and white, family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Bloomfield High School Varsity Softball Team throughout their journey as they ended their season with a remarkable overall 24-1 record; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as softball, which demands quick decision-making, intense concentration and a high level of physical prowess, Head Coach Mark Jacobs and his skilled coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and WHEREAS, Coach Mark Jacobs and all of the outstanding athletes on the Bloomfield High School Varsity Softball Team have clearly displayed growth and success, contributing to their school's history of achievements and academic rigor; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Bloomfield High School Varsity Softball Team: Addison Falsone, Sydney Falsone, Celia Falsone, Evalynn Howlett, Calla McCombs, Loghan Nowak, Abby O'Brien, Marlee Poole, Brianna Leitten, Sawyer Gallup, and Ashlyn Wright; Head Coach Mark Jacobs; and Assistant Coach Cody Domville on their outstanding season and winning the 2025 Class C NYSPHSAA Softball Championship; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Bloomfield High School Varsity Softball Team, Head Coach Mark Jacobs, Assistant Coach Cody Domville, Superintendent Andrew Doell, Principal Amy Shannon, Athletic Director Greg Parzych, and School Board President Pamela Nakoski.
