Paragraphs 2 and 3 of subdivision 3 of section 11-0701 of 2 the environmental conservation law, as amended by section 1-a of part R 3 of chapter 58 of the laws of 2013, are amended and a new paragraph 4 is 4 added to read as follows: 5 (2) who is eighteen years of age or older to hunt wild deer and bear 6 with a longbow, as provided in title [9] nine of this article, in a 7 special longbow season; [and] 8 (3) who is sixteen or seventeen years of age to exercise the same 9 privileges subject to the provisions of section 11-0929 and subdivision 10 3 of section 11-0713 of this article[.]; and 11 (4) who is sixty years of age or older to hunt deer with a crossbow, 12 as provided in title nine of this article, in a special archery season. 13 The department shall adopt regulations to allow the hunting of deer by 14 the use of a crossbow to any person sixty years of age or older in a 15 special archery season, subject to such restrictions as the department 16 may adopt by regulation.
