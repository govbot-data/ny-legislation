Paragraph (a) of subdivision 7 of section 182 of the lien 2 law, as amended by chapter 424 of the laws of 2019, is amended to read 3 as follows: 4 (a) An owner's lien may be enforced by public or private sale of the 5 occupant's goods that remain in the self-storage facility, in block, or 6 in parcel, at any time or place and on any terms which are commercially 7 reasonable after notice to all persons known to claim an interest in the 8 goods.
