AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to including a statement
on the envelopes of ballots for absentee voters and special presiden-
tial voters for signature affirmation
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 7-122 of the election law, as
2 amended by chapter 411 of the laws of 2019, is amended to read as
3 follows:
4 6. On the reverse side of such inner affirmation envelope shall be
5 printed the following statement:
6 AFFIRMATION
7 I do declare that I am a citizen of the United States, that I am duly
8 registered in the election district shown on the reverse side of this
9 envelope and I am qualified to vote in such district; that I will be
10 unable to appear personally on the day of the election for which this
11 ballot is voted at the polling place of the election district in which I
12 am a qualified voter because of the reason given on my application here-
13 tofore submitted; that I have not qualified nor do I intend to vote
14 elsewhere, that I have not committed any act nor am I under any imped-
15 iment which denies me the right to vote.
16 I hereby declare that the foregoing is a true statement to the best of
17 my knowledge and belief, and I understand that if I make any material
18 false statement in the foregoing statement of absentee voter, I shall be
19 guilty of a misdemeanor.
20 Date.................20..... .....................................
21 Signature or mark of voter
