AN ACT to amend the multiple dwelling law, in relation to requiring
certain owners of multiple dwellings in cities with a population of
one million or more to designate a secure space for packages to be
delivered to
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The section heading of section 57 of the multiple dwelling
2 law is amended and a new subdivision 3 is added to read as follows:
3 Bells; mail receptacles; packages.
4 3. (a) In cities with a population of one million or more, whenever
5 the owner of a multiple dwelling with fifty or more units has not
6 arranged for packages to be delivered to occupants thereof by such
7 owner, or such owner's agent or employees, such owner shall designate a
8 secure space within the building for packages to be delivered to. Such
9 space may be a package room, management office, closet, or other simi-
10 larly designated space, and such space shall be:
11 (i) locked;
12 (ii) accessible only to staff, residents or delivery people with a
13 key;
14 (iii) monitored by a camera or other surveillance device; and
15 (iv) a space other than the building vestibule or lobby.
16 (b) The creation of such package room shall not result in any
17 increased cost to the tenant. If no such space is available in the
18 building, then building staff are required to deliver packages to the
19 doorways of the residents to which they are addressed.
