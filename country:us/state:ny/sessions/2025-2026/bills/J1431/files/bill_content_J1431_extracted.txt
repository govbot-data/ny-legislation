Duff, HPIC, Imperial Deputy for the Desert of New York, Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine, upon the occasion of the 2026 Desert Conference WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor and commend individuals whose distinguished leadership, deep commitment to service, and profound community engagement have advanced the causes of fellowship, philanthropy, and fraternal excellence across the State of New York; and WHEREAS, In full accord with this long-standing tradition, this Legislative Body is proud to commend Dr. Cassandra B. Duff, HPIC, Imperial Deputy for the Desert of New York, Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine, upon the occasion of the 2026 Desert Conference, to be held Thursday, May 14 through Sunday, May 17, 2026; and WHEREAS, Dr. Cassandra B. Duff, HPIC, is a respected and accomplished leader of the Desert of New York; she proudly represents Medina Court No. 11, Oasis of New York, and serves with humility and purpose alongside the Daughters and Nobles of the Desert; and WHEREAS, A lifelong advocate for learning, justice, and human dignity, Dr. Duff holds a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from Stony Brook University, a Master of Science in Social Work from Columbia University, and a Master of Science in Administration from Metropolitan College; she has also received a Ph.D. (Honorary) in Philosophy and Humanities in recognition of her contributions to scholarship and community engagement; and WHEREAS, In addition to her academic achievements, Dr. Duff has dedicated more than 20 years to serving others as a psychotherapist in private practice, guiding individuals on their journeys toward healing, self-discovery, and emotional well-being, and further demonstrating a lifetime commitment to compassion and care; and WHEREAS, A proud member of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Dr. Duff has embraced the values of sisterhood, scholarship, and service, fostering community bonds, mentoring others, and advancing initiatives that uplift individuals and neighborhoods throughout New York State; and WHEREAS, As Imperial Deputy for the Desert, Dr. Cassandra B. Duff, HPIC, has brought to her role a rare blend of scholarly insight, strategic leadership, and heartfelt devotion to service, helping to ensure that the Desert Conference remains a vibrant, purpose-driven forum for fellowship, training, and fraternal advancement; and WHEREAS, Through her unwavering advocacy for education, community empowerment, and charitable engagement, Dr. Cassandra B. Duff, HPIC, exemplifies the highest ideals of the A.E.A.O.N.M.S. and continues to inspire Nobles throughout the Desert of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Dr. Cassandra B. Duff, HPIC, Imperial Deputy for the Desert of New York, A.E.A.O.N.M.S., upon the occasion of the 2026 Desert Conference, to be held from May 14-17, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Dr. Cassandra B.
