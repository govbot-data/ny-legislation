Paragraphs (b-2) and (b-3) of subdivision 2 of section 2 168-f of the correction law, as added by section 2 of part O of chapter 3 56 of the laws of 2005, are amended to read as follows: 4 (b-2) If the sex offender has been given a level two or level three 5 designation, [he or she] such offender shall personally appear at the 6 law enforcement agency having jurisdiction within twenty days of the 7 first anniversary of the sex offender's initial registration and every 8 year thereafter during the period of registration for the purpose of 9 providing a current photograph of such offender.
