and WHEREAS, Tony Kostecki has given not only of his time and energies but also of his competence, intelligence and leadership and consequently has been designated for special honor; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Tony Kostecki upon the occasion of being recognized as a 2025 Trailblazer in Arts & Culture honoree by City & State NY magazine; and WHEREAS, City & State's Trailblazers in Arts & Culture, written and researched in partnership with journalist Lon Cohen, is a prestigious award that honors visionary leaders who drive critical work and improve the lives of New Yorkers through the State's vibrant arts and culture sector; and WHEREAS, Tony Kostecki and his fellow honorees will be celebrated at an awards ceremony in Manhattan, New York where they will honor his decades-long dedication to the arts and education; and WHEREAS, Tony Kostecki has been a central figure in New York's North Country arts community for decades, most notably through his leadership of Seagle Festival, rebranded from Seagle Music Colony in 2021 to better reflect its modern mission, the oldest opera training program in the United States, founded in 1915; and WHEREAS, Tony Kostecki holds a Bachelors of Arts in Music from Benedictine College and a Masters of Music in Piano Accompanying and Organ Performance from the University of Kansas; and WHEREAS, With a robust performance resume, Tony Kostecki's experience includes work as a pianist/coach for companies like the Florida Grand Opera, Shreveport Opera, and Utah Opera; and WHEREAS, Prior to his full-time commitment to the Seagle Festival, Tony Kostecki served as Director of Education for the Fort Worth Opera Studio from 2002 to 2007, where he was instrumental in creating the renowned Young Artists Program; and WHEREAS, Since being appointed General Director in January 2008, Tony Kostecki's tenure has been marked by a period of dramatic expansion for the institution; the Festival has grown from a summer-only performance and training festival to a stalwart year-round presence in the local and regional arts scene; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by an individual for the benefit of others which Tony Kostecki has displayed throughout his career; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Tony Kostecki upon the occasion of being recognized as a 2025 Trailblazer in Arts & Culture honoree by City & State NY magazine; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Tony Kostecki.
