Senate Resolution No. 829
 
BY: Senator COMRIE
 
        COMMEMORATING  the 15th Anniversary of the Cambria
        School of Excellence to be celebrated  on  June  12,
        2025
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those institutions of true purpose and  high  achievement  whose
exemplary  programs  and accomplishments clearly demonstrate an enduring
pursuit of excellence in the education of the youth of the Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate the 15th Anniversary of the Cambria School of Excellence, to
be celebrated on Thursday, June 12, 2025, at the  Crest  Hollow  Country
Club in Woodbury, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,   The  steadfast  and  unyielding  commitment  of  parents,
educators, and administrators to provide the highest  quality  education
for  students  is  the  hallmark  and tradition of the Cambria School of
Excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Since its founding in 1980, graduates of the Cambria Center
for the Gifted Child would typically  move  on  to  private  independent
preparatory  schools  and programs, but many were unwilling to leave the
Queens area and did not wish to leave a  prestigious  organization  that
has served them well, which resulted in a demand for the continuation of
the Cambria Center for the Gifted Child's programs; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Cambria School of Excellence is a direct outgrowth and
continuation of the work of the Cambria Center for the Gifted Child, and
is a result of the clear community demand for a  quality  middle  school
program  that  prioritizes  rigorous accelerated academic programs while
maintaining small classrooms and a nurturing environment; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Cambria  School  of  Excellence  opened  its  doors  in
September  2009  with  its  first  group  of  6th  grade  students;  the
organization has since been guided by the mission to provide a  rigorous
and  comprehensive academic program which will produce critical thinkers
who solve problems while being ethical and socially responsible; and
 
  WHEREAS, Together, staff, students,  administrators,  families,  and
community   members   have   fostered  an  environment  that  cultivates
self-confidence, leadership, character, and excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Teaching  methods  at  the  Cambria  School  of  Excellence
prioritize  the  belief  that  every  student  possesses a wide range of
talents and strengths which, if realized  and  fostered,  will  lead  to
purposeful and fulfilled lives; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Graduates of the Cambria School of Excellence are lifelong
learners  who  are  able  to  demonstrate  entrepreneurship  and  public
speaking  skills  and are ready to lead an ethical life of integrity and
service to the global community; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Cambria  School of Excellence is proudly aware of its
distinguished past, eager to fulfill its present commitment to  parents,
students,  and  the  community, and planning with vision and purpose for
the ebb and swell of growth and change it will encounter in the years to
come; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the 15th Anniversary of the Cambria School of Excellence to
be celebrated on June 12, 2025; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That this Resolution, suitably engrossed,  be  transmitted
to the Cambria School of Excellence.
