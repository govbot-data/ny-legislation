AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to the dedi-
cation of the Franklin Delano Roosevelt Mid-Hudson bridge
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new
2 section 526-c to read as follows:
3 § 526-c. Ceremonial designation of the Franklin Delano Roosevelt Mid-
4 Hudson bridge. 1. The Franklin Delano Roosevelt Mid-Hudson bridge, as
5 defined in subdivision five of section five hundred twenty-six of this
6 title, shall be dedicated to Franklin and Eleanor Roosevelt.
7 2. The chairperson of the New York State Bridge Authority shall
8 provide for the installation and maintenance of adequate signing of the
9 state highway system as designated pursuant to subdivision one of this
10 section within such time and in such manner as they shall deem appropri-
11 ate. However, to avoid confusion and to limit any possible disruption of
12 commerce, the designation called for pursuant to subdivision one of this
13 section shall be one of ceremonial nature and the official name of such
14 bridge shall not be changed as a result of this section.
15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
