AN ACT to amend chapter 117 of the laws of 1983 incorporating the Mace-
don Center Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association,
Inc., and providing for its powers and duties, in relation to the
purpose of such association and the use of foreign fire insurance
premium taxes
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Sections 1, 2, 3 and 7 of chapter 117 of the laws of 1983
2 incorporating the Macedon Center Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevo-
3 lent Association, Inc., and providing for its powers and duties, are
4 amended to read as follows:
5 Section 1. Incorporation; membership. A not-for-profit corporation by
6 the name of the Macedon Center Volunteer and Exempt [Firemen's] Fire-
7 fighter's Benevolent Association, Inc., is hereby created. It shall be
8 composed of such persons eligible to membership therein as hereinafter
9 provided as shall (a) notify the secretary of the Macedon Center Volun-
10 teer [Fireman's] Firefighter's Benevolent Association, Inc., in writing
11 prior to the organization meeting of such corporation hereinafter
12 provided for of their desire to become members, or (b) shall attend such
13 meeting or an adjournment thereof, and also such persons so eligible as
14 may become members of such corporation, pursuant to its by-laws.
15 § 2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now or have
16 been, or hereafter shall be active volunteer members of the fire depart-
17 ment of the Macedon Center [Fire District] Volunteer Firemen's Associ-
18 ation, Inc. and who have heretofore acquired or shall hereafter acquire
19 the status of exempt volunteer [firemen] firefighters as defined in
20 section two hundred of the general municipal law or any other applicable
21 law by virtue of having rendered service in said fire department or fire
22 company in such fire district, and all persons who are now or hereafter
23 shall be active volunteer members of said fire department shall be
24 eligible for membership in the corporation hereby created; provided that
25 any person who has been expelled or removed for cause from membership in
26 any fire department or company shall not be eligible to membership in
27 such corporation. The membership of any person in such corporation shall

21 injured in the performance of their duties, and their families; to main-

23 ration; to promote and safeguard the welfare of the volunteer members of
24 the corporation, and their families; to enhance the morale of the volun-
25 teer members of the corporation by providing social and recreational
26 activities for the volunteer members of the corporation; to provide for
27 the health and safety of the volunteer members of the corporation; and
28 to promote the volunteer fire service within the territory protected by
29 the Macedon Center volunteer fire department.
30 § 7. Precept for payment of foreign fire insurance premium taxes. Such
31 corporation shall collect and there shall be paid to it all taxes
32 imposed by section[five hundred fifty-three] nine thousand one hundred
33 four of the insurance law for fire department use and benefit upon
34 premiums for insurance against loss or damage by fire covering property
35 situated within the Macedon Center [Fire District] Volunteer Firemen's
36 Association, Inc. located in Wayne county, New York. The officers of
37 said corporation designated by its by-laws to collect and receive the
38 aforesaid tax shall have all the powers and be subject to all the
39 provisions of the insurance law, relating to treasurers of fire depart-
40 ments. Such corporation shall also be entitled to receive a share of the
41 tax distributed pursuant to the provisions of section [five hundred
42 fifty-four] nine thousand one hundred five of the insurance law, based
43 upon the business written in the territory with respect to which it is
44 entitled to collect and receive the tax under section [five hundred
45 fifty-three] nine thousand one hundred four of the insurance law. Such
46 taxes shall only be used [for the care and relief of disabled or indi-
47 gent volunteer and exempt volunteer firemen and their families] by the
48 corporation in furtherance of its purposes as set forth in section three
49 of this act.
50 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that
51 the amendments to section 7 of chapter 117 of the laws of 1983 incorpo-
52 rating the Macedon Center Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent
53 Association, Inc. and providing for its powers and duties, made by
54 section one of this act shall apply to taxes due on premiums received on
55 and after January 1, 2027.
