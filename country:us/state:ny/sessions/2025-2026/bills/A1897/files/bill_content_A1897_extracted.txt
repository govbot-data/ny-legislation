Subdivision 2 of section 365-a of the social services law 2 is amended by adding a new paragraph (nn) to read as follows: 3 (nn) care and services provided by school psychologists certified, or 4 otherwise authorized to provide such care and services within their 5 scope of practice, under the education law where such services and care 6 would otherwise be covered under this title.
