The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 149 to 10 read as follows: 11 § 149. Tower technician protections. 1. Definitions. For the purposes 12 of this article, the following terms shall have the following meanings: 13 (a) "State agency" or "state agencies" means all state departments, 14 boards, commissions, offices or institutions. 15 (b) "Telecommunications tower" means any freestanding tower, monopole 16 or similar structure or facility used for the provision of wireless 17 services including ancillary telecommunications equipment required to 18 integrate such structure or facility into an existing or proposed wire- 19 less network.
