Amd §508, Ed L
 
Prohibits investment managers from assisting in investing moneys belonging to retirement systems; defines who qualifies as an investment manager.

A11078 Text:




 
                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          11078
 
                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                     April 24, 2026
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced by M. of A. RAJKUMAR -- read once and referred to the Commit-
          tee on Governmental Employees
 
        AN  ACT  to  amend  the  education  law, in relation to prohibiting fund
          managers in certain retirement systems
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section  1.  Subdivision  18  of  section 508 of the education law, as
     2  added by chapter 779 of the laws of 1982, is amended to read as follows:
     3    18. [Notwithstanding any other provision of  this  section,  the]  (a)
     4  Beginning  on the effective date of the chapter of the laws of two thou-
     5  sand twenty-six which amended this  subdivision,  the  retirement  board
     6  shall  [have  the  power  to delegate to one or more] be prohibited from
     7  entering into any contract to engage, hire, invest with or commit to  an
     8  investment  [managers  its authority] manager to invest moneys belonging
     9  to the retirement system, other than real property, by  the  fund.  [The
    10  term "investment managers" as used in this subdivision shall include but
    11  not  be  limited  to  independent contractors selected by the retirement
    12  board.]
    13    (b) Beginning on the effective date of the chapter of the laws of  two
    14  thousand  twenty-six  which amended this subdivision, the fund shall not
    15  renew any existing contracts to engage, hire, invest with or  commit  to
    16  an investment manager to assist in obtaining investments by the fund.
    17    (c) For the purposes of this subdivision:
    18    (i) "Investment manager" shall mean any person, other than an employee
    19  of  the  comptroller, or entity engaged by the common retirement fund to
    20  manage a part or all of an investment portfolio of the fund, other  than
    21  real property owned by the fund.
    22    (ii)  "Manage"  shall  include, but is not limited to, the analysis of
    23  portfolio holdings, and the purchase, sale, and lending thereof.
    24    § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of January next  succeed-
    25  ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
 
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
