BILL NOA01480
 
SAME ASSAME AS S00942
 
SPONSORAngelino
 
COSPNSRBarclay, Beephan, Bendett, Blankenbush, Brabenec, Brook-Krasny, Brown K, Chang, DiPietro, Durso, Fitzpatrick, Gandolfo, Giglio, Gray, Hawley, Jensen, Lemondes, Maher, Manktelow, McDonough, Miller, Novakhov, Palmesano, Slater, Smullen, Tague, Chludzinski, Molitor, Friend
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §§60.06, 70.00 & 125.25, Pen L
 
Makes life imprisonment without parole mandatory for defendants convicted of murder in the first degree or second degree and the victim is a police officer, peace officer, first responder or correctional officer.
