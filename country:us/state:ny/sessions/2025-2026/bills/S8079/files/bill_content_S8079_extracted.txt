ROLISON, SKOUFIS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to directing the Triborough Bridge and Tunnel Authority to implement a toll discount plan for residents of Orange, Rockland, Dutchess and Putnam counties; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon the expiration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 572 to read as follows: 3 § 572. Congestion pricing relief program. 1. The authority shall 4 implement a toll discount program for qualifying commuters travelling 5 into the city of New York from Orange, Rockland, Dutchess or Putnam 6 counties for employment. 7 2. Under the program, a qualifying commuter shall receive a twenty- 8 five percent discount on tolls under the central business district toll- 9 ing program for each of the first ten trips made each calendar month 10 from the commuter's residence into the city of New York, and a fifty 11 percent discount on tolls under the central business district tolling 12 program for all subsequent trips made from the commuter's residence into 13 the city of New York during the same calendar month. A minimum of ten 14 trips per month is required. If fewer than ten trips are taken per 15 month, the commuter shall be charged for each trip not taken. There 16 shall be no discount for commuters who make less than ten trips. Commu- 17 ters shall be required to enroll in the program in order to receive the 18 discount. 19 3.
