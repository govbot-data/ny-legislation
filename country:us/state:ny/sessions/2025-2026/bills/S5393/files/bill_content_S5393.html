S05393 Summary:

BILL NOS05393
 
SAME ASNo Same As
 
SPONSORSCARCELLA-SPANTON
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §§130.65, 245.03 & 140.17, add §70.11, Pen L; add §380.67, CP L
 
Relates to sexual abuse in the first degree where a person is incapable of consent while a passenger on public transportation and the contact was intentional and with the sole purpose of sexual gratification; relates to public lewdness while a passenger on public transportation; establishes enhanced penalties for offenses committed while in or upon a building, facility, property, vehicle or train owned, leased or operated by the metropolitan transportation authority; provides for the suspension or revocation of the use of a building, facility, property, vehicle or train owned, leased or operated by the metropolitan transportation authority.

        AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to sexual abuse in the  first
          degree  where  a  person  is incapable of consent while a passenger on
          public transportation; in relation to public lewdness while a  passen-
          ger  on  public  transportation;  in relation to establishing enhanced
          penalties for offenses committed while in or upon a building,  facili-
          ty, property, vehicle or train owned, leased or operated by the metro-
          politan  transportation authority; and to amend the criminal procedure
          law, in relation to the suspension or  revocation  of  the  use  of  a
          facility,  property, vehicle or train owned, leased or operated by the
          metropolitan transportation authority
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1.  Section 130.65 of the penal law is amended by adding a new
     2  subdivision 2-a to read as follows:
     3    2-a. Intentionally, and with the sole purpose of sexual gratification,
     4  and  without the consent of the other person while a passenger on public
     5  transportation; or
     6    § 2. Subdivision 2 of section 245.03 of the penal  law,  as  added  by
     7  chapter  186  of the laws of 2014, is amended and a new subdivision 3 is

    12  section[.]; or
    13    3. such person commits the crime of public  lewdness,  as  defined  in
    14  section  245.00 of this article, while a passenger on public transporta-
    15  tion.
