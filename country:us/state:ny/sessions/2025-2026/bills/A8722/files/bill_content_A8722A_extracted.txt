AN ACT to amend the civil service law, in relation to providing a
service credit to military spouses and to surviving spouses of veter-
ans in competitive examinations
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 1 of section 85 of the civil service law is
2 amended by adding two new paragraphs (e) and (f) to read as follows:
3 (e) The term "military spouse" shall mean the spouse of a veteran or
4 non-disabled veteran.
5 (f) The term "surviving spouse" shall mean the spouse of a member of
6 any branch of the armed forces of the United States who served and
7 subsequently died due to a cause attributed to their military service.
8 § 2. Paragraph (a) of subdivision 2 of section 85 of the civil service
9 law, as added by chapter 790 of the laws of 1958, is amended to read as
10 follows:
11 (a) On all eligible lists resulting from competitive examinations, the
12 names of eligibles shall be entered in the order of their respective
13 final earned ratings on examination, with the name of the eligible with
14 the highest final earned rating at the head of such list, provided,
15 however, that for the purpose of determining final earned ratings,
16 (1) Disabled veterans shall be entitled to receive ten points addi-
17 tional in a competitive examination for original appointment and five
18 points additional credit in a competitive examination for promotion,
19 [and]
20 (2) Non-disabled veterans shall be entitled to receive five points
21 additional credit in a competitive examination for original appointment
