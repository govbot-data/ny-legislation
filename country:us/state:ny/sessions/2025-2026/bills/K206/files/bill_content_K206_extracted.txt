WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the achievements of those individuals and businesses that contribute substantially to the economic vitality of their communities, thereby enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 20th Anniversary of Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc., at its Anniversary Celebration at its Flatware Factory, in Sherrill, New York, on Tuesday, March 25, 2025; and WHEREAS, Liberty Tabletop, a division of Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc., is a family-owned-and-operated business co-owned by Matthew Roberts, Greg Owens, and Drummond, LLC; its factory has proudly produced quality flatware for more than a century; and WHEREAS, Liberty Tabletop is the only flatware manufacturer in the United States, fostering growth of the national economy, providing career jobs to people in the community, and preserving the craft of flatware manufacturing and tool making; and WHEREAS, Always socially conscientious, Liberty Tabletop uses American-made products in its manufacturing processes and hires local accounting, legal, and marketing firms; these efforts drive the American economy in a safe, ethical, and environmentally friendly way; and WHEREAS, Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc., prides itself on quality production, earning the trust and loyalty of its customers who will receive excellent customer service and products of only the highest quality; and WHEREAS, President and Co-Founder of Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc., Matthew Roberts, emphasizes that the anniversary is a time to celebrate and honor everyone who has contributed to the company's success; and WHEREAS, It is with great pride that Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc., and its dedicated workers celebrate this milestone 20th Anniversary and anticipate many more years of success for generations to come; and WHEREAS, The progression of Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc., is in direct correlation to the efforts of its owners and employees, whose involvement is, and always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of the business and the community it serves; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business, its contributions to the local and State economies, and its vibrant role in improving the quality of life in its community, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the success it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 20th Anniversary of Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc.; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sherrill Manufacturing, Inc.
