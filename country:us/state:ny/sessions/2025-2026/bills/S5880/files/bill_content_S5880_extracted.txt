Paragraph (d) of subdivision 4 of section 510.10 of the 2 criminal procedure law, as amended by section 2 of part UU of chapter 56 3 of the laws of 2020, is amended to read as follows: 4 (d) a class A felony defined in the penal law[, provided that for 5 class A felonies under article two hundred twenty of the penal law, only 6 class A-I felonies shall be a qualifying offense]; 7 § 2.
