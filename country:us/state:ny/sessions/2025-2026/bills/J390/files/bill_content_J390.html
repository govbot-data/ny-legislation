J00390 Text:

 
Senate Resolution No. 390
 
BY: Senator STEC
 
        HONORING  Monaghan  Medical  Corporation  upon the
        occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2025
        Business of the Year  Award  by  the  North  Country
        Chamber of Commerce
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and  businesses  that   contribute
substantially  to  the  economic  vitality of their communities, thereby
enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Monaghan  Medical  Corporation  upon  the occasion of its designation as
recipient of the 2025 Business of the Year Award by  the  North  Country
Chamber of Commerce at its 113th Annual Awards Dinner on Friday, January
31, 2025, at the Warren Ballroom at SUNY Plattsburgh; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  its  significant contributions to both the healthcare
industry and the North Country community, Monaghan  Medical  Corporation
was named Business of the Year; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  esteemed  company specializes in the development and
manufacture of innovative, high-quality aerosol  drug  delivery  devices
and   respiratory   management   products,  improving  patient  outcomes
worldwide; with the  support  of  a  state-of-the-art  aerosol  research
laboratory,  Monaghan  Medical Corporation continues to set the standard
for advanced, therapeutic, and cost-effective respiratory solutions that
help people breathe easier; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to its industry  leadership,  Monaghan  Medical
Corporation is a committed community ally, consistently supporting local
health  initiatives,  educational  programs,  and  wellness  events; the
company's focus on regional partnerships  and  charitable  contributions
demonstrates   a   strong   dedication   to   public  health,  workforce
development, and economic progress; and
 
  WHEREAS, As an employer of many local  citizens  and  as  a  leading
manufacturer  in its field, Monaghan Medical Corporation's investment in
the area and its inherent benefits to the community at large have proven
invaluable; and
 
  WHEREAS, Businesses that give of their time and  energies  to  serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges this exceptional business  and  its  contributions  to  the
local  and  State  economies,  fully  confident that it will continue to
enjoy  the  sparkling  successes  it  has  experienced  thus  far;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Monaghan Medical Corporation upon the occasion of its  designation
 
as recipient of the 2025 Business of the Year Award by the North Country
Chamber of Commerce; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Monaghan Medical Corporation.
