STEC -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT permitting commercial sales during the Adirondack Family Book Festival at the John Brown Farm State Historic Site in North Elba, Essex County; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any inconsistent provision of law, includ- 2 ing any applicable rules and regulations, the department of environ- 3 mental conservation and/or the office of parks, recreation and historic 4 preservation may allow, on August 16, 2025, the use of the John Brown 5 Farm State Historic Site in North Elba, Essex County, and any structures 6 or improvements thereon, for the sale of books and other related items 7 at the Adirondack Family Book Festival, provided that any such use be 8 subject to reasonable restrictions and other applicable permit require- 9 ments, conditions, and fees. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall expire and be 11 deemed repealed August 17, 2025.
