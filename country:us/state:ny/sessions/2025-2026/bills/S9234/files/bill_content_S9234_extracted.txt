SCARCELLA-SPANTON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Cities 1 AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to requiring the department of transportation to mark certain potholes during paving projects The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The administrative code of the city of New York is amended 2 by adding a new section 19-116.1 to read as follows: 3 § 19-116.1 Marking of potholes during paving projects. During any 4 street paving project undertaken by the department, the department shall 5 mark any pothole deeper than one inch located within the area of such 6 paving project with fluorescent paint. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
