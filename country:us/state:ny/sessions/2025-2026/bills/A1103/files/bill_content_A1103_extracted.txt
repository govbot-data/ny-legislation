Section 11-0305 of the environmental conservation law is 2 amended by adding a new subdivision 19 to read as follows: 3 19. To review, in consultation with the conservation fund advisory 4 board established pursuant to section 11-0327 of this title, existing 5 state policies regarding the provision of free or reduced fee hunting 6 and fishing licenses.
