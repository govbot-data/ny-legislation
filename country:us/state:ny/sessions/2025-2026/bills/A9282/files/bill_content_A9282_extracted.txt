KASSAY -- read once and referred to the Committee on Economic Development AN ACT to amend the cannabis law, in relation to prohibiting the location of cannabis retail locations within two hundred feet of an addiction treatment center The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 3 of the cannabis law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 56 to read as follows: 3 56. "Addiction treatment center" shall mean a facility or program that 4 is: (a) licensed, certified, or otherwise authorized by the office of 5 addiction services and supports to provide clinical treatment, rehabili- 6 tation, or recovery services to individuals with a substance use disor- 7 der, gambling disorder or other addictive condition; or (b) operated 8 pursuant to the mental hygiene law for the treatment of individuals with 9 mental illness or chemical dependence, including residential, inpatient, 10 outpatient, or medically supervised withdrawal services. For the 11 purposes of this subdivision, "addiction treatment center" shall not 12 include peer support meetings, mutual aid groups, or other non-clinical 13 gatherings not licensed or certified to provide addiction or mental 14 health treatment services under state law. 15 § 2.
