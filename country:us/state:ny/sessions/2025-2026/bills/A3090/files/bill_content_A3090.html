        AN  ACT  to  amend  the  executive law, in relation to the creation of a
          "purple alert system" for missing victims of domestic violence

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 837-y
     2  to read as follows:
     3    § 837-y. Purple alert system; missing victims of domestic violence. 1.
     4  With the cooperation of the department of transportation, the office  of
     5  the  governor,  and  other  appropriate  law enforcement agencies in the
     6  state, the division shall develop and implement a statewide purple alert
     7  system to be activated  on  behalf  of  a  missing  victim  of  domestic
     8  violence,  as  defined in subdivision one of section four hundred fifty-
     9  nine-a of the social services law, who has elected to participate in the
    10  purple alert system.
    11    2. The commissioner shall designate a  statewide  coordinator  of  the
    12  purple alert system. The commissioner shall adopt rules and issue direc-
    13  tives  as  necessary to ensure proper implementation of the purple alert
    14  system.  The rules and directives shall include:
    15    (a) the procedures to be used by a law enforcement  agency  to  verify
    16  whether a victim of domestic violence is missing;
    17    (b)  a  description of the circumstances under which a law enforcement
    18  agency is required to report a missing victim of  domestic  violence  to
    19  the division;
    20    (c)  the  procedures  to  be used by an individual or entity to report
    21  information about a missing victim of domestic  violence  to  designated
    22  media outlets in the state;
    23    (d)  guidelines  for releasing only the information necessary in order
    24  to protect the privacy of a missing victim of domestic violence for whom

     7    3.  (a) The purple alert system shall include all state agencies capa-

     9  with  broadcasters,  private  commercial  entities  and  state  or local
    10  governmental entities who volunteer to participate in the  dissemination
    11  of urgent public information.
    12    (b)  Such alert system shall include, but shall not be limited to, the
    13  division, the New York lottery and  the  department  of  transportation,
    14  which  shall  establish  a  plan for providing information to the public
    15  through an existing system of dynamic message signs located  across  the
    16  state.
    17    4.  A  law  enforcement agency shall notify the division if the agency
    18  receives a report regarding a missing victim of domestic violence who is
    19  determined by the agency to be a participant in the purple alert system.
    20    5. (a) When a law enforcement agency notifies the division, the  divi-
    21  sion  shall  confirm  the accuracy of the information and, if confirmed,
    22  immediately issue an alert under this section in accordance  with  divi-
    23  sion rules.
    24    (b)  In  issuing the alert, the division shall send the alert to media
    25  outlets in the state, in  accordance  with  subdivision  three  of  this
    26  section. Following receipt of the alert, participating media outlets may
    27  issue  the alert at designated intervals to assist in locating the miss-
    28  ing victim of domestic violence.
    29    (c) Upon activation of the  purple  alert  system,  the  division  may
    30  request  interstate  activation in a neighboring state or Canadian prov-
    31  ince if warranted. If an activation area is adjacent to  a  border,  the
    32  United  States Customs and Border Patrol and Canadian Customs shall also
    33  receive notification.
    34    (d) The alert must include:
    35    (i) all appropriate information that is provided by the  law  enforce-
    36  ment agency under this section and that may lead to the safe recovery of
    37  the missing victim of domestic violence; and
    38    (ii)  a  statement  instructing any person with information related to
    39  the missing victim of domestic violence to contact  the  applicable  law
    40  enforcement agency.
    41    (e)  The commissioner shall terminate any activation of the alert with
    42  respect to a particular missing victim of domestic  violence  not  later
    43  than the earlier of the date on which:
    44    (i)  the  missing victim of domestic violence is located, or the situ-
    45  ation is otherwise resolved; or
    46    (ii) the notification period ends, as  determined  by  the  division's
    47  rules.
    48    (f) A law enforcement agency that locates a missing victim of domestic
    49  violence  who is the subject of an alert under this section shall notify
    50  the division as soon as possible that the  missing  victim  of  domestic
    51  violence has been located.
    52    6.  The department of transportation is not required to use any exist-
    53  ing system of dynamic message signs in a statewide alert system  created
    54  under  this  section if the department of transportation receives notice
    55  from the United States  Department  of  Transportation  Federal  Highway
    56  Administration  that  the  use  of the signs would result in the loss of
