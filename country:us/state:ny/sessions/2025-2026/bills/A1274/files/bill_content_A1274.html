        AN ACT to amend the education  law,  in  relation  to  establishing  the
          "Purple  Star School Program" to emphasize the importance of assisting
          military-connected students and to recognize  the  value  of  military
          service and civic responsibility
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. This act shall be known and may be  cited  as  the  "Purple
     2  Star School Program Act".
     3    § 2. The education law is amended by adding a new article 66-A to read
     4  as follows:
     5                                ARTICLE 66-A
     6                         PURPLE STAR SCHOOL PROGRAM
     7  Section 3330. Purple star school program.
     8    §  3330. Purple star school program. 1. The commissioner, in consulta-
     9  tion with the state council  created  pursuant  to  subdivision  one  of
    10  section  thirty-three  hundred  eight of this title, shall establish the
    11  "Purple Star School Program" to recognize schools and  school  districts
    12  which  demonstrate a commitment to assisting military-connected students
    13  and their families.
    14    2. As used in  this  section,  "military-connected  student"  means  a
    15  school-aged  child enrolled in kindergarten through twelfth grade in the
    16  household of an active duty military member as that term is  defined  in
    17  subdivision  one  of  section  thirty-three hundred two of this title, a
    18  school-aged child enrolled in kindergarten through twelfth grade in  the
    19  household  of a member of the military reserves, or national guard, or a
    20  school-aged child in the household of a recently  discharged  member  of

    35  field trip, or participate in such other educational activities  as  the
    36  commissioner or the commissioner's designee deem appropriate.
