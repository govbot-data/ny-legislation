        Introduced by M. of A. PAULIN -- read once and referred to the Committee
          on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions
 
        AN  ACT  to  amend the limited liability company law and the partnership
          law, in relation to the  definition  of  office  and  the  content  of
          notices published in newspapers

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision (s) of section 102  of  the  limited  liability
     2  company law is amended to read as follows:
     3    (s)  "Office of the limited liability company" means the office of the
     4  limited liability company, the location of which is stated in the  arti-
     5  cles  of organization of a domestic limited liability company, or in the
     6  application for authority of a foreign limited liability  company.  Such
     7  office  [need  not be a place where business activities are conducted by
     8  such limited liability company] shall be a place in  the  county  within
     9  this state of the principal and actual business location of the company.
    10  In no event shall it only be the address of its registered agent, a post
    11  office  mailbox,  or  a  private  mailbox at a mail logistics center for
    12  purposes of satisfying the requirements  of  section  two  hundred  six,
    13  eight  hundred two, one thousand two hundred three or one thousand three
    14  hundred six of this chapter.
    15    § 2. Paragraph 3-a of subdivision (a) of section 206  of  the  limited
    16  liability  company law, as amended by chapter 44 of the laws of 2006, is
    17  amended to read as follows:
    18    (3-a) the street address of the principal business location[, if any];
    19    § 3. Subparagraph 4-a of paragraph (i) of subdivision (b)  of  section
    20  802  of  the  limited liability company law, as amended by chapter 44 of
    21  the laws of 2006, is amended to read as follows:
    22    (4-a) the street address of the principal business location[, if any];
