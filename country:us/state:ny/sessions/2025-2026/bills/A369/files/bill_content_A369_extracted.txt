Subdivision (c) of section 304-a of the vehicle and traffic 2 law, as added by chapter 61 of the laws of 1989, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 (c) The commissioner may, by regulation, establish standards of compe- 5 tency and fitness for certification as an inspector.
