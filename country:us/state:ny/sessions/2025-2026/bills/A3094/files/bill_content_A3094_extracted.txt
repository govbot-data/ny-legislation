The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive "Blue Star Mother" and "Blue Star Family" 4 license plates. 1. Any person residing in this state whose child, 5 spouse, parent, sibling, or grandchild is actively serving in the armed 6 forces of the United States shall, upon request, be issued a distinctive 7 license plate bearing the words "Blue Star Mother" or "Blue Star Fami- 8 ly", respectively. Application for said license plate shall be filed 9 with the form and detail as the commissioner shall prescribe. 10 2.
