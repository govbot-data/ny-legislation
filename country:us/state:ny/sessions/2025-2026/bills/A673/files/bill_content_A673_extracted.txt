CRUZ, BORES, ROSENTHAL, KELLES, JACKSON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Codes -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the criminal procedure law, in relation to facilitating appellate review of rulings that implicate issues of public concern The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 710.70 of the criminal procedure 2 law is amended to read as follows: 3 2. An order finally denying a motion to suppress evidence [may] shall 4 be [reviewed] reviewable upon an appeal raised by the defendant from an 5 ensuing judgment of conviction notwithstanding the fact that such judg- 6 ment is entered upon a plea of guilty and not withstanding an otherwise 7 enforceable waiver of the right to appeal. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth day after it shall 9 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
