and WHEREAS, The Queen Geniuses Annual Conference has consistently provided a platform for young women, ages 15 to 19, to develop leadership skills, enhance social and emotional intelligence, and engage in transformative personal growth; and WHEREAS, On Saturday, May 3, 2025, the 7th Annual Empowerment Conference, themed "Worthiness is M.E.S.S.Y.," will continue this tradition by offering interactive workshops, dynamic activities, and insightful discussions aimed at fostering self-worth, resilience, and empowerment among participants; and WHEREAS, The conference's dedication to addressing relevant social issues through transparent and powerful dialogues equips attendees with practical tools to navigate their futures with confidence and purpose; and WHEREAS, The commitment of the conference to uplift and motivate young women aligns with the values of fostering a supportive environment where youth can connect, share ideas, and be inspired to create positive change in their communities; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the Queen Geniuses 7th Annual Empowerment Conference: "Worthiness is M.E.S.S.Y." on May 3, 2025, and to commend the organizers, speakers, and participants of the Queen Geniuses 7th Annual Empowerment Conference for their unwavering dedication to the empowerment of young women; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the organizers of the Queen Geniuses 7th Annual Empowerment Conference as an expression of our esteem and best wishes for continued success in their noble endeavors.
