REILLY -- read once and referred to the Committee on Cities AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to permits required for the removal of trees The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivisions b, c, and e of section 18-107 of the adminis- 2 trative code of the city of New York, subdivisions b and c as added by 3 local law number 3 of the city of New York for the year 2010, subdivi- 4 sion e as amended by local law number 141 of the city of New York for 5 the year 2021, are amended to read as follows: 6 b. The department shall charge a fee for each permit issued pursuant 7 to this section, which shall [be sufficient to cover the cost of replac- 8 ing any tree proposed to be removed] not exceed two dollars per square 9 foot on all properties. In calculating such fee the department shall 10 use parcel dimensions on file with the county clerk to determine the 11 total square footage of the lot. In applying for a permit pursuant to 12 this section, a person shall specify whether such person intends to 13 plant replacement trees as directed by the department or have the 14 department plant replacement trees. If replacement trees are to be 15 planted by the person applying for the permit and are planted as 16 directed by the department, then the department shall return such fee to 17 such person upon completion of such planting. In all other cases, the 18 department shall retain such fee for purposes of planting replacement 19 trees. 20 c. Upon determining that a person may be issued a permit pursuant to 21 this section, the department shall inform such person in writing of the 22 fee required, the number and size of the replacement trees to be plant- 23 ed, [the method used in making these calculations,] and the period of 24 time prescribed by subdivision d or f of this section during which 25 replacement trees shall be planted.
