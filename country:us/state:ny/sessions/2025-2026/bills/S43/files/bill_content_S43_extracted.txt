WALCZYK, BORRELLO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Environmental Conservation AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to shoreline hardening The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a 2 new section 16-0121 to read as follows: 3 § 16-0121. Shoreline hardening. 4 1. For purposes of this section, the following terms shall have the 5 following meanings: 6 (a) "Shoreline" shall mean the line along which a large body of water 7 meets the land. 8 (b) "Shoreline resident" or "resident" shall mean any resident of the 9 state who owns property which is situated on a shoreline on Lake Ontario 10 or the St. Lawrence River which is prone to flooding. 11 (c) "Shoreline hardening" shall mean the process of adding materials 12 or structures to property located on a shoreline on Lake Ontario or the 13 St. Lawrence River in order to minimize damage caused by flooding. 14 2. (a) The department shall provide oversight and assist with the 15 implementation of shoreline hardening construction plans for shoreline 16 residents along Lake Ontario and the St. Lawrence river. Shoreline resi- 17 dents shall work in conjunction with the department to develop a design 18 plan for each property located on a shoreline along Lake Ontario and the 19 St. Lawrence river. Such design plan shall be subject to final approval 20 by the department prior to any construction work being commenced on a 21 property.
