BRABENEC -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Local Governments AN ACT to amend the general municipal law, in relation to liability for outside aid The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 1 of section 209-g of the general municipal 2 law, as amended by section 9 of part A of chapter 101 of the laws of 3 2013, is amended to read as follows: 4 1. Notwithstanding any inconsistent provision of law, general, special 5 or local, any county, city, town, village or fire district requesting 6 fire aid pursuant to section two hundred nine-e of this article or any 7 county, city, town or village requesting police aid pursuant to section 8 two hundred nine-f of this article, shall be liable and responsible to 9 the assisting municipal corporation or fire district for any loss of or 10 damage, which it is deemed to be responsible for, to apparatus or equip- 11 ment or supplies and shall bear and pay the expense incurred in the 12 operation and maintenance of any such apparatus or equipment and the 13 cost of materials and supplies used or consumed in rendering such aid 14 and assistance, but such liability and responsibility shall not apply or 15 extend to apparatus, equipment, materials and supplies owned or supplied 16 by the state. 17 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
