and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor The Training Edge upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the People's Choice Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated at its 53rd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 19, 2026, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, The Training Edge is a martial arts school whose mission is to foster a family environment that develops strong character rooted in faith, hope, and love by inspiring students to believe in a better future and find the strength to overcome life's challenges; and WHEREAS, The Training Edge has been teaching martial arts in the greater Clarence community since 1996, with this year marking the momentous 30-year anniversary of the business; and WHEREAS, A core focus of The Training Edge is to teach its karate students aged 5-12 years old how to develop a positive attitude toward themselves and others, exhibit self-control and a non-quitting spirit, and how to develop their character based on honesty and integrity; and WHEREAS, Students 13-years-old and up can learn Krav Maga, an approachable and practical, real-world self-defense system that teaches simple, effective techniques that work under stress, regardless of size, strength, or experience; and WHEREAS, The teachings of The Training Edge go beyond the physical; instructors aim to build a strong and well-balanced body and mind relationship, teaching mental toughness and helping students build confidence, awareness, and resilience, which serve them both on and off the mat; and WHEREAS, The Clarence Chamber of Commerce's designation of The Training Edge as recipient of the People's Choice Award is a fitting tribute to an organization whose passion, care for others, and unwavering service have strengthened the Clarence community and enriched the lives of countless individuals and families; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor The Training Edge upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the People's Choice Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 19, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to The Training Edge of Williamsville, New York.
