BOTTCHER, ASHBY, BROUK, MAY, MAYER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Procurement and Contracts -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to requiring certain state owned and operated parking facilities to install charging stations for electric vehicles The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 201-b 2 to read as follows: 3 § 201-b. Electric vehicle charging stations. 1. Definitions. For the 4 purposes of this section: 5 a. "parking facility" shall mean a parking garage, parking structure, 6 or open parking lot that is paved and striped to designate individual 7 parking spaces for vehicles. 8 b. "electric vehicle ready infrastructure" shall have the same meaning 9 as in subparagraph (i) of paragraph (a) of subdivision nine of section 10 11-104 of the energy law. 11 c. "electric vehicle charging station" shall have the same meaning as 12 in subparagraph (ii) of paragraph (a) of subdivision nine of section 13 11-104 of the energy law. 14 d. "power-based allocation method" shall mean a compliance approach 15 that allows electric vehicle charging infrastructure requirements to be 16 met based on the total kilowatt power capacity available for charging 17 electric vehicles, without mandating a specific number or type of charg- 18 ing stations. 19 2. Any parking facility owned and operated by the state of New York, 20 that offers fifty or more parking spaces for the purposes of public use, 21 state employee use and pay-per-usage, is hereby required to install a 22 minimum quantity of electric vehicle charging stations based on a 23 percentage of the total number of parking spaces offered by such facili- 24 ty or computed using the power-based allocation method, as set forth in 25 rules and regulations promulgated by the office of general services.
