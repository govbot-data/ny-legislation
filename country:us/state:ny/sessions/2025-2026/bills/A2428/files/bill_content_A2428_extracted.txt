The public health law is amended by adding a new section 2 267-c to read as follows: 3 § 267-c. Menstrual products in private colleges and universities. All 4 private colleges and universities in the state shall provide menstrual 5 products in the restrooms of such school building or buildings. Such 6 products shall be provided at no charge to students. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
