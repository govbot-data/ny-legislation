AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to authority for
existing assisted living programs to expand capacity by nine or fewer
beds under an expedited process
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Paragraph (k) of subdivision 3 of section 461-l of the
2 social services law, as added by section 2 of part B of chapter 57 of
3 the laws of 2018, is amended to read as follows:
4 (k) (i) Existing assisted living program providers may apply to the
5 department of health for approval to add up to nine additional assisted
6 living program beds that do not require major renovation or
7 construction. Eligible applicants are those that agree to dedicate such
8 beds to serve only individuals receiving medical assistance, are in good
9 standing with the department of health, and are in compliance with
10 appropriate state and local requirements as determined by the department
11 of health.
12 (ii) Existing assisted living program providers licensed on or before
13 April first, two thousand eighteen may submit applications under this
14 paragraph beginning no later than June thirtieth, two thousand eighteen
15 and until a deadline to be determined by the department of health.
16 Existing assisted living program providers licensed on or before April
17 first, two thousand twenty may submit such applications beginning no
18 later than June thirtieth, two thousand twenty and until a deadline to
19 be determined by the department of health.
20 (iii) Notwithstanding paragraph (m) of this subdivision, existing
21 assisted living program providers licensed on or before April first, two
