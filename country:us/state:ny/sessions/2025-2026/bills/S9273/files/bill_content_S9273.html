S09273 Summary:

BILL NOS09273
 
SAME ASSAME AS A10620
 
SPONSORKRUEGER
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Rpld §105 sub 25, amd §§105 & 106, ABC L
 
Exempts certain parcels of land from licensing restrictions prohibiting manufacturers, wholesalers and retailers of alcoholic beverages from sharing an interest in a licensed premises and from selling at retail for consumption on and off the premises.

        Introduced  by  Sen. KRUEGER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when

        AN ACT to amend the alcoholic beverage control law, in relation  to  the
          exemption  of  certain  parcels  of  land  from licensing restrictions
          prohibiting manufacturers,  wholesalers  and  retailers  of  alcoholic
          beverages  from  sharing an interest in a licensed premises or to sell
          at retail for consumption off and on the premises; to  repeal  certain
          provisions  of such law relating thereto; and providing for the repeal
          of such provisions upon expiration thereof
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivision  25  of section 105 of the alcoholic beverage
     2  control law is REPEALED and a new subdivision 25 is  added  to  read  as
     3  follows:
     4    25.  (a)  The provisions of subdivisions sixteen and seventeen of this
     5  section shall not apply to any interest  in  a  manufacturer  inside  or
     6  outside the United States, held by any business or entity located on any
     7  parcels of land in the Borough of Manhattan, City and State of New York,
     8  bounded and described as follows:
     9    Beginning at a point on the northerly side of West 48th Street distant
    10  200  feet  westerly  from  the  corner formed by the intersection of the
    11  north side of West 48th Street with  the  west  side  of  Fifth  Avenue;
    12  running  thence  westerly  along  the north side of West 48th Street 175
    13  feet to the westerly side of Rockefeller Plaza; thence  northerly  along
    14  the  westerly  side of Rockefeller Plaza 200 feet 10 inches to the south
    15  side of West 49th Street; thence easterly along the south side  of  West
    16  49th  Street 150 feet to a point distant 225 feet westerly from the west
    17  side of Fifth Avenue; thence southerly parallel with  Fifth  Avenue  and
    18  for  part of the distance through a party wall 100 feet 5 inches; thence
    19  easterly parallel with West  49th  Street,  25  feet;  thence  southerly
    20  parallel  with Fifth Avenue, 100 feet 5 inches to the north side of West
    21  48th Street at the place of beginning.
    22    (b) Provided, however, that with respect  to  such  retail  licensee's
    23  interest in a business engaged in the manufacture of alcoholic beverages
    24  described in subdivision sixteen or seventeen of this section:

     1    (i) such interest shall have been acquired prior to the effective date

    35    §  3.  This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that
    36  upon the sale or transfer of the parcels detailed in  paragraph  (a)  of
    37  subdivision  25 of section 105 or subparagraph (i) of paragraph (a-2) of
    38  subdivision 13 of section 106 of the alcoholic beverage control law,  as
    39  added  by  sections one and two of this act, respectively, such subdivi-
    40  sion 25 and paragraph (a-2), respectively, shall expire  and  be  deemed

    43  licensee  obtains  such  a leasehold, then subdivision 25 of section 105
    44  and paragraph (a-2) of subdivision 13 of section 106, of  the  alcoholic
    45  beverage  control  law,  pertaining  to  such parcel shall expire and be
    46  deemed repealed upon the end or termination of  such  lease.  The  state
    47  liquor  authority  shall notify the legislative bill drafting commission
    48  upon notification by the licensee of the sale, transfer  or  termination
    49  of the leasehold, or non-renewal of the leasehold of the parcel detailed
    50  in  paragraph  (a) of subdivision 25 of section 105, or subparagraph (i)
    51  of paragraph (a-2) of subdivision 13 of section 106,  of  the  alcoholic
    52  beverage control law, in order that the commission may maintain an accu-
    53  rate  and timely effective data base of the official text of the laws of
    54  the state of New York in furtherance of effectuating the  provisions  of
    55  section  44  of the legislative law and section 70-b of the public offi-
    56  cers law.
