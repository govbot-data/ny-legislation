PHEFFER AMATO -- read once and referred to the Committee on Racing and Wagering AN ACT to amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, in relation to enacting the "gamblers unfairly abused by reward dynamics (GUARD) act" The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "gamblers unfairly abused by reward dynamics (GUARD) act". 3 § 2. Subparagraphs (xiv) and (xv) of paragraph (a) of subdivision 4 of 4 section 1367-a of the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, as 5 added by section 4 of part Y of chapter 59 of the laws of 2021, are 6 amended and a new subparagraph (xvi) is added to read as follows: 7 (xiv) maintain a publicly accessible internet page dedicated to 8 responsible play, a link to which must appear on the mobile sports 9 wagering operator's website and in any mobile application or electronic 10 platform on which a bettor may place wagers. The responsible play page 11 shall include: a statement of the mobile sports wagering operator's 12 policy and commitment to responsible gaming; information regarding, or 13 links to information regarding, the risks associated with gambling and 14 the potential signs of problem gaming; the availability of self-imposed 15 responsible gaming limits; a link to a problem gaming webpage maintained 16 by the office of addiction services and supports; and such other infor- 17 mation or statements as the commission may require by rule; [and] 18 (xv) submit annually a problem gaming plan that was approved by the 19 commission in consultation with the office of addiction services and 20 supports that includes: the objectives of and timetables for implement- 21 ing the plan; identification of the persons responsible for implementing 22 and maintaining the plan; procedures for identifying users with 23 suspected or known problem gaming behavior; procedures for providing 24 information to users concerning problem gaming identification and 25 resources; procedures to prevent gaming by minors and self-excluded 26 persons; and such other problem gaming information as the commission may 27 require by rule[.]; and EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
