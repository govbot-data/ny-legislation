and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend One Hundred Black Men, Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its 45th Annual Days of the One Hundred Benefit Gala, to be held on Thursday, March 20, 2025, at Pier Sixty, Chelsea Piers, New York City; and WHEREAS, Established in 1963, One Hundred Black Men, Inc. is an esteemed organization dedicated to improving the quality of life within communities of color by fostering opportunities in education, mentorship, economic empowerment, health and wellness, and leadership development; and WHEREAS, For more than six decades, One Hundred Black Men, Inc. has inspired hope, uplifted communities, and championed initiatives designed to provide equitable access to opportunities for all, particularly those who are underserved and underrepresented; and WHEREAS, The Days of the One Hundred Benefit Gala serves as the organization's premier annual event, convening influential leaders from the fields of business, government, education, media, and the arts for an evening of celebration, recognition, and philanthropy; and WHEREAS, Funds raised during this signature event support vital programs, including the Dr. Marcella Maxwell and Betty King Obiajulu Scholarship Program, initiatives in mentoring, economic empowerment, health and wellness, and the promotion of academic excellence among youth; and WHEREAS, This year's gala celebrates both the remarkable more than 60 years of service by One Hundred Black Men, Inc. and its unwavering dedication to building pathways to success, while honoring the corporate, civic, and community leaders who exemplify the principles of positive change, leadership, and service; and WHEREAS, The extraordinary success of One Hundred Black Men, Inc. is in direct correlation to the dedication and vision of its leadership, members, and supporters, whose enduring commitment to their mission has fostered meaningful change throughout New York and the nation; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and recognize the achievements of One Hundred Black Men, Inc., and to extend its highest commendation to this exceptional organization which has dedicated itself to empowering communities and uplifting generations; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend One Hundred Black Men, Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its 45th Annual Days of the One Hundred Benefit Gala and to celebrate its profound contributions to education, equity, and excellence; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to One Hundred Black Men, Inc.
