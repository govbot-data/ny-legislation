PARKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Veterans, Homeland Securi- ty and Military Affairs AN ACT to amend the civil service law, in relation to the use of credits for veterans and disabled veterans The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 4 of section 85 of the civil service law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph (e) to read as follows: 3 (e) Every person who has served in more than one time of war shall be 4 entitled to the additional credit provided herein as a veteran or disa- 5 bled veteran, in competitive examinations for original appointment or 6 promotion for every such time of war during which such person served, 7 regardless of whether any such person has utilized such credit for a 8 competitive examination for original appointment or a competitive exam- 9 ination for promotion. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
