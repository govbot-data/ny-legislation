Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "CALM act". 3 § 2. The general business law is amended by adding a new section 392-m 4 to read as follows: 5 § 392-m. Commercial advertisement volume. 1. For the purposes of this 6 section, the following terms shall have the following meanings: 7 (a) "Video programming" shall have the same meaning as defined in 8 Section 613(h) of Title 47 of the United States Code. 9 (b) (i) "Video streaming service" shall mean an entity that makes 10 available directly to the consumer, through a distribution method that 11 uses internet protocol, either of the following: 12 (1) video programming; or 13 (2) video content such entity makes available for users to view. 14 (ii) "Video streaming service" shall not include a television broad- 15 cast station, cable operator, or other multichannel video programming 16 distributor, or an entity that services video programming or video 17 content without commercial advertisements. 18 2. No person, firm, partnership, association or corporation providing 19 a video streaming service that operates in this state shall transmit the 20 audio of commercial advertisements louder than the video content the 21 advertisements accompany, consistent with the regulations adopted by the 22 federal communications commission pursuant to the commercial advertise- 23 ment loudness mitigation act for television broadcast stations, cable 24 operators, and other multichannel video programming distributors. 25 3.
