COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the civil rights law, the general municipal law and the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to requiring universally accessible playgrounds in New York city and state parks The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The civil rights law is amended by adding a new section 2 79-r to read as follows: 3 § 79-r. Universally accessible playground equipment. 1. Pursuant to 4 section forty of this chapter, all playgrounds in state parks shall be 5 universally accessible. To the extent practicable, such accessible play- 6 ground equipment shall be incorporated into the layout of the existing 7 playground or proposed playground plan. 8 2. For the purposes of this section, the following terms shall have 9 the following meanings: 10 (a) "Disability" shall have the same meaning as defined in section two 11 hundred ninety-two of the executive law. 12 (b) "Universally accessible" shall mean that children with disabili- 13 ties can use their typical means of mobility to access a minimum of 14 seventy percent of play activities. 15 § 2.
