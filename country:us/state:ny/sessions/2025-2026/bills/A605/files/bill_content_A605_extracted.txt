BUTTENSCHON, JACKSON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to authorizing the issuance of licenses and non-driver identification cards contain- ing a Lifetime Liberty Pass distinguishing mark The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 1 of section 504 of the vehicle and traffic law 2 is amended by adding a new paragraph (a-2) to read as follows: 3 (a-2) Every license or renewal thereof issued to an applicant who: 4 (i) was a member of the armed forces of the United States; (ii) received 5 an honorable discharge or was released therefrom under honorable condi- 6 tions; and (iii) qualifies for and has received a Lifetime Liberty Pass 7 permitting qualified veterans with disabilities discounted use of state 8 parks, historic sites, and recreational facilities operated by the 9 office of parks, recreation and historic preservation and the state 10 department of environmental conservation, shall, upon such person's 11 request and submission of proof as set forth herein, contain a distin- 12 guishing mark, in such form as the commissioner shall determine, indi- 13 cating that such person is a holder of a Lifetime Liberty Pass. Such 14 proof shall consist of a certificate of release or discharge from active 15 duty including but not limited to a DD Form 214, a copy of such person's 16 Lifetime Liberty Pass or other proof satisfactory to the commissioner. 17 The commissioner shall not require fees for the issuance of such 18 licenses or renewals thereof to persons requesting a Lifetime Liberty 19 Pass distinguishing mark which are different from fees otherwise 20 required; provided, however, that notwithstanding the provisions of this 21 section, the commissioner shall not require fees for a duplication or 22 amendment of a license prior to its renewal if such duplication or 23 amendment was solely for the purpose of adding to such license. Such proof shall consist of a certificate of release or discharge 15 from active duty including but not limited to a DD Form 214, a copy of 16 such person's Lifetime Liberty Pass or other proof satisfactory to the 17 commissioner. The commissioner shall not require fees for the issuance 18 of such identification cards or renewals thereof to persons requesting a 19 Lifetime Liberty Pass distinguishing mark which are different from fees 20 otherwise required; provided, however, that notwithstanding the 21 provisions of this section, the commissioner shall not require fees for 22 a duplication or amendment of an identification card prior to its 23 renewal if such duplication or amendment was solely for the purpose of 24 adding a Lifetime Liberty Pass distinguishing mark to such identifica- 25 tion card. 26 § 3.
