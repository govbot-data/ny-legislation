This act shall be known and may be cited as "AJ's Law". 2 § 2. This act enacts into law major components of legislation neces- 3 sary to implement certain provisions regarding the donation of anatom- 4 ical gifts. Each component is wholly contained within a Part identified 5 as Parts A through E. The effective date for each particular provision 6 contained within such Part is set forth in the last section of such 7 Part.
