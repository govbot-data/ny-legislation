HELMING -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to issuance of distinctive plates for members of the Red Knights International Motor- cycle Club The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive plates for members of the Red Knights Interna- 4 tional Motorcycle Club. 1. Any member of the Red Knights International 5 Motorcycle Club residing in this state shall, upon request, be issued a 6 license plate bearing the words "Red Knights International Motorcycle 7 Club". Application for such license plate shall be filed with the 8 commissioner in such form and detail as the commissioner shall 9 prescribe. 10 2. The distinctive plate authorized pursuant to this section shall be 11 issued upon proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that the applicant 12 is a member of the Red Knights International Motorcycle Club. 13 3. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 14 in the same manner as other number plates upon payment of the regular 15 registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article, 16 provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of thirty 17 dollars shall be charged for such plate. 18 § 2. 1. A distinctive plate established pursuant to section 404-ff of 19 the vehicle and traffic law as added by section one of this act shall 20 only be designed, produced and issued upon the delivery to the depart- 21 ment of motor vehicles of a surety bond in the amount of six thousand 22 dollars, which shall be executed by a surety company authorized by the 23 department of financial services to transact business in this state. If fewer than two hundred sets of 16 such plates are sold by such time, the department of motor vehicles 17 shall be entitled to recover against the bond in an amount proportionate 18 to such shortfall. 19 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that 20 section one of this act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth 21 day after it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the addi- 22 tion, amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for 23 the implementation of this act on its effective date are authorized to 24 be made and completed on or before such effective date.
