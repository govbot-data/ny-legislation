AN ACT to amend the highway law and the vehicle and traffic law, in
relation to enacting "Alexander John Smullen Traffic Safety Memorial
Law"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "Alexander John Smullen Traffic Safety Memorial Law".
3 § 2. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 24 to read as
4 follows:
5 § 24. Memorial sign program for collision victims. 1. The commissioner
6 shall establish and administer a memorial sign program to publicly memo-
7 rialize the victims of fatal vehicle collisions.
8 2. As used in this section, "victim" means a person killed in a high-
9 way collision.
10 3. A sign designed and posted under this section shall include:
11 (a) the phrase "Please Don't Drink and Drive", "Please Don't Drive
12 Impaired", "Please Don't Text and Drive", "Please Don't Speed", "Please
13 Buckle Up", "Please Drive Sober", "Please Drive Safely", "Please Ride
14 Safely", "Please Watch for Bicyclists", "Please Watch for Motorcycles",
15 "Please Watch for Pedestrians", "Reckless Driving Costs Lives", "Seat
16 Belts Save Lives", "Slow Down for Workers", or "Stay Alert Drive Safe";
17 (b) the phrase "In Memory Of" and the name of one or more victims in
18 accordance with the rules promulgated by the department; and
19 (c) the date of the collision that resulted in the victim's death.
20 4. The sign may include the names of more than one victim so long as
21 the total length of the names does not exceed one line of text.
22 5. A person may request that a sign be posted under this section by:
