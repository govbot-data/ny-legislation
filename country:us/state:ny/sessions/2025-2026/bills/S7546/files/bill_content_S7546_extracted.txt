Subdivision 2 of section 380.50 of the criminal procedure 2 law is amended by adding a new paragraph (g) to read as follows: 3 (g) Following the acquittal after trial or the sentencing of any 4 defendant for a felony, the district attorney, or their designee, shall 5 mail a written summary of the disposition of such felony to any victim 6 who was not present at the time such defendant was sentenced or acquit- 7 ted after trial. 8 § 2.
