PERSAUD -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the civil rights law, in relation to prohibiting tran- scripts being made from video conference meetings by artificial intel- ligence without conspicuous disclosure during such meeting The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The civil rights law is amended by adding a new section 2 50-h to read as follows: 3 § 50-h. Right to privacy; transcription. No person shall permit a 4 video conference meeting to be transcribed by artificial intelligence 5 without such meeting having a conspicuous disclosure during such meeting 6 that every person who attended such meeting may have their words from 7 such meeting transcribed by artificial intelligence. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect on the thirtieth day after it shall 9 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
