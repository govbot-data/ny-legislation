AN ACT in relation to a feasibility study and report on the potential
reestablishment of the empire state summer games
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. (a) The commissioner of parks, recreation and historic
2 preservation, in conjunction with the department of economic develop-
3 ment, shall conduct a feasibility study and prepare a report on the
4 potential reestablishment of the empire state summer games. Such study
5 shall explore the overall costs of reestablishing the empire state
6 summer games, as well as costs associated with the marketing of such
7 games through the department of economic development. Such study shall
8 also explore the potential revenue generated by hosting and marketing
9 the empire state summer games.
10 (b) On or before December 31, 2026, the commissioner of parks, recre-
11 ation and historic preservation and the department of economic develop-
12 ment shall issue a report of the findings of the feasibility study to
13 the governor, the temporary president of the senate, and the speaker of
14 the assembly.
15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
