Formation; duration; non-contestability. 1. The governing 27 bodies of any two or more municipalities hereby authorized to form a 28 broadband union district for the creation, maintenance and adminis- 29 tration of broadband infrastructure and delivery of broadband services, 30 which district shall be a body politic and corporate, provided that all 31 of the territory of such broadband union district shall be contiguous. A 32 municipality that is a county shall not be prohibited from forming a 33 broadband union district with a municipality that is a city, town or 34 village located within such county. 35 2. (a) To form a broadband union district, the governing body of each 36 municipality seeking to form such district shall approve a resolution or 37 local law so authorizing the formation of such district.
