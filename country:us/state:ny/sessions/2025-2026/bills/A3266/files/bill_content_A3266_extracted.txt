Section 606 of the tax law is amended by adding a new 2 subsection (qqq) to read as follows: 3 (qqq) Fine reimbursement credit. (1) For taxable years beginning Janu- 4 ary first, two thousand twenty and ending before January first, two 5 thousand twenty-six, a taxpayer who has been levied a fine between March 6 fifteenth, two thousand twenty and April fifteenth, two thousand twen- 7 ty-one for a violation of executive order number two hundred two of two 8 thousand twenty or any subsequent order relating to the operation of a 9 bar or restaurant during the COVID-19 pandemic shall be allowed a credit 10 against the tax imposed by this article in the amount of such fine.
