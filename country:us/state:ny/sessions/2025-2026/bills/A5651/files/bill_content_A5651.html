Amd §§8-0111, 70-0109 & 8-0109, En Con L; amd §14.07, Pks & Rec L; add §99-ss, St Fin L
 
Relates to the environmental assessment of a historic preservation plan; addresses environmental impact statements, dates for filing drafts of such statewide comprehensive historic preservation plan, funds available for historic preservation purposes, and the New York state archaeological survey assistance fund.

    27    §  99-ss. The New York state archaeological survey assistance fund. 1.
    28  There is hereby established in the joint custody of the comptroller  and

    35  administration of such fund shall be  the  sole  responsibility  of  the
    36  commissioner  of  the office of parks, recreation and historic preserva-
    37  tion.
    38    2. The fund will consist of appropriated federal funds for the purpose
    39  of archaeological surveying or appropriated state funds for the  purpose
    40  of archaeological surveying.

    42  the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation,  in  conjunc-
