and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Sunshine Chapter No. 34, Order of the Eastern Star upon the occasion of hosting its "Sisterhood in Bloom: A Five Point Tea Floral Celebration," an event to be held on Sunday, April 27, 2025, which honors the principles of sisterhood, leadership, and service; and WHEREAS, As a distinguished chapter within the Order of the Eastern Star, Sunshine Chapter No. 34 has long been a pillar of community engagement, charitable service, and fellowship, dedicated to uplifting women and strengthening their commitment to faith, family, and philanthropy; and WHEREAS, The Five Point Tea Floral Celebration serves as a time-honored gathering that reflects the values of the Five Heroines of the Order of the Eastern Star, symbolizing the virtues of fidelity, constancy, loyalty, faith, and love, and encouraging attendees to embody these guiding principles in their daily lives; and WHEREAS, This vibrant occasion not only celebrates sisterhood and unity but also supports philanthropic efforts that make a lasting difference in the lives of those in need, reaffirming Sunshine Chapter No. 34's commitment to service and excellence; and WHEREAS, Through this event, members and guests alike are inspired to cultivate leadership, embrace tradition, and strengthen bonds of fellowship, ensuring that the legacy of the Order of the Eastern Star continues to flourish for future generations; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to acknowledge the significance of this event and recognize the dedicated members of Sunshine Chapter No. 34 for their enduring contributions to community service and sisterhood; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Sunshine Chapter No. 34, Order of the Eastern Star upon the occasion of hosting its "Sisterhood in Bloom: A Five Point Tea Floral Celebration" on April 27, 2025, and to extend its best wishes for a successful and meaningful gathering; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sunshine Chapter No. 34, Order of the Eastern Star.
