CHAN -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Housing, Construction and Community Development AN ACT to amend the real property actions and proceedings law, in relation to requiring that all petitions have a judgment rendered within six months of issue being joined The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 745 of the real property actions and proceedings 2 law is amended by adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows: 3 3. The court shall render a final judgment on a petition no later than 4 six months from the date upon which issue is joined. 5 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
