The highway law is amended by adding a new section 256 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 256. Scajaquada pedestrian bridge to be designated as the "9.11 4 Heroes Bridge". The pedestrian bridge that spans New York State Route 5 198, known as the Scajaquada Expressway, from Nottingham Terrace to Hoyt 6 Lake in Delaware Park in the city of Buffalo, county of Erie, shall be 7 designated and known as the "9.11 Heroes Bridge". 8 § 2.
