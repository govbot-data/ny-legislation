and WHEREAS, Sports and athletic activities promote physical, mental, and emotional prowess within our young New Yorkers, instilling a fire to strive for excellence that matures alongside the athlete, driving them to unparalleled heights; and WHEREAS, Student athletes challenge their body and minds, whether on the court or classroom, they juggle their responsibilities while bolstering their teamwork, excellence, and competitive spirit, qualities that will benefit their futures greatly; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is immensely proud to congratulate the Ardsley High School Varsity Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2025 NYSPHSAA Basketball Class A Championship on Saturday, March 22, 2025, at Hudson Valley Community College in Troy, New York; and WHEREAS, Surrounded by enthusiastic family members, friends, and fans, the Ardsley Panthers team was victorious over the Wantagh Warriors, ending with a final score of 39-28, finalizing their 24-3 season record and winning Ardsley High School's first basketball state championship title; and WHEREAS, The members of the Ardsley High School Varsity Girls Basketball Team display incredible mastery and professionalism in the realm of basketball, forming intense bonds of sisterhood and friendship that will last long after they retire their blue and gold uniforms; and WHEREAS, Head Coach Nick Resavy and Assistant Coach Danielle Scaperrotta have worked tirelessly to mold each and every athlete since their elementary school travel basketball team; ensuring their skills, mentality, and teamwork were worthy of a championship team and instilling values that transcend both on and off the court; and WHEREAS, The athletes on the Ardsley High School Varsity Girls Basketball Team have utilized dedication and drive that have become a staple of Ardsley High School, bringing home the sixth overall state championship title in school history to a supportive and adoring Ardsley community, truly exemplifying Ardsley High School's tradition of excellence; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Ardsley High School Varsity Girls Basketball Team: Joie M. Levy, Madison Bortstein, Iva Corluka, Lily Tiernan, Dylan Paley, Whitney Butler, Elise O'Brien, Morgan Macri, Teagan Kenny, Ava Santavicca, Leah Burriss, and Mia Stanton; Head Coach Nick Resavy; and Assistant Coach Danielle Scaperrotta upon the occasion of capturing the 2025 NYSPHSAA Basketball Class A Championship on March 22, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Ardsley High School Varsity Girls Basketball Team; Head Coach Nick Resavy; and Assistant Coach Danielle Scaperrotta.
