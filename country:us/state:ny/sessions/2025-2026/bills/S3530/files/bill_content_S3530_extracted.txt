The veterans' services law is amended by adding a new 2 section 25-a to read as follows: 3 § 25-a. Veterans' dental insurance coverage program. 1. The commis- 4 sioner shall establish a veterans' dental insurance coverage program, in 5 consultation with the commissioner of civil service, within the state 6 employee dental insurance plan. Such program shall provide comprehensive 7 and routine dental care services to New York state resident veterans as 8 such term is defined in this article. 9 2. Eligibility under this section shall not be granted to any person 10 who at the time an application is made is receiving equivalent or better 11 coverage from any other public or private third party payment source or 12 insurance plan than those benefits provided for under this section. 13 3. The commissioner shall establish a sliding scale for copayments 14 which shall be based on income and disability level of the veteran. 15 Veterans with a disability rating of between ten and fifty percent shall 16 be subject to copayment amounts as set by the director based on income. 17 Veterans with a disability rating of fifty percent or higher shall not 18 be subject to copayments for treatment or services under this program. 19 4. Program regulations shall: 20 (a) provide for a process of determining and re-determining eligibil- 21 ity for participation in this program including provisions for 22 submission of proof of veteran status, residency, income, disability 23 rating and information on existing complete or partial coverage of 24 dental expenses under a third party assistance or insurance plan; EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
