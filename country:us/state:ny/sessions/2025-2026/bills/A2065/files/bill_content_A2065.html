SAME ASNo Same As

Add §240.80, Pen L
 
Provides that it is a class E felony to operate a model or unmanned aircraft over a critical infrastructure facility including an electrical power generating facility, petroleum or natural gas refinery or stage facility, or an aboveground oil, gas or chemical pipeline.

     3  §  240.80  Unlawful operation of model or unmanned aircraft over a crit-

     5    1. It is unlawful for a person to (a) knowingly operate or use a model

    11    2. This section does not apply to:

    13    (b) a public body;
    14    (c) a law enforcement agency;

    18    (e)  an owner or operator of the critical infrastructure facility or a
    19  person who has the prior written consent of the owner or operator of the
    20  critical infrastructure facility;
    21    (f) the owner or occupant  of  the  property  on  which  the  critical
    22  infrastructure facility is located or a person who has the prior written
    23  consent  of  the owner or occupant of the property on which the critical
    24  infrastructure facility is located; or

     1    (g) a person operating an  unmanned  aircraft  system  for  commercial
     2  purposes  in  compliance  with  authorization  granted  by  the  Federal
     3  Aviation Administration.
     4    3. For the purposes of this section:
     5    (a)  (i)  "Unmanned aircraft" means an aircraft, including an aircraft
     6  commonly known as a drone, that is operated without the  possibility  of
     7  direct human intervention from within or on the aircraft; and
     8    (ii)   "Unmanned aircraft system" means an unmanned aircraft and asso-
     9  ciated elements, including any communication links and  components  that
    10  control the unmanned aircraft.
    11    (b)  "Commercial  purposes"  means  the use of an unmanned aircraft in
    12  return for financial compensation and includes aerial photography, aeri-
    13  al mapping or geospatial imaging.
    14    (c) "Critical infrastructure facility"  means  any  of  the  following
    15  facilities,  if completely enclosed by a fence or other physical barrier
    16  that is obviously designed to exclude intruders, or  if  marked  with  a
    17  sign  conspicuously  posted on the property that indicates that entry is
    18  forbidden:
    19    (i) a petroleum or alumina refinery;
    20    (ii) an electrical power generating  facility,  substation,  switching
    21  station or electrical control center;
    22    (iii) a chemical, polymer or rubber manufacturing facility;
    23    (iv)  a  water  intake structure, water treatment facility, wastewater
    24  treatment plant or pump station;
    25    (v) a natural gas compressor station;
    26    (vi) a liquid natural gas terminal or storage facility;
    27    (vii) a telecommunications central switching office;
    28    (viii) a port, railroad switching yard,  trucking  terminal  or  other
    29  freight transportation facility;
    30    (ix) a gas processing plant, including a plant used in the processing,
    31  treatment or fractionation of natural gas;
    32    (x)  a  transmission  facility  used  by a federally licensed radio or
    33  television station;
    34    (xi) a steelmaking facility that uses an electric arc furnace to  make
    35  steel;
    36    (xii)  a  dam  that  is  classified as a high hazard by any regulatory
    37  body;
    38    (xiii) any portion of an aboveground oil,  gas  or  chemical  pipeline
    39  that  is enclosed by a fence or other physical barrier that is obviously
    40  designed to exclude intruders; or
    41    (xiv) a correctional facility or law enforcement facility.
    42    (d) "Public unmanned aircraft" means an unmanned aircraft or  unmanned
    43  aircraft system that is operated by a public agency for a government-re-
    44  lated purpose.
    45    Unlawful  operation  of  model  or  unmanned  aircraft over a critical
    46  infrastructure facility is a class E felony.
    47    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
