AN ACT in relation to authorizing the town of Niagara to alienate and
discontinue the use of certain parklands and to convey such parklands
to Rajendra Sharma
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of Niagara,
2 county of Niagara, is hereby authorized, acting by and through its
3 governing body, and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such
4 governing body, to discontinue the use as parkland and alienate the
5 lands described in section three of this act, and to sell and convey
6 property interests in such lands at fair market value to Rajendra Sharma
7 for the purpose of adding sufficient additional land to adjacent land
8 owned by Rajendra Sharma to construct a residence.
9 § 2. The authorization contained in section one of this act shall only
10 be effective on the condition that the town of Niagara dedicate an
11 amount equal to or greater than the fair market value of the parklands
12 being alienated pursuant to section one of this act towards the acquisi-
13 tion of new parklands and/or capital improvements to existing park and
14 recreational facilities.
