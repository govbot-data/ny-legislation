and WHEREAS, Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region is a nationally recognized nonprofit organization dedicated to inspiring all girls to be strong, smart, and bold through direct service and advocacy; and WHEREAS, For over 85 years, Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region has provided thousands of girls across the Capital Region with life-changing programs focused on education, health, and leadership development, equipping them with the skills and confidence necessary to succeed in life; and WHEREAS, Through mentorship, research-based programming, and advocacy, Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region fosters an environment where girls are encouraged to pursue their dreams, break barriers, and achieve economic independence; and WHEREAS, The work of Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region is particularly vital in promoting gender equity, increasing access to STEM education, and providing leadership opportunities for young women, helping them become the leaders of tomorrow; and WHEREAS, On May 8, 2025, Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region and its supporters will come together to celebrate and recognize the organization's dedication and impact on young women's lives; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proud to honor Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region for its continued dedication to strengthening communities by investing in the potential of girls and young women throughout the Capital Region; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize May 8, 2025, as a day to honor the work of Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region and its invaluable contributions to the success and empowerment of young women in the Capital Region; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Girls Inc. of the Greater Capital Region.
