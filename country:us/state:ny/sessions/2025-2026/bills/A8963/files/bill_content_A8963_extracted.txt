If a covered user requests a copy of such 42 covered user's social graph under this article, a social media company 43 shall, within five business days, provide the social graph or user-se- 44 lected parts of the social graph, in a format that: 45 (a) allows for data portability, to the extent technically feasible; 46 (b) is readily usable by the covered user; and 47 (c) allows the covered user to transmit the data to another social 48 media platform or third party without impediment if the social media 49 platform or third party processes the data by automated means. 50 2.
