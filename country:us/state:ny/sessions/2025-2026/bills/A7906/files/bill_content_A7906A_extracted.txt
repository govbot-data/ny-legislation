AN ACT to amend the insurance law and the public health law, in relation
to clarifying the provision of donate life registration to certain
insured persons
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Paragraph 17 of subsection (a) of section 3221 of the
2 insurance law, as amended by chapter 29 of the laws of 2023, is amended
3 to read as follows:
4 (17) [Space shall be provided] Every policy delivered or issued for
5 delivery in this state which provides major medical or similar compre-
6 hensive-type coverage shall provide space on any enrollment, renewal or
7 initial online portal process setup forms required of an insured or
8 applicant for insurance so that the insured or applicant for insurance
9 shall register or decline registration in the donate life registry for
10 organ, eye and tissue donations under this section of the enrollment,
11 renewal or initial online portal process setup forms and that the
12 following is stated on the form in clear and conspicuous type:
13 "You must fill out the following section: Would you like to be added
14 to the Donate Life Registry? Check box for 'yes' or 'skip this ques-
15 tion'."
