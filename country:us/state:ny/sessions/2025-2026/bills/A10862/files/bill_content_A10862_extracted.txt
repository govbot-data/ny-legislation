JACKSON -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to including a component on water safety in kindergarten through twelfth grade The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 804-e 2 to read as follows: 3 § 804-e. Instruction in water safety. The regents shall ensure that 4 the course of instruction in grades kindergarten through twelve includes 5 a component on water safety. Such component shall instruct students on: 6 1. identifying risks associated with drowning or other injuries in 7 connection to any body of water; 2. the behaviors necessary for both 8 children and adults to reduce such risks; and 3. how to respond in an 9 emergency if a drowning or other such injuries occur. The regents shall 10 determine how to incorporate such component in existing curricula and 11 the commissioner shall promulgate any regulations needed to carry out 12 such determination of the regents. The regents may consider components 13 including, but not limited to, an annual thirty-minute interactive 14 assembly, a storyboard presentation, or in-classroom activities. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of July next succeeding 16 the date on which it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the 17 addition, amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary 18 for the implementation of this act on its effective date are authorized 19 to be made and completed on or before such effective date.
