Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01914 Summary:BILL NOJ01914  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORSTAVISKY COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01914 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1914 BY: Senator STAVISKY COMMEMORATING the 10th Anniversary of the Foster Youth College Success Initiative WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to take note of and publicly acknowledge the purposeful endeavors that serve to enhance the lives of the youth of our great State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the Foster Youth College Success Initiative, to be celebrated on Tuesday, April 28, 2026, at the Corning Tower Observation Deck in Albany, New York; and WHEREAS, The Foster Youth College Success Initiative (FYCSI) is a state program specifically for foster youth who are in college or entering college and is designed to provide support and financial assistance to help them achieve academic success and meet college expenses; and WHEREAS, The program's intention is to meet the full range of needs of each FYCSI student to ensure that unnecessary obstacles do not derail a student's educational pursuits; and WHEREAS, Funding through the Foster Youth College Success Initiative is provided based on need and supports the costs necessary for eligible students to attend college including tuition and fees, meals, housing, books and supplies, transportation, academic support, and personal expenses; and WHEREAS, The Foster Youth College Success Initiative serves foster youth across SUNY, CUNY, and independent colleges and universities throughout New York State, reflecting a statewide commitment to equitable access to higher education; and WHEREAS, Foster youth are a population to whom New York State bears a particular responsibility, and supporting their success in higher education reflects this Legislative Body's commitment to equity and opportunity for every young New Yorker; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge that those initiatives which enhance the well-being and success of citizens of New York State and have shown a long and sustained commitment to education, certainly have earned the recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the Foster Youth College Success Initiative.
