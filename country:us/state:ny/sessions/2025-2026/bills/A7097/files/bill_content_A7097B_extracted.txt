AN ACT to amend the labor law, in relation to including public employers
in wage payment provisions
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 3 of section 190 of the labor law, as amended
2 by chapter 281 of the laws of 2002, is amended to read as follows:
3 3. "Employer" includes any person, corporation, including a municipal
4 corporation or public benefit corporation, public authority, limited
5 liability company, or association employing any individual in any occu-
6 pation, industry, trade, business or service. The term "employer" shall
7 not include a state governmental agency.
8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
