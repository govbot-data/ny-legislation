GRIFFO, LANZA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Veterans, Homeland Security and Military Affairs AN ACT to amend the veterans' services law, in relation to the estab- lishment of the New York state veterans cemetery act The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "New York state veterans cemetery act". 3 § 2. The veterans' services law is amended by adding a new section 4 24-a to read as follows: 5 § 24-a. Veterans cemetery funding promotion program. The department is 6 hereby directed to establish, produce and conduct a New York state 7 veterans cemetery funding promotion program. The purpose of the New York 8 state veterans cemetery funding promotion program would be to encourage 9 the giving of financial contributions, by individuals, groups and corpo- 10 rations, to the veterans remembrance and cemetery maintenance and opera- 11 tion fund, created pursuant to section ninety-seven-mmmm of the state 12 finance law, which was established to provide funding for veterans ceme- 13 teries pursuant to the New York state veterans cemetery program created 14 under section twenty-four of this article. In establishing the New York 15 state veterans cemetery funding promotion program, the department shall 16 establish, produce and conduct programs, projects, and materials 17 designed to encourage the giving of financial contributions, by individ- 18 uals, groups and corporations, to the veterans remembrance and cemetery 19 maintenance and operation fund. The department shall further seek, and 20 may apply on behalf of the state of New York, for grants and contrib- 21 utions, from governments and/or other institutional funding sources, for 22 deposit in, and use by, the veterans remembrance and cemetery mainte- 23 nance and operation fund, for the purpose of constructing, operating and 24 financing New York state veterans cemeteries.
