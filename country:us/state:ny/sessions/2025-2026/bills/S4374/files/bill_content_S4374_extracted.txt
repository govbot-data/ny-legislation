COMRIE, SALAZAR -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on New York City Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to prohibiting co-loca- tion of a charter school in a public school building in a city school district in a city having a population of one million or more inhabit- ants; and to repeal certain provisions of the education law related thereto The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraphs (a-3), (a-4), (a-5), (d) and (e) of subdivision 2 3 of section 2853 of the education law are REPEALED and a new paragraph 3 (d) is added to read as follows: 4 (d) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, no 5 charter school shall be co-located in a public school building in a city 6 school district in a city having a population of one million or more 7 inhabitants. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of July next succeeding 9 the date on which it shall have become a law.
