The executive law is amended by adding a new section 214-j 2 to read as follows: 3 § 214-j. Training. 1. The superintendent shall establish policies and 4 procedures requiring that state police basic training and pre-employment 5 course curricula include training in the areas of cultural competency, 6 including training pertaining to appropriate sensitivity to issues of 7 race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, age and disabili- 8 ties. Such training shall comprise not less than five percent of the 9 basic training and pre-employment course training curricula. Cultural 10 competency training shall be provided by an accredited educator and/or 11 accreditation body.
