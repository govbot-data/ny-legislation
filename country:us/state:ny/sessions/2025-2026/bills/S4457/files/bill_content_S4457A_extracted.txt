AN ACT authorizing the town of Brookhaven to alienate certain parklands
for use as a recharge basin and to dedicate other lands as replacement
parklands
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of Brookha-
2 ven, located in the county of Suffolk, acting by and through its govern-
3 ing body and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such body,
4 is hereby authorized to discontinue as parklands the lands more partic-
5 ularly described in section three of this act, for the purposes of
6 constructing a stormwater runoff and recharge basin.
7 § 2. The authorization provided in section one of this act shall be
8 effective only upon the condition that the town of Brookhaven dedicate
9 an amount equal to or greater than the fair market value of the park-
10 lands being discontinued towards the acquisition of new parklands and/or
11 capital improvements to existing park and recreational facilities.
12 § 3. The parklands authorized by section one of this act to be discon-
13 tinued as parklands are described as follows:
14 Parcel I
15 ALL that certain piece or parcel of land situate, lying and being near
16 Bellport, Town of Brookhaven, County of Suffolk, State of New York,
17 known and designated as Lots 55 through 64, inclusive, in Block 508, as

19 Brooklyn Suburban Investment Company" and filed in the office of the
20 Clerk of Suffolk County on June 9, 1890 as number 444; also filed April
21 9, 1890 as number 83 and April 15, 1892 as number 26 and being more
22 particularly bounded and described as follows:
