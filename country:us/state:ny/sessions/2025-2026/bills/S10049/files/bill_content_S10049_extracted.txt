JACKSON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to updating the defi- nition of cyberbullying in the dignity for all students act The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 8 of section 11 of the education law, as added 2 by chapter 102 of the laws of 2012, is amended to read as follows: 3 8. "Cyberbullying" shall mean harassment or bullying as defined in 4 subdivision seven of this section, including paragraphs (a), (b), (c) 5 and (d) of such subdivision, where such harassment or bullying occurs 6 through any form of electronic communication. "Cyberbullying" shall 7 include, but not be limited to, intentionally using artificial intelli- 8 gence to mimic or alter a person's likeness or voice without their 9 consent. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
