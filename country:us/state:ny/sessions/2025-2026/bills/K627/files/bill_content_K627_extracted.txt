McMahon CONGRATULATING Dr. Varun Jeph upon the occasion of the completion of his service as Deputy Consul General of India in New York WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and pay tribute to those dedicated individuals who commit their professional lives, energy, and resources to improving the quality and character of life in their community, State, and Nation; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Dr. Varun Jeph upon the occasion of the completion of his service as Deputy Consul General of India in New York; and WHEREAS, Dr. Varun Jeph is a medical graduate from one of the most prestigious medical schools in India; he has devoted his esteemed professional career to serving as a diplomat with around 15 years of experience in the Indian Foreign Service; and WHEREAS, With a rich and diverse career background, Dr. Varun Jeph served as Political Officer in the Indian Embassy in Myanmar at a significant time when the country was undergoing political transition; he also served at the Indian High Commission in Malaysia, where he headed the Political & Commercial Wings of the Mission; and WHEREAS, Between his overseas assignments, Dr. Varun Jeph served in New Delhi, India at the Ministry of External Affairs and was involved in administration and human resource management of India's Foreign Service; and WHEREAS, In September of 2021, Dr. Varun Jeph joined the Consulate General of India in New York, where his duties involved supervising the daily functions of the Consulate-including its commercial and economic outreach, media engagements, cultural activities, and delivery of consular services; and WHEREAS, With him throughout his illustrious career have been his wife Pallavi and their two sons-all of whom have felt privileged to be a part of his fruitful life and rejoice in his many achievements; and WHEREAS, At the conclusion of his service as Deputy Consul General of India in New York, Dr. Varun Jeph hopes to spend time traveling, meditating, reading, and enjoying the company of his family; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Dr. Varun Jeph upon the occasion of the completion of his service as Deputy Consul General of India in New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Dr. Varun Jeph.
