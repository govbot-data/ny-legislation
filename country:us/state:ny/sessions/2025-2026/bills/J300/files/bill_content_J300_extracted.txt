and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Dave Fontaine upon the occasion of his retirement after a total of 24 years of service at Utica University; and WHEREAS, David Fontaine retired from Utica University's Athletics Department on Friday, January 31, 2025; and WHEREAS, Dave Fontaine graduated from Notre Dame Junior-Senior High School in Utica; he earned an Associate's in Liberal Arts and Sciences degree from Mohawk Valley Community College, a Bachelor's in Psychology degree from Utica College (now Utica University), and a Master's in Physical Education, Administration, and Management degree from Syracuse University; and WHEREAS, Dave Fontaine was a four-year collegiate student-athlete and filled the role of Captain of the Pioneers Baseball Team in his senior season, leading the team to an Eastern College Athletic Conference (ECAC) Upstate County Championship; and WHEREAS, A Utica University alumnus, Dave Fontaine has spent the last 13 years leading the Athletic Department for the Pioneers as Director of Athletics and Physical Education; and WHEREAS, Prior to his time as Director of Athletics, Dave Fontaine also spent 11 years at Utica University, from 1989 through 2000, serving in a variety of roles, including Director of Intramurals, Head Softball Coach, Home Event Manager, Compliance Director, Scheduling Coordinator, and Assistant Director of Athletics; and WHEREAS, Since his return to the Athletics Department in January of 2012, Dave Fontaine has been at the forefront of several improvements and developments; he was also instrumental in creating, developing, and enhancing athletic department operations and countless enhancements to facilities and traditions; and WHEREAS, Dave Fontaine feels fortunate to have been an integral part of countless program-defining moments for the Pioneers, including 20 conference championships, 15 NCAA Tournament appearances, 65 All-Americans, and high individual placements which featured some of the top finishes in the country; today, Utica University boasts one of the largest athletic programs in the Empire 8 Conference; and WHEREAS, The many contributions of which Dave Fontaine has played a vital role are a testament to his legacy which will inspire future students and athletes of Utica University for generations to come; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those dedicated individuals who have shown a long and sustained commitment to excellence unquestionably have earned the recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Dave Fontaine upon the occasion of his retirement and to wish him well in all his future endeavors; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Dave Fontaine.
