HELMING -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Local Government AN ACT to amend the village law, in relation to authorizing the village of Sodus Point, in the town of Sodus, county of Wayne to allow golf carts on certain streets The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The village law is amended by adding a new section 6-634 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 6-634 Village of Sodus Point; golf carts. The trustees of the 4 village of Sodus Point may authorize the use of golf carts on certain 5 streets under the exclusive control and jurisdiction of such village. 6 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
