A03952 Summary:

Amd §401, V & T L
 
Establishes a single registration requirement for non-commercial vehicles; provides that the registration for non-commercial vehicles shall not expire until the title of such motor vehicle is transferred to a new owner.

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to establishing
          a single registration requirement for non-commercial vehicles
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivision  5  of section 401 of the vehicle and traffic
     2  law, as amended by chapter 136 of the  laws  of  1969,  paragraph  a  as
     3  amended  by  chapter  137 of the laws of 1989, paragraph b as amended by
     4  chapter 826 of the laws of 1985 and paragraph c as  amended  by  chapter
     5  356 of the laws of 1990, is amended to read as follows:
     6    5.  Times  for registration, reregistration and renewal; proportionate
     7  fees. a. Registrations, reregistrations and renewals shall  take  effect
     8  and  expire  on dates determined by the commissioner; provided, however,
     9  the registration for a non-commercial vehicle for which  a  registration
    10  fee  established  in  paragraph  a of subdivision six of this section is
    11  required shall not expire until the  title  of  such  motor  vehicle  is
    12  transferred  to  a  new  owner  as provided in article forty-six of this
    13  chapter. The registration of any such older model  year  motor  vehicles
    14  that  are  currently registered shall not expire until the title of such
    15  motor vehicle is transferred to a  new  owner  as  provided  in  article
    16  forty-six  of this chapter. Upon such transfer of a certificate of title
    17  the new owner shall register the motor vehicle and pay the  registration
    18  fee  established  in  paragraph  a  of  subdivision six of this section.
    19  However, where the expiration date of the registration of any commercial
    20  vehicle, except a taxi or an omnibus, falls on  a  Saturday,  Sunday  or
    21  state  holiday,  such  registration  shall be valid for the operation of
    22  such vehicle until midnight of the next day on which state offices shall
    23  be open for business. Provided, however, that renewal of a  registration
    24  may be used preceding the expiration date of such registration including
    25  such  expiration  date.    Provided,  further,  however,  that where the
