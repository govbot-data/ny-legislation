AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to establishing a
usage monitor program
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 44 of the public service law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 8 to read as follows:
3 8. The commission shall require every gas and electric utility corpo-
4 ration utilizing smart meters to establish a usage monitor program for
5 the purposes of notifying residential customers when energy usage
6 exceeds a cost or usage threshold in a given billing period determined
7 by the residential customer.
8 a. The usage monitor program shall provide residential customers with
9 daily updates on their electricity and/or gas usage. This information
10 shall be viewable to the customer on the gas and/or electricity utility
11 corporation website within a customer's online account. Participating
12 residential customers shall be able to utilize within their customer
13 account a function to set a monthly usage threshold that is measured in
14 kilowatt hours (kWh) for electricity service, and therms or hundred
15 cubic feet (CCF) for gas service.
16 b. The usage monitor program shall notify residential customers elec-
17 tronically or by mail when half of their determined energy usage thresh-
18 old is met, and when their usage threshold exceeds their determined
19 threshold. This electronic and paper notification shall include an esti-
20 mate of their monthly bill.
