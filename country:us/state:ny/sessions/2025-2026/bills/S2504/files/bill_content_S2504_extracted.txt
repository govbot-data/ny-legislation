GOUNARDES -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to increasing penalties imposed on owners for failure of an operator to comply with posted speed limits in the city of New York The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision (e) of section 1180-b of the vehicle and traf- 2 fic law, as added by chapter 189 of the laws of 2013, is amended to 3 read as follows: 4 (e) An owner liable for a violation of subdivision (b), (c), (d), (f) 5 or (g) of section eleven hundred eighty of this article pursuant to a 6 demonstration program established pursuant to this section shall be 7 liable for monetary penalties in accordance with a schedule of fines and 8 penalties to be promulgated by the parking violations bureau of the city 9 of New York. The liability of the owner pursuant to this section shall 10 not exceed [fifty dollars for each violation] the dollar amounts 11 prescribed herein for the following number of violations, committed 12 within a period of two years: fifty dollars for the first two 13 violations, one hundred dollars for a third violation, one hundred twen- 14 ty-five dollars for a fourth violation, one hundred fifty dollars for a 15 fifth violation, two hundred dollars for a sixth violation, and two 16 hundred fifty dollars for a seventh and any subsequent violations; 17 provided, however, that such parking violations bureau may provide for 18 an additional penalty not in excess of twenty-five dollars for each 19 violation for the failure to respond to a notice of liability within the 20 prescribed time period. 21 § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth day after it shall 22 have become a law; provided that the amendments to section 1180-b of the 23 vehicle and traffic law made by section one of this act shall not affect 24 the expiration of such section and shall expire and be deemed repealed 25 therewith.
