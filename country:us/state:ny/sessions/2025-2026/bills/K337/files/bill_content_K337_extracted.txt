McMahon HONORING Kathleen Achibar-Boyd upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by Buffalo Business First WHEREAS, Individuals who give of their time and energies and serve the best interests of their communities are assets beyond remuneration and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Kathleen Achibar-Boyd, upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition, as a member of the 2025 Class of 30 Under 30, to be recognized by Buffalo Business First at the Hyatt Regency in Buffalo, New York, on Wednesday, April 16, 2025; and WHEREAS, Buffalo Business First's 30 Under 30 recognizes 30 young professionals in Western New York for their achievements and contributions to their industries and communities; and WHEREAS, Kathleen Achibar-Boyd is a Partner at Bolton-St. Johns LLC, one of New York's prominent lobbying firms, located in Albany; in her role, she represents and supports Bolton's Western New York operations and clientele, bringing her passion for advocacy to support the firm's strategic government relations services; and WHEREAS, Originally from Syosset, New York, Kathleen Achibar-Boyd takes great pride in her alma mater, the University at Buffalo; she ambitiously embarked on her political journey in 2017; and WHEREAS, Kathleen Achibar-Boyd serves as a valued member of the Eleanor's Legacy Innovation Council, driving innovative initiatives for the organization and playing an integral role in its advancement; and WHEREAS, Throughout her career, Kathleen Achibar-Boyd has actively participated in numerous political campaigns across Western New York, spanning federal, state, and local levels; her impactful contributions within the political sphere fueled her passion for government, leading her to pursue roles as a staff member in the distinguished offices of New York State Legislators; and WHEREAS, Throughout the years of her community service, a period of constructive involvement, Kathleen Achibar-Boyd has stood constant in dignity; her advocacy empowers young individuals to engage in politics; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by individuals for the benefit of others which Kathleen Achibar-Boyd has displayed throughout her life; and WHEREAS, It has always been the objective of this Legislative Body to honor and support those individuals who have displayed their commitment to the betterment of their communities; and WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records this tribute to Kathleen Achibar-Boyd, that future generations may know and appreciate her admirable character and the respect and esteem in which she is held by her peers; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Kathleen Achibar-Boyd upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by Buffalo Business First; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Kathleen Achibar-Boyd.
