Purpose. The purposes of the New York combat veterans health- 27 care choice program are to authorize the establishment of veterans 28 healthcare choice accounts and provide guidelines for the maintenance of 29 such accounts to: 30 1. Enable resident veteran members of the organized militia of this 31 state who were on active duty in a combat zone, designated by the presi- 32 dent of the United States during Operation Enduring Freedom or Operation 33 Iraqi Freedom, to obtain healthcare from the provider of their choice, 34 with funds from a New York combat veterans healthcare choice account 35 funded by the state and the service member in accordance with this arti- 36 cle, when after ninety days of making application to or attempting to 37 schedule an appointment with the federal Veterans Administration for 38 such care, the service member is unable to secure timely or adequate 39 healthcare services for injuries and disabilities sustained in the line 40 of active duty in such a combat zone, whenever ordered into such combat 41 zone for three hundred sixty days or more by the governor, the command- 42 ing general of the New York army national guard, the commanding officer 43 of the New York air national guard or the commanding officer of the 44 naval militia; 45 2. Provide funding from the state for New York combat veterans health- 46 care choice accounts in an amount of up to five thousand dollars per 47 calendar year for a period not to exceed three years, when in accordance 48 with this article, a qualified state resident combat veteran is unable 49 to secure healthcare services within ninety days of making application 50 or scheduling an appointment to make such an application for such 51 services from the federal Veterans Administration; and 52 3. To create a state personal income tax deduction for amounts 53 contributed by qualified resident combat veterans to a New York combat 54 veterans healthcare choice account in an amount not to exceed five thou- 55 sand dollars per calendar year as provided in this article.
