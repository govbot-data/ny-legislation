Section 212 of the retirement and social security law is 2 amended by adding a new subdivision 2-a to read as follows: 3 2-a. Notwithstanding the provisions of subdivision two of this 4 section, there shall be no earning limitation for retired persons in 5 positions of public service from the year two thousand twenty-five and 6 thereafter. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
