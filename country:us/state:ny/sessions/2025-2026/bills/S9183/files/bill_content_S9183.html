        AN  ACT  to  amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, in
          relation to eligibility requirements for horses participating  in  the
          three racing levels of the New York Sire Stakes program

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The opening paragraph of subdivision 1 and subdivisions  2,
     2  3  and 4 of section 334 of the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding
     3  law, the opening paragraph of subdivision 1 and subdivision 2 as amended
     4  by chapter 39 of the laws of 2024, subdivision 3 as amended  by  chapter
     5  170  of the laws of 2014, and subdivision 4 as amended by chapter 445 of
     6  the laws of 1997, are amended to read as follows:
     7    The fund is further authorized and directed to conduct each  year,  at
     8  the  New York state exposition, with the approval of the director of the
     9  exposition, or at any licensed pari-mutuel track in New York state, with
    10  a preference given to any available licensed pari-mutuel track  that  is
    11  five-eighths  of a mile long or larger, colt, stake and overnight events
    12  for standardbred horses to provide contests for two year old  and  three
    13  year  old  colts  and  fillies  at each gait of trotting and pacing. The
    14  colt, stake and overnight events so conducted for two year old and three
    15  year old colts and fillies at each gait of trotting and pacing hereunder
    16  shall be conditioned to admit only those colts and fillies [dropped from
    17  a mare bred in this state and] sired by a stallion owned or  leased  and
    18  permanently standing for service at and within this state at the time of
    19  the said foal's conception, provided, however, that such mare, stallion,
    20  and foal shall be microchipped with such microchip information which the
    21  commission  may  request  be  provided  and  made  available pursuant to
    22  section two hundred twenty-five of this chapter. Such  colt,  stake  and
    23  overnight  events  shall  be  opened for nomination not earlier than the
    24  first day of January in the year the event is to be held and only  colts
    25  and  fillies  and horses complying with the following standards shall be
    26  eligible for such nomination:

     1    2. The fund is further authorized and directed in each year, in  coop-
     2  eration  with  each licensee harness track in this state, to provide for
     3  the running of stake events conditioned to admit only two year  old  and
     4  three year old colts and fillies sired by a stallion owned or leased and
     5  permanently standing for service at and within this state at the time of
     6  the  said  foal's conception, at each gait of trotting and pacing, to be
     7  known as "New York sire stakes program" and to contribute to the purses,
     8  stakes or prizes to be awarded in such "New York sire  stakes  program",
     9  such  sums  as  the fund shall deem advisable. [The fund may establish a
    10  system that distinguishes between (a) foals sired by a stallion standing
    11  in this state and bred within this state, (b) foals sired by a  stallion
    12  standing  in this state and with a mare bred outside this state, and (c)
    13  foals sired by a stallion standing in this state and dropped from a mare
    14  who was bred, and resided, in this state for at least one hundred eighty
    15  days in the year of conception. The fund shall provide bonus payments to
    16  those foals bred within the state that meet other conditions]  The  fund
    17  shall  provide  bonus payments to those foals conceived within the state
    18  and dropped from mares that reside  in  the  state  for  a  time  period
    19  defined  and  imposed by the fund. The fund may prescribe a starting fee
    20  for [such "New York-bred harness horse stakes"]  all  races  within  the
    21  "New  York sire stakes program" set forth in this subdivision, but in no
    22  event shall such fee be less than two per centum of the estimated  purse
    23  for each entry. The fee prescribed shall be added to the purses awarded.
    24    3.  [Subsequent  to  the year two thousand fourteen a] A foal shall be
    25  eligible for all races within the "New York  sire  stakes  program"  set
    26  forth  in  subdivision  two of this section, and all stake and overnight
    27  events conducted at the New York state exposition set forth in  subdivi-
    28  sion  one  of  this  section if [conceived from a mare bred in the state
    29  and] sired by a stallion owned by a resident of this state or leased  to
    30  a  resident  of  this  state  for  a period of no less than one year and
    31  standing for service  within  the  state  at  the  time  of  the  foal's
    32  conception.
    33    4. The fund shall have the power to prescribe rules and regulations to
    34  determine  the  eligibility  of entries in ["New York-bred harness horse
    35  races"] all races within the "New York sire stakes program" set forth in
    36  subdivision two of this section, and all stake  and  overnight    events
    37  conducted at the New York state exposition set forth in  subdivision one
    38  of  this  section  and  to  effectuate the purposes and requirements set
    39  forth in this section.
    40    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
