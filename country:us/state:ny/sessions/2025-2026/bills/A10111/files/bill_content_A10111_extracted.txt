Emergency housing transfer prioritization and program portabil- 20 ity. 1. Each authority shall ensure that emergency housing transfer 21 requests submitted by tenants are processed with the highest urgency, 22 prioritizing tenants in dwelling units deemed uninhabitable or struc- 23 turally compromised. 24 2.
