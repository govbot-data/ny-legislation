Chevon McFadden upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Hadji Court No. 62 at its Illustrious Commandress Ball to be held on September 5, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and celebrate individuals and organizations whose leadership and service have enriched the civic and cultural fabric of their communities; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to honor Illustrious Commandress Dt. Chevon McFadden upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Hadji Court No. 62 at its Illustrious Commandress Ball, to be held on Friday, September 5, 2025; and WHEREAS, Hadji Court No. 62 is a distinguished court under the Daughters of the Imperial Court, dedicated to scholarship, community service, and cultural preservation; and WHEREAS, Illustrious Commandress Dt. Chevon McFadden of Hadji Court No. 62 has exemplified exceptional leadership, integrity, and commitment to community empowerment throughout her tenure, guiding the Court with vision and grace; and WHEREAS, Under her stewardship, Hadji Court No. 62 has continued to foster sisterhood, advance charitable initiatives, and uphold the principles of the Order through impactful programming and civic engagement; and WHEREAS, This annual celebration is a moment to honor her exemplary service and to recognize the collective accomplishments of the Court's members in advancing its mission of benevolence, leadership, and unity; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Illustrious Commandress Dt. Chevon McFadden upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Hadji Court No. 62 at its Illustrious Commandress Ball; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Illustrious Commandress Dt.
