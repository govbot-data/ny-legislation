Novakhov COMMEMORATING May 26, 2025, as Georgian Independence Day in the State of New York WHEREAS, The State of New York takes great pride in recognizing the rich heritage, history, and contributions of diverse communities, including the Georgian-American community; and WHEREAS, On May 26, 1991, the Republic of Georgia formally declared its independence from the Soviet Union, restoring its sovereignty and paving the way for a democratic future; and WHEREAS, Georgian Independence Day is a time to honor the resilience, courage, and determination of the Georgian people in their pursuit of freedom, self-governance, and national identity; and WHEREAS, The Georgian-American community has made valuable contributions to the cultural, economic, and civic life of New York, enriching the state's diverse social fabric; and WHEREAS, The strong bilateral relationship between the United States and Georgia is rooted in shared values of democracy, human rights, and mutual cooperation, strengthening ties between both nations; and WHEREAS, The State of New York recognizes May 26, 2025, as Georgian Independence Day, celebrating Georgia's rich cultural heritage and its ongoing journey as a proud and independent nation; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate May 26, 2025, as Georgian Independence Day, paying tribute to the people of Georgia and their enduring spirit.
