AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to dedicating a portion of
the state highway system to Clara H. Barton
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 343-ee
2 to read as follows:
3 § 343-ee. Portion of the state highway system to be dedicated to Clara
4 H. Barton. All that portion of the state highway constituting state
5 route 63 in the village of Dansville, county of Livingston, from its
6 intersection with Zerfass Road to its intersection with Hubbard Lane
7 shall be dedicated to Clara H. Barton.
8 § 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal-
9 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system
10 as dedicated pursuant to section one of this act, provided that such
11 signage shall state "Highway Dedicated to Clara H Barton". However, to
12 avoid confusion and to limit any possible disruption of commerce, the
13 dedication called for pursuant to section one of this act shall be one
14 of ceremonial nature and the official name of such highway shall not be
15 changed as a result of this act.
16 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
