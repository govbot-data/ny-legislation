        AN ACT to amend the legislative law, in relation to reducing the  salary
          of  members  of  the  state legislature and providing that the regular
          legislative session shall end by March 31 annually

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivision  1  of  section  5 of the legislative law, as
     2  amended by chapter 635 of the laws of 1998,  the  opening  paragraph  as
     3  amended  by  chapter  841  of  the  laws  of 2022, is amended to read as
     4  follows:
     5    1. Effective January first, two  thousand  [twenty-three]  twenty-six,
     6  each  member  of  the legislature shall receive a salary of [one hundred
     7  forty-two] forty-eight thousand dollars per  annum.  Such  salary  of  a
     8  member  of  the  legislature  shall  be  payable in twenty-six bi-weekly
     9  installments provided, however, that if legislative passage of the budg-
    10  et as defined in subdivision three of  this  section  has  not  occurred
    11  prior  to  the  first day of any fiscal year, the net amount of any such
    12  bi-weekly salary installment payments to be paid on or  after  such  day
    13  shall  be  withheld  and  not paid until such legislative passage of the
    14  budget has occurred  whereupon  bi-weekly  salary  installment  payments
    15  shall  resume  and  an  amount equal to the accrued, withheld and unpaid
    16  installments shall be promptly paid to each member.
    17    For purposes of this section, net amount shall mean gross salary minus
    18  any or all of the following  deductions:  federal  taxes,  state  taxes,
    19  social security taxes, city taxes, payments on retirement loans, retire-
    20  ment  contributions,  contributions  to  health insurance or other group
    21  insurance programs, child support and court ordered payments.
    22    § 2. The legislative law is amended by adding a  new  article  1-B  to
    23  read as follows:
