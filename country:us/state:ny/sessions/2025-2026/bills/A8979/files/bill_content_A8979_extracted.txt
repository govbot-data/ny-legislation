STECK -- read once and referred to the Committee on Consumer Affairs and Protection AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to the sale of medetomidine The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section 2 392-k to read as follows: 3 § 392-k. Sale of medetomidine. 1. For purposes of this section, the 4 term "medetomidine" shall mean a sedative used for its hypnotic and 5 analgesic effects and that has been approved for use in humans and dogs. 6 2. The provisions of this section shall apply to the sale, offer for 7 sale, delivery, or distribution of all medetomidine regardless of the 8 weight. 9 3. No corporation, partnership, limited liability company, firm, 10 online platform, or any other business entity doing business within this 11 state shall knowingly sell, offer for sale, or deliver medetomidine to 12 individuals without proof of its intended use for institutional, veteri- 13 nary, or scientific purposes. 14 4. No corporation, partnership, limited liability company, firm, 15 online platform, or other business entity doing business within this 16 state shall knowingly sell, offer for sale, or deliver medetomidine to 17 individuals under the age of twenty-one. 18 5. Any corporation, partnership, limited liability company, firm, 19 online platform, or other business entity doing business within this 20 state which knowingly sells, offers for sale, or distributes medetomi- 21 dine shall require and retain sales records, proof of age, and proof of 22 use from all purchasers. 23 6. Any corporation, partnership, limited liability company, firm, 24 online platform, or other business entity doing business within this 25 state that violates the provisions of this section by knowingly selling, EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
