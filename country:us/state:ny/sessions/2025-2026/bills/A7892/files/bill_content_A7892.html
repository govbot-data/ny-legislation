          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Paragraph (d-1) of subdivision 1  of  section  2-b  of  the
     2  religious  corporations  law,  as  amended by chapter 193 of the laws of
     3  1985, is amended to read as follows:

     5  profit  corporation  law,  any  church  referred  to in subdivision two,
