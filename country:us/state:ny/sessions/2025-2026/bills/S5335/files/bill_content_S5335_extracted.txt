The legislature 16 hereby finds and declares that: 17 (a) this state and the United States have experienced the collapse of 18 a sizeable primary and secondary real estate market bubble which few 19 foresaw and many now suffer the wealth destructive effects of; 20 (b) this state and the United States are in consequence now suffering 21 an economic slowdown as consumers trim spending and concentrate upon EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
