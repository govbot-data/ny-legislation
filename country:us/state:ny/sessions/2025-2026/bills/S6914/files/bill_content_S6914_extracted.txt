The real property law is amended by adding a new section 2 226-d to read as follows: 3 § 226-d. Rental assistance payment standard pilot program. 1. Where 4 the requirements of this section are satisfied, the owner of a housing 5 accommodation shall be entitled to charge and collect a rent equal to 6 the maximum amount that the rental assistance program prescribes for 7 like housing accommodations, subject to rent reasonableness calcu- 8 lations, notwithstanding that such rental amount might exceed rent limi- 9 tations under any other applicable law or regulation. 10 2.
