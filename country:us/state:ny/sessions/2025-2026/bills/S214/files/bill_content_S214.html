        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to the  display
          of blue and green lights on certain motor vehicles

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subparagraph a of paragraph 4 and subparagraph (a) of para-
     2  graph 5 of subdivision 41 of section 375 of the vehicle and traffic law,
     3  subparagraph a of paragraph 4 as amended by section 1 of part C of chap-
     4  ter 58 of the laws of 2020, and  subparagraph  (a)  of  paragraph  5  as
     5  amended  by  chapter  71  of  the  laws  of 2024, are amended to read as
     6  follows:
     7    a. One or more blue [light] lights may be affixed to any motor vehicle
     8  owned by a volunteer member of a fire department or on a  motor  vehicle
     9  owned by a member of such person's family residing in the same household
    10  or  by  a  business  enterprise  in  which such person has a proprietary
    11  interest or by which [he or she] such person is employed, provided  such
    12  volunteer firefighter has been authorized in writing to so affix [a] one
    13  or  more  blue  [light]  lights  by  the chief of the fire department or
    14  company of which [he or she] such person is  a  member,  which  authori-
    15  zation  shall  be  subject  to  revocation  at any time by the chief who
    16  issued the same or [his or her] such person's successor in office.  Such
    17  blue [light] lights may be displayed exclusively by such volunteer fire-
    18  fighter  on  such a vehicle only when engaged in an emergency operation.
    19  The use of blue lights on vehicles shall be restricted for use only by a
    20  volunteer firefighter except as otherwise provided for in  subparagraphs
    21  b and b-1 of this paragraph.
    22    (a) One or more green [light] lights may be affixed to any motor vehi-
    23  cle owned by a member of a volunteer ambulance service or by a member of
    24  a  mobile  crisis  team, or on a motor vehicle owned by a member of such
