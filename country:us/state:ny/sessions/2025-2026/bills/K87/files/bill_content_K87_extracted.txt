and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor BankOnBuffalo upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Flora Leamer Community Involvement Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce at its 52nd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 20, 2025, at Samuel's Grand Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, BankOnBuffalo was selected for this prestigious award for its steadfast and unremitting commitment to its beloved community; and WHEREAS, This esteemed accolade, named after the late Flora Leamer, a passionate and cherished citizen who was devoted to the growth and success of Clarence, honors citizens, organizations, and institutions which have demonstrated the utmost care and concern for their surrounding community, and have created an enduring impact by result of their active involvement; and WHEREAS, From its inception in 2017, BankOnBuffalo has prided itself on its consumer-focused, community-oriented approach in its daily operations; the business emphasizes local decision making and strong relationships with its costumers to meet the diverse financial needs of its clients, ranging from personal banking solutions to commercial lending; and WHEREAS, BankOnBuffalo stands strong in its core values of integrity, trust, and collaboration; these principles have contributed to the institution's continued growth and renowned reputation; and WHEREAS, In addition to its innovative products, hands-on customer service, and deep understanding of community needs, BankOnBuffalo has remained committed to supporting the local economy through community engagement and investments; small businesses, large organizations, and families alike have placed their trust in this financial institution largely due to its strong focus on building lasting, meaningful relationships with the communities it serves; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those who enhance the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown a long and sustained commitment to excellence certainly have earned the recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honoring BankOnBuffalo upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Flora Leamer Community Involvement Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to BankOnBuffalo.
