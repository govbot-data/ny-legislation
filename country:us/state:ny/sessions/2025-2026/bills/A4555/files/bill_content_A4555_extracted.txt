MORINELLO -- read once and referred to the Committee on Environmental Conservation AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to waiv- ing the thirty day residency requirement for certain hunting licenses for active duty military members and their families on permanent orders to be stationed within the state The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 11-0703 of the environmental conservation law is 2 amended by adding a new subdivision 7 to read as follows: 3 7. For the purposes of this title, an applicant shall be deemed to 4 have been a resident in the state for a period of thirty days immediate- 5 ly prior to the date of application for a license issued under this 6 title where the applicant is either: 7 a. an active duty military member on permanent orders to be stationed 8 within the state; or 9 b. a spouse or child of an active duty military member on permanent 10 orders to be stationed within the state. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
