AN ACT to amend chapter 885 of the laws of 1953 authorizing the city of
Albany to discontinue the use of certain park lands for the purpose of
using such lands for public school purposes, in relation to authoriz-
ing the use of such lands for any school purposes
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 1 of chapter 885 of the laws of 1953 authorizing
2 the city of Albany to discontinue the use of certain park lands for the
3 purpose of using such lands for public school purposes, is amended to
4 read as follows:
5 Section 1. [Notwithstanding any inconsistent general, special or local
6 law,] Subject to the provisions of this act, the city of Albany, acting
7 by and through its governing body, may discontinue as park lands and
8 sell and convey at fair market value four and one-half acres of land
9 within the hereinafter described bounds and heretofore acquired and used
10 by such city for park purposes and constituting a portion of the park
11 commonly known as Lincoln Park, in the city of Albany, for the purpose
12 of using such lands for [public] any school purposes and the
13 construction of [public] any school buildings, including streets for
14 ingress and egress.
15 § 2. Chapter 885 of the laws of 1953 authorizing the city of Albany to
16 discontinue the use of certain park lands for the purpose of using such
17 lands for public school purposes, is amended by adding two new sections
18 1-a and 2-a to read as follows:
19 § 1-a. The authorization contained in section one of this act shall
20 only be effective on the condition that the city of Albany dedicate an
21 amount equal to or greater than fair market value of the parklands being
