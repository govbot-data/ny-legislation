Amd §17-812, NYC Ad Cd; amd §404-p, V & T L; amd §97-xx, St Fin L
 
Allocates funding for the New York city animal population control fund for the animal population control program; monies that emanate from any resident or source in NYC from certain violations and fees shall be allocated to the NYC animal population control fund.

        AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city  of  New  York,  the
          vehicle  and  traffic  law  and  the state finance law, in relation to
          funding the animal population control fund

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Section  17-812 of the administrative code of the city of
     2  New York is amended by adding a new subdivision 4 to read as follows:
     3    4. An amount not to exceed fifteen percent of the balance of the  fund
     4  at the beginning of each fiscal year shall be available for the purposes
     5  of implementation and promotion of the animal population control program
     6  established  pursuant  to section 17-811 of this chapter. Such promotion
     7  shall include educating the public about the  benefits  associated  with
     8  spaying and neutering and where to obtain low-cost spaying and neutering
     9  services.
    10    § 2. Subdivision 2 of section 404-p of the vehicle and traffic law, as
    11  amended  by  chapter  577  of  the  laws  of 2007, is amended to read as
    12  follows:
    13    2. A distinctive Animal Population Control Fund plate issued  pursuant
    14  to  this  section  shall  be  issued  in the same manner as other number
    15  plates upon the payment of the regular registration  fee  prescribed  by
    16  section  four  hundred  one  of  this article provided, however, that an
    17  additional annual service charge of twenty-five dollars shall be charged
    18  for such plate. All monies received as annual service charges under this
    19  section shall be deposited  to  the  credit  of  the  animal  population
    20  control  fund  established  pursuant  to  section ninety-seven-xx of the
    21  state finance law, and shall  be  used  for  animal  population  control
    22  programs  undertaken  pursuant to section one hundred seventeen-a of the
    23  agriculture and markets law, except that all monies received  as  annual
    24  service charges under this section from New York city residents shall be
