GRIFFO, MATTERA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to authorizing a nursing home patient to install, operate and maintain an electronic monitoring device or a connected device in such patient's room The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 3 of section 2803-c of the public health law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph s to read as follows: 3 s. Pursuant to regulations promulgated by the commissioner, every 4 patient shall have the right, at such patient's own expense, to install, 5 operate and maintain an electronic monitoring device or connected device 6 in such patient's room. For the purposes of this section, the term 7 "electronic monitoring device" shall mean a surveillance instrument with 8 a fixed position video camera or an audio recording device, or a combi- 9 nation thereof, which is installed in a patient's room and broadcasts or 10 records activity or sounds occurring in such room. For the purposes of 11 this section, the term "connected device" shall mean any device or other 12 physical object that is capable of directly or indirectly connecting to 13 the internet and that is assigned an internet protocol address or blue- 14 tooth address. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 16 have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amendment 17 and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implementation 18 of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and 19 completed on or before such effective date.
