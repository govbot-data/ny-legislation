ROSENTHAL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Social Services AN ACT to amend the education law and the social services law, in relation to notifying individuals that certain work-study, internship, externship or other work placements offered by institutions of higher education meet the work requirements of title nine-B of article five of the social services law The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Part 4 of article 14 of the education law is amended by 2 adding a new section 685 to read as follows: 3 § 685. Work-study, internship, externship or other work placements. 4 Any CUNY, SUNY or other degree granting institution providing work-stu- 5 dy, internship, externship or other work placements that are part of the 6 curriculum shall notify applicants on the application forms that such 7 work-study, internship, externship or other work placements that are 8 part of the curriculum meet the work requirements of title nine-B of 9 article five of the social services law for public assistance. 10 § 2. The social services law is amended by adding a new section 337-a 11 to read as follows: 12 § 337-a. Notification to applicants. The department shall notify 13 applicants on application forms that certain CUNY, SUNY or other degree 14 granting institution providing work-study, internship, externship or 15 other work placements that are part of the curriculum, meet the work 16 requirements of this title. 17 § 3. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth day after it shall 18 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
