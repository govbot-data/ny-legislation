Santabarbara COMMENDING the Capital Region Chamber of Commerce upon the occasion of hosting its Military Appreciation event on May 21, 2026 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to take note of and publicly recognize those organizations within the State of New York which have positively impacted their communities; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Capital Region Chamber of Commerce's Unified Military Affairs Council program upon the occasion of hosting its Military Appreciation event on Thursday, May 21, 2026, in Albany, New York; and WHEREAS, The Unified Military Affairs Council program highlights the importance of the military presence in the Capital Region and helps ensure its long-term viability and success; and WHEREAS, The Unified Military Affairs Council program increases public knowledge of the contributions made by military units in the Capital Region, all of which impact our global, national, state, and regional security and economy; and WHEREAS, The Military Appreciation event will recognize and pay just tribute to the men and women of the Capital Region who have served, or who are serving, our Nation so valiantly and honorably; and WHEREAS, Stratton Air National Guard Base, located in Scotia, New York, is home to the New York Air National Guard's 109th Airlift Wing; and WHEREAS, The 109th Airlift Wing flies the world's only ski-equipped LC-130 "Skibirds," as well as traditional C-130 "Wheelbirds," in support of Arctic and Antarctic operations; and WHEREAS, The New York Army National Guard is the fourth-largest Army National Guard in the Nation; and WHEREAS, The New York Army National Guard traces its history back to the origins of the militia system in colonial America; and WHEREAS, The headquarters of the New York Air National Guard and the New York Army National Guard are located within the Division of Military and Naval Affairs complex in Latham, New York; and WHEREAS, The United States Army Watervliet Arsenal is an Army-owned and operated manufacturing facility located in Watervliet, New York; and WHEREAS, Known as "America's Cannon Factory," the Watervliet Arsenal is the Nation's oldest continuously operating arsenal, having begun manufacturing military hardware during the War of 1812; and WHEREAS, Navy Reserve Center Schenectady, located in Scotia, New York, is one of 118 Navy Reserve Centers throughout the United States; and WHEREAS, The mission of the Navy Reserve is to provide strategic depth and deliver operational capabilities to the Navy, Marine Corps, and Joint Forces in times of peace and war; and WHEREAS, The United States Army Albany Recruiting Battalion, headquartered at the Watervliet Arsenal, encompasses approximately 38,760 square miles; and WHEREAS, The Albany Recruiting Battalion includes the 27 northeastern counties of New York, the five westernmost counties of Massachusetts, and the states of Vermont and Connecticut, and is responsible for the Army's recruiting mission in Europe, Northern Africa, and the Middle East; and WHEREAS, The Albany Recruiting Battalion operates 51 recruiting stations in the continental United States and four recruiting stations in Germany; and WHEREAS, The Capital Region is honored to be home to these critically important military installations, as well as the proud and dedicated service members who carry out their respective missions; and WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to salute the Soldiers, Airmen, and Sailors stationed within the Capital Region, and to honor their dedication and collective contributions to their communities and to the State of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Capital Region Chamber of Commerce's Unified Military Affairs Council program upon the occasion of hosting its Military Appreciation event on May 21, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Capital Region Chamber of Commerce; the Adjutant General of the State of New York; the Commanding Officers of the 109th Airlift Wing, the United States Army Watervliet Arsenal, and Navy Reserve Center Schenectady; the Commanding Officer of the United States Army Albany Recruiting Battalion; and the Members of Congress representing the Capital Region of the State of New York.
