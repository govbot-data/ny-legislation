The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive "POW-MIA" license plates. 1. Any person residing 4 in this state shall, upon request, be issued a distinctive "POW-MIA" 5 motor vehicle or motorcycle license plate of a design approved by the 6 commissioner. Application for said license plate shall be filed in such 7 form and detail as the commissioner shall prescribe. 8 2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 9 in the same manner as other number plates upon the payment of the regu- 10 lar registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this 11 article, provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of 12 twenty-five dollars shall be charged for such plate. Such additional 13 service charge shall be deposited pursuant to section four hundred four- 14 oo of this article to the credit of the department of motor vehicles 15 distinctive plate development fund, established by section ninety-five-g 16 of the state finance law, and shall be used for the production, design, 17 advertising and marketing of distinctive number plates; provided, howev- 18 er, that additional annual service charges collected in excess of six 19 thousand dollars shall be deposited to the credit of the POW-MIA aware- 20 ness fund established pursuant to section ninety-seven-ccc of the state 21 finance law. 22 § 2.
