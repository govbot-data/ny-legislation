WOERNER -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Racing and Wagering AN ACT to amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, in relation to welfare, medical, and retirement plans provided by a horsemen's organization in the absence of contractual obligations The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 318 of the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breed- 2 ing law is amended by adding a new subdivision 6 to read as follows: 3 6. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, in the 4 absence of a contract between the licensed harness racing corporation or 5 association and the representative horsemen's organization, the state 6 gaming commission shall require such corporation or association to pay 7 to the horsemen's organization an amount determined in accordance with 8 the previously negotiated contract for welfare, medical, or retirement 9 plans, including any associated costs, provided by the horsemen's organ- 10 ization to participants in racing at the licensed harness racing facil- 11 ity. The amounts shall be paid monthly from the horsemen's gross purse 12 enhancement amount from video lottery gaming. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
