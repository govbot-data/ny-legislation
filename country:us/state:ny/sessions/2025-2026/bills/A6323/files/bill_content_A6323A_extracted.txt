AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to number
stickers for motor vehicles
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 402 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 9 to read as follows:
3 9. Notwithstanding any other provision of this article, the commis-
4 sioner may develop and offer number stickers to be used on motor vehi-
5 cles in the place of number plates. Such number stickers shall have the
6 same appearance, numbering system and fees as number plates pursuant to
7 this article and shall adhere directly to the body of the vehicle in the
8 same position as a number plate.
9 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
10 have become a law.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
