Subparagraph (A) of paragraph (c) of subdivision 2 of 2 section 259-i of the executive law, as amended by chapter 322 of the 3 laws of 2021, is amended to read as follows: 4 (A) Discretionary release on parole shall not be granted merely as a 5 reward for good conduct or efficient performance of duties while 6 confined but after considering if there is a reasonable probability 7 that, if such incarcerated individual is released, [he or she] such 8 incarcerated individual will live and remain at liberty without violat- 9 ing the law, and that [his or her] their release is not incompatible 10 with the welfare of society and will not so deprecate the seriousness of 11 [his or her] their crime as to undermine respect for law.
