Heastie IN relation to the election and seating of Diana C. Moreno as a Member of the Assembly from the 36th Assembly District WHEREAS, There is a vacancy existing in the membership of this House, which was filled at a special election held on February 3, 2026; and WHEREAS, The final official canvass of the votes cast at said election and certification thereof of the person elected thereat, cannot be made at this time; and WHEREAS, The Board of Elections in the City of New York has advised that the votes cast for the Office of Member of Assembly in the 36th Assembly District in the County of Queens, shown by its canvass were respectively as follows: Diana C. Moreno, Democratic, Working Families six thousand two hundred nine; Rana Abdelhamid, Queens for All, one thousand four hundred thirty-five; Mary Jobaida, People First, six hundred fifty-one; and WHEREAS, It appears that the results of the votes cast in said Assembly District cannot be affected by the final official canvass of such voters; and WHEREAS, Diana C. Moreno has duly taken an oath of office as a Member of Assembly; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That Diana C. Moreno, Democratic, Working Families is duly elected and qualified as a Member of the Assembly for her respective district and that she be seated in this House as such Member.
