Subdivision 20 of section 2.10 of the criminal procedure 2 law, as amended by chapter 693 of the laws of 2023, is amended to read 3 as follows: 4 20. [Bridge and tunnel officers, sergeants, lieutenants, captains, 5 inspectors, deputy chiefs, assistant chiefs, and chiefs] Sworn officers 6 of the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
