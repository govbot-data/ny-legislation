PARKER, COONEY, RAMOS, SEPULVEDA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Energy and Telecommunications AN ACT directing the New York state energy research and development authority to conduct a study on powering cryptocurrency mining centers with renewable energy The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. a. The state energy research and development authority is 2 hereby directed to conduct a study on powering cryptocurrency mining 3 centers in New York state. 4 b. For purposes of this act: 5 (i) "cryptocurrency" shall mean a digital currency in which encryption 6 techniques are used to regulate the generation of units and currency and 7 verify the transfer of funds, operating independently from a central 8 bank; 9 (ii) "mining" shall mean the process of spending computer power to 10 process transactions and add such transactions to a cryptocurrencies' 11 blockchain; 12 (iii) "blockchain" shall mean a digital ledger in which transactions 13 made in cryptocurrencies are recorded chronologically and publicly. 14 c. The study shall include, but not be limited to: 15 (i) a survey of existing cryptocurrency mining centers in New York 16 state, and whether and to what extent such centers are powered by renew- 17 able energy; 18 (ii) an evaluation of the economic and technical feasibility of power- 19 ing all cryptocurrency mining centers in the state with renewable energy 20 and the construction of additional facilities powered by renewable ener- 21 gy; EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
