Declaration of purpose. The legislature declares that no elder- 17 ly person in the state shall be subjected to abuse or deprivation. 18 Towards that end the "elderly abuse protective act" is enacted. 19 § 311. Definitions. For purposes of this title: 20 1. The term "elderly person" means any resident of the state who is 21 sixty-two years of age or older. 22 2. An elderly person shall be deemed to be "in need of protective 23 services" if such person is unable to perform or obtain services which 24 are necessary to maintain physical and mental health.
