VANEL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Consumer Affairs and Protection AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to online market- place verification The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section 2 396-ddd to read as follows: 3 § 396-ddd. Online marketplace verification. 1. An online marketplace 4 shall require every person engaged in a transaction on its marketplace 5 website, application or software, to verify their identity with the 6 online marketplace. For the purposes of this section, "online market- 7 place" shall mean any website, application or software connected to the 8 internet which, in the regular course of business, allows members of the 9 public to buy and sell goods and services. 10 2. The verification required by subdivision one of this section shall 11 be conducted through a method reasonably designed to identify the true 12 identity of a person. 13 3. An online marketplace shall be liable for a civil penalty of five 14 hundred dollars per sale made between any parties which is made in 15 violation of this section. 16 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 17 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
