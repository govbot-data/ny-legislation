and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to commemorate the 2025 IFN Investor Americas Forum, to be held on Tuesday, June 17, 2025, in Manhattan, New York; and WHEREAS, The IFN Investor Americas Forum is organized by REDmoney Events and marks its inaugural return to the United States since 2019, reflecting the evolving landscape of Islamic finance and investment engagement between North America and the Gulf Cooperation Council region; and WHEREAS, The Forum brings together leading institutional investors, asset managers, sovereign wealth funds, Shariah advisory firms, and regulatory professionals to explore two-way investment flows and the rise of Shariah-compliant finance in the American marketplace; and WHEREAS, The Forum's bespoke format features high-level panel discussions, targeted networking, and strategic dialogues designed to foster collaboration, promote market accessibility, and align global capital with socially responsible and Shariah-compliant investment standards; and WHEREAS, The State of New York stands as a global hub of commerce and finance, and the presence of the IFN Investor Americas Forum in New York City further affirms the state's role in shaping inclusive, ethical, and forward-looking financial markets; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 2025 IFN Investor Americas Forum, to be held on June 17, 2025, and to applaud REDmoney Events and all participating institutions for their role in advancing global financial dialogue and innovation; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the organizers of the IFN Investor Americas Forum.
