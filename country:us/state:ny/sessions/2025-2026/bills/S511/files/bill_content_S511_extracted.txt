PALUMBO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Investigations and Govern- ment Operations AN ACT to amend the civil rights law, in relation to preventing unfounded complaints against police officers, peace officers, fire- fighters, correction officers and emergency medical service providers from being disclosed or added to a personnel record The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The civil rights law is amended by adding a new section 2 50-a to read as follows: 3 § 50-a. Personnel records of police officers, peace officers, fire- 4 fighters, correction officers and emergency medical service providers. 5 1.
