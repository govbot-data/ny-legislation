Section 2045 of the education law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 5 to read as follows: 3 5. No charge for the instruction of a nonresident pupil shall be made 4 if the nonresident pupil is enrolled in a school where the pupil's 5 parent or guardian is a principal, a teacher or a paraprofessional or 6 the nonresident pupil is enrolled at another school within the same 7 district as a school where the pupil's parent or guardian is a princi- 8 pal, a teacher or a paraprofessional. 9 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall apply to school 10 years commencing on and after July 1, 2025.
