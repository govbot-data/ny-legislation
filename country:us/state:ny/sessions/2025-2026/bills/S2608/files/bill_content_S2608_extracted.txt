ADDABBO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT in relation to virtual or remote instruction days for public schools and private schools for students who require nursing services The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. A day of virtual or remote instruction, if instituted under 2 a program approved by the commissioner, shall be considered the equiv- 3 alent of a full day of school attendance for the purposes of meeting 4 state and local graduation requirements, the awarding of course credit, 5 and such other matters as determined by the commissioner. 6 If a program of virtual or remote instruction is implemented for the 7 general education students, the same educational opportunities shall be 8 provided to students who require nursing services. Nursing services may 9 be delivered to students with disabilities through the use of electronic 10 communication or a virtual or online platform or in the home of the 11 student and as required by the student's individualized education 12 program, to the greatest extent practicable. 13 Nursing services shall be reimbursed for by the school district at the 14 rate of a full instructional day of school attendance whether on the 15 school campus or through the use of electronic communication or a virtu- 16 al or online platform or in the home of the student. 17 Guardians of students who require nursing services may retain the 18 provider of nursing services of their choice or accept the provider 19 offered by the school district. 20 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
