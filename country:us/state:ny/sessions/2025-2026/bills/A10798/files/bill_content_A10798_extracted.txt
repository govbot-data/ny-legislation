BENEDETTO -- read once and referred to the Committee on Judiciary AN ACT to amend the real property law, in relation to the recovery of attorneys' fees in the event of certain default judgments where a tenant is a dwelling unit owner or shareholder of a cooperative hous- ing corporation The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 234 of the real property law, as 2 added by chapter 789 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as follows: 3 2. Notwithstanding the provisions of subdivision one of this section, 4 where a tenant is a dwelling unit owner or shareholder of a cooperative 5 housing corporation, [other than a cooperative housing corporation 6 subject to the provisions of article two, article four, article five or 7 article eleven of the private housing finance law,] attorney's fees may 8 be awarded to either party in the event of default judgment if recovery 9 of attorney's fees is provided for in the proprietary lease or occupancy 10 agreement. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
