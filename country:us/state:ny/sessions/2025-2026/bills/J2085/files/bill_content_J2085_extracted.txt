and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Superintendent William Dowsland for 30 years of service in education; he will officially retire at the end of the 2025-2026 school year; and WHEREAS, William Dowsland has served the Hamilton, New York, community for most of his career, initially joining the school district as a substitute teacher in 1998 and becoming Superintendent of Schools in 2019; through the years, he came to view his Hamilton colleagues as his extended family; and WHEREAS, Prior to joining Hamilton, William Dowsland worked for a year as a teacher at Madison-Oneida BOCES and spent six years in professional hockey, working for the New Jersey Devils Organization's American Hockey League affiliate; and WHEREAS, William Dowsland spent 13 years as a physical education and health teacher; later, he held the titles of Director of Physical Education in the Health and Athletics Department as well as Assistant Principal and Principal of the Junior-Senior High School; during that time, he coached the softball team, earning honors of Coach of the Year from New York State, the Utica Observer-Dispatch, and the Syracuse Post-Standard; and WHEREAS, William Dowsland's tenure with Hamilton Central School District boasts many achievements: the District's budget approved by voters every year; two successful capital projects completed; implementation of social-emotional learning initiatives; commencement of a student board member program prior to the recent State mandate; and expansion of academic offerings in Advanced Placement, dual credit, foreign language, the arts, and CTE programs; and WHEREAS, Additionally, Hamilton Central School District received State and National recognitions for academics, extracurricular activities, music, and the honor of being recognized as one of America's Healthiest Schools, a National initiative which honors schools that support the whole child's health and well-being; and WHEREAS, William Dowsland and his wife have three sons, all Hamilton Central School graduates, and five grandchildren, all with whom he plans to enjoy more time during his retirement; and WHEREAS, The many contributions, of which William Dowsland has played a vital role, are a testament to his legacy which will inspire future students of Hamilton Central School District for generations to come; throughout the three decades of tireless service to others, he led with integrity, care, and a genuine commitment to the students, staff, and Hamilton community; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those dedicated individuals who have shown a long and sustained commitment to excellence unquestionably have earned the recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Superintendent William Dowsland upon the occasion of his retirement and to wish him well in all his future endeavors; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to William Dowsland.
