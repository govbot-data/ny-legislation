HOYLMAN-SIGAL, GALLIVAN, HARCKHAM, MAYER, SERRANO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Insurance AN ACT to amend the insurance law, in relation to coverage of long term medical care for Lyme disease and other tick borne related pathogens; and to amend the tax law and the state finance law, in relation to providing for taxpayer gifts for tick borne illness research, detection and education and establishing the tick borne illness research, detection and education fund The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1.
