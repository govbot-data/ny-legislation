Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01523 Summary:BILL NOJ01523  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORSCARCELLA-SPANTON COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01523 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1523 BY: Senator SCARCELLA-SPANTON HONORING Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon upon the occasion of her designation as New Yorker of the Week by Spectrum News NY1 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and honor those outstanding individuals whose commitment, dedication, and service contribute significantly to the vitality and well-being of the communities of New York State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon upon the occasion of her designation as New Yorker of the Week by Spectrum News NY1 on Monday, January 26, 2026; and WHEREAS, Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon is a resident of West Brighton, a neighborhood in Staten Island, New York, and she helps stock and maintain a Little Free Library along the North Shore Esplanade; and WHEREAS, Little Free Libraries are small boxes scattered throughout communities where people can take or leave a book of their choosing with no membership, card, or payment necessary; to Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon, these boxes are a way to foster community connection while encouraging reading in her neighborhood; and WHEREAS, Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon has spent over a decade tending to dozens of Little Free Libraries throughout Staten Island, New York, although three of her favorite locations are along the North Shore, due in part to their scenic location which allows passersby to enjoy water views while exploring new books; and WHEREAS, An avid reader and passionate lover of literature, Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon comes from a family that values books, and she dedicated her professional career to working in operations in publishing and news; and WHEREAS, The skills that Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon has developed throughout her lifetime assist her in her community work, and she has made it her mission to breathe new life into areas that she feels are often overlooked; and WHEREAS, In addition to her great work with Little Free Libraries throughout Staten Island, New York, Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon works in groups to help organize chess tournaments, Veterans Day commemorations, neighborhood cleanups, and voter registration efforts; she has also been a vital part of launching an initiative to bring resources and improved lighting to a nearby commercial corridor; and WHEREAS, Spectrum News NY1's recognition of Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon as New Yorker of the Week is a fitting tribute to an individual whose commitment to service and love of her neighborhood has strengthened the Staten Island community and enriched the lives of countless individuals and families; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon upon the occasion of her designation as New Yorker of the Week by Spectrum News NY1 on January 26, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Veronica "Ronnie" Gambon.
