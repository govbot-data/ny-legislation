WALSH -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the public officers law, in relation to personally iden- tifying information in municipal land use documents subject to FOIL The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subparagraphs vii and viii of paragraph (b) of subdivision 2 2 of section 89 of the public officers law, as amended by section 2 of 3 part GGG of chapter 59 of the laws of 2019, are amended and a new 4 subparagraph ix is added to read as follows: 5 vii. disclosure of electronic contact information, such as an e-mail 6 address or a social network username, that has been collected from a 7 taxpayer under section one hundred four of the real property tax law; 8 [or] 9 viii. disclosure of law enforcement arrest or booking photographs of 10 an individual, unless public release of such photographs will serve a 11 specific law enforcement purpose and disclosure is not precluded by any 12 state or federal laws; or 13 ix. disclosure of personally identifying information of private indi- 14 viduals contained in an application and/or supporting materials submit- 15 ted to a municipal planning board, zoning board of appeals, or similar 16 local land-use body, where such disclosure would constitute an unwar- 17 ranted invasion of personal privacy or create a risk of fraud or identi- 18 ty theft; provided, however, that this subparagraph shall not be 19 construed to authorize the redaction of information necessary to under- 20 stand the nature of such application or the basis of such municipal 21 planning board's, zoning board of appeals', or similar local land-use 22 body's determination regarding such application. 23 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
