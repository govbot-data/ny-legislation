BURDICK -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Governmental Employees AN ACT to amend the retirement and social security law, in relation to establishing a retirement benefit review panel The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The retirement and social security law is amended by adding 2 a new section 803-b to read as follows: 3 § 803-b. Retirement benefit review panel. a. The comptroller shall 4 establish a retirement benefit review panel consisting of at least 5 fifteen members, any three of whom may sit as a team to review a partic- 6 ular case. Members of the panel shall have expertise in the various 7 retirement systems of the state, including, but not limited to, member- 8 ship, service credit, retirement options and the transfer of retirement 9 service between retirement systems within and without the state. To the 10 extent practicable, the workload of the panel should be evenly distrib- 11 uted among its members. Members of the panel should be free to exercise 12 independent professional judgment without pressure or retaliation for 13 the exercise of that judgment from any source. 14 b. Any member of a retirement system in this state, after seeking 15 relief pursuant to: (1) section eight hundred three of this article; (2) 16 a hearing; and/or (3) any other provision of law, who has failed to 17 obtain the relief sought, may have their case heard before the retire- 18 ment benefit review panel. Such panel is authorized to hear testimony 19 and admit evidence that such panel deems relevant to the case before it. 20 Upon the hearing of all testimony and admission of all evidence, the 21 panel shall make its determination regarding the relief sought. Any 22 determination made by such panel shall be final. 23 c. The comptroller is authorized to promulgate rules and regulations 24 to implement the provisions of this section. 25 § 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred twentieth day after 26 it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amend- 27 ment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implemen- 28 tation of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and 29 completed on or before such effective date.
