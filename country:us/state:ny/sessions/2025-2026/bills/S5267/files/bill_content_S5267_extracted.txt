The section heading and subsections (a) and (b) of section 2 2606 of the insurance law, as amended by chapter 159 of the laws of 3 1993, are amended and a new subsection (g) is added to read as follows: 4 Discrimination [because of race, color, creed, national origin, or 5 disability]; prohibited.
