AN ACT prohibiting the diminution of retiree health benefits by a public
employer
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. On and after the effective date of this act, a public
2 employer shall be prohibited from diminishing the health insurance bene-
3 fits provided to retirees, who retired before, on or after the effective
4 date of this act, and their dependents or the contributions such employ-
5 er makes for such health insurance coverage below the level of such
6 benefits or contributions made on behalf of such retirees and their
7 dependents by such employer as of the retiree's date of retirement. For
8 the purpose of this act, "public employer" shall mean any professional
9 police and fire fighter in the state of New York.
10 § 2. Nothing contained in this act shall supersede or diminish the
11 terms of a collective bargaining agreement.
12 § 3. Notwithstanding the provisions of section one of this act to the
13 contrary, a public employer shall be prohibited from diminishing the
14 health insurance benefits provided to retirees who retire after the
15 effective date of this act from positions not subject to collective
16 bargaining agreement and their dependents, or contributions such employ-
