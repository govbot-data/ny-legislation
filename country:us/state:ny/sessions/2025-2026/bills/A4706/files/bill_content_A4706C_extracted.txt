37 d. Any graduate of a chiropractic school which meets standards satis-

39 supervision of a licensed chiropractor for a maximum period of twelve
40 months from date of graduation.
41 2. Limit of practice. A permittee shall be authorized to practice
42 chiropractic only under the supervision of a licensed chiropractor and
43 only in a licensed chiropractic office or clinical setting.

45 permit may be renewed annually at the discretion of the department.
