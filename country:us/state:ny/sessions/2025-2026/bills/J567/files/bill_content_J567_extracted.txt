and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Illustrious Potentate Noble Calvin Robinson and Illustrious Commandress Daughter Deborah Crouch upon the occasion of their designation for special recognition at the Aleppo Temple No. 88 and Aleppo Court No. 140 Joint Ball Weekend on April 11-13, 2025; and WHEREAS, Aleppo Temple No. 88 and Aleppo Court No. 140 will host their esteemed Joint Ball Weekend from April 11- to April 13, 2025, bringing together Nobles and Daughters of the Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine (A.E.A.O.N.M.S.), Inc. for a celebration of excellence, camaraderie, and service; and WHEREAS, This grand occasion serves to honor Illustrious Potentate Noble Calvin Robinson and Illustrious Commandress Daughter Deborah Crouch for their outstanding leadership, unwavering dedication, and profound commitment to the mission of the Shrine; and WHEREAS, Through their vision and service, Illustrious Potentate Robinson and Illustrious Commandress Crouch have worked tirelessly to uphold the principles of charity, integrity, and fraternal unity, fostering a spirit of goodwill and collective progress within Aleppo Temple No. 88 and Aleppo Court No. 140; and WHEREAS, The Joint Ball Weekend not only highlights the achievements of these esteemed leaders but also serves as a testament to the enduring impact of the Shrine in promoting community engagement, education, and philanthropy; and WHEREAS, The members of Aleppo Temple No. 88 and Aleppo Court No. 140 remain steadfast in their commitment to uplifting their communities through charitable initiatives, youth mentorship programs, and civic engagement, reflecting the highest ideals of their noble order; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper to honor these remarkable individuals and recognize the meaningful contributions of Aleppo Temple No. 88 and Aleppo Court No. 140 in strengthening the bonds of unity, service, and excellence; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize and commend Aleppo Temple No. 88 and Aleppo Court No. 140 upon the occasion of their Joint Ball Weekend, and to honor Illustrious Potentate Noble Calvin Robinson and Illustrious Commandress Daughter Deborah Crouch for their exemplary leadership and dedication to service; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Aleppo Temple No. 88 and Aleppo Court No. 140, as well as Illustrious Potentate Noble Calvin Robinson and Illustrious Commandress Daughter Deborah Crouch.
