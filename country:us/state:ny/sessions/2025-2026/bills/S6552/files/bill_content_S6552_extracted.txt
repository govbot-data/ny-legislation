GRIFFO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to the use of flashing green lights on snow plows The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subparagraph c of paragraph 5 of subdivision 41 of section 2 375 of the vehicle and traffic law, as amended by chapter 71 of the laws 3 of 2024, is amended to read as follows: 4 (c)(i) In addition to the amber light authorized to be displayed 5 pursuant to paragraph three of this subdivision, one or more rear-facing 6 green lights or combination green and amber lights may be affixed to a 7 hazard vehicle designed for ice and snow removal owned and operated by 8 the state or by a county, city, town or village. Such green light or 9 lights may be displayed on a hazard vehicle designed for ice and snow 10 removal owned and operated by the state or by a county, city, town or 11 village when such vehicle is engaged in a hazardous operation and is 12 also displaying the amber light or lights required to be displayed 13 during a hazardous operation pursuant to paragraph three of this subdi- 14 vision. Green lights or combination green and amber lights authorized 15 by this subparagraph shall be displayed and directed only in a rear-fac- 16 ing manner.
