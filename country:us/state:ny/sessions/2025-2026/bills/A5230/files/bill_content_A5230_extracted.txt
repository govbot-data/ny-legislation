SOLAGES -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, the highway law and the public health law, in relation to establishing the lactation accommo- dation in public transportation facilities act The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "lactation accommodation in public transportation facilities act". 3 § 2. Section 361-c of the public authorities law, as added by chapter 4 876 of the laws of 1976, is amended to read as follows: 5 § 361-c. [Tourist information] Service facilities. 1. The authority 6 shall provide tourist information services at all service areas where 7 food is offered for sale, provided that: the department of commerce and 8 or local and regional tourist promotion agencies shall provide the 9 authority with ample supplies of such literature and brochures necessary 10 for distribution to the traveling public. 11 2. When there is construction of a new service area, or the replace- 12 ment, expansion, or substantial renovation of an existing service area, 13 the authority shall provide a private room for nursing mothers at all 14 service areas where food is offered for sale. A private room for nursing 15 mothers must meet the following conditions: 16 (a) includes, at a minimum, a chair and an electrical outlet; and 17 (b) is located outside the confinement of a public restroom. 18 § 3. The public health law is amended by adding a new section 2505-c 7 to read as follows: 8 § 2505-c. Private place for nursing mothers. 1. On or before January 9 first, two thousand twenty-six, the manager of a railroad station or bus 10 station, consisting of an indoor waiting area with public restrooms, 11 operated by a city, county, city and county, state, or public benefit 12 corporation, that conducts commercial operations and that has more than 13 five hundred thousand passengers a year, shall provide a room or other 14 location for members of the public to express breast milk in private 15 that meets both of the following conditions: 16 (a) includes, at a minimum, a chair and an electrical outlet; and 17 (b) is located outside of the confines of a public restroom. 18 2. Upon construction of a new station that has indoor waiting areas 19 with public restrooms or the replacement, expansion, or renovation of an 20 existing station, a railroad or bus station shall provide a sink in any 21 room or other location designated to comply with the provisions of this 22 section. 23 3.
