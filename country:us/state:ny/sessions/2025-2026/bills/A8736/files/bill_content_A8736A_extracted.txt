AN ACT to amend the correction law, in relation to requirements for
vendors who make products available for order and delivery to incar-
cerated individuals and provide for safe food storage in correctional
facilities
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 112 of the correction law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 8 to read as follows:
3 8. (a) The commissioner shall promulgate rules and regulations requir-
4 ing every vendor which contracts with the department to provide ordering
5 and delivery of products to incarcerated individuals housed at correc-
6 tional facilities to:
7 (i) verify with the department that all items available for purchase
8 on behalf of an incarcerated individual are in full compliance with all
9 applicable rules, regulations and directives;
10 (ii) coordinate with the department to track the number, content and
11 weight of orders shipped to an incarcerated individual and notify a
12 purchaser if an order would exceed a limit for such incarcerated indi-
13 vidual and include information on what modifications, if any, to the
14 order would make it compliant with applicable restrictions;
15 (iii) have a system for notifying purchasers when all or part of an
16 order has been rejected by a correctional facility and provide a full or
17 partial refund to the purchaser as applicable; and
18 (iv) conspicuously label packages with perishable food items in a form
19 and manner as determined by the department to facilitate the timely
20 processing of such packages by a correctional facility.
