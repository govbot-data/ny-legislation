Notwithstanding section 134 of the civil service law or any 2 other provision of law, rule or regulation to the contrary, effective 3 April 1, 2025, the director of the budget shall amend the state's over- 4 time compensation eligibility policy as specified in Budget Bulletin 5 G-1024, as issued July 27, 1986, to include all positions allocated up 6 to and including salary grade 23. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall be deemed to 8 have been in full force and effect on and after April 1, 2025.
