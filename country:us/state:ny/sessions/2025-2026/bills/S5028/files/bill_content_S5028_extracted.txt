GRIFFO, STEC, TEDISCO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Trans- portation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to distinctive plates for legion of valor recipients The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive plates for legion of valor recipients. 1. Any 4 person who is a recipient of the army medal of honor, navy medal of 5 honor, air force medal of honor, army distinguished service cross, navy 6 cross, coast guard cross, or air force cross residing in this state 7 shall, upon request, be issued a license plate bearing the words "legion 8 of valor" and depicting the individual's military award decal. Applica- 9 tion for said license plate shall be filed with the commissioner in such 10 form and detail as the commissioner shall prescribe. 11 2. The distinctive plate authorized herein shall be issued upon proof, 12 satisfactory to the commissioner, that the applicant is an army medal of 13 honor, navy medal of honor, air force medal of honor, army distinguished 14 service cross, navy cross, coast guard cross, or air force cross recipi- 15 ent. 16 3. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 17 in the same manner as other number plates, provided, however, that no 18 registration fee or service charge shall be charged for such plate. 19 § 2. 1. A distinctive plate established pursuant to section 404-ff of 20 the vehicle and traffic law as added by section one of this act shall 21 only be designed, produced and issued upon the delivery to the depart- 22 ment of a surety bond in the amount of six thousand dollars, which shall 23 be executed by a surety company authorized by the department of finan- 24 cial services to transact business in this state.
