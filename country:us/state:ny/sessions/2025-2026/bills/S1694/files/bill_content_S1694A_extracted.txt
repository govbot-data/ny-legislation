AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to prohibiting recording of
the entrance of a school ground
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 240.26 of the penal law, as amended by chapter 109
2 of the laws of 1994, is amended to read as follows:
3 § 240.26 Harassment in the second degree.
4 A person is guilty of harassment in the second degree when, with
5 intent to harass, annoy or alarm another person:
6 1. [He or she] The actor strikes, shoves, kicks or otherwise subjects
7 such other person to physical contact, or attempts or threatens to do
8 the same; or
9 2. [He or she] The actor follows a person in or about a public place
10 or places; or
11 3. [He or she] The actor engages in a course of conduct or repeatedly
12 commits acts which alarm or seriously annoy such other person and which
13 serve no legitimate purpose[.]; or
14 4. The actor either (a) broadcasts or records the entrance or exit to
15 school grounds or the entrance or exit to a property that contains
16 school grounds within its boundaries; or (b) uses or installs, or
17 permits the utilization or installation of a mechanical, digital or
18 electronic viewing device, camera, cellular phone or any other instru-
19 ment capable of recording, storing or transmitting visual images to
20 view, broadcast or record an entrance or exit to a school ground or an
21 entry or exit to a property that contains school grounds within its
22 boundaries.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
