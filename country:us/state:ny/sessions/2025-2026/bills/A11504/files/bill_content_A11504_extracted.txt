No election of a comptroller or an 8 attorney-general shall be had except at the time of electing a governor. 9 The comptroller shall be required: (1) to audit all vouchers before 10 payment and all official accounts; (2) to audit the accrual and 11 collection of all revenues and receipts; [and] (3) to prescribe such 12 methods of accounting as are necessary for the performance of the fore- 13 going duties; and (4) to review and approve contracts made for or by any 14 state agency, department, board, officer, commission, or institution 15 prior to being executed or becoming effective except that the legisla- 16 ture shall set a minimum contract value for such review and approval by 17 joint resolution of the senate and assembly upon recommendation of the 18 comptroller, provided, however, any such review and approval may be 19 temporarily suspended by the governor during a declared disaster emer- 20 gency as provided for by law, which the legislature may terminate at any 21 time by concurrent resolution.
