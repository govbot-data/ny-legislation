AN ACT to amend the private housing finance law, in relation to estab-
lishing the block by block homeownership program
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The private housing finance law is amended by adding a new
2 article 33 to read as follows:
3 ARTICLE XXXIII
4 BLOCK BY BLOCK HOMEOWNERSHIP PROGRAM
5 Section 1300. Legislative purpose.
6 1301. Block by block homeownership program.
7 § 1300. Legislative purpose. It is hereby declared and found that
8 there exists across upstate New York, and particularly in the cities of
9 Buffalo, Rochester, Syracuse, Albany, and Binghamton, a shortage of
10 affordable housing units available for purchase. It is further found
11 that homeownership is essential for building generational wealth, and
12 that the state therefore has an interest in promoting homeownership. In
13 order, further, to promote homeownership, it is hereby declared that
14 additional provisions should be made to provide public monies for the
15 purposes of constructing, preserving, and rehabilitating one- to two-fa-
16 mily dwellings on underutilized land throughout the state, with an
17 emphasis on the cities of Buffalo, Rochester, Syracuse, Albany, and
18 Binghamton for purchase by low and moderate income buyers. The necessi-
19 ty in the public interest for the provisions of this article is hereby
20 declared as a matter of legislative determination.
21 § 1301. Block by block homeownership program. 1. The division of
22 housing and community renewal shall, subject to appropriation, develop a
23 program to make capital subsidies available for the purpose of
24 constructing, preserving, and rehabilitating one- to two-family dwell-

15 4. The division of housing and community renewal shall restrict any

18 who intends to make the dwelling their primary residence.
19 5. Nothing in this article shall preclude the use of additional loans
