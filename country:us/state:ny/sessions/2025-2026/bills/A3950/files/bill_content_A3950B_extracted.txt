AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to the submission of
requests to the committee on special education for the educational
services mandated by an individualized education services program
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 3602-c of the education law is amended by adding
2 two new subdivisions 2-d and 2-e to read as follows:
3 2-d. For education provided to students with disabilities beginning in
4 the two thousand twenty-five--two thousand twenty-six school year and
5 each school year thereafter, the school district of location shall annu-
6 ally provide written notice to parents or persons in parental relation
7 to all students with disabilities who have been parentally-placed in and
8 are attending nonpublic schools within their district regarding the
9 process for requesting special education services. Such notice shall:
10 (i) be provided in any of the twelve most common non-English languages
11 spoken by limited-English proficient individuals in the state, based on
12 the data in the most recent American community survey published by the
13 United States census bureau, except that any city school district in a
14 city having a population of one million or more inhabitants shall
15 provide translations in accordance with that district's established
16 policies and practices for parent-facing translation services; (ii) be
17 provided at least twenty-eight days before any deadlines by which
18 requests for special education services must be submitted; (iii) specify
19 all deadlines by which requests for special education services must be
20 submitted; (iv) clearly describe the procedures for submitting such
21 requests and detail any required documentation; and (v) provide contact
