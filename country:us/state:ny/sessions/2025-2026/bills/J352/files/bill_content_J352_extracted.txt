Repollet, President of Kean University, upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition at The Positive Community Media's 6th Annual Great American Emancipation Day Awards Celebration on February 22, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend individuals whose leadership and dedication to education have transformed lives and strengthened communities; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Dr. Lamont O. Repollet, President of Kean University, upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition at The Positive Community Media's 6th Annual Great American Emancipation Day Awards Celebration on Saturday, February 22, 2025; and WHEREAS, As a respected educator and transformative leader, Dr. Lamont O. Repollet has dedicated his career to expanding access to higher education, advancing academic excellence, and fostering student success; and WHEREAS, Under his visionary leadership, Kean University has expanded its programs, partnerships, and community impact, ensuring that students from diverse backgrounds receive a world-class education; and WHEREAS, A strong advocate for equity in education, Dr. Lamont O. Repollet has worked to increase opportunities for underserved students and champion policies that drive academic innovation and institutional growth; and WHEREAS, His commitment to higher education, leadership development, and student achievement has left a lasting impact on generations of scholars and the broader academic community; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body recognize the extraordinary achievements of Dr. Lamont O. Repollet and commend him for his dedication to higher education and community empowerment; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Dr. Lamont O. Repollet upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition at The Positive Community Media's 6th Annual Great American Emancipation Day Awards Celebration; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Dr. Lamont O. Repollet, President of Kean University. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
