BRISPORT -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Environmental Conservation AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to prohibiting the department of environmental conservation from issuing or authorizing any permits to new or expanding large concentrated animal feeding operations The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a 2 new section 17-0709 to read as follows: 3 § 17-0709. Applications for a permit for the operation of concentrated 4 feeding operations in which livestock are kept; prohibi- 5 tion. 6 1. The department shall not issue a license or permit or approval for 7 the operation of a new large concentrated animal feeding operation, or 8 an addition to, or expansion of, an existing animal feeding operation if 9 the addition or expansion would cause the animal feeding operation to 10 become a large concentrated animal feeding operation or increase the 11 animal stabling or confinement capacity of an existing large concen- 12 trated animal feeding operation. 13 2. As used in this section, "large concentrated animal feeding opera- 14 tion" shall have the same meaning as defined in 6 NYCRR 750-1.2(a)(23). 15 3. Nothing in this section shall be interpreted to prohibit a person 16 from operating an animal feeding operation, if prior to the effective 17 date of this section, such person has already received a permit for the 18 operation of an animal feeding operation as otherwise allowed under this 19 chapter. 20 § 2. The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a new 21 section 17-0833 to read as follows: 22 § 17-0833. General permit for concentrated animal feeding operations; 23 modification. If a proposed expansion to the operation of an existing concen- 7 trated animal facility operation would cause the animal feeding opera- 8 tion to become a large concentrated animal feeding operation, as defined 9 in 6 NYCRR 750-1.2(a)(23), or increase the stabling or confinement 10 capacity of an existing large concentrated animal feeding operation, the 11 department shall deny the proposed expansion. 12 § 3.
