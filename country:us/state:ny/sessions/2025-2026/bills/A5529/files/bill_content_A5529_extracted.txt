This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Chisholm 2 Chance Act". 3 § 2. The legislature hereby finds and 14 declares that severe pregnant and birthing person morbidity constitutes 15 a public health crisis that poses a serious threat to the health and 16 quality of life of many pregnant and birthing persons and children. New 17 York is among the bottom third of states for the number of pregnant and 18 birthing persons dying, with pregnant and birthing persons of color 19 dying at a rate three and one-half times more than other non-pregnant or 20 birthing persons who could bear children. The highest rates of severe 21 pregnant and birthing persons morbidity occur in two counties, Kings and 22 Bronx county, with the highest rate occurring in north and central 23 Brooklyn. State university of New York center of pregnant and birthing 7 persons and childcare services. 1. The center shall also collaborate with the state university of New 22 York downstate health sciences center for the conduction of research to 23 inform and support health care professionals providing pregnant and 24 birthing persons and childcare services at the center.
