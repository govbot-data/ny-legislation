AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to requiring that two
electronic benefit transfer credit or debit cards be issued to certain
households receiving special supplemental nutrition program for women,
infants and children (WIC) benefits
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The section heading of section 2507 of the public health
2 law, as amended by chapter 12 of the laws of 2017, is amended and a new
3 subdivision 3 is added to read as follows:
4 Special supplemental nutrition program for women, infants and chil-
5 dren[; specialty formula].
6 3. Any electronic benefit transfer services for WIC benefits shall
7 ensure that two electronic benefit transfer credit or debit cards are
8 issued for use by any recipient household where such additional house-
9 hold member is either a parent, guardian or caretaker of the covered
10 infant or a spouse, domestic partner or parent of the covered mother.
11 No card shall be issued to any person under the age of eighteen unless
12 such person is a parent or guardian of a minor child.
13 (a) The department shall be responsible for any additional expenses
14 associated with the provision of additional electronic benefit transfer
15 cards.
16 (b) The department shall not reduce the availability or the level of
17 benefits provided to new or existing covered mothers, infants and chil-
18 dren in association with providing additional electronic benefit trans-
19 fer cards.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
