O'MARA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to authorizing any justice or judge in the state to obtain a license to have and carry concealed a pistol or revolver The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (d) of subdivision 2 of section 400.00 of the 2 penal law, as amended by chapter 371 of the laws of 2022, is amended to 3 read as follows: 4 (d) have and carry concealed by a justice of the supreme court [in the 5 first or second judicial departments], or by a judge of [the New York 6 city civil court or the New York city criminal] any court in the state; 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
