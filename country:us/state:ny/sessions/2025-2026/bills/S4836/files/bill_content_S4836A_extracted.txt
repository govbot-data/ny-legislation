AN ACT to amend the judiciary law, in relation to increasing the number
of county court judges in certain jurisdictions
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 42 of section 182 of the judiciary law, as
2 added by chapter 699 and as renumbered by chapter 751 of the laws of
3 1969, is amended to read as follows:
4 42. Schenectady, [one] two;
5 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that
6 the additional county court judge shall first be elected at the general
7 election to be held on November 3, 2026 and shall take office January 1,
8 2027.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
