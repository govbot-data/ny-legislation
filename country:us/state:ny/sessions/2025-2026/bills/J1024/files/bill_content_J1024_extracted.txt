and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the City of Schenectady upon the occasion of hosting its 20th Annual Little Italy StreetFest on Saturday, September 6, 2025, on North Jay Street; and WHEREAS, The Schenectady Digital History Archive contains a chronicle entitled, "Electric City Immigrants," which connotes the significant contributions made by Italian Americans in the city's early years; and WHEREAS, For many decades, the State of New York has held the distinction of having the highest population of Italian Americans in the United States; and WHEREAS Italian American food and winemaking are based primarily on the culinary traditions of Southern Italian immigrants, although a significant number of Northern Italian immigrants also came to the United States and influenced this style of cuisine to some extent; and WHEREAS, Established in 2006, the Annual Little Italy StreetFest is held each year after Labor Day, and with the help of The Little Italy Business Association, has been a staple in the Schenectady community ever since; and WHEREAS, The Little Italy StreetFest attracts renowned local establishments such as Perreca's, More Perreca's, Civitello's, and Cornell's, which bring the flavors of Italy to the heart of Schenectady and serve up Italian favorites such as pasta, meatballs, Italian ice, gelato, tomato pie, and arancini; and WHEREAS, The city closes off North Jay Street for the day so residents can enjoy authentic Italian family fun, with a full music lineup and numerous children's activities, including face painting, balloons, and improvisation classes with MopCo Theatre; and WHEREAS This auspicious day provides an opportunity to celebrate the significant contributions of Italian Americans so that all people may know and appreciate Italian history, traditions, and of the role Italian Americans have played in Schenectady, and will continue to play, in our communities, State and Nation; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the City of Schenectady upon the occasion of hosting its 20th Annual Little Italy StreetFest on September 6, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Little Italy Business Association in the City of Schenectady.
