WILLIAMS -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Energy AN ACT in relation to directing the New York state energy research and development authority to establish a pilot program to install a micro- grid in Glenwood Houses The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. 1. No later than one hundred eighty days after the effec- 2 tive date of this act, the New York state energy research and develop- 3 ment authority shall establish, within amounts appropriated therefor, a 4 pilot program to install a microgrid in Glenwood Houses in Kings county. 5 Such microgrid shall be used for local energy production and distrib- 6 ution which can function independently if disconnected from the main 7 electricity grid or a supplement for peaks in demand. The microgrid 8 shall be powered by renewable energy sources including but not limited 9 to solar panels, hydrogen or wind, and energy efficient generators which 10 shall be combined with energy storage units such as batteries with low 11 environmental impacts. 12 2. The authority shall provide an annual report on or before the first 13 day of January to the governor, the temporary president of the senate 14 and the speaker of the assembly, on the pilot program and any recommen- 15 dations for expansion of such pilot program. 16 § 2. This act shall take effect on the thirtieth day after it shall 17 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
