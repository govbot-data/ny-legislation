                   IN ASSEMBLY

        Introduced by M. of A. BRABENEC -- read once and referred to the Commit-
          tee on Economic Development
 
        AN  ACT  to  amend  the  alcoholic  beverage control law, in relation to
          providing for a license to sell liquor at retail  for  consumption  on
          certain premises
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. Subdivision 7 of  section  64  of  the  alcoholic  beverage
     2  control  law  is  amended  by  adding  a new paragraph (e-18) to read as
     3  follows:
     4    (e-18) Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph (a) of this  subdi-
     5  vision,  the  authority  may  issue  a  retail  license  for on-premises
     6  consumption for a premises which shall be  located  within  two  hundred
     7  feet  of  a  building  occupied  as a school, church, synagogue or other
     8  place of worship, provided such premises constitute a premises  for  the
     9  sale  of  food  or  beverages  at retail for consumption on the premises
    10  located wholly within the boundaries of the  county  of  Orange  in  the
    11  village  of  Florida,  at  22  N  Main  Street, bounded and described as
    12  follows:
    13    ALL that lot of land with the building and improvements thereon  situ-
    14  ate  in  the  Town  of Warwick, Village of Florida, County of Orange and
    15  State of New York, bounded and described as follows:
    16    BEGINNING on the West line of Main Street in Florida at the  Northeast
    17  corner of L. B. Vail's Hotel; thence along said street North six degrees
    18  East,  forty-one  feet;  thence  North  eighty-five degrees West, ninety
    19  feet; thence South six degrees West, forty feet;  thence  South  eighty-
    20  four  degrees  forty-five  minutes  East,  ninety  feet; to the place of
    21  beginning.
    22    TOGETHER with a right of way to pass and re-pass to the  rear  end  of
    23  the said lot on the land lying between the store house of W. L. Vail and
    24  the  land  conveyed hereby; such right of way being about eleven and one
    25  half feet in width.
