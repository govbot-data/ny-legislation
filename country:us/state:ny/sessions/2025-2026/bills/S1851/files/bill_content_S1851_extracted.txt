The legislature hereby finds that with 9 affordable housing in increasingly short supply, the New York Main 10 Street development center will not only be the catalyst for community 11 renewal, but also address the critical need to provide affordable oppor- 12 tunities across the state. Main streets and downtown buildings could 13 serve as the primary location for new housing opportunities throughout 14 this state. Main streets and Downtown Business Districts, are uniquely 15 equipped to serve this function since these areas boast underutilized 16 water and sewer infrastructure, are the hubs of public transportation, 17 and are the centers of commerce. 18 § 1301. New York Main Street development center. The division of hous- 19 ing and community renewal shall work in coordination with the department 20 of economic development, the department of state, the department of 21 transportation, and the office of parks, recreation and historic preser- 22 vation to establish a "New York Main Street development center" within 23 such division.
