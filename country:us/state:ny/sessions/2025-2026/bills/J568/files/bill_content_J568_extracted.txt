Morton, Ed.D., Grand Master, along with Sister Marsha Mack, Grand Worthy Matron, and Brother Grant M. Valentine, Grand Worthy Patron; and WHEREAS, The Eureka Grand Chapter Esther Day Women's History Month Celebration serves as a beacon of inspiration and a platform for recognizing women whose dedication to leadership, service, and community advancement has left an indelible mark on society; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize and honor the Eureka Grand Chapter upon the occasion of hosting the Esther Day Women's History Month Celebration and for its continued commitment to honoring and uplifting women of distinction; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Eureka Grand Chapter.
