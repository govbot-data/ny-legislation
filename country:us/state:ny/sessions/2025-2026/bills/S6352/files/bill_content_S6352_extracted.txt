As used in this section, the following terms shall have 5 the following meanings: 6 a. "Clinical services" shall mean the collection and interpretation of 9 patient data for the purpose of initiating, modifying and monitoring 10 drug therapy with associated accountability and responsibility for 11 outcomes in a direct patient care setting. 12 c. "Collaborative drug therapy management" shall mean the performance 13 of clinical services by a pharmacist relating to the review, evaluation 14 and management of drug therapy to a patient, who is being treated by a 15 physician or nurse practitioner for a specific disease or associated 16 disease states, in accordance with a written agreement or protocol with 17 a voluntarily participating physician or nurse practitioner and in 18 accordance with the policies, procedures, and protocols of the facility.
