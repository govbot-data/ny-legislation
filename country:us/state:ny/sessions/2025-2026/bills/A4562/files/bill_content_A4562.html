A04562 Summary:

BILL NOA04562
 
SAME ASSAME AS S01524
 
SPONSORLavine (MS)
 
COSPNSRBurdick, Otis, Kelles, Simon, Shimsky, Carroll R, Levenberg, Weprin, Cruz, Davila, Alvarez, Eachus, Lupardo, Reyes, Raga, Bichotte Hermelyn, McDonough, Chang, Hevesi
 
MLTSPNSRWoerner
 
Add §17-213, El L
 
Enacts the "election workers and polling places protection act" to protect poll watchers, election officials, voters and physical property being used as a polling place or by the board of elections.
