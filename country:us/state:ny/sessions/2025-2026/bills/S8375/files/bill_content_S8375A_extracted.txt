AN ACT to amend the cannabis law, in relation to establishing the "small
cannabis farmer relief act"; and providing for the repeal of such
provisions upon expiration thereof
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "small
2 cannabis farmer relief act".
3 § 2. The cannabis law is amended by adding a new section 68-c to read
4 as follows:
5 § 68-c. Limited canopy expansion. 1. (a) Notwithstanding any
6 provision of law to the contrary, any licensee who qualifies as a
7 distressed farmer and legally cultivated cannabis during the two thou-
8 sand twenty-two or two thousand twenty-three calendar year shall be
9 eligible to expand their canopy as provided in this subdivision.
10 (b) Licensees shall be eligible to expand their cultivation canopy to
11 up to twice the maximum square footage cultivated in two thousand twen-
12 ty-two or two thousand twenty-three, provided that the expanded area
13 shall not exceed the maximum limits established by the corresponding
14 cultivation tier.
15 (c) Verification of canopy cultivated in two thousand twenty-two or
16 two thousand twenty-three shall be submitted to the office in a form
17 prescribed by the office. Denials of applications under this subdivision
18 may be appealed to the cannabis control board.
19 (d) No additional fees shall be assessed for canopy expansions author-
20 ized pursuant to this subdivision, including standard license renewal
21 fees.
