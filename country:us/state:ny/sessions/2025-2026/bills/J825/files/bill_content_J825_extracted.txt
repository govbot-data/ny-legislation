this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Bolivar-Richburg High School's Teegan Sibble upon the occasion of her capturing the historic first-ever NYSPHSAA Girls 114-pound Wrestling Championship; and WHEREAS, To the praise and applause of her family and exited fans, Teegan Sibble defeated her opponent from Bethpage Central School by a pin in 1:11, on February 27, 2025, at the MVP Arena in Albany, New York; and WHEREAS, This outstanding performance earned Teegan Sibble the New York State Girls 114-pound Wrestling Championship; and WHEREAS, Teegan Sibble went 3-0 at the State Tournament, defeating her first opponent in :44, and her second opponent with a technical fall 13-2; and WHEREAS, Teegan Sibble is the second State Wrestling Champion in Bolivar-Richburg school history, the only other school champion is her brother, Trent Sibble; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as wrestling, which demands focus, strength and skill, Bolivar-Richburg High School Wrestling Coaches Andrew Taylor and Todd Taylor worked hard to mentor and inspire Teegan Sibble in her record-breaking season, teaching this outstanding athlete life lessons which will prove invaluable on and off the mat; and RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Teegan Sibble on an outstanding season which culminated in her winning the 2025 NYSPHSAA Girls 114-pound Wrestling Championship, and to Coach Andrew Taylor and Coach Todd Taylor for guiding her during her history-making season; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution be transmitted to Teegan Sibble, Coach Andrew Taylor, Coach Todd Taylor and Athletic Director Dustin Allen.
