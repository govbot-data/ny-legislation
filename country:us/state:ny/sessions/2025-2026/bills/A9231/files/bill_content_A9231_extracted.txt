Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "New York state catastrophe fund authority act". 3 § 2. Legislative findings and declaration. The legislature finds and 4 declares: 5 1. There is a compelling state interest to maintain a viable and 6 orderly private sector property/casualty insurance market for both resi- 7 dential and commercial properties in this state and across the United 8 States.
