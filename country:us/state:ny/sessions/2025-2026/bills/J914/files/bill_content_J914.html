Senate Resolution No. 914
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
        HONORING  Perry  Nizzi  upon  the  occasion of his
        retirement after 28 years of  distinguished  service
        to Hamilton College
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge
those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Perry Nizzi upon the occasion  of  his  retirement  after  28  years  of
distinguished service to Hamilton College on Monday, June 30, 2025; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the  past  28  years,  Perry Nizzi rendered faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to Hamilton College; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his capacity as head coach of the Hamilton College men's
soccer team and as a physical education instructor, Perry  Nizzi  always
served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Hired  at  Hamilton  College in 1997, Perry Nizzi spent 26
seasons  with  the  Continentals,  posting  a  commendable   record   of
211-146-48,  including  a  team-record  12  wins in 2001; his teams also
reached double figures in wins an impressive eight times; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, in his 17 years as the head coach at  Herkimer
College  from  1981 to 1997, Perry Nizzi's teams won 291 matches, giving
him a total of 502 victories in 43 seasons between the two schools; and
 
  WHEREAS, Perry Nizzi coached an all-American and 23 all-region  team
picks,  and  since  2011,  had 16 all-New England Small College Athletic
Conference (NESCAC) selections; and
 
  WHEREAS, This esteemed and respected coach has led Hamilton teams to
much success with  17  trips  to  the  postseason  including  five  NCAA
Division  III championship appearances; he also received the 2016 NESCAC
Coach  of  the  Year  designation,  guiding  the  Continentals  to   the
conference championship final that same year; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Perry  Nizzi  led  the men's soccer team to Liberty League
tournament titles in 2004 and 2007, and regular season  crowns  in  2000
and  2006;  in 2000, Hamilton College advanced to the NCAA quarterfinals
and was ranked 16th in the final Division III coaches poll; and
 
  WHEREAS, In Perry Nizzi's last year as coach, the men's soccer  team
received  an  at-large  bid to the NCAA championship and defeated Calvin
University 3-2 in the first round and tied Kenyon  College  2-2  in  the
second  round  before  Kenyon  advanced  on  penalty kicks; the Hamilton
team's final overall record was 8-6-4; and
 
  WHEREAS, A member of five halls of fame, including Rome Sports  Hall
of  Fame,  Greater Utica Sports Hall of Fame, NJCAA Soccer Hall of Fame,
Herkimer College Athletic Hall of Fame and SUNY Cortland's  C-Club  Hall
 
of  Fame,  Perry  Nizzi has demonstrated excellence in all facets of his
life of service and in his storied career; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Regarded  for  his  unique  coaching style, his ability to
foster intensely focused and remarkably jovial teams, as well as for his
discipline and strong work ethic across the positions  held  during  his
tenure,  Perry  Nizzi's  historic  legacy  will remain as a testament to
superlative athletic and professional achievement; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his official acts, Perry Nizzi was governed  by  a  keen
sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and
 
  WHEREAS, During his tenure with Hamilton College, Perry Nizzi earned
the  admiration, esteem and affection of his colleagues; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Perry Nizzi upon the occasion of his retirement after 28 years of
distinguished service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Perry Nizzi.
