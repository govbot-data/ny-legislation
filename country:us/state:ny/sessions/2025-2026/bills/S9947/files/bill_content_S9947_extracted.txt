Sections 1, 2 and 7 of chapter 63 of the laws of 1971 2 incorporating the "Centerport Volunteer Exempt Firemen's Benevolent 3 Association" and providing for its powers and duties, are amended to 4 read as follows: 5 Section 1. Incorporation: membership. A not-for-profit corporation by 6 the name of "Centerport Volunteer Exempt [Firemen's] Firefighter's 7 Benevolent Association" is hereby created. It shall be composed of such 8 persons eligible to membership therein as hereinafter provided as shall 9 (a) notify the secretary of the Centerport Fire Department Company, a 10 volunteer fire company, in writing prior to the organization meeting of 11 such corporation hereinafter provided for of their desire to become 12 members, or (b) shall attend such meeting or an adjournment thereof, and 13 also such persons so eligible as may thereafter become members of such 14 corporation, pursuant to its by-laws. 15 § 2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now, or have 16 been, or hereafter shall be active volunteer members of the Centerport 17 Fire Department Company, in the town of Huntington, County of Suffolk, 18 or of any fire corporation in the Centerport Fire District in such town 19 and who have acquired or shall hereafter acquire the status of exempt 20 volunteer [fireman] firefighter as defined in section two hundred of the 21 general municipal law by virtue of having rendered service in the 22 Centerport Fire Department Company or in a fire corporation in the 23 Centerport Fire District, and all persons who are now or hereafter shall 24 be active volunteer members of the Centerport Fire Department Company EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
