An excess profit shall be a 15 profit beyond a percentage rate of return on net worth attributable to 16 such policies, computed in accordance with the regulation required by 17 section two thousand three hundred twenty-three of this article, and 18 determined by the superintendent to be so far above a reasonable average 19 profit as to amount to an excess profit, taking into consideration the 20 fact that losses or profits below a reasonable average profit will not 21 be recouped from such policyholders.
