Section 19.09 of the mental hygiene law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision (m) to read as follows: 3 (m) The commissioner shall promulgate rules and regulations for 4 providers who operate in rural locations that take into account the 5 extra challenges encountered by such providers, including but not limit- 6 ed to, increased and additional expenses. 7 § 2.
