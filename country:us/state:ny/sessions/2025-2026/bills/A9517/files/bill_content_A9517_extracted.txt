Subdivisions 1 and 3 of section 3.16 of the parks, recre- 2 ation and historic preservation law, as added by a chapter of the laws 3 of 2025 amending the parks, recreation and historic preservation law 4 relating to mandating the development and approval of operational safety 5 plans, as proposed in legislative bills numbers S. 7680-A and A. 6634-A, 6 are amended to read as follows: 7 1. The office shall develop and approve operational safety plans prior 8 to the deployment of any employee in hazardous conditions. The plan shall require that any employee check 18 in with the incident commander or safety officer for incident specific 19 procedures prior to taking any action at the incident location.
