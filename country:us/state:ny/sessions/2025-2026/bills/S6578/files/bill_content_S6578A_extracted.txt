AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to the release of
identifying information of consenting registrants from the adoption
registry
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 4138-c of the public health law,
2 as amended by chapter 181 of the laws of 2010, is amended to read as
3 follows:
4 6. Upon receipt of a final consent or pre-authorization consent form
5 by the adoptee, by either of the birth parents, and/or by a biological
6 sibling of the adoptee, the department shall, unless the adoptee or
7 biological sibling registrant shall elect otherwise, if an authorized
8 agency was involved in such adoption, release identifying information to
9 such agency; such agency shall thereafter promptly release identifying
10 information about the consenting registrants to the consenting regis-
11 trants. If no authorized agency was involved, or if any registrant shall
12 have elected not to have release of the information by the authorized
13 agency involved in such adoption the department shall release identify-
14 ing information to the consenting registrants. Such identifying informa-
15 tion shall be limited to the names and addresses of the consenting
16 registrants and shall not include any other information contained in the
17 adoption or birth records. However, nothing in this section shall be
18 construed to prevent the release of adoption records as otherwise
19 permitted by law.
20 § 2. Section 4138-c of the public health law is amended by adding a
21 new subdivision 6-b to read as follows:
