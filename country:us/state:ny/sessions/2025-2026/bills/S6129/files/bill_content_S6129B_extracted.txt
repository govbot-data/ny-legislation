AN ACT to permit structures to remain within a certain erosion hazard
area or tidal wetland adjacent area
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. For purposes of this act, the term "eligible properties"
2 shall mean residential properties in a costal erosion hazard area or
3 tidal wetland area, as determined by the department of environmental
4 conservation and located southeast of Hylan Blvd between Wiman Ave and
5 Arbutus Ave in the County of Richmond, where: (a) the local permitting
6 authority has issued a permit prior to December 31, 2022 for
7 construction of structures within the coastal erosion hazard area or
8 tidal wetland adjacent area; and
9 (b) the property owner has submitted documentation and a written
10 certification, affirmed under penalty of perjury that:
11 (i) the applicable permits were issued by the local permitting author-
12 ity prior to the earlier of December 31, 2022, or the date on which the
13 property owner became aware of a violation of article 25 or 34 of the
14 environmental conservation law on the property;
15 (ii) the property owner exercised reasonable due diligence when
16 purchasing the property and had no knowledge of any violation of article
17 25 or 34 of the environmental conservation law, including any require-
18 ment to obtain a coastal erosion hazard area permit or tidal wetlands
19 permit in addition to the locally issued permit for construction; and
