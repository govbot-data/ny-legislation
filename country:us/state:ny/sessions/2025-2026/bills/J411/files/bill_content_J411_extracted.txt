Hall, and Imperial Deputy for the Desert, Dr. Cassandra Duff, HPIC, the 2025 Desert Conference will bring together Shriners from across New York to engage in fellowship, training, and discussions on the continued growth and mission of the organization; and WHEREAS, Through its dedication to scholarship programs, youth mentorship, health awareness initiatives, and charitable contributions, the Desert of New York continues to make a profound difference in the lives of countless individuals, furthering its commitment to philanthropy, leadership, and service; and WHEREAS, The 2025 Desert Conference marks a significant moment to reflect on the legacy, strength, and future of the Desert of New York, celebrating its continued excellence and dedication to service; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to acknowledge the significance of this event and to recognize the profound contributions of the Desert of New York, A.E.A.O.N.M.S., in advancing education, charity, and civic engagement; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 2025 Desert Conference of the Desert of New York, A.E.A.O.N.M.S., to be held May 1-4, 2025, and to extend sincere appreciation to Imperial Deputy of the Desert, HPIP Damien E. Hall, Imperial Deputy for the Desert, Dr. Cassandra Duff, HPIC, and all Nobles for their unwavering commitment to service, excellence, and empowerment; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Imperial Deputy of the Desert, HPIP Damien E.
