Add §27-1435, En Con L
 
Directs the department of environmental conservation to promulgate regulations to implement a state-owned property remediation program for brownfield properties owned by the state, or any department or agency thereof; makes related provisions.

        AN ACT to amend the  environmental  conservation  law,  in  relation  to
          expanding the brownfields cleanup program to include state-owned prop-
          erties affected by certain kinds of structural contamination

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The environmental conservation law is amended by  adding  a
     2  new section 27-1435 to read as follows:
     3  § 27-1435. State-owned property remediation program.
     4    1.  The  department shall promulgate regulations to implement a state-
     5  owned property remediation program for applicants that meet the require-
     6  ments of subdivision two of this section, provided that:
     7    (a) the activity is conducted in a manner which satisfies all require-
     8  ments applicable to like activity conducted pursuant to sections 27-1415
     9  and 27-1417 of this title; and
    10    (b) the remedial program pursuant to section  27-1415  of  this  title
    11  shall  achieve  a  cleanup level that will allow the site to be used for
    12  any purpose without restriction by the department and  without  reliance
    13  on the long-term employment of institutional or engineering controls.
    14    2.  Applicants under this section shall meet the following criteria in
    15  order to be eligible for the state-owned property remediation program:
    16    (a) the property that is the subject of the application must  be  real
    17  property  owned  by  the  state  or  any  department  or agency thereof,
    18  provided, however, that real property disposed of by the  state  or  any
    19  department  or agency thereof shall be eligible for the program pursuant
    20  to this section; and
    21    (b) the property that is the subject of the application is affected by
    22  contamination of structures located at the site due to stored materials,
    23  electrical appurtenances, asbestos, lead, polychlorinated biphenyls,  or
