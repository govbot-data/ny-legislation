AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to ensuring the
public has virtual access to public service commission proceedings and
may file documents electronically
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The public service law is amended by adding a new section
2 28 to read as follows:
3 § 28. Virtual access by the public to commission proceedings and elec-
4 tronic filing of documents. 1. As used in this section:
5 (a) "Document" shall include, but not be limited to, oral and written
6 testimony, exhibits, interrogatories, and motions, briefs, comments,
7 rulings, orders and any other materials filed in or generated as part of
8 a proceeding.
9 (b) "Proceedings" shall mean any formal or informal matter, case,
10 rulemaking, rate proceeding, generic proceeding, or any other action of
11 the commission or the department or any component of a proceeding that
12 may result in decisions by the commission or recommendations to the
13 commission. Such term shall include evidentiary hearings conducted
14 pursuant to section sixty-six of this chapter.
15 2. The commission shall provide for virtual and in-person access and
16 participation for all proceedings regarding gas, electric, combination
17 gas and electric, and water utilities. In the case of settlement negoti-
18 ations, virtual and in-person access and participation shall be limited
19 to persons admitted as parties and who subscribe to the applicable
20 commission rules and standards governing confidentiality.
