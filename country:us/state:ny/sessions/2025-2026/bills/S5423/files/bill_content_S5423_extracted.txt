Subdivision 3 of section 123 of the agriculture and markets 2 law, as added by chapter 392 of the laws of 2004 and such section as 3 renumbered by section 18 of part T of chapter 59 of the laws of 2010, is 4 amended to read as follows: 5 3. Upon a finding that a dog is dangerous, the judge or justice may 6 order humane euthanasia or permanent confinement of the dog if one of 7 the following aggravating circumstances is established at the judicial 8 hearing held pursuant to subdivision two of this section: 9 (a) the dog, without justification, attacked a person causing serious 10 physical injury or death; or 11 (b) the dog has a known vicious propensity as evidenced by a previous 12 unjustified attack on a person, which caused serious physical injury or 13 death; or 14 (c) the dog, without justification and while trespassing on another 15 person's property, caused death to a companion animal, farm animal or 16 domestic animal; or 17 (d) the dog, without justification, caused serious physical injury or 18 death to a companion animal, farm animal or domestic animal, and has, in 19 the past two years, caused unjustified physical injury or death to a 20 companion or farm animal as evidenced by a "dangerous dog" finding 21 pursuant to the provisions of this section. 22 An order of humane euthanasia shall not be carried out until expiration 23 of the thirty day period provided for in subdivision five of this 24 section for filing a notice of appeal, unless the owner of the dog has EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
