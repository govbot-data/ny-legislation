Lupardo COMMEMORATING the 50th Anniversary of The Addiction Center of Broome County WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to recognize that the quality of life in the communities across this great State of New York is enriched by the concerned and dedicated efforts of those organizations of distinction whose singular purpose is the care and welfare of others; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 50th Anniversary of The Addiction Center of Broome County; and WHEREAS, Founded in 1975, The Addiction Center of Broome County, known as ACBC, is a longstanding community-based organization in Binghamton, New York, dedicated to helping individuals and families affected by substance use disorders; and WHEREAS, ACBC provides outpatient and intensive outpatient treatment options, including individual, group, and family counseling, relapse prevention programs, and co-occurring disorder support for those facing both mental health and substance use challenges; and WHEREAS, Utilizing a trauma-informed approach, ACBC ensures that clients receive compassionate care that addresses both addiction and its root causes, helping to build a foundation for lasting recovery; it offers Health Home Care Management, which connects clients to medical, housing, and social services, as well as peer recovery programs; and WHEREAS, ACBC works across Broome and surrounding counties, including locations in Binghamton and Endicott, to make treatment accessible throughout the region; and WHEREAS, Committed to removing barriers to treatment, The Addiction Center of Broome County offers a sliding fee scale to ensure that no one is denied care due to financial limitations; and WHEREAS, By combining evidence-based practices with human connection, ACBC continues to serve as a trusted provider of addiction treatment and recovery support; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body recognizes the vital importance of The Addiction Center of Broome County in providing excellence in care through prevention, education and treatment, and its evident commitment to this mission; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 50th Anniversary of The Addiction Center of Broome County, a trusted and vital institution within the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to The Addiction Center of Broome County.
