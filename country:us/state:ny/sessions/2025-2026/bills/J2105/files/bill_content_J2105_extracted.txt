Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J02105 Summary:BILL NOJ02105  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORSTEWART-COUSINS COSPNSRMAYER MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ02105 Text: Senate Resolution No. 2105 BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS CONGRATULATING the Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2026 NYSPHSAA Basketball Class B Championship on March 21, 2026 WHEREAS, Individual and team sports are highly competitive, holding championship tournaments for competing teams, reserving the state championship title for only the best team; and WHEREAS, Sports and athletic activities promote physical, mental, and emotional prowess within our young New Yorkers, instilling a fire to strive for excellence that matures alongside the athlete, driving them to unparalleled heights; and WHEREAS, Student athletes challenge their bodies and minds, whether on the court or classroom, they balance their responsibilities while bolstering their teamwork, excellence, and competitive spirit, qualities that will benefit their futures greatly; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is immensely proud to congratulate the Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2026 NYSPHSAA Basketball Class B Championship on March 21, 2026, at Hudson Valley Community College in Troy, New York; and WHEREAS, Surrounded by enthusiastic family members, friends, and fans, the Lady Eagles exemplified tenacity and composure in their overtime win over the Chenango Forks Blue Devils, ending with a final score of 65-60, finalizing their 24-3 season record; and WHEREAS, The members of the Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team display incredible mastery and professionalism in the realm of basketball, forming intense bonds of sisterhood that will last long after they retire their red and white uniforms; and WHEREAS, Head Coach Elie Moise and Assistant Coach Michael Marino have shown outstanding leadership, dedication, and commitment throughout the season, instilling discipline, unity, and a winning mindset in their players both on and off the court; and WHEREAS, The Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team continued its run of excellence by capturing its second consecutive Sectional title during the 2026 season, further demonstrating the program's consistency and competitive strength; and WHEREAS, Building upon this success, the Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team demonstrated poise, discipline, and determination against postseason competition, advancing through each round with confidence and purpose; and WHEREAS, The members of the Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team have shown remarkable commitment and perseverance, culminating in a historic achievement as the first girls team in Yonkers history to win a state championship and the first Yonkers team to win a state championship since 1932, bringing pride and recognition to their school and community while setting a new standard for excellence in Yonkers athletics; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team: Anisa Mustafaj, Emilee Doherty, Grace Gleeson, Naomi De La Cruz, Fiona Kenny, Liliana Toro, Jodi O'Donnell, Vera Ndoci, Zion Frazier, Gianna Ruiz, Keira Peters, Jevaeh Thompson, and Arsela Mustafaj; Head Coach Elie Moise; and Assistant Coach Michael Marino upon the occasion of capturing the 2026 NYSPHSAA Basketball Class B Championship on March 21, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Yonkers Montessori Academy Varsity Girls Basketball Team; Head Coach Elie Moise; and Assistant Coach Michael Marino.
