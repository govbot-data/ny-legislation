GRAY -- read once and referred to the Committee on Judiciary AN ACT to amend the family court act, in relation to the number of judg- es of the family court in St. Lawrence county The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 131 of the family court act is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision (y) to read as follows: 3 (y) In the county of St. Lawrence there shall be one additional family 4 court judge and the number of such judges now existing is hereby 5 increased accordingly. The compensation of the additional family court 6 judge shall be the same as the existing family court judge in such coun- 7 ty. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
