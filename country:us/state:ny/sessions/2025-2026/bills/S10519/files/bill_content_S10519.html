BILL NOS10519A
 
SAME ASSAME AS A11602
 
SPONSORGONZALEZ
 
COSPNSR

Amd §21, Tax L
 
Extends the tangible property credit component of the brownfield redevelopment tax credit for certain qualified sites which contain 40% open space and 25% affordable housing units.
