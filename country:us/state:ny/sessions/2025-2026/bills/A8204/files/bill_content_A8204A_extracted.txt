AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring immediate
notification of a child's parent when an allegation of child abuse in
an educational setting has been made regarding such child
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivisions 1 and 3 of section 1128 of the education law,
2 as added by chapter 180 of the laws of 2000, are amended to read as
3 follows:
4 1. Where the subject child has made the allegation: (a) [promptly]
5 immediately notify the parent of such child that an allegation of child
6 abuse in an educational setting has been made regarding such child and
7 promptly provide the parent with a written statement prepared pursuant
8 to regulations of the commissioner setting forth parental rights,
9 responsibilities and procedures under this article; (b) where a school
10 administrator receives a written report, promptly provide a copy of such
11 report to the superintendent; and (c) promptly forward such report to
12 appropriate law enforcement authorities. In no event shall reporting to
13 law enforcement be delayed by reason of an inability to contact the
14 superintendent.
15 3. Where a person other than the subject child or the parent of a
16 subject child has made the allegation: (a) [promptly] immediately notify
17 the parent of the subject child that an allegation of child abuse in an
18 educational setting has been made regarding [his or her] such child and
19 promptly provide the parent with a written statement prepared pursuant
20 to regulations of the commissioner setting forth parental rights,
21 responsibilities and procedures under this article; (b) ascertain from
