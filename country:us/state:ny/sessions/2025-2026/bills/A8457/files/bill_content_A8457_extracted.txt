WILLIAMS -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to providing reimbursements to sworn officers of the metropolitan transportation authority police department non-revenue E-ZPasses The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1270-k to read as follows: 3 § 1270-k. Toll reimbursement. 1. For the purposes of this section, the 4 term "sworn officer of the metropolitan transportation police depart- 5 ment" shall include every sworn police officer, the chief of police, the 6 deputy chief, captains, lieutenants, and sergeants employed by the 7 metropolitan transportation authority. 8 2. Each sworn officer of the metropolitan transportation police 9 department shall be entitled to a non-revenue E-ZPass or a successor 10 device exclusively for commutation only. Unless otherwise defined in a 11 represented memorandum of agreement with the authority, each sworn offi- 12 cer of the metropolitan transportation police department shall surrender 13 such officer's E-ZPass upon retirement, resignation or termination. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
