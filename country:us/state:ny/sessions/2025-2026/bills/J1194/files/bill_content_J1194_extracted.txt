and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 100th Anniversary of Roosevelt High School - Early College Studies of Yonkers, New York; and WHEREAS, An educational cornerstone of the City of Yonkers, Roosevelt High School was opened in 1926, and has grown into a revered modern institution of excellence regarded for blending tradition with innovation; and WHEREAS, Roosevelt High School is renowned for its Early College Studies program which offers students the opportunity to earn college credits while enrolled in high school; with over 25 college-level courses and two New York State-approved Career and Technical Education programs, the school well-prepares students for entrance into both higher education and the workforce; and WHEREAS, The school's transformation throughout the course of its distinguished history from a traditional high school to a forward-thinking academic hub reflects its commitment to excellence, equity, and opportunity, as well as to its diverse community of students and families; and WHEREAS, The steadfast and unyielding commitment of parents, educators, and administrators to provide the highest quality education for students has become the hallmark and tradition of Roosevelt High School; and WHEREAS, Today, Roosevelt High School stands prepared to reach out, in earnest and enthusiastic quest of its mission, to promote the maximum growth of every student, establishing standards and building skills which will carry its students and the citizens of its community into the future; and WHEREAS, Roosevelt High School is proudly aware of its distinguished past, eager to fulfill its present commitment to parents, students, and the community, and is planning with vision and purpose for the prosperous growth and change it will encounter in the years to come; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 100th Anniversary of Roosevelt High School - Early College Studies of Yonkers, New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Roosevelt High School - Early College Studies.
