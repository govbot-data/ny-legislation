FAHY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to the application of certain provisions relating to the operating component of rates of payment made by governmental agencies for certain services The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (c) of subdivision 2-c of section 2808 of the 2 public health law, as added by section 95 of part H of chapter 59 of the 3 laws of 2011, is amended to read as follows: 4 (c) The non-capital component of the rates for: (i) AIDS facilities or 5 discrete AIDS units within facilities; (ii) discrete units for residents 6 receiving care in a long-term inpatient rehabilitation program for trau- 7 matic brain injured persons; (iii) discrete units providing specialized 8 programs for residents requiring behavioral interventions; (iv) discrete 9 units for long-term ventilator dependent residents; and (v) facilities 10 or discrete units within facilities that provide extensive nursing, 11 medical, psychological and counseling support services solely to chil- 12 dren shall reflect the rates in effect for such facilities on January 13 first, two thousand nine, as adjusted for inflation and rate appeals in 14 accordance with applicable statutes, provided, however, that such rates 15 for facilities described in this subparagraph shall reflect the applica- 16 tion of the provisions of subdivision twenty-four of this section, and 17 provided, [however] further, that such rates for facilities described in 18 subparagraph (i) of this paragraph shall reflect the application of the 19 provisions of section twelve of part D of chapter fifty-eight of the 20 laws of two thousand nine, and provided further, however, that insofar 21 as such rates reflect trend adjustments for trend factors attributable 22 to the two thousand eight and two thousand nine calendar years the 23 aggregate amount of such trend factor adjustments shall be subject to 24 the provisions of section two of part D of chapter fifty-eight of the 25 laws of two thousand nine, as amended. 26 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
