FAHY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Racing, Gaming and Wager- ing AN ACT to amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, in relation to requiring mobile sports wagering operators to establish default betting limits on their platforms and to notify authorized sports bettors of their ability to opt-out of the default betting limits The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "choice in responsible gaming act". 3 § 2. Section 1367-a of the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding 4 law is amended by adding a new subdivision 4-a to read as follows: 5 4-a. (a) For the purposes of this subdivision, "default betting limit" 6 shall mean a defined, maximum monetary amount that an authorized sports 7 bettor may wager through a mobile sports wagering platform over a 8 defined period of time. 9 (b) All mobile sports wagering operators shall implement default 10 betting limits on their mobile sports wagering platforms. The default 11 betting limit shall be determined by the commission prior to the effec- 12 tive date of this subdivision and shall be based on the best practices 13 to encourage responsible gambling. 14 (c) When an authorized sports bettor registers for a mobile sports 15 wagering operator's platform, such authorized sports bettor shall be 16 opted-in to the default betting limit. Authorized sports bettors who are 17 already registered for the mobile sports wagering platform prior to the 18 effective date of this subdivision shall also be opted-in to the default 19 betting limit. All authorized sports bettors shall have the option to 20 opt-out of the default betting limit. 21 (d) The mobile sports wagering operator or platform shall notify 22 authorized sports bettors of the existence of the default betting limit 23 and the option to opt-out at the time of registration.
