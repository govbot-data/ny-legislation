AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to authorizing
domestic violence shelters to be reimbursed for housing a single indi-
vidual in a room intended for double occupancy
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 131-u of the social services law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows:
3 3. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a resi-
4 dential program for victims of domestic violence, as defined in section
5 four hundred fifty-nine-a of this chapter, which provides temporary
6 housing assistance to a single individual who meets such program's
7 necessary eligibility requirements, shall be reimbursed for the payment
8 differential, if any, for housing a single individual in a room intended
9 for double occupancy, if no other room intended for single occupancy is
10 available within the facility in accordance with the following:
11 (a) for the fiscal year beginning April first, two thousand twenty-six
12 the reimbursement shall be twenty-five percent of any cost differential;
13 (b) for the fiscal year beginning April first, two thousand twenty-
14 seven the reimbursement shall be fifty percent of any cost differential;
15 (c) for the fiscal year beginning April first, two thousand twenty-
16 eight the reimbursement shall be seventy-five percent of any cost
17 differential; and
18 (d) for the fiscal year beginning April first, two thousand twenty-
19 nine the reimbursement shall be one hundred percent of any cost differ-
20 ential.
21 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
