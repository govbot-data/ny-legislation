LANZA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to establishing the crime of larceny of a controlled substance The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 220.80 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 220.80 Larceny of a controlled substance. 4 A person is guilty of larceny of a controlled substance when such 5 person commits the crime of larceny and the stolen property consists of 6 more than twenty-five dosage units of a schedule II, III, IV, or V 7 controlled substance which is stolen from a pharmacy. For the purposes 8 of this section: 9 1. "Larceny" has the same meaning as defined in section 155.05 of this 10 part. 11 2. "Pharmacy" means any place in which drugs, prescriptions or poisons 12 are possessed for the purpose of compounding, preserving, dispensing or 13 retailing, or in which drugs, prescriptions or poisons are compounded, 14 preserved, dispensed or retailed, or in which such drugs, prescriptions 15 or poisons are by advertising or otherwise offered for sale at retail. 16 3. "Schedule II, III, IV, or V controlled substance" means any 17 substance listed in schedules II, III, IV, and V of section thirty-three 18 hundred six of the public health law. 19 Larceny of a controlled substance is a class A-II felony. 20 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of November next succeed- 21 ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
