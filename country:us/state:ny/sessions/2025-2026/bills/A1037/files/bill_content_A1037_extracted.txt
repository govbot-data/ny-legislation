Subdivision 3-a of section 296 of the executive law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph (a-1) to read as follows: 3 (a-1) For any employer, licensing agency or employment agency to 4 request or require that a prospective employee disclose their date of 5 graduation from an educational institution on an initial employment 6 application or during an interview, except in the case of a bona fide 7 occupational qualification or need. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of January next succeed- 9 ing the date upon which it shall have become a law.
