and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 100th Anniversary of the Long Lake Central School District, to be recognized at its Centennial Celebration on Friday, May 30, 2025; and WHEREAS, Since its founding in 1925, the Long Lake Central School District has been an institution dedicated to academic excellence, student success, and community engagement; and WHEREAS, For 100 years, the Long Lake Central School District has provided a high-quality education, fostering a tradition of academic distinction, innovation, and leadership among its students, faculty, and staff; and WHEREAS, Situated within the Adirondack Park, the Long Lake Central School District offers a unique natural setting that enhances environmental education and outdoor learning opportunities; and WHEREAS, The Long Lake Central School District has consistently upheld its commitment to maintain strong community ties, collaborating with local organizations, such as the Long Lake Historical Society, to preserve and celebrate the region's rich heritage; and WHEREAS, Students and graduates of the Long Lake Central School District are highly regarded for their excellence, which is a product of diverse extracurricular activities and educational trips that extends their academic experiences beyond the classroom; and WHEREAS, The Long Lake Central School District is renowned for their high graduation rates, low drop out rates, and high proficiency rates in several subjects according to New York State standards; and WHEREAS, The educators, administrators, and staff of the Long Lake Central School District have played a vital role in shaping generations of students, instilling in them the values of hard work, perseverance, and intellectual curiosity; and WHEREAS, The Long Lake Central School District's legacy of educational excellence is a testament to the commitment and support of its students, parents, teachers, administrators, alumni, and the entire Long Lake and Hamilton County community; and WHEREAS, In celebration of this historic milestone, the Long Lake Central School District continues to inspire and empower future generations, ensuring a bright and promising future for all who pass through its halls; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 100th Anniversary of the Long Lake Central School District, recognizing its outstanding contributions to education and the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Long Lake Central School District.
