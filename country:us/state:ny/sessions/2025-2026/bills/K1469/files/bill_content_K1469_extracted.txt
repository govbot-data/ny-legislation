and WHEREAS, The Civil Air Patrol (CAP), founded on December 1, 1941, stands as the official Auxiliary of the United States Air Force, and for more than eight decades has exemplified the highest ideals of volunteer service, aviation skill, and civic responsibility; and WHEREAS, The Civil Air Patrol New York Wing has proudly served the citizens of this State since its establishment, embodying CAP's core missions of Emergency Services, Cadet Programs, and Aerospace Education; and WHEREAS, During World War II, Civil Air Patrol members courageously defended our coasts, patrolled vital shipping lanes, and performed essential missions that protected our nation's security-an enduring legacy that continues through the New York Wing's modern-day search and rescue, disaster relief, and homeland security missions; and WHEREAS, CAP members assisted rescuers and state agencies immediately following the Sept. 11, 2001, terrorist attacks, heavily supported hurricane relief efforts in 2012 and 2021 during Superstorm Sandy, Hurricanes Ida and Henry, and in response to the COVID pandemic, as well as aided communities during other natural disasters, such as floods, wildfires, ice storms, and tornadoes; and WHEREAS, Today, more than 2,200 volunteers across the New York Wing contribute thousands of hours annually in service to their communities, supporting emergency management operations, search and rescue, conducting aerial reconnaissance, mentoring youth through cadet leadership programs, and inspiring the next generation of aviators and innovators; and WHEREAS, The New York Wing's dedication to integrity, volunteer service, excellence, and respect reflects the shared values of our State and Nation, strengthening communities across New York from Buffalo to Long Island; and WHEREAS, On this momentous occasion, the members of the New York State Legislature join in recognizing the 85th Anniversary of the Civil Air Patrol and in honoring the men and women of the Civil Air Patrol New York Wing who have given so selflessly in service to others; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 85th Anniversary of the Civil Air Patrol New York Wing, extending heartfelt appreciation and congratulations to its members-past, present, and future-for their distinguished service to the State of New York and the United States of America; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the leadership and membership of the Civil Air Patrol New York Wing with best wishes for continued success in their noble mission and enduring contribution to the safety and education of our citizens.
