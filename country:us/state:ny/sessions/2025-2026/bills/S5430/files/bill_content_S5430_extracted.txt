Paragraph (a) of subdivision 1 of section 168-b of the 2 correction law, as amended by chapter 67 of the laws of 2008, is amended 3 to read as follows: 4 (a) The sex offender's name, social security number, all aliases used, 5 date of birth, sex, race, height, weight, eye color, driver's license 6 number, past home addresses, current home address and/or expected place 7 of domicile, any internet accounts with internet access providers 8 belonging to such offender and internet identifiers that such offender 9 uses.
