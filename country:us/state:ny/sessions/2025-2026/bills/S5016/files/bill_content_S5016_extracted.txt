SEPULVEDA, GOUNARDES, PERSAUD, STAVISKY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Crime Victims, Crime and Correction AN ACT to amend the correction law, in relation to authorizing daily visitation at general confinement correctional facilities The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The correction law is amended by adding a new section 138-b 2 to read as follows: 3 § 138-b. Daily visitation. Visitation shall be offered seven days a 4 week at all general confinement correctional facilities. 5 § 2. This act shall take effect one year after it shall have become a 6 law.
