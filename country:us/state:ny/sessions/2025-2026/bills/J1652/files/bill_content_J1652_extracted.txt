and WHEREAS, Edward Robert Grexa of Cortlandt Manor, New York, died on February 7, 2026, at the age of 89; and WHEREAS, Born on December 29, 1936, in the Bronx, New York, to the late Joseph and Caroline (Krankus) Grexa, Edward Robert Grexa's journey through life was marked by devotion to his family and service to his country and community; and WHEREAS, Edward Robert Grexa enjoyed an amazing 66-year marriage to his beloved wife, Karen; together they raised their daughters in their home in the town of Cortlandt Manor, NY; and WHEREAS, Edward Robert Grexa distinguished himself in his profession and by his sincere dedication and substantial contribution to the welfare of his community; and WHEREAS, A proud veteran, Edward Robert Grexa served his country with distinction in the United States Army National Guard from 1955 to 1996; and WHEREAS, A man of many interests, Edward Robert Grexa was often found tinkering with his 1951 Willy's Jeep, with a fishing rod in hand, pondering over military history, or scouting the next great find at a gun show; and WHEREAS, With a knack for making lifelong friends in the span of a single conversation, Edward Robert Grexa seemed to collect friends and stories that enriched his life; and WHEREAS, Edward Robert Grexa's commitment to excellence, and his spirit of humanity, carried over into all fields of enterprise, including charitable and civic endeavors; and WHEREAS, Edward Robert Grexa's commitment to service extended into his civilian life as well, where he was a former commissioner and a 69-year member of the Mohegan Volunteer Fire Association and an active member of the Verplanck American Legion Post 1030; and WHEREAS, A proud family man, Edward Robert Grexa was always the first to raise a glass in celebration of his family's achievements, no matter how big or small; and WHEREAS, Edward Robert Grexa is survived by his beloved wife of 66 years, Karen; his daughter Alisa (Michael); grandchildren, Lauren (Gil), Cody (Amanda), Ciarra (Bradley), and Alana; great-grandchildren, Cassidy and Ryder; his brothers, William (Grace) and George (Barbara); and his many nieces and nephews; he is predeceased by his brothers, Rudolph and Joseph; and his daughter Denise; and WHEREAS, Armed with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of compassion, Edward Robert Grexa leaves behind a legacy which will long endure the passage of time and will remain as a comforting memory to all he served and befriended; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to mourn the death of Edward Robert Grexa, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the family of Edward Robert Grexa.
