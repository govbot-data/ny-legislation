Subdivision 3 of section 15 of the workers compensation law 2 is amended by adding a new paragraph (m-1) to read as follows: 3 (m-1) Loss of a fetus. Compensation for the loss of a fetus shall be 4 for three hundred twelve weeks. 5 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
