        AN ACT to amend the alcoholic beverage control law, in relation  to  the
          exemption  of  certain parcels of land from the licensing restrictions
          prohibiting manufacturers,  wholesalers  and  retailers  of  alcoholic
          beverages from sharing an interest in a licensed premises; and provid-
          ing for the repeal of such provision upon expiration thereof
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Paragraph (a) of subdivision 1 of section 101 of the  alco-
     2  holic beverage control law is amended by adding a new subparagraph (xix)
     3  to read as follows:
     4    (xix)  Any  licensed  manufacturer  or wholesaler located on the fifth
     5  floor of all that certain plot, piece or parcel of land, situate,  lying
     6  and  being  in  the  borough of Manhattan, city, county and state of New
     7  York, bounded and described as follows:
     8    Beginning at a point on the easterly side of  Greene  Street,  distant
     9  138  feet  6 inches southerly from the corner formed by the intersection
    10  of the easterly side of Greene Street with the southerly side of  Prince
    11  Street;  running  thence  easterly on a line parallel with the southerly
    12  side of Prince Street and for part of the distance through a party wall,
    13  100 feet 1/2 of an inch to the easterly face of the easterly wall of the
    14  building on the premises herein described; thence southerly  along  said
    15  easterly face and on a line parallel or nearly so with the easterly side
    16  of  Greene  Street,  12  feet;  thence easterly nearly parallel with the
    17  southerly side of Prince Street and along the southerly line  of  ground
    18  now  or  formerly  belonging to William M. Simore, 100 feet 2-1/2 to the
    19  westerly side of Mercer  Street,  with  the  southerly  side  of  Prince
    20  Street;  thence  southerly  along the westerly side of Mercer Street, 50
    21  feet to the southerly line of Lot 136 on the map of Bayard's West Farms;
    22  thence westerly along said  southerly  line  nearly  parallel  with  the
    23  southerly  side  of  Prince  Street  and part of the distance through  a
    24  party wall, 100 feet 2 inches to the easterly face of the easterly  wall
