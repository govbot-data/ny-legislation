and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 2025 Gone But Not Forgotten Mother's Day Brunch to be held on Saturday, May 10, 2025; and WHEREAS, The Gone But Not Forgotten Mother's Day Brunch stands as a heartfelt tribute to mothers who have passed on, as well as to those who have served as maternal figures in our lives; and WHEREAS, This annual gathering offers an inclusive and uplifting environment for individuals to honor and celebrate their beloved mothers, grandmothers, aunts, godmothers, and other cherished maternal figures, fostering a sense of unity and healing among attendees; and WHEREAS, The 2025 event promises a vibrant program, creating a space for reflection, joy, and communal support; and WHEREAS, The organizers of the Gone But Not Forgotten Mother's Day Brunch have demonstrated a steadfast commitment to honoring the legacy of mothers and maternal figures, providing a platform for shared memories and collective healing; and WHEREAS, It is fitting to recognize and commend the efforts of the event organizers and participants for their dedication to preserving the memory of loved ones and strengthening community bonds through this meaningful celebration; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 2025 Gone But Not Forgotten Mother's Day Brunch, and to extend its heartfelt appreciation to the organizers and attendees for their contributions to the cultural and emotional fabric of our community; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the organizers of the Gone But Not Forgotten Mother's Day Brunch.
