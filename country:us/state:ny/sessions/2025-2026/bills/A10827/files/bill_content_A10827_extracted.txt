BURROUGHS -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to prohibiting charter schools from employing teachers who are uncertified or do not meet the same teaching certification requirements as public school teachers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (a-1) of subdivision 3 of section 2854 of the 2 education law, as amended by section 1 of subpart A of part B of chapter 3 20 of the laws of 2015, is amended to read as follows: 4 (a-1) The board of trustees of a charter school shall employ and 5 contract with necessary teachers, administrators and other school 6 personnel. Such teachers shall be certified in accordance with the 7 requirements applicable to other public schools[; provided, however, 8 that a charter school may employ as teachers (i) uncertified teachers 9 with at least three years of elementary, middle or secondary classroom 10 teaching experience; (ii) tenured or tenure track college faculty; (iii) 11 individuals with two years of satisfactory experience through the Teach 12 for America program; and (iv) individuals who possess exceptional busi- 13 ness, professional, artistic, athletic, or military experience, 14 provided, however, that such teachers described in clauses (i), (ii), 15 (iii), and (iv) of this paragraph shall not in total comprise more than 16 the sum of: (A) thirty per centum of the teaching staff of a charter 17 school, or five teachers, whichever is less; plus (B) five teachers of 18 mathematics, science, computer science, technology, or career and tech- 19 nical education; plus (C) five additional teachers. A teacher certified 20 or otherwise approved by the commissioner shall not be included in the 21 numerical limits established by the preceding sentence]. 22 § 2. This act shall take effect July 1, 2028. Effective immediately, 23 the addition, amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation neces- 24 sary for the implementation of this act on its effective date are 25 authorized to be made and completed on or before such date.
