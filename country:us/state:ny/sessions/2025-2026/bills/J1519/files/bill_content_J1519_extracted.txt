and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Alexis "Lexi" Varecka of Flex Yoga Buffalo upon the occasion of her designation as Emerging Entrepreneur by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated at its 53rd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 19, 2026, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, Alexis Varecka is the founder of Flex Yoga Buffalo, a community-centered yoga studio in Clarence, New York, that is dedicated to creating a safe space where people can slow down, reconnect with themselves, and leave feeling grounded, balanced, and connected; and WHEREAS, Originally from Clarence, New York, Alexis Varecka has always had a passion for yoga, movement, community, and wellbeing, which inspired her to open a studio of her own; and WHEREAS, In 2019, Alexis Varecka earned her 200-hour Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT) certification from CorePower Yoga in Georgetown, Washington, D.C., she then continued developing her expertise by participating in a 300-hour training in Los Angeles, California, and she is now a 500-hour RYT; and WHEREAS, Alexis Varecka's teaching is informed by experience and authenticity, and she specializes in hot power fusion, which is a dynamic blend of 26+2 and power yoga; at the core of her teaching philosophy is her desire to make yoga approachable for all individuals at all levels; and WHEREAS, In 2024, Alexis Varecka left California to move back to Clarence, New York, and in 2025 she decided to open Flex Yoga Buffalo across from Clarence High School, where she is surrounded by local small businesses; and WHEREAS, Founded and operated upon Alexis Varecka's vision, Flex Yoga Buffalo has become a welcoming hub for movement, mindfulness, and meaningful connection in Clarence, New York, and all who have visited her studio can attest to feeling seen, supported, and inspired; and WHEREAS, The Clarence Chamber of Commerce's recognition of Alexis Varecka as Emerging Entrepreneur is a fitting tribute to an individual whose innovation and service has strengthened the Clarence community and enriched the lives of countless individuals and families; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Alexis "Lexi" Varecka of Flex Yoga Buffalo upon the occasion of her designation as Emerging Entrepreneur by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 19, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Alexis "Lexi" Varecka of Flex Yoga Buffalo.
