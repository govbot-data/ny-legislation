(a) Each casino and mobile sports wagering licensee shall adopt 11 comprehensive house rules and implement a fair play policy governing 12 sports wagering transactions with its authorized sports bettors. The 13 rules shall specify the amounts to be paid on winning wagers and the 14 effect of schedule changes. The house rules, together with the fair play 15 policy and any other information the commission deems appropriate, shall 16 be conspicuously displayed in the sports wagering lounge and included in 17 the terms and conditions of the account wagering system, and copies 18 shall be made readily available to authorized sports bettors. Mobile 19 sports wagering operators shall develop and prominently display proce- 20 dures on the main page of such mobile sports wagering operator's plat- 21 form for the filing of complaints by authorized sports bettors against 22 such mobile sports wagering operator. An initial response shall be given 23 by such mobile sports wagering operator to such bettor filing the 24 complaint within forty-eight hours. A complete response shall be given 25 by such mobile sports wagering operator to such bettor filing the 26 complaint within ten business days. An authorized sports bettor may file 27 a complaint alleging a violation of the provisions of this article with 28 the commission. 29 (b) A fair play policy shall establish categories of sports wagers 30 eligible for adjustment, refund or voiding based on objective criteria 31 when circumstances beyond the control of an authorized sports bettor 32 compromise the validity or settlement of a sports wager. 33 (i) Such categories shall include but not be limited to the following 34 circumstances: 35 (A) a player or athlete does not participate in or withdraws from a 36 sports event; 37 (B) an event is cancelled or suspended; 38 (C) a statistical error or scoring correction occurs; or 39 (D) other extraordinary circumstances as determined by the commission. 40 (ii) Such fair play policy shall: (A) be applied without requiring 41 action by an authorized sports bettor where reasonably practicable; (B) 42 be applied consistently and in a nondiscriminatory manner to similarly 43 situated authorized sports bettors; (C) require that the terms and 44 conditions of such policy are clearly disclosed to authorized sports 45 bettors prior to sports wager placement; and (D) be readily accessible 46 on a mobile sports wagering licensee's website and platform. 47 § 3.
