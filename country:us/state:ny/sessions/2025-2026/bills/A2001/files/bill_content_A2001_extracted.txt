SLATER -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the state administrative procedure act, in relation to requiring the approval of the legislature for certain proposed rule changes The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The state administrative procedure act is amended by adding 2 a new section 201-b to read as follows: 3 § 201-b. Legislative approval of certain rules. 1. Prior to commence- 4 ment of the public comment period of a proposed rule requiring legisla- 5 tive approval under this section, such proposed rule shall be approved 6 by the assembly and the senate after being submitted to the speaker of 7 the assembly, the majority leader of the assembly, the minority leader 8 of the assembly, the temporary president of the senate, and the minority 9 leader of the senate. 10 2. A proposed rule shall require legislative approval when such rule: 11 (a) Includes civil or criminal penalties; 12 (b) Impacts civil or criminal penalties; 13 (c) Impacts the fiscal liability or responsibility of a municipality 14 or school district within the state; 15 (d) Impacts the costs due for a resident of the state, including, but 16 not limited to; 17 (i) Tuition; 18 (ii) Hunting license fees; or 19 (iii) DMV fees; or 20 (e) Changes the state's education curriculum. 21 3. A proposed rule shall be approved upon the adoption of a resolution 22 submitted by the agency seeking to adopt such rule by simple majority 23 vote in the assembly and a simple majority vote in the senate.
