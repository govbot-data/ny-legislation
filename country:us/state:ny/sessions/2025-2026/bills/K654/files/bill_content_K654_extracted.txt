Matt Brewing Company; and WHEREAS, As a nonprofit foundation, the Boilermaker continues to promote year-round wellness initiatives and community programming, including the Access Program, the Charity Bib Program, and the Community Garden, ensuring that its positive impact extends far beyond race day; and WHEREAS, The 48th Anniversary of the Boilermaker Road Race is a testament to the tireless efforts of its organizers, the dedication of its volunteers, and the enthusiastic support of the Utica community and beyond; and WHEREAS, The Boilermaker represents the best of what New York State has to offer--determination, inclusivity, partnership, and pride in place; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Boilermaker Road Race, upon the occasion of its 48th Anniversary, and to recognize its continued tradition of excellence, service, and celebration of the human spirit; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Boilermaker Road Race and its organizing committee with the sincere congratulations and appreciation of this Legislative Body.
