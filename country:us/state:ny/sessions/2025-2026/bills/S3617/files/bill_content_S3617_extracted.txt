The public service law is amended by adding a new section 2 66-y to read as follows: 3 § 66-y. Community wind energy pilot program. 1. The commission shall 4 establish a New York state pilot program promoting the use of community 5 wind energy. 6 2. The commission shall oversee the implementation of the pilot 7 program and shall issue an order of the commission authorizing the 8 construction of a wind energy system installation. 9 3. The commission shall invite consumers to participate in the program 10 as subscribers, provided that: 11 (a) the commission shall set a subscription schedule, including the 12 price per watt to subscribe and the percentage of the total installation 13 each subscription level shall represent; 14 (b) subscribers shall make a one time payment to the commission and, 15 in return, subscribers shall be paid a quarterly dividend for the energy 16 generated by the watts covered by their subscription level; 17 (c) a consumer shall be paid on a quarterly basis based on the consum- 18 er's subscription level; 19 (d) the commission shall set a maximum number of subscriptions at each 20 level; and 21 (e) the minimum price per subscription shall not exceed five hundred 22 dollars. 23 4. All energy generated by the wind energy system installation shall 24 be sold back to an investor-owned utility.
