AN ACT in relation to authorizing the Jamesville-DeWitt central school
district to discontinue the use of school district-owned parklands and
to lease such parklands to the town of Onondaga
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the Jamesville-DeW-
2 itt central school district, in the county of Onondaga, acting by and
3 through its board of education, and upon such terms and conditions as
4 determined by such board, is hereby authorized to discontinue the use of
5 the school district-owned parkland more particularly described in
6 section three of this act and to lease such parklands at fair market
7 value, for a term not to exceed thirty years, to the town of Onondaga,
8 located in the county of Onondaga, for use by such town for park and/or
9 recreational purposes, provided, however, that such town of Onondaga
10 shall continue to provide access to such parkland and/or recreational
11 facilities to all residents of the Jamesville-DeWitt central school
12 district and the town shall not enact any fees or charges which are
13 higher for residents of the Jamesville-DeWitt central school district
14 who are not town residents.
15 § 2. The authorization contained in section one of this act shall only
16 be effective on the condition that the Jamesville-DeWitt central school
17 district dedicate the proceeds from the lease towards the acquisition of
18 new parklands and/or capital improvements to existing park and recre-
19 ational facilities.
20 § 3. The parklands authorized by section one of this act to be leased
21 from the Jamesville-DeWitt central school district pursuant to section
22 one of this act are described as follows:
