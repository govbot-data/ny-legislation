Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "Drink Up Act". 3 § 2. The education law is amended by adding a new section 916-c to 4 read as follows: 5 § 916-c. Water during lunch; requirement. Every public school shall 6 offer water in a sealed bottled container as an additional drink option 7 to any student during lunch at no cost to such student. 8 § 3.
