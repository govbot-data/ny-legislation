Subdivision (a) of section 36.04 of the mental hygiene law, 2 as added by section 1 of part HH of chapter 57 of the laws of 2023, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 (a) The commissioners are authorized to jointly certify community 5 behavioral health clinics, subject to the availability of state and 6 federal funding.
