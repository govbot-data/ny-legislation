TAPIA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to specifying that certain teachers, teacher aides, and teacher assistants shall be included in the excessive teacher turnover prevention program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 4411 2 to read as follows: 3 § 4411. Inclusion in excessive teacher turnover prevention program. 1. 4 For the purposes of this section, the term "covered school" shall mean a 5 school or preschool receiving funding under section forty-four hundred 6 ten of this article for the provision of special education, as defined 7 by subdivision one of section forty-four hundred one of this article, 8 and shall include private schools established under chapter eight 9 hundred fifty-three of the laws of nineteen hundred seventy-six. 10 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, rule or regulation, the 11 department's excessive teacher turnover prevention program shall include 12 support for teachers, teacher aides, and teacher assistants whose duties 13 include working with children with a disability, and who are employed by 14 a covered school. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
