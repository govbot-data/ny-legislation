Requires health and motor vehicle insurance policies to notify policyholders of cancellation, discontinuance or major changes to their policy via email and to offer paperless notification upon the issuance of such policies.

        AN ACT to amend the insurance law, in relation to requiring  health  and
          motor  vehicle insurance policies to notify policyholders of cancella-
          tion, discontinuance or major changes to their policy via email and to
          offer paperless notification upon the issuance of such policies
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The opening paragraph of subparagraph (H) of paragraph 2 of
     2  subsection (d) of section 3216 of the insurance law, as amended by chap-
     3  ter 344 of the laws of 1994, is amended to read as follows:
     4    CANCELLATION:  Within  the  first ninety days after the date of issue,
     5  the insurer may cancel this policy by written notice  delivered  to  the
     6  insured, or sent by first class mail to [his] the insured's last address
     7  as  shown by the records of the insurer, and shall also give such notice
     8  by email if the insurer has the insured's email address on file  stating
     9  when,  not  less  than  ten  days thereafter, such cancellation shall be
    10  effective. In the event of cancellation, the insurer will return prompt-
    11  ly the pro-rata unearned portion of any premium paid. Cancellation shall
    12  be without prejudice to any claim originating  prior  to  the  effective
    13  date of cancellation.
