Subdivision 2 and the closing paragraph of section 2012 of 2 the education law, subdivision 2 as amended by chapter 115 of the laws 3 of 1973 and the closing paragraph as amended by chapter 919 of the laws 4 of 1974, are amended to read as follows: 5 2.
