and WHEREAS, Music competition provides an opportunity for students to exhibit their practical skills to an audience, thereby giving them a sense of pride in their hard work and efforts; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Felicia Garramone upon the occasion of winning first-place in the prestigious International Piano Competition "Andret Stoyanov" in Sofia, Bulgaria; and WHEREAS, A junior at General Douglas MacArthur High School in the Levittown School District, Felicia Garramone began playing the piano at the age of four and quickly progressed through the NYSSMA levels, achieving Level 6 with a score of 100 in fifth grade; and WHEREAS, Felicia Garramone subsequently began competing in online and in-person competitions, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic; in eighth grade, she earned first place in a competition which allowed her to perform at Carnegie Hall; and WHEREAS, In addition to piano, Felicia Garramone has played the cello since third grade and has been consistently selected for the All-County and LISFA programs throughout her elementary school years; by eighth grade, she also completed NYSSMA Level 6 with a score of 100; and WHEREAS, During her sophomore year, Felicia Garramone auditioned for All-State and received a perfect score of 100; in December 2024, she was privileged to perform with the All-State Orchestra in Rochester; and WHEREAS, Felicia Garramone's remarkable achievements in both piano and cello exemplify her exceptional talent, dedication, and passion for music; with a bright future ahead, her musical achievements highlight her relentless drive for excellence; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Felicia Garramone upon the occasion of winning first-place in the prestigious International Piano Competition "Andret Stoyanov" in Sofia, Bulgaria; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Felicia Garramone.
