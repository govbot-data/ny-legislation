Subdivision 4 of section 1367-a of the racing, pari-mutuel 2 wagering and breeding law is amended by adding a new paragraph (k) to 3 read as follows: 4 (k) Mobile sports wagering operators and mobile sports wagering plat- 5 forms shall be prohibited from allowing more than five deposits from an 6 authorized sports bettor in a twenty-four-hour period. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
