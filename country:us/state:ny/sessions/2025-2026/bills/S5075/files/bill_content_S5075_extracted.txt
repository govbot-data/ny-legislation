Paragraph (e) of subdivision 2 of section 433 of the 2 economic development law, as added by section 1 of part A of chapter 68 3 of the laws of 2013, is amended to read as follows: 4 (e) medical or dental practices, except medical practices which solely 5 provide primary care services; 6 § 2. Subparagraph (i) of paragraph (c) of subdivision 1 of section 432 7 of the economic development law, as added by section 1 of part A of 8 chapter 68 of the laws of 2013, is amended to read as follows: 9 (i) a state university campus other than the following: (A) any empire 10 state college campus except for the empire state college campus in Sara- 11 toga Springs, [(B) any property of downstate medical center located in 12 Nassau county, Suffolk county, Westchester county or New York city 13 except for property affiliated with downstate medical center that 14 constitutes a New York state incubator as the term is used in subdivi- 15 sion four of section four hundred thirty-three of this article,] and 16 [(C)] (B) any property of the college of optometry or maritime college 17 located in Nassau county, Suffolk county, Westchester county or New York 18 city. 19 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
