the Eagle Court of Honor ceremony will be held on Sunday, March 22, 2026, at the Manhasset Lakeville Fire Department Company No. 2 in Manhasset, New York; and WHEREAS, Owen Lambert is a member of Scouts BSA Troop 71, Plandome, New York; and WHEREAS, Scouts BSA and its members are dedicated to the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in Scouting, earned by approximately five percent of all Scouts, is the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout; and WHEREAS, Owen Lambert joined Scouting in the third grade as a Tiger Cub in Pack 101 and, after earning the Arrow of Light, the highest honor in Cub Scouts, crossed over to Troop 71; and WHEREAS, Throughout his Scouting journey, Owen Lambert served as a positive role model for younger Scouts, demonstrating commitment, patience, and enthusiasm, and held several leadership positions within Troop 71, including Assistant Patrol Leader and Scribe; and WHEREAS, Owen Lambert further developed his leadership skills by participating in the week-long National Youth Leadership Training program at the Schiff Scout Reservation in Wading River, New York, where he camped independently and completed more than 30 hours of classroom instruction in leadership; and WHEREAS, Inspired by a deep love of the outdoors, Owen Lambert attended summer camp for three years at the Onteora Scout Reservation, where he formed lasting friendships and cherished memories of campfire stories and nights under the stars; and WHEREAS, Scouting has been a formative experience in Owen Lambert's life, providing him with meaningful opportunities to lead, communicate effectively, plan, and stay organized, and he remains profoundly grateful to the adult leadership of Troop 71 for their steadfast guidance and support; and WHEREAS, Owen Lambert earned 33 Merit Badges, citing among his favorites Cooking, Architecture, and Disabilities Awareness, each of which broadened his knowledge and perspective; and WHEREAS, This outstanding young man has been involved in Scouting for many years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all the necessary achievements, including completion of a community service project, which was successfully planned, developed, and executed; and WHEREAS, For his project, Owen Lambert constructed and painted ten bat houses which now hang in Waldmann Memorial Park in the Village of Munsey Park, providing a natural and environmentally friendly method of mosquito control that will benefit residents for years to come; and WHEREAS, Owen Lambert graduated from Manhasset High School in 2025, where he completed multiple Honors and Advanced Placement courses, was an active member of Athletes Helping Athletes for three years, and served as a lead volunteer with Manhasset Adaptive Sports; and WHEREAS, During his high school years, Owen Lambert earned both the Presidential Bronze and Silver Service Awards and rowed for Manhasset Crew for five years, serving as Captain in his senior year, earning recognition as a two-time Most Valuable Player, competing twice in the Head of the Charles Regatta, and culminating his rowing career as a National Champion in the Boys Lightweight Double at Youth Scholastic Nationals; and WHEREAS, Owen Lambert is currently completing his freshman year at James Madison University in Harrisonburg, Virginia, where he is majoring in Business Management and is an active member of the nationally ranked Club Golf Team and the Phi Kappa Psi fraternity; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school, his community, his State, and his Nation, and may now stand with pride as he assess his achievements, experiences the satisfaction of his labors and the joy of his accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Owen Lambert on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Owen Lambert.
