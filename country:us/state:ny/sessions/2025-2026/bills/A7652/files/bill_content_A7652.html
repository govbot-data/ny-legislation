A07652 Summary:

BILL NOA07652
 
SAME ASNo Same As
 
SPONSORStern
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §606, Tax L
 
Increases the real property tax circuit breaker credit income limits to $100,000; removes real property tax rebates from real property taxes that qualify under such credit.
