and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body records, with profound sorrow, the passing of Anthony John Gagliotti on Saturday, March 8, 2025, at the age of 93, recognizing his significant life and contributions; and WHEREAS, A lifelong resident of Croton-on-Hudson, New York, born on October 20, 1931, to John and Agnesa (Ventrone) Gagliotti, Anthony John Gagliotti embodied the values of his upbringing in the Great Depression, where neighbor depended on neighbor, forging community connections; and WHEREAS, For more than 75 years, Anthony John Gagliotti was an active member of the Croton Volunteer Fire Department, including service as Secretary of his company and Chief of Department; he demonstrated dedication to public safety and concern for his neighbors; and WHEREAS, Anthony John Gagliotti was a devoted member of the Holy Name of Mary Catholic Church, the Knights of Columbus, and the Lions Club, contributing significantly to the life of his community; and WHEREAS, Through his marriages to Jiovanna Canizzaro, Helen Ubaldi, and Veronica Barnes, Anthony John Gagliotti created a loving blended family, including his children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren; and WHEREAS, His professional life, including terms as Assessor and Assistant Building Inspector for the Village of Croton-on-Hudson, was distinguished by professionalism and consideration; and WHEREAS, Anthony John Gagliotti's vibrant spirit, love for people, and community involvement made him a beloved figure, leaving a legacy of service and earning him the Key to the Village of Croton-on-Hudson and the honor of leading the 2024 Summerfest parade as its Grand Marshal; and WHEREAS, Anthony John Gagliotti's legacy of compassion and service merits the grateful tribute of this Legislative Body; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to mourn the death of Anthony John Gagliotti and express its deepest condolences to his family; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the family of Anthony John Gagliotti.
