and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to commend the illustrious bands of the State of New York, the focus and intent of which have made a substantial impact on the quality of life in their communities; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 25th Anniversary of the band Slug; and WHEREAS, Stephen "Fletch" Fletcher, Bobby Moore, and Joe Trisolino have been at the heart of Slug as its core three members for twenty-five years, remaining constant and faithful to the act through a revolving door of more than thirty other members; joined now by Michael Finley, Walt "Gator" Darman, and TJ Rycraft, the band has recorded a new album, "Slug 25," to commemorate its inimitable twenty-five years; and WHEREAS, Blending the influences of bluegrass, country rock, blues, Americana, and the Grateful Dead, Slug has cultivated an eclectic, diverse sound that defies genres and has amassed a cult following; and WHEREAS, After a long hiatus from recording, Slug stepped into the studio again with Bob Aquaviva to create a polished record composed of songs previously left unfinished or unrecorded, as well as brand-new material; and WHEREAS, Slug celebrated its twenty-five-year anniversary with a limited-seating album release party at the Stanley Theater on Friday, October 10, 2025; and WHEREAS, Throughout its illustrious history, Slug has provided continuous entertainment to its surrounding communities; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 25th Anniversary of the band Slug; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the band Slug;
