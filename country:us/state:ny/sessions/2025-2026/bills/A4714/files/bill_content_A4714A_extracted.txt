AN ACT to amend the tax law, in relation to exemptions for any not-for-
profit tax exempt corporation operated for conservation, environ-
mental, parks or historic preservation purposes
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 1405 of the tax law is amended by adding a new
2 subdivision (c) to read as follows:
3 (c) Conveyances of real property for open space, parks, or historic
4 preservation purposes to any not-for-profit tax exempt corporation oper-
5 ated for conservation, environmental, parks or historic preservation
6 purposes shall be exempt from payment of additional taxes imposed pursu-
7 ant to section fourteen hundred two-a of this article.
8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
