AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to authorizing the
public service commission to establish rules to limit a utility's
ability to recover certain operating expenses
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 12 of section 66 of the public service law is
2 amended by adding a new paragraph (m) to read as follows:
3 (m) (i) The commission is authorized and directed to, no later than
4 January first, two thousand twenty-seven, establish rules to limit a
5 utility's ability to recover its direct or indirect costs associated
6 with its attendance in, participation in, preparation for, or appeal of
7 any rate proceeding conducted before the commission. Such costs shall
8 include, but need not be limited to, attorneys' fees, fees to engage
9 expert witnesses or consultants, the portion of employee salaries asso-
10 ciated with such attendance, participation, preparation or appeal of a
11 rate proceeding and related costs identified by the commission.
12 (ii) In establishing such rules the commission may consider: (A)
13 setting an overall percentage of the utility's expenses in a rate case
14 that are not recoverable; (B) setting a baseline of the reasonable cost
15 of participation in a rate case; and (C) establishing discovery parame-
16 ters and what information in a proceeding must be disclosed to interven-
17 ers and to the commission to reduce time and costs associated with a
18 lengthy discovery process.
19 § 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after
20 it shall have become a law.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
