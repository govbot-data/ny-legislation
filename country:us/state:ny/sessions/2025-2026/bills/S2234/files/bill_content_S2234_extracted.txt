Whenever a hearing must be continued or adjourned because the 27 carrier or employer has engaged in dilatory tactics or exhibited unjus- 28 tified lack of preparedness, the board shall impose a penalty of twen- 29 ty-five dollars to be paid to the fund created by subdivision two of 30 section one hundred fifty-one of this chapter and shall in addition make 31 an award of seventy-five dollars payable to the injured worker or [his 32 or her dependants] their dependents. Dilatory tactics may include but 33 shall not be limited to: failing to subpoena medical witnesses or to 34 secure an order to show cause as directed by the referee, failing to 35 bring proper files, failing to appear, failing to produce witnesses or 36 documents after they have been requested by the referee or examiner or 37 as directed by the hearing notice, unnecessarily protracting the 38 production of evidence, or engaging in a pattern of delay which unduly 39 delays resolution, except that no penalty shall be imposed nor award 40 made under this subdivision if the carrier or employer produces evidence 41 sufficient to excuse its conduct to the satisfaction of the referee. 42 § 4.
