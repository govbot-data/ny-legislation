and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Karen Mitchell upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition at the Young, Gifted, and Black Entrepreneurial Awards 20th Anniversary Gala on Wednesday, February 26, 2025, at Terrace on the Park, Queens, New York; and WHEREAS, The Young, Gifted, & Black (YGB) Entrepreneurial Awards were established in 2006 by Carl Gray as a platform to spotlight and celebrate everyday individuals who serve as positive role models, inspiring their peers through their accomplishments, perseverance, and contributions; and WHEREAS, Carl Gray's vision for the YGB Entrepreneurial Awards has remained steadfast in ensuring recognition is given to those whose contributions, though not always highlighted in mainstream media, have a profound and lasting influence within their fields and society; and WHEREAS, Karen Mitchell is a Jamaican-born licensed cosmetologist, renowned hair extension expert, and owner of the True Indian Hair franchise under the umbrella of the True Hair Company; this esteemed honoree began her mission to provide high quality extensions for career women in 2004, when she noticed a lack of diversity in ownership roles in the hair care industry; and WHEREAS, Under Karen Mitchell's leadership, True Hair Company has emerged as a leader in the industry, earning accolades from Essence and Cosmopolitan Magazines; the brand has also been featured in prominent publications like InStyle, Vogue, and Elle, as well as on various primetime TV shows; True Hair Company successfully continues to grow in the market, reaching company record highs in 2023; and WHEREAS, In response to increasing demand, True Hair Company has expanded from its flagship Brooklyn boutique, to over a 3,000 square foot midtown Manhattan flagship store; and WHEREAS, Karen Mitchell and her team intend to continue expanding to other markets in 2025, including Atlanta, and over the next six years, they plan to establish several locations in Houston, Miami, Washington, DC, and London; and WHEREAS, True Hair Company has gained widespread recognition, counting celebrities like Rihanna, Cardi B, and Taraji P. Henson as part of its clientele; in addition to her business achievements, Karen Mitchell founded True Strength, a non-profit organization that provides free wigs and makeovers to women struggling with hair loss; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those individuals and organizations which give positive definition to the profile and disposition of the communities of the State of New York, do so profoundly strengthen our shared commitment to the exercise of freedom; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Karen Mitchell upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition at the Young, Gifted, and Black Entrepreneurial Awards 20th Anniversary Gala on Wednesday, February 26, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Karen Mitchell.
