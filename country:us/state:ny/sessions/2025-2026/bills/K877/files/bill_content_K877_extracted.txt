Gallahan CONGRATULATING the Whitney Point Senior High School Girls Field Hockey Team and Coach Nicole Huston upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Class C State Championship WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly sought after in high school sports; this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Whitney Point Senior High School Girls Field Hockey Team and Coach Nicole Huston upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Class C State Championship on Saturday, November 15, 2025 at Arlington High School in LaGrangeville, New York; and WHEREAS, Family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Eagles throughout their journey as they captured the NYSPHSAA Class C State Championship; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as field hockey, which demands athletic prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Nicole Huston and her skilled coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and WHEREAS, Coach Nicole Huston and all of the outstanding athletes on the Whitney Point Senior High School Girls Field Hockey Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Whitney Point Senior High School Girls Field Hockey Team: Charlie Abbott, Bree Bechtel, Peyton Brown, Emma Davies, Addison Feathers, Brooke Feathers, Leah Griffith, Mackenzie Harvey, Colleen Hilderbrant, Anah Huston, Genevieve Huston, Destiny Keller, Ashlyn Lee, Grace Lewis, Lauren Magill, Payton Martin, Archer McIntyre, Ava Oliver, Siri Schafer, Sade Short, Brinley Smith, Kylan Smith, Cecelia Stiner, Lacey Tillotson, Ava Vuille; Head Coach Nicole Huston; and Assistant Coach Danielle Montesano on their outstanding season and capturing the NYSPHSAA Class C State Championship; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Whitney Point Senior High School Girls Field Hockey Team, Head Coach Nicole Huston, and Assistant Coach Danielle Montesano.
