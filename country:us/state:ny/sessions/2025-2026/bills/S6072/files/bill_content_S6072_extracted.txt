This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Modernized 2 Voter Registration Act of New York". 3 § 2. Notwithstanding any other 6 manner of registration required by this article, each person in the 7 state qualified to vote pursuant to section 5-102 of this article, shall 8 be automatically registered to vote as provided in this section, 9 provided that the person consents to voter registration. 10 2. Same day registration. Each county shall allow any eligible 47 individual on the day of an election and on any day when voting, includ- 48 ing early voting, to register to vote in such election at the polling 49 place and to cast a vote in such election. 50 § 5-205. Internet registration. 1. The board shall ensure that the 51 following services are available to the public at any time on the offi- 52 cial public websites of the appropriate county or municipality, in the 53 same manner and subject to the same terms and conditions as the services 54 provided by voter registration regulations: (a) online application for 55 voter registration; (b) online assistance to applicants in applying to 56 register to vote; (c) online completion and submission by applicants of S. 6072 4 1 the mail voter registration application including assistance with 2 providing a signature in electronic form; and (d) online receipt of 3 completed voter registration applications. 4 2. Any county or municipality shall accept an online voter registra- 5 tion application provided by an individual under this section, and 6 ensure that the individual is registered to vote in the state, if (a) 7 the individual meets the same voter registration requirements applicable 8 to individuals who register to vote by mail in accordance with the law 9 using the mail voter registration application form and (b) the individ- 10 ual provides a signature in electronic form. 11 3. For purposes of this section, an individual provides a signature in 12 electronic form by (a) executing a computerized mark in the signature 13 field on an online voter registration application; or 14 (b) submitting with the application an electronic copy of the individ- 15 ual's handwritten signature through electronic means. 16 4. (a) Upon the online submission of a completed voter registration 17 application by an individual under this section, the appropriate state 18 or local election official shall send the individual a notice confirming 19 the state's receipt of the application and providing instructions on how 20 the individual may check the status of the application, and (b) as soon 21 as the appropriate election official has approved or rejected an appli- 22 cation submitted by an individual under this section, the official shall 23 send the individual a notice of the disposition of the application. 24 5.
