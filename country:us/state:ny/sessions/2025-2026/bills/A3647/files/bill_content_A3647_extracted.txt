Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "faith-based affordable housing act". 3 § 2. The general municipal law is amended by adding a new section 96-c 4 to read as follows: 5 § 96-c. Residential development on religious land. 1. For the purposes 6 of this section, the following terms shall have the following meanings: 7 (a) "Covered site" shall mean any parcel of land, or group of one or 8 more contiguous parcels of land, that lies within an urban area as 9 defined by the U.S. Census Bureau but does not lie within a locally 10 designated manufacturing or industrial zoning district, where such 11 parcel or group of one or more contiguous parcels of land is owned sole- 12 ly, directly or indirectly (e.g. via a wholly owned limited liability 13 company) by a religious corporation as of the effective date of this 14 section.
