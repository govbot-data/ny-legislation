The purpose of this title is to create a 14 streamlined adoption process for a child's parents to confirm their 15 parent-child relationship through an adoption in order to protect the 16 child's best interests. Such adoption process shall ensure all parents 17 are afforded full rights and protections equivalent to that of biolog- 18 ical parents, especially for children conceived through assisted reprod- 19 uction. 20 § 119-a. Those eligible include the child's intended parents as 23 defined in section 581-102 of the family court act and the current or 24 former spouse of a birthing parent, provided that the person is named on 25 the child's birth certificate as a parent.
