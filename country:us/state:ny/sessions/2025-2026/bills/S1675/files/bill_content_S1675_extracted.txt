GOUNARDES, COMRIE, GIANARIS, HOYLMAN-SIGAL, JACKSON, KRUEGER, MYRIE, RIVERA, SALAZAR, SEPULVEDA, STAVISKY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to creating a pedestrian safety rating system for motor vehicles The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 216-b to read as follows: 3 § 216-b. Pedestrian safety rating system for motor vehicle models. 1. 4 The commissioner shall cause to be created a pedestrian safety rating 5 system for every motor vehicle model registered by individuals in this 6 state which shall be made publicly available on the department's inter- 7 net website. Such pedestrian safety rating system shall provide a rating 8 between one and five stars for every motor vehicle model based on: 9 (a) the frequency of collisions and number and severity of injuries 10 resulting from such collisions involving a bicyclist or pedestrian as 11 reported in the state for such motor vehicle model, compared to the 12 total number of such motor vehicle model registered in the state; 13 (b) safety features that the commissioner determines are relevant to 14 pedestrian and bicyclist safety, including but not limited to, automatic 15 collision warning or pedestrian detection features, automatic braking, 16 and visibility; and 17 (c) any other factors the commissioner determines are relevant to 18 pedestrian and bicyclist safety. 19 2. The commissioner shall issue regulations to all motor vehicle 20 manufacturers, dealers, brokers, distributors, and other business enti- 21 ties required to register in section four hundred fifteen of this chap- 22 ter to require such entities to adopt the pedestrian safety rating 23 system prescribed in subdivision one of this section at such entity's EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
