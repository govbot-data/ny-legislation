Assembly Resolution No. 1497
 
BY: M. of A. Buttenschon
 
        COMMENDING Walter Constantini upon the occasion of
        his  designation for special recognition by the Lake
        Delta Kiwanis Club of Rome on June 6, 2026
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
commend  those  distinguished  citizens  of  the State of New York whose
purposeful  lives  and  exemplary  service  contribute  greatly  to  the
strength and vitality of their communities and this great Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  Walter  Constantini  upon  the  occasion of his designation for
special recognition by the Lake  Delta  Kiwanis  Club  of  Rome,  to  be
celebrated on Saturday, June 6, 2026, at Gone Coastal in Lee Center, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Walter  "Walt"  Constantini has long distinguished himself
through his steadfast dedication to his country, his community, and  his
fellow  veterans, earning the admiration and respect of all who have had
the privilege to know him; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Born  in  Mineola,  New  York,  and  raised   in   Bethel,
Connecticut, Walter Constantini answered the call to serve his Nation by
joining the United States Air Force in June of 1961; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Following  completion  of  basic  training at Lackland Air
Force Base, Walter Constantini attended technical school at Amarillo Air
Force Base for jet engine training, beginning a  distinguished  military
career marked by professionalism, commitment, and honor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  his  years  of  faithful  military service, Walter
Constantini was assigned to Ellsworth Air Force Base,  Thule  Air  Force
Base  in  Greenland,  Sewart Air Force Base in Nashville, Tennessee, Dow
Air Force Base in Bangor, Maine, RAF Lakenheath in England,  Loring  Air
Force  Base  in  Maine,  and  Griffiss Air Force Base in Rome, New York,
where he concluded his exemplary Air Force career upon retirement; and
 
  WHEREAS, Walter Constantini also proudly served with the last active
squadron  of  General  Claire  Chennault's  famed  Flying  Tigers  while
stationed  at  Dow  Air  Force  Base,  adding  to  a  legacy of military
excellence and dedication; and
 
  WHEREAS, Following his retirement from the United States Air  Force,
Walter  Constantini  continued  his  strong work ethic and commitment to
excellence in the private sector, dedicating  17  years  to  Orion  Bus,
three  years  to  Carrier  Air Conditioning, and nine years to Commander
Aviation and Empire Aviation before his retirement; and
 
  WHEREAS, Walter Constantini's devotion to veterans and civic service
is further demonstrated through his extraordinary 48 years of membership
in the American Legion, during which time he served with distinction  as
Commander of the Lee Center American Legion Post for eight years, County
 
Commander  of  the Oneida County American Legion, Oneida County Adjutant
for two years, and Fifth District Chaplain for two years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Walter Constantini has also found great fulfillment in his
devotion to family; he shared 31 years  of  marriage  with  his  beloved
wife, Joanne, until her passing five years ago, and together they raised
a   loving   family  of  five  children,  11  grandchildren,  and  three
great-grandchildren; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout his life, Walter Constantini has exemplified the
highest ideals of patriotism, integrity, public service,  and  community
spirit, leaving an enduring legacy of honorable achievement and selfless
dedication; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Walter Constantini upon the  occasion  of  his  designation  for
special  recognition  by  the Lake Delta Kiwanis Club of Rome; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Walter Constantini.
