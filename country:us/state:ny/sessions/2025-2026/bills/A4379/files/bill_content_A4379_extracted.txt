VANEL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Judiciary AN ACT to amend the real property actions and proceedings law and the New York city civil court act, in relation to the establishment of an escrow account The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 701 of the real property actions and proceedings 2 law is amended by adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows: 3 3.
