AN ACT to amend chapter 280 of the laws of 2016 authorizing the village
of Plandome Manor to enter into a contract to sell or pledge as colla-
teral for a loan some or all of the delinquent liens held by such
village to a private party or engage a private party to collect some
or all of the delinquent tax liens held by it, in relation to the
effectiveness thereof

bly, do enact as follows:

4 such village to a private party or engage a private party to collect
