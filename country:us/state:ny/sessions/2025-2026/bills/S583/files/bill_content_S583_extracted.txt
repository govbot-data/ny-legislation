HELMING, BORRELLO, OBERACKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Agriculture AN ACT to amend the agriculture and markets law, in relation to the establishment of the "Farmland for a New Generation" program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The agriculture and markets law is amended by adding a new 2 section 323-a to read as follows: 3 § 323-a. Farmland for a new generation. 1. The commissioner, together 4 with American Farmland Trust, shall oversee and administer a program 5 entitled "Farmland for a New Generation". Such program shall establish a 6 statewide resource center for farmland transition and conservation, and 7 a network of regional navigators to assist with farmland access and 8 transfer throughout New York. 9 2. The "Farmland for a New Generation" program may include, but shall 10 not be limited to: helping new farmers find affordable land to start an 11 agricultural business; enabling farmers to acquire additional land for 12 their agricultural business; assisting established farmers to find a 13 successor for their agricultural business; aiding farmland owners in 14 finding farmers willing to farm such farmland; supporting farm families 15 in transferring ownership of the farmland to successors; and assisting 16 farmers in protecting their land using conservation easements. 17 § 2. Subject to appropriation, funds shall be allocated to the depart- 18 ment of agriculture and markets for the purpose of carrying out the 19 provisions of section 323-a of the agriculture and markets law, as added 20 by section one of this act, by establishing a statewide resource center 21 in collaboration with American Farmland Trust for farmland transition 22 and conservation.
