CUNNINGHAM -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to providing a defense from liability for certain department of sanitation vehicles standing, stopped, or parked in violation of certain bus lane restrictions in New York city The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 1111-c of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision (p) to read as follows: 3 (p) It shall be a defense to any notice of liability issued to the 4 owner of a vehicle of the department of sanitation of the city of New 5 York for standing, stopping, or parking in a bus lane in violation of 6 the applicable provisions of section 4-08 of chapter four of title thir- 7 ty-four of the rules of the city of New York pursuant to a bus rapid 8 transit program adopted pursuant to this section that the operator of 9 the vehicle was an employee of the department of sanitation of the city 10 of New York performing authorized duties involving a vehicle of the 11 department of sanitation of the city of New York in such bus lane. 12 § 2. Section 1111-c-1 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by 13 adding a new subdivision (r) to read as follows: 14 (r) It shall be a defense to any notice of liability issued to the 15 owner of a vehicle of the department of sanitation of the city of New 16 York for a violation of section 4-08(f)(4) of chapter four of title 17 thirty-four of the rules of the city of New York pursuant to a demon- 18 stration program adopted pursuant to this section that the operator of 19 the vehicle was an employee of the department of sanitation of the city 20 of New York performing authorized duties involving a vehicle of the 21 department of sanitation of the city of New York in such bus lane. 22 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that 23 the amendments to sections 1111-c and 1111-c-1 of the vehicle and traf- 24 fic law made by sections one and two of this act shall not affect the 25 repeal of such sections and shall be deemed repealed therewith.
