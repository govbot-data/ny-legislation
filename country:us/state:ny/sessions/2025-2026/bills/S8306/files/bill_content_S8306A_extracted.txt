AN ACT to amend chapter 978 of the laws of 1966 relating to incorporat-
ing the South Lockport Volunteer Firemen's Benevolent Association, and
providing for its powers and duties, in relation to amendments to such
association's charter
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Sections 1, 2, 3, 4 and 7 of chapter 978 of the laws of
2 1966 relating to incorporating the South Lockport Volunteer Firemen's
3 Benevolent Association, and providing for its powers and duties, are
4 amended to read as follows:
5 Section 1. Incorporation; membership. A [membership] not-for-profit
6 corporation by the name of "South Lockport Volunteer Exempt [Firemen's]
7 Firefighters' Benevolent Association" is hereby created. It shall be
8 composed of such persons eligible to membership therein as hereinafter
9 provided as shall (a) notify the secretary of the South Lockport Fire
10 Company, Incorporated in writing prior to the organization meeting of
11 such corporation hereinafter provided of their desire to become members,
12 or (b) shall attend such meeting or an adjournment thereof, and also
13 such persons so eligible as may become members of such corporation,
14 pursuant to its by-laws.
15 § 2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now, or have
16 been, or who hereafter shall be volunteer members of the South Lockport
17 Fire Company, Incorporated, and all persons resident in the territory
18 provided with fire protection by contract with such fire company who are
19 exempt volunteer [firemen] firefighters as defined by section two
20 hundred of the general municipal law, shall be eligible to membership in
21 the corporation hereby created; provided that any person who has been
