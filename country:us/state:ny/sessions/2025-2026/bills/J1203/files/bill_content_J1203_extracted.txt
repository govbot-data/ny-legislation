Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01203 Summary:BILL NOJ01203  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORPARKER COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01203 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1203 BY: Senator PARKER HONORING Victoria Chapter No. 45 upon the occasion of hosting its Five Rays Fashion Show & Five Point Tea on October 12, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the esteemed practice of this Legislative Body to recognize organizations whose unwavering devotion to service, sisterhood, and community enhancement contributes profoundly to the cultural and social vitality of New York State; and WHEREAS, In full accord with this noble tradition, this Legislative Body is proud to honor Victoria Chapter No. 45, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, as it presents the "Five Rays Fashion Show & Five Point Tea," an elegant celebration of fellowship, fashion, and philanthropy, to be held on Sunday, October 12, 2025; and WHEREAS, This distinguished event brings together members and supporters in a refined setting to showcase the creativity and unity that define Victoria Chapter No. 45; and WHEREAS, Since its founding, Victoria Chapter No. 45 has championed the principles of faith, hope, charity, and fidelity, cultivating a robust legacy of spiritual enrichment, educational advancement, and benevolent outreach; and WHEREAS, The Five Rays Fashion Show & Five Point Tea exemplifies the Chapter's vision-merging grace and community stewardship-while furthering its mission to support local charitable initiatives and empower women through fellowship and mentorship; and WHEREAS, Under the compassionate leadership of its Worthy Matron and dedicated officers, the Chapter continues to elevate the values of the Prince Hall fraternity, celebrating the elegance, dignity, and strength of its members; and WHEREAS, It is fitting that this Legislative Body recognize and commend Victoria Chapter No. 45 for its continuing dedication to service, its celebration of unity and excellence, and its enduring influence on the communities it serves; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Victoria Chapter No. 45 upon the occasion of hosting its Five Rays Fashion Show & Five Point Tea, and to extend its warmest congratulations and best wishes for an event marked by elegance and purpose; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Victoria Chapter No. 45, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, and to its Worthy Matron, officers, and members.
