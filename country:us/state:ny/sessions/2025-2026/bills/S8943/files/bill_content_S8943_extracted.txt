Section 837-m of the executive law, as amended by chapter 255 of 21 the laws of 2014, is amended to read as follows: 22 § 837-m. Reporting duties of law enforcement departments with respect 23 to missing women and children and vulnerable adults. The chief of every 24 police department, each county sheriff and the superintendent of state 25 police shall report, at least semi-annually, to the division with 26 respect to specified cases of missing women and children that are 27 closed. Such reports shall be in the form and manner prescribed by the 28 division and shall contain such information as the division deems neces- 29 sary including, but not limited to, information regarding recovered 30 women and children who were arrested, women and children who were the 31 victims of criminal activity or exploitation and women and children who 32 were found deceased and information regarding the alleged abductor or 33 killer of such women and children. Any law enforcement department 34 referred to in this section may, in its discretion, include in such 35 semi-annual reports information relating to missing vulnerable adults as 36 such term is defined in section eight hundred thirty-seven-f-one of this 37 article. 38 § 4.
