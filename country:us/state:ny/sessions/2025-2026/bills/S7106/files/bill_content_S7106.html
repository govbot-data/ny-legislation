Add §137, Gen Muni L; add §10-124, NYC Ad Cd
 
Prohibits the placement of fifth generation (5G) telecommunications towers within 250 feet of a business or residence in cities with a population of one million or more without the owner's consent; requires community board approval and the completion of a city environmental quality review before the placement of any 5G tower can be approved.

        AN ACT to amend the general municipal law and the administrative code of
          the city of New York, in relation to the regulation  of  fifth  gener-
          ation  (5G)  telecommunications  towers in cities with a population of
          one million or more
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The  general  municipal  law  is  amended by adding a new
     2  section 137 to read as follows:
     3    § 137. Regulation of fifth generation (5G)  telecommunications  towers
     4  in  cities  with a population of one million or more. 1. For purposes of
     5  this section, "fifth generation (5G) telecommunications tower" means any
     6  freestanding tower, monopole or similar structure used for the provision
     7  of fifth generation wireless services including  ancillary  telecommuni-
     8  cations  equipment required to integrate such tower, monopole or similar
     9  structure into an existing or proposed wireless network.
    10    2. In cities with a population of one million or  more,  it  shall  be
    11  prohibited  for any 5G telecommunications tower to be located within two
    12  hundred fifty feet of a business or personal residence,  unless  written
    13  consent  is provided by the owner of such business or personal residence
    14  permitting the placement of such tower.
    15    3. In cities with a population of one million or more, no 5G  telecom-
    16  munications tower may be placed without the approval of the local commu-
    17  nity board. In addition to such requirement, before the placement of any
    18  5G telecommunications tower may be approved, a city environmental quali-
    19  ty review shall be conducted to determine if the placement of such tower
    20  will  have  any  deteriorating impact on the environment. The results of
    21  such review shall be provided to the applicable  local  community  board
    22  once completed.
