        AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation  to  restricting  the
          sale of tobacco products; and to repeal certain provisions of such law
          relating thereto
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 1399-bb of the public  health  law
     2  is REPEALED.
     3    § 2. Subdivisions 4 and 5 of section 1399-bb of the public health law,
     4  as  amended  by  section 4 of part EE of chapter 56 of the laws of 2020,
     5  are amended to read as follows:
     6    4. No retail dealer engaged in the business of  selling  or  otherwise
     7  distributing electronic cigarettes or vapor products intended or reason-
     8  ably  expected  to  be  used with or for the consumption of nicotine for
     9  commercial purposes, or any agent or  employee  of  such  person,  shall
    10  knowingly,  in  furtherance  of such business, distribute without charge
    11  any electronic cigarettes to any individual [under twenty-one  years  of
    12  age] born after December thirty-first, two thousand seven.
    13    5.  The distribution of tobacco products, electronic cigarettes, vapor
    14  products intended or reasonably expected to be  used  with  or  for  the
    15  consumption  of  nicotine,  or herbal cigarettes pursuant to subdivision
    16  two of this section or the distribution  without  charge  of  electronic

    25  ment  of  any other country, or (c) an identification card issued by the
    26  armed forces of the United States, indicating that the individual [is at
    27  least twenty-one years of age. Such identification need not be  required
