The county law is amended by adding a new section 680 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 680. Reimbursement. The county with jurisdiction over a decedent may 4 seek reimbursement for services rendered and fees generated by the 5 coroner, coroner's physician or medical examiner from the county in 6 which the decedent resided. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
