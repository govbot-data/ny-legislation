and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the 2025 Brooklyn Borough Debate Championships, to be held on Saturday, March 15, 2025; and WHEREAS, Organized by the American Debate League (ADL), this prestigious tournament will bring together nearly 300 elementary and middle school students and their families from Brooklyn school districts 14, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, and 32, fostering an environment where young scholars can develop essential skills in debate, argumentation, and public speaking; and WHEREAS, The Brooklyn Borough Debate Championships serves as a qualifier for the 2025 New York State Debate Championships, which will be held on Saturday, May 17, 2025, giving students the opportunity to showcase their talent and intellectual rigor on an even larger stage; and WHEREAS, The American Debate League (ADL), founded in 2014, has made a significant impact in the New York City, Long Island, Westchester, Rockland, New Jersey, and Connecticut school communities, helping to establish debate teams in over 200 schools and offering students the opportunity to compete at prestigious tournaments such as those held at Harvard and Princeton; and WHEREAS, The art of debate fosters confidence, analytical thinking, and civic engagement, equipping students with the tools to become future leaders, advocates, and informed citizens; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body recognize and commend the American Debate League, the participating students, educators, and families, and all those involved in making the 2025 Brooklyn Borough Debate Championships a success; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the 2025 Brooklyn Borough Debate Championships for its commitment to fostering academic excellence, student leadership, and the power of discourse; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the American Debate League in recognition of its outstanding contributions to youth education and civic engagement.
