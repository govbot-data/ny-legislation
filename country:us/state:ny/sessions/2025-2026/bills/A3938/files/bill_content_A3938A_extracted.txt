AN ACT to amend the highway law and the vehicle and traffic law, in
relation to enacting "Alexander John Smullen Traffic Safety Memorial
Law"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "Alexander John Smullen Traffic Safety Memorial Law".
3 § 2. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 24 to read as
4 follows:
5 § 24. Memorial sign program for collision victims. 1. The commissioner
6 shall establish and administer a memorial sign program to publicly memo-
7 rialize the victims of fatal vehicle collisions.
8 2. As used in this section, "victim" means a person killed in a high-
9 way collision.
10 3. A sign designed and posted under this section shall include:
11 (a) the phrase "Please Don't Drink and Drive", "Please Don't Drive
12 Impaired", "Please Don't Text and Drive", "Please Don't Speed", "Please
13 Buckle Up", "Please Drive Sober", "Please Drive Safely", "Please Ride
14 Safely", "Please Watch for Bicyclists", "Please Watch for Motorcycles",
15 "Please Watch for Pedestrians", "Reckless Driving Costs Lives", "Seat
16 Belts Save Lives", "Slow Down for Workers", or "Stay Alert Drive Safe";

1 (b) the phrase "In Memory Of" and the name of one or more victims in
2 accordance with the rules promulgated by the department; and
3 (c) the date of the collision that resulted in the victim's death.
4 4. The sign may include the names of more than one victim so long as
5 the total length of the names does not exceed one line of text.
6 5. A person may request that a sign be posted under this section by:
7 (a) making an application to the department on a form prescribed by
8 the department; and
9 (b) submitting a fee to the department, in an amount reasonably deter-
10 mined by the department, to help defray the costs of posting the memori-
11 al sign, provided that such fee shall not be excessive or prohibitive.
12 6. If the application meets the department's requirements and the
13 applicant pays the memorial sign fee, the department shall erect a sign.
14 A sign posted under this section may remain posted for two years. At the
15 end of the two-year period the department may release the sign to the
16 applicant. The department is not required to release a sign that has
17 been damaged.
18 7. A sign posted under this section that is damaged shall be removed
19 by the department. Except as provided in subdivision eight of this
20 section, the department may post a new sign if it has been less than two
21 years from the posting of the original sign and a person:
22 (a) submits a written request to the department to replace the sign;
23 and
24 (b) submits a replacement fee in the amount provided under paragraph
25 (b) of subdivision five of this section.
26 8. During the two-year posting period the department shall replace a
27 sign posted under this section that is damaged because of the depart-
28 ment's negligence.
29 9. The commissioner shall promulgate such rules and regulations as may
30 be necessary to effectuate the provisions of this section.
31 10. All signs posted pursuant to this section shall comply with all
32 applicable federal law and regulations.
