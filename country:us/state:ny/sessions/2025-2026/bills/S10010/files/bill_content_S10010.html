        AN ACT to amend the facilities development corporation act, in  relation
          to  the  transfer  and  conveyance  of certain property to the town of
          Goshen
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section  1.  Paragraph (a) of subdivision 13 of section 5 of section 1
     2  of chapter 359 of the laws of 1968, constituting the facilities develop-
     3  ment corporation act, as separately amended by chapters 479 and  551  of
     4  the laws of 2022, is amended and a new paragraph (c) is added to read as
     5  follows:
     6    (a)  Subject  to the terms and conditions of any lease, sublease, loan
     7  or other financing agreement with the state housing  finance  agency  or
     8  the  state  medical  care facilities finance agency, and to the determi-
     9  nation of the appropriate commissioner of the  department,  and  in  the
    10  case  of  community mental health and developmental disabilities facili-
    11  ties, of the city or county,  that  such  real  property  held  for  the
    12  purposes  of a mental hygiene facilities improvement program is unneces-
    13  sary for the present or foreseeable future needs  of  a  mental  hygiene
    14  facility, with the approval of the director of the budget, to convey for
    15  fair  value,  except as otherwise provided in [paragraph] paragraphs (b)
    16  and (c) of this subdivision, any right, title or interest of the  people
    17  of the state of New York in and to such real property to any appropriate
    18  state agency, or public corporation, city or county for other public use
    19  or  for  sale,  lease  or other disposition in accordance with law, real
    20  property held by the corporation, provided,  however,  nothing  in  this
    21  subdivision shall be deemed to supersede the provisions of section 41.34
    22  of  the mental hygiene law and provided further that any such conveyance
    23  shall be subject to, and consistent with the terms  and  objectives  of,
    24  any  plan  developed  by the state interagency council on mental hygiene
    25  property utilization. The corporation shall provide  written  notice  at
    26  least  thirty days in advance of the effective date of any conveyance to
    27  the governor, the majority leader of the senate and the speaker  of  the

     1  assembly.  No  conveyance  as  authorized  in  this subdivision that may

     7  and federal  laws,  rules  and  regulations,  and  with  deeds,  leases,
     8  subleases,  loan  agreements, financing agreements, and bond resolutions
     9  relating to or affected by the conveyance, and that the conveyance  does

    11  the state housing finance agency or the  New  York  state  medical  care
    12  facilities   finance   agency   to  finance  or  refinance  the  design,
    13  construction, acquisition, reconstruction, rehabilitation or improvement
    14  of mental health service facilities as defined in  the  New  York  state
    15  medical care facilities finance agency act.
    16    (c)  Notwithstanding  any  provision  of  law to the contrary, for the
    17  purposes of transferring and conveying certain property to the  Town  of
    18  Goshen  that  is  located  on  the  office of mental health's Mid-Hudson
    19  Forensic Psychiatric Center campus, constituting the existing wastewater
    20  treatment plant, including but not limited to, all structures and equip-
    21  ment, buried infrastructure, as  well  as  spare  parts  and  consumable
    22  supplies  utilized  in  the  sewer  treatment process and located at the
    23  plant, together with the nineteen (19) acres of land on which the waste-
    24  water treatment plant is situated, a subdivided portion  of  2834  State
    25  Route  17M in the Town of Goshen, Orange County, New York and identified
    26  with a Tax Parcel No. to be assigned, for the purposes described herein,
    27  the term "fair value" shall be deemed to be a nominal amount  as  deter-
    28  mined  by  the  commissioner of the office of mental health to be in the
    29  public interest, in order to facilitate  the  above-referenced  transfer
    30  for public benefit and the continuation of wastewater treatment services
    31  to the office of mental health's Mid-Hudson Forensic Psychiatric Center.
    32  Any  conveyance  pursuant to this paragraph shall be consistent with and
    33  contingent upon any such terms and conditions imposed by the commission-
    34  er of the office of mental health including, but not limited  to,  those
    35  terms  and  conditions  such  commissioner  deems  proper  to ensure the
    36  continual operation and maintenance of the  wastewater  treatment  plant
    37  and provision of services to the office of mental health at a reasonable
    38  cost,  as  memorialized in a separate written agreement. The description
    39  of the lands to be transferred and conveyed as set forth herein  is  not
    40  intended to be a legal description but intended to identify the property
    41  subject to this paragraph.
