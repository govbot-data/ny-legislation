BARRETT -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to requiring the attorney general to provide certain information to charitable organizations upon registration The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 172 of executive law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 11 to read as follows: 3 11. The attorney general shall develop guidance and educational mate- 4 rials that shall, to the extent practicable, be provided to charitable 5 organizations upon their registration with the attorney general. Such 6 guidance and educational materials shall, at a minimum, contain the 7 following: 8 (a) information on continued registration and financial reporting 9 obligations under this article; and 10 (b) information on any additional registration requirements that must 11 be met for a charitable organization to receive financial assistance or 12 any funds from the state. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
