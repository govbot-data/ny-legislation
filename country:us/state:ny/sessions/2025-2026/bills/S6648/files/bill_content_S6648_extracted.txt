SANDERS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation to enacting the COVID-19 essential workers appreciation act The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "COVID-19 essential workers appreciation act". 3 § 2. COVID-19 essential workers appreciation awards. (a) Notwithstand- 4 ing any provision of law to the contrary, all businesses with profits 5 over $500,000 dollars that do business in the state that during the 6 state disaster emergency declared pursuant to executive order 202 have 7 seen an increase in profits by 20% over the same time period in 2019 8 shall deposit 5% of such business' gross profits into the COVID-19 9 essential workers appreciation fund established pursuant to section 10 99-ss of the state finance law. 11 (b) There is hereby established the COVID-19 essential workers appre- 12 ciation task force. Such task force shall be comprised of 7 members to 13 be appointed as follows: 14 (i) 2 members appointed by the temporary president of the senate; 15 (ii) 2 members appointed by the speaker of the assembly; 16 (iii) 1 member appointed by the governor; 17 (iv) 1 member appointed by the governor who is the representative of a 18 small business organization; and 19 (v) 1 member appointed by the governor who is the representative of a 20 retail union. 21 (c) The COVID-19 essential workers appreciation task force established 22 pursuant to subdivision (b) of this section shall develop eligibility 23 requirements and make awards utilizing the monies in the COVID-19 essen- 24 tial workers appreciation fund. Such awards shall be made to: EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
