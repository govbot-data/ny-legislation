S06648 Summary:

BILL NOS06648
 
SAME ASNo Same As

COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add §99-ss, St Fin L
 
Enacts the COVID-19 essential workers appreciation act which requires businesses who have increased their profits due to the outbreak of COVID-19 to deposit five percent of gross profits into a fund to be used by the COVID-19 essential workers appreciation task force to make awards to essential employees and families of essential employees who have died as a result of contracting COVID-19.

    18  small business organization; and
    19    (v)  1 member appointed by the governor who is the representative of a
    20  retail union.

    22  pursuant to subdivision (b) of this section  shall  develop  eligibility
    23  requirements and make awards utilizing the monies in the COVID-19 essen-
    24  tial workers appreciation fund. Such awards shall be made to:
