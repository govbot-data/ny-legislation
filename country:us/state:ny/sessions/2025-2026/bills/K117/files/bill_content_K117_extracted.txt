Ketcham High School; and WHEREAS, The Town of Wappinger is also home to the Hudson Valley Regional Airport, formerly known as New Hackensack Field, a historic site which has played a significant role in the region's development; established during World War II, the airport has served as a hub for both military and civilian aviation, contributing to the growth of the community and the Greater Hudson Valley; today, the airport continues to be a vital resource for both business and leisure travelers, enhancing Wappinger's connectivity and economic vitality; and WHEREAS, Rooted in history and thriving through the changes and challenges over the years, the Town of Wappinger continues to exemplify an exceptional quality of life for its residents, fostering a vibrant environment for both work and recreation, while promoting strong community connections; and WHEREAS, The residents of Wappinger remain hopeful and optimistic about the future of their community, which continues to be a picturesque and dynamic place to call home, where families can flourish and grow; and WHEREAS, Honoring the Town of Wappinger for its rich past and lasting impact on the State of New York, this Legislative Body is proud to celebrate the 150th Anniversary of this resilient and thriving town; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 150th Anniversary of the Town of Wappinger, New York, recognizing the significance of the role it continues to play in the life of the community of the State of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Town of Wappinger, New York.
