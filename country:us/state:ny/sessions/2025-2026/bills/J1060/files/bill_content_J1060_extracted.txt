and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to commemorate the Annual Temple Ball Weekend hosted by Elejmal Temple No. 185, Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine of North and South America and Its Jurisdictions, Inc., to be held July 18-20, 2025; and WHEREAS, This weekend celebration is a cherished tradition within the Shrinedom, symbolizing the Temple's steadfast dedication to brotherhood, philanthropy, and outstanding community service; and WHEREAS, The 2025 Temple Ball Weekend will be held in tribute to Illustrious Potentate Jonathan Greene, a distinguished and visionary leader whose service, integrity, and dedication have strengthened the mission and reach of Elejmal Temple No. 185; and WHEREAS, Illustrious Potentate Greene has exemplified the highest tenets of Prince Hall Shrinedom, guiding the Temple with purpose, expanding its community impact, and inspiring Nobles to uphold the noble traditions of compassion, discipline, and unity; and WHEREAS, Under his stewardship, Elejmal Temple No. 185 has continued to flourish in civic engagement and philanthropic outreach, standing as a pillar of strength within the Desert of New York and the greater community it serves; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body commends both Elejmal Temple No. 185 and Illustrious Potentate Greene for their exceptional leadership and longstanding commitment to uplifting communities through service, brotherhood, and cultural pride; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the Elejmal Temple No. 185 Annual Temple Ball Weekend, to be held July 18-20, 2025, and to honor Illustrious Potentate Jonathan Greene for his exemplary leadership and continued service to the people of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Elejmal Temple No. 185 and Illustrious Potentate Jonathan Greene in recognition of this meaningful occasion.
