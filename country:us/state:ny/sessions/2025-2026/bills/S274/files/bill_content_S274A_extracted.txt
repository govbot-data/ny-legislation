AN ACT to amend the veterans' services law, in relation to establishing
the "Vets to Vollies" program
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The veterans' services law is amended by adding a new
2 section 29-e to read as follows:
3 § 29-e. Vets to vollies. 1. The department shall create a statewide
4 vets to vollies program for counties that wish to participate in such
5 program. The purpose of such program is to encourage veterans or
6 discharged military personnel to join their local fire department or
7 emergency medical service agency.
8 2. Such program shall collaborate and coordinate with all applicable
9 county agencies to conduct extensive outreach programs within the local
10 community, including but not limited to, active military installations
11 and local veteran service organizations. Such outreach programs shall
12 include information on the many benefits and resources available to
13 prospective volunteer first responders on a local and statewide level
14 and how to further become involved.
15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
