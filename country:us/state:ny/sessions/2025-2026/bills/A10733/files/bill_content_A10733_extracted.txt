Subject to the provisions of this act but notwithstanding 2 any other provision of law to the contrary, the commissioner of general 3 services, upon the consent of the commissioner of mental health, is 4 hereby authorized to transfer and convey to the Richardson Center Corpo- 5 ration the state land described in section two of this act for fair- 6 market value, said property being a portion of the former Buffalo 7 Psychiatric Center. The description in section two of this act of the lands to be 22 conveyed is not intended to be a legal description, but is intended only 23 to identify the premises to be conveyed. As a condition of purchase, the 24 Richardson Center Corporation shall submit to the commissioner of gener- 25 al services for such commissioner's approval an accurate survey and 26 description of the lands generally described in section two of this act, 27 which may be used in the conveyance thereof. 28 § 4. The commissioner of general services shall not transfer or convey 29 the land described in section two of this act unless application is made 30 therefore to the office of general services by the Richardson Center 31 Corporation within one year after the effective date of this act. 32 § 5. Any lands transferred pursuant to this act shall be used exclu- 33 sively for the purposes of the Richardson Center Corporation to 34 construct a mixed-use campus for the community benefit, including 35 affordable housing use, and upon termination of such use, title to the 36 lands so transferred shall revert to the state of New York. The forgoing 37 sentence shall not negatively impact a certain right of reversion of 38 such lands that runs to the benefit of the city of Buffalo. 39 § 6. This act shall take effect immediately.
