Paragraph (a) of subdivision 3 of section 400.00 of the 2 penal law, as amended by chapter 212 of the laws of 2022, is amended to 3 read as follows: 4 (a) Applications shall be made and renewed, in the case of a license 5 to carry or possess a pistol or revolver or to purchase or take 6 possession of a semiautomatic rifle, to the licensing officer in the 7 city or county, as the case may be, where the applicant resides, is 8 principally employed or has [his or her] a principal place of business 9 as merchant or storekeeper; and, in the case of a license as gunsmith or 10 dealer in firearms, to the licensing officer where such place of busi- 11 ness is located.
