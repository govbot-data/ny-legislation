COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to prohibiting electric corporations and gas corporations from increasing charges to certain ratepayers due to the building or operation of a data center or semiconductor fabrication plant The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 66 of the public service law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 33 to read as follows: 3 33. Prohibit electric corporations, gas corporations, and utilities 4 from passing along costs or increasing charges to ratepayers for elec- 5 tricity and natural gas, or any associated goods and services, as a 6 result of increased costs incurred by the electric corporation, gas 7 corporation, or utility due to the costs of capital expenditures or 8 maintenance of infrastructure resulting from the building or operation 9 of a data center or semiconductor fabrication plant; provided, however, 10 that such costs or increased charges shall be charged to ratepayers 11 involved with or associated with the building or operation of such data 12 center or semiconductor fabrication plant. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth day after it shall 14 have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amendment 15 and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implementation 16 of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and 17 completed on or before such effective date.
