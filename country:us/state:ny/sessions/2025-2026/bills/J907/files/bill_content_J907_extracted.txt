and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Captain Thomas Breslin upon the occasion of his designation as Junior Firefighter of the Year by the Mineola Junior Fire Department, to be celebrated at the 21st Mineola Junior Fire Department Installation Dinner Dance on Saturday, June 21, 2025, at the Mineola Fire Department Headquarters; and WHEREAS, The Mineola Junior Fire Department is a prestigious program that will proudly celebrate its 50th anniversary as an Explorer program in 2026; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, many young men and women from Mineola, New York, and its neighboring communities have participated in the program, and many have gone on to join their local fire department; and WHEREAS, Since joining the Mineola Junior Fire Department in October of 2023, Thomas Breslin quickly distinguished himself through excellence, leadership, and service; in November of 2024, Thomas Breslin's excellence and potential was recognized when he was elected Lieutenant; and WHEREAS, In April of 2025, a mere 18 months from joining the department, Thomas Breslin rose to the role of captain, which is the highest role attainable in the Junior Firefighters program; and WHEREAS, In his esteemed capacity as captain, Thomas Breslin organizes internal committees, oversees turnout gear logistics, and promotes the Juniors' presence at public events throughout the Village and County; and WHEREAS, Thomas Breslin has shown exceptional promise and dedication, and aspires to continue serving the Village of Mineola as a firefighter, a student, and in future public service; and WHEREAS, The leadership and passion that Thomas Breslin has demonstrated have contributed to the continued success of the Mineola Junior Fire Department, and his unwavering commitment will ensure the Junior Fire Department remains a lasting opportunity for local youth; and WHEREAS, A young man of singular distinction, Thomas Breslin has distinguished himself as an individual of superlative ability, establishing a high standard of excellence and purposefulness of life rarely achieved in youthful years; and WHEREAS, It takes a special dedication, a strong desire to help others, and a tireless sense of community to forsake precious time with family and friends to respond to the signal that a neighbor is in need; Thomas Breslin is such an individual; and WHEREAS, Poised eagerly with enthusiasm and determined purpose, Thomas Breslin now stands honorably, with just pride in his accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the caring counsel and support provided by senior firefighters, mentors, and his family; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Captain Thomas Breslin upon the occasion of his designation as Junior Firefighter of the Year by the Mineola Junior Fire Department; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Captain Thomas Breslin.
