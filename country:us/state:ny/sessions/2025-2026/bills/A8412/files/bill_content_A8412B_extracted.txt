AN ACT to amend the public housing law, in relation to succession rights
for New York city housing authority properties
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The public housing law is amended by adding a new section
2 402-h to read as follows:
3 § 402-h. Succession rights. 1. The New York city housing authority
4 includes, and shall continue to include, policies and procedures allow-
5 ing individuals to claim succession rights to public housing units in
6 properties owned and operated by the New York city housing authority
7 upon the permanent departure or death of the tenant of record from the
8 unit.
9 2. The New York city housing authority shall establish written poli-
10 cies and procedures for asserting succession rights consistent with the
11 requirements of applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations,
12 and guidance, as they may change from time to time. Such written poli-
13 cies and procedures shall also be consistent with the New York city
14 housing authority's occupancy standards. Such policies and procedures
15 shall include, but not be limited to:
16 (a) eligibility requirements for lease succession;
17 (b) relocation requirements, if any, arising out of lease succession;
18 (c) the requirement to pay use and occupancy during the pendency of
19 succession rights proceedings; and
20 (d) the right to an administrative grievance of the New York city
21 housing authority's denial of succession rights, provided that the
22 grievant is up to date in use and occupancy.
