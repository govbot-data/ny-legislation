Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01510 Summary:BILL NOJ01510  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORGALLIVAN COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01510 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1510 BY: Senator GALLIVAN HONORING Hayley Brown, Taylor Prost, and Brittany Newman of Signature Hair Salon upon the occasion of their designation as Emerging Entrepreneurs by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated on March 19, 2026 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge and commend those individuals whose entrepreneurial spirit, initiative, and commitment to excellence strengthen the economic and social fabric of the State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Hayley Brown, Taylor Prost, and Brittany Newman of Signature Hair Salon upon the occasion of their designation as Emerging Entrepreneurs by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated at its 53rd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 19, 2026, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, In October of 2024, Hayley Brown, Taylor Prost, and Brittany Newman proudly opened the doors of Signature Hair Salon, transforming their shared vision into a thriving business built upon professionalism, creativity, and individualized client care; and WHEREAS, With more than 30 years of combined professional experience in the beauty industry, these three entrepreneurs brought both expertise and passion to the establishment of Signature Hair Salon, creating a space that reflects beauty, individuality, and excellence; and WHEREAS, Signature Hair Salon was founded on the belief that no two clients are the same, and that each individual deserves a personalized and signature experience in an environment that is welcoming, warm, and attentive to detail; and WHEREAS, Through their dedication, artistry, and commitment to listening and understanding their clients, Hayley Brown, Taylor Prost, and Brittany Newman have cultivated a salon culture in which clients feel valued, heard, and respected, contributing to strong community relationships and customer loyalty; and WHEREAS, Their entrepreneurial journey reflects gratitude and appreciation for the support of spouses, families, friends, clients, and the greater community, whose encouragement has been instrumental in the growth and success of Signature Hair Salon; and WHEREAS, The Clarence Chamber of Commerce's designation of Hayley Brown, Taylor Prost, and Brittany Newman as Emerging Entrepreneurs recognizes not only their business success, but also their leadership, vision, and positive impact on the local economy; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Hayley Brown, Taylor Prost, and Brittany Newman of Signature Hair Salon upon the occasion of their designation as Emerging Entrepreneurs by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 19, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Hayley Brown, Taylor Prost, and Brittany Newman, Signature Hair Salon.
