GONZALEZ-ROJAS -- read once and referred to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to companion animals The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new article 243 to 2 read as follows: 3 ARTICLE 243 4 COMPANION ANIMALS 5 Section 243.00 Companion animals are sentient beings. 6 § 243.00 Companion animals are sentient beings. 7 1. Companion animals, including dogs, cats, and such other animals as 8 defined in section three hundred fifty of the agriculture and markets 9 law, are sentient beings capable of experiencing pain, stress, and fear, 10 as well as love and affection, and are entitled to respect and consider- 11 ation, protection from abuse, neglect, and cruelty, and the maximization 12 of their welfare. For that reason, such companion animals shall be 13 considered victims of animal cruelty crimes, including but not limited 14 to all offenses as defined in article twenty-six of the agriculture and 15 markets law, and shall be treated as such in a court of law. 16 2. Nothing in this section shall be construed to reduce any 17 protections afforded to any animal under any other provision of law, 18 rule or regulation or article twenty-six of the agriculture and markets 19 law. 20 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
