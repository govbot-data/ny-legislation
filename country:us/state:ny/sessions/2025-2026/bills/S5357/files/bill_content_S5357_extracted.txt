The social services law is amended by adding a new section 2 43-a to read as follows: 3 § 43-a. Contracting with hotels to provide rooms as shelter for home- 4 less persons; payment standards. 1. In a city with a population of more 5 than one million, notwithstanding any provision of any federal law, rule 6 or regulation to the contrary, whenever the commissioner, social 7 services agency, or a contracted entity subject to the provisions of 8 section forty-three of this article enters into contracts with hotels to 9 provide rooms as shelter for homeless persons, the daily maximum amount 10 the department shall pay under a contract to provide rooms for homeless 11 persons shall be equal to seventy-five percent of the New York city 12 housing authority Section 8 voucher payment standard for a studio apart- 13 ment pro-rated daily. 14 2.
