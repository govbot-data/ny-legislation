Sections 1 through 32, 37 through 43, 45 through 56 of 2 chapter 1 of the laws of 2013, amending the criminal procedure law and 3 other laws relating to suspension and revocation of firearms licenses, 4 are REPEALED. 5 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall be deemed to 6 have been in full force and effect on and after January 15, 2013.
