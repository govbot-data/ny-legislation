FERNANDEZ -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Procurement and Contracts AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to including middle east- ern and north African persons in the definition of "minority group member" for purposes of participation by minority group members and women with respect to state contracts The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraphs (c) and (d) of subdivision 8 of section 310 of 2 the executive law, as added by chapter 261 of the laws of 1988, are 3 amended and a new paragraph (e) is added to read as follows: 4 (c) Native American or Alaskan native persons having origins in any of 5 the original peoples of North America[.]; 6 (d) Asian and Pacific Islander persons having origins in any of the 7 Far East countries, South East Asia, the Indian subcontinent or the 8 Pacific Islands[.]; 9 (e) Middle Eastern and North African persons. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that 11 the amendments to subdivision 8 of section 310 of the executive law made 12 by section one of this act shall not affect the repeal of such section 13 and shall be deemed repealed therewith.
