        Introduced  by  Sen. MATTERA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when

        AN ACT authorizing the county of Suffolk to alienate certain lands  used
          as parklands and to dedicate certain other lands as parklands
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subject to the  provisions  of  this  act,  the  county  of
     2  Suffolk,  acting  by  and through its governing body and upon such terms
     3  and conditions as determined by  such  board  is  hereby  authorized  to
     4  discontinue  as  parklands  and  alienate the lands described in section
     5  three of this act to Russel and Deana Galindo for the purpose  of  resi-
     6  dential improvements.
     7    §  2.  The  authorization provided in section one of this act shall be
     8  effective only upon the condition that Russel and Deana  Galindo  convey
     9  to  the  county  of  Suffolk, and that the county of Suffolk acquire and
    10  dedicate the lands described in section four of this act  as  parklands,
    11  provided that the county of Suffolk has never used such lands for public
    12  open space or park purposes.
    13    §  3. The parklands authorized by section one of this act to be alien-
    14  ated are described as follows:  All that certain plot, piece  or  parcel
    15  of  land,  with the buildings and improvements thereon erected, situate,
    16  lying and being in Smithtown, town of Smithtown, county of  Suffolk  and
    17  the  state  of  New  York  situated  being  more  particular bounded and
    18  described as follows:
    19  BEGINNING at a point of being south 80 degrees  13  minutes  30  seconds
    20  west 75.71 feet from the south easterly corner of map entitled "prestige
    21  estates  at  smithtown"  filed  in  the Suffolk county clerk's office on
    22  September 9, 2005 under map no. 11304
    23  THENCE running south 09 degrees 46 minutes 04 seconds east 56.91 feet.
    24  THENCE running south 79 degrees 36 minutes 19 seconds west 227.94 feet.
    25  THENCE running north 27 degrees 33 minutes27 seconds west 14.99 feet.

    30    §  5.  In  the  event  that  the  fair  market  value of the parklands
    31  described in section four of this act to be dedicated by the  county  of
    32  Suffolk  pursuant  to this act are not equal to or greater than the fair
    33  market value of the parklands to be alienated as  described  in  section
    34  three  of  this act, the county of Suffolk shall dedicate the difference
    35  of the fair market value of the lands to be alienated and the  lands  to
    36  be  dedicated for the acquisition of additional parklands and/or capital
    37  improvements to existing park and recreational facilities.
    38    § 6. In the event that the county  of  Suffolk  received  any  funding
    39  support  or  assistance  from  the  federal government for the purchase,
    40  maintenance or improvement of the parklands set forth in  section  three
    41  of  this act, the discontinuance and alienation of such parkland author-
    42  ized by the provisions of this act shall not occur until Suffolk  county
    43  has  complied with any federal requirements pertaining to the alienation
    44  or conversion of parklands, including satisfying the  secretary  of  the
    45  interior  that the alienation or conversion complies with all conditions
    46  which the secretary of  the  interior  deems  necessary  to  assure  the
    47  substitution of other lands shall be equivalent in fair market value and
    48  usefulness to the lands being alienated or converted.
    49    § 7. This act shall take effect immediately.
