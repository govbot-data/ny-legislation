JACKSON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Agriculture AN ACT to amend the agriculture and markets law, in relation to provid- ing fresh produce to food deserts in the state The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 16 of the agriculture and markets law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 53 to read as follows: 3 53. (a) Establish a program in the state to purchase fresh produce 4 from farms located in the state utilizing all monies appropriated to 5 such program and distribute such fresh produce in areas of the state 6 determined by the commissioner to be food deserts. The commissioner 7 shall promulgate for such program: 8 (i) eligibility and application criteria for individuals in areas in 9 the state designated as food deserts to receive fresh produce pursuant 10 to the program; 11 (ii) eligibility and application criteria for farms in the state to 12 register as vendors to expedite the acquisition and distribution of 13 fresh produce from such farms; 14 (iii) criteria that promotes opportunities for market participation by 15 farmers from under-represented communities, including but not limited to 16 farmers who are black, indigenous, or from communities of color. The 17 program shall promote a consistent demand of products and services from 18 under-represented farmers, including opportunities for farmers who are 19 black, indigenous, or from communities of color to re-establish farm 20 ownership. 21 (b) As used in this subdivision "food desert" shall have the same 22 meaning as defined by the United States department of agriculture.
