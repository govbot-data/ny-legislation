RAMOS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to metropolitan transportation authority contracts involving industrial painting and industrial coatings The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1265-c to read as follows: 3 § 1265-c. Contracts involving industrial painting and industrial coat- 4 ings. 1. Any authority contract for the application of industrial paint- 5 ing or industrial coating shall include provisions regarding contractor, 6 subcontractor and worker certification as follows: 7 a. Contractors and subcontractors doing work involving industrial 8 painting and industrial coating shall certify that all work completed 9 meets the minimum standards established under this section by the comp- 10 troller regarding industrial painting and industrial coating. 11 b. Contractors and subcontractors doing work involving industrial 12 painting and industrial coating that require the disturbance of lead- 13 based paint or other hazardous materials shall certify that all work 14 conducted on the project meets the minimum standards established under 15 this section by the comptroller. 16 2. Any certifications required by this section shall be kept current 17 for the duration of the contract. 18 3. The comptroller, in coordination with the office of general 19 services, shall establish guidelines concerning the procurement of 20 industrial paints and industrial coatings as well as the qualifications 21 required for the application of industrial paints and industrial coat- 22 ings by contractors and subcontractors pursuant to section twenty-eight 23 hundred seventy-nine-a of this chapter. Such guidelines shall implement 24 a certification process with minimum standards for contractors and EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after 13 it shall have become a law and shall apply to any metropolitan transpor- 14 tation authority contracting opportunities advertised on or after such 15 date and shall exclude contracts for which an invitation for bid, 16 request for proposal, or similar solicitation has been issued prior to 17 the effective date of this act.
