        Introduced  by M. of A. SIMPSON -- read once and referred to the Commit-

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic  law,  in  relation  to  waiving
          metered  parking  fees  for  certain vehicles displaying a handicapped
          permit or with handicapped plates
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section  1. Section 1203-h of the vehicle and traffic law, as added by
     2  chapter 243 of the laws of 2007, is amended to read as follows:
     3    § 1203-h. Metered parking [waiver] waived for certain disabilities. 1.
     4  [The commissioner shall distribute metered parking waiver permits to the
     5  governing bodies of all cities, villages and towns;  provided,  however,
     6  that the commissioner shall not distribute such permits to cities having
     7  a  population  of  one million or more which issue special vehicle iden-
     8  tification parking permits pursuant to paragraph fifteen of  subdivision
     9  a  of  section  twenty-nine  hundred  three of the New York city charter
    10  which waive the payment of metered parking  fees.]  Notwithstanding  any
    11  local law or ordinance to the contrary, [such] parking permits for hand-
    12  icapped  persons  issued  by  a  municipality  of  the state pursuant to
    13  section twelve hundred three-a of this article and plates  issued  to  a
    14  severely disabled person under section four hundred four-a of this chap-
    15  ter  shall  entitle any vehicle displaying such [waiver] permit or plate
    16  to park at any metered parking space in any city, village or town of the
    17  state and such permits and  plates  shall  waive  metered  parking  fees
    18  imposed  by  such city or village pursuant to paragraph nine of subdivi-
    19  sion (a) of section sixteen hundred forty of this chapter or imposed  by
    20  such  town  pursuant  to  paragraph  nine  of subdivision (a) of section
    21  sixteen hundred sixty of this chapter.   Provided,  however,  that  such
    22  permit  or  plate  shall  not  waive any time limit at a metered parking
    23  space imposed pursuant to paragraph nine of subdivision (a)  of  section
    24  sixteen  hundred  forty of this chapter or paragraph nine of subdivision
    25  (a) of section sixteen hundred sixty of this chapter that is  applicable
    26  to  a  motor  vehicle parking in such metered parking space without such
    27  permit or plate.
