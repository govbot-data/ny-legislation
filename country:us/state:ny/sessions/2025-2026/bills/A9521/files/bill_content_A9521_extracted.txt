Section 387 of the judiciary law, as amended by chapter 15 2 of the laws of 1975, is amended to read as follows: 3 § 387. Temporary appointment of interpreters. If the services of an 4 interpreter be required in any court and there be no unemployed official 5 interpreter to act therein, the court may appoint an interpreter to act 6 temporarily in such court. Such interpreter shall before entering upon 7 [his] such interpreter's duties file with the clerk of the court the 8 constitutional oath of office.
