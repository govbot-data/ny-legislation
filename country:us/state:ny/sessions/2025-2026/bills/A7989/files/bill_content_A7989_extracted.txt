GRIFFIN -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Governmental Employees AN ACT to amend the civil service law, in relation to providing for board of cooperative educational services employees who have been provisionally appointed to a position for at least nine months without an exam being offered for such position to receive a permanent appointment in such position The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 65 of the civil service law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 5 to read as follows: 3 5. Provisional appointments within boards of cooperative educational 4 services. Notwithstanding any inconsistent provision of this chapter or 5 any other law or rule to the contrary, where a board of cooperative 6 educational services has made a provisional appointment to a position 7 and no civil service examination for such position has been conducted 8 within nine months of such appointment, the provisional appointment 9 shall not expire under subdivision two of this section and instead the 10 provisional appointee shall be considered to have been appointed 11 consistent with section sixty-one of this title. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
