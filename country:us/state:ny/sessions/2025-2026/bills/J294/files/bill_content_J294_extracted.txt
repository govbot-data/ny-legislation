and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Greater New York Chapter of The Links, Incorporated upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Spring Luncheon, to be held on Saturday, April 12, 2025, at Chelsea Piers; and WHEREAS, Established in 1946 as the first Links chapter in the State of New York, the Greater New York Chapter of The Links, Inc. has consistently demonstrated an unwavering commitment to service, excellence, and the advancement of social change through advocacy, philanthropy, and community engagement; and WHEREAS, The Annual Spring Luncheon stands as a hallmark event celebrating a legacy of empowerment, culture, and fellowship, while raising critical funds to sustain and expand the Chapter's transformative programs; and WHEREAS, This year's luncheon will further strengthen The Links' commitment to maternal health, financial empowerment, and education, ensuring continued support for vital initiatives, including The Dr. Marcella Maxwell and Betty King Obiajulu Scholarship Program, which provides educational opportunities for promising students; and WHEREAS, Through its distinguished history of leadership and impact, the Greater New York Chapter of The Links, Inc. has uplifted countless individuals, fostered partnerships, and championed social justice, leaving an indelible mark on communities across New York; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is moved to recognize and applaud the extraordinary contributions of the Greater New York Chapter of The Links, Inc., and to extend best wishes for a successful and memorable Spring Luncheon; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Greater New York Chapter of The Links, Incorporated upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Spring Luncheon on April 12, 2025, and to celebrate its continued dedication to service and excellence; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Greater New York Chapter of The Links, Incorporated.
