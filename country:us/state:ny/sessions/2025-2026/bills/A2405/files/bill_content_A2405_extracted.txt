The economic development law is amended by adding a new 2 article 8-A to read as follows: 3 ARTICLE 8-A 4 NEW YORK STATE PRODUCT PROMOTION ACT 5 Section 207. Short title. 6 208. New York state product promotion registry. 1. The commissioner 11 shall develop and implement a New York state product promotion program. 12 The commissioner shall establish a registry for use on the internet that 13 would highlight businesses that manufacture or produce products in this 14 state. All New York state business owners that manufacture or produce 15 products in this state shall have the option to join such online regis- 16 try. Such registry shall include, but not be limited to; name of busi- 17 ness; address and county; contact information, including telephone 18 number, fax, e-mail and website address; manufactured or produced 19 products with a brief description; and any additional information, 20 including history, hours and dates of operation, and number of employ- 21 ees. 22 2. The commissioner shall name such registry and promulgate any rules 23 and regulations necessary for the administration of the registry. The 24 department shall be allowed to charge a reasonable registration fee, to 25 be determined by the commissioner, to cover such administrative costs.
