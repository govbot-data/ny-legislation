For the purpose of this section, the following terms 7 shall have the following meanings: 8 (a) "Biodiesel" shall mean a fuel, designated B100, that meets the 9 requirements of the ASTM international specification D6751. 10 (b) "Renewable Hydrocarbon Diesel" shall mean a North American-pro- 11 duced fuel derived from vegetable oils, animal fats, and other renewable 12 feedstocks that meet the requirements of ASTM international specifica- 13 tion D975.
