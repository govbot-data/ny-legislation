Legislative findings. The legislature finds that the duties 2 of state legislators have expanded substantially in scope, complexity, 3 and time commitment, effectively constituting a full-time public service 4 role. Competitive and transparent compensation is necessary to attract 5 qualified candidates, promote diversity of representation, and support 6 ethical and independent governance. 7 § 2. The opening paragraph of subdivision 1 of section 5 of the legis- 8 lative law, as amended by chapter 841 of the laws of 2022, is amended to 9 read as follows: 10 Effective January first, two thousand [twenty-three] twenty-eight, 11 each member of the legislature shall receive a salary of one hundred 12 [forty-two] eighty thousand dollars per annum. Beginning on the first 13 of January in the first calendar year following the effective date of 14 the chapter of the laws of two thousand twenty-six that amended this 15 paragraph, and annually thereafter, such annual salary shall be adjusted 16 for inflation. Such annual adjustment shall be equal to the percentage 17 increase, if any, in the consumer price index for all urban consumers 18 (CPI-U), as published by the United States bureau of labor statistics, 19 measured over the twelve-month period ending September thirtieth of the 20 preceding year. If such index reflects no increase or reflects a 21 decrease, no adjustment shall be made, and compensation shall not be 22 reduced.
