The public service law is amended by adding a new section 2 48-b to read as follows: 3 § 48-b. Priority call system and reporting. 1. The commission shall 4 establish a priority call system for use by police and fire departments 5 and agencies in the event of an emergency requiring disconnection of 6 utility services provided by a corporation subject to this article. Such 7 system shall be in operation at all times, and shall provide access to 8 one or more employees of a corporation, which employees shall be author- 9 ized to direct the immediate disconnection.
