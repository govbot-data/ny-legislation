The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding two new 2 sections 107-a and 159-b to read as follows: 3 § 107-a. Clean fuel vehicle. Every motor vehicle which is four- 4 wheeled, has a gross vehicle weight rating of not more than eight thou- 5 sand five hundred pounds, has a maximum speed of at least fifty-five 6 miles per hour, and can be propelled at least in part by an electric 7 motor and associated power electronics that draws electricity for a 8 hydrogen fuel cell or from a battery that has a battery capacity of at 9 least four kilowatt-hours, and is capable of being charged from an 10 external source of electricity. 11 § 159-b. Vehicle that meets the clean vehicle standards. Every motor 12 vehicle which (a) is certified, as provided for under the provisions of 13 the federal Clean Air Act (42 U.S.C. section 7401 et seq., as amended) 14 as meeting exhaust emission standards for super-ultra-low emission vehi- 15 cles which the state of California has adopted (California Code of Regu- 16 lations, Title 13) and is permitted to adopt under 42 U.S.C. section 17 7543, and which other states are permitted to adopt under 42 U.S.C. 18 sections 7503 and 7507, and (b) has a fuel efficiency that is at least 19 one and one-half times the average fuel efficiency for vehicles in its 20 weight class. 21 § 2.
