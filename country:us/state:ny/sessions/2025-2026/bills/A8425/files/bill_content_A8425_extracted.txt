STERN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Veterans' Affairs AN ACT to amend the veterans' services law, in relation to directing the department of veterans' services to conduct a study about veterans' employment review The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The veterans' services law is amended by adding a new 2 section 29-e to read as follows: 3 § 29-e. Veterans' employment review. 1. Legislative intent. It is only 4 proper that those in government help the heroes who volunteered to serve 5 in the military make a successful transition to civilian life and 6 employment. 7 Leaving military service for civilian employment can be challenging 8 for many veterans who may have difficulty translating military quali- 9 fications, skills, and experiences when applying to civilian jobs. Yet, 10 veterans have a diverse pool of skill sets that make them invaluable 11 assets in a wide variety of fields across the public and private sectors 12 and possess a wealth of experience that would be helpful in any employ- 13 ment situation, whether as a team member or in a leadership role. 14 The legislature is aware that the current federal government has been 15 reducing the federal workforce with a disproportionate impact on veter- 16 ans. It is therefore, important that the state of New York increase its 17 efforts to help those veterans who have recently been displaced from 18 their careers in the federal government and help them find a path to 19 employment and success in the state of New York. 20 2.
