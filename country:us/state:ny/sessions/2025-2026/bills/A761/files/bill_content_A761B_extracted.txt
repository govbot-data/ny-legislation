AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to reducing tuition rates
for certain Olympic athletes
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Paragraph h of subdivision 2 of section 355 of the educa-
2 tion law is amended by adding a new subparagraph 11 to read as follows:
3 (11) (i) For purposes of this subparagraph:
4 (A) "Team USA athlete" means any student athlete who meets the eligi-
5 bility standards defined and approved by the United States Olympic and
6 Paralympic Committee or by the national governing body for the sport in
7 which the athlete competes.
8 (B) "International athlete" means any student athlete who meets the
9 eligibility standards defined and approved by their sport's interna-
10 tional federation.
11 (ii) Notwithstanding the provisions of any other general, special or
12 local law, rule or regulation and pursuant to an appropriation and fund-
13 ing being made for such purpose, such regulations shall further provide
14 that the payment of tuition and fees by any student who is not a resi-
15 dent of New York state shall be paid at a rate or charge no greater than
16 that imposed for students who are residents of this state if such
17 student is enrolled in an institution or educational unit of the state
18 university, has been selected to be a Team USA athlete or international
19 athlete, and is training in New York State in an elite level program
20 approved by the United States Olympic and Paralympic Committee or the
21 international federation for the sport in which the athlete competes.
