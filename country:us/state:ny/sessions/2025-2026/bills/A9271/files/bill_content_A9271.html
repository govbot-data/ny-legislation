A09271 Summary:

BILL NOA09271
 
SAME ASSAME AS S07837
 
SPONSORConrad
 
COSPNSRSteck, Wright, Reyes, Burdick, Eachus, Colton, Hooks, DeStefano, Levenberg, Wieder
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add Art 23 §§450 - 457, Ec Dev L; add §98-e, St Fin L
 
Establishes the buy American, buy union grant program and the buy American, buy union grant fund for the purpose of providing grants to municipal subdivisions to reimburse such municipal subdivisions for the costs of procuring consumer or commercial vehicles which are union-made.

    21  sation.
    22    (b) "Bus" shall not include a vehicle used in a  ridesharing  arrange-

     1    (b)  The  application  form  may  specify the most common consumer and
     2  commercial vehicles that  would  be  eligible  for  a  grant  under  the
     3  program.
     4    (c)  The department shall post and make publicly accessible the appli-

     7  shall provide the following information to the department:
     8    (a) the name of and contact information for the political subdivision;
     9    (b) the amount of the grant requested;
    10    (c) the number of consumer or commercial vehicles which are union-made
    11  for  which  a grant under the program is sought, including a description

    13    (d) evidence of procurement and union-made status; and

    17  including but not limited to deadlines for submittal.
    18    § 454. Limitations. 1. The amount of a grant under the program  for  a
    19  political subdivision applicant shall be limited to not more than twenty
    20  percent of the costs incurred by the political subdivision regarding the
    21  procurement of the consumer or commercial vehicles which are union-made.
    22    2. Grants under the program shall be disbursed on a first-come, first-
    23  served  basis  determined  by  the department and until all money in the
    24  fund is exhausted.
    25    § 455. Determination. 1. The department shall  review  each  completed
    26  application  for a grant under the program and shall determine whether a
    27  grant shall be awarded.
    28    2. The department shall provide notice of the  determination  pursuant
    29  to  subdivision  one  of  this  section.  The following shall apply with
    30  respect to such notice:
    31    (a) if the department  shall  award  a  grant,  the  department  shall
    32  include  the  amount  of the grant and any conditions on the use of such
    33  grant; and
    34    (b) If the department does not award a grant in whole or in part,  the
    35  department shall include the reasons for the full or partial denial.
    36    §  456.  Reports. 1. No later than September first, two thousand twen-
    37  ty-six, and annually on September first thereafter, the department shall
    38  issue an annual report detailing the operation of the program  and  fund
    39  during the preceding year.
    40    2.  Each annual report required pursuant to this section shall include
    41  the following:
    42    (a) the number of applications received for grants under  the  program
    43  and the total dollar amount of such grants applied for;
    44    (b)  the  number  of  applicants  who  were  awarded a grant under the
    45  program, including the amount of each grant awarded;
    46    (c) the number of  applicants  who  were  denied  a  grant  under  the
    47  program,  in  whole  or  in  part, including the reasons for the full or
    48  partial denial;
    49    (d) the amount of money remaining in the fund at such time; and
    50    (e) any other information the department deems necessary.
    51    3. The report required pursuant to this section shall be submitted  to
    52  the  governor,  the temporary president of the senate and the speaker of
    53  the assembly.
    54    4. The department shall post and make publicly accessible each  annual
    55  report required pursuant to this section on the department's website.
