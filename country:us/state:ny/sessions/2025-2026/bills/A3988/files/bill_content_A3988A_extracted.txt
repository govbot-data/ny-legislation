AN ACT to amend the criminal procedure law, in relation to granting
security officers serving at the village of Ardsley village court
peace officer status
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 2.10 of the criminal procedure law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 88 to read as follows:
3 88. Employees of the village court of the village of Ardsley serving
4 as security officers at such village court. Such officers shall only
5 have the powers listed in paragraphs (b), (h), and (i) of subdivision
6 one of section 2.20 of this article; provided, however that nothing in
7 this subdivision shall be deemed to authorize such security officers to
8 carry, possess, repair or dispose of a firearm unless the appropriate
9 license has been issued pursuant to section 400.00 of the penal law; and
10 provided further, that if such a security officer is not otherwise a
11 police officer pursuant to section 1.20 of this title or a peace officer
12 pursuant to this section, such security officer shall successfully
13 complete the training required under section 2.30 of this section.
14 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
