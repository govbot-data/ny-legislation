        AN ACT to amend the public health law and the social  services  law,  in
          relation to rates of reimbursement for private duty nursing
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 11 of section 3614 of the public health law  is
     2  amended by adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows:
     3    (d)  For  enrollees covered by Medicaid as of October first, two thou-
     4  sand twenty-six under a payment rate  methodology  agreement  negotiated
     5  with  a  licensed home care services provider, including but not limited
     6  to services provided  through  licensed  home  care  services  agencies,
     7  payments for all hourly home care services provided through private duty
     8  nursing,  including  but  not  limited  to nursing services, provided to
     9  patients eligible for medical assistance pursuant  to  title  eleven  of
    10  article  five of the social services law made by organizations operating
    11  in accordance with the provisions of article forty-four of this  chapter
    12  or  by  health  maintenance  organizations  organized  and  operating in
    13  accordance with article forty-three of the insurance  law  shall,  at  a
    14  minimum,  be at a benchmark rate which is to be the fee-for-service rate
    15  calculated pursuant to this chapter and as in effect at  the  time  such
    16  services were provided.

    19    41. For enrollees covered by Medicaid as of October first,  two  thou-
    20  sand  twenty-six  under  a payment rate methodology agreement negotiated
    21  with a licensed home care services provider, including but  not  limited

     1  payments for all hourly home care services provided through private duty
     2  nursing, including but not limited  to  nursing  services,  provided  to
     3  patients eligible for medical assistance under this title made by organ-
     4  izations  operating  in accordance with the provisions of article forty-
     5  four of the public health law or  by  health  maintenance  organizations
     6  organized  and  operating  in accordance with article forty-three of the
     7  insurance law shall, at a minimum, be the rates of payment that would be
     8  paid for such patients pursuant to subdivision eleven of  section  thir-
     9  ty-six hundred fourteen of the public health law.
    10    §  3.  Effective  October  1,  2026, payments for private duty nursing
    11  services shall be  no  less  than  eighty  percent  of  the  appropriate
    12  CHAMPUS/TRICARE maximum allowable charge based on the New York locality.
    13  For  purposes of implementation, the appropriate CHAMPUS/TRICARE maximum
    14  allowable charge shall be the CHAMPUS/TRICARE maximum  allowable  charge
    15  in  effect  as of April 1 of the state fiscal year in which the services
    16  are rendered. The department of health shall publish a private duty rate
    17  schedule at least thirty days prior to  the  effective  date  of    such
    18  increase and annually thereafter.
    19    §  4.  This act shall take effect immediately; provided, however, that
    20  the amendments to section 364-j of  the  social  services  law  made  by
    21  section  two of this act shall not affect the repeal of such section and
