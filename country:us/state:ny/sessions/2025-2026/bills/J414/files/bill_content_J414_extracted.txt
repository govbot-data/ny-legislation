and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Adah Chapter No. 82, Order of the Eastern Star upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Five Point Tea, an event to be held on Sunday, March 2, 2025, which celebrates the principles of sisterhood, leadership, and community service; and WHEREAS, As a distinguished chapter within the Order of the Eastern Star, Adah Chapter No. 82 has long upheld the mission of faith, charity, and dedication to service, strengthening the bonds of fellowship among its members and throughout the community; and WHEREAS, The Five Point Tea serves as a time-honored gathering that reflects the values of the Five Heroines of the Order of the Eastern Star, symbolizing the virtues of Fidelity, Constancy, Loyalty, Faith, and Love, and encouraging attendees to embody these guiding principles in their daily lives; and WHEREAS, This special occasion not only celebrates sisterhood and tradition but also supports charitable initiatives that uplift individuals and families, reaffirming Adah Chapter No. 82's commitment to service and excellence; and WHEREAS, Through this event, members and guests alike are inspired to cultivate leadership, embrace tradition, and strengthen the bonds of fellowship, ensuring that the legacy of the Order of the Eastern Star continues to thrive for future generations; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to acknowledge the significance of this event and recognize the dedicated members of Adah Chapter No. 82 for their enduring contributions to community service and sisterhood; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Adah Chapter No. 82, Order of the Eastern Star upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Five Point Tea, and to extend its best wishes for a successful and meaningful gathering; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Adah Chapter No. 82, Order of the Eastern Star.
