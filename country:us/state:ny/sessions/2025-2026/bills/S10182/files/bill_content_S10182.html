        AN ACT to amend chapter 117 of the laws of 1983 incorporating the  Mace-
          don  Center  Volunteer  and  Exempt  Firemen's Benevolent Association,
          Inc., and providing for its powers and  duties,  in  relation  to  the
          purpose  of  such  association  and  the use of foreign fire insurance
          premium taxes
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Sections 1, 2, 3 and 7 of chapter 117 of the laws of 1983
     2  incorporating the Macedon Center Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's  Benevo-
     3  lent  Association,  Inc.,  and  providing for its powers and duties, are
     4  amended to read as follows:
     5    Section 1. Incorporation; membership. A not-for-profit corporation  by
     6  the  name  of  the Macedon Center Volunteer and Exempt [Firemen's] Fire-
     7  fighter's Benevolent Association, Inc., is hereby created. It  shall  be
     8  composed  of  such persons eligible to membership therein as hereinafter
     9  provided as shall (a) notify the secretary of the Macedon Center  Volun-
    10  teer  [Fireman's] Firefighter's Benevolent Association, Inc., in writing
    11  prior to  the  organization  meeting  of  such  corporation  hereinafter
    12  provided for of their desire to become members, or (b) shall attend such
    13  meeting  or an adjournment thereof, and also such persons so eligible as
    14  may become members of such corporation, pursuant to its by-laws.
    15    § 2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now  or  have
    16  been, or hereafter shall be active volunteer members of the fire depart-
    17  ment  of  the  Macedon  Center  Fire  District  and  who have heretofore
    18  acquired or shall hereafter  acquire  the  status  of  exempt  volunteer
    19  [firemen]  firefighters as defined in section two hundred of the general

     1  municipal law or any other applicable law by virtue of  having  rendered
     2  service  in  said fire department or fire company in such fire district,
     3  and all persons who are now  or  hereafter  shall  be  active  volunteer
     4  members  of said fire department shall be eligible for membership in the
     5  corporation hereby created;  provided  that  any  person  who  has  been
     6  expelled  or removed for cause from membership in any fire department or
     7  company shall not be eligible to membership  in  such  corporation.  The
     8  membership  of any person in such corporation shall terminate when [his]
     9  their membership as an active volunteer member of the fire department of
    10  the Macedon Center Fire District  shall  terminate  before  such  person
    11  shall have acquired the status of exempt volunteer [firemen] firefighter
    12  as defined in section two hundred of the general municipal law by virtue
    13  of having rendered services to such fire department.
    14    §  3.  Purpose. [The purposes of such corporation shall be the mainte-
    15  nance of suitable headquarters for, and the promotion of friendly  asso-
    16  ciation  for the betterment of the members of such corporation and their
    17  relations with the community, for the promotion of fraternal intercourse
    18  among the members of such corporation, to study  and  disseminate  among
    19  the  members  of  such corporation the most efficient manner of fighting
    20  fires, the relief, aid and assistance of such members and their families
    21  who are disabled or  indigent and to acquire real and personal  property
    22  such  as  may  be  necessary  for  the purposes herein set forth and the
    23  promotion of the welfare of the volunteer fire service within the terri-
    24  tory now served or which may hereafter be served by the fire  department
    25  of  the  Macedon  Center Fire District.] The purposes of the corporation
    26  created by this act shall be: to provide relief, aid and  assistance  to
    27  the  volunteer members of such corporation who are disabled, indigent or
    28  injured in the performance of their duties, and their families; to main-
    29  tain a suitable headquarters for the volunteer members  of  such  corpo-
    30  ration; to promote and safeguard the welfare of the volunteer members of
    31  the corporation, and their families; to enhance the morale of the volun-
    32  teer  members  of  the  corporation by providing social and recreational
    33  activities for the volunteer members of the corporation; to provide  for
    34  the  health  and safety of the volunteer members of the corporation; and
    35  to promote the volunteer fire service within the territory protected  by
    36  the Macedon Center volunteer fire department.
    37    § 7. Precept for payment of foreign fire insurance premium taxes. Such
    38  corporation  shall  collect  and  there  shall  be  paid to it all taxes
    39  imposed by section[five hundred fifty-three] nine thousand  one  hundred
    40  four  of  the  insurance  law  for  fire department use and benefit upon
    41  premiums for insurance against loss or damage by fire covering  property
    42  situated  within the Macedon Center Fire District located in Wayne coun-
    43  ty, New York. The officers of said corporation designated by its by-laws
    44  to collect and receive the aforesaid tax shall have all the  powers  and
    45  be subject to all the provisions of the insurance law, relating to trea-
    46  surers  of  fire departments. Such corporation shall also be entitled to
    47  receive a share of the tax distributed pursuant  to  the  provisions  of
    48  section  [five hundred fifty-four] nine thousand one hundred five of the
    49  insurance law, based upon the business written  in  the  territory  with
    50  respect  to  which  it  is entitled to collect and receive the tax under
    51  section [five hundred fifty-three] nine thousand one hundred four of the
    52  insurance law. Such taxes shall only be used [for the care and relief of
    53  disabled or indigent volunteer and exempt volunteer  firemen  and  their
    54  families] by the corporation in furtherance of its purposes as set forth
    55  in section three of this act.
