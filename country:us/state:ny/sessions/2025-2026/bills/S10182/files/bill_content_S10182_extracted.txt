[The purposes of such corporation shall be the mainte- 15 nance of suitable headquarters for, and the promotion of friendly asso- 16 ciation for the betterment of the members of such corporation and their 17 relations with the community, for the promotion of fraternal intercourse 18 among the members of such corporation, to study and disseminate among 19 the members of such corporation the most efficient manner of fighting 20 fires, the relief, aid and assistance of such members and their families 21 who are disabled or indigent and to acquire real and personal property 22 such as may be necessary for the purposes herein set forth and the 23 promotion of the welfare of the volunteer fire service within the terri- 24 tory now served or which may hereafter be served by the fire department 25 of the Macedon Center Fire District.] The purposes of the corporation 26 created by this act shall be: to provide relief, aid and assistance to 27 the volunteer members of such corporation who are disabled, indigent or 28 injured in the performance of their duties, and their families; to main- 29 tain a suitable headquarters for the volunteer members of such corpo- 30 ration; to promote and safeguard the welfare of the volunteer members of 31 the corporation, and their families; to enhance the morale of the volun- 32 teer members of the corporation by providing social and recreational 33 activities for the volunteer members of the corporation; to provide for 34 the health and safety of the volunteer members of the corporation; and 35 to promote the volunteer fire service within the territory protected by 36 the Macedon Center volunteer fire department. 37 § 7. Such 38 corporation shall collect and there shall be paid to it all taxes 39 imposed by section[five hundred fifty-three] nine thousand one hundred 40 four of the insurance law for fire department use and benefit upon 41 premiums for insurance against loss or damage by fire covering property 42 situated within the Macedon Center Fire District located in Wayne coun- 43 ty, New York. Such corporation shall also be entitled to 47 receive a share of the tax distributed pursuant to the provisions of 48 section [five hundred fifty-four] nine thousand one hundred five of the 49 insurance law, based upon the business written in the territory with 50 respect to which it is entitled to collect and receive the tax under 51 section [five hundred fifty-three] nine thousand one hundred four of the 52 insurance law. Such taxes shall only be used [for the care and relief of 53 disabled or indigent volunteer and exempt volunteer firemen and their 54 families] by the corporation in furtherance of its purposes as set forth 55 in section three of this act.
