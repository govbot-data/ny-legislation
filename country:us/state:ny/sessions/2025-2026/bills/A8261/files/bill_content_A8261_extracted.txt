The public health law is amended by adding a new section 2 2184 to read as follows: 3 § 2184. Novel coronavirus/COVID-19; immunization. 1. No immunization 4 used for the purposes of inducing immunity against COVID-19 in humans in 5 this state shall be made a mandatory immunization issued by an adminis- 6 trative agency.
