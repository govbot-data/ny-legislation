The legislature finds that any relief 2 and long-term recovery from the economic fallout of the COVID-19 pandem- 3 ic must recognize the disproportionate burden mothers have weathered. 4 Women, especially mothers, have faced the brunt of the economic chal- 5 lenges of the pandemic due to social barriers and policy failures that 6 have been compounded by enduring racism and gender injustices, including 7 the lack of care infrastructure, lack of family-supportive workplaces, 8 and gender and racial pay inequities. 9 Women continue to face unjust gender and racial wage gaps, and are 10 overrepresented in low-wage jobs, despite their gains in workforce 11 participation.
