        AN  ACT  to  amend  the  education law, in relation to prohibiting state
          funding of any college if such institution's instructional staff advo-
          cates or engages in activities on campus in  support  of  a  terrorist
          organization
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new  article  13-D
     2  to read as follows:
 
     3                                ARTICLE 13-D
     4         DUTY TO ADDRESS TERRORISTIC ACTIVITY BY INSTRUCTIONAL STAFF
 
     5  Section 641. Duty  to  address  terroristic  activity  by  instructional
     6                 staff.
     7    § 641. Duty to address terroristic activity  by  instructional  staff.
     8  1.  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no college shall receive
     9  state funding if a member of such college's instructional staff directly
    10  or indirectly promotes, encourages, advocates or engages in any activity
    11  on campus in support of a terrorist organization as defined by 8  U.S.C.
    12  § 1189, unless such college has taken appropriate corrective steps, in a
    13  time and manner as determined by the department.
    14    2.  The  commissioner  shall notify any college that they deem to have
    15  not taken appropriate corrective steps pursuant to  subdivision  one  of
    16  this section and that such college shall not receive state funding until
    17  appropriate  corrective  steps  are  taken, which the commissioner shall
    18  describe. The commissioner shall also notify the director of  the  divi-
    19  sion  of  budget  when  a college is ineligible to receive state funding
