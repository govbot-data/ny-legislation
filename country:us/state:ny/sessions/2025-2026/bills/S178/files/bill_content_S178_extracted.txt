The education law is amended by adding a new section 409-o 2 to read as follows: 3 § 409-o. School air conditioning system grant program. 1. For purposes 4 of this section, the following terms shall have the following meanings: 5 (a) "Covered area" shall include, but not be limited to, classrooms, 6 cafeterias, gymnasiums, libraries, and other areas in which students 7 congregate for educational purposes. 8 (b) "School" shall include, but not be limited to, a school district, 9 public school, board of cooperative educational services, special act 10 school district as defined in section four thousand one of this chapter, 11 approved preschool special education program pursuant to section forty- 12 four hundred ten of this chapter, schools and programs authorized by 13 articles eighty-one and eighty-nine of this chapter, or any public, 14 private or charter primary or secondary education school. 15 2. There is hereby established within the department a school air 16 conditioning system grant program. The purpose of the program shall be 17 to provide grants from amounts appropriated therefor to schools to 18 provide schools with funding for the installation of air conditioning 19 systems in covered areas. Grants shall be made with priority given to 20 schools that currently have no air conditioning systems in any covered 21 areas. 22 § 2.
