Section 72-g of the general municipal law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 4 to read as follows: 3 4. Within amounts appropriated therefor and based on availability of 4 funds, the chief officer of the fire department of a municipal corpo- 5 ration or fire district, subject to any rules or regulations governing 6 such department, may apply for money from the volunteer firefighter 7 training fund established under section ninety-nine-ss of the state 8 finance law for any payment made under this section. 9 § 2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 99-ss to 10 read as follows: 11 § 99-ss. Volunteer firefighter training fund. 1. There is hereby 12 established in the joint custody of the state comptroller and the 13 commissioner of taxation and finance a fund to be known as the "volun- 14 teer firefighter training fund". 15 2. Such fund shall consist of all revenues received from contrib- 16 utions, donations and all other moneys appropriated, credited or trans- 17 ferred thereto from any other fund or source pursuant to law. 18 3. Moneys of the fund, following appropriation by the legislature, may 19 be expended for the training of volunteer firefighters under section 20 seventy-two-g of the general municipal law. Moneys shall be paid out of 21 the fund on the audit and warrant of the state comptroller on vouchers 22 certified or approved by the comptroller. 23 § 3.
