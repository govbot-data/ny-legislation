COONEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring instruction on work zone safety awareness as part of the pre-licensing course The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (c-2) of subdivision 4 of section 502 of the 2 vehicle and traffic law, as added by chapter 585 of the laws of 2002, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 (c-2) "Work Zone Safety" awareness component. (i) The commissioner 5 shall provide in the pre-licensing course, set forth in paragraph (b) of 6 this subdivision, a mandatory component in "Work Zone Safety" awareness 7 education, totaling no less than thirty minutes, as a prerequisite for 8 obtaining a license to operate a motor vehicle. 9 (ii) The purpose of the component is to educate prospective licensees 10 on: 11 (A) the potential dangers to construction workers, construction equip- 12 ment operators and operators of motor vehicles in a highway work 13 zone[.]; 14 (B) the legal obligation to yield the right of way and exercise 15 reasonable care in the approach of an authorized emergency vehicle, as 16 set forth in section eleven hundred forty-four of this chapter; 17 (C) the legal obligation to exercise due care to avoid colliding with 18 a parked, stopped or standing authorized emergency vehicle, hazard vehi- 19 cle, vehicle displaying a blue or green light or certain other motor 20 vehicles, as set forth in section eleven hundred forty-four-a of this 21 chapter; and 22 (D) the consequences of violations, including those captured by auto- 23 mated work zone speed enforcement systems. 24 (iii) For the purposes of this paragraph, the term "work zone" shall 25 include "work area" as defined by section one hundred [sixty] sixty-one 26 of this chapter, and "restricted highway" as authorized in section 27 sixteen hundred twenty-five of this chapter.
