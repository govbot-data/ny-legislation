McMahon HONORING Firefighter John Karl upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Main-Transit Fire Department WHEREAS, Citizens across our State and Nation are inspired by and indebted to our noble volunteer firefighters who exhibit courage and bravery every day in the course of their duties; and WHEREAS, Volunteer firefighters exemplify the power of human compassion and the strength of the American spirit through actions of the most heroic magnitude; their sacrifices and their selfless dedication merit tribute and recognition by all citizens of this great country; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Firefighter John Karl upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Main-Transit Fire Department, to be celebrated in 2026; and WHEREAS, On October 20, 1975, John Karl made the courageous decision to serve his local community by joining the Main-Transit Fire Department; and WHEREAS, John Karl has consistently demonstrated a level of commitment, dedication, integrity and professionalism which truly exemplifies excellence in the volunteer service; and WHEREAS, Born and raised in Williamsville, New York, John Karl and his beloved wife, Linda, had the pleasure of raising three children, Adam, Michael, and Nicholas; and WHEREAS, John Karl comes from a family of firefighters, and he has followed the footsteps of his father and grandfather by becoming a third generation firefighter; his father, George, was chief of the Main-Transit Fire Department and has been the only member to lose his life in the line of duty; and WHEREAS, John Karl's two brothers, William Karl and Robert Karl, have also served at the Main-Transit Fire Department, and both have held the role of chief; and WHEREAS, Filled with the same sense of duty as their father, all three of John Karl's children made the commitment to serve at the Main-Transit Fire Department; Adam serves as chief of the department, Michael as 1st assistant chief, and Nicholas as an exempt member; and WHEREAS, Outside of his commitment as a volunteer firefighter, John Karl is Co-Owner of Refrigeration Sales and Services, which is a multi-generational family company located in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, John Karl has served the Main-Transit Fire Department in various capacities, including as fire lieutenant, captain, assistant chief, chief, safety officer, director, and chairman of the board; and WHEREAS, In addition to his official positions, John Karl has also served on and chaired numerous committees, including the committees on House and Grounds, Trucks, Fire Station Renovations, and State and Local Tournament; and WHEREAS, John Karl was previously a member of the Slo-Pokes race team; and WHEREAS, Firefighters unwaveringly serve our great State with pride, valor, and integrity; these respected volunteers are often the first to respond to an emergency, whether the emergency is a fire, transportation accident, natural disaster, act of terrorism, medical crisis, or spill of hazardous materials; and WHEREAS, It takes a special dedication, a strong desire to help others and a tireless sense of community to forsake precious time with family and friends to respond to the signal that a neighbor is in need; John Karl is such a firefighter; throughout 50 years of devoted service, this volunteer heroically performed, above and beyond the call of duty, those responsibilities which define the task of fire protection; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Firefighter John Karl upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Main-Transit Fire Department; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Firefighter John Karl.
