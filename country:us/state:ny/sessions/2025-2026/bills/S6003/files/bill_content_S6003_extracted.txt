SKOUFIS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Commerce, Economic Devel- opment and Small Business AN ACT in relation to establishing the space exploration task force The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. a. A temporary task force, to be known as the temporary 2 task force on space exploration, is hereby created to study the poten- 3 tial for economic, academic, and cultural opportunities, ensuring the 4 future survival of humanity and developing strategic advantages for this 5 state by means of evolving space technology and a growing space economy. 6 Such task force shall be comprised of appropriate state agency person- 7 nel, scientists, and experts in the field of space exploration. 8 b. The task force shall be comprised of: 9 (i) two members appointed by the governor; 10 (ii) two members appointed by the temporary president of the senate; 11 (iii) two members appointed by the speaker of the assembly; 12 (iv) one member appointed by the minority leader of the senate; and 13 (v) one member appointed by the minority leader of the assembly. 14 c. The task force shall: 15 (i) examine and evaluate the economic opportunities provided by space 16 exploration and evolving space technologies in other states and coun- 17 tries; 18 (ii) establish comprehensive metrics to monitor the health and vitali- 19 ty of the entire New York space ecosystem to identify challenges and 20 track important trends over time; 21 (iii) identify the potential impacts on New York of the discovery and 22 exploration of celestial structures in outer space by means of evolving 23 space technology; and 24 (iv) make recommendations on how to utilize space exploration and 25 evolving space technologies for the purposes of advancing scientific EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
