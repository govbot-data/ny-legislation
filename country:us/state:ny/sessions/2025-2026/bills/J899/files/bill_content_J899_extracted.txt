and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Mike Colangelo Sr. upon the occasion of his induction into the Rome Arts Hall of Fame on Sunday, May 4, 2025, at the Capitol Theatre in Rome, New York; and WHEREAS, Established in 2005, The Rome Arts Hall of Fame aligns with their mission to honor individuals who have a strong connection to the Rome community and a significant involvement in the performing, visual, or literary arts; and WHEREAS, Mike Colangelo Sr. is a writer, historian, and photographer who is committed to sharing his passion and interest about the Rome, New York area; he has created and administers a social media page entitled "People and Places of Rome NY"; and WHEREAS, With his revered photography skills, Mike Colangelo Sr. creates an annual calendar which features his photographs of local historic interest; with a true positive impact on his community, the funds raised from the sale of this coveted calendar have benefited numerous nonprofit organizations; and WHEREAS, This esteemed inductee, Mike Colangelo Sr., is the author of the 2014 book "Rome Through Time," which showcases and describes the vast changes and developments that the city of Rome has experienced over centuries; and WHEREAS, For his outstanding service to the people of Rome, Mike Colangelo Sr. is the recipient of the 2015 Shirley B. Waters Civic Award from the City; and WHEREAS, New Yorkers who are devoted to the preservation and furtherance of the arts while resultantly strengthening the cultural fabric of this great State are deserving of the highest praise and recognition; Mike Colangelo Sr. is one such individual; and WHEREAS, The arts are the building blocks of civilization, are central to human expression, and inspire the imagination; educational institutions, individuals, and organizations which further such worthy purposes richly merit the recognition and appreciation of this Legislative Body and the people of the State of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Mike Colangelo Sr. upon the occasion of his induction into the Rome Arts Hall of Fame on May 4, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Mike Colangelo Sr. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
