MANKTELOW -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to permitting ATV use on certain roadways in the county of Wayne The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 2405 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 1-a to read as follows: 3 1-a. Exceptions. Notwithstanding the provisions of subdivision one of 4 this section, the commissioner, with the concurrence of the commissioner 5 of transportation, shall designate for temporary ATV use the trail 6 located on state route one hundred four between the western county line 7 of Wayne county and the northern town line of the town of Hannibal in 8 Oswego county. The commissioner's designation shall include conditions, 9 developed in consultation with the commissioner of transportation, on 10 the use of such trail by ATVs to provide for the safe use of such route 11 and the safe operation of ATVs on the trail. Designations made under 12 this subdivision shall be effective for no longer than a period of one 13 year and may be renewed with the concurrence of the commissioner of 14 transportation. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
