and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Michael Watson upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of distinguished service to the Corning Police Department; and WHEREAS, For the past 30 years, Michael Watson rendered faithful, conscientious, and valuable service to the Corning Police Department; and WHEREAS, At age 18, Michael Watson had a motocross accident that left him permanently disabled and altered his career path; it led him to become a dispatcher with the Corning Police Department on October 31, 1994; and WHEREAS, In his capacity as dispatcher, Michael Watson served with loyalty, honor and distinction, and brought a patient and calming presence to the Corning Police Department; and WHEREAS, In his official acts, Michael Watson was governed by a keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and WHEREAS, Michael Watson's last day with the Corning Police Department will be June 26, 2025; and WHEREAS, During his tenure with the Corning Police Department, Michael Watson earned the admiration, esteem and affection of his colleagues; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Michael Watson upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of distinguished service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Michael Watson.
