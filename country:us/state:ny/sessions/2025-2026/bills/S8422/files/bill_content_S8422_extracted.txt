SCARCELLA-SPANTON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the military law, in relation to covered operations with regard to certain calculations of compensation The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subparagraph (v) of paragraph (a) of subdivision 5-a of 2 section 242 of the military law, as added by chapter 238 of the laws of 3 2008, is amended to read as follows: 4 (v) "Covered operation" means those military operations designated as 5 active military, naval, air, or space service by the federal government 6 of the United States[, in support of "Operation Enduring Freedom", 7 "Operation Iraqi Freedom", "Operation Noble Eagle", or successors there- 8 to, or operations specifically connected by federal designation, action 9 or implication with homeland security] or the state of New York, defined 10 as active service under 10 USC § 101 (d)(3). The implementing agency 11 may make such additional designations on a case-by-case basis as it 12 shall deem, in its discretion, to be in keeping with the spirit and 13 intent of this subdivision. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
