A motorized scooter which is operated 14 anywhere other than on lands of the owner of such motorized scooter 15 shall be covered by a policy of insurance, in such language and form as 16 shall be determined and established by the superintendent of financial 17 services, issued by an insurance carrier authorized to do business in 18 this state.
