HEVESI, REILLY -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Employees AN ACT to amend the retirement and social security law, in relation to a waiver of earnings limitations for certain retired law enforcement officers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 211 of the retirement and social security law is 2 amended by adding a new subdivision 10 to read as follows: 3 10. Notwithstanding the provisions of this section, sections two 4 hundred twelve and four hundred one of this chapter and section five 5 hundred three of the education law and any other law, regulation, rule, 6 local law, or charter to the contrary, a retired law enforcement officer 7 may be employed and earn compensation in a position or positions in the 8 service of the office of the special commissioner of investigations for 9 the New York city school district without any effect on their status as 10 retired and without suspension or diminution of their retirement allow- 11 ance and without prior approval pursuant to subdivision two of this 12 section. Earnings received as a result of employment in a school 13 district or a board of cooperative educational services in the state 14 shall not be applied to a retired person's earnings when calculating the 15 earnings limitations imposed by subdivisions one and two of section two 16 hundred twelve of this article. 17 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
