Section 3602 of the education law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 21 to read as follows: 3 21. Supplemental low spending high need aid. a. For aid payable in the 4 two thousand twenty-six--two thousand twenty-seven school year and ther- 5 eafter, school districts shall be eligible for an additional apportion- 6 ment equal to the product of the aid per pupil as defined pursuant to 7 subparagraph six of paragraph b of this subdivision, multiplied by 8 public school district enrollment as computed pursuant to paragraph n of 9 subdivision one of this section. 10 b. For purposes of this subdivision, the following terms shall have 11 the following meanings: 12 (1) "Regional average spending" shall be equal to the sum of the 13 expense per pupil as computed pursuant to paragraph f of subdivision one 14 of this section for all districts within each labor force region, 15 divided by the number of districts in each respective labor force 16 region. 17 (2) "Labor force region" for each district shall have the same meaning 18 as defined pursuant to subparagraph two of paragraph a of subdivision 19 four of this section, provided that a city school district in a city 20 having a population of one million or more shall be assigned to the New 21 York City labor force region. 22 (3) "Needs resource index" shall be equal to the extraordinary needs 23 index divided by the combined wealth ratio computed pursuant to subpara- 24 graph one of paragraph c of subdivision three of this section.
