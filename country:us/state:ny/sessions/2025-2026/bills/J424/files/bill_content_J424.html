J00424 Text:

 
Senate Resolution No. 424
 
BY: Senator RYAN S
 
        CONGRATULATING   Buffalo  Bills  Quarterback  Josh
        Allen  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation  as
        recipient  of  the  2024 AP NFL Most Valuable Player
        Award, one of the NFL's highest honors
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  to  recognize
individuals  of rare and exceptional athletic achievement, acknowledging
their profound and lasting impacts in their sport and on  the  citizenry
of this great New York State for years to come; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Buffalo  Bills Quarterback Josh Allen upon the occasion of
his designation as recipient of the 2024 AP  NFL  Most  Valuable  Player
Award, one of the NFL's highest honors, at the completion of his seventh
career season; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Achieving  such  a  high honor in competitive sports takes
years of discipline, tireless conditioning, and an unwavering desire  to
win;  Josh  Allen  has  proven  himself to be an exemplary athlete whose
perseverance and commitment to excellence have earned him  this  coveted
award; and
 
  WHEREAS,   Josh   Allen's   myriad  unprecedented  achievements  are
reflective of his continued performance  at  the  highest  standards  of
excellence and thereby cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  27 first-place votes and a total of 383 points, Josh
Allen became the AP NFL Most Valuable Player of the  year,  beating  out
staunch  competitors to secure the prestigious award for his first time;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Josh Allen finished his  2024  regular  season  with  3,731
passing  yards  and  531  rushing yards for an impressive total of 4,262
total yards; he totaled 41 touchdowns - 28 passing, 12 rushing, and  one
receiving,  becoming the only player in NFL history with over 25 passing
touchdowns, over 10 rushing touchdowns,  and  a  receiving  score  in  a
season; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Further  cementing his name in history, Josh Allen had the
lowest percentage of plays that resulted in either a  sack,  fumble,  or
interception in a single season (4.17%) since 1970; and
 
  WHEREAS,  At  the  end of this season, Josh Allen became one of only
two players in NFL history with 40+ offensive  touchdowns  in  at  least
five  career  seasons;  on top of this monumental achievement, the Bills
quarterback is the first in NFL history to secure 40+ touchdowns in five
consecutive seasons; and
 
  WHEREAS, A further testament to his unprecedented talent, Josh Allen
also became the first quarterback  in  NFL  history  with  six  or  more
rushing touchdowns in seven consecutive seasons; he is also now the only
 
quarterback  in  the  NFL  to  have  ever scored a passing, rushing, and
receiving touchdown in a single game; and
 
  WHEREAS, Josh Allen's achievements this season are vast and notable;
he  is  now  the  first  quarterback  in  NFL history to record multiple
passing touchdowns, multiple rushing touchdowns, 350+ passing yards, and
60+ rushing yards in the same game; he is also the first NFL  player  of
all  time  with at least two touchdown passes and two rushing touchdowns
in consecutive games, and is the first player of  all  time  with  three
such games in one season; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  his  seventh  career season in the NFL, Josh Allen
became the first player in the league's history to score three  or  more
passing  touchdowns  and  three  or  more rushing touchdowns in the same
game; and
 
  WHEREAS, As a captain of his team, Josh Allen helped guide the Bills
to one of the best seasons in franchise history, with an overall  record
of  13-4:  the  beloved  New York team also won the AFC East by Week 13,
which marked the earliest a team had won their division since 2009; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Further,  under  Josh   Allen's   steady   and   respected
leadership,  the  Buffalo  Bills made history as a team this season; the
Bills set franchise records for touchdowns (63) and points scored (509),
tied an NFL record of  30+  points  in  eight  consecutive  games,  went
undefeated  at home for the third time in team history and for the first
time since 1990, and, among many other worthy accomplishments,  are  the
first  NFL  team  in  history  to  beat  two 14-win teams in the regular
season, with Josh Allen totaling 747 yards,  six  touchdowns,  and  zero
sacks in those pivotal games; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  his  historic contributions to the sport of football,
his renowned passion and support for charitable causes, and for  proudly
representing  New  York  State  in  his notable achievements and storied
Buffalo Bills career, Josh Allen is justly deserving of  the  praise  of
this  Legislative Body and of the citizenry of our beloved Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
applaud  the  achievements  of  outstanding  professional athletes whose
dedication and determination have brought honor  to  their  team,  their
community, and the entire State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Buffalo Bills Quarterback Josh Allen upon the  occasion  of
his  designation  as  recipient  of the 2024 AP NFL Most Valuable Player
Award, one of the NFL's highest honors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Buffalo Bills Quarterback Josh Allen.
