                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          5479
 
                               2025-2026 Regular Sessions
 
                    IN SENATE
 
                                    February 21, 2025
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by  Sen.  COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when
          printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes
 
        AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation  to  unauthorized  use  of  a
          smart phone or mobile device or any data contained therein
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 1 of  section  156.00  of  the  penal  law,  as
     2  amended  by  chapter  558  of  the laws of 2006, is   amended  and a new
     3  subdivision 10 is added to read as follows:
     4    1. "Computer" means a device or group of  devices  which,  by  manipu-
     5  lation  of  electronic,  magnetic,  optical or electrochemical impulses,
     6  pursuant to a computer program, can  automatically  perform  arithmetic,
     7  logical,  storage  or retrieval operations with or on computer data, and
     8  includes any connected or directly related device, equipment or facility
     9  which enables such computer to store, retrieve or communicate to or from
    10  a person, another computer or another device  the  results  of  computer
    11  operations,  computer  programs  or computer data.   For the purposes of
    12  this article, a smart phone or  mobile  device  shall  be  considered  a
    13  computer.
    14    10.  "Smart  phone or mobile device" means a portable computing device
    15  that: (a) has a small form factor such that it can easily be carried  by
    16  a  single  individual;  (b)  is  designed  to operate without a physical
    17  connection; (c) possesses local, non-removable or removable  data  stor-
    18  age; and (d) includes a self-contained power source.
    19    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
 
 
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
