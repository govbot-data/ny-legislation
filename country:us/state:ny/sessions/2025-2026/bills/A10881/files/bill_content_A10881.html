Amd §163, St Fin L; amd §362, Chap 83 of 1995
 
Provides greater accountability in how contracts are procured by the state; provides that contracts comply with conflict of interest laws.

        AN ACT to  amend  the  state  finance  law,  in  relation  to  providing
          increased  accountability where it concerns contract decisions; and to
          amend chapter 83 of the laws of 1995 amending the  state  finance  law
          and  other  laws relating to bonds, notes and revenues, in relation to
          the effectiveness of certain provisions thereof
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subparagraph (i) of paragraph b and paragraph d of subdivi-
     2  sion  10  of  section  163 of the state finance law, subparagraph (i) of
     3  paragraph b as amended by chapter 137 of the laws of 2008 and  paragraph
     4  d  as  added  by  chapter 83 of the laws of 1995, are amended to read as
     5  follows:
     6    (i) Single or sole source procurements for services or commodities, or
     7  procurements made to meet emergencies arising  from  unforeseen  causes,
     8  may  be made without a formal competitive process and shall only be made
     9  under [unusual circumstances] a  declaration  of  a  disaster  emergency
    10  pursuant  to section twenty-eight of the executive law and shall include
    11  a determination by the [commissioner or the] state [agency]  comptroller
    12  that  the  specifications  or  requirements  for said purchase have been
    13  designed in a fair and equitable manner.  The  purchasing  agency  shall
    14  document in the procurement record, subject to review by the state comp-
    15  troller,  the bases for a determination to purchase from a single source
    16  or sole source, or the nature  of  the  emergency  giving  rise  to  the
    17  procurement.
    18    d. It shall be in the discretion of the commissioner [or], state agen-
    19  cy,  state  comptroller or governor to require a bond or other guarantee
    20  of performance, and to approve the amount, form and sufficiency thereof.
    21    § 2. Subparagraphs (xii) and (xiii) of paragraph b of  subdivision  14
    22  of  section 163 of the state finance law, as added by chapter 317 of the
    23  laws of 2014, are amended and a new subparagraph (xiv) is added to  read
    24  as follows:
