In order to participate in the START-UP NY program, a business must 17 satisfy all of the following criteria. 18 (a) The mission and activities of the business must align with or 19 further the academic mission of the campus, college or university spon- 20 soring the tax-free NY area in which it seeks to locate, and the busi- 21 ness's participation in the START-UP NY program must have positive 22 community and economic benefits. 23 (b) The business must demonstrate that it will, in its first year of 24 operation, create net new jobs.
