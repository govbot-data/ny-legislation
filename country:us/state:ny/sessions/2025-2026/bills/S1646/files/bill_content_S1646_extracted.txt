Section 259-c of the executive law is amended by adding two 2 new subdivisions 18 and 19 to read as follows: 3 18. require every parole officer and employee of the department to 4 wear body cameras with video and audio whenever they are interacting 5 with a parolee. 6 19. implement the use of cameras in every official office and room in 7 the department. 8 § 2.
