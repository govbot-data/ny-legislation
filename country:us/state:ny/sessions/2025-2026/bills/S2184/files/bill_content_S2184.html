Add §402-h, Pub Hous L; add §1879-a, Pub Auth L; amd §6-104, Energy L
 
Relates to enacting the "NYCHA HVAC repair act"; provides for the heating and cooling of properties including dwelling units owned by the New York city housing authority through the installation of geothermal or air source heat pumps and for the replacement of lighting on New York city housing authority property with LED bulbs.

        AN  ACT  to amend the public housing law, the public authorities law and
          the energy law, in relation to providing for the heating  and  cooling
          of  properties  including  dwelling  units  owned by the New York city
          housing authority through the installation of geothermal or air source

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Short title.  This act shall be known and may be cited as
     2  the "NYCHA HVAC repair act".
     3    § 2. The public housing law is amended by adding a new  section  402-h
     4  to read as follows:
     5    §  402-h. NYCHA HVAC repair. 1. The New York city housing authority is
     6  authorized and directed to permit the New York state energy research and
     7  development authority to retrofit New York city housing authority  resi-
     8  dential properties for the installation of geothermal or air source heat
     9  pumps  in  dwelling units owned or operated by the New York city housing
    10  authority, completely replacing all oil or natural  gas  fired  heating,
    11  hot water and other combustion units.
    12    2.  The  New York city housing authority is authorized and directed to
    13  permit the New York state energy research and development  authority  to
    14  replace all compact fluorescent, fluorescent, incandescent, or arc vapor
    15  lamps  in  properties  owned  or  operated  by the New York city housing
    16  authority with light-emitting diode (LED)  bulbs  if  lighting  has  not
    17  already  been  replaced,  with the goal of replacing all lighting in New
    18  York city housing authority properties with high efficiency bulbs.
    19    § 3. The public authorities law is amended by  adding  a  new  section
    20  1879-a to read as follows:
