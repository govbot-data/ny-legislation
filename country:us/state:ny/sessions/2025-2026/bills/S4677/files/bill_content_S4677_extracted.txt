MURRAY, CANZONERI-FITZPATRICK, MARTINS, MATTERA, PALUMBO, RHOADS, WEBER, WEIK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Crime Victims, Crime and Correction AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to the award of crime victim assistance funds to victims of overdoses, where such overdoses were the result of deception, surreptitious delivery, or third-party administration of a controlled substance which causes death The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1.
