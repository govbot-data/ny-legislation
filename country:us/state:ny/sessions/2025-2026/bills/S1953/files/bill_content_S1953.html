Add §9, Pub Auth L
 
Relates to a joint report by the department of audit and control and the authorities budget office on the financial and operational practices of any state authority whose purpose is to operate commuter transportation within the metropolitan commuter transportation district.

        AN  ACT  to  amend  the public authorities law, in relation to an annual
          joint report on the financial and operational practices of  any  state
          authority  whose  purpose is to operate commuter transportation within
          the metropolitan commuter transportation district
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The  public  authorities  law  is amended by adding a new
     2  section 9 to read as follows:
     3    § 9. Joint report. (a) Beginning June first, two thousand  twenty-six,
     4  the  department  of  audit and control and the authorities budget office
     5  shall formulate an annual joint report on the financial and  operational
     6  practices  of  any  state authority whose purpose is to operate commuter
     7  transportation within the metropolitan commuter transportation district.
     8  Such report shall include a review of such practices and recommendations
     9  for improvement of operations with the  goal  of  making  services  more
    10  efficient  and  cost effective. No later than December thirty-first, two
    11  thousand twenty-six, such report shall be completed and submitted to the
    12  governor, the speaker of the assembly, the assembly minority leader, the
    13  temporary president of the senate, the senate minority leader,  and  any
    14  state authorities which are subjects of the report.
    15    (b)  Any authority who is a subject of a report shall submit all docu-
    16  ments requested by the department of audit and control or  the  authori-
    17  ties budget office within thirty days from such request.
    18    (c)  Upon  completion  of the report, the head of the public authority
    19  which the letter or report was about, shall submit  a  written  response
    20  acknowledging  receipt of the letter or report within thirty days of the
    21  receipt of the report. If the report created by the department of  audit
    22  and  control and the authorities budget office makes recommendations for
