Section 130.00 of the penal law is amended by adding a new subdi- 16 vision 14 to read as follows: 17 14. "Person in a position of trust" means any person who is charged 18 with any duty or responsibility for the health, education, welfare or 19 supervision of a child, either independently or through another person, 20 no matter how brief. 21 § 3. The penal law is amended by adding three new sections 130.97, 22 130.98 and 130.99 to read as follows: 23 § 130.97 Sexual assault against a child by a person in a position of 24 trust in the first degree. 25 A person is guilty of sexual assault against a child by a person in a 26 position of trust in the first degree when such person subjects a child 27 less than eleven years old to sexual contact and when such person is a 28 person in a position of trust with respect to such child. 29 Sexual assault against a child by a person in a position of trust in 30 the first degree is a class C felony. 31 § 130.98 Sexual assault against a child by a person in a position of 32 trust in the second degree. 33 A person is guilty of sexual assault against a child by a person in a 34 position of trust in the second degree when such person subjects a child 35 less than fourteen years old to sexual contact and when such person is a 36 person in a position of trust with respect to such child. 37 Sexual assault against a child by a person in a position of trust in 38 the second degree is a class E felony. 39 § 130.99 Sexual assault against a child by a person in a position of 40 trust in the third degree. 41 A person is guilty of sexual assault against a child by a person in a 42 position of trust in the third degree when such person subjects a child 43 less than eighteen years old to sexual contact and when such person is a 44 person in a position of trust with respect to such child. 45 Sexual assault against a child by a person in a position of trust in 46 the third degree is a class A misdemeanor. 47 § 4.
