Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "HOMES stability and community access act". 3 § 2. Article 2-A of the social services law is amended by adding a new 4 title 3 to read as follows: 5 TITLE III 6 HOUSING OPPORTUNITY AND MAINTENANCE THROUGH 7 EMPOWERMENT AND SUPPORTS 8 Section 47-a. Definitions. 9 47-b. HOMES program. 10 47-c. Housing portals. 11 § 47-a. Definitions. As used in this title, the following terms shall 12 have the following meanings: 13 1. "HOMES program" shall mean the Housing Opportunity and Maintenance 14 through Empowerment and Supports program established by section forty- 15 seven-b of this title. 16 2. "Homeless" shall mean an undomiciled household which is unable to 17 secure permanent and habitable housing without special assistance, as 18 determined by the commissioner, including but not limited to households 19 temporarily residing in emergency shelters, transitional facilities, 20 hotels/motels, or substandard conditions. 21 3. "Not-for-profit corporation" and "charitable organization" shall 22 mean entities established pursuant to the not-for-profit corporation law 23 or otherwise established pursuant to law. 24 4. "Household" shall mean a single individual or family, including 25 couples without dependent children who, or which, are eligible to 26 receive public assistance. 27 § 47-b. HOMES program. The commissioner shall, within the amounts 28 appropriated therefor: EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. Housing portals. Within ninety days of the effective date of 12 this section, the commissioner shall establish: 13 1. A landlord portal system allowing landlords to: 14 (a) Review voucher payment status; 15 (b) Submit required documentation; and 16 (c) Resolve payment issues directly with the state. 17 2. A tenant portal system allowing tenants and authorized community- 18 based organizations to: 19 (a) Track application status; 20 (b) Upload documents; 21 (c) Monitor rent payments; and 22 (d) Receive updates regarding their case. 23 3. A centralized portal allowing landlords, tenants and certified 24 not-for-profit organizations to submit any questions, complaints, 25 requests for review and appeals. 26 § 3. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after 27 it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amend- 28 ment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implemen- 29 tation of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and 30 completed on or before such effective date.
