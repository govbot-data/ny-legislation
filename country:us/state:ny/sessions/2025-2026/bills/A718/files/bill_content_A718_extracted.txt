Subdivision 2 of section 398-a of the social services law, 2 as amended by section 20 of part C of chapter 83 of the laws of 2002, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 (2) The office of children and family services shall promulgate, 5 subject to consultation with appropriate state agencies, the approval of 6 the director of the budget and certification to the [chairmen] chair- 7 persons of the senate finance and assembly ways and means committees, 8 regulations establishing standards of payment for care provided to 9 foster children when the care of such children is subject to public 10 financial support, when such care is provided by relatives, authorized 11 agencies, family boarding homes, or state agencies.
