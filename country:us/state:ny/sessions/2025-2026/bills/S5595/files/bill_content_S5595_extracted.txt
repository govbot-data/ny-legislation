The department shall establish, 32 regularly update and maintain and make publicly available on its 33 website, a list of the owners and managers of multifamily residential 34 building which: 35 (a) have at least twenty open housing code violations related to 36 health or safety, or more than four violations which have been falsely 37 certified as corrected; 38 (b) have open housing code violations for: failure to provide self- 39 closing public doors or adequate lighting in public areas; lack of post- 40 ed certificates of occupancy; lack of heat, hot water, gas and electric- 41 ity; failure to remove vermin or lead-based paint; cracks in tile 42 floors, broken or defective cabinets, broken or defective sinks, ceiling 43 water leaks, or mold; and 44 (c) do not have a qualified property manager. 45 2.
