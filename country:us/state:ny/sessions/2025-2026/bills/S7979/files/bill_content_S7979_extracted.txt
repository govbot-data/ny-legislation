ASHBY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to requiring a report on the utilization and activity of the statewide fiscal inter- mediary; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expira- tion thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 365-f of the social services law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 10 to read as follows: 3 10. Report on the utilization and activity of the statewide fiscal 4 intermediary. (a) The commissioner of health shall produce a report on 5 the utilization and activity of the statewide fiscal intermediary. Such 6 report shall include, but not be limited to: 7 (i) the number of consumers enrolled in the consumer directed personal 8 assistance program (CDPAP) as of March thirty-first, two thousand twen- 9 ty-five; 10 (ii) the number of consumers enrolled with the new statewide fiscal 11 intermediary, Public Partnerships, LLC at the time such report is 12 published; 13 (iii) the number of consumers who did not enroll in the new statewide 14 fiscal intermediary, Public Partnerships, LLC; and 15 (iv) the modes of outreach and number of attempts to connect with the 16 consumers under subparagraph (iii) of this paragraph who did not enroll, 17 and where applicable, the reasons for denial whether it be no response, 18 incomplete application or ineligibility. 19 (b) The report shall also include an evaluation of the performance of 20 the program contractor or contractors and of the cost effectiveness of 21 all outreach efforts enrollment and program cost projections for the 22 next two fiscal years.
