OBERACKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to establishing statewide uniformity of vehicle inspection fees The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision (c) of section 305 of the vehicle and traffic 2 law, as amended by chapter 608 of the laws of 1993, is amended to read 3 as follows: 4 (c) The commissioner may establish maximum fees to be charged by an 5 official inspection station, provided however that any such maximum fees 6 established by the commissioner shall be uniformly applicable statewide. 7 Such fees shall be prominently displayed at such station. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of January next succeed- 9 ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
