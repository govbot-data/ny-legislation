The commissioner of motor vehicles shall include a 2 violation involving operation of vehicles when approaching a parked, 3 stopped or standing authorized emergency vehicle, hazard vehicle, vehi- 4 cle displaying a blue or green light or certain other motor vehicles 5 pursuant to section 1144-a of the vehicle and traffic law as a five 6 point traffic violation in 15 NYCRR 131.3. 7 § 2.
