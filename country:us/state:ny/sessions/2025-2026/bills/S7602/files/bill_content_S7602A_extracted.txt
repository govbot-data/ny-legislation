AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to dedicating a portion of
the state highway system to "Fort de La Presentation"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 345-d to
2 read as follows:
3 § 345-d. Portion of the state highway system to be dedicated to "Fort
4 de La Presentation". All that portion of the state highway system
5 constituting state route sixty-eight beginning at the intersection of
6 state route sixty-eight and Albany Avenue, continuing in a generally
7 northeasterly direction, and terminating at the intersection of state
8 route sixty-eight and Commerce Street in the city of Ogdensburg, county
9 of St. Lawrence, shall be dedicated to "Fort de La Presentation".
10 § 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal-
11 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system
12 as dedicated pursuant to section one of this act. However, to avoid
13 confusion and to limit any possible disruption of commerce, the dedi-
14 cation called for pursuant to section one of this act shall be one of
15 ceremonial nature and the official name of such highway shall not be
16 changed as a result of this act.
17 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
