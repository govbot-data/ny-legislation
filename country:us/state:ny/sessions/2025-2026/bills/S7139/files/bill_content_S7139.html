        AN ACT authorizing the commissioner of general services to transfer  and
          convey certain lands in the town of Wilton, county of Saratoga, to the
          Veterans and Community Housing Coalition; and providing for the repeal
          of certain provisions upon expiration thereof
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act  but  notwithstanding
     2  any  other provision of law to the contrary, the commissioner of general
     3  services is hereby authorized to transfer and convey to the Veterans and
     4  Community Housing Coalition, in consideration of  one  dollar  and  upon
     5  such  other conditions as the commissioner may deem proper, the lands as
     6  identified in section two of this act.
     7    § 2. The lands authorized by section one of this act to be transferred
     8  and conveyed are as follows:
     9    ALL that piece or parcel of land situate, lying, and being in the town
    10  of Wilton, county of Saratoga, state  of  New  York,  more  particularly
    11  bounded and described as follows:
    12    BEGINNING  at  a point on the easterly boundary of Northern Pines Road
    13  (County Route No. 34) at the southwest corner of lands conveyed  to  the
    14  county  of  Saratoga  by  The  People of State of New York by deed dated
    15  January 18, 2008 and recorded in the Saratoga County Clerk's  Office  in
    16  Instrument No. 2008003271, said point being S 7° 22' 34" E a distance of
    17  260.00  feet  from  the southerly boundary of Camp Saratoga Road; thence
    18  from said point of beginning along the southerly, westerly and southerly
    19  boundary of said lands conveyed to the county of Saratoga the  following
    20  five (5) courses and distances:
    21    1. S 83° 01' 10" E a distance of 310.67 feet to a point;
    22    2. S 12° 14' 08" E a distance of 145.00 feet to a point;

    10  along the easterly boundary of Northern Pines Road (County Route No. 34)
    11  to the point or place of beginning. Containing 15 acres of land, more or
    12  less.
    13    §  3.  The  description  in  section two of this act of the land to be
    14  conveyed is not intended to be a legal description but  is  intended  to
    15  identify  the parcel to be conveyed. As a condition of the transfer, the
    16  Veterans and Community Housing Coalition shall submit to the commission-
    17  er of general services, for their approval: (i) an accurate  survey  and
    18  description  of  the  lands  to  be  conveyed,  which may be used in the
    19  conveyance thereof; and (ii) a site plan showing the  proposed  improve-
    20  ments  necessary  to  carry out the public purposes set forth in section
    21  five of this act.
    22    § 4. The office of general services shall not transfer or  convey  the
    23  land  described in section two of this act unless an application is made
    24  to the office of general services by the Veterans and Community  Housing
    25  Coalition  within  two  years  after the effective date of this act. The
    26  transfer or conveyance of such land shall be completed within  one  year
    27  after  the Veterans and Community Housing Coalition submits the applica-
    28  tion to the office of general services.
    29    § 5. Any lands transferred pursuant to this  act  shall  be  used  and
    30  improved solely for the provision of housing and supportive services for
    31  public  benefit,  including  but  not  limited  to providing housing and
    32  services to homeless and at-risk veterans. Upon termination of such use,
    33  title to the lands so transferred shall revert to the state of New York.
    34    § 6. This act shall take effect immediately and shall  expire  and  be
    35  deemed repealed three years after such date.
