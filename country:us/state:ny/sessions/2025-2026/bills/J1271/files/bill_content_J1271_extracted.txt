Pohorely, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to citizens of the State of New York whose lifework and civic endeavor served to enhance the quality of life in their communities and the great State of New York; and WHEREAS, Warren J. Pohorely of Highland Mills, New York, died on Tuesday, June 17, 2025, at the age of 90; and WHEREAS, Born on September 25, 1934, in Queens, New York, to Augustus and Alice (Mulligan) Pohorely, Warren J. Pohorely lived a life of devotion to family, community, and country; and WHEREAS, Warren J. Pohorely proudly served his country in the United States Army Reserves, exemplifying patriotism and a steadfast commitment to the values of freedom and service; and WHEREAS, Warren J. Pohorely had a long and dedicated career as an electrician with Con Edison in Brewster, New York, where his work contributed to the well-being and progress of his community; and WHEREAS, A beloved father, grandfather, and great-grandfather, Warren J. Pohorely is survived by his two loving daughters, Carolyn and Christine; his cherished grandson, Nicholas; his wife, Kaylie; and his adored great-grandchildren, Lena Ava and Alexandra Jade; and WHEREAS, Warren J. Pohorely leaves behind a legacy of love, devotion, and integrity which will long endure the passage of time and remain as a comforting memory to all who were privileged to know him; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to mourn the death of Warren J. Pohorely, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the family of Warren J.
