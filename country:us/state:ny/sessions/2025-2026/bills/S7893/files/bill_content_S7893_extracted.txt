JACKSON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Judiciary AN ACT to amend the real property law, in relation to requiring land- lords to provide tenants with a rent ledger The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The real property law is amended by adding a new section 2 235-ee to read as follows: 3 § 235-ee. Duty to provide a rent ledger. Upon the request of a 4 lessee, it shall be the duty of the lessor, or any agent of the lessor 5 authorized to receive rent, to provide the lessee with a written ledger 6 containing a detailed record, including the amount, date and method of 7 payment, of all financial transactions between such lessee and lessor, 8 including: 9 (a) Rent payments; 10 (b) Late fees; and 11 (c) Security deposits. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 13 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
