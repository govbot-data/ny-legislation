AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring colleges,
universities, professional, proprietary, and graduate schools to
provide a notice of tuition liability policies
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 212-d
2 to read as follows:
3 § 212-d. Notice of tuition liability policies. All colleges, universi-
4 ties, professional, proprietary and graduate schools shall, at least
5 annually, provide written notice to all students of the institution's
6 tuition liability policy. Such notice shall be provided in clear and
7 conspicuous language in electronic form, and shall include notice of
8 policies related to exceptions of tuition liability and process for
9 requesting any such exception.
10 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
11 have become a law.
