Senate Resolution No. 437
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
        COMMEMORATING the 30th Anniversary of Steet Toyota
        of Yorkville, New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the
achievements of those individuals  and  businesses  which  substantially
contribute  to the economic vitality of their communities and the entire
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate the 30th Anniversary of Steet Toyota of Yorkville, New York,
celebrated on Thursday, February 13, 2025; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Steet  Toyota  of  Yorkville  is  a  family-owned   Toyota
dealership situated in the heart of Central New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Steet  family  has  been  involved  in the automotive
industry since 1945 and is now in its third generation of ownership  and
operation, with both the Steet and Ponte families continuing the legacy;
the  locally  operated  auto  group  serves the Central New York region,
including Oneida, Herkimer, and Fulton Counties; and
 
  WHEREAS, With over 65 years in business, the Steet Auto Group  began
with United Auto Sales in Utica, now located at 4994 Commercial Drive in
Yorkville;  established  in 1945 by brothers Joseph G. and Thomas Steet,
United Auto is now led by Joseph A. and Joseph T.  Steet,  a  father-son
team,  continuing  the  family's  legacy  of  dedication and strong work
ethic; and
 
  WHEREAS, For more than 20 years, Joseph A. Steet  envisioned  owning
the Toyota dealership across the street from United Auto; on February 7,
1995,  he  partnered  with  Steven  M.  Ponte  to  purchase  the  Toyota
dealership at 4991  Commercial  Drive,  which  eventually  became  Steet
Toyota  Scion  of  Yorkville,  marking  the  beginning of the Steet Auto
Group's expansion; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  its  inception,  the  dealership  has  grown   from
employing  13 people to its current 55 workers - including two employees
who have been with the dealership since 1995; and
 
  WHEREAS, Steven M. Ponte, a Herkimer, New York resident,  recognized
an  opportunity  at  a  local Chevy dealership, which they acquired, now
known as Steet-Ponte Chevy  at  3036  State  Route  28;  by  2003,  they
expanded  further with Steet-Ponte Ford Lincoln, renovating the building
at 5074 Commercial Drive;  in  2007,  Toyota  invited  them  to  open  a
dealership  in  Johnstown,  New  York,  helping  the  local  economy and
providing jobs, which led to  the  opening  of  Steet  Toyota  Scion  of
Johnstown Gloversville; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In 2011, Steet and Ponte added Volkswagen and Mazda brands
to their portfolio; the Steet-Ponte Volkswagen dealership was  built  in
2013   at  5046  Commercial  Drive  in  Yorkville,  and  their  existing
locations-Steet Toyota  Scion  of  Yorkville,  United  Auto  Sales,  and
 
Steet-Ponte  Chevy-were  all renovated into state-of-the-art facilities;
Steet Ponte Mazda moved to its own location  in  late  2020,  and  Steet
Ponte Nissan was acquired in late 2021; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Customer  satisfaction is the top priority at Steet Toyota
of Yorkville, and they work hard to ensure that each sales experience is
smooth and enjoyable; their commitment extends beyond the  sale,  aiming
to  provide exceptional service long after customers have driven off the
lot; and
 
  WHEREAS, This remarkable milestone is a testament to the dedication,
hard work, and exceptional service that has earned the trust and loyalty
of customers throughout Central New York; over the past  three  decades,
Steet  Toyota  has  continued  to grow, evolve, and set the standard for
excellence in the automotive industry; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the  30th  Anniversary of Steet Toyota of Yorkville, and to
wish them many more years of success, providing top-quality vehicles and
outstanding service to the community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Steet Toyota of Yorkville, New York.
