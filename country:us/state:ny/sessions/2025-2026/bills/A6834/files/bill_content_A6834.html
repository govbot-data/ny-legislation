        AN ACT to  amend  the  education  law,  in  relation  to  transportation
          services for certain school districts
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 1 of section  3635  of  the  education  law  is
     2  amended by adding a new paragraph h to read as follows:
     3    h.  Notwithstanding  any  other  provision of law to the contrary, the
     4  city school districts of the cities of Buffalo, Rochester, Syracuse  and
     5  Yonkers,  pursuant to adoption of a resolution by the board of education
     6  setting forth a plan, and approval of such plan by the commissioner, may
     7  provide  transportation  services  for  children  residing  within   the
     8  district  attending  kindergarten through grade twelve who live one-half
     9  mile or more from the school they legally attend if such  transportation
    10  is  deemed necessary pursuant to a determination by the commissioner due
    11  to safety concerns. Such transportation services shall be restricted  to
    12  transportation  to  and  from children's homes or before or after-school
    13  child care locations and, if offered, shall be provided for each student
    14  up to a distance of fifteen miles. Transportation pursuant to this para-
    15  graph shall be offered equally to all  children  in  like  circumstances
    16  residing  in  the district. For the purpose of computing approved trans-
    17  portation expenses for transportation aid, expenses  for  transportation
    18  provided  pursuant  to this paragraph shall be included and subject to a
    19  one hundred percent aid ratio.   The school  district  shall  provide  a
    20  report to the governor, the speaker of the assembly, the temporary pres-
    21  ident  of  the  senate  and the commissioner on the results of the first
    22  three years of the program, including the cost and benefit  analysis  of
