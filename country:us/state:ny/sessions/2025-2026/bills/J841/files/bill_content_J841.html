J00841 Text:

 
Senate Resolution No. 841
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
        COMMENDING  Thomas  J.  Buono upon the occasion of
        his designation for special recognition by the  Lake
        Delta Kiwanis Club of Rome
 
  WHEREAS,  Members  of the Armed Services from the State of New York,
who have served so  valiantly  and  honorably  in  wars  in  which  this
country's  freedom was at stake, as well as in the preservation of peace
in peacetime, deserve a special salute from this esteemed Body; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Thomas  J.
Buono  upon  the  occasion of his designation for special recognition by
the  Lake  Delta  Kiwanis  Club  of  Rome;   this   prestigious   honor,
co-sponsored  by  the Vietnam Memorial Committee, will be awarded at the
8th Annual Veteran's Recognition Dinner at the Beeches  Manor  in  Rome,
New York, on Saturday, May 10, 2025; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Thomas J. Buono was born and raised in Utica, New York; he
attended  Utica  College  of  Syracuse  University  and  Mohawk   Valley
Community  College; he received a two-year degree from the University of
Maryland; and
 
  WHEREAS, Thomas J. Buono entered the United States Army  and  served
his country with the 21st Signal & Aviation Group in Vietnam, ending his
tour  as a Specialist 4th Class (Spec 4); he then served with the United
States Army National Guard; and
 
  WHEREAS, For 38 years, Thomas J. Buono was employed  by  the  United
States  Postal  Service; he was President of the American Postal Workers
Union for 32 years; and
 
  WHEREAS, Active in his community, Thomas J. Buono  is  President  of
the  Vietnam  Veterans  of  America Chapter 944, Chairman of the Captain
George A. Wood Post Office Veterans Committee, member of  the  Board  of
Directors  of  Honor  Flight Syracuse, Chairman of the Veterans Cemetery
Committee, Director of Wreaths Across America for six cemeteries located
in Rome, New York, and several other titles and affiliations with  other
organizations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In 2020, Thomas J. Buono was elected to the New York State
Veterans Hall of Fame; he works closely  with  the  Gold  Star  Families
throughout Oneida County; and
 
  WHEREAS, Thomas J. Buono is an asset to his family and community; he
and  his  wife,  Elaine,  reside  in  New  Hartford,  New York, and love
spending time with their three children, Gina, David, and Matthew, seven
grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren; and
 
  WHEREAS, Having exhibited his patriotism, both at home  and  abroad,
Thomas  J.  Buono  demonstrated  his  love  for  his  country and merits
forevermore the highest respect of his State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, Our Nation's veterans deserve to be recognized,  commended,
and thanked by the people of the State of New York for their service and
 
for  their  dedication  to  their  communities,  their  State, and their
Nation; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Thomas J. Buono upon the occasion of his designation for special
recognition by the Lake Delta Kiwanis Club of Rome; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Thomas J. Buono.
