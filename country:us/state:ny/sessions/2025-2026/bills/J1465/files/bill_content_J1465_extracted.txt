WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend individuals whose visionary leadership, innovation, and service have expanded access to information, strengthened cultural understanding, and amplified underrepresented voices across media, technology, and global communities; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to honor Nur-Muhammad Tofader, Ph.D., founder, media executive, scientist, and global communications leader, upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the Lifetime Achievement Award in Multimedia Services by Young, Gifted, & Black Entrepreneurial Awards, Inc., on Wednesday, February 25, 2026; and WHEREAS, Founded in 2006 by Carl Gray, Young, Gifted, & Black Entrepreneurial Awards, Inc., is a nonprofit organization dedicated to recognizing distinguished individuals of color across the Americas, the Caribbean, and Africa, celebrating "Unsung Heroes and Heroines" whose leadership, resilience, and service have made a lasting impact across business, media, technology, culture, and community development; and WHEREAS, Born and raised in Chandpur, Bangladesh, and shaped by a journey rooted in academic excellence and global curiosity, Nur-Muhammad Tofader immigrated to the United States in the 1970s to pursue higher education and advanced scientific research, embodying the perseverance and promise of the immigrant experience; and WHEREAS, Nur-Muhammad Tofader pursued rigorous academic training in applied physics and electronics at the University of Dhaka, furthered his studies at the University of California, Los Angeles in nuclear science and applied physics, and earned his Ph.D., establishing a foundation of intellectual discipline and technical expertise; and WHEREAS, Nur-Muhammad Tofader served the United States government as a nuclear science researcher, contributing alongside leading scientists in advanced research before transitioning into entrepreneurship, where he would apply scientific precision and ethical purpose to the field of media and broadcasting; and WHEREAS, Motivated by a commitment to independent journalism and the representation of community-centered perspectives, Nur-Muhammad Tofader co-founded Bangladesh Millennium TV in 2006-2007, later establishing Millennium Multimedia Corporation, creating a platform dedicated to free expression, public accountability, and cultural integrity; and WHEREAS, Expanding this mission globally, Nur-Muhammad Tofader launched Millennium TV USA in 2012, followed by Millennium News24, broadcasting from Dhaka to international audiences and establishing a multimedia network that reaches viewers worldwide through Roku, Apple TV, Amazon Fire TV, Horizon Satellite, mobile applications, and digital streaming platforms; and WHEREAS, Under Nur-Muhammad Tofader's visionary leadership, MillenniumTV delivers diverse programming across news, arts, culture, education, religion, and legal awareness, serving audiences of all backgrounds while fostering cross-cultural dialogue, civic engagement, and community empowerment; and WHEREAS, Guided by faith, humility, and service, Nur-Muhammad Tofader has dedicated his life's work to bridging communities across borders, elevating marginalized voices, and advancing a media landscape rooted in truth, dignity, and shared humanity; and WHEREAS, Through a lifetime of innovation, perseverance, and principled leadership in multimedia services, Nur-Muhammad Tofader has left an enduring imprint on global broadcasting and independent media, inspiring future generations of media professionals and community advocates; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Nur-Muhammad Tofader, Ph.D., upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the Lifetime Achievement Award in Multimedia Services by Young, Gifted, & Black Entrepreneurial Awards, Inc., and to commend his extraordinary contributions to independent media, global communication, and community empowerment; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Nur-Muhammad Tofader, Ph.D., as an expression of the highest respect and appreciation of this Legislative Body.
