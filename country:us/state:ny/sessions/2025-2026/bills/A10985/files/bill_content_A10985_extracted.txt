POWERS -- read once and referred to the Committee on Cities AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to collecting NYC 311 call data from incarcerated individuals The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 9-154 of the administrative code of the city of New 2 York, as added by local law number 144 of the city of New York for the 3 year 2018 and the section heading as amended by chapter 486 of the laws 4 of 2022, is amended to read as follows: 5 § 9-154 Telephone services to incarcerated individuals. 1. The city 6 shall provide telephone services to individuals within the custody of 7 the department in city correctional facilities at no cost to the indi- 8 viduals or the receiving parties for domestic telephone calls. The city 9 shall not be authorized to receive or retain any revenue for providing 10 telephone services. 11 2. The New York city office of technology and innovation shall provide 12 data on all outgoing NYC 311 calls from incarcerated individuals to the 13 board of corrections. Such data shall be collected in monthly incre- 14 ments. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
