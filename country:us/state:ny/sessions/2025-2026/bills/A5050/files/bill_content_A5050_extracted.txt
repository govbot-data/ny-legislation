JACOBSON, SIMON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Environmental Conservation AN ACT to amend the New York state urban development corporation act, in relation to the state and municipal facilities program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 49 of section 1 of chapter 174 of the laws of 1968, 2 constituting the New York state urban development corporation act, is 3 amended by adding a new subdivision 4 to read as follows: 4 4. Any grant issued by the dormitory authority or the corporation from 5 the state and municipal facilities program shall be sponsored by the 6 governor or have a sponsor from the state senate or the state assembly. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall be deemed to 8 have been in full force and effect on and after April 1, 2025.
