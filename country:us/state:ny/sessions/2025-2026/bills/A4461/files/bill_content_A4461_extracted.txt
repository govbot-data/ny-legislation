JENSEN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to providing for distinctive license plates for members of the Seabee Veterans of America The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive plates for members of the Seabee Veterans of 4 America. 1. Any member of the Seabee Veterans of America residing in 5 this state shall, upon request, be issued a license plate bearing the 6 Navy Seabee emblem. Application for said license plate shall be filed 7 with the commissioner in such form and detail as the commissioner shall 8 prescribe. 9 2. The distinctive plate authorized in this section shall be issued 10 upon proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that the applicant is a 11 member of the Seabee Veterans of America. 12 3. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 13 in the same manner as other number plates upon the payment of the regu- 14 lar registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this 15 article, provided, however, that an additional one-time service charge 16 of ten dollars shall be charged for such plate. 17 § 2. The commissioner may issue special number plates to applicants 20 who are members of veterans organizations in the same manner as other 21 number plates are issued pursuant to this article.
