Add §§1244, 1245, 1290 & 1291, V & T L
 
Requires bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters be registered with the department of motor vehicles, inspected and have liability insurance; and have a license plate affixed to such bicycle with electric assist or electric scooter.

        Introduced  by M. of A. RAJKUMAR, SEAWRIGHT -- read once and referred to

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in  relation  to  requiring
          bicycles  with  electric assist and electric scooters be inspected and
          registered with the department of motor vehicles and  carry  liability
          insurance
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by  adding  two  new
     2  sections 1244 and 1245 to read as follows:
     3    §  1244. Registration and inspection of bicycles with electric assist.
     4  1. Every bicycle with electric assist as defined in section one  hundred
     5  two-c  of  this chapter shall be registered and issued a number plate by
     6  the department.  No person shall operate a bicycle with electric  assist
     7  unless such bicycle with electric assist shall have a distinctive number
     8  assigned  to  it  by  the  commissioner and a number plate issued by the
     9  commissioner with a number corresponding to that of the  certificate  of
    10  registration.
    11    2.  Such  number  plates  shall  be of such material, form, design and
    12  dimensions and contain or set forth such distinguishing number or  other
    13  identification  marks  as  the  commissioner  shall prescribe, provided,
    14  however, that there shall be at all times a marked contrast between  the
    15  color of the number plates and that of the numerals or letters thereon.
    16    3. Each such number plate shall be conspicuously displayed on the rear
    17  of  the bicycle, securely fastened so as to prevent the same from swing-
    18  ing.
    19    4. The fee for such number plates shall be determined by  the  commis-
    20  sioner.
    21    5.  The  commissioner  shall  require that every bicycle with electric
    22  assist  registered  in  this  state  be  inspected  annually  and  shall

     1  prescribe  the  standards  and  the mechanisms to be inspected; provided
     2  that the commissioner shall require that such inspection  shall  include
     3  inspection  of  the  battery. The commissioner shall prescribe rules for
     4  the  issuance  of  separate inspection certificates for  the bicycle and
     5  the battery which shall contain the same serial number but  which  shall
     6  indicate which component the certificate is for.
     7    6.  Where  there  is  a  violation  of this section, the fine for such
     8  violation shall be determined by the commissioner.
     9    § 1245. Liability insurance. 1. A bicycle with electric assist  regis-
    10  tered  under  this article shall be covered by a policy of insurance, in
    11  such language and form as shall be determined  and  established  by  the
    12  superintendent  of  financial  services,  issued by an insurance carrier
    13  authorized to do business in this state. Such policy shall  provide  for
    14  coverage  against  loss  from  the liability imposed by law for damages,
    15  including damages for care and loss of services, because of bodily inju-
    16  ry to or death of any person and injury to or  destruction  of  property
    17  arising  out of the ownership, maintenance, use, or operation of a bicy-
    18  cle with electric assist as defined in section one hundred two-c of this
    19  chapter, in such amounts as the superintendent shall prescribe.
    20    2. Proof of insurance as required by this section  shall  be  produced
    21  and  displayed  by  the  owner or operator of such bicycle with electric
    22  assist upon the request of any person having authority  to  enforce  the
    23  provisions  of  this chapter. The failure to produce such proof upon the
    24  request of any such person shall not be an offense but shall be presump-
    25  tive evidence that such bicycle with electric assist is  being  operated
    26  without having such insurance in force and effect.
    27    3.  Proof  of  insurance as required by this section shall be produced
    28  and displayed by the owner or operator of  such  bicycle  with  electric
    29  assist  to any person who has suffered or claims to have suffered either
    30  personal injury or property damage as a result of the operation of  such
    31  bicycle with electric assist by the owner or operator, if such insurance
    32  coverage  was  required  under  the  circumstances of such operation. It
    33  shall be an affirmative defense to any prosecution for  a  violation  of
    34  this  subdivision  that  such  proof was so produced or displayed within
    35  twenty-four hours of receiving notice of such injury or damage,  or  the
    36  claim of such injury or damage.
    37    4.  No owner of a bicycle with electric assist shall operate or permit
    38  the same to be operated anywhere in this state other than  on  lands  of
    39  the  owner  of  the  bicycle with electric assist without having in full
    40  force and effect the  liability  insurance  coverage  required  by  this
    41  section,  and  no  person  shall  operate a bicycle with electric assist
    42  anywhere in this state other than on lands of the owner of  the  bicycle
    43  with  electric  assist  with the knowledge that such insurance is not in
    44  full force and effect.
    45    § 2. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding two new sections
    46  1290 and 1291 to read as follows:
    47    § 1290. Registration and inspection of  electric  scooters.  1.  Every
    48  electric  scooter, of any class, as defined in section one hundred four-
    49  teen-e of this chapter shall be registered and issued a number plate  by
    50  the  department. No person shall operate an electric scooter unless such
    51  device shall have a distinctive number assigned to it by the commission-
    52  er and a number plate issued by the commissioner with  a  number  corre-
    53  sponding to that of the certificate of registration.
    54    2.  Such  number  plates  shall  be of such material, form, design and
    55  dimensions and contain or set forth such distinguishing number or  other
    56  identification  marks  as  the  commissioner  shall prescribe, provided,

    41  scooter  by  the  owner  or  operator,  if  such  insurance coverage was

    51  operate  an  electric scooter anywhere in this state other than on lands
    52  of the owner of the electric scooter with the knowledge that such insur-
    53  ance is not in full force and effect.
    54    § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
