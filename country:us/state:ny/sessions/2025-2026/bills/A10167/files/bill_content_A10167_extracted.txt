DE LOS SANTOS -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to establishing a curric- ulum on healthy relationships building skills The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 804-e 2 to read as follows: 3 § 804-e. Instruction in healthy relationships. 1. The commissioner, in 4 consultation with the board of regents, is authorized and directed to 5 establish and develop a curriculum by ages and grades on healthy 6 relationship building skills. 7 2. Kindergarten, first and second grades shall focus on the founda- 8 tions of friendship and emotions, including, but not limited to: recog- 9 nizing emotions in self and others; respecting personal space; learning 10 empathy and kindness; and understanding basic concepts of friendship and 11 family relationships. 12 3. Third, fourth and fifth grades shall focus on the foundations of 13 trust, respect and conflict resolution, including, but not limited to: 14 building trust and managing conflicts with peers; practicing active 15 listening and communication; understanding personal boundaries and 16 differences; and early signs of bullying and unhealthy peer dynamics. 17 4. Sixth, seventh and eighth grades shall focus on emotional regu- 18 lation and early romantic relationships, including, but not limited to: 19 navigating peer pressure and social media interactions; understanding 20 consent and mutual respect; and identifying healthy vs. unhealthy behav- 21 iors in friendships and romantic relationships. 22 5. Ninth, tenth, eleventh and twelfth grades shall focus on intimacy, 23 consent and power dynamics, including, but not limited to: in-depth 24 education on consent, sexual boundaries, and bystander intervention; 25 recognizing manipulation, coercion, and abuse; understanding gender 26 roles, stereotypes, and power in relationships; and building long-term 27 communication and trust in partnerships.
