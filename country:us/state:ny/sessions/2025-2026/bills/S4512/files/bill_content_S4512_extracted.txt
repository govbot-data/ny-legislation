Paragraph (d) of subdivision 3 of section 1696 of the vehi- 2 cle and traffic law, as added by section 2 of part AAA of chapter 59 of 3 the laws of 2017, is amended to read as follows: 4 (d) A TNC shall implement and maintain a policy and an oversight proc- 5 ess of providing accessibility to passengers or potential passengers 6 with a disability, including any accommodations required by the Ameri- 7 cans with Disabilities Act of 1990, as amended and accommodation of 8 service animals as such term is defined in section one hundred twenty- 9 three-b of the agriculture and markets law and shall to the extent prac- 10 ticable adopt findings established by the New York state TNC accessibil- 11 ity task force adopted pursuant to section twenty-one of [the] part AAA 12 of chapter fifty-nine of the laws of two thousand seventeen [that added 13 this section].
