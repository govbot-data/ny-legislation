Student loan expense shall mean the cumula- 11 tive total of the annual student loans covering the cost of attendance 12 at either the city or state university of the state of New York which 13 are outstanding when application for such program is made pursuant to 14 subdivision six of this section. Interest paid or due on such student 15 loans that an applicant has taken out for use in paying for such educa- 16 tion shall be considered eligible for reimbursement under this program. 17 6.
