Subdivision 1 of section 1912 of the public authorities 2 law, as amended by chapter 106 of the laws of 2024, is amended to read 3 as follows: 4 1. Developing and enhancing a community education and engagement 5 program to inform New Yorkers about the climate crisis and the benefits 6 of shifting to a clean energy economy and conducting outreach, providing 7 information and education, and building consensus on the environmental 8 and local benefits of renewable energy technologies. Such strategic 9 education and outreach shall include: 10 (a) Effective communication, engagement and public outreach to commu- 11 nities, including disadvantaged communities, to provide education and 12 information on maximizing the benefits that renewable energy technolo- 13 gies can provide while demonstrating strategies and solutions that are 14 available to ensure that community benefits are meaningful and negative 15 impacts are avoided, reduced, or mitigated. Such education and informa- 16 tion shall include the provision of information to residential and 17 commercial consumers regarding currently available incentive and afford- 18 ability programs, including but not limited to home energy efficiency 19 programs and programs that increase home access to renewable energy; 20 (b) Comprehensive education and outreach to local governments that may 21 host renewable energy technologies to provide objective information 22 about the impacts of renewable energy development and mitigation oppor- 23 tunities; [and] 24 (c) Facilitation of forums for communities, regions, renewable energy 25 developers, and other stakeholders to exchange information and ensure 26 that all have access to the factual information necessary to support the 27 appropriate siting of facilities and acceptance of renewable energy 28 technologies[.]; and EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
