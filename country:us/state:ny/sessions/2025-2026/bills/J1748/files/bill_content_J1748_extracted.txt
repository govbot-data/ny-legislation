Buttenschon COMMEMORATING the 25th Anniversary of Assured Information Security WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and pay tribute to those organizations of high purpose and worthy accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the citizens of the State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 25th Anniversary of Assured Information Security in June 2026; and WHEREAS, Founded in 2001, Assured Information Security (AIS) employs approximately 200 people and provides cyber and information security services to businesses of all sizes, including federal government partners; and WHEREAS, AIS was working in the cyber and information security industry before it was even known as "cyber"; with founders who had worked in the Air Force Research Laboratory Information Directorate in Rome, New York, AIS started as a software company focused on Air Force and Department of Defense cyber technology requirements research, earning its first contract with AFRL in 2002; and WHEREAS, For the past 25 years, the cyber and information security landscape has changed dramatically, transforming from a niche discipline to national security; the industry has shifted from long-term research and development cycles to the rapid deployment of mission-critical capabilities; technology, requirements, and threats now evolve at an unprecedented pace, demanding constant adaptation; and WHEREAS, Artificial intelligence plays an increasingly important role in the field of cyber and information security, and AIS remains a leading voice in the industry due to its ability to adapt, innovate, and respond in an environment where cyber and information security are essential to success; and WHEREAS, AIS has become one of the earliest organizations in the country to attain Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification Level 2 which shows an advanced level of compliance with federal information safeguarding requirements for sensitive information; AIS was also awarded a contract on the MDA SHIELD IDIQ, further expanding its role in supporting critical missile defense initiatives; and WHEREAS, In 2025, the company experienced many successes which included earning its 25th patent and securing major contracts like EVEREST and OASIS+; additionally, AIS received the Hire Vets Medallion Award for the fifth consecutive year, reinforcing its commitment to hiring and supporting United States veterans; and WHEREAS, AIS invests in the community through initiatives such as blood drives with the American Red Cross, Black Girls Code, a local youth drone camp with SUNY Poly, Toys for Tots, and food drives, to name a few; the company also strengthened key partnerships by signing a Memorandum of Agreement with SUNY Poly and launching a new partnership with Modovolo to accelerate innovation in the Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS) space; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that when individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 25th Anniversary of Assured Information Security and to honor its members, past and present, who have labored so conscientiously to improve the security and quality of life for the members of the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Assured Information Security.
