and WHEREAS, Dino Magaletto of Mount Kisco, New York, died on April 23, 2025 at the age of 65; and WHEREAS, Born in Yonkers, New York, on May 11, 1959, to Anna Pinori and Dino Magaletto Sr., Dino Magaletto graduated from Fox Lane High School and acquired a multitude of certifications in IT and telecommunications; and WHEREAS, As a very young man, Dino Magaletto could often be found tinkering away at his Father's and Uncle's shop, Dino and Artie's transmission in Croton Falls, or in his cellar, diligently repairing some electronic device for a family member or friend; and WHEREAS, Dino Magaletto's commitment to excellence, and his spirit of humanity, carried over into all fields of enterprise, including charitable and civic endeavors; and WHEREAS, A talented mechanic, machinist, and IT person, Dino Magaletto lent his talents to many employers over his career including Verveba Telecom, whom he was still employed with until his passing; and WHEREAS, Dino Magaletto remained a long-time resident of Northern Westchester where he proudly served as a lifetime member of the Goldens Bridge Fire Department; and WHEREAS, Dino Magaletto is survived by several cousins throughout the tri-state area and Pennsylvania, and the many brothers and sisters within the Goldens Bridge Fire Department; and WHEREAS, Armed with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of compassion, Dino Magaletto leaves behind a legacy which will long endure the passage of time and will remain as a comforting memory to all he served and befriended; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to mourn the death of Dino Magaletto, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the family of Dino Magaletto.
