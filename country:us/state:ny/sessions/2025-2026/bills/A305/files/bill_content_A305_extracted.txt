Paragraph (a) of subdivision 8 of section 1308 of the real 2 property actions and proceedings law, as added by section 1 of part Q of 3 chapter 73 of the laws of 2016, is amended to read as follows: 4 (a) Violations of this section may be heard before a hearing officer 5 or a court of competent jurisdiction.
