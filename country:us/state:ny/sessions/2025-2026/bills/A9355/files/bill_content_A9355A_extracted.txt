AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to mandating that
hospitals report cases of poisonings to poison control centers
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The public health law is amended by adding a new section
2 2803-cc to read as follows:
3 § 2803-cc. Reporting of poisonings. All hospitals shall report every
4 case of poisoning to the regional poison control center, in a manner and
5 within time periods as may be specified by regulation by the department.
6 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
7 have become a law.
