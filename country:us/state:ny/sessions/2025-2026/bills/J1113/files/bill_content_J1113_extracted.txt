Annual Family Sports Day to be held on June 7, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend those outstanding organizations which faithfully serve their communities and create meaningful opportunities for civic engagement, health, and family enrichment; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to commemorate the 2025 Annual Family Sports Day hosted by Sesame Flyers International, Inc., to be held on Saturday, June 7, 2025, in celebration of the organization's 42nd year of community service; and WHEREAS, For more than four decades, Sesame Flyers International has provided vital services and cultural programming throughout Brooklyn and beyond, uplifting families through youth development, education, wellness, and the arts; and WHEREAS, The Annual Family Sports Day stands as a joyful tradition that gathers community members of all ages for a day of celebration, movement, and togetherness-featuring games, races, music, performances, and nourishing food, all in the spirit of unity and healthy living; and WHEREAS, Through spirited activities such as sack races, tug-of-war contests, and intergenerational games, the Sports Day fosters camaraderie, teamwork, and wellness while strengthening the social fabric of the communities it serves; and WHEREAS, As Sesame Flyers International celebrates its 42nd Anniversary, the organization continues to be a beacon of support and inclusion for families across Brooklyn, helping residents thrive through mentorship, advocacy, and a commitment to service; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body commends Sesame Flyers International for its extraordinary contributions to New York's cultural and social landscape, and for creating safe and joyous spaces that empower and uplift families; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 2025 Annual Family Sports Day hosted by Sesame Flyers International, Inc., and to honor the organization's 42 years of dedicated service, leadership, and community excellence; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sesame Flyers International, Inc. in recognition of this meaningful occasion.
