Such housing judges shall have the power of judges of the court to 47 punish for contempts. Rules of evidence shall be applicable in actions 48 and proceedings before the housing part. The determination of a housing 49 judge shall be final and shall be entered and may be appealed in the 50 same manner as a judgment of the court. 51 (f) The housing court judge may be an elected supreme court judge of 52 such jurisdiction.
