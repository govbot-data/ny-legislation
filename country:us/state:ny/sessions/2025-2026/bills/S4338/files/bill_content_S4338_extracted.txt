COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Banks AN ACT to amend the banking law, in relation to requiring ATM machines to release ATM cards with EVM chips prior to disbursing cash or a receipt The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 75-b of the banking law is amended by adding four 2 new subdivisions 10, 11, 12, and 13 to read as follows: 3 10. "EVM chip" means a data storage chip that implements the Europay, 4 Mastercard and Visa technical standard. 5 11. "Chip card" means an automated teller machine card, credit card, 6 or other form of secure-payment card with an EVM chip inset for the 7 purposes of customer data storage. 8 12. "Card dipping" means the insertion of a chip card into a card 9 reader to enable customer data to be read off the EVM chip. 10 13. "Chip enabled automatic teller machine" means an automatic teller 11 machine that reads customer data from a chip card and uses card dipping. 12 § 2. Section 75-c of the banking law is amended by adding a new subdi- 13 vision 4 to read as follows: 14 4. Notwithstanding the provisions of section seventy-five-i of this 15 article, no newly installed chip enabled automatic teller machine shall 16 disburse cash or print a deposit receipt without first releasing the 17 card to the customer and the customer withdrawing the card from the 18 reader. 19 § 3. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after 20 it shall have become a law and shall apply to any ATM installed after 21 such date.
