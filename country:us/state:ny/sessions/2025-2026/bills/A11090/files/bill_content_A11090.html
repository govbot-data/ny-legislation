Add §119-f, amd §32, Pub Serv L
 
Enacts the "automatic utility deferment act" which requires regulated utility providers to automatically suspend termination of service and waive late payment penalties for state employees who are subject to a payroll freeze because the state budget was not passed by April first.

        Introduced  by M. of A. HOOKS -- read once and referred to the Committee
          on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions
 
        AN ACT to amend the public service law,  in  relation  to  enacting  the
          "automatic utility deferment act"
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may  be  cited  as
     2  the "automatic utility deferment act".
     3    §  2.  The public service law is amended by adding a new section 119-f
     4  to read as follows:
     5    § 119-f. Automatic utility  deferment  for  state  employees.  1.  For
     6  purposes  of  this section, the following terms shall have the following
     7  meanings:
     8    (a) "Payroll freeze" shall mean any period in  which  New  York  state
     9  employees  do not receive scheduled compensation due to a lapse or delay
    10  in enacted appropriations.
    11    (b) "Covered employee" shall mean any individual employed on  the  New
    12  York state payroll, including state agency employees, legislative staff,
    13  and elected officials.
    14    (c) "Regulated utility provider" shall mean any gas corporation, elec-
    15  tric corporation, gas and electric corporation, water corporation, steam
    16  corporation,  municipality  rendering  equivalent  utility  services  or
    17  internet service provider subject to regulation by the commission.
    18    (d) "Deferment period" shall mean the duration beginning on the date a
    19  payroll freeze takes effect and ending  on  the  date  full  payroll  is
    20  restored.
    21    (e)  "Internet  service  provider"  means any telephone corporation or
    22  cable television company that  provides  individuals,  corporations,  or
    23  other entities with the ability to connect to the internet.
    24    2.  Upon the commencement of a payroll freeze resulting from a failure
    25  to enact the state budget by the first of April in  a  given  year,  all
    26  regulated  utility  providers shall automatically suspend termination of
    27  service and waive late payment penalties for covered employees  for  the
    28  duration of the deferment period.

                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.

     1    3.  Such deferment period shall apply automatically to covered employ-
     2  ees following the declaration of a payroll freeze. No application  shall
     3  be necessary for covered employees to be granted such deferment period.
     4    4.  Deferred  utility balances shall be repaid by the covered employee
     5  on a structured payment plan, commencing no sooner than sixty days after
     6  full payroll restoration, divided over a period  not  to  exceed  twelve
     7  months.
     8    5.  The  commission shall have the authority to enforce the provisions
     9  of this section, and a regulated utility corporation which violates  any
    10  provision  of  this section shall be subject to penalties as provided in
    11  section twenty-five of this chapter or any other applicable provision of
    12  this chapter.
    13    6. Regulated utility providers shall not report deferred  balances  to
    14  credit  reporting  agencies  during  any  deferment period or during any
    15  structure repayment period.
    16    7. Nothing in this section shall be construed to  permanently  forgive
    17  or eliminate any utility debt owed by a covered employee.
    18    §  3.  Section 32 of the public service law is amended by adding a new
    19  subdivision 3-a to read as follows:
    20    3-a. A utility corporation or municipality shall  be  prohibited  from
    21  terminating any residential utility service of a customer who is a state
    22  employee pursuant to the provisions of section one hundred nineteen-f of
    23  this chapter.
    24    § 4. This act shall take effect immediately.
