A06734 Summary:

BILL NOA06734
 
SAME ASSAME AS S07278
 
SPONSORDais
 
COSPNSRLevenberg, Burroughs, Meeks, Chandler-Waterman, Jackson, Anderson, Hooks
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add §§386-c, 553-l, 538-a & 1279-m, Pub Auth L; add §4B, Chap 47 of 1931
 
Requires certain public authorities to establish a cashless tolling amnesty program between August first, two thousand twenty-five and December thirty-first, two thousand twenty-five for cashless tolls collected between April twenty-third, two thousand sixteen and June twenty-ninth, two thousand twenty-five.

        Introduced  by  M. of A. DAIS -- read once and referred to the Committee

        AN ACT to amend the public authorities law and chapter 47 of the laws of
          1931 relating to declaring the policy of the states of  New  York  and
          New  Jersey  in regard to certain vehicular bridges and tunnels within
          the port of New York district, in relation to requiring certain public
          authorities to establish  a  cashless  tolling  amnesty  program;  and
          providing for the repeal of certain provisions upon expiration thereof
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The public authorities law  is  amended  by  adding  a  new
     2  section 386-c to read as follows:
     3    §  386-c.  Cashless  tolling  amnesty  program. 1. Notwithstanding any
     4  other provisions of this title or any other  law,  the  authority  shall
     5  provide  an  amnesty program for any person, firm, corporation, or other
     6  entity charged with the payment of a cashless toll fee for  the  use  of
     7  any  highway,  bridge  or tunnel operated by the authority between April
     8  twenty-third, two thousand sixteen and June twenty-ninth,  two  thousand
     9  twenty-five.
    10    2.  Such amnesty program shall allow any person, firm, corporation, or
    11  other entity charged with the payment of a cashless toll fee for the use
    12  of any highway, bridge or tunnel operated by the authority to  pay  such
    13  tolls  in full and to have any toll violation fees and penalties related
    14  to such paid tolls waived; provided such payment is made  on  and  after
    15  August  first,  two thousand twenty-five and on or before December thir-
    16  ty-first, two thousand twenty-five.
    17    3. The authority shall conspicuously post on its  website  information
    18  on such amnesty program.
    19    §  2.  The  public  authorities law is amended by adding a new section
    20  553-l to read as follows:
