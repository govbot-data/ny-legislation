Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J00587 Summary:BILL NOJ00587  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORSTEC COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ00587 Text: Senate Resolution No. 587 BY: Senator STEC HONORING Firefighter Cameron Herdman upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Constable Volunteer Fire Department WHEREAS, Citizens across our State and Nation are inspired by and indebted to our noble volunteer firefighters who exhibit courage and bravery every day in the course of their duties; and WHEREAS, Volunteer firefighters exemplify the power of human compassion and the strength of the American spirit through actions of the most heroic magnitude; their sacrifices and their selfless dedication merit tribute and recognition by all citizens of this great country; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Firefighter Cameron Herdman upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Constable Volunteer Fire Department; and WHEREAS, Cameron Herdman has served with distinction as a member of the Constable Volunteer Fire Department since 1974, demonstrating his exceptional dedication to the safety and well-being of his community for 50 years; and WHEREAS, Cameron Herdman has consistently demonstrated a level of commitment, dedication, integrity, and professionalism which truly exemplifies excellence in the volunteer service; and WHEREAS, Throughout his remarkable tenure, Cameron Herdman has demonstrated outstanding commitment as a dedicated firefighter, responding to countless emergency calls and protecting his community with courage and skill, an active committee member, serving on numerous committees and contributing his expertise to various aspects of department operations, and a mentor to fellow firefighters, sharing his extensive knowledge and experience gained over five decades of service; and WHEREAS, Cameron Herdman's 50 years of service represent countless hours of emergency response, training exercises, committee work, and unwavering dedication to public safety; and WHEREAS, Firefighters unwaveringly serve our great State with pride, valor, and integrity; these respected firefighters are often the first to respond to an emergency, whether the emergency is a fire, transportation accident, natural disaster, act of terrorism, medical crisis, or spill of hazardous materials; and WHEREAS, It takes a special dedication, a strong desire to help others, and a tireless sense of community to forsake precious time with family and friends to respond to the signal that a neighbor is in need; Cameron Herdman is such a firefighter; throughout 50 years of devoted service, this volunteer heroically performed, above and beyond the call of duty, those responsibilities which define the task of fire protection; and WHEREAS, Cameron Herdman's half-century of service has made a lasting impact on the Constable Volunteer Fire Department and the community it served; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Firefighter Cameron Herdman upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Constable Volunteer Fire Department; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Firefighter Cameron Herdman.
