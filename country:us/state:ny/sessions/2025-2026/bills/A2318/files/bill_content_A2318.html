Raises the exclusion of certain indebtedness of school districts other than school districts in a city with one hundred twenty-five thousand inhabitants or more according to the latest federal census.

        Introduced by M. of A. McDONALD -- read once and referred to the Commit-

        AN  ACT  to amend the local finance law, in relation to the exclusion of
          certain indebtedness of school districts in a city  with  one  hundred
          twenty-five thousand inhabitants or more

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 121.20 of the local  finance  law,  as  amended  by
     2  chapter  506  of the laws of 1964, paragraph b as amended by chapter 116
     3  of the laws of 1968, is amended to read as follows:
     4    § 121.20[.] Exclusion of  certain  indebtedness  of  school  districts
     5  other than school districts in a city with one hundred twenty-five thou-
     6  sand  inhabitants  or  more  according  to  the latest federal census in
     7  ascertaining their power to contract indebtedness. a. As  used  in  this
     8  section:
     9    1.  The term "indebtedness" shall mean indebtedness evidenced by bonds
    10  and bond anticipation notes.
    11    2. The term "school district" shall mean a school district other  than
    12  a school district in a city with one hundred twenty-five thousand inhab-
    13  itants or more according to the latest federal census.
    14    b.  In ascertaining the power of a school district to contract indebt-
    15  edness, there may be excluded outstanding  indebtedness  contracted,  or
    16  indebtedness to be contracted, for objects or purposes having periods of
    17  probable  usefulness  provided  in  paragraph [a.] a of section 11.00 of
    18  this chapter, to the extent to which the commissioner of  education,  or
    19  such  person  or persons as may be designated by [him] the commissioner,
    20  shall estimate based upon facts existing on the date  of  such  estimate
    21  that  moneys  receivable  by  the  school  district from the state as an
    22  apportionment for debt service for school building purposes, under  laws
    23  existing  on  the  date of such estimate, shall meet the interest on and
    24  the annual requirements for the payment of such indebtedness.  Any  such

     4    c.  The  commissioner of education by rule or regulation may prescribe

     9    §  2. Section 121.30 of the local finance law, as added by chapter 382
    10  of the laws of 1962, is amended to read as follows:
    11    § 121.30 Exclusion of certain indebtedness of school  districts  other
    12  than  school  districts  in a city with one hundred twenty-five thousand
    13  inhabitants or more according to the latest federal census.   In  ascer-
    14  taining  the power of a school district, other than a school district in
    15  a city with one hundred twenty-five thousand inhabitants or more accord-
    16  ing to the latest federal census, to contract indebtedness under section
    17  104.00 of this [chapter] article,  there  may  be  excluded  outstanding
    18  bonds  and  bond  anticipation  notes  or  indebtedness  proposed  to be
    19  contracted and to be evidenced by bonds or bond  anticipation  notes  to
    20  finance  the  acquisition, construction and installation of a shelter or
    21  fallout shelter by such school  district  pursuant  to  section  twenty-
    22  three-a  of  the  New  York state defense emergency act, but only to the
    23  extent to which such indebtedness, at the time it was contracted  or  is
    24  to be contracted, did not or will not exceed fifty dollars multiplied by
    25  the planned shelter occupancy of such shelter.
    26    §  3.  Section  137.00 of the local finance law, as amended by chapter
    27  760 of the laws of 1963, is amended to read as follows:
    28    § 137.00 Debt statement of school districts outside  cities  with  one
    29  hundred twenty-five thousand inhabitants or more according to the latest
    30  federal  census.   The debt statement of a school district, other than a
    31  school district in a city with one hundred twenty-five thousand inhabit-
    32  ants or more according to the latest federal census, shall  contain  the
    33  following  items  of indebtedness for the purpose of showing the indebt-
    34  edness of such school district:
    35    1. The amount of
    36    (a) Outstanding bonds;
    37    (b) Outstanding bond anticipation notes, other than  those  issued  in
    38  anticipation of the bonds of the issue in connection with which the debt
    39  statement is prepared;
    40    (c)  Bonds  of  the  proposed  issue in connection with which the debt
    41  statement is prepared; and
    42    (d) Bond anticipation notes proposed to be issued where a debt  state-
    43  ment is prepared in connection with the issuance of such notes.
    44    2.  The  amount of indebtedness of a merged or annexed school district
    45  determined pursuant to section 121.10 of this [chapter] article.
    46    For the purpose of showing the  indebtedness  of  a  school  district,
    47  other  than  a  school  district  in a city with one hundred twenty-five
    48  thousand inhabitants or more according to  the  latest  federal  census,
    49  there may be deducted the amount of any such indebtedness which has been
    50  contracted  or  is  proposed  to be contracted to the extent provided in
    51  section 121.20 of this [chapter] article, and in such case  a  certified
    52  copy  of the estimate of the commissioner of education shall be attached
    53  to and form a part of the debt statement  filed  with  the  state  comp-
    54  troller and the clerk of the school district.
    55    For the purpose of showing the indebtedness of a school district other
    56  than  a  school district in a city with one hundred twenty-five thousand
