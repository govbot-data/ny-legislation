AN ACT to amend the energy law and the public authorities law, in
relation to the availability of alternative fuels along the New York
state thruway
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "electric super highway act".
3 § 2. Section 1-103 of the energy law is amended by adding a new subdi-
4 vision 16 to read as follows:
5 16. "Alternative fuel" shall mean ethanol, bio-diesel, electricity
6 (including electricity from solar energy), green hydrogen, and any other
7 fuel identified which is not petroleum-based, which is consistent with
8 the goals of protecting the safety and health of the public and minimiz-
9 ing emissions of air pollutants, and which is consistent with the
10 climate justice and emission reduction mandates in chapter one hundred
11 six of the laws of two thousand nineteen, and such successors in law and
12 function as may arise from time to time, and any applicable federal
13 alternative fuel requirements.
14 § 3. The energy law is amended by adding a new section 5-129 to read
15 as follows:
16 § 5-129. Alternative fuels. The New York state energy research and
17 development authority shall provide the New York state thruway authority
18 with a list of the most feasible alternative fuels, which shall number
19 no fewer than three, and assist the authority in implementing the
20 requirements of section three hundred fifty-four of the public authori-
21 ties law.
