AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to the avail-
ability of alternative fuels along the New York state thruway
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "Greener Highways Act".
3 § 2. Section 1851 of the public authorities law is amended by adding a
4 new subdivision 19 to read as follows:
5 19. "Alternative fuel" shall mean cellulosic ethanol, renewable
6 diesel, renewable gasoline, bio-diesel, electricity, green hydrogen, and
7 any other fuel identified by the authority which is not petroleum-based,
8 and is consistent with the goals of protecting the safety and health of
9 the public and minimizing emissions of air pollutants, and which is
10 consistent with the climate justice and emission reduction mandates in
11 chapter one hundred six of the laws of two thousand nineteen, and such
12 successors in law and function as may arise from time to time, and any
13 applicable federal alternative fuel requirements.
14 § 3. Section 1855 of the public authorities law is amended by adding a
15 new subdivision 18 to read as follows:
16 18. The authority shall provide the New York state thruway authority
17 with a list of the most feasible alternative fuels, which shall number
18 no fewer than three, and assist the authority in implementing the
19 requirements of section three hundred fifty-four of this chapter.
