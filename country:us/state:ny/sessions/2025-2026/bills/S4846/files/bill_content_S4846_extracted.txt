FAHY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation to requiring the office for people with developmental disabilities to submit a detailed report of proposed savings and investment initiatives within the exec- utive budget The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 16 of section 22 of the state finance law is 2 renumbered subdivision 17 and a new subdivision 16 is added to read as 3 follows: 4 16. Within five calendar days of the executive department submitting 5 the executive budget to the legislature, the office for people with 6 developmental disabilities in conjunction with the division of the budg- 7 et shall provide to the chair of the senate finance committee and the 8 chair of the assembly ways and means committee a detailed report of 9 proposed savings and investment initiatives contained within the execu- 10 tive budget. Such report shall include the proposed effective date and 11 the legal authority for each initiative as well as the projected fiscal 12 impact of each initiative for no less than the upcoming fiscal year and 13 the following fiscal year. The office for people with developmental 14 disabilities and the division of the budget shall provide the legisla- 15 ture with a final enacted report in accordance with this section. 16 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
