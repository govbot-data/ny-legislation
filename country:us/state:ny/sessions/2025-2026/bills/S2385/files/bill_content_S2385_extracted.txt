GALLIVAN -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation to enhanced aid and incentives for municipalities The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 54 of the state finance law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 12 to read as follows: 3 12. a. Enhanced aid and incentives for municipalities. Notwithstand- 4 ing any inconsistent provision of this section or of any other provision 5 of law to the contrary, the payment of enhanced aid and incentives for 6 municipalities for the support of local government for state fiscal 7 years commencing April first, two thousand twenty-six and thereafter, 8 shall be paid from an appropriation made for the enhanced aid and incen- 9 tives for municipalities program pursuant to the aid to localities budg- 10 et for such state fiscal years in a manner consistent with this subdivi- 11 sion. Enhanced aid and incentives for municipalities will be apportioned 12 to eligible municipalities by the director of the budget on a per-capita 13 basis, based on population. Municipalities in Tier 1 would receive 14 eighty percent of the appropriation provided annually and municipalities 15 in Tier 2 would receive twenty percent of the appropriation provided 16 annually. 17 b. Definitions. As used in this subdivision, unless otherwise express- 18 ly stated: 19 (i) "Municipalities" means a city with a population less than one 20 million, town or village. 21 (ii) "Enhanced aid and incentives for municipalities" means aid in 22 addition to the total of all aid payable to municipalities pursuant to 23 this subdivision. 24 (iii) "Population" means population data based upon the most recent 25 federal decennial census.
