S01207 Text:




 
                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          1207
 
                               2025-2026 Regular Sessions
 
                    IN SENATE
 
                                     January 8, 2025
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by Sens. TEDISCO, SEPULVEDA -- read twice and ordered print-
          ed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance
 
        AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to grants to assist local
          governments with reducing paper
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 164-e
     2  to read as follows:
     3    § 164-e. Grants to local governments for the reduction of  paper  use.
     4  1.  Within  the  annual  amounts appropriated therefor, the secretary of
     5  state may award grants on a competitive basis to  local  governments  in
     6  order  to  reduce  paper  usage and to implement digital alternatives to
     7  paper. The secretary of state shall by regulation establish criteria for
     8  such competitive grants, which shall at a minimum:
     9    (a) provide that the maximum grant amount shall not exceed  ten  thou-
    10  sand dollars per local government;
    11    (b)  require  local matching funds equal to twenty-five percent of the
    12  total amount of such grant; and
    13    (c) establish an application, evaluation, and award process  for  such
    14  grant program, which shall be competitive and publicly available.
    15    2. Grants awarded to local governments pursuant to this section may be
    16  included  in  calculations  to  demonstrate  three year cost savings and
    17  efficiencies for government efficiency plans submitted to  the  director
    18  of the budget.
    19    3. For the purposes of this section, the term "local government" shall
    20  have  the  same  meaning  as in section three-c of the general municipal
    21  law.
    22    § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day  after  it  shall
    23  have become a law.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
