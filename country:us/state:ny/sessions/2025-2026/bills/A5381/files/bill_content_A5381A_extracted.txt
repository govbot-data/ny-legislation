AN ACT to amend the private housing finance law, in relation to annual
audits of limited-profit housing companies
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 3 of section 32-a of the private housing
2 finance law, as amended by chapter 684 of the laws of 2021, is amended
3 to read as follows:
4 3. Require every company to file with [him, her or it] the commission-
5 er or supervising agency semi-annual or quarterly financial statements
6 and an annual financial statement. Each annual financial statement shall
7 be accompanied by a certificate of the company's independent certified
8 public accountant. Such financial statements shall be filed at the times
9 and in the manner prescribed by the commissioner or supervising agency
10 and within fourteen days of such filing, the company shall post such
11 financial statement on a website available to all shareholders and main-
12 tained by the board of directors. Such annual financial statement shall
13 include, but not be limited to, documentation of the following:
14 (a) records of all service contracts;
15 (b) invoices and bank statements for all expenses;
16 (c) any reimbursement claims and petty cash transactions;
17 (d) reserve funding;
18 (e) staff salaries and bonus pay, if applicable;
19 (f) total amount of residential and commercial rent collected per
20 month;
21 (g) total number of residents receiving housing benefits, including
22 but not limited to, the senior citizen rent increase exemption, and
