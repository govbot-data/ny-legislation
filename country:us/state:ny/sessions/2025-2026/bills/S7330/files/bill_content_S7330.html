        Introduced  by Sen. BRISPORT -- read twice and ordered printed, and when

        AN ACT to amend the education  law,  in  relation  to  requiring  higher
          education  research  facilities  to make available certain information
          regarding the adoption of dogs or cats

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivision  2  of section 239-b of the education law, as
     2  added by chapter 240 of the laws of 2016, is amended and a new  subdivi-
     3  sion 4 is added to read as follows:
     4    2.  A  facility  that  is  required to offer dogs or cats for adoption
     5  under this section may enter into an agreement with an animal rescue and
     6  shelter organization, a duly incorporated society for the prevention  of
     7  cruelty to animals, a duly incorporated humane society, or a duly incor-
     8  porated  animal  protective  association  that  operates physical animal
     9  sheltering facilities and  offers  household  pets  to  the  public  for
    10  adoption  by  way  of an established adoption program[.], that is exempt
    11  from taxes pursuant to paragraph (3) of subsection (c) of section 501 of
    12  the federal Internal Revenue Code, 26  U.S.C.  501,  or  any  subsequent
    13  corresponding  sections  of  the  federal Internal Revenue Code, as from
    14  time to time amended.
    15    4. A facility that is required to offer  dogs  or  cats  for  adoption
    16  under this section shall make available on the facility's website:
    17    (a)  an  adoption  policy  that  details  the specific steps that such
    18  facility takes to provide for the adoption of a dog or cat deemed  suit-
    19  able for adoption pursuant to subdivision three of this section; and
    20    (b)  beginning  January  first, two thousand twenty-six, a report that
    21  shall include, but not be limited to, the following information for  the
    22  preceding year:
    23    (i)  the  total  number  of  dogs  and  cats the facility released for
    24  adoption through private placement;
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.

     1    (ii) the total number of dogs  and  cats  the  facility  released  for
     2  adoption  to  an animal rescue and shelter organization, a duly incorpo-
     3  rated society for the prevention of cruelty to animals, a duly  incorpo-
     4  rated  humane  society, or a duly incorporated animal protective associ-
     5  ation  that  operates  physical  animal sheltering facilities and offers
     6  household pets to the public for  adoption  by  way  of  an  established
     7  adoption program; and
     8    (iii)  a list of all such organizations, societies and associations to
     9  which a dog or cat was released by such facility for adoption.
    10    § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth  day  after  it  shall
    11  have become a law.
