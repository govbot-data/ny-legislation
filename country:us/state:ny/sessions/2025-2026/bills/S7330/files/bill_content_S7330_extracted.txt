BRISPORT -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Higher Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring higher education research facilities to make available certain information regarding the adoption of dogs or cats The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 239-b of the education law, as 2 added by chapter 240 of the laws of 2016, is amended and a new subdivi- 3 sion 4 is added to read as follows: 4 2. A facility that is required to offer dogs or cats for adoption 5 under this section may enter into an agreement with an animal rescue and 6 shelter organization, a duly incorporated society for the prevention of 7 cruelty to animals, a duly incorporated humane society, or a duly incor- 8 porated animal protective association that operates physical animal 9 sheltering facilities and offers household pets to the public for 10 adoption by way of an established adoption program[.], that is exempt 11 from taxes pursuant to paragraph (3) of subsection (c) of section 501 of 12 the federal Internal Revenue Code, 26 U.S.C. 501, or any subsequent 13 corresponding sections of the federal Internal Revenue Code, as from 14 time to time amended. 15 4.
