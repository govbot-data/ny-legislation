PHEFFER AMATO -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the public officers law, in relation to permitting poli- tical subdivisions and municipal corporations of the state to set the residency requirements for certain positions The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 3 of the public officers law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 1-b to read as follows: 3 1-b. Notwithstanding any provisions of this section or of any general, 4 special or local law, charter, code, ordinance, resolution, rule or 5 regulation, requiring a person to be a resident of the political subdi- 6 vision or municipal corporation of the state for which such person shall 7 be chosen or within which their official functions are required to be 8 exercised, the legislative body of a political subdivision or municipal 9 corporation of the state may by local law, ordinance or resolution, set 10 or waive such residency requirements. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 12 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
