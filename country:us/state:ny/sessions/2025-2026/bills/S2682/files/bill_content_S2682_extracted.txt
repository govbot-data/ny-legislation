CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Crime Victims, Crime and Correction AN ACT to amend the correction law, in relation to requiring the depart- ment of corrections and community supervision to publicly post certain information after the death of an individual in the custody of the department The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The correction law is amended by adding a new section 30 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 30. Posting of incident reports. The department shall post conspicu- 4 ously on the public website of such department the name, identifying 5 information and a summary incident report of any death that occurs while 6 an individual is in the custody of the department. Such posting shall 7 occur no later than twenty-four hours after the family or next of kin of 8 such deceased individual is notified. 9 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 10 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
