GRIFFO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Investigations and Govern- ment Operations AN ACT to amend the legislative law, in relation to requiring bills to embrace a single subject The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The legislative law is amended by adding a new section 49 2 to read as follows: 3 § 49. Bills to embrace one subject. No bill, with the exception of the 4 budget bills submitted by the governor pursuant to article seven of the 5 constitution and amendments thereto, shall be introduced or passed by 6 the legislature which embraces more than one subject, and that shall be 7 expressed in the title. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect March 31, 2025. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
