Article 19 of the environmental conservation law is amended 2 by adding a new title 13 to read as follows: 3 TITLE 13 4 LEADED AVIATION GASOLINE 5 Section 19-1301. Definitions. 6 19-1303. Leaded aviation gasoline prohibited. 7 19-1305. Federal grant exemption. 8 § 19-1301. Definitions. 9 As used in this section, the following terms shall have the following 10 meanings: 11 1. "aviation retail establishment" means any public or private entity 12 that sells aviation gasoline, or offers or otherwise makes available 13 aviation gasoline, to a customer, including other businesses or govern- 14 ment entities, for use in this state. 15 2. "leaded aviation gasoline" shall have the same meaning as in 16 section 47017(22) of Title 49 of the United States code. 17 § 19-1303. Leaded aviation gasoline prohibited. 18 An airport operator or aviation retail establishment shall not sell, 19 distribute, or otherwise make available leaded aviation gasoline to 20 consumers on or after January first, two thousand thirty-one, in compli- 21 ance with section 47107 of Title 49 of the United States code. 22 § 19-1305. Federal grant exemption. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
