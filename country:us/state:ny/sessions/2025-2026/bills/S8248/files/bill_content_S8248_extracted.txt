BASKIN -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Commerce, Economic Devel- opment and Small Business AN ACT authorizing the empire state development corporation to develop a public awareness campaign promoting businesses located in New York state The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. 1. The empire state development corporation shall develop, 2 establish and implement a public awareness campaign promoting businesses 3 located in New York state and encouraging individuals and businesses to 4 purchase products and services from such businesses, with an emphasis on 5 the need to shop at and support local small businesses as defined pursu- 6 ant to section 131 of the economic development law, as well as the need 7 to shop at and support local downtown areas. 8 2. The campaign shall include an internet website providing informa- 9 tion on businesses located in New York state and their available 10 products and services including a Buy New York Online Networking Direc- 11 tory. The owner of any business located and operated in this state may 12 apply to the empire state development corporation to have such owner's 13 business included on such directory. The directory shall include, but 14 not be limited to, participating businesses' names, addresses, contact 15 information, including telephone number, fax, e-mail and website 16 address, products or services offered with a brief description, and any 17 additional information, including history, hours and dates of operation, 18 and number of employees. The names of businesses participating in such 19 directory shall be organized by specific categories based on the various 20 goods or services that such business provides. Such directory shall be 21 searchable by such specific categories including, but not limited to, 22 small businesses as defined pursuant to section 131 of the economic 23 development law. Such directory shall also include a search capability EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
