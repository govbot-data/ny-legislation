and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Mr. Adika Roberts for his outstanding contributions to Career and Technical Education and his dedicated service to the Brooklyn community; and WHEREAS, Mr. Adika Roberts has demonstrated exceptional commitment as a Career and Technical Education educator at Eagle Academy for Young Men, equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to pursue successful futures; and WHEREAS, Through his steadfast leadership and passion for student success, Mr. Adika Roberts has inspired and mentored countless young men in the Brooklyn, New York community, fostering academic excellence, career readiness, and personal growth; and WHEREAS, In furtherance of his mission to provide meaningful, hands-on learning experiences, Mr. Adika Roberts has played an instrumental role in organizing and facilitating the 2nd Annual Barbershop Competition, to be held on Friday, April 25, 2025; and WHEREAS, This esteemed event offers students a valuable opportunity to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and professionalism in the field of barbering, promoting both career development and entrepreneurial spirit; and WHEREAS, Mr. Adika Roberts' impact extends beyond the classroom, strengthening the Brooklyn community through his dedication to empowering young men with the tools and confidence needed to excel in their chosen paths; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize and honor Mr. Adika Roberts for his outstanding contributions to Career and Technical Education, his tireless dedication to the scholars of Eagle Academy for Young Men, and his remarkable service to the Brooklyn community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Mr. Adika Roberts in recognition of his exceptional leadership and commitment to shaping the future of young men through education and mentorship.
