LANZA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Veterans, Homeland Securi- ty and Military Affairs AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to requiring elementary and secondary schools to close in the event the governor declares a state of emergency related to the weather in any city having a popu- lation of one million or more The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 27-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 27-a. Weather related state of emergency. Whenever the governor, on 4 such governor's own initiative or pursuant to a request from the mayor 5 of a city having a population of one million or more, finds that a state 6 of emergency exists because of the imminent or existing weather condi- 7 tions in a city having a population of one million or more, such gover- 8 nor shall declare a weather related state of emergency by executive 9 order. Upon such a declaration all public and private elementary and 10 secondary schools in such city shall be closed and remain closed until 11 such state of emergency is terminated. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
