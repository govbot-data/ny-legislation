BILL NOA06835

COSPNSRSeawright, Bendett, Clark

Amd §6527, Ed L; amd §2805-m, Pub Health L
 
Removes the prohibition relating to certain discovery of testimony in certain actions for medical, dental or podiatric malpractice.
