Hall, Imperial Deputy of the Desert of New York, and with the coordination of HPIP Tommy Wheeler, Recorder and Organizer, this year's Jubilee Day promises to uphold the sacred traditions of the Order and strengthen the bonds of fraternal unity; and WHEREAS, Events such as Jubilee Day deepen our appreciation for the enduring legacy of Prince Hall Affiliated bodies and their essential role in uplifting communities through faith, discipline, and service; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 2025 Jubilee Day Service hosted by the Desert of New York, Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine, to be held on June 1, 2025, and to commend the nobles, daughters, officers, and leadership for their enduring dedication to spiritual and civic excellence; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to HPIP Damien E.
