Subdivision 17-a of the executive law, as added by a chap- 2 ter of the laws of 2024 amending the executive law relating to requiring 3 new construction that includes dedicated off-street parking to provide 4 electric vehicle charging stations and electric vehicle ready parking 5 spaces, as proposed in legislative bills numbers S. 1736-E and A. 6 3780-E, is REPEALED. 7 § 2.
