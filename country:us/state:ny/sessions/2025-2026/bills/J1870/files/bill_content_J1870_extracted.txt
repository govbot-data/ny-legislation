and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Poinsettia Chapter No. 76, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, upon the occasion of hosting its Dinner Dance on Saturday, April 18, 2026; and WHEREAS, As a proud chapter within the Third District of the State of New York, Poinsettia Chapter No. 76 has long exemplified the highest ideals of the order, laboring faithfully in the vineyard of service and demonstrating a steadfast commitment to charity, truth, and loving-kindness; and WHEREAS, Under the distinguished leadership of Worthy Matron Tania Hollensworth and Worthy Patron James Johnson, the chapter continues to flourish, fostering unity, strengthening fraternal bonds, and advancing meaningful initiatives that benefit the community at large; and WHEREAS, The 2026 Dinner Dance stands as a cherished occasion of fellowship and celebration, bringing together members, family, and friends to reflect upon shared accomplishments and to honor those whose service has made a lasting impact; and WHEREAS, On this auspicious occasion, special recognition is afforded to Junior Past Matron Linda Valentine, whose dedicated service and commitment to the chapter have helped to guide and sustain its mission with grace and distinction; and WHEREAS, Further recognition is extended to Past Matron Margo E. Abraham, whose leadership, wisdom, and enduring contributions have strengthened the foundation of the chapter and inspired those who follow in her path; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body also recognizes Cynthia Settles, Director of the Falling Angels Sickle Cell Foundation, whose compassionate advocacy and tireless efforts to raise awareness and support individuals and families affected by sickle cell disease exemplify the highest ideals of service to humanity; and WHEREAS, Through their collective efforts and individual contributions, these honorees embody the principles and virtues that define the Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, serving as shining examples of leadership, compassion, and dedication; and WHEREAS, Special recognition is hereby extended to the evening's honorees; Junior Past Matron Linda Valentine, Past Matron Margo E. Abraham, and Cynthia Settles for their outstanding contributions and steadfast dedication to service; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper to recognize this meaningful occasion and to commend those whose service continues to uplift and inspire; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Poinsettia Chapter No. 76 upon the occasion of hosting its Dinner Dance on April 18, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Poinsettia Chapter No. 76, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star.
