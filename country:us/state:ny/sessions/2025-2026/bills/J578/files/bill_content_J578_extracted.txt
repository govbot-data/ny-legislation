their reputation is synonymous with excellence in the arduous field of fire protection and prevention; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Chief Christopher Foresto, M.D. upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 35 years of dedicated service to the Jericho Volunteer Fire Department at its 91st Annual Installation of Officers on Saturday, April 5, 2025, at Crescent Beach Club, Bayville, New York; and WHEREAS, Volunteer firefighters and fire departments not only provide the critical service of fire protection but also engage in other activities of great benefit and importance to the communities they serve; and WHEREAS, Christopher Foresto has performed his duties according to the Jericho Volunteer Fire Department's mission to provide prompt, professional, fire and emergency response to the hamlet of Jericho and the surrounding area, and his efforts have been recognized through his promotion to chief; and WHEREAS, As an active leader in both the department and community, Christopher Foresto has consistently demonstrated a level of commitment, dedication, integrity, and professionalism that exemplifies excellence in the volunteer service; and WHEREAS, Throughout his tenure at the Jericho Volunteer Fire Department, Christopher Foresto has served in various capacities, including service as lieutenant and captain for Engine Company 3 and service as 2nd Assistant Chief, 1st Assistant Chief, and Department Chief; and WHEREAS, Christopher Foresto has demonstrated his active commitment and desire to succeed by attending numerous trainings, meetings, conferences, and conventions; those who have the pleasure of engaging with Christopher Foresto can attest to his kind nature and willingness to share his vast knowledge and experiences with others; and WHEREAS, Beyond the firehouse, Christopher Foresto is devoted to his loving wife Lindsy and his three children, Michael, James, and William, and he has gracefully balanced the demands of his family, surgical career, and volunteer fire department service in addition to volunteering for the board of education in Jericho schools; and WHEREAS, It takes a special dedication, a strong desire to help others, and a tireless sense of community to forsake precious time with family and friends to respond to the signal that a neighbor is in need; Christopher Foresto is such a firefighter; throughout 35 years of dedicated service, this volunteer heroically performed, above and beyond the call of duty, those responsibilities which define the task of fire protection; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Chief Christopher Foresto, M.D. upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 35 years of dedicated service to the Jericho Volunteer Fire Department; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Chief Christopher Foresto, M.D. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
