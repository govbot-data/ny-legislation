OBERACKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Energy and Telecommuni- cations AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to allowing custom- ers of a utility to call for an independent audit of the utility's records The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 25 of the public service law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 8 to read as follows: 3 8. In the event that a public utility company, corporation or person 4 and the officers, agents and employees thereof knowingly fails or 5 neglects to obey or comply with a provision of this chapter or an order 6 adopted under authority of this chapter for a period of ninety days or 7 longer, the commission, at the request of one hundred or more current 8 customers of the entity aggrieved by the entity's failure to comply, 9 shall conduct a forensic audit of the records and operations of the 10 entity. This audit shall be in addition to any other audit authorized 11 under this chapter. The audit shall be completed within one hundred 12 eighty days of the date of the customer request. The results of the 13 audit shall be made public on the commission's website. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth day after it shall 15 have become a law.
