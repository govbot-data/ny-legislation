Grant, and Adam O'Neil; and WHEREAS, These three honorees have demonstrated exemplary commitment to uplifting seniors and supporting the broader community through their professional, civic, and personal endeavors, each leaving a meaningful impact through leadership, care, and advocacy; and WHEREAS, The Alpha Phi Alpha Senior Citizens Center continues to uphold the values of its founding fraternity, Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., by fostering a culture of respect, excellence, and lifelong learning, and by honoring those who have paved the way with grace and wisdom; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proud to join in the celebration of the Center's 50th Anniversary, and to recognize the exceptional individuals being honored at this milestone occasion; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 2025 Summer Social of the Alpha Phi Alpha Senior Citizens Center, to be held on June 28, 2025, in celebration of 50 years of excellence, and to honor Elizabeth Gill, Dr. Carol M. Grant, and Adam O'Neil for their remarkable service and contributions to the senior community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Alpha Phi Alpha Senior Citizens Center and the 2025 honorees.
