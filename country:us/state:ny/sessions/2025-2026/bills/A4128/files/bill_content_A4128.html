        AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to permitting funeral
          directors to sell insurance
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. The public health law is amended by adding  a  new  section
     2  3445 to read as follows:
     3    §  3445. Funeral directing; sale of insurance. A funeral establishment
     4  may provide for the sale of insurance by a person licensed under article
     5  eleven of the insurance law. Such  licensed  person  may  be  a  funeral
     6  director.
     7    §  2.  Paragraph  (l)  of  subdivision 1 of section 3450 of the public
     8  health law, as amended by chapter 642 of the laws of 2019, is amended to
     9  read as follows:
    10    (l) has paid, given, has caused to be paid or given or offered to  pay
    11  or  to  give  to any person a commission or other valuable consideration
    12  for the solicitation or procurement, either directly or  indirectly,  of
    13  funeral  patronage, or has accepted any consideration (including without
    14  limitation a commission, rebate, or discount or direct or indirect price
    15  reduction on merchandise from the current value thereof) from any finan-
    16  cial institution or trust company,  or  agent  thereof,  with  whom  the
    17  funeral  director deposits funds paid for funeral services in advance of
    18  need pursuant to subdivision one of section four hundred fifty-three  of
    19  the  general  business  law except that the funeral director may accept,
    20  from the financial institution, trust company or agent  thereof,  up  to
    21  twenty-five  hundredths of one percent of the fee authorized under para-
    22  graph (b) of subdivision three of section four  hundred  fifty-three  of
    23  the  general  business  law  for administrative services provided by the
    24  funeral director, or has accepted any consideration  (including  without
    25  limitation  a  commission or gift) from any insurer or agent thereof, to
