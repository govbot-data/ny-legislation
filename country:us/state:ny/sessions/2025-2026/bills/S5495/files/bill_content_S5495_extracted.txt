HARCKHAM -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Labor AN ACT to amend the labor law, in relation to establishing a model neurodiversity awareness training program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The labor law is amended by adding a new section 201-j to 2 read as follows: 3 § 201-j. Neurodiversity awareness training. 1. a. The department shall 4 consult with the division of human rights and the department of health 5 to produce a model training program on neurodiversity awareness. 6 b. Such model neurodiversity awareness training program shall be 7 interactive and include: 8 (i) a definition, explanation, and examples of neurodiversity consist- 9 ent with guidance issued by the department in consultation with the 10 division of human rights and the department of health; 11 (ii) examples of conduct designed to help participants understand and 12 appreciate the differences in cognitive processing and neurodevelopmen- 13 tal conditions of neurodiverse individuals; 14 (iii) information concerning best practices to avoid inequity and 15 foster a safe, supportive workplace for neurodiverse individuals; 16 (iv) information regarding the impact ignorance of neurodiversity may 17 have on individuals across the state; 18 (v) information concerning employees' rights of redress and all avail- 19 able forums for adjudicating complaints; and 20 (vi) any other information the department, the division of human 21 rights and the department of health deems relevant and appropriate for 22 such a course. 23 c. The model training program created pursuant to this section, and 24 any equivalent program shall be reviewed and reapproved by the depart- 25 ment and the division of human rights on a biennial basis.
