        AN ACT to amend the education law, in  relation  to  permitting  schools
          located within the state to purchase certain New York milk
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section  915-a
     2  to read as follows:
     3    § 915-a. Milk options. 1. As used in this section, the following terms
     4  shall have the following meanings:
     5    (a)  "New  York  milk"  shall mean raw milk produced by the milking of
     6  cows physically located within the geographic boundaries of  the  state,
     7  transported to a dairy processing facility located within the geographic
     8  boundaries  of  the  state  and  processed as fluid milk into containers
     9  intended for distribution to consumers.
    10    (b) "Reduced fat New York milk" shall mean New  York  milk  containing
    11  two  percent  milk  fat.   The term includes New York milk that has been
    12  flavored.
    13    (c) "Whole New York milk" shall mean New York milk containing at least
    14  three percent milk fat. The term includes New York milk  that  has  been
    15  flavored.
    16    2.  Notwithstanding  any  other  provision of law or regulation to the
    17  contrary, a school board or other governing entity of a  school  located
    18  in  the  state may elect to utilize funds from state or local sources to
    19  obtain whole New York milk or reduced fat New York milk  to  provide  or
    20  sell at such school.
    21    3.  The  commissioner  of education shall notify the superintendent or
    22  chief administrator of each school in the state  of  the  provisions  of
    23  this section.
    24    4.  The  attorney  general shall, on behalf of a school located within
    25  the state, bring a civil action against the federal  government  or  any

    23  results in the availability of whole milk or reduced fat milk,  flavored
    24  or unflavored, in elementary and secondary schools in the state.
    25    2.  An  update to the Dietary Guidelines for Americans published under

    28  availability of whole milk or reduced fat milk, flavored or  unflavored,
    29  in elementary and secondary schools in the state.
