S04665 Summary:

SAME ASNo Same As

Amd §54, St Fin L
 
Establishes a local government salt brine equipment grant program under which the secretary of state may award matching grants to municipalities to cover costs associated with purchasing salt brine equipment.

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-

     1    Section  1.  Subdivision  10 of section 54 of the state finance law is
     2  amended by adding a new paragraph w to read as follows:
     3    w. Local government salt brine equipment grant program.  (1)  For  the
     4  purposes of this paragraph, the term "total expenditures" shall mean the

     7    (2) Within the annual amounts appropriated therefor, the secretary  of

    13  of  the  capital  improvement  project  under  the grant approved by the

    22  ment shall so notify the applicant. Applicants  shall  be  permitted  to
    23  amend  an application found to be missing information, and such applica-
