The veterans' services law is amended by adding a new 2 section 29-f to read as follows: 3 § 29-f. Voluntary veterans' registry. 1. The department shall estab- 4 lish a voluntary veterans' registry for the purpose of providing infor- 5 mation on federal, state, and local governmental benefits, programs, and 6 services available to veterans, and to link veterans with resources that 7 can provide assistance. 8 2. For the purposes of this section, the following terms shall have 9 the following meanings: 10 (a) "State agency" means any department, board, bureau, division, 11 commission, council, or committee within the executive branch, the state 12 university of New York, the city university of New York, or any public 13 authority under control of the executive branch of the state of New 14 York. 15 (b) "Voluntary veterans' registry" or "registry" means the voluntary 16 veterans' registry established pursuant to subdivision one of this 17 section. 18 (c) "State veterans' service agency" means a state veterans' service 19 agency established pursuant to section thirteen of this article. 20 (d) "Local veterans' service agency" means a local veterans' service 21 agency established pursuant to section fourteen of this article. 22 3. Upon a veteran's registration with the registry, if permission is 23 granted by such veteran, such veteran's information shall be shared with 24 other state agencies, state veterans' service agencies, and local veter- 25 ans' service agencies. 26 4.
