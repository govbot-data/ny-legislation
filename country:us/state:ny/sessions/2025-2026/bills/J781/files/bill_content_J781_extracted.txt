Caemerrer Park in Albertson, New York; the new box helps the community to respectfully retire damaged or old flags, ensuring they are properly disposed of with dignity; he also led a team of fellow scouts in beautifying the park's entrance by restoring a fence, a walkway, and adding vibrant greenery to the landscape; and WHEREAS, Matthew Rapa began his scouting journey in 2013 as a Wolf in Pack 202 before continuing with Troop 201 in 2017; and WHEREAS, Throughout his path to Eagle Scout, Matthew Rapa attended seven summer camps, served as Den Chief, Instructor, Assistant Patrol Leader, and Patrol Leader of the Gorilla Patrol; he earned a total of 28 merit badges and the special awards of Kayaking BSA, Snorkeling BSA, and 50-Miler; and WHEREAS, Matthew Rapa was fortunate to attend all four High Adventure Trips; he served as Chaplain's Aid at Northern Tier in 2024, as a Crew Member for Philmont in 2023, Sea Base Out Island in 2022, and Summit in 2021, which earned him the Grand Slam of National High Adventure Award; and WHEREAS, His dedication to the safety and well-being of the citizens of Nassau County is represented through his project and demonstrates his devotion to both community and commitment to others; and WHEREAS, As he enters the next chapters of his life and is met with limitless opportunities, Matthew Rapa plans to continue to uphold the ideals and principals of Scouting America; and WHEREAS, Matthew Rapa's perseverance in rising through the ranks to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of his ability to master difficult tasks; his accomplishment will enable him to emerge as a leader, fully equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school, his community, his State and his Nation, and no doubt his future will be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Matthew Rapa on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Matthew Rapa.
