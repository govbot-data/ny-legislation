Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01785 Summary:BILL NOJ01785  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORMYRIE COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01785 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1785 BY: Senator MYRIE CONGRATULATING the Medgar Evers College Men's and Women's Indoor Track and Field Teams upon the occasion of capturing the 2026 CUNYAC Indoor Track and Field Championships on March 1, 2026 WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly sought after in collegiate sports; this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Medgar Evers College Men's and Women's Indoor Track and Field Teams upon the occasion of capturing the 2026 CUNYAC Indoor Track and Field Championships on Sunday, March 1, 2026, at the Nike Track & Field Center at The Armory in New York, New York; and WHEREAS, To the praise and applause of their excited fans, the Medgar Evers College Men's and Women's Indoor Track and Field Teams have captured their third consecutive CUNYAC Championship; and WHEREAS, The Men's team worked as a team to earn 169 points by the end of the meet, followed by Hunter College with 114 points; the Women's team finished with 168 points, surpassing Hunter's 150 as the runner-up; and WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's colors of black and gold, family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Cougars throughout their journey as they ended their seasons with impressive overall records; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as track and field, which demands athletic prowess, speed, and agility, Head Coach Hugh Reid and Assistant Coach Aaron Dickens worked hard to hone the skills of these championship teams, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the track; and WHEREAS, Coach Hugh Reid, Assistant Coach Aaron Dickens, and all the outstanding athletes on the Medgar Evers College Men's and Women's Indoor Track and Field Teams have clearly utilized dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Medgar Evers College Men's and Women's Indoor Track and Field Teams: David Awaye, Joash Beaton, Zachary Bussey, David Chisholm, Matthew Fleming, Pharell Fowler, Reco Griffith, Omari Hinds, Jahiem Johnson, Leshaun Miller, Chrisnel Nicolas, Michael Ortega, Nathan Sanderson, Ahmed Toure, Mario Wilson, Zion Young-baez, Martiza Argueta, Rinnah Brown, Nia Butler, Moutiatou Djibring, Keyona Evans, Kenia Irish-Bramble, Jada Lambert, Danajah Lawrence, Brianna Lindo, Jennifer Metra, Nana Samake, Thenica Thompson, and Sarah Vallon; Head Coach Hugh Reid; and Assistant Coach Aaron Dickens upon the occasion of capturing the 2026 CUNYAC Indoor Track and Field Championships on March 1, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Medgar Evers College Men's and Women's Indoor Track and Field Teams, Head Coach Hugh Reid, and Assistant Coach Aaron Dickens.
