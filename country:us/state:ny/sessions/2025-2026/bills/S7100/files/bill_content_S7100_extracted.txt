The area managed by the department of environmental conser- 2 vation known as Oakwood Beach wetland, located in Richmond county, shall 3 after the effective date of this act be known as the Oakwood Beach-De- 4 tective Russel Timoshenko Preserve and shall be suitably marked in a 5 manner prescribed by the commissioner of environmental conservation. 6 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
