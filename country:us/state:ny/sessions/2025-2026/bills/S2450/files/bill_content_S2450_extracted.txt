Section 1.03 of the mental hygiene law is amended by adding 2 a new subdivision 60 to read as follows: 3 60. "Gaming disorder" means a disorder due to addictive behavior, as 4 defined in the most recent edition of the international classification 5 of diseases (ICD), published by the World Health Organization. 6 § 2.
