SKOUFIS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Local Government AN ACT to amend the real property tax law, in relation to subjecting certain state lands to real property taxation The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The section heading and subdivision 1 of section 534 of the 2 real property tax law, the section heading as amended by chapter 736 of 3 the laws of 1962 and subdivision 1 as amended by chapter 351 of the laws 4 of 1977, are amended to read as follows: 5 Certain state lands subject to taxation for all purposes except 6 certain county purposes. 1. Lands owned by the state acquired for 7 reforestation purposes pursuant to section 9-0501 of the environmental 8 conservation law shall be subject to taxation for all purposes except 9 county purposes for all counties other than Orange county. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
