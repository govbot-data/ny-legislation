Jensen CONGRATULATING the Hilton High School Competitive Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHSAA Class B State Championship on March 7, 2026 WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly sought after in high school sports; this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Hilton High School Competitive Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHSAA Class B State Championship on Saturday, March 7, 2026, Visions Veterans Memorial Arena in Binghamton, New York; and WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's colors of red and white, family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Hilton High School Competitive Cheerleading Team throughout their journey as they ended their season with an impressive overall record; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as cheerleading, which demands athletic prowess, speed, and agility, Head Coach Michelle Caamano and her skilled coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both in and out of competitions; and WHEREAS, Head Coach Michelle Caamano and all the outstanding athletes on the Hilton High School Competitive Cheerleading Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Hilton High School Competitive Cheerleading Team: Kalei Brunson, Leah Dalle, Lillyann Dalle, Lia Debay, Kylie Dool, Irelyn Dooley, Grace Hereth, Mia Hicks, Ava Howell, Malena Kuzmanovski, Payton Lauber, Karleigh Lazarek, Morgan Maloney, Ellyson Pringle, Emily Sachs, Sophia Sciortino, Ava Sherwood, Adriana Sullivan, Olivia Verhey, Emma Ward; Head Coach Michelle Caamano; and Assistant Coach Samantha Grosodonia on their outstanding season and winning the NYSPHSAA Class B State Title; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Hilton High School Competitive Cheerleading Team, Head Coach Michelle Caamano, and Assistant Coach Samantha Grosodonia.
