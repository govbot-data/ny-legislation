Lam upon the occasion of receiving the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout, the most prestigious of Scouting honors, on Saturday, June 20, 2026 WHEREAS, Ethan P. Lam through his diligence and rigorous efforts, has achieved Scouting's highest and most prestigious rank, Eagle Scout; the Eagle Court of Honor ceremony to be held on Saturday, June 20, 2026, at Leonard's Palazzo in Great Neck, New York; and WHEREAS, Ethan P. Lam is a member of Great Neck Scout Troop 10; and WHEREAS, America has achieved her greatest heights of glory through the dedication, determination and tireless efforts of the outstanding citizens of this Nation; and WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain her glory; and WHEREAS, Scouting America and its members are dedicated to the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in Scouting, earned by approximately five percent of all Scouts, is the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout; and WHEREAS, This outstanding young man has been involved in Scouting for many years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all of the necessary achievements, including completion of a community service project, which was successfully planned, developed and executed; and WHEREAS, His dedication to the safety and well-being of the citizens of Great Neck is represented through his projects and demonstrates his devotion to both community and commitment to others; and WHEREAS, As he goes forward in his journey, Ethan P. Lam plans on continuing the ideals and principles of Scouting America; and WHEREAS, Ethan P. Lam's perseverance in rising through the ranks to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of his ability to master difficult tasks; his accomplishment will enable him to emerge as a leader, fully equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school, his community, his State and his Nation, and no doubt his future will be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Ethan P. Lam on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Ethan P. Lam.
