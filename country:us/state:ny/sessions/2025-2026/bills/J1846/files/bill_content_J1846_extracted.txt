and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Bangladesh-USA Chamber of Commerce and Industry (BUCCI) and USA-Bangladesh Business Links upon the occasion of hosting the 5th Annual Bangladesh Remittance Fair on April 17-18, 2026, in Jackson Heights, New York; and WHEREAS, The State of New York is home to a vibrant and diverse population, enriched by the contributions of individuals from all corners of the world, including the Bengali-speaking diaspora; and WHEREAS, New York City is recognized as a hub of cultural diversity and the largest concentration of Bengali-speaking individuals outside of Bangladesh and West Bengal, many of whom have made significant contributions to the social, cultural, and economic fabric of our State; and WHEREAS, Bangladesh, a nation with a rich history and cultural heritage, is the seventh-largest recipient of remittances in the world, a testament to the strong global connections of its people; and WHEREAS, The United States of America, and particularly the Bengali-speaking diaspora residing in New York, play a pivotal role in this global network, contributing nearly $3 billion annually in remittances to Bangladesh; and WHEREAS, These remittances are a cornerstone of Bangladesh's economy, bolstering foreign exchange reserves, supporting national income, and fostering economic stability in the current political and economic landscape; and WHEREAS, The Bangladesh-USA Chamber of Commerce and Industry and USA-Bangladesh Business Links, an organization dedicated to fostering business connections between the United States and Bangladesh through trade collaboration and strategic networking, have been instrumental in establishing the Bangladesh Remittance Fair, a groundbreaking initiative designed to bridge the gap between Bangladesh and the Bangladeshi diaspora across the United States; and WHEREAS, The Bangladesh Remittance Fair focuses on enhancing the remittance experience for both senders and receivers by providing a comprehensive view of options to make remittances more efficient, secure, and cost-effective; and WHEREAS, The Fair brings together key players from the banking, financial, and digital sectors, along with government agencies, development organizations, and community leaders, creating a dynamic ecosystem for sharing knowledge, solutions, and opportunities for collaboration; and WHEREAS, Through this initiative, the Bangladesh Remittance Fair fosters a stronger bond between the people of Bangladesh and the world, ensuring that every dollar sent home makes a meaningful impact on the lives of recipients and the broader economy; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper to recognize and honor the efforts of these organizations for their dedication and leadership in fostering economic and cultural ties between New York and Bangladesh; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Bangladesh-USA Chamber of Commerce and Industry and USA-Bangladesh Business Links for their efforts in establishing the Bangladesh Remittance Fair and to acknowledge the profound impact of their work in fostering connections and promoting economic growth; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Bangladesh-USA Chamber of Commerce and Industry and USA-Bangladesh Business Links.
