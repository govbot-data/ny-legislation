AN ACT to amend the correction law, in relation to prohibiting the
obscuring of body-worn or stationary cameras in correctional insti-
tutions
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The correction law is amended by adding a new section 127
2 to read as follows:
3 § 127. Obscuring body-worn or stationary cameras. No employee of the
4 department or a local correctional facility shall willfully obstruct,
5 block or otherwise obscure a body-worn or stationary camera from record-
6 ing within a correctional facility for the purpose of hindering an
7 existing or potential investigation, concealing a crime or criminal
8 activity, or concealing otherwise unlawful behavior. An employee who
9 violates the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemea-
10 nor.
11 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
12 have become a law.
