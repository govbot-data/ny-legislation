Senate Resolution No. 2310
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
        COMMENDING  Silver  Queen  Chapter  No. 43, Prince
        Hall Order of Eastern Star,  upon  the  occasion  of
        hosting its All White Affair on July 12, 2026
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose   and   worthy
accomplishment whose enduring commitment to sisterhood, service, and the
traditions  of  the  Prince  Hall Order of Eastern Star has enriched the
civic and spiritual life of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Silver Queen Chapter No. 43, Prince Hall Order of Eastern  Star,
upon  the  occasion  of hosting its All White Affair on Sunday, July 12,
2026, at The Pavilion in Staten Island, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The dedicated sisters and brothers of Silver Queen  Chapter
No.    43  are  bound together by the five-pointed star and the timeless
teachings drawn from the  lives  of  Adah,  Ruth,  Esther,  Martha,  and
Electa,  whose  stories of loyalty, constancy, faith, love, and fidelity
speak across the centuries to the enduring values of the Order; and
 
  WHEREAS, Silver Queen Chapter No. 43 has served its  community  with
distinction,  dedication, and an unwavering commitment to the principles
of the Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star  for  81  years,  a  legacy  of
sisterhood  and service that stands as one of the finest in the New York
jurisdiction and a testament to the enduring power of women and  men  of
conscience coming together in common purpose; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Chapter  is  led  with  grace,  vision, and exemplary
devotion by Worthy Matron Cynthia L. Chadwick and Worthy  Patron  Rhonny
King,  whose  leadership  reflects  the  highest ideals of the Order and
whose stewardship of this Chapter honors the 81 years  of  service  upon
which they now build; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  All  White  Affair  is a beloved tradition within the
Eastern Star family, a celebration of unity,  elegance,  and  fellowship
that brings together sisters, brothers, families, and guests in a spirit
of  joy  and  communal  pride,  honoring  the bonds of the Order and the
shared commitment to service that unites all who wear the star; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is  proudly  moved  to  honor  Silver
Queen  Chapter  No. 43, Worthy Matron Cynthia L. Chadwick, Worthy Patron
Rhonny King, and all officers and members whose 81 years of faithful and
dedicated service represent the living  tradition  of  the  Prince  Hall
Order of Eastern Star at its finest; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Silver Queen Chapter No. 43, Prince Hall Order of Eastern  Star,
upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  its All White Affair on July 12, 2026,
celebrating 81 years of  service,  and  to  extend  congratulations  and
deepest appreciation to Worthy Matron Cynthia L. Chadwick, Worthy Patron
Rhonny King, and the entire Chapter family; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Worthy Matron Cynthia L. Chadwick, Worthy  Patron  Rhonny
King,  and  the  officers  and  members  of Silver Queen Chapter No. 43,
Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star, State of New York, in recognition  of
81 years of steadfast commitment to the principles and traditions of the
Order.
