Chadwick and Worthy Patron Rhonny King, whose leadership reflects the highest ideals of the Order and whose stewardship of this Chapter honors the 81 years of service upon which they now build; and WHEREAS, The All White Affair is a beloved tradition within the Eastern Star family, a celebration of unity, elegance, and fellowship that brings together sisters, brothers, families, and guests in a spirit of joy and communal pride, honoring the bonds of the Order and the shared commitment to service that unites all who wear the star; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor Silver Queen Chapter No. 43, Worthy Matron Cynthia L. Chadwick, Worthy Patron Rhonny King, and all officers and members whose 81 years of faithful and dedicated service represent the living tradition of the Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star at its finest; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Silver Queen Chapter No. 43, Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star, upon the occasion of hosting its All White Affair on July 12, 2026, celebrating 81 years of service, and to extend congratulations and deepest appreciation to Worthy Matron Cynthia L. Chadwick, Worthy Patron Rhonny King, and the entire Chapter family; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Worthy Matron Cynthia L. Chadwick, Worthy Patron Rhonny King, and the officers and members of Silver Queen Chapter No. 43, Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star, State of New York, in recognition of 81 years of steadfast commitment to the principles and traditions of the Order.
