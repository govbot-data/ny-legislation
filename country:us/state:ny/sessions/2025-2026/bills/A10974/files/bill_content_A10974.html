Add §404-ff, V & T L; add §95-l, St Fin L
 
Relates to establishing distinctive "Women Veterans" plates and the women veterans support fund; establishes that moneys of the fund will be used for women veteran homelessness, counseling and other programs that support women veterans.

        Introduced  by M. of A. MEEKS -- read once and referred to the Committee

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to establishing
          distinctive "Women Veterans" license plates; and to  amend  the  state
          finance  law,  in relation to establishing the "women veterans support
          fund"
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The  vehicle  and  traffic law is amended by adding a new
     2  section 404-ff to read as follows:
     3    § 404-ff. Distinctive "Women Veterans" license plates. 1.  Any  person
     4  residing  in  this  state  shall,  upon request, be issued a distinctive
     5  "Women Veterans" license plate of a design approved by the commissioner.
     6  Application for said license plate shall be  filed  with  the  form  and
     7  detail as the commissioner shall prescribe.
     8    2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued
     9  in  the same manner as other number plates upon the payment of the regu-
    10  lar registration fee prescribed by section  four  hundred  one  of  this
    11  article,  provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of
    12  twenty-five dollars shall be charged for  such  plate.  Such  additional
    13  service charge shall be deposited pursuant to section four hundred four-
    14  oo  of  this  article  to the credit of the department of motor vehicles
    15  distinctive plate development fund, established by section ninety-five-g
    16  of the state finance law, and shall be used for the production,  design,
    17  advertising and marketing of distinctive number plates; provided, howev-
    18  er,  that  additional  annual service charges collected in excess of six
    19  thousand dollars shall be deposited to the credit of the women  veterans
    20  support  fund established pursuant to section ninety-five-l of the state
    21  finance law.
    22    § 2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 95-l  to
    23  read as follows:
    24    § 95-l. Women veterans support fund. 1. There is hereby established in
    25  the  joint  custody  of the commissioner of taxation and finance and the

     1  comptroller, a special fund to be known as the "women  veterans  support

     3    2. Such fund shall consist of all moneys collected pursuant to section
     4  four  hundred  four-ff  of  the  vehicle  and traffic law, and all other

     7    3. Moneys of the fund shall be expended by the New York state division

    10  support women veterans.
    11    §  3. 1. A distinctive plate established pursuant to section 404-ff of
    12  the vehicle and traffic law, as added by section one of this act,  shall
    13  only  be  designed, produced and issued upon the delivery to the depart-
    14  ment of motor vehicles of a surety bond in the amount  of  six  thousand
    15  dollars,  which  shall be executed by a surety company authorized by the
    16  department of financial services to transact  business  in  this  state.

    19  hundred  orders  for such distinctive plate together with the additional
    20  annual service charge applicable to each  such  order,  which  shall  be
    21  non-refundable,  no such surety bond shall be required. All such service
    22  charges shall be deposited pursuant to the provisions of section  404-oo
    23  of  the vehicle and traffic law to the credit of the department of motor
    24  vehicles distinctive plate development fund established by section  95-g
    25  of  the  state finance law and shall be used for the design, production,
    26  advertising and distribution of distinctive license plates in accordance
    27  with such section 95-g.
    28    2. If, upon the expiration of two years following the date upon  which
    29  distinctive  plates  in  the  series  are  first available for sale, two
    30  hundred or more sets of such plates are sold, a bond delivered  pursuant
    31  to this section shall be discontinued. If fewer than two hundred sets of
    32  such  plates  are  sold  by  such time, the department of motor vehicles
    33  shall be entitled to recover against the bond an amount proportionate to
    34  such shortfall.
    35    § 4. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after
    36  it shall have become a law; provided, however,  that  section  three  of
    37  this  act  shall  take  effect immediately.   Effective immediately, the
    38  addition, amendment and/or repeal of any rule  or  regulation  necessary
    39  for  the implementation of this act on its effective date are authorized
    40  to be made and completed on or before such effective date.
