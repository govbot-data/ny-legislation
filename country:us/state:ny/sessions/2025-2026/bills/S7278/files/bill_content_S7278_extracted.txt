SCARCELLA-SPANTON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law and chapter 47 of the laws of 1931 relating to declaring the policy of the states of New York and New Jersey in regard to certain vehicular bridges and tunnels within the port of New York district, in relation to requiring certain public authorities to establish a cashless tolling amnesty program; and providing for the repeal of certain provisions upon expiration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 386-c to read as follows: 3 § 386-c. Cashless tolling amnesty program. 1. Notwithstanding any 4 other provisions of this title or any other law, the authority shall 5 provide an amnesty program for any person, firm, corporation, or other 6 entity charged with the payment of a cashless toll fee for the use of 7 any highway, bridge or tunnel operated by the authority between April 8 twenty-third, two thousand sixteen and June twenty-ninth, two thousand 9 twenty-five. 10 2. Such amnesty program shall allow any person, firm, corporation, or 11 other entity charged with the payment of a cashless toll fee for the use 12 of any highway, bridge or tunnel operated by the authority to pay such 13 tolls in full and to have any toll violation fees and penalties related 14 to such paid tolls waived; provided such payment is made on and after 15 August first, two thousand twenty-five and on or before December thir- 16 ty-first, two thousand twenty-five. 17 3.
