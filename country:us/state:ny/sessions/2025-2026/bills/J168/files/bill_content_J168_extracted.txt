and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Sitrin Medical Rehabilitation Center upon the occasion of celebrating its 25th Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Sitrin Medical Rehabilitation Center of New Hartford, New York, offers memberships for its Wellness Center to not only former patients but also the public; with its state-of-the-art equipment and two heated swimming pools, the facility provides optimal care for those individuals who are recovering from surgery or injury; and WHEREAS, Former patients who have worked with physical therapists have access to the same equipment and seamlessly continue their prescribed treatment plans; Sitrin Medical Rehabilitation Center features cardiovascular, aerobic, and weightlifting equipment to serve an expansive clientele and their various requirements for long-term recovery; and WHEREAS, The only board-certified cardiovascular and pulmonary clinical physical therapy specialist in the New Hartford region suggests the continuation of exercises he prescribes to his patients even upon discharge from his care; Sitrin Medical Rehabilitation Center provides a level of comfort in addition to superior service; and WHEREAS, For 25 years, Sitrin Medical Rehabilitation Center has improved the health of patients who have required assistance with recovery, meeting their needs with compassion and dedication from the staff members who are devoted to individuals' well-being; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that when individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Sitrin Medical Rehabilitation Center, upon the occasion of celebrating its 25th Anniversary, and to honor its members, past and present, who have labored so conscientiously to secure and improve the quality of life for the members of the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sitrin Medical Rehabilitation Center.
