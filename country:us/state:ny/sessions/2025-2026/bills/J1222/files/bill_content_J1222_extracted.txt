(MIPO) is a nonprofit based in Brooklyn, New York, whose mission is to empower immigrant and underprivileged youth and their families by providing sustainable skills, mentoring, scholarship opportunities, life skills training, and educational support; and WHEREAS, Since its founding, MIPO has uplifted communities through its signature annual fundraising gala, now in its 14th year, which highlights career achievements, educational attainment, scholarship recipients, and the enduring power of collaboration; and WHEREAS, The 2025 Gala will spotlight honorees inducted into the MIPO Hall of Fame whose commitment and success exemplify the organization's spirit and values; and WHEREAS, This annual event showcases not only MIPO's achievements but also functions as a dynamic platform for cultivating partnerships among community leaders, supporters, and stakeholders to further educational and economic opportunities for youth; and WHEREAS, It is fitting that this Legislative Body recognize Making the Impossible Possible, Inc. for its sustained contributions to community enrichment and acknowledge the impact of its 14th Gala; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Making the Impossible Possible, Inc. upon the occasion of its 14th Power of Partnerships, Scholarships & Hall of Fame Gala, to be held October 5, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, duly engrossed, be transmitted to Making the Impossible Possible, Inc. as a token of this Legislative Body's highest admiration and support.
