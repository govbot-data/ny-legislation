MARTINS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Local Government AN ACT in relation to removing library taxes of the Shelter Rock library district from real property located in that portion of such library district that is located within the Bryant library district The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Shelter 2 Rock library district, located in the town of North Hempstead, county of 3 Nassau, shall not impose any library tax for the support of the library 4 district upon any parcel of real property located within such library 5 district which is also located within the Bryant library district and 6 subject to real property taxes to support the Bryant library district. 7 For all purposes, any real property removed from the library portion of 8 Shelter Rock library district taxes pursuant to this act shall be deemed 9 to be within the service area of the Bryant library district established 10 pursuant to an absolute charter granted by the board of regents on 11 behalf of the education department and shall be subject to taxation for 12 the support of such district. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall apply to assess- 14 ment rolls prepared on the basis of taxable status dates occurring on or 15 after such date.
