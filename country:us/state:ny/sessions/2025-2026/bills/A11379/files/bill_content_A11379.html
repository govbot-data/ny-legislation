SAME ASSAME AS S08690
 
SPONSORRules (Wright)
 
COSPNSR

Amd §64, ABC L
 
Exempts certain property from the prohibition of alcohol sales within a certain distance from a church for a certain premises located in the county of New York.

                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          11379
 
                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                      May 15, 2026
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by  COMMITTEE ON RULES -- (at request of M. of A. Wright) --
          read once and referred to the Committee on Economic Development
 
        AN ACT to amend the alcoholic beverage control law, in  relation  to  an
          exemption  for  certain property from the prohibition of alcohol sales
          within a certain distance from a church
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivision  7  of  section  64 of the alcoholic beverage
     2  control law is amended by adding a  new  paragraph  (e-18)  to  read  as
     3  follows:
     4    (e-18)  Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph (a) of this subdi-
     5  vision, the  authority  may  issue  a  retail  license  for  on-premises
     6  consumption  for  a  premises  which shall be located within two hundred
     7  feet of a building occupied exclusively as a school,  church,  synagogue
     8  or other place of worship, provided such premises constitutes a premises
     9  for the sale of food or beverages at retail for consumption on the prem-
    10  ises  located  wholly  within  the boundaries of the county of New York,
    11  bounded and described as follows:
    12    All that certain piece or parcel of land, situate, lying and being  in
    13  the Borough of Manhattan, City, County and State of New York, designated
    14  on the Tax Map of the City of New York, for the Borough of Manhattan, as
    15  said  Tax Map was on June 5, 1973 as Section 7 Block 1912 Lot 30 bounded
    16  and described as follows:
    17    BEGINNING at a point on the westerly side of Lenox Avenue, distant  20
    18  feet  Northerly from the corner formed by the intersection of the North-
    19  erly line of West 127th Street with the Westerly line of Lenox (formerly
    20  Sixth) Avenue;
    21    RUNNING THENCE Northerly along the Westerly line of Lenox  Avenue,  20
    22  feet;
    23    THENCE  Westerly  parallel  with  West  127th  Street  and part of the
    24  distance through a party wall, 100 feet;
    25    THENCE and Southerly parallel with Lenox Avenue, 20 feet;
    26    THENCE Easterly, 100 feet to the Westerly side  of  Lenox  Avenue  the
    27  point and place of BEGINNING.
    28    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
