ASHBY, BORRELLO, OBERACKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the executive law and the education law, in relation to establishing the school safety specialist act The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "school 2 safety specialist act". 3 § 2. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 844 to read 4 as follows: 5 § 844. School safety specialist certification program. 1. The division 6 shall establish and maintain a certification program for school safety 7 specialists, including: 8 (a) development of such program and its content and methodology; 9 (b) creation of the application and certification process; and 10 (c) maintenance and administration of such program. 11 2. Topics of training for school safety specialists shall include, but 12 not be limited to: how to prevent, prepare, and respond to school safety 13 incidents; how to create a culture of school safety; emergency prepared- 14 ness planning; school threat assessment; situational awareness; strate- 15 gic crisis leadership and risk communication; school-based mental health 16 mediation; drug prevention and awareness; cyberbullying; predatory sexu- 17 al behavior; substance use; violence; suicide prevention and mediation; 18 and gang resistance. 19 § 3. The education law is amended by adding a new section 2801-c to 20 read as follows: 21 § 2801-c. School safety specialist. 1. Any public or nonpublic school 22 may employ a school safety specialist certified pursuant to section 23 eight hundred forty-four of the executive law, for the purpose of 24 providing improved public safety and security on school grounds.
