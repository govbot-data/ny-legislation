Senate Resolution No. 1167
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
        HONORING  Justin  Davis  upon  the occasion of his
        induction into the Rome Sports Hall of Fame on  July
        27, 2025
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
outstanding  athletes  who  have  distinguished themselves through their
exceptional performance, attaining unprecedented success and the highest
level of personal achievement; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Justin  Davis  upon  the  occasion of his induction into the Rome Sports
Hall of Fame, to be celebrated on July 27, 2025; and
 
  WHEREAS, Justin Davis is the proud product of a  family  steeped  in
athletic  and  coaching  excellence;  his  father,  Mike  Davis,  was  a
three-sport standout at Rome Free Academy (RFA) and  long-time  football
coach,  while  his  mother, Debbie Davis, devoted many years to coaching
junior high school track and field; and
 
  WHEREAS, Raised on the sidelines, Justin Davis absorbed  the  values
of  teamwork, discipline, and leadership from an early age, which he put
into action as a  three-sport  varsity  athlete  at  RFA,  lettering  in
football, hockey, and baseball; and
 
  WHEREAS,  On  the  football  field,  Justin  Davis  was a three-year
starter who set an RFA school  record  for  career  interceptions  (16),
earned  the  prestigious  Black R, and was named one of New York State's
Top 50 players in 1996 after  leading  the  Black  Knights  to  a  state
championship game appearance; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Equally  accomplished  on  the ice, Justin Davis served as
co-captain of the hockey team and was  a  two-time  leading  scorer  who
earned All-League honors for his performance and leadership; and
 
  WHEREAS,  His  outstanding  high school career earned him a football
scholarship to the University  of  Maine,  where  he  was  a  three-year
starter  at linebacker and a three-time recipient of the team's academic
achievement  award,  exemplifying  excellence  in  both  athletics   and
academics; and
 
  WHEREAS,  After  graduating  in  2000,  Justin Davis transitioned to
coaching, beginning as  a  graduate  assistant  at  Wagner  College  and
Louisiana  Tech  where  he earned his master's degree before going on to
serve as an assistant at the University of Toledo; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 2008, Justin Davis joined  the  coaching  staff  at  the
United  States  Naval  Academy,  where  over the course of 12 seasons he
played an instrumental role in the success of  the  Midshipmen  football
program,  coaching  various  defensive  units  and serving as one of the
program's top recruiters; and
 
  WHEREAS, During his tenure at Navy, the team experienced one of  its
most  successful  eras,  with  10  bowl game appearances, victories over
rival Army, a dramatic triple-overtime win over Notre Dame, and multiple
Top 25 national rankings, all underscoring his effectiveness as  both  a
strategist and mentor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Justin  Davis  has brought great credit to his alma mater,
his hometown, and the many young athletes he has guided over the  years,
and  his  induction  into  the Rome Sports Hall of Fame is a well-earned
recognition of  a  life  dedicated  to  sportsmanship,  leadership,  and
excellence; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Justin Davis upon the occasion of  his  induction  into  the  Rome
Sports Hall of Fame on July 27, 2025, applauding his remarkable athletic
accomplishments,  coaching achievements, and continued commitment to the
values of teamwork and service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Justin Davis.
