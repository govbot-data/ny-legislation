AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring
all persons less than sixteen years of age to provide proof of age for
the purchase of bicycles with electric assist, electric scooters, and
class A and class B limited use motorcycles; and establishing penal-
ties therefor
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 1242 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 11-a to read as follows:
3 11-a. No person, firm, association or corporation engaged in the busi-
4 ness of selling or leasing bicycles with electric assist shall sell or
5 lease any bicycle with electric assist to any person less than sixteen
6 years of age. Valid proof of age shall be required at the point of
7 purchase.
8 § 2. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new section
9 1290 to read as follows:
10 § 1290. Additional provisions applicable to electric scooters. No
11 person, firm, association or corporation engaged in the business of
12 selling or leasing electric scooters shall sell or lease an electric
13 scooter to any person less than sixteen years of age. Valid proof of age
14 shall be required at the point of purchase.
15 § 3. Section 2261 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding
16 a new subdivision 7 to read as follows:
17 7. Sale of class A and class B limited use motorcycles to minors
18 prohibited. No person, firm, association or corporation engaged in the
19 business of selling limited use motorcycles shall sell a class A limited
20 use motorcycle or class B limited use motorcycle, as defined by section
21 one hundred twenty-one-b of this chapter, to any person less than
22 sixteen years of age. Valid proof of age shall be required at the point
23 of purchase.
