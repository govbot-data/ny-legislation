WOERNER -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Racing and Wagering AN ACT to amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, in relation to issuing temporary permits to certain fantasy sports opera- tors The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 1402 of the racing, pari-mutuel 2 wagering and breeding law, as added by chapter 237 of the laws of 2016, 3 is amended and subdivision 1 is amended by adding a new paragraph (c) to 4 read as follows: 5 (c) Additional interactive fantasy sports operators, who were not 6 offering contests to persons located in New York prior to the tenth of 7 November, two thousand fifteen, may apply for a temporary permit subject 8 to approval by the gaming commission, provided that such operator meets 9 all the requirements of this article. A temporary permit issued under 10 this paragraph shall be deemed effective as of the date that the opera- 11 tor first offered interactive fantasy sports contests in the state, 12 provided that the operator remits the tax provided in section one thou- 13 sand four hundred seven of this article within thirty days of issuance 14 of the temporary permit for all contests the operator previously offered 15 in the state. 16 2. The commission shall provide a temporary permit [to each operator 17 that was offering contests pursuant to paragraph (b) of subdivision one 18 of this section] to allow [such operator to continue] an interactive 19 fantasy sports operator to offer such contests, on a provisional basis, 20 until such operator's application for registration has been approved or 21 denied in accordance with section fourteen hundred three of this arti- 22 cle, provided that such operator meets all the requirements in section 23 fourteen hundred four of this article.
