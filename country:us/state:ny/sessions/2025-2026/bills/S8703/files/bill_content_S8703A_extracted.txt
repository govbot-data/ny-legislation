9 § 1240. Short title. This title shall be known and may be cited as the
10 "New York water authority act".
11 § 1240-a. Legislative findings and declaration of policy. It is hereby
12 found that small water companies across New York state are struggling.
13 Many are no longer financially capable of maintaining reliable,
14 adequate, and safe water service to their customers at just, reasonable,
15 and affordable rates. As a result, the state needs to step in and help
16 take over ownership of small water companies and assist in repairing the
17 infrastructure necessary to improve water quality, quantity, safety, and
18 compliance with state and federal law, and service to customers.

5 § 1240-c. New York water authority. 1. A public corporation to be
6 known as the "New York water authority" is hereby created for the public
7 purposes and charged with the duties and having the powers provided in
8 this title. The authority shall be a body corporate and politic consti-
9 tuting a public benefit corporation.
10 2. The governing body of the authority shall consist of a total of
11 three members, one of which is to be appointed by the governor, one to
12 be appointed by the temporary president of the senate at the recommenda-
13 tion of the senators representing portions of the upstate New York
14 region, and one to be appointed by the speaker of the assembly at the
15 recommendation of the assembly members representing portions of the
16 upstate New York region. At all times, all three members shall be resi-
17 dents of the upstate New York region. The member to be appointed by the
18 governor shall be appointed for a term of office ending on December
19 thirty-first of the third year following the year in which this title
20 shall have become a law, the member to be appointed by the temporary
21 president of the senate shall be appointed for a term ending on the
22 thirty-first day of December of the fourth year following the year in
23 which this title shall have become a law, and the member to be appointed
24 by the speaker of the assembly shall be appointed for a term ending on
25 the thirty-first day of December of the fifth year following the year in
26 which this title shall have become a law. Subsequent appointments of
27 members shall be made for a term of three years ending in each case on
28 December thirty-first of the last year of such term. All members shall
29 continue to hold office until their successors are appointed and quali-
30 fy. All members shall hold, at a minimum, a bachelor's degree from an
31 accredited college or university, with concentration or degree in one of
32 the following areas of study and at least five years of professional
33 experience therein, or without such degree, such member shall have at
34 least ten years of professional experience in one of the following
35 fields: legal, environmental, financial, management, engineering, human
36 resources, or science. Vacancies shall be filled in the manner provided
37 for original appointment. Vacancies, occurring other than by expiration
38 of term of office, shall be filled by appointment for the unexpired
39 terms. Members may be removed from office only for the same reasons and
40 in the same manner as provided by section twenty-eight hundred twenty-
41 seven of this chapter. Each member shall attend, in each fiscal year, at
42 least seventy-five percent of all meetings of the governing body. Any
43 member who ceases to be a resident of the upstate New York region shall
44 forfeit their membership on the governing body upon such member's termi-
45 nation of residence in the upstate New York region, which forfeiture
46 shall create a vacancy. The members of the authority shall receive no
47 compensation for their services but shall be reimbursed for their actu-
48 al, necessary expenses incurred in connection with the carrying out of
49 the purposes of this title; provided, however, that no member shall be
50 reimbursed for any expense of attending ordinary authority meetings or
51 any other expense exceeding one thousand dollars incurred with respect
52 to any individual purpose, unless the governing body at a meeting duly
53 called and held when all three members are present shall have authorized
54 such expenditure by such member. The powers of the authority shall be
55 vested in and be exercised by the governing body at a meeting duly
56 called and held where a quorum of at least two members are present. Any
