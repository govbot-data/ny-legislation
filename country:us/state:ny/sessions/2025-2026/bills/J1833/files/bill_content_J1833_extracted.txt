Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01833 Summary:BILL NOJ01833  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORMARTINS COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01833 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1833 BY: Senator MARTINS CONGRATULATING Honorary Chief Michael Athenas upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the New Hyde Park Fire Department WHEREAS, Citizens across our State and Nation are inspired by and indebted to our noble volunteer firefighters who exhibit courage and bravery every day in the course of their duties; and WHEREAS, Volunteer firefighters exemplify the power of human compassion and the strength of the American spirit through actions of the most heroic magnitude; their sacrifices and their selfless dedication merit the highest tribute and recognition by all citizens of this great country; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Honorary Chief Michael Athenas upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the New Hyde Park Fire Department, to be celebrated at its 116th Annual Installation-Inspection Dinner on Saturday, April 18, 2026, at the Cradle of Aviation Museum in Garden City, New York; and WHEREAS, Chief Michael Athenas has consistently demonstrated a level of commitment, dedication, integrity, and professionalism which truly exemplifies excellence in volunteer service; and WHEREAS, Chief Michael Athenas was appointed to membership in the New Hyde Park Fire Department on October 5, 1976, and has devoted five decades of faithful and distinguished service to its ranks; and WHEREAS, An active and valued member of Protection Engine Company No. 2, he has served with distinction in numerous leadership and operational roles, including Chief Driver, Second Lieutenant, First Lieutenant, and Captain, earning the respect and admiration of his fellow firefighters; and WHEREAS, In 1977, he joined the New Hyde Park Fire Department Drill Team, known as the Termites, and became a primary driver; demonstrating extraordinary dedication, he continues to serve in this capacity today, marking nearly 50 years as a committed and integral member of the team; and WHEREAS, Firefighters unwaveringly serve our great State with pride, valor, and integrity; these respected volunteers are often the first to respond to emergencies, whether fires, transportation accidents, natural disasters, acts of terrorism, medical crises, or hazardous material incidents; and WHEREAS, It takes special dedication, a strong desire to help others, and a tireless sense of community to forsake precious time with family and friends to respond to the signal that a neighbor is in need; Chief Michael Athenas is such a firefighter; throughout 50 years of devoted service, this volunteer has performed above and beyond the call of duty those responsibilities which define the task of fire protection; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Honorary Chief Michael Athenas upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the New Hyde Park Fire Department; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Honorary Chief Michael Athenas.
