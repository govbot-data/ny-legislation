SEPULVEDA, COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to prohibiting the sale of a firearm, rifle, shotgun or ammunition on state property The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 265.67 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 265.67 Unlawful sale of a firearm, rifle, shotgun or ammunition on 4 state property. 5 1. For the purposes of this section the term "state property" shall 6 mean all property owned by the state and shall include: 7 (a) all county, city and municipal property; and 8 (b) any fairground located on state, county, city or municipal proper- 9 ty. 10 2. It shall be unlawful for any state officer or employee, or opera- 11 tor, lessee, or licensee of any state property, to contract for, author- 12 ize, or allow the sale of any firearm, rifle, shotgun or ammunition on 13 state property or in the buildings that sit on state property or proper- 14 ty otherwise owned, leased, occupied, or operated by the state. 15 3. This section does not apply to any of the following: 16 (a) A gun buyback event held by a law enforcement agency. 17 (b) The sale of a firearm or ammunition on state property that occurs 18 pursuant to a contract that was entered into before January first, two 19 thousand twenty-six. 20 (c) The purchase of a firearm, rifle, shotgun or ammunition on state 21 property by a law enforcement agency in the course of its regular 22 duties. 23 Unlawful sale of a firearm, rifle, shotgun or ammunition on state 24 property is a class A misdemeanor. 25 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 26 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
