AN ACT to amend the cannabis law, in relation to further restricting
cannabis storefronts from being located within five hundred feet of a
school and two hundred feet of a house of worship if they are located
in a mixed use building
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 72 of the cannabis law is amended
2 to read as follows:
3 6. No cannabis retail licensee shall locate a storefront within five
4 hundred feet of [a] school grounds as such term is defined in the educa-
5 tion law and for the purposes of this subdivision shall also include all
6 entrances and exits in a mixed use building which contains or is used as
7 a school, or is within two hundred feet of a house of worship. For
8 purposes of this subdivision, a "house of worship" shall include any
9 area, including entrances and exits, in a mixed use building which
10 contains or is used as a house of worship.
11 § 2. Subdivision 4 of section 77 of the cannabis law is amended to
12 read as follows:

14 license for any premises within five hundred feet of school grounds as
15 such term is defined in the education law and for the purposes of this
