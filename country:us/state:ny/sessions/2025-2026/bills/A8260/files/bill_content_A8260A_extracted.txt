AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to banning push
notifications from a food service establishment or a third-party food
delivery platform to minors
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section
2 390-f to read as follows:
3 § 390-f. Push notifications from certain mobile applications to
4 minors. 1. Any mobile application or mobile platform, operated by, oper-
5 ated on behalf of, or owned by any food service establishment, as
6 defined by paragraph (b) of subdivision one of section three hundred
7 ninety-one-v of this article, or any third-party delivery platform, as
8 defined by paragraph (d) of subdivision one of section three hundred
9 ninety-one-v of this article, shall require an individual to manually
10 enter the individual's date of birth when registering for an account
11 with such platform or application.
12 2. Any mobile application or mobile platform which is operated by,
13 operated on behalf of, or owned by any food service establishment, as
14 defined by paragraph (b) of subdivision one of section three hundred
15 ninety-one-v of this article, or any third-party delivery platform, as
16 defined by paragraph (d) of subdivision one of section three hundred
17 ninety-one-v of this article, shall be prohibited from initiating or
18 sending mobile device push notifications or text message notifications,
19 including alerts, designed to encourage an individual to engage with
20 such application or platform, where such individual has registered for
21 an account with a date of birth which indicate that such person is a
22 minor.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
