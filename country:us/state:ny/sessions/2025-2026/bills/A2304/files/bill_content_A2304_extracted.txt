Implementation of state food guidelines. Within twenty-one 19 months of the effective date of this section, the department shall adopt 20 a plan for providing technical and supervisory assistance to state agen- 21 cies implementing the state food guidelines made under section two 22 hundred fifty-two of this title. A process by which the department shall monitor the extent to which 26 state agencies follow the state food guidelines on a routine basis and 27 follow up with additional technical assistance as needed. 28 3. Guidance for applicable state agencies to adopt internal policies 29 on the contracting and purchasing of food in accordance with the state 30 food guidelines. 31 § 2.
