        AN ACT to amend chapter 296 of the laws of 1992 incorporating the Rescue
          Volunteer  and  Exempt  Firemen's  Benevolent  Association  Inc.,  and
          providing for its powers and duties, in relation  to  the  purpose  of
          such corporation and the use of certain tax monies received
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Sections 3 and 7 of chapter 296 of the laws of 1992  incor-
     2  porating  the  Rescue  Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Associ-
     3  ation Inc., and providing for its powers and duties, are amended to read
     4  as follows:
     5    § 3. Purposes. The purposes of such corporation shall be  the  mainte-
     6  nance  of  suitable  headquarters  for the promotion of fraternal inter-
     7  course among the members of such corporation[,];  the  relief,  aid  and
     8  assistance  of [such] the volunteer members [and their families] of such
     9  corporation who are disabled [or], indigent [and the  promotion  of  the
    10  welfare  of  the  volunteer  fire  service] or injured in performance of
    11  their duties, and their families; to promote and safeguard  the  welfare
    12  of  the  volunteer  members  of such corporation, and their families; to
    13  provide for the health and safety of the volunteer member of the  corpo-
    14  ration; and to enhance the morale of the volunteer members of the corpo-
    15  ration by providing social and recreational activities for the volunteer
    16  members  of  the  corporation,  and  the promotion of the volunteer fire
    17  service within the territory protected by such fire  department  of  the
    18  Rescue  Volunteer Hose Company No.1 of the Walden Fire District No. 2 in
    19  the town of Cheektowaga, county of Erie.
    20    § 7. Precept for the payment of foreign fire insurance premium  taxes.
