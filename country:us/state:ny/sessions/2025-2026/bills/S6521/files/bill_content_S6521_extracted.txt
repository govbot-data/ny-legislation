The public housing law is amended by adding a new section 2 20-a to read as follows: 3 § 20-a. Housing succession rights task force. 1. There is hereby 4 established within the division of housing and community renewal a hous- 5 ing succession rights task force which shall meet at least once annually 6 in different geographical areas of the state. Task force members may use videocon- 9 ferencing for attendance and participation. The task force shall consist 10 of ten members and shall include representatives from the following 11 state agencies: the division of housing and community renewal, the 12 office for the aging, the department of economic development, and the 13 New York city department of housing preservation and development. Of the 14 six remaining members, two shall be appointed by the speaker of the 15 assembly, two shall be appointed by the temporary president of the 16 senate, one shall be appointed by the minority leader of the assembly 17 and one shall be appointed by the minority leader of the senate, and 18 shall broadly represent housing groups from all geographic areas of the 19 state including service providers, associations, advocates, proprietors, 20 and residents of rent stabilized, public housing and city or municipal 21 owned buildings. Members shall receive no compensation for their 22 services but shall be reimbursed for travel and other expenses actually 23 and necessarily incurred in the performance of their duties.
