Cook HONORING Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay, Esq. upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition at the Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Visionary Awards Gala on March 22, 2026 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend those extraordinary individuals whose humanitarian service, visionary leadership, and global advocacy reflect the enduring principles of justice, equality, and peace championed by Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay, Esq. upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition at the Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Visionary Awards Gala on Sunday, March 22, 2026, at Crest Hollow Country Club in Woodbury, New York; and WHEREAS, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay has devoted her professional endeavors to advancing justice, equality, and access to legal representation for injured and underserved communities throughout New York; and WHEREAS, Earning a Juris Doctor degree in 2013, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay established Grigor Law Group in 2016, guided by a clear mission: to fight for the rights of the injured, the voiceless, and working-class families frequently neglected by the legal system; and WHEREAS, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay has built Grigor Law into one of New York's leading personal injury and workers' compensation law firms, representing thousands of clients across Nassau County, Queens, the Bronx, and beyond; her practice is rooted in ensuring access to medical care, holding negligent parties accountable, and securing fair compensation for victims and their families; and WHEREAS, Chrissy Grigoropoulos and her firm maintain a strong and visible presence throughout New York City, Nassau, and Suffolk County by supporting community events, neighborhood outreach programs, and seasonal initiatives, reinforcing this esteemed honoree's commitment to serving not only her clients, but her community at large; and WHEREAS, Through Grigor Law's ongoing outreach initiatives, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay's firm has distributed over 2,000 turkeys, donated more than 1,500 toys, and delivered over 500 bags of groceries to families, churches, and community organizations across New York; and WHEREAS, Alongside its charitable toy and food drives, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay's Grigor Law firm is a proud supporter of youth athletics and mentorship initiatives, as well as other programs that promote teamwork, leadership, and positive youth development; and WHEREAS, Focusing increasingly on youth education and advocacy, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay is currently developing a scholarship initiative aimed at supporting disadvantaged children and helping them pursue their academic and professional goals; and WHEREAS, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay is the bestselling author of "LADYSHARK: How to Become a Millionaire in Your 30s," a motivational self-help book focused on mindset, discipline, and financial empowerment, encouraging readers to aim higher, reject limitations, and pursue financial freedom with confidence and persistence; and WHEREAS, Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay has been featured in numerous publications and recognized with several honors, a testament to her impactful life; and WHEREAS, Her career stands as a testament to the belief that true justice is achieved not only through courtroom victories, but through service, mentorship, and the empowerment of future generations; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by an individual for the benefit of others which Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay has displayed throughout her life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition at the Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Visionary Awards Gala on March 22, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Chrissy Grigoropoulos-Peleytay.
