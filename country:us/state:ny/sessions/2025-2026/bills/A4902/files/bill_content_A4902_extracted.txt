Paragraph 1 of subdivision (b) of section 1202 of the vehi- 2 cle and traffic law, as amended by chapter 574 of the laws of 2006, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 1. No person shall stop, stand or park a vehicle within [fifteen] ten 5 feet of a fire hydrant except when such vehicle is attended by a 6 licensed operator or chauffeur who is seated in the front seat and who 7 can immediately move such vehicle in case of emergency, unless a differ- 8 ent distance is indicated by official signs, markings or parking meters. 9 § 2.
