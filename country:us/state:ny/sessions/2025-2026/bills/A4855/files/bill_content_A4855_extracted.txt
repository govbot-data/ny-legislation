Section 155.35 of the penal law, as amended by section 5 of 2 part O of chapter 56 of the laws of 2024, subdivision 2 as separately 3 amended by and subdivision 3 as added by section 2 of part FF of chapter 4 55 of the laws of 2024 is amended to read as follows: 5 § 155.35 Grand larceny in the third degree. 6 A person is guilty of grand larceny in the third degree when such 7 person steals property and: 8 1. when the value of the property exceeds three thousand dollars, or 9 2. the property is an automated teller machine or the contents of an 10 automated teller machine, or 11 3. the property consists of retail goods or merchandise stolen pursu- 12 ant to a common scheme or plan or a single, ongoing intent to deprive 13 another or others of the property or to appropriate the property to the 14 actor or another person and the value of the property exceeds three 15 thousand dollars, which value may be determined by the aggregate value 16 of all such property regardless of whether the goods or merchandise were 17 stolen from the same owner.
