Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of Eden, 2 located in the county of Erie, acting by and through its governing body 3 and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such body, is hereby 4 authorized to discontinue as parklands and alienate the lands more 5 particularly described in section three of this act, and to sell and 6 convey such lands at fair market value, for the purpose of providing 7 needed resources to enhance the existing parks within the town. 8 § 2.
