PAULIN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to rates of hospital reimbursement for facilities designated by the federal department of health and human services as exempt extended neoplastic disease care hospitals The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (e-2) of subdivision 4 of section 2807-c of the 2 public health law is amended by adding a new subparagraph (iv-a) to read 3 as follows: 4 (iv-a) For facilities designated by the federal department of health 5 and human services as exempt extended neoplastic disease care hospitals 6 as of December thirty-first, two thousand twenty-three, for which a 7 discrete institutional cost report was filed for the two thousand twen- 8 ty-three calendar year, shall be determined in accordance with the 9 following: 10 (A) The operating cost component of such rates shall reflect the use 11 of two thousand twenty-three operating costs as reported by each facili- 12 ty to the department and adjusted for inflation pursuant to paragraph 13 (c) of subdivision ten of this section, as otherwise modified by any 14 applicable statutes, and as further adjusted as the commissioner deems 15 appropriate, including transition adjustments. Such rates shall be 16 determined on a per case basis or per diem basis, as set forth in regu- 17 lations promulgated by the commissioner. 18 (B) The operating component of outpatient specialty rates of hospitals 19 subject to this subparagraph shall reflect the use of two thousand twen- 20 ty-three operating costs as reported to the department prior to December 21 first, two thousand twenty-four, and shall include such adjustments as 22 the commissioner deems appropriate.
