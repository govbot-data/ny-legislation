BROWN -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to authorizing the Lawrence-Cedarhurst fire department to enter into a long term lease with the Nassau county bridge authority The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 16 of section 654 of the public authorities 2 law, as added by chapter 106 of the laws of 2001, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 16. To lease property of the authority to the Atlantic Beach fire 5 district, Lawrence-Cedarhurst fire department, inc. or [its designee] 6 their designees for the purpose of constructing and operating a rescue 7 station, fire department training facilities, or facilities to store 8 fire department equipment on such property; provided, however, that no 9 such lease shall be made except with the approval of the county legisla- 10 ture nor for a period of more than thirty years from the date when it is 11 made. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
