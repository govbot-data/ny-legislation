Section 305 of the education law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 63 to read as follows: 3 63. Notwithstanding any law, rule or regulation to the contrary, for 4 any school district that has twenty-five percent or greater of students 5 living in the district who attend a nonpublic school, such school 6 district shall provide no services to pupils attending a nonpublic 7 school pursuant to sections seven hundred one, seven hundred eleven, 8 seven hundred twelve, seven hundred fifty-one, nine hundred twelve, 9 thirty-six hundred two-c and thirty-six hundred thirty-five of this 10 chapter. Students attending a nonpublic school within such districts 11 shall be provided by the nonpublic school with the same services that 12 are made available to students attending the public school of where such 13 nonpublic school is located pursuant to sections seven hundred one, 14 seven hundred eleven, seven hundred twelve, seven hundred fifty-one, 15 nine hundred twelve, thirty-six hundred two-c and thirty-six hundred 16 thirty-five of this chapter. Prior to services being provided by a 17 nonpublic school, nonpublic schools will be required to submit certif- 18 ications in a system developed by the commissioner of the services 19 provided electronically or in any other media form which the commission- 20 er reasonably determines offers the same degree of accountability and 21 control provided by paper certifications. The commissioner shall have 22 the power to review and seek correction of any service provided by 23 nonpublic schools.
