and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Spring Forward Hope for Pink upon the occasion of hosting its Pink & Pearls Brunch: Pearls & Bows Edition on Sunday, April 12, 2026; this distinguished event celebrates women, fosters wellness, and inspires connection, reflection, and hope; and WHEREAS, Spring Forward Hope for Pink is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting and empowering women who are navigating the challenges of breast cancer by providing guidance, resources, and education to ensure that no woman faces this journey alone; and WHEREAS, Under the visionary leadership of Founder and Executive Director Cynthia Jean, Spring Forward Hope for Pink has become a beacon of advocacy, education, and empowerment, raising awareness about breast health, promoting early detection, and offering holistic support to strengthen both mind and body; and WHEREAS, The Pink & Pearls Brunch: Pearls & Bows Edition creates a space of elegance, community, and celebration, featuring inspiring speakers, meaningful connections, and a powerful expression of sisterhood while reinforcing the organization's commitment to wellness, resilience, and shared empowerment; and WHEREAS, Through its programming, fundraising initiatives, and outreach, Spring Forward Hope for Pink demonstrates the profound impact of compassionate leadership, community solidarity, and intentional service in promoting health equity and uplifting women and families throughout Brooklyn and beyond; and WHEREAS, This annual gathering not only celebrates survival, strength, and hope but also highlights the importance of education, self-care, and collective action in building a supportive and empowered community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Spring Forward Hope for Pink, upon the occasion of hosting its Pink & Pearls Brunch: Pearls & Bows Edition, and to extend heartfelt appreciation to Founder and Executive Director Cynthia Jean, as well as all organizers, participants, and supporters, for their dedication to health, empowerment, and community uplift; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Cynthia Jean and Spring Forward Hope for Pink in recognition of their exemplary service, inspiring leadership, and enduring impact.
