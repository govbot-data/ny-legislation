Subdivision 5 of section 314 of the executive law, as 2 amended by a chapter of the laws of 2025 amending the executive law 3 relating to providing a rebuttable presumption relating to recertif- 4 ication as a minority and women-owned business enterprise, as proposed 5 in legislative bills numbers S. 596 and A. 2296, is amended to read as 6 follows: 7 5.
