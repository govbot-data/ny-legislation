PARKER, SEPULVEDA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Internet and Technology AN ACT in relation to establishing a commission to study and report on the potential implementation of legislation, rules and regulations for an open internet for New Yorkers; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. A ten-member commission is hereby established to assess the 2 potential implementation of legislation, rules and regulations for an 3 open internet for New Yorkers. 4 § 2. The commission shall be comprised of: 5 (a) two members appointed by the temporary president of the senate; 6 (b) two members appointed by the speaker of the assembly; 7 (c) two members appointed by the governor; 8 (d) two members appointed by the chief information officer of the 9 office of information technology services; 10 (e) one member appointed by the minority leader of the senate; and 11 (f) one member appointed by the minority leader of the assembly. 12 § 3. The commission shall provide a report to the governor, the speak- 13 er of the assembly and the temporary president of the senate on or 14 before January 1, 2027.
