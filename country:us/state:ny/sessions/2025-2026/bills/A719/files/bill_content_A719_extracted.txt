Resolved (if the Senate concur), That article 17 of the 2 constitution be amended by adding a new section 8 to read as follows: 3 § 8. The prevention and mitigation of adverse childhood experiences is 4 a public concern and shall be addressed by the state and by such of its 5 subdivisions, and in such manner and by such means, as the legislature 6 may from time to time determine. 7 § 2.
