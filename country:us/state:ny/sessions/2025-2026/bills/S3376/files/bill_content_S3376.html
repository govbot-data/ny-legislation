BILL NOS03376

Add Art 4-B §§75 & 76, Leg L
 
Creates the New York state independent budget office with a director to be appointed, upon the recommendations of an independent budget office advisory committee, by the speaker of the assembly and the temporary president of the senate; establishes such office shall provide objective, non-partisan and timely analyses of state revenue, expenditures and management practices to members of the legislature for any legislation with fiscal impact or at the request of a leader or a committee.

    19    (c)  information  with respect to the performance and effectiveness of
    20  state agencies and programs; and
    21    (d) to the extent practicable, such other information or  analyses  as
    22  may be requested by such officials and bodies, and the general public.
    23    Requests  made by the speaker of the assembly, the temporary president
    24  of the senate, the chair of the assembly ways and  means  committee  and

    54  first  of  the  second  year  following  the  enactment of this article.
    55  Subsequent appointments shall be made no later than  February  fifteenth
    56  every sixth year thereafter.

    19  administration,  public  affairs or public administration located in New

    22  corporation;  and  one  officer  or  former officer of a civic or public
    23  interest advocacy organization involved in budget matters.  No  individ-
    24  ual shall serve consecutive terms.

    26  guidelines for the best practices of the independent budget office.  The
    27  independent  budget  office  advisory committee shall meet annually with
    28  the director  to  review  such  guidelines  and  to  make  comments  and
    29  suggestions on the overall practices of the office.
    30    (c)  Members  of  the advisory committee shall receive no compensation
