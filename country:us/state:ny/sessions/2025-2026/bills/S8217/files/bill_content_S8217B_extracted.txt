AN ACT to amend the criminal procedure law, in relation to requiring
certain covered platforms to provide a process for law enforcement
agencies to contact such platform and to comply with search warrants
within seventy-two hours
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The criminal procedure law is amended by adding a new
2 section 690.60 to read as follows:
3 § 690.60 Search warrants; covered platforms.
4 1. For the purposes of this section, the following terms shall have
5 the following meanings:
6 (a) "Adverse result" shall mean any of the following:
7 (i) danger to the life or physical safety of an individual;
8 (ii) flight from prosecution;
9 (iii) destruction of or tampering with evidence;
10 (iv) intimidation of potential witnesses; or
11 (v) serious jeopardy to an investigation.
12 (b) "Covered minor" shall mean any user who is determined by an opera-
13 tor, via one or more commercially reasonable age verification methods,
14 to be under the age of eighteen.
15 (c) "Covered platform" shall mean a public or semi-public website,
16 online service, online application, or mobile application that (i) is
17 used by covered minors in this state, (ii) allows users to construct a
18 public or semi-public profile for the purposes of using such website,
19 service, or application, and (iii) allows users to create or post

5 (d) "Law enforcement agency" shall mean any agency which is empowered
6 by law to conduct an investigation or make an arrest for an offense
7 under the penal law, and an agency which is authorized by law to prose-

15 (f) "Operator" shall mean any person, business, or other legal entity
16 who operates or provides a covered platform.
17 (g) "Search warrant" shall mean a search warrant duly executed pursu-
18 ant to the provisions of this article.
19 (h) "User" shall mean a user of a covered platform in New York not
20 acting as an operator, or agent or affiliate of such operator, of such
21 platform or any portion thereof.
22 2. An operator that provides a covered platform shall ensure that its
23 covered platform provides a streamlined process to allow law enforcement
24 agencies to contact such covered platform. Such process shall, at a
25 minimum, provide continuous availability of such process to law enforce-
26 ment agencies.
27 3. Except as provided in subdivision four of this section or any other
28 law, an operator that provides a covered platform shall comply with a
29 search warrant within seventy-two hours after receiving such search
30 warrant if all of the following apply:
31 (a) such search warrant is provided to such operator or covered plat-
32 form by a law enforcement agency;
33 (b) the subject of such search warrant is information associated with
34 a user's account on the covered platform operated by such operator; and
35 (c) the information is controlled by a user or operator of the covered
36 platform.
37 4. A court may reasonably extend the time required to comply with a
38 search warrant pursuant to subdivision three of this section, if the
39 court makes a written finding that the operator or covered platform has
40 shown good cause for the extension and that an extension would not cause
41 an adverse result.
42 5. The provisions of this section shall not apply to an online plat-
43 form with fewer than one million discrete monthly users.
44 6. On or after the effective date of this section, whenever it appears
45 to the attorney general, upon complaint or otherwise, that any person,
46 within or outside the state, has violated the provisions of this
47 section, the attorney general may bring an action or special proceeding
48 in the name and on behalf of the people of the state of New York to:
49 (a) enjoin any such violation;
50 (b) obtain restitution of any moneys or property obtained directly or
51 indirectly by any such violation;
52 (c) obtain disgorgement of any profits or gains obtained directly or
53 indirectly by any such violation;
54 (d) obtain damages caused directly or indirectly by any such
55 violation;
