Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01511 Summary:BILL NOJ01511  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORO'MARA COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01511 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1511 BY: Senator O'MARA CONGRATULATING the Tioga Central Tigers Football Team upon the occasion of winning the 2025 New York State Public High School Athletic Association Class D Boys Football Championship WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly sought after in high school sports; this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Tioga Central Tigers Football Team upon the occasion of winning the 2025 New York State Public High School Athletic Association Class D Boys Football Championship at the JMA Wireless Dome in Syracuse, New York; to the praise and applause of their excited fans, the Tioga Central Tigers defeated the Tuckahoe Tigers in a 42-31 victory to win the Championship; and WHEREAS, Proudly donning their school's colors, family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Tioga Central Tigers throughout their journey as they ended their season with an impressive 13-1 overall record, only losing their first game and continuing the season with a 13-game winning streak; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as football, which demands athletic prowess, speed, and agility, Varisty Coaches Nick Aiello, Adam MaCauley, Jordan Hathaway, and Josh Kithcart, and their expert coaches and staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and WHEREAS, The Tioga Central Tigers' coaches and all of the outstanding athletes on the Tioga Central High School Football Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence, which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Tioga Central Tigers Football Team: Gavin Albrecht, Logan Bellis, Elijah Bellis, Jackson Bombard, Caden Bradley, Brendan Clark, Kadin Cole, Liamm Comstock, Jayden Duncanson, Gage Hopkins, James Howey, Nate Hulbert, Gavin Kithcart, Carson Labour, Brayden Lounsbury, Kaleb Malone, Mark McCane, Derek Mills, Austin Mumbulo, Dominic Palmer, Owen Parker, Max Paugh, Shane Platukis, Jason Rifenburg, Cameron Ross, Josh Rought, Bailey Rugh, Andrew Sherman, Max Stauder, Connor Streeter, Jordan Vanderpool, Markel Watkins, and Allen Winans; Varsity Coaches Nick Aiello, Adam MaCauley, Jordan Hathaway, and Josh Kithcart; Junior Varsity Coaches, Jake Howland, Tyler Spires, Connor Hutchinson, and John Worthing; Modified Coaches, Stan Siberski, Jude Platukis, Dan Pearce, and Scott Siberski; Strength Coach, Cole Williams; and Athletic Trainer Jason Taylor on their outstanding season and overall team record; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Tioga Central Tigers Football Team and their coaches.
