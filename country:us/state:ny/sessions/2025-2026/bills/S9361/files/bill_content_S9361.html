Amd §§1238 & 1286, Art 34 Art Head, add §§123-a & 1244, V & T L
 
Requires operators of bicycles with electric assist, electric scooters, and motor-driven cycles to wear protective head gear regardless of age.

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in  relation  to  requiring
          operators  of  bicycles  with  electric assist, electric scooters, and
          motor-driven cycles wear protective head gear
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1. Subdivision 5-c of section 1238 of the vehicle and traffic
     2  law, as added by section 5 of part XX of chapter 58 of the laws of 2020,
     3  is amended to read as follows:
     4    5-c. No person shall ride upon, propel or otherwise operate  a  [class
     5  three]  bicycle  with  electric  assist  unless such person is wearing a
     6  helmet meeting  standards  established  by  the  commissioner.  For  the
     7  purposes  of  this subdivision, wearing a helmet means having a properly
     8  fitting helmet fixed securely on the head of such wearer with the helmet
     9  straps securely fastened.
    10    § 2. Subdivision 1 of section 1286 of the vehicle and traffic law,  as
    11  added  by  section  10  of part XX of chapter 58 of the laws of 2020, is
    12  amended to read as follows:
    13    1. No person [sixteen or seventeen years  of  age]  shall  ride  upon,
    14  propel  or  otherwise  operate an electric scooter unless such person is
    15  wearing a helmet  meeting  standards  established  by  the  commissioner
    16  pursuant  to  the  provisions  of  subdivision  two-a  of section twelve
    17  hundred thirty-eight of this title.  As used in this subdivision,  wear-
    18  ing  a  helmet  means having a properly fitting helmet fixed securely on
    19  the head of such wearer with the helmet straps securely fastened.
    20    § 3. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding  a  new  section
    21  123-a to read as follows:
    22    § 123-a. Motor-driven cycle. Every motor vehicle having:
    23    (a) a seat or saddle for the use of the operator;
    24    (b) two or three wheels in contact with the ground;
    25    (c)  an internal combustion engine, or electric, solar or hybrid motor
    26  which will propel the vehicle unassisted at a maximum speed  no  greater
    27  than thirty miles per hour. If such vehicle is equipped with an internal

     1  combustion  engine, such engine's piston or rotor displacement shall not

     4  miles per hour and the operator shall be required to have in such opera-

    10    § 4. The article heading of article 34 of the vehicle and traffic law,
    11  as amended by chapter 694 of the laws of 1995,  is  amended  and  a  new
    12  section 1244 is added to read as follows:
    13                 OPERATION OF BICYCLES [AND], PLAY DEVICES AND
    14                             MOTOR-DRIVEN CYCLES
    15    §  1244.  Motor-driven cycle operators to wear protective headgear. No
    16  person shall ride upon, propel or otherwise operate a motor-driven cycle
    17  unless such person is wearing a helmet meeting standards established  by
    18  the  commissioner.  For  the  purposes of this section, wearing a helmet
    19  means having a properly fitting helmet fixed securely  on  the  head  of
    20  such wearer with the helmet straps securely fastened.
    21    §  5.  This  act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
    22  have become a law.
