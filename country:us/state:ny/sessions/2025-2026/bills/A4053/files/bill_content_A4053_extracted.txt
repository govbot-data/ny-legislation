CUNNINGHAM -- read once and referred to the Committee on Energy AN ACT to amend the energy law and the public authorities law, in relation to the availability of alternative fuels along the New York state thruway The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "electric super highway act". 3 § 2. Section 1-103 of the energy law is amended by adding a new subdi- 4 vision 16 to read as follows: 5 16. "Alternative fuel" shall mean ethanol, methanol, natural gas, 6 propane, electricity (including electricity from solar energy), hydro- 7 gen, and any other fuel identified which is substantially not petrole- 8 um-based, which is consistent with the goals of protecting the safety 9 and health of the public and minimizing emissions of air pollutants, and 10 which is consistent with any applicable federal alternative fuel 11 requirements. 12 § 3. The energy law is amended by adding a new section 5-129 to read 13 as follows: 14 § 5-129. Alternative fuels. The New York state energy research and 15 development authority shall provide the New York state thruway authority 16 with a list of the most feasible alternative fuels, which shall number 17 no fewer than three, and assist the authority in implementing the 18 requirements of section three hundred fifty-four of the public authori- 19 ties law. 20 § 4.
