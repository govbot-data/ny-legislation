Amd §1-103, add §5-129, Energy L; amd §354, Pub Auth L
 
Enacts the "electric super highway act", requiring alternative fuels to be available for public use along the New York state thruway after November 1, 2025 and providing each recommended alternative fuel at least every one hundred twenty miles on both sides of the thruway; defines "alternative fuel".

        AN ACT to amend the energy  law  and  the  public  authorities  law,  in

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Short  title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
     2  the "electric super highway act".
     3    § 2. Section 1-103 of the energy law is amended by adding a new subdi-
     4  vision 16 to read as follows:
     5    16. "Alternative fuel" shall  mean  ethanol,  methanol,  natural  gas,
     6  propane,  electricity  (including electricity from solar energy), hydro-

     8  um-based,  which  is  consistent with the goals of protecting the safety
     9  and health of the public and minimizing emissions of air pollutants, and
    10  which  is  consistent  with  any  applicable  federal  alternative  fuel

    14    § 5-129. Alternative fuels.  The New York state  energy  research  and
    15  development authority shall provide the New York state thruway authority
    16  with  a  list of the most feasible alternative fuels, which shall number
    17  no fewer than three,  and  assist  the  authority  in  implementing  the
    18  requirements  of section three hundred fifty-four of the public authori-
    19  ties law.
    20    § 4. Subdivision 10 of section 354 of the public authorities  law,  as
    21  amended  by  section  2 of part TT of chapter 54 of the laws of 2016, is
    22  amended to read as follows:
    23    10. To construct, reconstruct or improve on or along  the  thruway  in
    24  the  manner  herein  provided,  suitable  facilities  for  gas stations,
