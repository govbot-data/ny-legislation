AN ACT to amend the alcoholic beverage control law, in relation to a
license to sell liquor at retail for consumption on certain premises
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 7 of section 64 of the alcoholic beverage
2 control law is amended by adding a new paragraph (e-17) to read as
3 follows:
4 (e-17) Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph (a) of this subdi-
5 vision, the authority may issue a retail license for on-premises
6 consumption for a premises which shall be located within two hundred
7 feet of a building occupied as a school, church, synagogue or other
8 place of worship, provided such premises constitute a premises for the
9 sale of food or beverages at retail for consumption on the premises
10 located wholly within the boundaries of the Borough of Brooklyn, county
11 of Kings, bounded and described as follows:
12 ALL that certain plot, piece or parcel of land, situate, lying and being
13 in the Borough of Brooklyn, County of Kings, City and State of New York,
14 bounded and described as follows:
15 BEGINNING at the corner formed by the intersection of the westerly side
16 of Nostrand Avenue with the northerly side of Rutland Road;
17 RUNNING THENCE northerly, along the westerly side of Nostrand Avenue, 20
18 feet,
19 THENCE westerly, parallel with Rutland Road and part of the distance
20 through party wall 80 feet;
21 THENCE southerly parallel with Nostrand Avenue, 20 feet to the northerly
22 side of Rutland Road;
23 THENCE easterly along the northerly side of Rutland Road, 80 feet to the
24 point or place of BEGINNING.
25 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
