Subdivision 5 of section 365-d of the social services law, 2 as added by section 46-a of part B of chapter 57 of the laws of 2015, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 5. Such report shall provide the date of applica- 21 tion, date of review, and a stated determination of sufficiency of 22 evidence warranting committee deliberation and recommendation.
