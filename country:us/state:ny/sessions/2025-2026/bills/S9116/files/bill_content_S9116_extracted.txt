HARCKHAM -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Racing, Gaming and Wager- ing AN ACT to amend the tax law and the state finance law, in relation to establishing an instant lottery scratch-off game to fund scholarships for veterans The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 1604 of the tax law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision d to read as follows: 3 d. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the division 4 shall establish an instant lottery scratch-off game designated as the 5 veterans' benefit game, the proceeds of which shall be applied exclu- 6 sively for the purpose of providing scholarships for veterans pursuant 7 to section six hundred sixty-nine-a of the education law. The tickets 8 for the instant lottery game created pursuant to this subdivision shall 9 clearly state that the profits derived from such game are used to bene- 10 fit veterans in the state. The division may establish additional 11 scratch-off games for the purposes of this subdivision one year after 12 the effective date of this subdivision. 13 § 2.
