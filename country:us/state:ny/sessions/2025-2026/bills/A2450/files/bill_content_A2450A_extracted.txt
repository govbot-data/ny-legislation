AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring instruction
in cursive writing as part of the basic education program
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 819 to
2 read as follows:
3 § 819. Courses of instruction in cursive writing. The standard course
4 of study shall include the requirement that public schools provide
5 instruction in cursive writing so that students create readable docu-
6 ments through legible cursive handwriting by the end of fifth grade.
7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall apply to school
8 years commencing on the first of July next succeeding the date on which
9 it shall have become a law.
