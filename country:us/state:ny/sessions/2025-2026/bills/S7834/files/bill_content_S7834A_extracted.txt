AN ACT in relation to authorizing the town of Ithaca to alienate and
discontinue the use of certain parklands
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of Ithaca,
2 located in the county of Tompkins, acting by and through its governing
3 body and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such body, is
4 hereby authorized to discontinue as parklands and alienate the lands
5 more particularly described in section three of this act, for the
6 purpose of maintaining and operating such lands for agricultural
7 purposes.
8 § 2. The authorization provided in section one of this act shall be
9 effective only upon the condition that the town of Ithaca, county of
10 Tompkins, shall dedicate the lands described in section four of this act
11 as parkland, provided that the town has never used such lands for public
12 open space or park purposes.

14 lands to be dedicated for the acquisition of additional parklands and/or
15 for capital improvements to existing park and recreational facilities.
16 § 6. In the event that the town of Ithaca received any funding support
17 or assistance from the federal government for the purchase, maintenance
18 or improvement of the parklands set forth in section three of this act,
19 the discontinuance and alienation of such parkland authorized by the
20 provisions of this act shall not occur until the town of Ithaca has
21 complied with any federal requirements pertaining to the alienation or
22 conversion of parklands, including satisfying the secretary of the inte-
23 rior that the alienation or conversion complies with all conditions
24 which the secretary of the interior deems necessary to ensure the
25 substitution of other lands shall be equivalent in fair market value and
26 usefulness to the lands being alienated or converted.
27 § 7. This act shall take effect immediately.
