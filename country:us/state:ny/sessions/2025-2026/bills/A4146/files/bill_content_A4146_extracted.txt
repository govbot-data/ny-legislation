GRAY, LEMONDES, BEEPHAN, PALMESANO -- read once and referred to the Committee on Codes AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to establishing the crime of theft of an impounded vehicle The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 165.14 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 165.14 Theft of an impounded vehicle. 4 A person is guilty of theft of an impounded vehicle if such person 5 removes an impounded vehicle without paying any necessary fees prior to 6 such removal. 7 Theft of an impounded vehicle is a class A misdemeanor. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
