        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation  to  permitting
          the  use  of  photo  speed  violation  monitoring  systems  in highway
          construction or maintenance work areas on all state highways
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Paragraph  1  of subdivision (a) of section 1180-e of the
     2  vehicle and traffic law, as amended by section 1 of part Q of chapter 58
     3  of the laws of 2025, is amended to read as follows:
     4    1. Notwithstanding any other provision of  law,  the  commissioner  of
     5  transportation is hereby authorized to establish a demonstration program
     6  imposing  monetary liability on the owner of a vehicle for failure of an
     7  operator thereof to comply with posted maximum speed limits in a highway
     8  construction or maintenance work area [located  on  a  controlled-access
     9  highway]  (i) when highway construction or maintenance work is occurring
    10  and a work area speed limit is in effect as provided in paragraph two of
    11  subdivision (d) or subdivision (f) of section eleven hundred  eighty  of
    12  this  article  or  (ii) when highway construction or maintenance work is
    13  occurring and other speed limits are in effect as provided  in  subdivi-
    14  sion  (b)  or  (g) or paragraph one of subdivision (d) of section eleven
    15  hundred eighty of this article. Such demonstration program shall empower
    16  the commissioner to install photo  speed  violation  monitoring  systems
    17  within no more than forty highway construction or maintenance work areas
    18  [located  on  controlled-access  highways]  and  to operate such systems
    19  within such work areas (iii) when highway  construction  or  maintenance
    20  work  is  occurring and a work area speed limit is in effect as provided
    21  in paragraph two of subdivision (d) or subdivision (f) of section eleven
    22  hundred eighty of this article or  (iv)  when  highway  construction  or
    23  maintenance  work  is  occurring and other speed limits are in effect as
    24  provided in subdivision (b) or (g) or paragraph one of  subdivision  (d)
    25  of  section  eleven hundred eighty of this article. The commissioner, in
    26  consultation with the superintendent of the division  of  state  police,
    27  shall  determine the location of the highway construction or maintenance
