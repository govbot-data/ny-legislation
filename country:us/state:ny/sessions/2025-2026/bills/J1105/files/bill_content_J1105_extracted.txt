Richard High School for Fire and Life Safety, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord with its long-standing traditions, to honor the youth of today, the leaders of tomorrow, whose character and achievements exemplify the ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of 2025 Salutatorian Mia Luna of FDNY Captain Vernon A. Richard High School for Fire and Life Safety, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and WHEREAS, Graduating on June 27, 2025, Salutatorian Mia Luna represents the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious resource, our youth; her achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community and should be recognized and saluted; and WHEREAS, As Salutatorian Mia Luna takes leave of FDNY Captain Vernon A. Richard High School for Fire and Life Safety, she bestows a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement and community service to which others may aspire; and WHEREAS, Salutatorian Mia Luna may now stand with pride as she assesses her achievements, experiences the satisfaction of her labor, and feels the joy of her accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Class of 2025 Salutatorian Mia Luna of FDNY Captain Vernon A. Richard High School for Fire and Life Safety, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Salutatorian Mia Luna.
