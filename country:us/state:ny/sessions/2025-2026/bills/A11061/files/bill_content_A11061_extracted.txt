Incorporation: membership. A not-for-profit corporation by 6 the name of "Centerport Volunteer Exempt [Firemen's] Firefighter's 7 Benevolent Association" is hereby created. It shall be composed of such 8 persons eligible to membership therein as hereinafter provided as shall 9 (a) notify the secretary of the Centerport Fire Department Company, a 10 volunteer fire company, in writing prior to the organization meeting of 11 such corporation hereinafter provided for of their desire to become 12 members, or (b) shall attend such meeting or an adjournment thereof, and 13 also such persons so eligible as may thereafter become members of such 14 corporation, pursuant to its by-laws. 15 § 2. Persons eligible to membership.
