The executive law is amended by adding a new section 204 to read 34 as follows: 35 § 204. Statewide database of all contracts awarded by the state. 1. 36 The commissioner shall establish a single statewide database of all 37 contracts awarded by the state or entity thereof and shall include ther- 38 ein information of all contracts awarded by the state or entity thereof 39 and shall include therein information related to all bids submitted in 40 response to any solicitations for such awards. 41 2. Every department, division, commission, agency, authority, board or 42 any other entity of the state shall submit all awarded contracts and 43 bids related to such contracts to the commissioner on a schedule and in 44 a format determined by the commissioner. 45 3. Such database shall be available online for public review in a 46 searchable, downloadable format. The commissioner shall promulgate any rules and regulations neces- 52 sary for the implementation of such database established pursuant to 53 this section and ensure the privacy of any confidential or proprietary 54 information that may be contained within a bid or contract. 55 § 9.
