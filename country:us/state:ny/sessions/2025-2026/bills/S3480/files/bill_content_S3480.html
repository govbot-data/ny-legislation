S03480 Summary:

BILL NOS03480
 
SAME ASSAME AS A04581
 
SPONSORHELMING
 
COSPNSRMAY
 
MLTSPNSR
 
 
Authorizes and directs the commissioner of transportation to conduct a study on the feasibility of removing all existing 3-cable guide rails on New York roadways and replacing such guide rails with safer alternatives; requires a report to be submitted to the governor, the temporary president of the senate, the speaker of the assembly, the minority leader of the senate, and the minority leader of the assembly containing the findings and recommendations for legislative or other actions.

        AN ACT in relation to authorizing  and  directing  the  commissioner  of

          existing 3-cable guide rails on New York roadways; and  providing  for

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. 1. The commissioner of  transportation  is  authorized  and
     2  directed  to conduct a study on the feasibility of removing all existing
     3  3-cable guide rails on New York roadways and replacing such guide  rails
     4  with  safer  modern  alternatives.  Such study shall include, but not be
     5  limited to:

    13  and
    14    d. The estimated impact on vehicle and motorist safety by removing and
    15  replacing 3-cable guide rails with modern alternatives.
