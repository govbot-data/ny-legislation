LIU -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to mandatory student enrollment reporting The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 319 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 319. Mandatory student enrollment reporting. 1. All public schools 4 located within the state, regardless of level, shall report the number 5 of students enrolled in such school to the department triennially, on 6 October thirty-first, January thirty-first, and April thirtieth of each 7 year. 8 2. Each report shall be accompanied with supporting documentation of 9 such enrollment. For purposes of this section, the commissioner shall 10 determine what supporting documentation is necessary. 11 3. Each report shall be published and publicly shared on the school 12 district's website within one week of submission to the department. 13 4. For purposes of this section, "enrollment" shall mean the undupli- 14 cated count of all children registered to receive educational services 15 in grades kindergarten through twelve, including children in ungraded 16 programs. 17 § 2. Section 2854 of the education law is amended by adding a new 18 subdivision 4 to read as follows: 19 4. Mandatory student enrollment reporting. (a) All charter schools 20 located within the state shall report the number of students enrolled to 21 the department triennially, on October thirty-first, January thirty- 22 first, and April thirtieth of each year. 23 (b) Each report shall be accompanied with supporting documentation of 24 such enrollment. For purposes of this section, the commissioner shall 25 determine what supporting documentation is necessary.
