Reynolds upon the occasion of the naming of a park in New Hartford, New York, in recognition of his remarkable life WHEREAS, From time to time this Legislative Body takes note of certain extraordinary individuals it wishes to recognize for their valued contributions to the success and progress of society and publicly acknowledge their endeavors which have enhanced the basic humanity among us all; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Chief David M. Reynolds upon the occasion of the naming of a park in New Hartford, New York, in recognition of his remarkable life on Saturday, November 30, 2024; and WHEREAS, David M. Reynolds joined the New Hartford Fire Department in March of 1967; throughout his illustrious tenure which traversed more than 50 years, he has held several positions within the Department including: Lieutenant (1974), 2nd Lieutenant (1975-76), Captain (1977), 2nd Deputy Chief (1978-79), Assistant Chief (1980), and Chief (1981-2005); and WHEREAS, Furthermore, he currently serves as Director of the New Hartford Fire Department Benevolent Association, a title he has held since 2013; and WHEREAS, In addition to his service with the New Hartford Fire Department, David M. Reynolds served as President of the Oneida County Fire Chiefs Association and as Chief of Utica's Engine 11 Foundation; he was elected as a New Hartford Town Councilman in 2006, and continues to serve today; and WHEREAS, With him throughout have been his wife of 56 years, Doreen, and their four daughters, as well as four grandchildren and two great-grandchildren, all of whom feel privileged to be a part of his life and rejoice in his many achievements; and WHEREAS, A true inspiration to all for his unwavering dedication to the community he so ably serves, David M. Reynolds has been called upon to contribute his time and talents to countless civic and charitable endeavors and has always given of himself unstintingly; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by an individual for the benefit of others which David M. Reynolds has displayed throughout his life; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that when individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Chief David M. Reynolds upon the occasion of the naming of a park in New Hartford, New York, in recognition of his remarkable life; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Chief David M. Reynolds. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
