DILAN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Real Property Taxation AN ACT in relation to placing a moratorium on tax lien sales in a city with a population of one million or more The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a 2 city with a population of one million or more shall place a moratorium 3 on tax lien sales until April 1, 2026 due to the financial hardships 4 placed on property owners as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic. 5 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
