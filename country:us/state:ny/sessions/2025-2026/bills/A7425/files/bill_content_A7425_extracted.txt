Resolved (if the Senate concur), That section 7 of article 2 5 of the constitution be amended by adding a new subdivision (e) to read 3 as follows: 4 (e) (i) Notwithstanding subdivision (a) of this section, the public 5 pension of a correction officer, as defined in paragraph (ii) of this 6 subdivision, who is found liable for the death or hospitalization of an 7 incarcerated individual, may be reduced to no less than twenty thousand 8 dollars to satisfy any judgment against such correction officer prior to 9 the payment of any public funds, following notice and a hearing by an 10 appropriate court, as provided by law. The court determination whether 11 to reduce such pension shall be based on the consideration of factors 12 including the severity of such correction officer's actions and the 13 proportionality of a reduction of such pension to such actions. When a 14 court issues an order to reduce such pension, the court shall consider 15 and determine specific findings as to the amount of such reduction, if 16 any, and whether the reduction would result in undue hardship or other 17 inequity upon any dependent children, spouse or other dependents; and 18 other factors as provided by law.
