STEWART-COUSINS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Higher Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to establishing a SUNY four-year college of engineering and applied sciences in the city of Yonkers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 3 of section 352 of the education law, as 2 amended by chapter 13 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 3. The state university shall consist of the four university centers 5 at Albany, Binghamton, Buffalo and Stony Brook, the designated colleges 6 of arts and sciences at Brockport, Buffalo, Cortland, Fredonia, Geneseo, 7 New Paltz, Old Westbury, Oneonta, Oswego, Plattsburgh, Potsdam and 8 Purchase, the college of engineering and applied sciences at Yonkers, 9 empire state college, the agricultural and technical colleges at Alfred, 10 Canton, Cobleskill, Delhi, Farmingdale and Morrisville, downstate 11 medical center, upstate medical center, the college of optometry, the 12 college of environmental science and forestry, maritime college, the 13 college of technology at Utica/Rome, the statutory or contract colleges 14 at Cornell university and Alfred university, and such additional univer- 15 sities, colleges and other institutions, facilities and research centers 16 as have been or hereafter may be acquired, established, operated or 17 contracted to be operated for the state by the state university trus- 18 tees. 19 § 2. (a) The trustees of the state university of New York are hereby 20 authorized and directed to immediately take all actions to create and 21 establish within the state university system, and subject to the juris- 22 diction of such board of trustees, a college of engineering and applied 23 sciences as a four-year state university of New York in the city of 24 Yonkers, county of Westchester.
