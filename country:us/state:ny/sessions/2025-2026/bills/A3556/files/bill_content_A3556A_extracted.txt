AN ACT to amend the mental hygiene law, in relation to requiring the
office for people with developmental disabilities to issue a residen-
tial request list and placement report to the governor and the legis-
lature
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The mental hygiene law is amended by adding a new section
2 13.28 to read as follows:
3 § 13.28 Office for people with developmental disabilities; residential
4 request list and placement report.
5 (a) The office shall issue a report to the governor, the speaker of
6 the assembly, and the temporary president of the senate, within eighteen
7 months of the effective date of this section, setting forth the number
8 of individuals with developmental disabilities who are on the residen-
9 tial request list seeking placement. The residential request report
10 shall include:
11 1. the statewide number of individuals awaiting placement broken down
12 into the total number of individuals from within each developmental
13 disability services office's geographic area;
14 2. the number of individuals awaiting placement in any certified resi-
15 dential setting categorized by the length of time such individuals have
16 been awaiting placement, and divided into certified out-of-home, super-
17 vised, supportive placement and the number of such persons who are
18 medically frail requiring intensive medical care; and
