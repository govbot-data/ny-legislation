CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Cities 1 -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to requiring notification of residents who are potentially affected by a serious health threat to their water system The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 17-194.1 of the administrative code of the city of 2 New York is amended by adding a new subdivision n to read as follows: 3 n. Notice requirements. In all cases where the results of the investi- 4 gation conducted pursuant to subdivision e of this section indicate a 5 serious health threat, the department shall notify all potentially 6 affected residents within 24 hours, including all adjacent buildings 7 that share the same underlying water system. The department shall 8 promulgate rules for compliance with the requirements of this subdivi- 9 sion, in accordance with applicable law. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 11 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
