Caemmerer Park in Albertson, New York, which is a park he enjoyed throughout his childhood; and WHEREAS, Brian Keating developed a picnic area and implemented a new recycling system at John D. Caemmerer Park, which would ultimately improve the park experience for community members utilizing this public space; and WHEREAS, Brian Keating began his scouting journey as a Webelo with Pack 201 at Center Street, and since joining Troop 201 in 2017, he has been entrusted with various leadership roles, including Quartermaster, Patrol Leader of the Panther Patrol, Assistant Senior Patrol Leader, Senior Patrol Leader, and Junior Assistant Scoutmaster; and WHEREAS, Brian Keating joined fellow scouts on several High Adventure outings, including backpacking for two weeks at Philmont, New Mexico in 2021 and 2023, dog sledding at Northern Tier, Minnesota, in 2024, and serving as Crew Leader at Out Island at Sea Base, Florida, in 2022; and WHEREAS, On his path to becoming an Eagle Scout, Brian Keating earned 32 merit badges, Kayaking BSA, Snorkeling BSA, the 50-Miler, and the Triple Crown Awards; and WHEREAS, His dedication to the safety and well-being of the citizens of Nassau County is represented through his project and demonstrates his devotion to both community and commitment to others; and WHEREAS, As he enters the next chapters of his life and is met with limitless opportunities, Brian Keating plans to continue to uphold the ideals and principals of Scouting America; and WHEREAS, Brian Keating's perseverance in rising through the ranks to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of his ability to master difficult tasks; his accomplishment will enable him to emerge as a leader, fully equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school, his community, his State and his Nation, and no doubt his future will be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Brian Keating on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Brian Keating.
