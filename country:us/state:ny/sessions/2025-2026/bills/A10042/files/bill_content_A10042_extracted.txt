SHIMSKY -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Energy AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to prohibiting electric corporations and gas corporations from increasing charges to certain ratepayers due to legal or regulatory proceeding costs The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 66 of the public service law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 33 to read as follows: 3 33. Prohibit electric corporations, gas corporations, and utilities 4 from passing along costs or increasing charges to ratepayers for elec- 5 tricity and natural gas, or any associated goods and services, as a 6 result of increased costs incurred by the electric corporation, gas 7 corporation, or utility due to legal or regulatory proceedings against 8 such electric corporation, gas corporation, or utility, including but 9 not limited to settlements, attorneys' fees, penalties, fines or costs. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth day after it shall 11 have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amendment 12 and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implementation 13 of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and 14 completed on or before such effective date.
