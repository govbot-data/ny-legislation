AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to the Fair
Fares act
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 1266 of the public authorities law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 16-a to read as follows:
3 16-a. (a) Notwithstanding any other provisions of law or the terms of
4 any contract, the authority, in consultation with the city of New York,
5 shall expand the Fair Fares NYC program to permit any individual who is
6 eligible for the program and any individual whose income is three
7 hundred percent of the federal poverty level to receive a fifty percent
8 discount on trips using the Long Island Rail Road, paratransit services,
9 New York city transit subways or buses, and/or Metro-North railroad.
10 (b) For purposes of this subdivision, "Fair Fares NYC program" shall
11 have the same meaning and eligibility standards as set forth in chapter
12 twelve of title sixty-eight of the rules of the city of New York, which
13 provides a fifty percent fare discount for designated transit options.
14 (c) Additionally, the authority shall consult with the city of New
15 York in conducting a public outreach campaign to increase public aware-
16 ness and expand usage of the Fair Fares NYC program by eligible individ-
17 uals.
18 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
