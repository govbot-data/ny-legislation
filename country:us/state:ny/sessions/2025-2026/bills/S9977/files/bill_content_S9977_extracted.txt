Sections 1, 2 and 7 of chapter 10 of the laws of 1958, 2 relating to incorporating the Islip Terrace Volunteer Exempt Firemen's 3 Benevolent Association, and providing for its powers and duties, are 4 amended to read as follows: 5 Section 1. Incorporation; membership. A membership corporation by the 6 name of "Islip Terrace Volunteer Exempt [Firemen's] Firefighter's Benev- 7 olent Association" is hereby created. It shall be composed of such 8 persons eligible to membership therein as hereinafter provided as shall 9 (a) notify the secretary of the Islip Terrace fire department in writing 10 prior to the organization meeting of such corporation hereinafter 11 provided for their desire to become members, or (b) shall attend such 12 meeting or an adjournment thereof, and also such persons so eligible as 13 may become members of such corporation pursuant to its by-laws. 14 § 2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now, or have 15 been, or who hereafter shall be volunteer members of the Islip Terrace 16 fire district fire department, or of any fire corporation in such fire 17 district, and all persons resident in such fire district or in territory 18 provided with fire protection by contract with such fire district who 19 are exempt volunteer [firemen] firefighters as defined by section two 20 hundred of the general municipal law, shall be eligible to membership in 21 the corporation hereby created; provided that any person who has been 22 expelled or removed for cause from membership in any fire company or 23 department shall not be eligible to membership in such corporation. 24 Whenever a member who is not an exempt [fireman] firefighter as defined EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
