WEBB -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to directing the department of health, in conjunction with other state agencies, to establish and maintain a directory webpage locating publicly accessi- ble lactation accommodations in state-owned and state-leased buildings The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public health law is amended by adding a new section 2 266-a to read as follows: 3 § 266-a. Publicly available lactation accommodations directory. 1. No 4 later than one year after the effective date of this section, the 5 department shall create, make available and maintain an internet webpage 6 for the purpose of providing information on the location of publicly 7 accessible lactation accommodations in state-owned buildings or state- 8 leased buildings. The department, in consultation with other state agen- 9 cies, shall include the following into the webpage: 10 (a) a comprehensive statewide directory of all publicly accessible 11 lactation accommodations in state-owned or state-leased buildings as 12 determined by the commissioner; 13 (b) an operating search function that allows users to input an address 14 to locate nearest publicly accessible lactation accommodations in state- 15 owned or state-leased buildings; 16 (c) information on the accessibility of the lactation accommodation, 17 including but not limited to, infant changing station, wheelchair acces- 18 sibility and possible security constraints; 19 (d) informational webpages including information and resources of the 20 department and other state agency programs and resources available to 21 prenatal, pregnant and postpartum individuals; and 22 (e) information available in multiple languages in accordance with 23 section two hundred two-a of the executive law.
