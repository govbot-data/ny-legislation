and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its longstanding traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Cumberland Bay Market upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2026 Small Business of the Year Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce at its 114th Annual Awards Dinner on Friday, January 30, 2026, at the Warren Ballroom at SUNY Plattsburgh; and WHEREAS, Owned and operated by Stacey Ambler and her husband, Kris, since 2018, Cumberland Bay Market, located at 1544 Cumberland Head Road in Plattsburgh, New York, has flourished as both a successful small business and a trusted neighborhood gathering place; and WHEREAS, Inspired by a shared vision of the creative possibilities a community market could offer, Stacey and Kris Ambler embraced the opportunity to purchase the market and bring that vision to life, transforming it into a dependable destination for residents and visitors alike; and WHEREAS, Over the past seven years, under Stacey Ambler's leadership, Cumberland Bay Market has evolved into more than a neighborhood store; it has become a welcoming community hub offering quality food, catering services, and a space that meets the daily needs of residents while strengthening the surrounding area; and WHEREAS, The market's success is rooted in its commitment to personal connection and neighborly care, fostering meaningful relationships with customers, staff, local vendors, and the broader community; and WHEREAS, Cumberland Bay Market stands as a model of small business excellence in the North Country, drawing patrons from across the region and demonstrating how resilience, hard work, and a community-focused mission can yield lasting success; and WHEREAS, Looking toward the future, Cumberland Bay Market continues to innovate and respond to community needs, with plans to expand healthy offerings in 2026, including additional salads, high-protein ready-made foods, gluten-free options, and the return of weekly dinner specials, reflecting a thoughtful and forward-looking approach to business; and WHEREAS, The designation of Cumberland Bay Market as the 2026 Small Business of the Year appropriately recognizes its outstanding service, entrepreneurial spirit, and steadfast commitment to enhancing the quality of life in Plattsburgh and the greater North Country region; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Cumberland Bay Market upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2026 Small Business of the Year Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Stacey and Kris Ambler, Cumberland Bay Market.
