COONEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Agriculture AN ACT to amend the agriculture and markets law, in relation to author- izing the issuance of delivery tickets electronically The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 4 of section 192 of the agriculture and markets 2 law, as amended by chapter 904 of the laws of 1983, is amended to read 3 as follows: 4 4. Except where otherwise agreed to by the parties in writing, all 5 petroleum products delivered from a vehicle shall be measured by meter 6 or other measuring device. Where petroleum products delivered from a 7 vehicle are measured by meter, the seller shall provide the buyer with a 8 mechanically prepared metered document which shall show the actual quan- 9 tities of all grades of petroleum products delivered. Where petroleum 10 products delivered from a vehicle are not measured by meter, the seller 11 shall provide the buyer a mechanically prepared metered document which 12 shall show the actual quantities of all grades of petroleum products 13 transferred to the vehicle. The delivery ticket shall also contain the 14 name and address of the seller and buyer, the date delivered, price per 15 unit measure and total price; provided, however, that such delivery 16 ticket need not set forth the total price if within five days after 17 delivery the seller provides the buyer with a written statement setting 18 forth all the foregoing information including the total price. All 19 deliveries of home heating fuel oil shall be measured by meter, the 20 delivery tickets shall be serially numbered, and a copy retained by the 21 seller for a period of one year. For systems equipped with the capabili- 22 ty of issuing an electronic receipt, ticket, or other recorded represen- 23 tation, the customer may be given the option to receive any required 24 information electronically by means of a cellular telephone, mobile 25 telephone, computer, computer tablet, or any other personal electronic 26 device, in lieu of or in addition to a hard copy. 27 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
