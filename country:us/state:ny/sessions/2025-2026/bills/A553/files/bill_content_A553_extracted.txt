Scope of field testing. (a) Every person operating a motor vehicle 35 which has been involved in an accident or collision involving damage to 36 real or personal property, personal injury or death, and who has in such 37 person's possession at or near the time of such accident or collision, a 38 mobile telephone or personal electronic device, shall at the request of 39 a police officer, submit such person's mobile telephone and/or portable 40 electronic device to the police officer solely for the purpose of field 41 testing such mobile telephone and/or portable electronic device. If such 42 field testing determines that the operator of the motor vehicle was 43 using their mobile telephone or portable electronic device in violation 44 of section twelve hundred twenty-five-c or twelve hundred twenty-five-d 45 of this article, the results of such testing shall constitute evidence 46 of any such violation. 47 (b) The department, Westchester county and any police departments and 48 their respective agents participating in the pilot program authorized by 49 this section may store log records collected during electronic scans and 50 use such log records only to determine the accuracy of field testing and 51 the prevalence of violations of section twelve hundred twenty-five-c or 52 twelve hundred twenty-five-d of this article. No user data may be 53 collected or stored by the department, Westchester county or any police 54 departments or their respective agents participating in the pilot 55 program authorized by this section.
