Santabarbara MOURNING the death of Daniel P. Wilson, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to citizens of the State of New York whose lifework and civic endeavors served to enhance the quality of life in their communities and the great State of New York; and WHEREAS, Daniel P. Wilson of the Town of Florida, New York, died on Wednesday, July 30, 2025, at the age of 76; and WHEREAS, Born to Robert D. Wilson and Agnes M. Monroe in Saratoga Springs, New York, Daniel P. Wilson graduated from Mohonasen High School and was a veteran of the U.S. Army Reserve during the Vietnam War era; and WHEREAS, Daniel P. Wilson distinguished himself in his profession and by his sincere dedication and substantial contribution to the welfare of his community; and WHEREAS, Daniel P. Wilson began his career as a union carpenter and worked various construction management roles with BBL; and WHEREAS, A man of purpose and adventurous curiosity, Daniel P. Wilson had a strong passion for aviation, veterans' causes, travel, and machinery; and WHEREAS, Inspired by his passion for aviation and community involvement, Daniel P. Wilson served as a longtime member of the Board of Trustees and recent Museum President of the Empire State Aerosciences Museum in Glenville, New York; and WHEREAS, Daniel P. Wilson's commitment to excellence, and his spirit of humanity, carried over into all fields of enterprise, including charitable and civic endeavors; and WHEREAS, Montgomery County was greatly improved throughout the years due to Daniel P. Wilson's strong leadership and dedication to his community; he began his political involvements as Town of Florida councilman, then as board member of the Montgomery County Industrial Development Agency and as Montgomery County Legislator for District 5; and WHEREAS, At the center of Daniel P. Wilson's life was his beloved wife, Jill, and his precious children, granddaughter, extensive family, and network of friends; and WHEREAS, Some of Daniel P. Wilson's most cherished moments were spent at his Florida, New York home which was his homebase for holiday meals, sledding, golf cart and ATV rides, and family gatherings; and WHEREAS, Daniel P. Wilson is survived by his son, Daniel P. Wilson II; his daughter, Melinda J. Wilson; and his granddaughter, Ava M. Wilson; and WHEREAS, Armed with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of compassion, Daniel P. Wilson leaves behind a legacy which will long endure the passage of time and will remain as a comforting memory to all he served and befriended; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to mourn the death of Daniel P. Wilson, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the family of Daniel P. Wilson.
