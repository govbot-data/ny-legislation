Senate Resolution No. 1742
 
BY: Senator STEC
 
        COMMEMORATING  the  100th Anniversary of Champlain
        Valley Physicians Hospital
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
commend  those  organizations and institutions which have demonstrated a
long and sustained commitment to improving the health and well-being  of
their communities through excellence, compassion, and service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate   the  100th  Anniversary  of  Champlain  Valley  Physicians
Hospital; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Champlain  Valley  Physicians  Hospital   first   welcomed
patients  to its Beekman Street facility in January of 1926, marking the
beginning  of  a  new  era  of  healthcare  in  the  North  Country  and
establishing  a  legacy of high-quality, patient-centered care guided by
the vision of hospital benefactor William H. Miner; and
 
  WHEREAS, Over the past century, Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital
has been shaped by generations  of  caregivers,  staff,  and  physicians
whose dedication and commitment have strengthened the hospital's role as
a trusted place of healing and a vital community resource; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  healthcare  has  evolved  over  the  last  100  years,
Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital has continued to meet the  changing
needs  of  the  community  while  remaining  grounded  in its mission to
provide compassionate, high-quality care to its North Country neighbors;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The centennial celebration of Champlain  Valley  Physicians
Hospital  stands  as  a  profound  reminder  of  the hospital's enduring
commitment to the health and well-being of the North Country  community;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Champlain  Valley  Physicians Hospital celebrates a legacy
built over a century through compassion, excellence, and  public  trust,
and  its great accomplishments are possible through the tireless efforts
of generations of caregivers, staff, physicians,  and  nurses  who  have
answered the call to serve; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate  the  100th  Anniversary  of  Champlain  Valley   Physicians
Hospital,  honoring  its  rich  history  and  celebrating  its continued
commitment to serving its community for generations to come; and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital.
