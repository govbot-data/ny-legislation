Miner; and WHEREAS, Over the past century, Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital has been shaped by generations of caregivers, staff, and physicians whose dedication and commitment have strengthened the hospital's role as a trusted place of healing and a vital community resource; and WHEREAS, As healthcare has evolved over the last 100 years, Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital has continued to meet the changing needs of the community while remaining grounded in its mission to provide compassionate, high-quality care to its North Country neighbors; and WHEREAS, The centennial celebration of Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital stands as a profound reminder of the hospital's enduring commitment to the health and well-being of the North Country community; and WHEREAS, Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital celebrates a legacy built over a century through compassion, excellence, and public trust, and its great accomplishments are possible through the tireless efforts of generations of caregivers, staff, physicians, and nurses who have answered the call to serve; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 100th Anniversary of Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital, honoring its rich history and celebrating its continued commitment to serving its community for generations to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Champlain Valley Physicians Hospital.
