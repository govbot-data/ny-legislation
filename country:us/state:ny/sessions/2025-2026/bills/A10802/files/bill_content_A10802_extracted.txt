BLUMENCRANZ -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the executive law, the public authorities law, the economic development law, the environmental conservation law and the transportation law, in relation to adding ethno-religious groups as minority group members The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "MWBE Equality Act". 3 § 2. For 37 purposes hereof, minority business enterprise shall mean any business 38 enterprise which is at least fifty-one per centum owned by, or in the 39 case of a publicly owned business, at least fifty-one per centum of the 40 stock of which is owned by citizens or permanent resident noncitizens 41 who are Black, Hispanic, Asian or American Indian, Pacific Islander 42 [or], Alaskan natives or ethno-religious, and such ownership interest is 43 real, substantial and continuing and have the authority to independently 44 control the day to day business decisions of the entity for at least one 45 year; and women-owned business enterprise shall mean any business enter- 46 prise which is at least fifty-one per centum owned by, or in the case of 47 a publicly owned business, at least fifty-one per centum of the stock of 48 which is owned by citizens or permanent resident noncitizens who are 49 women, and such ownership interest is real, substantial and continuing 50 and have the authority to independently control the day to day business 51 decisions of the entity for at least one year. 52 § 6.
