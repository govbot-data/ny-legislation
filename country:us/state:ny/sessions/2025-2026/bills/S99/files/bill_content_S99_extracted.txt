KRUEGER, GOUNARDES, MAY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Budget and Revenue AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to the creation of a public database of projects funded in the state budget The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 184 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 184. Budget-funded project database. The state division of budget 4 shall maintain a public database of all projects funded in the state 5 budget that shall include the dollar amount allocated to each project 6 funded in the budget and the last name of the member or members of the 7 legislature who submitted the funding request for such projects. Such 8 public database shall be published online on the division of budget 9 website, and shall be updated annually upon the passage of the state 10 budget. Such public database shall also include any funds allocated to 11 private organizations which are not otherwise required by law to 12 disclose the funding sources of such private organizations. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
