Subdivision 2 of section 605-a of the education law, as 2 amended by section 83 of part C of chapter 58 of the laws of 1998, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 2. In the event that a scholarship awarded is declined by a student, 5 or for any reason revoked by the commissioner or the president, its 6 benefits shall lapse and there shall be no further payments or awarding 7 of such scholarship; provided however, if an award is declined by a 8 student prior to the end of their high school program, the scholarship 9 shall be awarded, to the extent practicable, to the next most qualified 10 student pursuant to paragraph (a) of subdivision one of this section. 11 § 2.
