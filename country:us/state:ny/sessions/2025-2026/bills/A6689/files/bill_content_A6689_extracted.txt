Section 5 of the public service law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 7 to read as follows: 3 7. The commission shall evaluate hydrogen, sewage thermal energy, and 4 nuclear small modular reactors as renewable energy sources and shall 5 within one year of the effective date of this subdivision submit to the 6 governor and the legislature the results of such evaluation. 7 § 2.
