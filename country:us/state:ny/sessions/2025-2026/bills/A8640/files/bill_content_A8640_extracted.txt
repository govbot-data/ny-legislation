Legislative findings. The legislature finds and declares 2 that: 3 1. Recognition of essential services. Funeral service providers offer 4 critical support to families and communities during times of crisis and 5 loss. 6 2. Precedent in other states. Texas issues professional license plates 7 for categories such as emergency medical services personnel, peace offi- 8 cers, and professional firefighters, recognizing the unique nature of 9 their work. 10 3. Traffic and procession benefits. The identification of funeral 11 service vehicles can facilitate processions, allowing for greater coor- 12 dination with law enforcement and traffic control. 13 § 2. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new section 14 404-ff to read as follows: 15 § 404-ff. Distinctive plates for New York state licensed funeral homes 16 or funeral directors. 1. Any New York state funeral home or funeral 17 director licensed pursuant to title two of article thirty-four of the 18 public health law shall, upon request, be issued a license plate format- 19 ted at the discretion of the department bearing the words "funeral 20 service vehicle" or similar and a unique design in order to distinguish 21 it from standard commercial or passenger plates. Such license plates 22 shall be reserved for vehicles operating for the purpose of transporting 23 human remains. Such license plate need not contain the statue of liberty 24 logo.
