AN ACT to establish the "Supporting Families Together act"; and to
repeal section 420 of the social services law relating to certain
civil and criminal penalties for mandated reporters
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Supporting
2 Families Together act".
3 § 2. Section 420 of the social services law is REPEALED.
4 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
