and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Gabriella Scheer upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of a Liberty Medal, the highest honor bestowed upon an individual by the New York State Senate; and WHEREAS, The New York State Senate Liberty Medal was established by Resolution and is awarded to individuals who have merited special commendation for exceptional, heroic, or humanitarian acts on behalf of their fellow New Yorkers; and WHEREAS, On the night of April 28, 2025, Gabriella Scheer went to work at European Image in Franklin Square, New York, alongside her boss, Frank; and WHEREAS, Gabriella Scheer's shift began pleasantly as she had the opportunity to catch up with her boss, Frank, but the shift quickly turned for the worse when she heard a loud bang from the room Frank was in; and WHEREAS, When Gabriella Scheer went to investigate the loud crash, she found Frank unconscious and laying on the floor; and WHEREAS, Frank was not responding to any of Gabriella Scheer's actions, and he was quickly turning purple due to his lack of oxygen; without hesitation, Gabriella dialed 911 and was guided to begin CPR on Frank; and WHEREAS, Within minutes, a local volunteer firefighter arrived at the scene and directed Gabriella Scheer to continue CPR until more first responders arrived and transported Frank to the local hospital; and WHEREAS, After completing life-saving techniques on Frank, Gabriella Scheer, filled with a sense of duty and care, stayed at the scene to close the salon that night and volunteered to open the salon on the following mornings so Frank and his wife, Melissa, would have less to worry about; and WHEREAS, Gabriella Scheer successfully demonstrated how important it is to stay calm under pressure and to show up for others when they need it most, despite how daunting it may seem; without her heroic actions, the scene at European Image on April 28th, 2025, could have been much more tragic; and WHEREAS, Gabriella Scheer, in her spontaneous and heroic act, has so aptly demonstrated her character and compassion for the welfare of others, personifying, by virtue of her action, the collective concern of ordinary citizens across the community of New York State who voluntarily respond without thought of danger or reward when others need help; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Gabriella Scheer upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of a Liberty Medal, the highest honor bestowed upon an individual by the New York State Senate; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Gabriella Scheer.
