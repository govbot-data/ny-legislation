and WHEREAS, In keeping with this proud tradition, this Legislative Body is honored to commemorate the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program upon the occasion of its 25th Anniversary Gala to be held on Saturday, February 7, 2026, marking a quarter of a century of faithful ministry and service; and WHEREAS, Founded to meet both the spiritual and practical needs of the community of Brooklyn, New York, the International Pentecostal City Mission has served as a beacon of hope, worship, and restoration, providing a welcoming space for prayer, fellowship, Biblical teaching, and outreach rooted in Pentecostal faith and Christian compassion; and WHEREAS, For 25 years, the Mission has remained steadfast in its commitment to evangelism, discipleship, and community service, uplifting individuals and families through spiritual guidance, charitable initiatives, and programs designed to promote dignity, healing, and empowerment; and WHEREAS, The growth and impact of the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program are due, in large measure, to the visionary and devoted leadership of Bishop Petrona Reid whose faith, perseverance, and servant leadership have guided the Mission with clarity of purpose, pastoral care, and an unwavering commitment to Christian work; and WHEREAS, Under Bishop Reid's leadership, the Mission has continued to expand its reach, strengthen its ministry, and foster a faith community grounded in worship, service, and love, leaving a lasting and positive imprint on the lives of countless congregants and community members; and WHEREAS, The 25th Anniversary Gala stands as a moment of reflection, gratitude, and celebration, honoring the Program's past accomplishments while reaffirming its commitment to continued spiritual growth, service, and impact for generations to come; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor and commend the International Pentecostal City Mission Food Program upon the occasion of its 25th Anniversary Gala, to be held February 7, 2026, and to extend best wishes for continued strength, growth, and faithful service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Bishop Petrona Reid and the leadership and congregation of the International Pentecostal City Mission as a lasting expression of the respect and appreciation of this Legislative Body.
