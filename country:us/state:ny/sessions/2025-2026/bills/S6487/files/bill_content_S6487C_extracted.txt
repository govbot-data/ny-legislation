AN ACT to amend the arts and cultural affairs law, in relation to
authorizing the council on the arts to designate Little Yemen as a
cultural district
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 11 of section 3.05 of the arts and cultural
2 affairs law, as amended by chapter 323 of the laws of 2025, is amended
3 to read as follows:
4 11. To promote regions of cultural significance by providing cultural
5 institutions within such regions grants, technical assistance, or other
6 assistance authorized by this section as the council deems appropriate,
7 including, but not limited to:
8 (a) East Harlem, also known as "El Barrio", this region shall be
9 bounded by East 96th Street on the South, 5th Avenue on the West, East
10 125th Street on the North and the East River on the East; [and]
11 (b) the Harlem Renaissance Cultural District, this region shall be
12 bounded by 110th Street on the South, the Hudson River on the West,
13 155th Street on the North and the 5th Avenue on the East; and
14 (c) "Little Yemen" which shall be a region in the city of New York
15 bounded by White Plains Road on the West, Bronxdale Avenue on the East
16 and North, and Van Nest Avenue on the South.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
