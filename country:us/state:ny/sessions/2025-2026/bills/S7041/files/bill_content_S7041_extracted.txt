LANZA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Cities 1 AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to the application of special rebates and discounts to certain eligible energy users The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph 7 of subdivision (a) of section 22-602 of the 2 administrative code of the city of New York, as added by chapter 107 of 3 the laws of 2003, is amended to read as follows: 4 (7) Notwithstanding any provisions of this subdivision, special 5 rebates and discounts shall not exceed ten thousand dollars per year per 6 employee or full-time equivalent with respect to applications certified 7 pursuant to this chapter after June thirtieth, two thousand three; 8 except that such limit shall not apply to an eligible energy user that 9 conducts a manufacturing activity within the city producing new goods 10 primarily from waste paper or other recyclables collected from residen- 11 tial sources in the city. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
