Paragraph (b) of subdivision 2 of section 1676 of the 2 public authorities law is amended by adding a new undesignated paragraph 3 to read as follows: 4 Vanderbilt University for financing, refinancing, construction, 5 design, rehabilitation and improvement for facilities on the Chelsea 6 Square campus. 7 § 2. Subdivision 1 of section 1680 of the public authorities law is 8 amended by adding a new undesignated paragraph to read as follows: 9 Vanderbilt University. 10 § 3.
