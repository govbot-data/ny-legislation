The correction law is amended by adding a new section 137-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 137-a. Disciplinary hearings to be recorded. The department shall 4 ensure that every incarcerated individual disciplinary hearing held at a 5 correctional facility shall be recorded with both audio and visual 6 components. The department shall maintain and preserve the full audio 7 and video recording and shall add such recordings to the subject incar- 8 cerated individual's file within the department's database. The commis- 9 sioner shall promulgate rules and regulations to allow for access to 10 such audio and video recordings, including but not limited to when such 11 audio and video recordings can be accessed, and by whom such audio and 12 video recordings can be accessed by. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
