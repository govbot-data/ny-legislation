and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of 2025 Valedictorian Shebeke Thompson and Salutatorian Juan Lopez of East New York Family Academy, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and WHEREAS, Valedictorian Shebeke Thompson and Salutatorian Juan Lopez represent the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious resource, our youth; their achievements have brought enduring honor to their families and communities and should be recognized and saluted; and WHEREAS, As Valedictorian Shebeke Thompson and Salutatorian Juan Lopez take leave of East New York Family Academy, they bestow a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement and community service to which others may aspire; and WHEREAS, Valedictorian Shebeke Thompson and Salutatorian Juan Lopez may now stand with pride as they assess their achievements, experience the satisfaction of their labors and the joy of their accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Class of 2025 Valedictorian Shebeke Thompson and Salutatorian Juan Lopez of East New York Family Academy, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Valedictorian Shebeke Thompson and Salutatorian Juan Lopez.
