Gallahan CONGRATULATING the Cincinnatus High School Girls Soccer Team and Head Coach Lisa Sustad upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Class D State Championship WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly sought after in high school sports; this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Cincinnatus High School Girls Soccer Team and Head Coach Lisa Sustad upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Class D State Championship on Sunday, November 16, 2025 at Homer High School in Homer, New York; and WHEREAS, Family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Lady Lions throughout their journey as they ended their season with an impressive 20-2 overall record; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as soccer, which demands athletic prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Lisa Sustad and her skilled coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and WHEREAS, Head Coach Lisa Sustad and all of the outstanding athletes on the Cincinnatus High School Girls Soccer Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Cincinnatus High School Girls Soccer Team: Nevaeh Billings, Linette Custer, Hannah Ellerson, Lucy Finch, Payton Gerrard, Kimberly Hill, Payton Landers, Makayla Mills, Madelyn Pittsley, Kinsley Raymond, Jelsie Stafford, Addison Stith, Kendall Sustad, Peyton Tefft, Aubrey Tunning, Jocelyn Turshman, and Maudiline Waltz; Head Coach Lisa Sustad; and Assistant Coach T.J. Sustad on their outstanding season and capturing the NYSPHSAA Class D State Championship; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Cincinnatus High School Girls Soccer Team, Head Coach Lisa Sustad, and Assistant Coach T.J.
