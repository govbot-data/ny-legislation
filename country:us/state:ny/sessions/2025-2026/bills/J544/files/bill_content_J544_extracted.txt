and WHEREAS, Pace Windows & Doors, a trusted provider of high-quality windows and doors, is celebrating its 40th Anniversary, marking four decades of dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and exceptional customer service; and WHEREAS, Founded in 1985 by Steve Abramson, Pace Windows & Doors has continued to grow under the leadership of General Manager Greg Hahn and CEO Michael Jordano, maintaining its commitment to a high level of excellence and delivering exceptional results; and WHEREAS, Pace Windows & Doors has established strong partnerships with premier manufacturers to bring industry-leading products to homeowners, ensuring superior quality, energy efficiency, and durability; and WHEREAS, Pace Windows & Doors specializes in providing homeowners with state-of-the-art windows and doors that are designed specifically for Upstate New York's unique climate, offering energy-efficient solutions with sleek, modern aesthetics and a complete lifetime warranty; and WHEREAS, The success of Pace Windows & Doors is driven by its loyal customers, whose trust and satisfaction have fueled four decades of growth, and its dedicated staff whose commitment, expertise, and hard work have upheld the company's reputation for excellence; and WHEREAS, With its headquarters in Victor, New York, Pace Windows & Doors has played a significant role in supporting local economic growth by providing jobs and sustainable home improvement solutions as well as contributing to the economic vitality of Upstate New York; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge and celebrate the achievements of those institutions which substantially contribute to the economic and social welfare of the State of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 40th Anniversary of Pace Windows & Doors, applauding its history of excellence and wishing continued success in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Pace Windows & Doors in recognition of this significant milestone.
