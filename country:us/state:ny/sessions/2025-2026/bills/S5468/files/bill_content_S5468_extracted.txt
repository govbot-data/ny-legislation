Subdivision (a) of section 1115 of the tax law is amended 2 by adding a new paragraph 47 to read as follows: 3 (47) Fire extinguishers, fire alarms, heat alarms or carbon monoxide 4 alarms purchased for residential use during the month of October. 5 § 2.
