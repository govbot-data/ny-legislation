FAHY, CLEARE, RAMOS -- read twice and ordered print- ed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT to amend the state finance law and the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to enacting the "Creating Lasting Access to Supported and Inclusive Careers for New York (CLASSIC NY) act" The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "Creating Lasting Access to Supported and Inclusive Careers for New 3 York (CLASSIC NY) act". 4 § 2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 99-uu to 5 read as follows: 6 § 99-uu. Disability employment innovation fund. 1. There is hereby 7 established in the custody of the state comptroller a special fund to be 8 known as the "disability employment innovation fund". 9 2. Such fund shall consist of all moneys collected pursuant to section 10 four hundred four-ff of the vehicle and traffic law, and any other 11 moneys appropriated, credited, or transferred thereto. 12 3. Moneys of the fund shall be made available for programs supporting 13 employment opportunities for individuals with disabilities and shall be 14 administered through a coordinated interagency process led jointly by 15 the office for people with developmental disabilities, the adult career 16 and continuing education services - vocational rehabilitation program 17 within the department of education, and the office of mental health, in 18 consultation with the department of labor and the commissioner of motor 19 vehicles. 20 4. On or before February first of each year, the administering agen- 21 cies designated under subdivision three of this section shall jointly 22 submit a report to the governor, the temporary president of the senate, 23 and the speaker of the assembly detailing revenues received, expendi- 24 tures made, and outcomes achieved, including but not limited to the 25 number of individuals served and employment outcomes. 26 § 3. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new section 27 404-ff to read as follows: EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
