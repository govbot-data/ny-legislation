and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Gamma Rho Sigma Alumni Chapter of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., upon the occasion of hosting its 70th Anniversary at a commemorative Gala to be held on Saturday, May 30, 2026; and WHEREAS, Founded in 1914, Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., has long stood as a pillar of advocacy, service, and intellectual advancement, guided by the ideals of Brotherhood, Scholarship, and Service and dedicated to addressing the needs of the communities it serves; and WHEREAS, For 70 years, the Gamma Rho Sigma Alumni Chapter has faithfully upheld these principles through sustained civic leadership, mentorship of youth, community service initiatives, and strategic partnerships that have strengthened neighborhoods and uplifted families across New York and beyond; and WHEREAS, The milestone of this 70th Anniversary reflects not only longevity but also the steadfast dedication of generations of Brothers whose collective efforts have advanced social justice, educational opportunity, and community empowerment; and WHEREAS, Leading the chapter with vision and purpose is Donovan Fraser, President of the Gamma Rho Sigma Alumni Chapter, whose guidance ensures that the Chapter continues to exemplify the principles of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., fostering scholarship, service, and brotherhood within the community; and WHEREAS, This celebratory occasion is proudly hosted in partnership with the Nu Beta Sigma Alumnae Chapter of Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc., exemplifying the historic bond between Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., and Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc., an alliance rooted in shared values, mutual respect, and collaborative service; and WHEREAS, The presence of the Nu Beta Sigma Alumnae Chapter at this Gala underscores the enduring legacy of fraternal-sororal unity and collective commitment to uplifting the communities which both organizations are pledged to serve; and WHEREAS, The Gamma Rho Sigma Alumni Chapter's 70th Anniversary Gala serves as a moment of reflection, celebration, and recommitment, honoring the Chapter's storied past while inspiring continued leadership, excellence, and service for generations to come; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize this historic milestone and commend the Gamma Rho Sigma Alumni Chapter for its distinguished legacy and continued contributions to civic life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Gamma Rho Sigma Alumni Chapter of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., upon the occasion of hosting its 70th Anniversary Gala, and to extend heartfelt congratulations and best wishes for continued growth, unity, and service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Donovan Fraser, President, Gamma Rho Sigma Alumni Chapter of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc., with appreciation to the Nu Beta Sigma Alumnae Chapter of Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc., for its partnership in commemorating this distinguished occasion.
