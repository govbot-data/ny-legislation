Buttenschon COMMEMORATING the 58th Anniversary of Stevener's Backhoe Service WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and honor those businesses whose longevity, dedication, and service have contributed significantly to the economic vitality and quality of life of the communities they serve; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its longstanding traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 58th Anniversary of Stevener's Backhoe Service, a family-owned business whose commitment to excellence has made it a trusted name throughout the Mohawk Valley; and WHEREAS, Stevener's Backhoe Service was founded in 1968 by Glenn Stevener, whose vision, hard work, and dedication established a reputation for quality workmanship, reliability, and integrity that remains the cornerstone of the company today; and WHEREAS, Through decades of steady growth and unwavering commitment to customer service, the company evolved into Glenn Stevener Inc., while maintaining the core values upon which it was founded; and WHEREAS, During the 1980s, a second generation of the Stevener family joined the business, helping to expand its operations while preserving the standards of professionalism and customer satisfaction that had earned the company the respect of its clients and peers; and WHEREAS, Today, Stevener's Backhoe Service is owned and operated by the third generation of the Stevener family, continuing the tradition of committed, quality, and reliable service; and WHEREAS, Over the course of nearly six decades, the company has provided vital excavation and site development services, including land grading, water and sewer line installation and repair, leak detection, subdivision development, land clearing, and snowplowing services, supporting residential, commercial, and municipal projects throughout the region; and WHEREAS, Stevener's Backhoe Service has earned the trust of countless customers through its dedication to quality workmanship, innovative solutions, and a hands-on approach that allows clients to work directly with company leadership to meet their project needs; and WHEREAS, The success of Stevener's Backhoe Service stands as a testament to the value of family-owned businesses, which help strengthen local economies, create employment opportunities, and serve as pillars of their communities; and WHEREAS, As Stevener's Backhoe Service celebrates 58 years of service, it is fitting and proper to recognize the contributions of the Stevener family and their employees, whose hard work and dedication have left a lasting mark on the Mohawk Valley and Central New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 58th Anniversary of Stevener's Backhoe Service, and to extend its sincere gratitude and best wishes to the Stevener family and all those associated with the company for their many years of service and success; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Stevener's Backhoe Service.
