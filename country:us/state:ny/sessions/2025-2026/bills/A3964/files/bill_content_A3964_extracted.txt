The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1244 to read as follows: 3 § 1244. Registration of bicycles with electric assist used in deliv- 4 eries. 1. In a city of one million or more, every bicycle with electric 5 assist as defined in section one hundred two-c of this chapter which is 6 used by a person to make commercial deliveries, either as an employee or 7 as an independent contractor, shall be registered and issued a number 8 plate by the department. No person shall use a bicycle with electric 9 assist in such city for the purpose of making commercial deliveries 10 unless such bicycle with electric assist shall have a distinctive number 11 assigned to it by the commissioner and a number plate issued by the 12 commissioner with a number corresponding to that of the certificate of 13 registration. 14 2. Each such number plate shall be conspicuously displayed on the rear 20 of the bicycle, securely fastened so as to prevent the same from swing- 21 ing. 22 4. A fee of up to twenty-five dollars for such number plates may be 23 charged by the commissioner.
