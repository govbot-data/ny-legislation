HARCKHAM -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Local Government AN ACT to amend the general municipal law, in relation to permitting the village of Croton-on-Hudson, Westchester county, to lease certain sports field fences for advertisements The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The general municipal law is amended by adding a new 2 section 77-k to read as follows: 3 § 77-k. Sports field fence leases in the village of Croton-on-Hudson, 4 Westchester county. Notwithstanding any provision of any general, 5 special or local law or of any charter to the contrary, the village of 6 Croton-on-Hudson, Westchester county, may lease any and all sports field 7 fences at Dobbs Park, Duck Pond Park, Firefighters Memorial Field and 8 David J. Manes Memorial Field, annually, at a nominal rent, to a not- 9 for-profit corporation or organization, organized under the laws of the 10 state of New York for the purpose of permitting the sale of advertising, 11 with the revenues to be used solely for the care of such Dobbs Park, 12 Duck Pond Park, Firefighters Memorial Field and David J. Manes Memorial 13 Field that are all in the village of Croton-on-Hudson, Westchester coun- 14 ty. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
