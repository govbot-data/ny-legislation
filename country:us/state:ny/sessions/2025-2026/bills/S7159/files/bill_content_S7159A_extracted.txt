AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to compensation for
required code enforcement personnel training
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 376-a of the executive law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 5 to read as follows:
3 5. Subject to the amounts appropriated for this purpose, all code
4 enforcement personnel shall be paid for any training required pursuant
5 to the provisions of this section at a rate set by the secretary of
6 state. Such rate shall not be less than the code enforcement person-
7 nel's regular rate of pay, or less than any rate of pay specified in a
8 collective bargaining agreement covering such code enforcement person-
9 nel.
10 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
11 have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amendment and/or
12 repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implementation of
13 this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and completed
14 on or before such effective date.
