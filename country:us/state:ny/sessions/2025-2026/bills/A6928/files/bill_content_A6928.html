BILL NOA06928
 
SAME ASSAME AS S06530
 
SPONSORRosenthal
 
COSPNSRGallagher, Simon, Paulin, Tapia, Carroll R
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add §§17-0709 & 17-0833, amd §§17-0701 & 17-0703, En Con L
 
Prohibits the department of environmental conservation from issuing or authorizing any permits to new or expanding large concentrated animal feeding operations.

        AN ACT to amend the  environmental  conservation  law,  in  relation  to
          prohibiting  the department of environmental conservation from issuing
          or authorizing any permits to  new  or  expanding  large  concentrated
          animal feeding operations
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The environmental conservation law is amended by  adding  a
     2  new section 17-0709 to read as follows:
     3  §  17-0709. Applications  for a permit for the operation of concentrated
     4               feeding operations in which livestock  are  kept;  prohibi-
     5               tion.
     6    1.  The department shall not issue a license or permit or approval for
     7  the operation of a new large concentrated animal feeding  operation,  or
     8  an addition to, or expansion of, an existing animal feeding operation if
     9  the  addition  or  expansion would cause the animal feeding operation to
    10  become a large concentrated animal feeding  operation  or  increase  the
    11  animal  stabling  or  confinement  capacity of an existing large concen-
    12  trated animal feeding operation.
    13    2. As used in this section, "large concentrated animal feeding  opera-
    14  tion" shall have the same meaning as defined in 6 NYCRR 750-1.2(a)(23).
    15    3.  Nothing  in this section shall be interpreted to prohibit a person
    16  from operating an animal feeding operation, if prior  to  the  effective
    17  date  of this section, such person has already received a permit for the
    18  operation of an animal feeding operation as otherwise allowed under this
    19  chapter.
    20    § 2. The environmental conservation law is amended  by  adding  a  new
    21  section 17-0833 to read as follows:
    22  § 17-0833. General  permit  for  concentrated animal feeding operations;
    23               modification.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
                                                                   LBD10880-01-5

        A. 6928                             2
 
     1    1. The department shall revise the SPDES general  permit  for  concen-
     2  trated  animal feeding operations, permit number GP-0-22-001, to require
     3  additional, independent approval from the department  for  any  proposed
     4  expansion  to  the  operation of a concentrated animal feeding operation
     5  which will result in an increased animal confinement capacity.
     6    2.  If  a  proposed  expansion to the operation of an existing concen-
     7  trated animal facility operation would cause the animal  feeding  opera-
     8  tion to become a large concentrated animal feeding operation, as defined
     9  in  6  NYCRR  750-1.2(a)(23),  or  increase  the stabling or confinement
    10  capacity of an existing large concentrated animal feeding operation, the
    11  department shall deny the proposed expansion.
    12    § 3. Paragraph a of subdivision 5 of section 17-0701 of  the  environ-
    13  mental  conservation  law, as amended by chapter 801 of the laws of 1973
    14  and such subdivision as renumbered by chapter 701 of the laws  of  1994,
    15  is amended and a new subdivision 10 is added to read as follows:
    16    a.  except  as  provided  in  subdivision  ten of this section, if the
    17  commissioner finds on the basis of the submitted  plans  or  application
    18  that  the discharge from the outlet or point source or modified disposal
    19  system will not be in contravention of the standards, criteria,  limita-
    20  tions, rules and regulations adopted or applied by the department; or
    21    10. In the case of a concentrated animal feeding operation, as defined
    22  in  6  NYCRR  750-1.2(a)(23),  the  commissioner shall not issue a SPDES
    23  permit of any kind, regardless of whether the permit constitutes a SPDES
    24  general permit or an individual SPDES permit, to an owner or operator of
    25  an operation that fits the regulatory definition of a large concentrated
    26  animal feeding operation.
    27    § 4. Subdivision 2 of section 17-0703 of the  environmental  conserva-
    28  tion  law,  as separately amended by chapters 400 and 801 of the laws of
    29  1973, is amended and a new subdivision 3 is added to read as follows:
    30    2. [Public] Except as provided in subdivision three of  this  section,
    31  public  hearings,  on due notice, shall be conducted by the commissioner
    32  or by [his] such commissioner's designated representative in  connection
    33  with  the  issuance  of  any  order  or determination denying, revoking,
    34  continuing or modifying a permit as provided by section 17-0701 of  this
    35  title,  paragraph  f of subdivision 4 of section 17-0303 of this article
    36  and title 8 [hereof] of this article, unless the applicant or  permittee
    37  waives  said  hearing in writing and no request for a public hearing has
    38  been received from any interested party.
    39    3. In the case of a large concentrated animal  feeding  operation,  as
    40  defined  in  6  NYCRR  750-1.2(a)(23), an applicant is not entitled to a
    41  public hearing in response to the denial of a permit application.
    42    § 5. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day  after  it  shall
    43  have become a law.
