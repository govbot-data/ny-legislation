Section 8-0107 of the environmental conservation law, as 2 added by chapter 612 of the laws of 1975, is amended to read as follows: 3 § 8-0107. Agency implementation. 4 1. All agencies shall review their present statutory authority, admin- 5 istrative regulations, and current policies and procedures for the 6 purpose of determining whether there are any deficiencies or inconsist- 7 encies therein which prohibit full compliance with the purposes and 8 provisions of this article, and shall recommend or effect such measures 9 as may be necessary to bring their authority and policies into conformi- 10 ty with the intent, purposes, and procedures set forth in this article. 11 They shall carry out its terms with minimum procedural and administra- 12 tive delay, shall avoid unnecessary duplication of reporting and review 13 requirements by providing, where feasible, for combined or consolidated 14 proceedings, and shall expedite all proceedings hereunder in the inter- 15 ests of prompt review.
