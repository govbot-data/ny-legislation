AN ACT in relation to directing the New York state bridge authority to
provide a report outlining their efforts and progress on decreasing
and deterring suicide attempts on authority bridges; and providing for
the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. On July 1, 2027, the New York state bridge authority shall
2 provide to the governor, the speaker of the assembly, and the temporary
3 president of the senate a report outlining their efforts and progress on
4 decreasing and deterring suicide attempts on authority bridges. Such
5 report shall include at a minimum:
6 1. Information on the recommendations outlined in the Comprehensive
7 Plan for Suicide Prevention, Education, and Awareness report;
8 2. The current status of the implementation of recommendations from
9 this report on each bridge;
10 3. A timeline of any current projects on each bridge related to climb
11 deterrent fencing or for the purposes of decreasing suicide attempts on
12 authority bridges;
13 4. Any barriers to implementing the previous recommendations made,
14 including capital needs and structural challenges on one or more bridg-
15 es;
16 5. Alternative measures or additional actions discovered that may
17 further the purpose of decreasing suicide attempts on authority bridges;
