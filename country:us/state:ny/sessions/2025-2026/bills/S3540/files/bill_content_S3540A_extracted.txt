AN ACT authorizing the county of Cattaraugus to discontinue as refor-
ested lands certain reforested lands in the town of Perrysburg
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Notwithstanding section 219 of the county law to the
2 contrary, the county of Cattaraugus acting by and through its governing
3 body and upon such terms and conditions as determined by such body, is
4 hereby authorized to discontinue the use of lands described in section
5 two of this act acquired for reforestation purposes for the purposes of
6 rehabilitating the dam and developing the land for future use. The lands
7 described in section two of this act are currently vacant property not
8 used by the public for recreational or reforestation purposes. The
9 authorization provided in this section shall be effective only upon the
10 acquisition by the county of Cattaraugus of lands described in section
11 three of this act.

44 § 3. The authorization provided in section one of this act shall be
45 effective only upon the condition that on or before the alienation and
46 conveyance of lands described in section two of this act, the county of
47 Cattaraugus shall acquire and dedicate real property for reforestation
48 purposes, of equal or greater fair market value to the lands being
49 alienated. In the event that the lands to be acquired by the county of
50 Cattaraugus for reforestation purposes pursuant to this act are not
51 equal to or greater than the fair market value of the lands to be alien-
52 ated by the county of Cattaraugus, the county shall dedicate the differ-
53 ence between the fair market value of the lands to be alienated and the
54 fair market value of the lands to be acquired for the acquisition of
55 additional lands for reforestation purposes or for the improvement of
56 existing reforested lands.
