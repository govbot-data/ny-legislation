AN ACT in relation to authorizing Michael O'Brien to take the compet-
itive civil service examination for the position of police officer and
be placed on the eligible list for employment as a full-time police
officer for the village of Le Roy
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Notwithstanding the provisions of section 58 of the civil
2 service law, including the age requirements under paragraph (a) of
3 subdivision 1 of such section, or any other provision of law to the
4 contrary, Michael O'Brien, who is a resident of the hamlet of Getzville,
5 in the town of Amherst, in the county of Erie, shall be eligible to take
6 the competitive civil service examination for the position of police
7 officer and shall, contingent upon his score on such examination, be
8 eligible to be placed on the eligible list for appointment as a full-
9 time police officer with the Le Roy police department in the village of
10 Le Roy, county of Genesee.
11 § 2. Michael O'Brien shall otherwise be subject to the merit and
12 fitness provisions of the civil service law applicable to full-time
13 police officers.
14 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
