No. 1281 IN SENATE April 6, 2026 ___________ Introduced by Sens. C. RYAN, BORRELLO, MYRIE, SCARCELLA-SPANTON, SKOUF- IS, WEBER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation -- reported favorably from said committee, ordered to first and second report, amended on second report, ordered to a third reading, and to be reprinted as amended, retaining its place in the order of third reading AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to authorizing early renewal of vehicle registration for members of the armed forces who will be engaged in the performance of their official military duties upon its expiration The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 4 of section 251 of the vehicle and traffic law 2 is renumbered subdivision 5 and a new subdivision 4 is added to read as 3 follows: 4 4. If a member of the armed forces is expected to be engaged in the 5 performance of ordered military duties at the time of their motor vehi- 6 cle registration expiration, the commissioner shall issue a renewal of 7 their vehicle registration prior to its expiration upon presentation of 8 such active military service member's military orders or a certified 9 letter from such active military service member's commanding officer 10 verifying their duties, regardless of the time remaining between such 11 orders being presented and the expiration of the vehicle's registration. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect March 1, 2028 and shall apply to vehi- 13 cle registrations expiring on and after such date.
