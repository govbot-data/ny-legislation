Subdivision (l) of section 19.09 of the mental hygiene law, 2 as added by chapter 434 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 (l)(1) The office, in consultation with the department of health, 5 shall maintain on its website a publicly available directory of all 6 distributors of opioid [antagonists] reversal agents to the public, 7 including but not limited to, pharmacies, prevention programs and not- 8 for-profits.
