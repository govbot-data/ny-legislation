FALL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to parking permit systems for park-and-rides on Staten Island The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1640-t to read as follows: 3 § 1640-t. Parking permit systems for park-and-rides on Staten Island. 4 1. Notwithstanding the provisions of any law to the contrary, the city 5 of New York may, by adoption of a local law or ordinance, provide for a 6 parking permit system for park-and-rides and fix and require the payment 7 of fees applicable to parking within the area in which such parking 8 system is in effect in accordance with the provisions of this section. 9 2. Such parking permit systems for park-and-rides shall only be estab- 10 lished within the borough of Staten Island for park-and-rides located 11 within such borough. 12 3. Residents of Staten Island shall receive a permit free of charge 13 and out-of-state residents shall pay an annual fee for such permit. 14 4. The local law or ordinance providing for such permit systems shall: 15 (a) set forth the factors which necessitate the enactment of such 16 permit system; 17 (b) provide that motor vehicles registered pursuant to section four 18 hundred four-a of this chapter shall be exempt from any permit require- 19 ment; 20 (c) provide the times of the day and the days of the week during which 21 permit requirements shall be in effect; 22 (d) provide the schedule of fees to be paid for the parking permits; 23 and 24 (e) provide that such fees shall be credited to the general fund of 25 the city. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. LBD08724-01-5 A. 4552 2 1 5.
