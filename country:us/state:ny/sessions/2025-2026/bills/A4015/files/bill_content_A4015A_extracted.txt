AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to increasing
penalties for certain speed violations
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Paragraph 3 of subdivision h of section 1180 of the vehicle
2 and traffic law, as amended by section 6 of part C of chapter 62 of the
3 laws of 2003, is amended to read as follows:
4 3. Every person convicted of a violation of paragraph two of subdivi-
5 sion (d), subdivision (f) or (g) of this section shall be punished as
6 follows:
7 (i) Where the court or tribunal records or enters that the speed upon
8 which the conviction was based exceeded the applicable speed limit by
9 not more than ten miles per hour, by a fine of not less than ninety nor
10 more than one hundred [fifty] seventy-five dollars;
11 (ii) Where the court or tribunal records or enters that the speed upon
12 which the conviction was based exceeded the applicable speed limit by
13 more than ten miles per hour, but not more than thirty miles per hour,
14 by a fine of not less than one hundred eighty nor more than three
15 hundred fifty dollars or by imprisonment for not more than thirty days,
16 or by both such fine and imprisonment, provided, however, that where the
17 vehicle is either (A) in violation of any rules or regulations involving
18 an out-of-service defect relating to brake systems, steering components
19 and/or coupling devices, or (B) transporting flammable gas, radioactive
20 materials or explosives, the fine shall be three hundred fifty dollars
21 or imprisonment for not more than thirty days, or both such fine and
22 imprisonment;
