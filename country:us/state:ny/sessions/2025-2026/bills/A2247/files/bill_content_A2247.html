Amd §§81-a & 27, Gen City L; amd §§267-a & 271, Town L; amd §§7-712-a & 7-718, Vil L; amd §§239-c & 239-h, Gen Muni L
 
Relates to providing notice of quorum requirements to applicants for building or site permits, variances, or subdivision plats at zoning board of appeals, local planning board, county planning board, and regional planning council meetings.

        Introduced  by  M.  of  A. SANTABARBARA -- read once and referred to the
          Committee on Local Governments
 
        AN ACT to amend the general city law, the town law, the village law  and
          the  general  municipal law, in relation to providing notice of quorum
          requirements to applicants for a building or site permit, variance, or
          subdivision plat at zoning board of appeals and planning  board  meet-
          ings
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 13 of section 81-a of the general city  law  is
     2  amended by adding a new paragraph (c) to read as follows:
     3    (c)  Notice  of quorum requirements. At the start of each meeting of a
     4  board of appeals at which the adoption of  a  motion  or  resolution  to
     5  approve  an  application  for  a  building  or site permit, variance, or
     6  subdivision plat shall be voted upon pursuant to paragraph (a)  of  this
     7  subdivision,  the  chairperson  of the board of appeals shall inform any
     8  applicant for a building or site permit, variance, or  subdivision  plat
     9  of the following:
    10    (i)  the  number of members of the board of appeals who are present or
    11  absent at such meeting;
    12    (ii) the number of votes of the members of the board  of  appeals  who
    13  are  currently  present  at  such meeting that is required to approve an
    14  application for a building or site permit, variance, or subdivision plat
    15  pursuant to paragraph (a) of this subdivision; and
    16    (iii) the right of such applicant to table  their  application  for  a
    17  building  or  site  permit, variance, or subdivision plat until a future
    18  meeting of the board of appeals if  such  applicant  believes  that  the
    19  number  of  members of the board of appeals who are present or absent at
    20  such meeting will  substantially  impair  such  applicant's  chances  of
    21  getting  their  application  for a building or site permit, variance, or
    22  subdivision plat approved.
