Trump State Park, in Putnam and Westchester counties to a new name.Go to topS10137 Text: STATE OF NEW YORK ________________________________________________________________________ 10137 IN SENATE April 30, 2026 ___________ Introduced by Sen. BOTTCHER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Cultural Affairs, Tourism, Parks and Recreation AN ACT directing the commissioner of the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation to conduct a legal review for the re-designation of Donald J. Trump State Park, in Putnam and Westchester counties to a new name The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The commissioner of the office of parks, recreation and 2 historic preservation shall conduct a legal review of the re-designation 3 of Donald J. Trump State Park, in Putnam and Westchester counties, to 4 determine whether the authority exists to change the name of such park. 5 Within one year of the effective date of this act, the commissioner 6 shall submit the findings of such review to the governor, the temporary 7 president of the senate, and the speaker of the assembly. If such 8 review determines that such legal authority exists, the commissioner 9 shall change the name of such park. In the event the name of such park 10 is changed, the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation 11 shall provide for the installation and maintenance of appropriate 12 signage and any other administrative actions necessary to implement the 13 name change and to improve the park for the enjoyment of the public. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
