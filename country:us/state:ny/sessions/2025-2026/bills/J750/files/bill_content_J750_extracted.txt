and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 40th Anniversary of Rancho Sonhos E Juventude de Portugal, to be celebrated on Saturday, April 5, 2025, at the Portuguese Heritage Center in Mineola, New York; and WHEREAS, The history, heritage, and the lives of the people of all countries and all cultures are portrayed in their music and dance; the art and act of sharing them enriches us altogether; and WHEREAS, In 1985, a group of Portuguese people formed by Avelino Rlivo, David Fernandes, Domingos Cunha, Etlario Oliveira had a dream of creating a folklore group in Mineola; they formed Sonhos de Portugal and later changed their name to Ranchos Juventude de Portugal in 1986; and WHEREAS, Rancho Sonhos E Juventude de Portugal aims to maintain Portuguese culture through music, song, dance, and traditional costumes; and WHEREAS, Organizations which provide a means of preserving a part of humanity's diverse and rich cultural past and which contribute to the community in such noble endeavors as aforementioned, are held in the highest regard by this Legislative Body; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 40th Anniversary of Rancho Sonhos E Juventude de Portugal; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Rancho Sonhos E Juventude de Portugal.
