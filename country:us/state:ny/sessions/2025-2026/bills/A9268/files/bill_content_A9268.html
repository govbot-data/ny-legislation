        Introduced  by M. of A. STECK -- read once and referred to the Committee

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in  relation  to  requiring
          instruction on work zone safety awareness as part of the pre-licensing
          course
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Paragraph (c-2) of subdivision 4  of  section  502  of  the
     2  vehicle and traffic law, as added by chapter 585 of the laws of 2002, is
     3  amended to read as follows:
     4    (c-2)  "Work  Zone  Safety"  awareness component. (i) The commissioner
     5  shall provide in the pre-licensing course, set forth in paragraph (b) of
     6  this subdivision, a mandatory component in "Work Zone Safety"  awareness
     7  education,  totaling  no less than thirty minutes, as a prerequisite for
     8  obtaining a license to operate a motor vehicle.
     9    (ii) The purpose of the component is to educate prospective  licensees
    10  on:
    11    (A) the potential dangers to construction workers, construction equip-
    12  ment  operators  and  operators  of  motor  vehicles  in  a highway work
    13  zone[.];
    14    (B) the legal obligation to  yield  the  right  of  way  and  exercise
    15  reasonable  care  in the approach of an authorized emergency vehicle, as
    16  set forth in section eleven hundred forty-four of this chapter;
    17    (C) the legal obligation to exercise due care to avoid colliding  with
    18  a parked, stopped or standing authorized emergency vehicle, hazard vehi-
    19  cle,  vehicle  displaying  a  blue or green light or certain other motor
    20  vehicles, as set forth in section eleven hundred  forty-four-a  of  this
    21  chapter; and
    22    (D)  the consequences of violations, including those captured by auto-
    23  mated work zone speed enforcement systems.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
                                                                   LBD14081-01-5

        A. 9268                             2
 
     1    (iii) For the purposes of this paragraph, the term "work  zone"  shall
     2  include  "work area" as defined by section one hundred [sixty] sixty-one
     3  of this chapter, and  "restricted  highway"  as  authorized  in  section
     4  sixteen hundred twenty-five of this chapter.
     5    [(ii)]  (iv)  The  commissioner  shall  establish a curriculum for the
     6  "Work Zone Safety" component which shall include but not be limited to:
     7    (A) instruction [describing the potential hazards of driving through a
     8  work  zone,  whether  or  not  work,  maintenance   or   other   related
     9  construction  is  being  undertaken  therein,  and  information  on  the
    10  provisions of law relating to driving within a work zone  and  sanctions
    11  for violations of such provisions, including speeding in a work zone] on
    12  the  identification  and  navigation of highway construction and mainte-
    13  nance zones;
    14    (B) education topics, including, but not limited to, signage, flagging
    15  operations, barriers, lane shifts, and variable speed limits;
    16    (C) education on the  legal obligation to yield the right of  way  and
    17  exercise  reasonable  care  in  the  approach of an authorized emergency
    18  vehicle, as set forth in section eleven hundred forty-four of this chap-
    19  ter, and penalties for noncompliance;
    20    (D) education on the legal obligation to exercise due  care  to  avoid
    21  colliding  with a parked, stopped or standing authorized emergency vehi-
    22  cle, hazard vehicle, vehicle displaying a blue or green light or certain
    23  other  motor  vehicles,  as  set  forth  in   section   eleven   hundred
    24  forty-four-a of this chapter, and penalties for noncompliance;
    25    (E)  statistics  and real-life consequences of unsafe driving behavior
    26  in work zones;
    27    (F) strategies for safe driving behavior in the presence of work  zone
    28  equipment and personnel; and
    29    (G)  information  on  the  photo speed violation monitoring systems in
    30  highway construction or maintenance work areas and associated penalties,
    31  as set forth by section eleven hundred eighty-e of this chapter.
    32    [(iii)] (v) In developing  such  curriculum,  the  commissioner  shall
    33  consult  with  the commissioner of transportation, the superintendent of
    34  the state police, representatives of the highway construction  industry,
    35  representatives  of  highway  construction workers, highway safety offi-
    36  cials, road safety experts, and any other group  that  the  commissioner
    37  [believes  can  contribute to] deems necessary to ensure a comprehensive
    38  and current presentation of the issue.
    39    (vi) Such curriculum shall be adaptable to both in-person  and  online
    40  formats  and  include  a  certification  of  completion  and tracking of
    41  student performance.
    42    (vii) The commissioner shall ensure a portion of  the  examination  on
    43  traffic  laws  contains  at  least one question relating to the driver's
    44  ability to understand how to drive safely in a work zone.
    45    (viii) The commissioner shall ensure that completion of the "Work Zone
    46  Safety" component is documented and retained as part of the  applicant's
    47  licensing record.
    48    §  2.  This  act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
    49  have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amendment and/or
    50  repeal of any rule or regulation necessary  for  the  implementation  of
    51  this  act  on its effective date are authorized to be made and completed
    52  on or before such effective date.
