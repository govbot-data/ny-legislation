The not-for-profit corporation law is amended by adding a 2 new section 1506-f to read as follows: 3 § 1506-f. Memorial lands facility. 4 (a) Any person who operates an entity on land, that is primarily used 5 only for the respectful disposition of cremated and naturally organ- 6 ically reduced remains shall hold a valid memorial lands facility permit 7 issued and approved by the cemetery board. 8 (b) To receive a memorial lands facility permit the operator of a 9 memorial lands facility shall be a not-for-profit corporation subject to 10 the regulatory oversight of the cemetery board, incorporated in the 11 state, that shall file with the department and adhere to an operation 12 plan for the maintenance of the land and the disposition of cremated and 13 naturally organically reduced remains.
