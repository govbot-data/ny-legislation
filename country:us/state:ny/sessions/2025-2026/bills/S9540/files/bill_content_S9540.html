                    IN SENATE
 
                                     March 23, 2026

        Introduced by Sen. SEPULVEDA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when
          printed to be committed to the Committee on Consumer Protection
 
        AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to requiring motor
          vehicle rental companies to provide consumers with all associated fees
          at the time of rental
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may  be  cited  as
     2  the "car rentals integrity act".
     3    §  2.  Subdivision  1  of section 396-z of the general business law is
     4  amended by adding three new paragraphs (m),  (n)  and  (o)  to  read  as
     5  follows:
     6    (m)  "Mandatory  fee"  means  any  fee or surcharge that a consumer is
     7  required to pay before obtaining a rental motor vehicle.
     8    (n) "Extra cost" means any  fee  or  surcharge  relating  to  services
     9  requested  by the renter of the motor vehicle including, but not limited
    10  to, optional equipment, child safety seats, additional driver fees,  and
    11  toll service charges.
    12    (o)  "Total  price"  means the complete cost of the rental vehicle and
    13  shall contain any mandatory fees and extra costs.
    14    § 3. Subdivision 8 of section 396-z of the general  business  law,  as
    15  amended  by  chapter  109  of  the  laws  of 2018, is amended to read as
    16  follows:
    17    8. No rental vehicle company shall advertise or quote  a  rental  rate
    18  that  does  not  include  all  charges,  except taxes [or optional items
    19  and/or services or any mileage charge], which the  renter  must  pay  to
    20  obtain  a  rental  vehicle. [Provided, however, a rental vehicle company
    21  shall be permitted to  separately  quote  and  charge  airport  fees  as
    22  defined  herein,  which  shall  be  in  addition to the rental rate; and
    23  provided further that advertised rental rates that include locations  at
    24  which  airport  fees  apply  shall clearly indicate that additional fees
    25  apply.] All rental vehicle companies shall provide renters with  a  list
    26  of  all  mandatory  fees  and extra costs including, but not limited to,
    27  airport  surcharges,   concession   recovery   fees,   energy   recovery
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.

     1  surcharges,  young  driver surcharges, additional driver fees, and daily
     2  service fees.
     3    §  4.  This  act shall take effect on the thirtieth day after it shall
     4  have become a law; provided, however  that  the  amendments  to  section
     5  396-z  of  the general business law made by sections one and two of this
     6  act shall not affect the expiration and reversion of  such  section  and
     7  shall expire and be deemed repealed therewith.
