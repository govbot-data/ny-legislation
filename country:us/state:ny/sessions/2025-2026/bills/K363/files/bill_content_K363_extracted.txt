Barclay CONGRATULATING the Daldrop Group on the groundbreaking of its new facility in Oswego County, New York, on April 3, 2025 WHEREAS, It is with great pride that this Legislative Body recognizes the groundbreaking of Daldrop Group's new manufacturing facility in Oswego County, New York, on Tuesday, April 3, 2025, marking a historic milestone in the company's growth and a major investment in the region; and WHEREAS, Daldrop SBB, a subsidiary of Daldrop Group, has selected the L. Michael Treadwell Oswego County Industrial Park in Schroeppel, New York, for its new manufacturing site, reinforcing Daldrop's leadership in the cleanroom manufacturing industry and its ongoing commitment to innovation in the pharmaceutical sector; and WHEREAS, The new facility will represent the largest expansion outside of Germany in Daldrop Group's 70-year history, further solidifying the company's presence in the United States and contributing to the region's growing prominence in high-tech manufacturing; and WHEREAS, The state-of-the-art facility, which will span 40,000 square feet, will adhere to LEED construction standards and provide cutting-edge manufacturing capabilities for Daldrop's SHELMEQ Cleanroom systems, helping the pharmaceutical industry continue to deliver vital medications to the market faster and more affordably; and WHEREAS, The expansion is expected to create numerous employment opportunities for local laborers, technicians, and engineers, contributing to the growth of Oswego County and the broader central New York region, while fostering the continued development of highly skilled workers in cleanroom technology; and WHEREAS, The successful realization of this expansion is supported by the collaborative efforts of Empire State Development, Operation Oswego County Inc., and CenterState CEO, as well as local workforce development organizations such as Train, Develop, Optimize (TDO) and the Board of Cooperative Educational Services (BOCES), which will work with Daldrop to provide training and development programs for the new facility's workforce; and WHEREAS, Daldrop President of U.S. Operations, Mike Sullivan, expressed his pride in bringing manufacturing jobs to upstate New York, further emphasizing that Daldrop's expansion represents a continued commitment to local communities and the partnership between global leadership and regional economic development; and WHEREAS, Daldrop's expansion and investment in the Syracuse area serves as a testament to the company's commitment to innovation, economic growth, and community development, while also enhancing the region's global competitiveness in high-tech manufacturing; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the Daldrop Group on the groundbreaking of its new manufacturing facility in Oswego County, and to express its full support for the company's continued success and its positive impact on the local community, economy, and workforce; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Daldrop Group.
