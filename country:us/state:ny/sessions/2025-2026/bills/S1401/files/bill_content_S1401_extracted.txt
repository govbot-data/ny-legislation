There is hereby created within the division an independent police and 12 peace officer licensing review board composed of seven members, who 13 shall be selected as follows: 14 (a) three shall be appointed by the governor; 15 (b) one shall be appointed by the temporary president of the senate; 16 (c) one shall be appointed by the speaker of the assembly; and 17 (d) two shall be appointed by the attorney general. 18 2. Any member chosen to fill a vacancy created otherwise than by 23 expiration of term shall be appointed for the unexpired term of the 24 member whom they are to succeed. The review board shall be permitted to review individual cases 29 pertaining to police and peace officers. In response, the review board 30 may do the following if the board finds that such officer has committed 31 professional misconduct: 32 (a) Issue licensing penalties for professional misconduct; 33 (b) Revoke and/or suspend licenses issued by the municipal police 34 training council; 35 (c) Issue fines; 36 (d) Require such police or peace officer to undergo therapy or treat- 37 ment; and/or 38 (e) Require such police or peace officer to fulfill additional contin- 39 uing education courses. 40 5.
