BASKIN -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to E-ZPass availability and waiving deposit fees for a certain period after a rate increase The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 378-b to read as follows: 3 § 378-b. E-ZPass availability. 1. If the authority shall vote to 4 increase any fees, rentals or charges for the use of the thruway or any 5 part thereof pursuant to subdivision eight of section three hundred 6 fifty-four of this title for cash or users who do not use E-ZPass to pay 7 such fees, rentals or charges, the requirement to make a deposit owed to 8 secure a new E-ZPass transponder shall be waived for a period of no less 9 than sixty days from the date that the increase takes effect. 10 2. Any retail location in the state which sells E-ZPass shall be 11 required to accept cash payment as a valid method to purchase an E-ZPass 12 or for E-ZPass holders to reload their E-ZPass transponder. 13 3. For purposes of this section, "E-ZPass" and "E-ZPass transponder" 14 shall mean a receiver-transmitter issued by the authority in connection 15 with electronic toll collection when such receiver-transmitter is 16 affixed to the exterior or interior of a vehicle in accordance with 17 mounting instructions provided by the authority. 18 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately, provided however, that 19 subdivision 2 of section 378-b of the public authorities law as added by 20 section one of this act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it 21 shall have become a law. Effective immediately the addition, amendment 22 and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implementation 23 of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and 24 completed on or before such date.
