Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01204 Summary:BILL NOJ01204  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORPARKER COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01204 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1204 BY: Senator PARKER HONORING One Girl Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its Inaugural Icon Awards Luncheon Benefit to be held on June 18, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the esteemed practice of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend organizations whose unwavering commitment to empowerment, innovation, and community advancement significantly enriches the cultural and social fabric of New York State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to honor One Girl Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its Inaugural Icon Awards Luncheon Benefit, to be held on Wednesday, June 18, 2025; and WHEREAS, This distinguished event will pay homage to exemplary and innovative women of the past, present, and future-true groundbreakers in their respective fields-who have made their mark on yesterday and today, and who will change the course of our future for the better, embodying the ideals One Girl Inc. holds dear; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, One Girl Inc. has been dedicated to uplifting and empowering women and girls through initiatives that promote leadership, education, and social impact, fostering a community where every girl can realize her full potential; and WHEREAS, The Inaugural Icon Awards Luncheon Benefit represents not only a celebration of individual achievements but also a testament to the collective strength and resilience of women who inspire change and drive progress in their communities and beyond; and WHEREAS, It is fitting that this Legislative Body recognize and commend One Girl Inc. for its unwavering dedication to fostering empowerment, celebrating excellence, and building a more inclusive and equitable society; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor One Girl Inc. upon the occasion of its Inaugural Icon Awards Luncheon Benefit, and to extend its highest commendation and best wishes for continued success in its vital mission; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to One Girl Inc.
