AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to dedicating a portion of
the state highway system to Hector L. Soto
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 345-d to
2 read as follows:
3 § 345-d. Portion of the state highway system to be dedicated to Hector
4 L. Soto. All that portion of the state highway system constituting
5 United States route two hundred two from its intersection with Rosman
6 Road to its intersection with state route forty-five in the town of
7 Haverstraw, county of Rockland, shall be dedicated to Hector L. Soto.
8 § 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal-
9 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system
10 as dedicated pursuant to section one of this act, provided that such
11 signing shall state "Highway Dedicated to Hector L Soto". However, to
12 avoid confusion and to limit any possible disruption of commerce, the
13 dedication called for pursuant to section one of this act shall be one
14 of ceremonial nature and the official name of such highway shall not be
15 changed as a result of this act.
16 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
