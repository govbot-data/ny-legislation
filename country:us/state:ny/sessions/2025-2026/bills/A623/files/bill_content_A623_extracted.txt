Section 6802 of the education law is amended by adding four 2 new subdivisions 31, 32, 33 and 34 to read as follows: 3 31. A remote site shall comply with section 20 sixty-eight hundred eight of this article, governing requirements for 21 permit to operate a pharmacy.
