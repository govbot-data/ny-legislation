A05897 Summary:

BILL NOA05897
 
SAME ASSAME AS S03959
 
SPONSORBronson
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §§70, 140 & 140-a, Judy L
 
Creates the fourteenth judicial district consisting of the county of Monroe; provides for continuation of the seventh judicial district and the positions of certain seventh and fourteenth judicial district justices; makes related provisions.

        Introduced  by M. of A. BRONSON -- read once and referred to the Commit-

        AN ACT to amend the judiciary law, in relation to the  creation  of  the
          fourteenth judicial district consisting of the county of Monroe
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 70 of the judiciary law, as amended by chapter  690
     2  of the laws of 2007, is amended to read as follows:
     3    §  70.  Judicial  departments.  The  state is hereby divided into four
     4  judicial departments. The first department shall consist of the counties
     5  embraced within the first and twelfth  judicial  districts;  the  second
     6  department  shall  consist  of  the counties embraced within the second,
     7  ninth, tenth, eleventh and  thirteenth  judicial  districts;  the  third
     8  department  shall  consist  of  the  counties embraced within the third,
     9  fourth and sixth judicial districts; the fourth department shall consist
    10  of the counties embraced within the fifth,  seventh  [and],  eighth  and
    11  fourteenth judicial districts.
    12    §  2.  The opening paragraph and the seventh undesignated paragraph of
    13  section 140 of the judiciary law, the opening paragraph  as  amended  by
    14  chapter  690  of the laws of 2007 and the seventh undesignated paragraph
    15  as amended by chapter 10 of the laws of 1948,  are  amended  and  a  new
    16  fourteenth undesignated paragraph is added to read as follows:
    17    The   state  is  hereby  divided  into  [thirteen]  fourteen  judicial
    18  districts, pursuant to the provisions of the first section of the  sixth
    19  article  of  the  constitution,  which  districts  shall  be arranged as
    20  follows:
    21    The seventh judicial district shall consist of the counties of Living-
    22  ston, Wayne, Seneca, Yates, Ontario, Steuben[, Monroe] and Cayuga;
    23    The fourteenth judicial  district  shall  consist  of  the  county  of
    24  Monroe.    The  creation  of such fourteenth judicial district shall not
    25  affect or impair the rights, privileges or compensation of  any  officer
