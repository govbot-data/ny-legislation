Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 "Sasha's Law". 3 § 2. Subdivision 5 of section 125.25 of the penal law, as amended by 4 chapter 23 of the laws of 2024, is amended and a new subdivision 6 is 5 added to read as follows: 6 5. Knowingly, the defendant distributes fentanyl or any substance 14 containing fentanyl and causes the death of another person. 15 § 3. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 120.12-a to read 16 as follows: 17 § 120.12-a Aggravated criminal sale of a controlled substance resulting 18 in serious physical injury. 19 1. A person is guilty of aggravated criminal sale of a controlled 20 substance resulting in serious physical injury when such person knowing- 21 ly manufactures, sells, or distributes fentanyl or any substance 22 containing fentanyl, and as a result of such manufacture, sale or 23 distribution, causes the serious physical injury of another person.
