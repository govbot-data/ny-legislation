OBERACKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to establishing an adopt-a-rural cemetery program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 144-b 2 to read as follows: 3 § 144-b. Adopt-a-rural cemetery program. 1. The secretary of state 4 shall develop and implement an adopt-a-rural cemetery program the 5 purposes of which shall include, but not be limited to, improving, 6 repairing, and maintaining cemeteries in rural communities which are 7 registered with and overseen by the division of cemeteries and are 8 located in any municipality except in a city having a population of one 9 million or more inhabitants. A municipality may opt to participate in 10 the program. The program shall include but not be limited to: 11 (a) coordinating services by volunteers or groups to improve, repair 12 and maintain cemeteries; and 13 (b) providing and installing appropriate signs identifying those 14 volunteers or groups adopting particular rural cemeteries. 15 2.
