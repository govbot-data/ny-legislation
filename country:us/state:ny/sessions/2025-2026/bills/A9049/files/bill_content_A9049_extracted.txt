SIMON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to the regional cost index for purposes of the calculation of total foundation aid The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subparagraph 2 of paragraph a of subdivision 4 of section 2 3602 of the education law, as amended by section 10-a of part A of chap- 3 ter 56 of the laws of 2025, is amended to read as follows: 4 (2) The regional cost index shall reflect an analysis of labor market 5 costs based on median salaries in professional occupations that require 6 similar credentials to those of positions in the education field, but 7 not including those occupations in the education field, provided that 8 the regional cost indices for the two thousand seven--two thousand eight 9 school year and thereafter shall be as follows: 10 Labor Force Region Index 11 Capital District 1.124 12 Southern Tier 1.045 13 Western New York 1.091 14 Hudson Valley 1.314 15 Long Island/NYC [1.425] 1.496 16 Finger Lakes 1.141 17 Central New York 1.103 18 Mohawk Valley 1.000 19 North Country 1.000 20 Provided that in the two thousand twenty-five--two thousand twenty-six 21 school year and thereafter, the regional cost index for Westchester 22 county shall be 1.351 and the regional cost index for Long Island/NYC 23 shall be 1.496. 24 § 2. This act shall take effect July 1, 2025; provided, however, that 25 if section 10-a of part A of chapter 56 of the laws of 2025 shall not 26 have taken effect on or before such date then section one of this act 27 shall take effect on the same date and in the same manner as such part 28 of such chapter of the laws of 2025, takes effect.
