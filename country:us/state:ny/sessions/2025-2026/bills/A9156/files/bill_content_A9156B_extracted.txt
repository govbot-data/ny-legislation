AN ACT to amend the general business law, in relation to prohibiting the
sale of 7-hydroxymitragynine products
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section
2 392-l to read as follows:
3 § 392-l. Sale of 7-hydroxymitragynine products. 1. For purposes of
4 this section, the term "7-hydroxymitragynine products" shall mean any
5 substance, material, mixture, compound, preparation, extract, or
6 finished goods that contains 7-hydroxymitragynine at a level that
7 exceeds two percent of total alkaloids or one milligram per serving.
8 2. The provisions of this section shall apply to the sale, offer for
9 sale, delivery, or distribution of all 7-hydroxymitragynine products.
10 3. No corporation, partnership, limited liability company, firm,
11 online platform, or any other business entity doing business within this
12 state shall knowingly sell, offer for sale, or deliver 7-hydroxymitragy-
13 nine products.
14 4. Any corporation, partnership, limited liability company, firm,
15 online platform, or other business entity doing business within this
16 state that violates the provisions of this section by knowingly selling,
17 offering for sale, or delivering 7-hydroxymitragynine products shall be
18 subject to a civil penalty of not more than five hundred dollars for a
19 first violation, and one thousand dollars for each subsequent violation,
