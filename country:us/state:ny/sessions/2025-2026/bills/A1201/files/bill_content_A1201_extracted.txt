The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1700-a to read as follows: 3 § 1700-a. Response to reports. 1. Every TNC shall notify any passenger 4 reporting an incident of sexual assault that occurred during a TNC 5 prearranged trip that the TNC will not report such incident to law 6 enforcement. 7 2. Every TNC shall ensure that every passenger that reports an inci- 8 dent of sexual assault that occurred during a TNC prearranged trip is 9 advised of their right to make a report to local law enforcement and/or 10 state police. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
