ZACCARO -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to eliminating fees for the provisions of transcripts for recipients of a general education diploma or high school equivalency diploma The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 317 of the education law, as added by chapter 170 2 of the laws of 1994, is amended to read as follows: 3 § 317. General education development exam; high school equivalency 4 diploma. 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law, no fee shall be 5 established for admission to the general education development exam. 6 2. No fee shall be charged for providing transcripts or proof of award 7 of a high school equivalency diploma. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect on the thirtieth day after it shall 9 have become a law.
