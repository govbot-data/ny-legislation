SOLAGES, GALLAGHER, KELLES, KIM, EPSTEIN, LUNS- FORD, RAGA, HEVESI -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the urban development corporation act, the state finance law, the general municipal law and the economic development law, in relation to prohibiting confidentiality and non-disclosure provisions from inclusion in certain contracts entered into by the state or a municipality The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 1 of chapter 174 of the laws of 1968, constituting 2 the urban development corporation act, is amended by adding a new 3 section 11-a to read as follows: 4 § 11-a. Non-disclosure agreements; prohibitions. Notwithstanding any 5 other provision of law to the contrary, no agreement or contract entered 6 into by the state or any instrumentality thereof, any party negotiating 7 on behalf of such governmental entity, any municipality or public corpo- 8 ration for projects, including but not limited to tax incentives, 9 payments in lieu of taxes, tax abatements, bonds, notes, loans, grants 10 or rebates, shall contain any provision, clause or language that 11 provides that the contract or agreement or any of its terms are confi- 12 dential or that the parties to the agreement or contract are prohibited 13 from disclosing, discussing, describing or commenting upon the terms of 14 the agreement or contract. 15 § 2.
