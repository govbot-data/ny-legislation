and WHEREAS, The quality of life of the citizens of the State of New York is greatly enriched by its exceptional cultural, educational, and recreational institutions; these institutions give positive definition to the profile and disposition of the communities of the State of New York and profoundly strengthen our society with their commitment; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce upon the occasion of celebrating its 100th Anniversary in 2025; the organization is honored to celebrate the past and shape the future of the Ticonderoga Area; and WHEREAS, Since its founding in 1925, the mission of the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce (TACC) has been to initiate and provide programs, services, and leadership which will enhance a cooperative business community and create a vibrant economy, making the Ticonderoga Area an exceptional place to live, work, and visit; and WHEREAS, TACC's members have focused on the organization's vision to be the leading advocate for business, serving as a driving force in the economic vitality of the Greater Ticonderoga Area, uniting communities, and maximizing resources; and WHEREAS, Additionally, the key areas of focus for the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce are membership, business, tourism, communication, leadership, and organization; and WHEREAS, As one of the area's leaders in economic development, TACC and the Ticonderoga Area have expanded significantly within the last decade; and WHEREAS, The members of the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce have striven to provide benefits, events, and initiatives to create a vibrant and cooperative business community; and WHEREAS, In 2024, TACC made an addition to the name of the community's business organization; thus, the full title is Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce & Center For Economic Vitality; and WHEREAS, The expanded name denotes attributes of a successful business, including business development services, business trainings, employment and workforce development programs, tourism and marketing initiatives, and partnerships; and WHEREAS, The tireless effort of the members of the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce showcases their commitment to remain business focused, community minded, and future driven; and WHEREAS, For the past 100 years, the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce has contributed to the growth of the business community and the economic future of the Ticonderoga Area; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce upon the occasion of celebrating its 100th Anniversary in 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Ticonderoga Area Chamber of Commerce & Center For Economic Vitality.
