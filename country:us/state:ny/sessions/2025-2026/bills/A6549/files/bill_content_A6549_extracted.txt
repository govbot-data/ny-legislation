Privacy by default. 1. No operator shall offer a covered plat- 44 form in this state without conducting commercially reasonable age 45 verification to determine whether a user is a covered minor. The attor- 46 ney general shall promulgate regulations identifying methods for commer- 47 cially reasonable, privacy protective, and technically feasible age 48 verification, which shall consider the size, financial resources, and 49 technical capabilities of covered platforms, the costs and effectiveness 50 of available age determination techniques for users of such platforms, 51 and the audience of such platforms. Such methods shall meet commercial- 52 ly reasonable accuracy minimums, to be prescribed by the attorney gener- 53 al, that can reduce instances of false negatives and false positives and 54 detect and prevent method circumvention in light of available technology 55 and existing legal obligations of covered platforms to conduct age 56 verification. Information collected for the purpose of determining a A. 6549--A 3 1 covered user's age under this subdivision shall not be used for any 2 purpose other than age determination and shall be deleted immediately 3 after an attempt to determine a covered user's age, except where neces- 4 sary for compliance with any applicable provisions of state or federal 5 law or regulation. 6 2. For all users determined under a commercially reasonable age 7 verification method by an operator to be a covered minor, such operator 8 shall utilize the following settings by default for covered minors, 9 which shall ensure that no user who is not already connected to a 10 covered minor may: 11 (a) communicate directly and privately within the covered platform 12 with such minor; 13 (b) view the full profile of such minor; 14 (c) tag such minor in posted content; 15 (d) view the geographic location of such minor if a covered platform 16 provides a mechanism by which users share their locations within such 17 platform; 18 (e) download media posted by a covered minor on such minor's profile; 19 and/or 20 (f) engage in a financial transaction with such minor. 21 3. No operator shall suggest or recommend the profile of a covered 22 minor to another user not already connected to another minor; provided, 23 however, that this subdivision shall not apply to profile suggestions or 24 recommendations that are made as a result of a covered minor or user 25 syncing contacts with a covered platform. 26 4. A parent of a covered minor or a covered minor themself may over- 27 ride the default privacy settings provided in subdivisions two and three 28 of this section, provided, however, that if the covered minor is deter- 29 mined by an operator to be under the age of sixteen, a parent of such 30 minor must approve any privacy setting that is less protective than that 31 prescribed in subdivisions two and three of this section.
