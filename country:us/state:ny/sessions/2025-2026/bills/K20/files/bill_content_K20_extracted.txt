Slater COMMEMORATING the 80th Anniversary of the Rotary Club of the Town of Brewster, New York WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and celebrate significant milestones of organizations that contribute to the well-being of their communities and make a lasting impact on society; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 80th Anniversary of the Rotary Club of the Town of Brewster, New York, a momentous occasion for this exemplary organization; and WHEREAS, The Rotary Club of the Town of Brewster, New York, located in Putnam County, was founded in 1945 and has been a driving force in fostering civic responsibility, promoting humanitarian service, and enhancing the quality of life in the community; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, members of the Rotary Club of Brewster have worked tirelessly to support various charitable projects, service initiatives, and community development efforts in Putnam County and beyond, embodying the Rotary motto, "Service Above Self"; and WHEREAS, The Rotary Club of Brewster has provided educational scholarships, organized local food drives, assisted in public health initiatives, supported youth programs, and improved public infrastructure in ways that have had a positive and far-reaching impact on local residents; and WHEREAS, The members of the Rotary Club of Brewster have exemplified the true spirit of volunteerism and community service, providing a model of dedication and leadership for others to follow; and WHEREAS, The Rotary Club of Brewster is also an integral part of the global Rotary organization whose efforts in fighting diseases, supporting education, and promoting peace and goodwill throughout the world continue to inspire individuals and groups to engage in acts of kindness and compassion; and WHEREAS, The Rotary Club of Brewster has made invaluable contributions to local communities through service projects, fundraising events, and countless volunteer hours, benefiting all members of the community, particularly those in need; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body acknowledges the Rotary Club of the Town of Brewster for its remarkable contributions to the quality of life in the region and expresses its appreciation for steadfast commitment to the people it serves; and WHEREAS, It is appropriate to recognize and commend the Rotary Club of the Town of Brewster for its ongoing legacy of service, its contributions to the betterment of society, and the remarkable 80 years of accomplishments it has achieved to date; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the Rotary Club of the Town of Brewster, New York, upon the occasion of its 80th Anniversary, and to commend its members for their remarkable service to the community and the greater good; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Rotary Club of the Town of Brewster.
