The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 99-ss to 25 read as follows: 26 § 99-ss. Resilient retrofits loan and grant fund. 1. There is hereby 27 established in the joint custody of the state comptroller and the 28 commissioner of the division of housing and community renewal a special 29 fund to be known as the "resilient retrofit loan and grant fund" to be 30 administered in accordance with this section and section twenty-nine-k 31 of the executive law. 32 2. The fund shall consist of all moneys appropriated for its purpose, 33 all moneys transferred to such fund pursuant to law, any repayments of 34 principal and interest from the resilient retrofit loan and grant 35 program administered pursuant to section twenty-nine-k of the executive 36 law, and all other moneys required by this section or any other law to 37 be paid into or credited to this fund. 38 3. Moneys in such fund shall be kept separate from and shall not be 39 commingled with any other moneys in the custody of the comptroller or 40 the commissioner of taxation and finance. Any moneys of the fund not 41 required for immediate use may, at the discretion of the comptroller, in 42 consultation with the director of the budget, be invested by the comp- 43 troller in obligations of the United States or the state, or in obli- 44 gations the principal and interest on which are guaranteed by the United 45 States or by the state. Any income earned by the investment of such 46 moneys shall be added to and become a part of and shall be used for the 47 purposes of such fund. 48 4. Moneys expended from such fund shall be used to supplement and not 49 supplant or replace any other available recovery or relief funds, 50 including federal or state funding, which would otherwise have been 51 expended for reimbursement or appropriated to local governments for 52 natural hazard mitigation or resilience projects. 53 5. The moneys of the fund shall be paid out, without appropriation, on 54 the audit and warrant of the state comptroller on vouchers certified or 55 approved by the commissioner of the division of housing and community 56 renewal as provided in section twenty-nine-k of the executive law.
