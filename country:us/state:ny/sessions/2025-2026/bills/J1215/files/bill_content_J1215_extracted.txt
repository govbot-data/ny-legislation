Smalls and Illustrious Commandress Lauren B. Wiggins WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and pay tribute to those esteemed fraternal organizations and exemplary individuals who devote themselves to uplifting their communities and fostering a legacy of service, unity, and excellence; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to honor Al Maseer No. 251 upon the occasion of hosting its Joint Ball Weekend, to be held from Friday, September 26 through Sunday, September 28, 2025; and WHEREAS, This celebratory weekend stands as a distinguished moment in the life of the Temple and Court, as it brings together Nobles and Daughters, friends, and supporters in recognition of shared service and accomplishments; and WHEREAS, The 2025 Joint Ball Weekend will especially honor two outstanding leaders: Illustrious Potentate Kenyell T. Smalls and Illustrious Commandress Lauren B. Wiggins, who have served with distinction and dedication throughout their respective terms; and WHEREAS, Illustrious Potentate Kenyell T. Smalls is celebrated for his steadfast commitment to leadership, brotherhood, and community engagement; through his visionary guidance of Al Maseer Temple No. 251, he has advanced the Temple's mission of charitable service, civic responsibility, and fraternal excellence; and WHEREAS, Illustrious Commandress Lauren B. Wiggins is likewise recognized for her tireless dedication, compassionate leadership, and unwavering support for the Daughters of Al Maseer Court No. 251; her stewardship has strengthened the Court's impact on the lives of women and families through mentorship, service, and empowerment; and WHEREAS, The Joint Ball Weekend is a moment to reflect on the achievements of these exceptional leaders, celebrate their enduring legacies, and reaffirm the bonds of unity and purpose that define the Prince Hall Shriners and Daughters; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Al Maseer No. 251 upon the occasion of hosting its 2025 Joint Ball Weekend, and to recognize Illustrious Potentate Kenyell T. Smalls and Illustrious Commandress Lauren B. Wiggins for their extraordinary leadership and service; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Al Maseer Temple No. 251, Al Maseer Court No. 251, and the distinguished honorees as an expression of high respect and admiration.
