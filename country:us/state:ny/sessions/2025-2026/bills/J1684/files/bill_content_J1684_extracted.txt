Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01684 Summary:BILL NOJ01684  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORGRIFFO COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01684 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1684 BY: Senator GRIFFO COMMEMORATING the 5th Anniversary of Lafa Mediterranean by Zeina's WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the achievements of those individuals and businesses that contribute substantially to the economic vitality of their communities, thereby enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 5th Anniversary of Lafa Mediterranean by Zeina's, to be celebrated on Monday, March 2, 2026; and WHEREAS, Founded in 2021 by the Zeina family, Lafa Mediterranean by Zeina's has become a local favorite known for fresh Mediterranean food, welcoming hospitality, and strong community roots; and WHEREAS, The Zeina family, which includes Albert, Layla, Elias, Melissa, and Sarkis, is proud to provide unique and memorable cuisine to the New Hartford, New York, community; and WHEREAS, Currently owned and operated by Elias Zeina, Lafa Mediterranean by Zeina's will be celebrating its five successful years of business by offering discounts, merchandise, giveaways, and free Turkish coffee to customers who visit during the celebration; and WHEREAS, The success of Lafa Mediterranean by Zeina's is in direct correlation to the efforts of its staff and owners, whose involvement is, and always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of the community; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business and its contributions to the local and State economies, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the sparkling successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 5th Anniversary of Lafa Mediterranean by Zeina's, to be celebrated on Monday, March 2, 2026; and RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Lafa Mediterranean by Zeina's.
