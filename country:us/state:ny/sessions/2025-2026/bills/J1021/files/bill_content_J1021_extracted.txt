Aguilar upon the occasion of being named the 2025 Youth of the Year by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor the youth of today, and leaders of tomorrow, whose character and achievements best exemplify the ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Valentina C. Aguilar upon the occasion of being named the 2025 Youth of the Year by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club; and WHEREAS, Each year since 1947, Boys & Girls Clubs of America has selected members for this honor who embody the values of leadership, community service, academic excellence and good character; Valentina C. Aguilar truly exemplifies those qualities; and WHEREAS, A member of the organization since elementary school, Valentina C. Aguilar has greatly benefited from the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club which has enabled her to reach her full potential as a productive, caring, responsible citizen; and WHEREAS, Valentina C. Aguilar credits the Boys & Girls Club for helping her shape into the person she is today, and for giving her the freedom to be herself at a place that feels like home; and WHEREAS, As Youth of the Year, Valentina C. Aguilar will take on additional responsibilities, including serving as president of Grenville Baker's Keystone Club, acting as a role model for other club kids, and attending public events such as the Donor Recognition Reception, Banquet of Champions, and golf outings; and WHEREAS, Poised eagerly with enthusiasm and determined purpose, this young woman now stands honorably, with just pride in her accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the caring counsel and support provided by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club and her family; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Valentina C. Aguilar upon the occasion of being named the 2025 Youth of the Year by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Valentina C.
