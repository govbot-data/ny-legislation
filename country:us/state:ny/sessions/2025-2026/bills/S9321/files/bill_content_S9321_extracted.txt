Subsequent financial verification 47 shall comply with the home energy fair practices act and minimize 48 burdensome requirements. 49 (d) Utility corporations shall accept applications or forms for 50 medical emergency, life-support equipment, and elderly, blind or disa- 51 bled designation via email, regular mail, in-person delivery, online 52 accounts, a dedicated online portal for such purpose and facsimile. 53 (e) Utility corporations shall review and act upon submitted applica- 54 tions or forms under this subdivision, within five business days of 55 receipt.
