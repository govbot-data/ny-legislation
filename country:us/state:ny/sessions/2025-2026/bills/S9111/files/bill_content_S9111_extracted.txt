Subdivision 11 of section 310 of the executive law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows: 3 (d) the following to the extent that any projects are under the over- 4 sight or relating to the powers of: 5 Indian Point Decommissioning Oversight Board as established by the 6 Public Service Commission; 7 Indian Point Closure Task Force, as established in part RR of chapter 8 fifty-eight of the laws of two thousand seventeen. 9 § 2.
