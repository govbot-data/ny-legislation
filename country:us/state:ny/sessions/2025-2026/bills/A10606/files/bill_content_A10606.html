BILL NOA10606
 
SAME ASSAME AS S02302
 
SPONSORHyndman
 
COSPNSRColton
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §6902, add §6913, Ed L
 
Establishes certification procedures for the profession of nurse anesthesia; provides for the issuance of a certification to practice as a certified registered nurse anesthetist upon the filing of an application, having a license as a registered professional nurse, having the appropriate educational requirements and paying the fifty dollar initial certification fee and the triennial thirty dollar registration fee.

        Introduced  by M. of A. HYNDMAN -- read once and referred to the Commit-
          tee on Higher Education
 
        AN ACT to amend the education law, in  relation  to  the  profession  of
          nurse anesthesia
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Legislative  intent.  The  legislature  hereby  finds  that
     2  certified  registered nurse anesthetists are highly educated and trained
     3  advanced practice nurses who hold a national professional certification,
     4  and whose competency in the professional practice of nurse anesthesia in
     5  hospitals, free-standing ambulatory surgery  centers,  clinics,  profes-
     6  sional  offices  and  other facilities is well known. Nurse anesthetists
     7  are integral to the continued professional delivery of services in  such
     8  health care facilities throughout the state.
     9    The  legislature  further  finds  that  statutory recognition of nurse
    10  anesthetists will further enhance  patient  safety  by  codifying  their
    11  professional  scope of practice that is currently governed in this state
    12  by regulations promulgated pursuant to the public health law in the case
    13  of hospitals and ambulatory surgery centers.
    14    The legislature also finds that while nurse anesthetists are  advanced
    15  practice  nurses, licensed as registered professional nurses under arti-
    16  cle 139 of the education law, no provision  of  this  act  is  meant  to
    17  limit,  diminish  or in any way impinge upon the practice of the profes-
    18  sion of nursing as a registered professional nurse under such article.
    19    Therefore, the legislature hereby declares that, due to the  intrinsic
    20  nature  of  the administration of anesthesia services, the integral part
    21  that certified registered nurse anesthetists play in the  safe  delivery
    22  of  such  services and the need to protect and enhance patient safety in
    23  all health care facilities, it is in the interest of the people of  this
    24  state to recognize the national professional certification of registered
    25  nurse  anesthetists  and  that  the  existing scope of practice of nurse
    26  anesthesia, as currently practiced in this state, be codified  in  stat-
    27  ute.

     1    §  2.    Section  6902 of the education law is amended by adding a new
     2  subdivision 4 to read as follows:
     3    4.  (a) The practice of registered professional nursing by a certified
     4  registered nurse anesthetist, certified under section sixty-nine hundred
     5  thirteen of this article may include the practice of  nurse  anesthesia.
     6  Nurse  anesthesia  includes:  the administration of anesthesia and anes-
     7  thesia related care to patients; preanesthetics evaluation and  prepara-
     8  tion;  anesthetic  induction, maintenance and emergence; post anesthesia
     9  care; perianesthetics and clinical support functions; and  pain  manage-
    10  ment,  in  collaboration  with  a licensed physician qualified by law to
    11  determine the need for anesthesia services, provided such  services  are
    12  performed  in  accordance  with a written practice agreement and written
    13  practice protocols. The written practice agreement shall include explic-
    14  it provisions for the resolution of any disagreement between the collab-
    15  orating physician and the certified registered nurse anesthetist regard-
    16  ing a matter of  anesthesia  treatment  that  is  within  the  scope  of
    17  practice  of  both.  To  the  extent  the practice agreement does not so
    18  provide, then the collaborating physician's treatment shall prevail.
    19    (b) Prescriptions for drugs, devices  and  anesthetic  agents  may  be
    20  issued  by a certified registered nurse anesthetist, under this subdivi-
    21  sion and section sixty-nine hundred thirteen of this article, in accord-
    22  ance with the practice agreement and practice protocols.  The  certified
    23  registered nurse anesthetist shall obtain a certificate from the depart-
    24  ment  upon  successfully  completing  a program including an appropriate
    25  pharmacology component, or its equivalent, as established by the commis-
    26  sioner's regulations, prior to prescribing under this  subdivision.  The
    27  certificate  issued  under  section  sixty-nine hundred thirteen of this
    28  article shall state whether the certified registered  nurse  anesthetist
    29  has  successfully  completed such a program or equivalent and is author-
    30  ized to prescribe under this subdivision.
    31    (c) Each practice agreement shall provide for patient  records  review
    32  by  the collaborating physician in a timely fashion but in no event less
    33  often than every three months. The names  of  the  certified  registered
    34  nurse anesthetist and the collaborating physician shall be clearly post-
    35  ed  in  the  practice setting of the certified registered nurse anesthe-
    36  tist.
    37    (d) The practice protocol shall reflect current accepted  medical  and
    38  nursing practice. The protocols shall be filed with the department with-
    39  in  ninety  days  of the commencement of the practice and may be updated
    40  periodically. The commissioner shall make regulations  establishing  the
    41  procedure  for the review of protocols and the disposition of any issues
    42  arising from such review.
    43    (e) No physician shall enter into practice agreements with  more  than
    44  four  certified registered nurse anesthetists who are not located on the
    45  same physical premises as the collaborating physician.
    46    (f) Nothing in this subdivision shall be deemed to limit  or  diminish
    47  the  practice  of the profession of nursing as a registered professional
    48  nurse under this article or any other law, rule, regulation  or  certif-
    49  ication,  nor  to deny any registered professional nurse the right to do
    50  any act or engage in any practice authorized  by  this  article  or  any
    51  other law, rule, regulation or certification.
    52    (g) The provisions of this subdivision shall not apply to any activity
    53  authorized,  pursuant to statute, rule or regulation, to be performed by
    54  a registered professional nurse in a  hospital  as  defined  in  article
    55  twenty-eight of the public health law.

     1    § 3. The education law is amended by adding a new section 6913 to read
     2  as follows:
     3    § 6913. Certificates for nurse anesthesia practice. 1. For issuance of
     4  a  certificate  to  practice as a certified registered nurse anesthetist
     5  under subdivision four of section sixty-nine hundred two of  this  arti-
     6  cle, the applicant shall fulfill the following requirements:
     7    (a) Application: file an application with the department;
     8    (b)  License:  be  licensed  as a registered professional nurse in the
     9  state;
    10    (c) Education: (i) have satisfactorily completed educational  prepara-
    11  tion  for  provision  of  these  services in a program registered by the
    12  department or in a program accredited by a national body  recognized  by
    13  the department or determined by the department to be the equivalent; and
    14  (ii)  submit  evidence  of current certification or recertification by a
    15  national certifying body, recognized by the department;
    16    (d) Fees: pay a fee to the department of fifty dollars for an  initial
    17  certificate authorizing nurse anesthesia practice and a triennial regis-
    18  tration fee of thirty dollars.
    19    After a certified registered nurse anesthetist's initial registration,
    20  registration  under this section shall be coterminous with the certified
    21  registered nurse anesthetist's registration as a professional nurse.
    22    2. Only a person certified under this  section  shall  use  the  title
    23  "certified registered nurse anesthetist".
    24    3.  The provisions of this section shall not apply to any act or prac-
    25  tice authorized by any other law, rule, regulation or certification.
    26    4. The provisions of this section shall  not  apply  to  any  activity
    27  authorized,  pursuant to statute, rule or regulation, to be performed by
    28  a registered professional nurse in a  hospital  as  defined  in  article
    29  twenty-eight of the public health law.
    30    5.  An  individual who is authorized to practice as a certified regis-
    31  tered nurse anesthetist in a hospital under regulations of  the  depart-
    32  ment  of  health  may use the title "certified registered nurse anesthe-
    33  tist" in connection with that practice.  If such an  individual  applies
    34  for  a  certificate  under  this section, they may satisfy the education
    35  requirement of subdivision one of this section by  presenting  satisfac-
    36  tory evidence of such authority.
    37    6.  (a)  A registered professional nurse licensed under section sixty-
    38  nine hundred five of this article who  has  satisfactorily  completed  a
    39  program  of  educational  preparation  as provided in subdivision one of
    40  this section may, for a period not to exceed  twenty-four  months  imme-
    41  diately  following  the completion of such educational program, practice
    42  nurse anesthesia under subdivision four of  section  sixty-nine  hundred
    43  two  of  this article as a graduate nurse anesthetist in the same manner
    44  as a certified registered nurse anesthetist under that subdivision.
    45    (b) A registered professional nurse licensed under section  sixty-nine
    46  hundred five of this article who is duly enrolled in a program of educa-
    47  tional  preparation,  may  practice  nurse anesthesia as a student nurse
    48  anesthetist under the supervision of an anesthesiologist or a  certified
    49  registered nurse anesthetist, who is immediately available as needed.
    50    7.  The commissioner is authorized to promulgate regulations to imple-
    51  ment the provisions of this section.
    52    § 4. This act shall take effect immediately.
