and WHEREAS, Athletic competition enhances the moral and physical development of the young people of this State, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the value of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, imparting a desire for success, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Jaxon Egloff upon the occasion of reaching 1,000 points in his career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team; and WHEREAS, Jaxon Egloff is an outstanding scholar-athlete who excels in many aspects in his community; from the court to the classroom, he brings joy and hard work to all he does; and WHEREAS, As a student at Bolton Central High School, Jaxon Egloff has made a profound impact on many of his peers, teammates, coaches, teachers, and community; this exceptional athlete has been a major contributing factor to the success of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team and continues to bring his leadership skills and his positivity to the program; and WHEREAS, During the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team's game against Loudonville Christian, Jaxon Egloff scored his 1000th career point, leading him to become the youngest Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball student-athlete to reach this milestone; and WHEREAS, Jaxon Egloff has been rightfully praised for his many achievements, and it is his strong work ethic, kind heart, and willingness to always help and encourage others that makes him an inspiration and significant part of his community; and WHEREAS, Poised eagerly with enthusiasm and determined purpose, this exemplary athlete now stands honorably, with just pride in his accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the caring counsel and support provided by his coaches, teachers, and parents; and WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to commend and pay tribute to those young people who, by achieving outstanding success in athletic competition, have inspired and brought pride to our great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Jaxon Egloff upon the occasion of reaching 1,000 points in his career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Jaxon Egloff.
