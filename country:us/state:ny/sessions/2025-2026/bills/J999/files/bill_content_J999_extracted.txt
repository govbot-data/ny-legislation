Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J00999 Summary:BILL NOJ00999  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORMARTINS COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ00999 Text: Senate Resolution No. 999 BY: Senator MARTINS HONORING Sally Hogenhauer upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Port Jewish Center on June 14, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend individuals of exceptional character and commitment whose purposeful endeavors serve to enhance the quality of life in their communities and across the great State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Sally Hogenhauer upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Port Jewish Center at its Annual Gala, "Fueling Our Future," on Saturday, June 14, 2025, at Harbor Links in Port Washington, New York; and WHEREAS, The Port Jewish Center is recognizing the dedicated leaders of its committees whose tireless efforts ensure the Center's spiritual, financial, and physical vitality; through their work, these committed congregants uphold the core tenets of Reform Judaism-pursuing justice, fostering community, caring for others, and repairing the world-thus safeguarding the Center's mission and lasting presence for generations to come; and WHEREAS, Sally Hogenhauer has been a valued member of the Port Jewish Center for 14 years, during which time she has contributed to the vibrancy and warmth of the community through her dedication and creative spirit; and WHEREAS, As the leader of the Beautification Committee, Sally Hogenhauer has transformed the Port Jewish Center's grounds into a place of color, tranquility, and welcome; her horticultural talents and thoughtful stewardship of the Center's gardens have resulted in a landscape that reflects both care and community pride; and WHEREAS, With each new season, the blooming plants and vibrant greenery nurtured by Sally Hogenhauer bring joy and serenity to congregants and visitors alike, creating an inviting and uplifting environment that enhances the spiritual life of the Port Jewish Center; and WHEREAS, In addition to her work in beautification, Sally Hogenhauer serves as a member of the Port Jewish Center's Board of Trustees, where her leadership and vision contribute to the governance and future of the congregation; and WHEREAS, Through her dedication to both the physical and communal well-being of the Port Jewish Center, Sally Hogenhauer has made a lasting impact, ensuring that all who enter are greeted by beauty, warmth, and a sense of home; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the occurrence of such a thoughtful and committed individual, whose quiet efforts speak volumes and whose service enhances the daily experience of an entire community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Sally Hogenhauer upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Port Jewish Center, and to extend its sincere appreciation for her generous service and meaningful contributions; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sally Hogenhauer.
