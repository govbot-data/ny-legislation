This act shall be known and may be cited as the "straphan- 2 ger protection act". 3 § 2. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 120.80 to read 4 as follows: 5 § 120.80 Assault on a straphanger. 6 A person is guilty of assault on a straphanger when such person 7 strikes, shoves, kicks, or otherwise subjects another person to physical 8 contact that results in such person falling onto subway tracks. 9 Assault on a straphanger is a class C felony. 10 § 3.
