AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to increasing the
transparency of tariff and delivery charges in electricity and natural
gas bills
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 44 of the public service law is amended by adding
2 two new subdivisions 8 and 9 to read as follows:
3 8. (a) For purposes of this subdivision, "tariff cost estimate" means
4 an estimate of any increase in the price or prices of electricity and
5 natural gas, or any associated goods and services, resulting directly or
6 indirectly from tariffs imposed by the federal government on the impor-
7 tation of goods, services or materials from countries outside the United
8 States or due to tariffs placed by governments of countries or their
9 political subdivisions outside the United States on the exportation of
10 goods, services or materials to the United States. Such estimate shall
11 include all increases in price attributable to the tariffs.
12 (b) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, as part of every bill-
13 ing statement, any utility corporation that serves customers in this
14 state shall provide a tariff cost estimate in a clear, conspicuous and
15 easily understandable manner.
16 9. (a) Each electric corporation and gas corporation subject to the
17 jurisdiction of the commission shall include on each customer bill for
18 delivery services an itemized disclosure of the primary cost components
19 comprising the delivery portion of the bill. Such disclosure shall, at a
20 minimum, provide average allocations per customer based on the utility
