AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to prohibiting elec-
tric corporations and gas corporations from increasing charges due to
tariffs imposed by the United States or governments of countries or
their political subdivisions outside the United States
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 66 of the public service law is amended by adding a
2 new subdivision 33 to read as follows:
3 33. Prohibit electric corporations, gas corporations, and utilities
4 from passing along costs or increasing charges to ratepayers for elec-
5 tricity and natural gas, or any associated goods and services, as a
6 result of increased costs incurred by the electric corporation, gas
7 corporation, or utility due to tariffs placed by the government of the
8 United States on the importation of goods, services or materials from
9 countries outside the United States or due to tariffs placed by govern-
10 ments of countries or their political subdivisions outside the United
11 States on the exportation of goods, services, or materials to the United
12 States.
13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
