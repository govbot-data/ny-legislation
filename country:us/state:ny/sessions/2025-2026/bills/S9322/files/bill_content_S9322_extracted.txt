Subdivision (b) of schedule I of section 3306 of the public 2 health law is amended by adding a new paragraph 93 to read as follows: 3 (93) 7-Hydroxymitragynine including its salts, isomers and salts of 4 isomers, whenever the existence of such salts, isomers and salts of 5 isomers is possible within the specific chemical designation. 6 § 2.
