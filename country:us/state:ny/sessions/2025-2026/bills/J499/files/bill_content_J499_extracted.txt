and WHEREAS, Sarah Jean McAllister of Dover, New York, died on Thursday, February 27, 2025, at the age of 18; and WHEREAS, Sarah Jean McAllister was born on November 13, 2006, to her adoring parents, Joseph Pyre McAllister and Kathy Slavin-Rothstein; and WHEREAS, In the 18 years she graced this earth, Sarah Jean McAllister touched the lives of everyone she met; she had a deep love for children and animals and found peace in art and music; and WHEREAS, Sarah Jean McAllister was preparing to graduate Hendrick Hudson High School this year and aspired to become a social worker; and WHEREAS, Predeceased by her maternal grandfather, Kevin Slavin, Sarah Jean McAllister is survived by her loving parents: her mother Kathy and stepfather Josh, along with her stepsister Haylee; her father Joseph and stepmother Jennifer, along with her stepbrother JD; she also leaves behind her cherished siblings, Joey and Julie; her maternal grandmother, Jean Slavin; and her paternal grandparents, Sarah and Thomas McAllister; Sarah was a beloved niece to her Aunt Diane and Uncle Diego, Uncle Michael and Aunt Amanda, Uncle Anthony, and Uncle Kevin; she was also a dear cousin to Dillon, Genevieve, Derek, and Sebastian; and WHEREAS, Sarah Jean McAllister's infinite selflessness and kindness will shine on through her family's vivid and happy memories; her insight and strength will forever serve as a beacon of love, light and hope to the countless lives she touched; and WHEREAS, Armed with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of compassion, Sarah Jean McAllister leaves behind a legacy which will long endure the passage of time and will remain as a comforting memory to all who were privileged to have known and loved such an amazing young woman; she will be deeply missed and truly merits the grateful tribute of this Legislative Body; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to mourn the death of Sarah Jean McAllister, and to express its deepest condolences to her family; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the family of Sarah Jean McAllister.
