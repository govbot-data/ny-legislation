Rose, whose more than fifty years of dedication helped inspire and mentor thousands of young people, leaving an indelible legacy of community uplift, opportunity, and hope that continues to guide the program following his passing in 2023; and WHEREAS, The 2025 championship season was made possible through the tireless commitment, guidance, and mentorship of the program's coaches and staff-William Kent, Sean Kent, Garry Murray, Edward Rucker, Kawaun Wiggins, Nahvasia Estepa, and Sarah Bailey-whose leadership fostered discipline, teamwork, resilience, and belief in every athlete under their care; and WHEREAS, This remarkable achievement reflects the determination, talent, and unity of the Belmont Pop Warner players, including Yuriah Bailey, Elias Bowen, Dominic Coleman, Brian Custodio, Javonni Dobbs, JaDon Ferguson, Ah'nyai Graves, Kian Henry, Messiah Hicks, Termaine Holloway Jr., Aiden Jenkins, Kobe Jenkins, Chaz Johnson, Dominic Jones, Xaiden Jones, Chase Lewis, Gauntlett Lewis, Nathan Mayer, Quemahji Murray, Sergino Newton, Damian Rucker, Jaycion Sanchez, Alaun Smith, Dumar Smith Jr., Amaree Spencer, Luis Tapia, and Josiah Womack, whose hard work and sportsmanship brought pride to their families, coaches, and community; and WHEREAS, By securing a regional championship title and earning a top-five national finish, Schenectady Belmont Pop Warner has demonstrated the power of community-based youth athletics to cultivate confidence, leadership, and excellence, while honoring a legacy built on service, mentorship, and belief in the potential of every child; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor the Schenectady Belmont Pop Warner Football Program upon winning its regional championship during the 2025 season and achieving a fifth-place finish at the national level, and to commend its players, coaches, staff, families, and supporters for an outstanding season and exemplary representation of their community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Schenectady Belmont Pop Warner Football Program as an expression of the highest esteem and appreciation of this Legislative Body.
