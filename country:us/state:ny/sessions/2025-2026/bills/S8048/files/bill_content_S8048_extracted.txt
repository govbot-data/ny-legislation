COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Cities 1 AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the city of New York, in relation to requiring the department of parks, recreation and historic preservation to cut down trees in a timely manner The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The section heading of section 18-151 of the administrative 2 code of the city of New York, as added by local law number 65 and as 3 renumbered by local law number 133 of the city of New York for the year 4 2017, is amended and a new subdivision 5 is added to read as follows: 5 Street tree maintenance [information posted online]. 6 5. The department upon receiving an initial request for tree service 7 received through the 311 citizen service center request or the depart- 8 ment's webpage shall complete such request within two weeks of receiving 9 such initial request. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
