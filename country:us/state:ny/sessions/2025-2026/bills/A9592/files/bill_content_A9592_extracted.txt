Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "student safety, parent notification, and transparency act". 3 § 2. The education law is amended by adding a new article 2-B to read 4 as follows: 5 ARTICLE 2-B 6 STUDENT SAFETY, PARENT NOTIFICATION, AND TRANSPARENCY ACT 7 Section 24. Use of physical restraint or force on students; notification, 10 documentation, review and reporting requirements. 1. The parent or guar- 11 dian of any student shall be notified either verbally or electronically 12 on the same day any physical restraint or physical force is used on such 13 student in a school setting. Such notice shall also include a written 14 incident report within twenty-four hours. 15 2. Any time any physical restraint or physical force is used on a 16 student, the school district shall document the date, time, location and 17 duration of such use of restraint or force, the reason for such use of 18 restraint or force, any de-escalation efforts used, the staff involved 19 in such use of restraint or force, and any injuries or observable 20 distress caused by such use of restraint or force. 21 3. Any use of physical restraint or physical force involving students 22 with disabilities, including incidents involving prone or floor-based 23 restraint, injury, or extended duration, conducted by personnel outside 24 the immediate school building, shall be independently reviewed. 25 4. Parents and guardians of students shall be provided access to inci- 26 dent records related to the use of physical restraint or physical force 27 on such students, including written reports and any photographs or video EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
