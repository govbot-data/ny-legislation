and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud honor Chris Vaccaro upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition by the New York Conference of Italian American Legislators on Monday, May 19, 2025; and WHEREAS, Chris Vaccaro, a proud Italian American and distinguished leader in media, education, and community advocacy, has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to preserving and promoting his heritage and the shared values of the Italian American experience; and WHEREAS, Chris Vaccaro currently serves as U.S. Press Officer for the Federation of Italian Baseball and Softball, Executive Trustee of the Italian American Baseball Foundation, President and Founder of the Italian American Heritage Society of Long Island, and Ambassador of Long Island for We the Italians, contributing to the growth and visibility of Italian heritage at both the national and international levels; and WHEREAS, A national voice within the Italian American community, Chris Vaccaro contributes regularly as a columnist for Ambassador Magazine published by the National Italian American Foundation, co-hosts the Renaissance Minds Podcast on WRHU 88.7-FM, and serves on the committee of the National Italian American Sports Hall of Fame, lending his voice and expertise to the ongoing celebration of Italian American achievement; and WHEREAS, In the field of journalism and media, Chris Vaccaro stands among the most decorated professionals of his generation; he is a two-time Emmy Award winner and ten-time recipient of the Edward R. Murrow Award; he has been inducted into the Long Island Journalism Hall of Fame, the New York State Baseball Hall of Fame, and the Sachem Athletic Hall of Fame in recognition of his enduring contributions; and WHEREAS, Chris Vaccaro's professional career is distinguished by executive leadership roles, including his current position as Vice President of Digital Technology and Communications at Ark Technology, as well as influential appointments at Topps, News 12, Cheddar, and Playfly Sports, where he has been at the forefront of transforming legacy media and sports organizations through innovative digital and AI-driven strategies; and WHEREAS, A devoted educator, Chris Vaccaro has significantly contributed to the next generation of journalists as a professor and former Director of Graduate Journalism at Hofstra University; his excellence in education and media has earned him appointment as a Fulbright Specialist by the U.S. Department of State, recognizing his international impact in these fields; and WHEREAS, Chris Vaccaro currently serves as President of the Suffolk County Sports Hall of Fame, is a Past President and Trustee of the Press Club of Long Island, and holds the office of Vice President and President-Elect of the National Society of Professional Journalists, affirming his leadership and influence across the journalism profession; and WHEREAS, As an accomplished author, his most recent book, America's Front Line, debuted at No. 1 on Amazon's Law Enforcement list, further cementing his voice as a trusted authority in public affairs; and WHEREAS, Chris Vaccaro's journalism has been published in The New York Times, Rolling Stone, Associated Press, The Athletic, among many others, and he has appeared as an expert commentator on platforms including ESPN, CBS, FOX, and Sirius XM; and WHEREAS, Among the many prestigious honors recognizing his outstanding achievements are the Beacon Award from the Ellis Island Honors Society, the Leadership Award from the Italian Language Foundation, the AATI Dante Award, Hofstra University's Young Alumnus of the Year, as well as repeated inclusion on Long Island's "Power List" and 40-under-40 distinctions from both Stony Brook University and Long Island Business News; and WHEREAS, Chris Vaccaro exemplifies the very best of Italian American excellence-upholding tradition, fostering mentorship, and advancing cultural pride through leadership, innovation, and public service; and WHEREAS, In recognition of his outstanding contributions to heritage, media, and civic life, it is most fitting that Chris Vaccaro be honored not only as a recipient of the Italian American Heritage Award but also as a Grand Marshal for his enduring commitment to the values and legacy of the Italian American community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Chris Vaccaro upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition by the New York Conference of Italian American Legislators on May 19, 2025, and to extend its highest commendation for his tireless efforts to champion Italian American culture, civic engagement, and professional excellence; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Chris Vaccaro.
