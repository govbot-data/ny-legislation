Molitor CONGRATULATING the Frewsburg Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHSAA Class C State Championship WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly sought after in high school sports; this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Frewsburg Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHSAA Class C State Championship at Hudson Valley Community College in Troy, New York, on Friday, March 21, 2025; to the praise and applause of their excited fans, the Frewsburg Girls Basketball Team defeated Stillwater in a 59-44 victory to capture the Championship; and WHEREAS, Family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Frewsburg Girls Basketball Team throughout their journey as they ended their season with an impressive overall record; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as basketball, which demands athletic prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Sierra Beaver and her skilled coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the court; and WHEREAS, Coaches Sierra Beaver, Sandy Ruhlman, Jamie Sposato, and Mackenzie Collver, and all of the outstanding athletes on the Frewsburg High School Girls Basketball Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Frewsburg High School Girls Basketball Team: Jadyn Trocki, Teghan Trocki, Jaden Jimerson, Presleigh Stelmack, Taytum Jimerson, Kaylee Cappa, Jade LaCross, Rozlyn Haglund, Ava Reale, Abery Harvey, Ava Jimerson, and Elysse Gruber; Head Coach Sierra Beaver; Assistant Coaches Sandy Ruhlman, Jamie Sposato, and Mackenzie Collver; Managers Griffin Beaver, Greta Gilevski, Brynne Butzer, Oaklynn Moeller, Aubrie Swanson, and Myah LaCross; and Administrators Sarah Olson, Daniell Cook, and Terry Gray on their outstanding season and winning the NYSPHSAA Class C State Championship; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Frewsburgh High School Girls Basketball Team, Head Coach Sierra Beaver, and Assistant Coaches Sandy Ruhlman, Jamie Sposato, and Mackenzie Collver.
