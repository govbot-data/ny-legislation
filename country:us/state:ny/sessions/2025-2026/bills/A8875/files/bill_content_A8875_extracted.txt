BORES -- read once and referred to the Committee on Economic Development AN ACT to amend the alcoholic beverage control law, in relation to creating a special on-premises license for certain properties The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The alcoholic beverage control law is amended by adding a 2 new section 97-e to read as follows: 3 § 97-e. Special on-premises license for certain properties. 1. 4 Notwithstanding any provisions of law to the contrary, any person may 5 apply to the liquor authority for a license to sell liquor, wine, beer, 6 and cider at retail for on-premises consumption at a facility which: 7 (a) is located within a designated industrial or economic zoning 8 district; 9 (b) occupies not less than twenty thousand square feet of floor area; 10 (c) is utilized for the purpose of hosting entertainment or cultural, 11 social, or community events; and 12 (d) is located at least five hundred feet from a residential building. 13 2. Any license issued pursuant to this section shall be subject to all 14 provisions of this chapter not inconsistent with subdivision one of this 15 section and may be subjected to such rules by the liquor authority as it 16 deems necessary and such rules as are in conformity with the provisions 17 of subdivision one of this section. 18 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
