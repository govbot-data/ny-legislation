and WHEREAS, Kelsey Broadley has been selected as an Emerging Entrepreneur by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, an honor to be bestowed at the Chamber's 53rd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 19, 2026, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, In late 2024, Kelsey Broadley proudly opened Emma's Place, a boutique home and gift shop created to serve women through all phases of life, offering a warm and thoughtfully curated shopping experience rooted in style, connection, and personal expression; and WHEREAS, Emma's Place is named in honor of Kelsey Broadley's daughter, Emma, and was intentionally designed as a Mother/Daughter shopping destination, reflecting family values and the joy of shared experiences across generations; and WHEREAS, Guided by the belief that every home should reflect the personality and warmth of those who inhabit it, Emma's Place bridges the gap between ordinary living spaces and enchantingly personalized environments, offering handpicked decor and gifts that transform houses into homes; and WHEREAS, Drawing upon years of experience collaborating with diverse businesses, Kelsey Broadley brings a wealth of knowledge, a keen eye for style, and a genuine passion for hosting and home aesthetics to her boutique, turning her creative vision into a thriving and welcoming enterprise; and WHEREAS, Emma's Place offers a carefully curated selection of unique, fun, and expressive decor, ranging from contemporary art pieces to classic furnishings, each chosen to inspire, delight, and celebrate individuality; and WHEREAS, Since opening its doors, Emma's Place has become more than a retail store-it is a reflection of community, creativity, and connection, providing customers with a space that values warmth, inspiration, and personal style; and WHEREAS, The Clarence Chamber of Commerce's recognition of Kelsey Broadley as an Emerging Entrepreneur honors her innovation, dedication, and positive contribution to the local business community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Kelsey Broadley upon the occasion of her designation as an Emerging Entrepreneur by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 19, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Kelsey Broadley, Emma's Place.
