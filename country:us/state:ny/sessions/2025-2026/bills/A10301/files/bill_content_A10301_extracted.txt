The Nassau county law 15 enforcement exploring program shall be defined as a youth police train- 16 ing program which provides youths between the ages of fourteen and twen- 17 ty with training and on the purpose, mission and objectives of the 18 Nassau county police department, public safety and law enforcement. 19 (a) On all eligible lists resulting from competitive examinations, the 20 names of eligible persons shall be entered in the order of respective 21 final earned ratings on examinations, with the name of the eligible 22 person with the highest final earned ratings at the head of such list, 23 provided, however, that for the purpose of determining final earned 24 ratings, Nassau county law enforcement explorers shall be entitled to 25 receive additional points in a competitive examination for original 26 appointment in Nassau county. An eligible person who served as a Nassau 27 county law enforcement explorer for a minimum of four years shall 28 receive an additional five points.
