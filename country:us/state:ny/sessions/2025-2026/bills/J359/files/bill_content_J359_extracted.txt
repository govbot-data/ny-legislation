Morton, Ed.D., Prince Hall Lodge No. 38 has played a pivotal role in advancing education, economic empowerment, and charitable initiatives that benefit individuals and families throughout the region; and WHEREAS, The Annual Dinner Dance serves as an opportunity to reflect on the Lodge's ongoing commitment to community engagement, honor the achievements of its members, and celebrate the enduring bonds of Masonic brotherhood and fellowship; and WHEREAS, Through scholarships, mentorship, and charitable outreach, Prince Hall Lodge No. 38 continues to uplift and support those in need, demonstrating an unwavering dedication to the betterment of society; and WHEREAS, Under the leadership of Most Worshipful Grand Master Darren M. Morton, Ed.D., Prince Hall Freemasonry in New York remains a beacon of service and integrity, carrying forth the vision of Prince Hall and strengthening the legacy of excellence; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to acknowledge the significance of this event and to recognize the profound contributions of Prince Hall Lodge No. 38 in fostering leadership, service, and excellence within its community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Prince Hall Lodge No. 38 upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Dinner Dance on June 28, 2025, and to extend sincere appreciation to its members and leadership for their enduring commitment to the ideals of Freemasonry and public service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Prince Hall Lodge No. 38 and Most Worshipful Grand Master Darren M.
