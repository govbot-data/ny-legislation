the Eagle Scout Court of Honor ceremony will be held at St. Edward the Confessor Church in Syosset, New York, on Saturday, March 28, 2026; and WHEREAS, Bryanna Hoang, of Old Bethpage, is a member of Scouting America Troop 205 in Syosset, New York; and WHEREAS, America has achieved her greatest heights of glory through the dedication, determination, and tireless efforts of the outstanding citizens of this Nation; and WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain her glory; and WHEREAS, Scouting America and its members are dedicated to the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in Scouting is the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout; and WHEREAS, Bryanna Hoang has been involved in Scouting for many years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals, and has attained all the necessary achievements, including completion of a community service project, which was successfully planned, developed, and executed; she officially became an Eagle Scout on January 6, 2026; and WHEREAS, Bryanna Hoang contributed her time and hard work to complete her project for West Hills County Park; and WHEREAS, For her Eagle Scout project, Bryanna Hoang designed and constructed an obstacle course to benefit the local community; the project involved building five new obstacles, including a rock wall, teeter-totter, balance beam, stepping logs, and quintuple steps, as well as restoring existing features to improve safety and accessibility; and WHEREAS, Additionally, Bryanna Hoang cleared hazardous and overgrown areas, transforming the course into a safe, engaging, and welcoming space for families, Scouts, and visitors of all ages and affording them the opportunity to enjoy outdoor recreation, develop skills, and strengthen their connection to nature; she undertook efforts to restore and revitalize the area with a focus on public safety, accessibility, and long-term usability while preserving the park's natural character; and WHEREAS, The successful execution of this project required extensive planning, leadership, and coordination of volunteers; and WHEREAS, Bryanna Hoang's perseverance in rising through the ranks to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of her ability to master difficult tasks; her accomplishment will enable her to emerge as a leader, fully equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and WHEREAS, Bryanna Hoang's dedication to the enhancement of West Hills County Park is represented through her devotion to community and commitment to others; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to her family, her school, her community, her State, and her Nation; undoubtedly, her future will be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Bryanna Hoang on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Bryanna Hoang.
