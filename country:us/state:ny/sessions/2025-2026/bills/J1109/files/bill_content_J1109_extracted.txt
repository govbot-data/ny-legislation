and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of 2025 Valedictorian Benedicta Agoke of The Performing Arts & Technology High School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and WHEREAS, Graduating on June 24, 2025, Valedictorian Benedicta Agoke represents the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious resource, our youth; their achievements have brought enduring honor to their family and community and should be recognized and saluted; and WHEREAS, As Valedictorian Benedicta Agoke takes leave of The Performing Arts & Technology High School, they bestow a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement and community service to which others may aspire; and WHEREAS, Valedictorian Benedicta Agoke may now stand with pride as they assess their achievements, experiences the satisfaction of their labor, and feels the joy of their accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Class of 2025 Valedictorian Benedicta Agoke of The Performing Arts & Technology High School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Valedictorian Benedicta Agoke.
