        AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to the establishment
          of reduced residential rates for electric and natural gas  service  to
          furloughed or non-pay federal workers; and providing for the repeal of
          such provisions upon expiration thereof
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The public service law is amended by adding a  new  section
     2  66-x to read as follows:
     3    § 66-x. Reduced residential rates for electric and natural gas service
     4  to  furloughed  or  non-pay  federal  customers. 1. The commission shall
     5  require electric and natural gas corporations to establish  programs  of
     6  rate  reduction and other assistance to residential electric and natural
     7  gas customers who have been  either  furloughed  or  designated  non-pay
     8  federal  employees due to a period of a lapse in discretionary appropri-
     9  ations by the federal government, the revenue impact of which shall  not
    10  be  borne  solely  by any single class of customer. The commission shall
    11  ensure appropriate levels  of  rate  reduction  for  such  electric  and
    12  natural  gas customers to mitigate hardship.  In establishing the appro-
    13  priate percentage reduction the commission shall ensure  to  the  extent
    14  feasible  that ratepayers who have been furloughed or designated non-pay
    15  federal employees are not jeopardized or overburdened by rates for elec-
    16  tric and natural gas service.
    17    2. The commission shall  establish  customer  income  and  categorical
    18  eligibility  standards  for  reduced rates, which shall include, without
    19  limitation, customers providing documentation  demonstrating  that  they
    20  have  been furloughed or designated non-pay as a result of a period of a
    21  lapse in discretionary appropriations by the federal government.
    22    3. The commission shall require providers of electric and natural  gas
    23  service  to  provide information and application forms for reduced rates
