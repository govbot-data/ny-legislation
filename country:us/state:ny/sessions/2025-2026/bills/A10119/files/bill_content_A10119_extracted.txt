LBD11622-03-6 A. 10119--A 2 1 § 353-g. Animal abuser registry. 1. For purposes of this section, the 2 term "animal abuser" means a person sixteen years of age or older who 3 has been convicted of a felony or misdemeanor violation of any provision 4 of this article or of the comparable statutes of another state. 5 2. The New York state division of criminal justice services shall 6 maintain a registry of animal abusers required to register pursuant to 7 the provisions of this section. The central registry of animal abusers 8 shall be made available to the public through internet access, telephone 9 access, written access and in-person access. All of the information 10 contained in an animal abuser's registration shall be made available. 11 Records of each animal abuser's registration shall be maintained for the 12 duration of the period in which the animal abuser is required to be 13 registered. 14 3. (a) A person convicted of a felony or misdemeanor violation of any 15 provision of this article shall register with the department of criminal 16 justice services within ten days of their release from incarceration or 17 on the date of conviction, whichever is later. 18 (b) Any animal abuser who enters the state and physically remains 19 within the boundaries of this state for more than ten consecutive days 20 shall register with the department of criminal justice services before 21 the end of the eleventh day in the state. 22 4. Any previously registered animal abuser shall reregister no later 23 than ten days after moving to a new location within the state. 24 5. When an animal abuser registers, the animal abuser shall provide 25 the following registration information: 26 (a) the legal name and any other names or aliases that the animal 27 abuser is using or has used; 28 (b) the date of birth of the animal abuser; 29 (c) the current address or location of the animal abuser; 30 (d) the animal protection offense for which the animal abuser was 31 convicted, including the date and place of such conviction; and 32 (e) a current photograph of the animal abuser. 33 6. Following an animal abuser's initial registration pursuant to the 34 provisions of this section, an animal abuser shall annually renew the 35 animal abuser's registration with the department of criminal justice 36 services prior to December thirty-first of each subsequent calendar year 37 for a period of seven years from the animal abuser's date of conviction 38 or release from incarceration for the underlying violation of this arti- 39 cle, whichever is later. An animal abuser who is convicted of a subse- 40 quent felony or misdemeanor violation of any provision of this article 41 or of the comparable statutes of another state shall be required to 42 annually renew such animal abuser's registration with the department of 43 criminal justice services prior to December thirty-first of each subse- 44 quent calendar year for the rest of their life. 45 7. An animal abuser who intentionally or knowingly fails to comply 46 with the registration requirements, or provides false information when 47 complying with the registration requirements set forth in this section, 48 is guilty of a class E felony. 49 8. Each county sheriff shall provide links to register with or view 50 the department of criminal justice services' registry of animal abusers 51 on their public website. 52 9.
