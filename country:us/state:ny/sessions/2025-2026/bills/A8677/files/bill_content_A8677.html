        AN ACT in relation to authorizing the city of Yonkers  to  alienate  and
          discontinue the use of certain parklands
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the city of  Yonkers
     2  is hereby authorized, acting by and through its governing body, and upon
     3  such  terms  and  conditions  as  determined  by such governing body, to
     4  discontinue the use as parkland and  alienate  the  lands  described  in
     5  section  three  of  this  act, and to sell and convey such lands at fair
     6  market value to St. John's Riverside Hospital for the purpose of restor-
     7  ing the Color Gardens once present on the Untermyer estate for residents
     8  and visitors of the city of Yonkers to enjoy.

    10  be effective on the condition that the  city  of  Yonkers  dedicate  the

    13  park purposes since the parkland alienation authorized by chapter 286 of
