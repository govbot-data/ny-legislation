DURSO -- read once and referred to the Committee on Local Governments AN ACT to amend chapter 459 of the laws of 1962 incorporating the West Babylon Volunteer Firemen's Benevolent Association, and providing for its powers and duties, in relation to the use of certain moneys received by such benevolent association and modernization of certain language The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Sections 1, 2, 6 and 7 of chapter 459 of the laws of 1962 2 incorporating the West Babylon Volunteer Firemen's Benevolent Associ- 3 ation, and providing for its powers and duties, section 6 as amended by 4 chapter 203 of the laws of 1964, are amended to read as follows: 5 Section 1. Incorporation; membership. A membership corporation by the 6 name of "West Babylon Volunteer [Firemen's] Firefighter's Benevolent 7 Association" is hereby created. It shall be composed of such persons 8 eligible to membership therein as hereinafter provided as shall (a) 9 notify the secretary of the West Babylon volunteer fire department in 10 writing prior to the organization meeting of such corporation hereinaft- 11 er provided for of their desire to become members, or (b) shall attend 12 such meeting or an adjournment thereof, and also such persons so eligi- 13 ble as may become members of such corporation pursuant to its by-laws. 14 § 2. Persons eligible to membership. All persons who are now, or who 15 have been, or who hereafter shall be active volunteer members of the 16 West Babylon volunteer fire department, or of any fire corporation in 17 the West Babylon fire district, and who have heretofore acquired or 18 shall hereafter acquire the status of exempt volunteer [fireman] fire- 19 fighters as defined in section two hundred of the general municipal law 20 or any other applicable law by virture of having rendered service in the 21 West Babylon volunteer fire department, or of any fire corporation 22 created by this act, except that any [fireman] firefighter who has been 23 removed for cause, expelled or dropped from the rolls, of any fire 24 company or department, and who has not been reinstated therein, shall 25 not be eligible for membership in such corporation.
