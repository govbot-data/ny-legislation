McMahon, Conrad, McDonald) -- read once and referred to the Committee on Educa- tion AN ACT in relation to authorizing the commissioner of education to conduct a study regarding courses of study on the mass starvation in Ireland from 1845 until 1850 within the state The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The commissioner of education is authorized and directed to 2 conduct a survey regarding instruction on the mass starvation in Ireland 3 from 1845 until 1850 within the state. Such survey shall focus on iden- 4 tifying which school districts subject to section eight hundred one of 5 the education law provide instruction on such mass starvation in 6 Ireland. Such survey shall include as appropriate, questions on how the 7 district is meeting the learning standards for instruction requirements 8 on such mass starvation in Ireland from 1845 until 1850 pursuant to 9 section eight hundred one of the education law. Such survey shall also 10 require an attestation from each school district superintendent that 11 indicates instruction related to the mass starvation in Ireland is 12 provided at all appropriate grade levels in compliance with section 13 eight hundred one of the education law. 14 § 2. Findings shall include detailed results of the survey, the extent 15 the survey data submitted was compliant with section eight hundred one 16 of the education law, and if needed, recommendations to strengthen 17 provisions of law if not compliant. A report of the findings of such 18 survey shall be delivered to the governor, the temporary president of 19 the senate, the speaker of the assembly, the minority leader of the 20 senate and the minority leader of the assembly the first of January 21 following the effective date of this act or one hundred twenty days 22 after the effective date of this act, whichever is later. 23 § 3.
