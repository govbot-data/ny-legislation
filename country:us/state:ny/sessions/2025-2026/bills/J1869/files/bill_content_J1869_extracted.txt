Dyson II for his recognition at the Lewis Hayden Chapter No. 47 "Splash of Green" Community Service Awards Banquet WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend those distinguished individuals whose lives exemplify the highest ideals of leadership, service, and fraternal commitment; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Past Grand Worthy Patron William A. Dyson II for his recognition at the Lewis Hayden Chapter No. 47 "Splash of Green" Community Service Awards Banquet to be held on Sunday, April 19, 2026; and WHEREAS, A devoted member of the Prince Hall Masonic family, Past Grand Worthy Patron William A. Dyson II has rendered exemplary service to the Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, demonstrating steadfast dedication to its guiding principles of Charity, Truth, and Loving Kindness; and WHEREAS, Having attained the esteemed rank of Past Grand Worthy Patron, William A. Dyson II has distinguished himself through visionary leadership, faithful stewardship, and a deep commitment to the growth and stability of the Order, inspiring all who labor alongside him; and WHEREAS, In his service, William A. Dyson II has worked to strengthen the bonds of the Eastern Star, uplift its members, and ensure that its mission of benevolence and community engagement continues to flourish for generations to come; and WHEREAS, William A. Dyson's contributions reflect not only a devotion to fraternal excellence but also a broader commitment to the uplift of humanity, embodying the timeless virtues upon which Prince Hall Affiliated bodies are founded; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that such a distinguished individual be recognized and celebrated by this Legislative Body on this special occasion; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Past Grand Worthy Patron William A. Dyson II for his outstanding service, leadership, and enduring contributions to the Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star and the community at large; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Past Grand Worthy Patron William A.
