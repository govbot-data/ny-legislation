COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Cities 1 AN ACT to amend the general municipal law, in relation to requiring further source separation of waste in cities with a population of one million or more The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 120-aa of the general municipal law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows: 3 3. Pursuant to the authority of this section, no later than the first 4 of September two thousand twenty-nine, each city with a population of 5 one million or more residents shall adopt such a local law or ordinance 6 to require that solid waste which has been left for collection shall be 7 separated not only as otherwise required by this section, but that such 8 waste which is eligible to be redeemed under title ten of article twen- 9 ty-seven of the environmental conservation law is separated from other 10 recyclable, reusable, or otherwise marketable components. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
