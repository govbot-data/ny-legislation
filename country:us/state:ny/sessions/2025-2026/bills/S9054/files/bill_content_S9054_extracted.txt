COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation -- commit- tee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recom- mitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring electric scooter service platforms to maintain insurance coverage The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 article 34-E to read as follows: 3 ARTICLE 34-E 4 ELECTRIC SCOOTER SERVICE PLATFORM INSURANCE 5 Section 1290. Definitions. 6 1291. Liability insurance. 7 § 1290. Definitions. For the purposes of this article, the following 8 terms shall have the following meanings: 9 1. "Digital network" means any electronic system or service offered or 10 utilized by an electric scooter service platform that through the use of 11 an app accessed through a phone, tablet, computer, or a digital token, 12 digital key, or substantially similar technology, enables the unlocking, 13 access, operation, docking, charging, and payment for use of a scooter. 14 2. "Electric scooter service platform" means a person, corporation, 15 partnership, sole proprietorship, or other entity operating in New York 16 state that owns, leases, or controls more than two electric scooters and 17 provides them for use by the public, where access to and operation of 18 such scooters is through using a digital network. 19 3. "Trip" means the lawful operation of an electric scooter owned or 20 controlled by an electric scooter service platform, subsequent to 21 accessing such electric scooter through a digital network. 22 § 1291. Liability insurance. 1. No electric scooter, as defined in 23 section one hundred fourteen-e of this chapter shall be offered for use 24 through an electric scooter service platform unless it is covered by a 25 policy of insurance, in such language and form as shall be determined 26 and established by the superintendent of financial services. Such policy 18 shall not provide personal injury protection pursuant to article fifty- 19 one of the insurance law. 20 4. Proof of insurance as required by this section shall be produced 21 and displayed by an electric scooter service platform upon the request 22 of any person having authority to enforce the provisions of this chap- 23 ter. The failure to produce such proof upon the request of any such 24 person shall not be an offense but shall be presumptive evidence that 25 such electric scooter is being operated without having such insurance in 26 force and effect. 27 5. Proof of insurance as required by this section shall be produced 28 and displayed by the owner or operator of such electric scooter to any 29 person who has suffered or claims to have suffered either injury or 30 property damage as a result of the operation of such electric scooter, 31 if such insurance coverage was required under the circumstances of such 32 operation. It shall be an affirmative defense to any prosecution for a 33 violation of this subdivision that such proof was so produced or 34 displayed within twenty-four hours of receiving notice of such injury or 35 damage, or the claim of such injury or damage. 36 § 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after 37 it shall have become a law.
