Subdivision 2 of section 365-a of the social services law 2 is amended by adding a new paragraph (nn) to read as follows: 3 (nn) comprehensive neuropsychological examinations for dyslexia, upon 4 the referral of a physician, when performed by a health care profes- 5 sional licensed, certified, or authorized pursuant to title eight of the 6 education law and acting within their scope of practice and in accord- 7 ance with this paragraph.
