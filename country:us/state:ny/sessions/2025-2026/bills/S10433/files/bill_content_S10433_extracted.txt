Subdivision 1 of section 4 of the public service law, as 2 amended by chapter 594 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 1. (a) There shall be in the department of public service a public 5 service commission, which shall possess the powers and duties hereinaft- 6 er specified, and also all powers necessary or proper to enable it to 7 carry out the purposes of this chapter. The commission shall consist of 8 [five] seven members, to be appointed by the governor, by and with the 9 advice and consent of the senate. A commissioner shall be designated as 10 [chairman] chairperson of the commission by the governor to serve in 11 such capacity at the pleasure of the governor or until [his] such chair- 12 person's term as commissioner expires, whichever first occurs. 13 (b) At least one commissioner shall have experience in utility consum- 14 er advocacy. 15 (c) When filling a vacancy or vacancies after the effective date of 16 this paragraph, at least one commissioner shall have not less than ten 17 years of demonstrated, operational experience in the utility industry. 18 For the purposes of this subdivision, "operational experience in the 19 utility industry" shall mean direct, hands-on employment or professional 20 service in electric, gas, or steam utility operations, including, but 21 not limited to, electric or gas generation, transmission, distribution, 22 system operations, maintenance, engineering, or field services. Such 23 experience shall be operational or technical in nature and shall not 24 consist of executive management, senior supervisory, financial advisory, 25 or legal advisory roles performed on behalf of a utility corporation or 26 holding company.
