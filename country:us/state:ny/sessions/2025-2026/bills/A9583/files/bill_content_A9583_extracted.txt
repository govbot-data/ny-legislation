SMULLEN -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law and the state finance law, in relation to providing a research grant to fund clinical trials of ibogaine; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expira- tion thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "veterans' ibogaine PTSD research grant program". 3 § 2. The public health law is amended by adding a new article 33-C to 4 read as follows: 5 ARTICLE 33-C 6 VETERANS' IBOGAINE PTSD RESEARCH GRANT PROGRAM 7 Section 3399. Ibogaine research grant program. 8 § 3399. Ibogaine research grant program. 1. The commissioner, subject 9 to appropriation, shall establish and administer a competitive grant 10 program to fund United States Food and Drug Administration-compliant 11 clinical trials of ibogaine for the treatment of veterans for post-trau- 12 matic stress disorder (PTSD). 13 2. Grants shall only be awarded to entities capable of conducting 14 Phase II or Phase III trials and seeking FDA approval of ibogaine for 15 the treatment of post-traumatic stress disorder in the veteran popu- 16 lation. 17 3. No state funds shall be disbursed unless the grantee agrees to 18 provide dollar-for-dollar matching funds from non-state sources. 19 4. Preference shall be given to applicants that: (a) commit to estab- 20 lishing or maintaining a physical presence in New York for research, 21 manufacturing, or distribution; and (b) include veterans in trial 22 design, recruitment, and aftercare plans. 23 5. The commissioner shall convene an advisory committee, in consulta- 24 tion with the office of mental health and the office of addiction 25 services and supports, to review applications and make recommendations 26 for awards. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 99-uu to 18 read as follows: 19 § 99-uu. New York state veterans' ibogaine PTSD research grant program 20 fund. 1. There is hereby established in the joint custody of the state 21 comptroller and commissioner of taxation and finance a special fund to 22 be known as the "New York state veterans' ibogaine PTSD research grant 23 program fund". 24 2. Such fund shall consist of all monies appropriated to such fund and 25 any grant, gift or bequest made to the fund. 26 3. Monies of the fund shall be available for the purposes of the 27 veterans' ibogaine PTSD research grant program, pursuant to section 28 thirty-three hundred ninety-nine of the public health law. 29 4. The monies of the fund shall be paid out on the audit and warrant 30 of the comptroller on vouchers certified or approved by the commissioner 31 of health, or by an officer or employee of the department of health 32 designated by such commissioner. 33 § 4. This act shall take effect immediately and shall expire and be 34 deemed repealed ten years after such effective date; provided, however, 35 such act shall expire and be deemed repealed five years after the food 36 and drug administration approves ibogaine or an ibogaine derivative for 37 any indication, whichever occurs sooner; and provided, further, that the 38 commissioner of health shall notify the legislative bill drafting 39 commission upon the food and drug administration approving ibogaine or 40 an ibogaine derivative for any indication as provided for in this 41 section in order that the commission may maintain an accurate and timely 42 effective data base of the official text of the laws of the state of New 43 York in furtherance of effectuating the provisions of section 44 of the 44 legislative law and section 70-b of the public officers law.
