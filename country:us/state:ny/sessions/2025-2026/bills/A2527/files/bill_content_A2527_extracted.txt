The sum of one billion dollars ($1,000,000,000), or so much 2 thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated to the office for 3 people with developmental disabilities out of any moneys appropriated to 4 the metropolitan transportation authority in the 2025-2026 state fiscal 5 plan, and made immediately available for programs and services for 6 persons with developmental disabilities.
