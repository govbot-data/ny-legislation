AN ACT to amend the town law and the public officers law, in relation to
authorizing the town justice of the town of West Almond, county of
Allegany, to be a nonresident of such town
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 23 of the town law is amended by adding a new
2 subdivision 37 to read as follows:
3 37. Notwithstanding the provisions of subdivision one of this section,
4 in the town of West Almond, in the county of Allegany, the person
5 performing the function of town justice, need not be an elector of such
6 town; provided that such person resides in the county of Allegany.
7 Provided, however, the person performing the function of town justice in
8 any other town shall be an elector of such town, unless otherwise
9 provided by an act of the state legislature.
10 § 2. Section 3 of the public officers law is amended by adding a new
11 subdivision 76 to read as follows:
12 76. In the town of West Almond, in the county of Allegany, the
13 provisions of this section requiring a person to be a resident of the
14 political subdivision or municipal corporation of the state for which
15 such person shall be chosen or within which such person's official func-
16 tions are required to be exercised, shall not prevent a person from
17 holding the office of town justice of the town of West Almond; provided
18 that such person resides in the county of Allegany. Provided, however,
19 the person performing the function of town justice in any other town
20 shall be a resident of such town, unless otherwise provided by law.
21 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
