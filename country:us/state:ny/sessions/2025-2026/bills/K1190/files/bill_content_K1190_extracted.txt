Station for public access; it offers specialized programs for local schools and the public, often collaborating with the Vestal Historical Society to provide guided tours and historical lectures; and WHEREAS, The success of the Vestal Museum is in direct correlation to the efforts of its members, whose involvement is, and always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard of excellence in all endeavors; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that when individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 50th Anniversary of the Vestal Museum and to honor its members, past and present, who have labored so conscientiously to secure and improve the quality of life for the citizens of the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Vestal Museum.
