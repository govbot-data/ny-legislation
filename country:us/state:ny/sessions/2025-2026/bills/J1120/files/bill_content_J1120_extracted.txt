and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 160th Anniversary of the Spackenkill Union Free School District; and WHEREAS, Since its founding in 1865, the Spackenkill Union Free School District has been an institution dedicated to academic excellence, student success, and community engagement; and WHEREAS, Having begun as a mere one-room schoolhouse, the Spackenkill Union Free School District has since grown to include four schools and serves around 1,500 students; and WHEREAS, For 160 years, the Spackenkill Union Free School District has provided a high-quality education, fostering a tradition of academic distinction, innovation, and leadership among its students, faculty, and staff; and WHEREAS, The Spackenkill Union Free School District has consistently upheld its mission to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and character necessary to become responsible and contributing members of society, ensuring all graduates embody a commitment to lifelong learning and civic responsibility; and WHEREAS, The educators, administrators, and staff of the Spackenkill Union Free School have played a vital role in shaping generations of students, instilling in them the values of hard work, perseverance, and intellectual curiosity; and WHEREAS, Throughout its history, the Spackenkill Union Free School District has received numerous accolades for academic achievement, extracurricular excellence, and community service, reflecting its long-term dedication to fostering well-rounded students prepared for success in higher education, careers, and beyond; and WHEREAS, The Spackenkill Union Free School District's legacy of educational excellence is a testament to the commitment and support of its students, parents, teachers, administrators, alumni, and the entire Spackenkill community; and WHEREAS, In celebration of this historic milestone, the Spackenkill Union Free School District continues to inspire and empower future generations, ensuring a bright and promising future for all who pass through its halls; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 160th Anniversary of the Spackenkill Union Free School District, recognizing its outstanding contributions to education and the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Spackenkill Union Free School District.
