The railroad law is amended by adding a new section 72-a to 2 read as follows: 3 § 72-a. Freight train inspections. 1. Every railroad corporation which 4 operates any freight train within the state shall conduct a comprehen- 5 sive safety inspection when a freight train is parked in a train yard 6 prior to traveling on tracks within the state.
