Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 "Kane's Law". 3 § 2. The second undesignated paragraph of section 125.12 of the penal 4 law, as amended by chapter 732 of the laws of 2006, is amended to read 5 as follows: 6 Vehicular manslaughter in the second degree is a class [D] C felony. 7 § 3.
