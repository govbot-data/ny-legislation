AN ACT to amend the insurance law, in relation to homeowner natural
disaster preparedness, home safety and loss prevention courses
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subparagraphs 3 and 4 of paragraph (a) and paragraph (d) of
2 subsection 5 of section 2346 of the insurance law, as added by section 2
3 of part BB of chapter 54 of the laws of 2016, are amended and a new
4 paragraph (e) is added to read as follows:
5 (3) "Homeowner natural disaster preparedness, home safety and loss
6 prevention course" means a natural disaster preparedness, home safety
7 and loss prevention course that has been submitted to the superintendent
8 for [their information and review] approval by an applicant, and which
9 has been approved by the superintendent, pursuant to paragraph (d) of
10 this subsection. Such course [may] shall provide useful information to
11 participants on items including, but not limited to: courses of action
12 that can be taken before, during and after the occurrence of a natural
13 disaster, strategies to reduce risk exposure to insured residential
14 property owners and renters, and information about the installation of
15 equipment, devices or other capital improvements to real property which
16 can help to eliminate or mitigate damage to real or personal property,
17 personal injury or the loss of life caused by a natural disaster or
18 other insurable event or occurrence of a fire, theft, burglary, personal
19 injury or property damage.
20 (4) "Applicant" means an insurer, or any other person, agency or
21 organization which submits a proposed homeowner natural disaster
22 preparedness, home safety and loss prevention course to the superinten-
