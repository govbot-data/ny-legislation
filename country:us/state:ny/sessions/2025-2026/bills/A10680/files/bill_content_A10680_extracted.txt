BORES -- read once and referred to the Committee on Energy AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to voluntary rates for residential customers utilizing heat pumps as the primary heating source The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public service law is amended by adding a new section 2 66-x to read as follows: 3 § 66-x. Voluntary rates for heat pumps. 1. For the purposes of this 4 section, unless the context otherwise requires, "heat pump" means an 5 electrically powered device that uses the refrigeration cycle to trans- 6 fer thermal energy from one location to another. 7 2. On or before August first, two thousand twenty-seven, an investor- 8 owned gas or electrical corporation that provides electric or thermal 9 energy shall, within a general rate case request, submit to the commis- 10 sion a proposal for a voluntary rate or rates for energy supplied to 11 residential customers who utilize a heat pump as their primary heating 12 source. Such voluntary rate or rates: 13 (a) May be new rates, new or existing riders, or incorporated into an 14 existing time-of-use rate; 15 (b) If cost-justified, shall be designed to lower the average monthly 16 energy bill of residential customers who utilize a heat pump as their 17 primary heating source; and 18 (c) Avoid cross-subsidies from other customers. 19 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
