Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "New York City Training and Comprehension of Trauma in Children 3 (TACTIC) Act". 4 § 2. "Trauma informed care" shall mean trauma informed care as described 11 by the substance abuse and mental health services administration of the 12 United States department of health and human services, or any successor 13 agency, department, or governmental entity. 14 2. "Teacher" shall mean all persons engaged in classroom teaching of 15 students, on a full-time or part-time basis, including but not limited 16 to, instructors, counselors, assistants or aides. 17 (b) (i) There is hereby established within the office a New York city 18 training and comprehension of trauma in children (TACTIC) council. 19 (ii) The TACTIC council shall consist of eight members, four of whom 20 shall be appointed by the speaker of the assembly and four of whom shall 21 be appointed by the temporary president of the senate. Title 21-A of the administrative code of the city of New York is 30 amended by adding a new chapter 8-A to read as follows: 31 CHAPTER 8-A 32 DEPARTMENT OF CHILD TRAUMA INFORMED CARE 33 § 21-970 Department of child trauma informed care. 34 a. There is hereby established within the department, a department of 35 child trauma informed care. 36 b. The department of child trauma informed care shall: 37 1. Assist in establishing trauma informed care parenting workshops 38 pursuant to section 7.51 of the mental hygiene law. 39 2. Compile and report to the training and comprehension of trauma in 40 children council, pursuant to section 7.51 of the mental hygiene law, on 41 child trauma informed care provided to schools within the city school 42 district, including, but not limited to, providing information regarding 43 school involvement, the number of students identified as experiencing 44 trauma, details of teacher certification, trauma incidents occurring in 45 schools and parental assistance provided. 46 3. Administer the mandatory trauma informed care certification program 47 for all teachers within the city school district. 48 4. Assign trauma informed care specialists to each borough superinten- 49 dent's office to supervise district trauma informed care specialists. 50 5. Assign district trauma informed care specialists to each school 51 located within the city school district to implement the annual trauma 52 informed care training program to teachers and to present the parenting 53 workshops within the district. Such district trauma informed care 54 specialists shall report to the trauma informed care specialists 55 assigned to each borough superintendent's office. 56 § 4.
