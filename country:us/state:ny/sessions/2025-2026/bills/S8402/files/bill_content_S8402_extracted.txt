Section 3 of chapter 801 of the laws of 2021, amending the 2 state administrative procedure act relating to public hearings on 3 proposed rules, as amended by chapter 116 of the laws of 2022, is 4 amended to read as follows: 5 § 3. This act shall take effect on January 1, 2023, and shall expire 6 and be deemed repealed on [the thirty-first day of December of the 7 second calendar year following such effective date] January 1, 2027, and 8 shall apply to all rules for which a notice of proposed rule making or a 9 description in a regulatory agenda is published during such time period. 10 § 2.
