LUNSFORD, STECK, COLTON, SIMONE, CUNNINGHAM -- read once and referred to the Committee on Environmental Conservation -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to agreements and easements related to renewable energy interconnection across reforestation areas The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a 2 new section 9-0508 to read as follows: 3 § 9-0508. Agreements and easements related to renewable energy intercon- 4 nection. 5 Notwithstanding any provisions of article seven of the public lands 6 law to the contrary, the department may, in any lands acquired as 7 reforestation areas by the state in accordance with section 9-0501 of 8 this title, enter into agreements, including but not limited to leases, 9 or easements, for the sole purpose of aiding in the interconnection of 10 renewable energy installations located outside such lands and the trans- 11 mission and distribution of electricity therefrom. Such interconnection 12 rights shall not interfere with the operation of such reforestation 13 areas for the purposes for which they were acquired and as defined in 14 section three of article fourteen of the New York state constitution. 15 Any agreement made and entered into pursuant to this section shall be 16 recorded in the office of general services and be made available to the 17 public on the official website of such office. 18 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
