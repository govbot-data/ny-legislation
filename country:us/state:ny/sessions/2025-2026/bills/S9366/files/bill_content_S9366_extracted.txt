BAILEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Investigations and Govern- ment Operations AN ACT to amend the legislative law, in relation to establishing a uniform effective date for health insurance benefit mandates The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The legislative law is amended by adding a new section 43-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 43-a. Time of taking effect of laws related to health insurance 4 coverage. 1. Every law enacted after the effective date of this section 5 that adds provisions to or amends provisions of section three thousand 6 two hundred sixteen, three thousand two hundred twenty-one, and four 7 thousand three hundred three of the insurance law requiring a policy of 8 health insurance to provide coverage for a new health care service, 9 treatment, or benefit, or that otherwise mandates coverage under such 10 policy shall take effect no earlier than the first of January next 11 succeeding the date on which it shall have become a law and shall apply 12 to policies and contracts issued, renewed, modified, altered or amended 13 on or after such effective date. 14 2. The superintendent of financial services may promulgate such rules 15 and regulations as are necessary to implement the provisions of this 16 section. 17 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
