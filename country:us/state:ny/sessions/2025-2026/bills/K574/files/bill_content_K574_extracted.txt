Buttenschon COMMEMORATING the 75th Anniversary of AmeriCU Credit Union WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the achievements of those individuals and businesses that contribute substantially to the economic vitality of their communities, thereby enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 75th Anniversary of AmeriCU Credit Union; and WHEREAS, AmeriCU Credit Union is a not-for-profit financial institution owned by its members and headquartered in Rome, New York; and WHEREAS, AmeriCU Credit Union was founded under the name Griffiss Employees Credit Union in 1950 by a group of civilian employees at the Griffiss Air Force Base; these founders held a shared vision and a mere $68 in pooled assets, which would be the groundwork for one of the largest credit unions headquartered in New York State; and WHEREAS, Since its founding in 1950, AmeriCU Credit Union has grown to possess $2.7 billion in assets and 20 financial centers while serving more than 167,000 members spread across 24 counties in Central New York, the Mohawk Valley, Northern New York, and the Southern Tier; and WHEREAS, With its substantial growth, AmeriCU Credit Union has become a leader in the credit union industry and has preserved its shared values and commitment to empowering its members and supporting its surrounding communities; and WHEREAS, To celebrate its 75th Anniversary, AmeriCU Credit Union has planned a year-long celebration filled with special events, promotions, giveaways, and community-focused initiatives to honor its members and the dedication of its team; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business, its contributions to the local and State economies, and its vibrant role in improving the quality of life in its community, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the sparkling success it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 75th Anniversary of AmeriCU Credit Union; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to AmeriCU Credit Union.
