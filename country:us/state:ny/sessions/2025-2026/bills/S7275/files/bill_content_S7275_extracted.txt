HOYLMAN-SIGAL -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Investigations and Government Operations -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the cannabis law, in relation to further restricting cannabis storefronts from being located within five hundred feet of a school and two hundred feet of a house of worship if they are located in a mixed use building The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 72 of the cannabis law is amended 2 to read as follows: 3 6. No cannabis retail licensee shall locate a storefront within five 4 hundred feet of [a] school grounds as such term is defined in the educa- 5 tion law and for the purposes of this subdivision shall also include all 6 entrances and exits in a mixed use building which contains or is used as 7 a school, or is within two hundred feet of a house of worship. For 8 purposes of this subdivision, a "house of worship" shall include any 9 area, including entrances and exits, in a mixed use building which 10 contains or is used as a house of worship. 11 § 2. Subdivision 4 of section 77 of the cannabis law is amended to 12 read as follows: 13 4. No applicant shall be granted an adult-use on-site consumption 14 license for any premises within five hundred feet of school grounds as 15 such term is defined in the education law and for the purposes of this 16 subdivision shall also include all entrances and exits in a mixed use 17 building which contains or is used as a school, or is two hundred feet 18 from a house of worship. For purposes of this subdivision, a "house of 19 worship" shall include any area, including entrances and exits, in a 20 mixed use building which contains or is used as a house of worship. 21 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately and shall apply to any new 22 license issued on or after such effective date.
