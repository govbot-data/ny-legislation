Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01574 Summary:BILL NOJ01574  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORPARKER COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01574 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1574 BY: Senator PARKER HONORING Ashley Oliver upon receiving the Rising Star Influencer Award in Promotional Modeling presented by the Young, Gifted, & Black Entrepreneurial Awards February 25, 2026 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend individuals whose leadership, creativity, and commitment to excellence uplift communities and reflect the power of cultural expression and purposeful influence; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Ashley Oliver upon the occasion of her receiving the Rising Star Influencer Award in Promotional Modeling from the Young, Gifted, & Black Entrepreneurial Awards on Wednesday, February 25, 2026; and WHEREAS, Ashley Oliver is an accomplished educator, hospitality manager, and cultural creative whose professional journey bridges leadership, community impact, and artistic expression across multiple disciplines; and WHEREAS, As a dedicated educator at Pioneer Academy in Queens, New York, Ashley Oliver is widely recognized for creating inclusive, engaging learning environments that empower students to feel seen, motivated, and inspired to reach their full potential; and WHEREAS, In the hospitality industry, Ashley Oliver has demonstrated exceptional professionalism, team leadership, and problem-solving ability, consistently cultivating upscale and welcoming environments that reflect her unwavering commitment to excellence; and WHEREAS, As @trinigyalwiner, a multicultural Caribbean-American influencer and emerging brand ambassador, Ashley Oliver uses her platform to celebrate culture, storytelling, and personal style while collaborating with lifestyle and beauty brands in a manner rooted in authenticity and representation; and WHEREAS, Her creative vision further extends to entrepreneurship and event curation through her growing lifestyle brand, "Lime & Wine," which highlights refined Caribbean culture, meaningful community connection, and elevated shared experiences; and WHEREAS, With heritage rooted in Trinidad, East Africa, and India, Ashley Oliver embodies multicultural pride and empowerment, leading with compassion, purpose, and integrity across education, hospitality, and digital media; and WHEREAS, The Rising Star Influencer Award in Promotional Modeling, presented by the Young, Gifted, & Black Entrepreneurial Awards, fittingly recognizes Ashley Oliver's emerging influence, creative leadership, and positive impact within her fields; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Ashley Oliver, upon the occasion of receiving the Rising Star Influencer Award in Promotional Modeling, and to commend her for her dedication to excellence, cultural expression, and community uplift; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Ashley Oliver and the Young, Gifted, & Black Entrepreneurial Awards as a lasting expression of the highest respect and appreciation of this Legislative Body.
