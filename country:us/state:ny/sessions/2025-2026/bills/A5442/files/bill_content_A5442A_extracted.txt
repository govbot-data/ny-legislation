AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to enacting the
"rate hike notice act"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "rate hike notice act".
3 § 2. Subdivision 12 of section 66 of the public service law is amended
4 by adding a new paragraph (m) to read as follows:
5 (m) Within one business day of the publication of the information
6 required pursuant to paragraph (l) of this subdivision, the commission
7 shall require each utility to send electronic notice of a proposed rate
8 increase via text to all customers for whom such utility contacts
9 through text. The text shall inform such customer that such utility is
10 proposing a rate increase in the customer's service area. Such text
11 shall contain the following:
12 (i) the name of this act, "the rate hike notice act";
13 (ii) the name of the utility proposing a rate increase;
14 (iii) the percentage increase to electricity and gas rates for the
15 typical residential customer;
16 (iv) the percentage increase to electricity and gas rates for the
17 typical commercial customer; and
18 (v) instructions for where ratepayers can find additional information
19 pertaining to the rate case. Further, within one business day of the
20 publication of the information required pursuant to paragraph (l) of
21 this subdivision, the commission shall also require each utility to send
22 electronic notice of a proposed rate increase via email to all customers
23 for whom such utility contacts through email and such email notice shall
