The public service law is amended by adding a new section 2 121-b to read as follows: 3 § 121-b. Filing of certain information. 1. All persons who intend to 4 construct a gas gathering line or a pipeline facility, shall file with 5 the department spatial mapping data, including but not limited to 6 geographic information system data, detailing the location of such 7 proposed gathering line or pipeline facility. 8 2. The department, in consultation with the department of environ- 9 mental conservation, shall establish and maintain a computer mapping 10 system for plotting the location of gathering lines and pipeline facili- 11 ties. Such maps shall overlay locations subject to the provisions of 12 article twenty-three of the environmental conservation law. 13 3. For purposes of this section, the following terms shall have the 14 following meanings: 15 (a) "Gathering line" means a pipeline that transports gas from a 16 current production facility to a transmission line, main, or directly to 17 an end user. 18 (b) "Pipeline facility" means new and existing pipeline, rights-of- 19 way, and any related equipment, facility, or building, used in the 20 transportation of gas, or in the treatment of gas during the course of 21 transportation, in connection with the production, gathering, treatment 22 or processing of such gas, but does not include any: (i) transmission 23 and distribution system operated by a local distribution company subject 24 to the jurisdiction of the commission for purposes of providing retail EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
