        AN  ACT  to  amend  the  environmental  conservation law, in relation to
          extended producer responsibility for carpet
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The title heading of title 33 of article 27 of the environ-
     2  mental conservation law, as added by chapter 795 of the laws of 2022, is
     3  amended to read as follows:
     4                     EXTENDED PRODUCER RESPONSIBILITY FOR
     5                         CARPET [COLLECTION PROGRAM]
     6    §  2.  Section 27-3319 of the environmental conservation law is renum-
     7  bered section 27-3321 and  a new section 27-3319 is  added  to  read  as
     8  follows:
     9  § 27-3319. Limits  on  liability  for  antitrust, restraint of trade, or
    10               unfair trade practices.
    11    1. A producer, representative organization, or retailer shall  not  be
    12  liable for any claim of a violation of antitrust, restraint of trade, or
    13  unfair  trade  practice  laws  arising from the performance of an action
    14  required to be undertaken by this title or which is necessary to  imple-
    15  ment  or  participate  under a producer plan approved by the department,
    16  solely to increase the collection and recycling of carpet, which affects
    17  the types and quantities being collected and recycled or  the  cost  and
    18  structure  of  such collection program that the producer, representative
    19  organization, or retailer is participating in pursuant to this title.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
                                                                   LBD04801-03-5

        S. 2097--A                          2
 
     1    2. Provided, however, subdivision one of this section shall not  apply
     2  to  any agreement establishing or affecting the price of carpet, except,
     3  as necessary, to fulfill producer responsibility under subdivision  five
     4  of section 27-3305 of this title, the output or production of carpet, or
     5  restricting  the  geographic  area  or customers to which carpet will be
     6  sold.
     7    § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
