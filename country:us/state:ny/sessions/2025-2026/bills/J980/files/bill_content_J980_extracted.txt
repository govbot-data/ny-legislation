and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the 1940 Nott Terrace High School Relay Team upon the occasion of being honored as part of a special Legacy Tribute during the 27th Annual Schenectady City School District Athletic Hall of Fame and Reunion Dinner on Monday, September 15, 2025, at the Event Center at Rivers Casino & Resort in Schenectady, New York; and WHEREAS, Excellence and success in competitive sports can be achieved only through strenuous practice, teamwork, and team spirit, nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition enhances the moral and physical development of the youth of this State, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the value of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, imparting a desire for success, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, Coached by Athletic Hall of Famer Bill Eddy, the 1940 Nott Terrace High School Relay Team won the 1940 Penn Relays Two-Mile Relay Championship of America; and WHEREAS, The Penn Relays are a very significant event in the world of track and field as the oldest and largest track and field competition in the United States, attracting thousands of participants and spectators annually; and WHEREAS, Members of 1940 Nott Terrace High School Relay Team included Reggie Crocker, Tony Giavannone, Rob Russell and Schenectady City School District Athletic Hall of Famer and future Notre Dame standout, Bill Leonard; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as track and field, which demands stamina, discipline and determination, Head Coach Bill Eddy worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching them outstanding lessons which proved invaluable both on and off the track; and WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body that when individuals and teams of such outstanding athletic accomplishments are brought to our attention, they should be recognized by all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the 1940 Nott Terrace High School Relay Team members: Reggie Crocker, Tony Giavannone, Rob Russell and Bill Leonard; and Head Coach Bill Eddy on their sustained excellence and overall team record; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members and coaches of the 1940 Nott Terrace High School Relay Team.
