Conrad HONORING Lieutenant Lee Bolsover after 26 years of dedicated service to the North Tonawanda Police Department WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and honor those distinguished officers who would devote themselves to public service, demonstrating great courage and diligence in providing for the care and welfare of the citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Lieutenant Lee Bolsover after 26 years of dedicated service to the North Tonawanda Police Department; and WHEREAS, Within every community of the State of New York there are certain individuals who, by virtue of their commitment and dedication, command the respect and admiration of their community for their exemplary contributions and service on behalf of others; and WHEREAS, Lee Bolsover started with North Tonawanda Police Department in 2000 and quickly rose through the ranks of the department; he became a field training officer in 2002, and later was assigned to the SWAT team in 2004, before earning the rank of SWAT team commander, the esteemed role from which he will retire; and WHEREAS, In 2013, Lee Bolsover was promoted to the rank of Lieutenant and continued to in the Patrol Division for the rest of his career; and WHEREAS, Exemplifying true heroism and pride in his career, Lieutenant Lee Bolsover received a lifesaving award in 2018 for saving a citizen from the freezing waters of the Erie Canal; and WHEREAS, In his official acts, Lieutenant Lee Bolsover was governed by a keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and WHEREAS, During his tenure with the North Tonawanda Police Department, Lieutenant Lee Bolsover earned the admiration, esteem, and affection of his colleagues; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those dedicated public servants who unselfishly devote their lives to the preservation of order and the protection of others are worthy and due full praise for their commitment and noble endeavors; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Lieutenant Lee Bolsover after 26 years of dedicated service to the North Tonawanda Police Department; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to honoring Lieutenant Lee Bolsover.
