MAY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the public service law and the public authorities law, in relation to establishing a grid modernization surcharge; and to amend the state finance law, in relation to establishing the grid modernization fund The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public service law is amended by adding a new section 2 66-x to read as follows: 3 § 66-x. Grid modernization surcharge. 1. For the purposes of this 4 section, the following terms shall have the following meanings: 5 (a) "Data center" means any facility with an information-technology 6 (IT) load exceeding ten megawatts (MW) of electrical demand, used prima- 7 rily for the storage, management, processing, or transmission of digital 8 information; and 9 (b) "High-intensity data center" means a data center with an annual 10 power-usage effectiveness (PUE) greater than 1.3 or an annual electric- 11 ity consumption exceeding 50 gigawatt-hours. 12 2. (a) The commission shall by regulation establish a grid moderniza- 13 tion surcharge to be collected by each electric corporation from every 14 covered data center account as a separate line item on the customer 15 bill. The commission shall set the surcharge as a rate per kilowatt-hour 16 or per megawatt-hour billed to such accounts and may differentiate by 17 voltage level or service class. 18 (b) A covered data center that demonstrates, through independent 19 verification acceptable to the commission, that at least ninety percent 20 of its annual electricity consumption is met by renewable resources 21 under contracts that meet commission-established additionality and 22 deliverability criteria may receive a partial or full reduction of the 23 surcharge. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new section 23 1005-e to read as follows: 24 § 1005-e. Grid modernization surcharge. 1. The authority shall adopt 25 and enforce an equivalent surcharge for covered data center customers 26 served by the authority and shall remit all receipts to the grid modern- 27 ization fund. The authority shall coordinate with the public service 28 commission to ensure consistency with regulations adopted under section 29 sixty-six-x of the public service law. 30 2. Such surcharge shall be deposited into the grid modernization fund 31 established pursuant to section eighty-five of the state finance law. 32 § 3. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 85 to 33 read as follows: 34 § 85. Grid modernization fund. 1. There is hereby established in the 35 joint custody of the state comptroller and the public service commis- 36 sion, a special fund to be known as the "grid modernization fund". 37 2. Such fund shall consist of all moneys collected pursuant to subdi- 38 vision two of section sixty-six-x of the public service law. Nothing 39 contained in this section shall prevent the state from receiving grants, 40 gifts, or bequests for the purposes of the fund as defined in this 41 section and depositing them into the fund according to law. 42 3. Monies of the fund shall be expended only to finance investments in 43 system reliability, capacity expansion, and integration of clean energy 44 resources. 45 4. Monies shall be payable from the fund on the audit and warrant of 46 the comptroller on vouchers approved and certified by the public service 47 commission. 48 § 4. This act shall take effect one year after it shall have become a 49 law.
