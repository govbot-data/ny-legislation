AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to enacting the
"Jamie Rose care provider act"
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "Jamie Rose care provider act".
3 § 2. The public health law is amended by adding a new section 3623 to
4 read as follows:
5 § 3623. Home care domestic violence and elder abuse policies and
6 procedures. 1. For the purposes of this section:
7 (a) "Domestic abuse victim" shall have the same meaning as such term
8 is defined in subdivision one of section four hundred fifty nine-a of
9 the social services law.
10 (b) "Elder abuse victim" shall mean an individual aged sixty years of
11 age or older subject to abuse and maltreatment. Abuse and maltreatment
12 shall mean actions as defined in paragraphs (a), (b), (c), (d), (e), and
13 (g) of subdivision six of section four hundred seventy-three of the
14 social services law.
15 2. Every home care services agency as defined in this article shall:
16 (a) develop, maintain and disseminate written policies and procedures
17 for the identification, assessment, treatment and referral of confirmed
18 or suspected cases of domestic violence or elder abuse; (b) establish,
19 and implement on an ongoing basis, a training program for all home care
20 personnel regarding the policies and procedures established pursuant to
21 this section; and (c) designate a staff member to contact the domestic
22 violence or elder abuse victim assistance organization identified by the
23 commissioner under subdivision four of this section providing victim
24 assistance to the geographic area served by such home care services
25 agency to establish the coordination of services to domestic violence or
26 elder abuse victims.
