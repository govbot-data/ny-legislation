Section 3205 of the education law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 4 to read as follows: 3 4. Upon registration of a minor to attend full time instruction pursu- 4 ant to this section, parents shall check a space on such registration 5 authorizing such minor to receive a library card from the respective 6 local library.
