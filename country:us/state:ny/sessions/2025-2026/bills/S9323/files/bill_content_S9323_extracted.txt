Short title. This act shall be known as "pediatric pallia- 2 tive care act". 3 § 2. The public health law is amended by adding new section 2801-I to 4 read as follows: 5 § 2801-I. For the purposes of 6 this section, "pediatric palliative care patient" means an individual 7 who: 8 (a) is under twenty-one years of age; 9 (b) has been diagnosed with a chronic, complex, life-threatening 10 illness that is expected to shorten the individual's life expectancy; 11 and 12 (c) is not expected to survive past twenty-one years of age. 13 2.
