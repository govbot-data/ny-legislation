AN ACT to amend the mental hygiene law, in relation to holders of
certain operating certificates authorized to provide behavioral analy-
sis services, or to employ or contract with applied behavior analysts
and mental health counselors
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 16.03 of the mental hygiene law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision (h) to read as follows:
3 (h) Any provider of services that holds an operating certificate
4 pursuant to subdivision (a) of this section shall be authorized to
5 provide behavioral analysis services, in accordance with article one
6 hundred sixty-seven of the education law and with persons licensed to
7 provide mental health counseling pursuant to section eighty-four hundred
8 two of the education law, so long as the professional providing such
9 services is acting within their lawful scope of practice. Additionally,
10 any such provider that holds an operating certificate pursuant to subdi-
11 vision (a) of this section shall also be authorized to employ or
12 contract with persons licensed or certified to practice applied behavior
13 analysis pursuant to article one hundred sixty-seven of the education
14 law and with persons licensed to provide mental health counseling pursu-
15 ant to section eighty-four hundred two of the education law.
16 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
