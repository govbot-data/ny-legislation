This act shall be known and may be cited as the "wig insur- 2 ance guarantee (W.I.G) act". 3 § 2. Subsection (i) of section 3216 of the insurance law is amended by 4 adding a new paragraph 42 to read as follows: 5 (42) (A) Every policy which provides coverage for prostheses shall 6 provide coverage for cranial prostheses for individuals experiencing 7 permanent or temporary medical hair loss provided: 8 (i) a licensed provider prescribes such cranial prosthesis for a 9 covered person's course of treatment for the diagnosed health condition, 10 chronic illness, or injury, including, but not limited to, alopecia 11 areata, alopecia medicamentosa, scarring alopecia, and lupus; 12 (ii) the policy provides coverage for a cranial prosthesis to a 13 covered person no more frequently than once every twelve months; and 14 (iii) the coverage is limited to seven hundred fifty dollars for each 15 instance of coverage, and shall be subject to any cost-sharing require- 16 ments that are otherwise applicable. 17 (B) For purposes of this paragraph, the term "cranial prosthesis" 18 means a wig or hairpiece. 19 § 3.
