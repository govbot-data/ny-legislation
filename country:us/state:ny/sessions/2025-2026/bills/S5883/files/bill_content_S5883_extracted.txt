LANZA, HELMING, PALUMBO, ROLISON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to establishing distinctive "Alzheimer's Awareness" license plates; and to amend the state finance law, in relation to providing for further funding for the "Alzheimer's disease assistance fund" The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive "Alzheimer's Awareness" license plates. 1. Any 4 person residing in this state shall, upon request, be issued a distinc- 5 tive "Alzheimer's Awareness" license plate of a design approved by the 6 commissioner. Application for said license plate shall be filed with the 7 form and detail as the commissioner shall prescribe. 8 2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 9 in the same manner as other number plates upon the payment of the regu- 10 lar registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this 11 article, provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of 12 twenty-five dollars shall be charged for such plate. Such additional 13 service charge shall be deposited pursuant to section four hundred four- 14 oo of this article to the credit of the department of motor vehicles 15 distinctive plate development fund, established by section ninety-five-g 16 of the state finance law, and shall be used for the production, design, 17 advertising and marketing of distinctive number plates; provided, howev- 18 er, that additional annual service charges collected in excess of six 19 thousand dollars shall be deposited to the credit of the Alzheimer's 20 disease assistance fund established pursuant to section eighty-nine-e of 21 the state finance law.
