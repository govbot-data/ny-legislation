PAULIN, SANTABARBARA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring stretch limousines to be retired from use after ten years from manu- facture or the accumulation of three hundred fifty thousand miles The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 375 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 58 to read as follows: 3 58. (a) Any stretch limousine registered in this state which is used 4 in the business of transporting passengers for compensation shall be 5 retired from use once such stretch limousine reaches ten years of age 6 from its original date of manufacture, or accumulates three hundred 7 fifty thousand miles, whichever comes first. 8 (b) For purposes of this subdivision, "stretch limousine" shall mean 9 an altered motor vehicle having a seating capacity of nine or more 10 passengers, including the driver, commonly referred to as a "stretch 11 limousine" and which is used in the business of transporting passengers 12 for compensation. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
