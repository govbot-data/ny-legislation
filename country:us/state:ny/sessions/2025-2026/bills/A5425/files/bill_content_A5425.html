SAME ASSAME AS S04329-A
 
SPONSORBerger

MLTSPNSR
 
Add §162-b, St Fin L; add §104-e, Gen Muni L; add §2877-b, Pub Auth L; ren Art 50 §§1000 - 1003 to be Art 60 §§1101 - 1103, add Art 50 §§1010 - 1012, Exec L
 
Enacts the "New York State buy North American act" which requires the state, municipalities, public authorities and covered affordable housing projects to award contracts for the supply of manufactured products used in building projects to bidders who produce such manufactured products used in building projects primarily in North America; defines terms; makes related provisions.

    16    §  1010.  Statement  of  purpose. It is the purpose of this article to
    17  encourage and promote the purchase of North American  made  manufactured
    18  products  used  in  building  projects  for  covered  affordable housing
    19  projects paid for in whole or in part out of public funds.
    20    § 1011. Definitions. For purposes of this article, the following terms
    21  shall have the following meanings:
    22    1. "Covered affordable housing project" shall mean  construction  work
    23  done  under contract for an affordable housing project which is paid for
    24  in whole or in part out of public funds, as such term is defined in this
    25  section, where the amount of all such public funds, when aggregated,  is
    26  at  least  thirty  percent  of  the total construction project costs and
    27  where such project costs are over five million dollars.
    28    2. "Paid for in whole or in part out of public funds" shall  mean  any
    29  of the following:
    30    (a)  the payment of money, by a public entity, or a third party acting
    31  on behalf of and for the benefit of a public entity, directly to  or  on
    32  behalf  of  a  contractor, subcontractor, developer or owner that is not
    33  subject to repayment;
    34    (b) the savings achieved from fees, rents, interest  rates,  or  other
    35  loan  costs,  or  insurance costs that are lower than market rate costs;
    36  savings from reduced taxes as a result of tax credits,  tax  abatements,
    37  tax exemptions or tax increment financing; savings from payments in lieu
    38  of  taxes; and any other savings from reduced, waived, or forgiven costs
    39  that would have otherwise been at a higher or market rate  but  for  the
    40  involvement of a public entity;
    41    (c)  money loaned by a public entity that is to be repaid on a contin-
    42  gent basis; or
    43    (d) credits that are applied by a public entity against  repayment  of
    44  obligations to such public entity.
    45    3.  "Manufactured  products used in building projects produced or made
    46  in whole or in part in North America" means the total value,  in  United
    47  States  dollars,  of  the fabrication materials, extrusions, components,
    48  hardware, and labor sourced, manufactured,  and/or  performed  in  North
    49  America equals or exceeds seventy-five percent of the total value of all
    50  materials,  extrusions, components, hardware, and labor for the finished
    51  manufactured product used in building projects.
    52    4. "United States" means the United States of America and includes all
    53  territory, continental or insular, subject to the  jurisdiction  of  the
    54  United States.
    55    5. "North America" means the United States, Canada and Mexico.
