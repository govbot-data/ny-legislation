this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Mylie Weyant upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Division I Championship in Girls Bowling during the state composite division tournament at AMF Syracuse Lanes in Syracuse, New York, on Saturday, March 15, 2025; and WHEREAS, Rome Free Academy junior Mylie Weyant was crowned Division I Champion with the highest average of 229.16 and an individual series score of 1,375; and WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's colors of black and orange, family members, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported Mylie Weyant throughout her journey as she ended her regular season with an impressive overall average 216; and WHEREAS, Sports competition instills the values of teamwork, pride and accomplishment, and Mylie Weyant, who is in her fifth-year bowling on the varsity team, has clearly made a contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of her school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Mylie Weyant upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Division I Championship in Girls Bowling on March 15, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Mylie Weyant.
