        AN ACT to amend chapter 118 of the laws of 1969, relating to a  separate
          union  free school district in the town of Greenburgh in the county of
          Westchester, in relation to property  used  by  the  Greenburgh  North
          Castle  Union  Free  School  District  number  12 and authorizing such
          school district to affiliate with Rising Ground, Inc.; and  to  repeal
          subdivision  b of section 5 of chapter 118 of the laws of 1969, relat-
          ing to a separate union free school district in the town of Greenburgh
          in the county of Westchester, relating to authorizing  the  Greenburgh
          North  Castle  Union  Free School District number 12 to affiliate with
          any child care institution or  entity  other  than  St.  Christopher's
          School, Inc.
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 5 of chapter 118 of the laws of 1969, relating to a
     2  separate union free school district in the town  of  Greenburgh  in  the
     3  county  of  Westchester, as added by chapter 179 of the laws of 2024, is
     4  amended to read as follows:
     5    § 5. a. Notwithstanding section three and four of this act, the Green-

    14  North  Castle Union Free School District number 12 created by this chap-
