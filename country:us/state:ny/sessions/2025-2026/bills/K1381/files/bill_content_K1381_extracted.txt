Rules (Alvarez) MEMORIALIZING Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim August 16, 2026, as Hispanic Media Day in the State of New York WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the impact, in a positive and substantial way, the contributions the Hispanic media has had on all the communities in the State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim, August 16, 2026, as Hispanic Media Day; and WHEREAS, Since the Hispanic media has more than 50 years of experience in the communication of radio, press and television, their contributions are vital to the development of the community at large; and WHEREAS, A majority of the Hispanic media are truly professionals in their areas of work; and WHEREAS, The Hispanic media's contributions have allowed the growth of the Hispanic community and the overall development of media established in the State of New York; and WHEREAS, The growth of the Hispanic media has constituted in the growth of small businesses and the community at large; and WHEREAS, The Hispanic media in general has made significant contributions to the overall development of American society; and WHEREAS, The Hispanic media has and will always play a decisive role for generations in the State of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim August 16, 2026, as Hispanic Media Day in the State of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of New York.
