Section 132 of the cannabis law is amended by adding a new subdi- 45 vision 1-b to read as follows: 46 1-b. Any commercial establishment, store, club, or facility, that 47 knowingly and unlawfully sells, transfers, gifts or trades any amount of 48 cannabis without an appropriate license or authority under this chapter 49 shall be subject to a civil penalty of not more than ten thousand 50 dollars for a first violation; not more than twenty-five thousand 51 dollars for a second violation; and not more than fifty thousand dollars 52 for a third violation and each subsequent violation thereafter. The 53 civil penalty shall be payable to the office of cannabis management, who 54 shall be required to remit such payments to the county in which the 55 violating establishment is located. 56 § 5.
