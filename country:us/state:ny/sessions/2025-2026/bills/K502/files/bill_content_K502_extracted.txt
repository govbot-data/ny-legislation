Conrad COMMENDING Class of 2025 Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo of Mount St. Mary Academy, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord with its long-standing traditions, to honor the youth of today, the leaders of tomorrow, whose character and achievements exemplify the ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of 2025 Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo of Mount St. Mary Academy, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and WHEREAS, Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo represents the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious resource, our youth; her achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community and should be recognized and saluted; and WHEREAS, Graduating with outstanding honors, Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo has earned several awards throughout her academic career, including the New York State Senate Youth Leadership Award, the University of Rochester George Eastman Young Leaders Award, Student Councilor of the Year Award, Blue Rose Award, and the College Board AP Scholar Award; and WHEREAS, In addition to her stellar academic achievements, Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo was a dedicated student-athlete, serving as captain of the dance team and member of the Senior Competition Team at The Grand Island Dance Center; and WHEREAS, Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo engrossed herself in extracurricular activities and community involvement at Mount St. Mary Academy, having served as president of the Student Council; co-CEO of Mount St. Mary Academy's student-run virtual enterprise business, Fly By Fashion; and ambassador for The Girl Scouts of Western New York; and WHEREAS, Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo intends on continuing her academic journey by studying public health and policy; and WHEREAS, As Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo takes leave of Mount St. Mary Academy, she bestows a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement and community service to which others may aspire; and WHEREAS, Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo may now stand with pride as she assesses her achievements, experiences the satisfaction of her labor, and feels the joy of her accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Class of 2025 Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo of Mount St. Mary Academy, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Salutatorian Diana DeAngelo.
