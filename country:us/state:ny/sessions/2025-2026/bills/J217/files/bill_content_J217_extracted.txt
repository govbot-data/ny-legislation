and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 75th Anniversary of Eck Plastic Arts of Binghamton, New York; and WHEREAS, Eck Plastic Arts, the producer of quality plastic parts for original equipment manufacturers, was founded in 1949 and has been a steadfast presence in the community for 75 years; still today, the company maintains an unbreakable commitment to its employees, customers, and community; and WHEREAS, The Mission Statement of Eck Plastic Arts asserts the company will hire and train good people, investing in them so they can invest in their community; continually improve its processes, ensuring on-time, cost-effective projects; and deliver the highest quality plastic parts, motivating confidence in every partnership; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, Eck Plastic Arts has remained in Binghamton, committed to growing alongside the community it calls home; the company has expanded from 14 employees seven years ago to 25 workers today; frequently, there is a need for a second shift to meet the increasing demand; and WHEREAS, The dedication to excellence has earned Eck Plastic Arts industry-wide recognition; the company was bestowed the BAE Systems Partner 2 Win Supplier of the Year Award in both 2018 and 2023; and WHEREAS, Eck Plastic Arts prides itself on producing parts right the first time and delivering them on time, earning the trust and loyalty of its customers; regardless of the size of an order, consumers will receive excellent customer service and products of only the highest quality; and WHEREAS, Eck Plastic Arts is always expanding and exemplifying its Mission Statement; possessing its proud heritage, the company anticipates a future of continued innovation and contribution to the plastics industry; there are plans to foster growth locally, continuing to contribute to the Binghamton area's economy and job market; and WHEREAS, It is with great pride that Eck Plastic Arts and its dedicated workers celebrate this milestone 75th Anniversary and anticipate many more years of success for generations to come; and WHEREAS, The progression of Eck Plastic Arts is in direct correlation to the efforts of its owners and employees, whose involvement is, and always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of the business and the community it serves; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business, its contributions to the local and State economies, and its vibrant role in improving the quality of life in its community, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the success it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 75th Anniversary of Eck Plastic Arts; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Eck Plastic Arts.
