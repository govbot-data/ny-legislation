Subdivision 7 of section 7-104 of the election law, as 2 amended by chapter 411 of the laws of 2019, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 7. The ballot shall have printed upon it in black ink for each party 5 or independent row, at the head of the column or the beginning of the 6 row containing the names of candidates, the name of the party or inde- 7 pendent body [and the designating letter of the row or column]. 8 § 2.
