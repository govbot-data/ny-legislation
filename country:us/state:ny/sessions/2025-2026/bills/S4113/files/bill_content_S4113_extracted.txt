COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to capital program plans The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraphs (b) and (c) of subdivision 1 of section 1269-b 2 of the public authorities law, as added by chapter 637 of the laws of 3 1996, are amended and two new paragraphs (d) and (e) are added to read 4 as follows: 5 (b) not later than ten days after the effective date of this paragraph 6 the authority shall submit to the metropolitan transportation authority 7 capital program review board two capital program plans for the five-year 8 period commencing January first, nineteen hundred ninety-five; [and] 9 (c) on or before October first, nineteen hundred ninety-nine and every 10 fifth year thereafter, the authority shall submit to the metropolitan 11 transportation authority capital program review board two capital 12 program plans for the five-year period commencing January first of the 13 following year[.]; and 14 (d) on and after January first, two thousand twenty-six, the authority 15 shall submit to the metropolitan transportation authority capital 16 program review board on or before January thirty-first, two thousand 17 thirty and every fifth year thereafter, two capital program plans for 18 the five-year period commencing January first of the following year. 19 (e) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the capital 20 program plans required to be submitted to the metropolitan transporta- 21 tion authority capital program review board by the authority shall be 22 submitted on or before January thirty-first of the year prior to the 23 period for which the submitted capital program plans take place. 24 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
