        AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to guidance to facilities
          and programs overseen by state agencies on interactions  with  Federal
          immigration authorities

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Paragraphs (o) and (p) of subdivision 5 of section 94-b  of
     2  the  executive  law,  as  added  by chapter 625 of the laws of 2024, are
     3  amended and a new paragraph (q) is added to read as follows:
     4    (o) Encourage the development of and provide for the establishment  of
     5  a state military immigrant family legacy program liaison, as provided in
     6  section twenty-nine-b of the veterans' services law; [and]
     7    (p)  Request individuals seeking assistance from the office answer the
     8  following questions: "Have you served in the  United  States  military?"
     9  "Has  someone  in  your  family  served  in the United States military?"
    10  Individuals identifying themselves  or  a  family  member  as  "intended
    11  recipients"  of  the staff sergeant Alex R. Jimenez New York state mili-
    12  tary immigrant family legacy program as such term is  defined  in  para-
    13  graph  (e)  of subdivision one of section twenty-nine-b of the veterans'
    14  services law, shall be advised of such program. In addition, such  indi-
    15  viduals  shall be informed that the department of veterans' services and
    16  local veterans' service agencies established pursuant to section  seven-
    17  teen  of  the  veterans'  services  law  provide assistance to uniformed
    18  service members, veterans and their families regarding  benefits  avail-
    19  able  under  federal  and  state law. Information regarding veterans and
    20  military status provided by assisted individuals shall be  protected  as
    21  personal  confidential material, and used only to identify such individ-
    22  uals as "intended recipients" of the staff sergeant Alex R.  Jimenez New
    23  York State military immigrant family legacy program, and to assist  such
    24  individuals  in  matters relating to immigration status and citizenship,

     5    (q) Assist state agencies in the preparation of guidance to be dissem-
