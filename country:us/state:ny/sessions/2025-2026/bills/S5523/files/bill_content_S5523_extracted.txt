SANDERS -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to the creation of a pre-specialized high schools admissions test and preparation program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 2590-h of the education law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 57 to read as follows: 3 57. Establish, in conjunction with the appropriate New York city 4 department of education officials, a test preparation program for 5 students in the sixth grade culminating in a pre-specialized high 6 schools admissions test to be administered in May or June of each year. 7 Such test preparation and the pre-specialized high schools admissions 8 test shall be available to all sixth grade students free of charge 9 during the regular operating hours of the school day. Parents may 10 choose for their student to not be a part of the test preparation 11 program. Upon completion of the program, each student, their parents, 12 and their school shall receive a gap analysis. Schools shall use each 13 student's gap analysis to tailor the student's instruction to meet the 14 needs of the student including, but not limited to, the use of the 15 specialized high schools admissions test intensive or any other New York 16 city department of education program available. 17 § 2. Section 2590-h of the education law is amended by adding a new 18 subdivision 39 to read as follows: 19 39.
