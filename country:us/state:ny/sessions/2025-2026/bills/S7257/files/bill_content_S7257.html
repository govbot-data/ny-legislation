S07257 Summary:

BILL NOS07257
 
SAME ASNo Same As
 
SPONSORLANZA
 
COSPNSR

Amd Pen L, generally
 
Redesignates certain sex, prostitution, obscenity and sexual performance offenses committed against a child as class A-I felonies with a sentence of life imprisonment without parole.
