BROWN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Alcoholism and Drug Abuse AN ACT to require that the siting, construction and establishment of methadone program facilities in cities having a population of one million or more be dispersed in the boroughs of such cities in propor- tion to the population of each such borough The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, with- 2 in cities having a population of one million or more, methadone program 3 facilities registered with the office of addiction services and supports 4 pursuant to section 19.16 of the mental hygiene law within each such 5 city shall be sited, constructed and/or established throughout the 6 boroughs of such city in proportion to each borough's population to the 7 city's total population. Provided, however, that the provisions of this 8 section shall not apply to the siting, construction or establishment of 9 any methadone program facility for which the appropriate community board 10 or, if there be no community board, the appropriate neighborhood board 11 shall have granted an exemption from such provisions. 12 § 2. Methadone program facilities sited, constructed and/or estab- 13 lished within cities having a population of one million or more after 14 the effective date of this act shall be first located in those boroughs 15 of the city which have the lowest concentration of such facilities in 16 proportion to the borough population to the city's population. Once each 17 borough has achieved proper proportionality pursuant to this section, 18 siting, construction, and/or establishment of such facilities shall 19 continue within a city having a population of one million or more with 20 such proportionality requirements in mind. 21 § 3.
