In the event that an operator of 4 a nursing home elects to close the facility, the following shall apply: 5 (a) The operator shall submit written notice to the department at 6 least ninety days prior to the anticipated date of closure of the nurs- 7 ing home. 8 (b) The written notice shall include a proposed plan for closure of 9 the nursing home.
