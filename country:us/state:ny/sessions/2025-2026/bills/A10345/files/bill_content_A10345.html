Dissolves the Rockland county drainage agency and transfers its powers to the Rockland county highway department; repeals provisions relating to creating a stream control act for the county of Rockland and defining its powers and duties.

A10345 Text:




 
                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          10345
 
                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                    February 26, 2026
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced by M. of A. WIEDER -- read once and referred to the Committee
          on Local Governments
 
        AN  ACT  in  relation to dissolving the Rockland county drainage agency;
          and to repeal chapter 846 of the laws of 1975, relating to creating  a
          stream  control  act  for  the  county  of Rock- land and defining its
          powers and duties

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1. 1. Dissolution of the Rockland county drainage agency. The
     2  Rockland county drainage agency is hereby  dissolved  and  discontinued,
     3  and all functions, duties, debts, and obligations are hereby transferred
     4  to  the  Rockland  county  highway  department  in  accordance  with the
     5  provisions of this act.
     6    2. Transfer of functions. All of the functions  and  powers  possessed
     7  by,  and all of the obligations and duties of the Rockland county drain-
     8  age agency are transferred and assigned to, assumed by and devolved upon
     9  the Rockland county highway department, in accordance with and  pursuant
    10  to the provisions of this act.
    11    3.  Transfer of employees. Notwithstanding any other provision of law,
    12  rule, or regulation to the contrary, upon the transfer of functions from
    13  the Rockland county drainage agency pursuant to this act, all  employees
    14  of the Rockland county drainage agency shall be transferred to the Rock-
    15  land  county  highway department. Employees transferred pursuant to this
    16  section shall be transferred without further examination  or  qualifica-
    17  tion  and  shall  retain their respective civil service classifications,
    18  status  and  collective  bargaining  unit  designations  and  collective
    19  bargaining agreements.
    20    4.  Transfer  of records. All books, papers, and property of the Rock-
    21  land county drainage agency shall be provided to and deemed to be in the
    22  possession of the Rockland county highway department.
    23    5. Continuity of authority. For the purpose of succession of all func-
    24  tions, powers, duties  and  obligations  transferred  and  assigned  to,
    25  devolved upon and assumed by it pursuant to this act, the Rockland coun-

     1  ty highway department shall be deemed and held to constitute the contin-

     3    6.  Completion  of  unfinished  business. Any business or other matter
     4  undertaken or commenced by the Rockland county drainage agency  pertain-
     5  ing  to  or connected with the functions, powers, obligations and duties
     6  hereby transferred and assigned to the Rockland county  highway  depart-
     7  ment and pending on the effective date of this act, may be conducted and
     8  completed  by  the Rockland county highway department in the same manner
     9  and under the same terms and conditions and with the same effect  as  if
    10  conducted and completed by the Rockland county drainage agency.
    11    7.  Continuation  of  rules  and  regulations. All rules, regulations,
    12  acts, orders, determinations,  and  decisions  of  the  Rockland  county
    13  drainage  agency  pertaining to the functions and powers transferred and
    14  assigned pursuant to this act, in force at the time of such transfer and
    15  assumption, shall continue in full force  and  effect  as  rules,  regu-
    16  lations,  acts,  orders,  determinations  and  decisions of the Rockland
    17  county highway department until  duly  modified  or  abrogated  by  such
    18  department.
    19    8.  Terms  occurring  in laws, contracts and other documents. Whenever
    20  the Rockland county drainage agency, or the board thereof,  is  referred
    21  to  or  designated  in  any contract or document pertaining to the func-
    22  tions, powers, obligations and duties hereby transferred to and assigned
    23  to the Rockland county highway department, such reference or designation
    24  shall be deemed to refer to the Rockland county highway department.
    25    9. Existing rights and remedies preserved. No existing right or remedy
    26  of any character shall be lost, impaired or affected by  any  provisions
    27  of this act.
    28    10.  Pending  actions and proceedings. No action or proceeding pending
    29  at the time when this act shall take effect, brought by or  against  the
    30  Rockland county drainage agency, or the board thereof, shall be affected
    31  by any provision of this act, but the same may be prosecuted or defended
    32  in  the  name  of  the  Rockland  county highway department. In all such
    33  actions and proceedings, the Rockland county  highway  department,  upon
    34  application of the court, shall be substituted as a party.
    35    11.  Transfer of assets and liabilities. All assets and liabilities of
    36  the Rockland county  drainage  agency  are  hereby  transferred  to  and
    37  assumed by the Rockland county highway department.
    38    §  2.  Chapter  846 of the laws of 1975, relating to creating a stream
    39  control act for the county of  Rockland  and  defining  its  powers  and
    40  duties, is REPEALED.
    41    §  3. This act shall take effect one year after it shall have become a
    42  law.
