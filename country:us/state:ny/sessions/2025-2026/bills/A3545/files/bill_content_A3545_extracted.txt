SANTABARBARA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to creating a live kidney donor follow-up study; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public health law is amended by adding a new section 2 4311 to read as follows: 3 § 4311. New York living kidney donor follow-up study. 1. The depart- 4 ment shall contract with a qualified research team to conduct a prospec- 5 tive study of living kidney donor health. Such study shall follow people 6 who donate a kidney in New York between January first, two thousand 7 twenty-six and December thirty-first, two thousand thirty for ten years 8 whose transplant surgery takes place. All donors who agree to provide 9 their follow-up data for use in such study will receive a stipend of one 10 thousand dollars for each year of follow-up they fully participate in. 11 The commissioner shall submit a report including recommendations and 12 findings based on this study to the governor, the temporary president of 13 the senate, the speaker of the assembly and the chairs of the senate and 14 assembly standing committees on health no later than December thirty- 15 first, two thousand twenty-six and each December thirty-first thereaft- 16 er. 17 2. For the first five years post donation, kidney donors shall each be 18 entitled to up to two thousand dollars to pay copayments and other 19 health-related expenses. 20 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall expire and be 21 deemed repealed January 1, 2041.
