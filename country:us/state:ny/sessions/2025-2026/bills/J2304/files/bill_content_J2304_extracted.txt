Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J02304 Summary:BILL NOJ02304  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORTEDISCO COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ02304 Text: Senate Resolution No. 2304 BY: Senator TEDISCO COMMENDING St. George Greek Orthodox Church upon the occasion of hosting its 50th Annual Greek Festival taking place September 4-6, 2026 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and pay tribute to those municipalities that partner with their local business communities while recognizing celebrations which foster ethnic pride and enhance the profile of cultural diversity which strengthens the fabric of the communities of New York State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend St. George Greek Orthodox Church, in the City of Schenectady, upon the occasion of 50th Annual Greek Festival taking place September 4-6, 2026, on Liberty Street in Schenectady, New York ; and WHEREAS, Parish priest, Rev. Father Neofitos Sarigiannis has described the event as bringing "a touch of Greece to Schenectady" in order to create a homelike feel for all members of the local community who attend; and WHEREAS, For many decades, the State of New York has held the distinction of having the highest population of Greek Americans in the United States; and WHEREAS, Greek food, entertainment, and musical traditions are long withstanding and developed throughout the many diverse regions of the nation, such as Attica, Peloponnese, Crete, and ten others; and WHEREAS, Established in 1976, the Annual St. George Orthodox Church Greek Festival is held each year around Labor Day, and with the help of the St. George's parish members and local affiliations, has been a staple in the Schenectady community ever since; and WHEREAS, The St. George Orthodox Church Greek Festival allows for community members to take tours of the church and its attractions, provides shops where individuals can purchase goods, tables where children can perform arts and crafts projects, and stands featuring homemade authentic Greek foods such as Moussaka, Olympian Kotta, Lamb Shanks Yiovetsi, and Stuffed Peppers; and WHEREAS, The city closes off Liberty Street for the three-day period of the festival so residents can enjoy authentic Greek family fun, with a full music Lineup, other forms of entertainment, and numerous additional children's activities; and WHEREAS, This auspicious day provides an opportunity to celebrate the significant contributions of Greek Americans so that all people may know and appreciate Greek history, traditions, and of the role Greek Americans have played in Schenectady, and will continue to play, in our communities, State and Nation; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend St. George Orthodox Church upon the occasion of hosting its 50th Annual Greek Festival taking place September 4-6, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of St. George Orthodox Church in the City of Schenectady.
