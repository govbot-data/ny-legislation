SUTTON -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to providing for the financing and construction of capital facilities for Yeshiva Ohr Yoseph The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (b) of subdivision 2 of section 1676 of the 2 public authorities law is amended by adding a new undesignated paragraph 3 to read as follows: 4 Yeshiva Ohr Yoseph, an independent, not-for-profit private, accredited 5 college/school of higher learning, that is exempt from federal income 6 tax as an organization described in § 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue 7 Code for the financing, acquisition and equipping of a building to be 8 located at 1128 36th Street, Kings County, New York to be used as a 9 school. 10 § 2. Subdivision 1 of section 1680 of the public authorities law is 11 amended by adding a new undesignated paragraph to read as follows: 12 Yeshiva Ohr Yoseph, an independent, not-for-profit private, accredited 13 college/school of higher learning, that is exempt from federal income 14 tax as an organization described in § 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue 15 Code for the financing, acquisition and equipping of a building to be 16 located at 1128 36th Street, Kings County, New York to be used as a 17 school. 18 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
