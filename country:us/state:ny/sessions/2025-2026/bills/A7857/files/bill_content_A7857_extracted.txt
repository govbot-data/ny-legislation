WALKER -- read once and referred to the Committee on Election Law AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to polling places in detention facilities and the voting rights and status of incarcerated persons; and to amend the correction law, in relation to persons who may visit correctional facilities The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Legislative intent. The intention of this bill is to bring 2 vote method equity to New York's detention facilities. Thousands of New 3 Yorkers that are otherwise eligible to vote are held in facilities where 4 they do not have access to a polling place.
