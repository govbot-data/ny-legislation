AN ACT to amend the private housing finance law, in relation to the
amount of taxes paid by a cooperative
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 1 of section 125 of the private housing finance
2 law is amended by adding a new paragraph (a-6) to read as follows:
3 (a-6) In a city having a population of one million or more, the amount
4 of real estate taxes to be paid by a cooperative shall be the lesser of
5 (i) five per centum of the annual shelter rent or carrying charges of
6 such rehabilitation project or (ii) such amount as is set forth in a
7 regulatory agreement to which the cooperative is a party.
8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
