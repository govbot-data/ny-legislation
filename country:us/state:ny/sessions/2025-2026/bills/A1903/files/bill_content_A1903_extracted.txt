The section heading and subdivisions 1, 2, 3 and 4 of 2 section 167 of the labor law, as amended by chapter 815 of the laws of 3 2022, paragraph (c) of subdivision 3 as amended by chapter 27 of the 4 laws of 2023, are amended to read as follows: 5 Restrictions on consecutive hours of work for nurses and health care 6 workers. 1. When used in this section: 7 a. "Nurse" shall mean a registered professional nurse or a licensed 20 practical nurse as defined by article one hundred thirty-nine of the 21 education law who provides direct patient care. 22 c.
