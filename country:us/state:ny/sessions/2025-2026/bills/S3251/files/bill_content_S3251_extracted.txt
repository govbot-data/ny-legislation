Subdivision (f) of section 2101 of the vehicle and traffic 2 law, as added by chapter 1134 of the laws of 1971, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 (f) To "mail" means to deposit in the United States mail properly 5 addressed and with postage prepaid, or electronically transmitted. 6 § 2.
