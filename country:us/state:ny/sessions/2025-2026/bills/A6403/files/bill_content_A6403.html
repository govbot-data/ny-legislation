Requires license plates for certain bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters; requires a person be eighteen years of age to operate or purchase bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters.

                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                      March 4, 2025

        Introduced  by M. of A. JACKSON -- read once and referred to the Commit-
          tee on Transportation
 
        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in  relation  to  requiring
          license  plates for certain bicycles with electric assist and electric
          scooters and requiring a person be eighteen years of age to operate or
          purchase bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1. The article heading of article 34 of the vehicle and traf-
     2  fic law, as amended by chapter 694 of the laws of 1995,  is  amended  to
     3  read as follows:
     4            REGISTRATION AND OPERATION OF BICYCLES AND PLAY DEVICES
     5    § 2. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding two new sections
     6  1244 and 1245 to read as follows:
     7    §  1244.  Registration  of  certain  bicycles with electric assist. 1.
     8  Every bicycle with electric assist which has an electric motor that does
     9  not cease to provide assistance when such bicycle or scooter  reaches  a
    10  speed  of  forty miles per hour shall be registered and issued a license
    11  plate by the department. No person shall operate  such  a  bicycle  with
    12  electric assist on the public highways unless such bicycle with electric
    13  assist shall have a distinctive number assigned to it by the commission-
    14  er  and  a  number plate issued by the commissioner with a number corre-
    15  sponding to that of the certificate of registration.
    16    2. Such license plates shall be of such  material,  form,  design  and
    17  dimensions  and contain or set forth such distinguishing number or other
    18  identification marks as  the  commissioner  shall  prescribe,  provided,
    19  however,  that there shall be at all times a marked contrast between the
    20  color of the number plates and that of the numerals or letters  thereon,
    21  and  provided  further  that  no such bicycle with electric assist shall
    22  display the number plates of more than one state at a  time,  nor  shall
    23  any plate be used other than those issued by the commissioner.

     1    3.  Each  such  license plate shall identify whether such bicycle with
     2  electric assist is personal or commercial in nature.
     3    4.  Each  such  license  plate shall be conspicuously displayed on the
     4  rear of the bicycle with electric assist, securely  fastened  so  as  to
     5  prevent the same from swinging.
     6    5.  The  fee for such license plates shall be a nominal fee determined
     7  by the commissioner.
     8    § 1245. Sale of bicycles with electric assist. 1. No person shall sell
     9  a bicycle with electric assist to a person less than eighteen  years  of
    10  age.
    11    2.  No  person  shall  accept  as  written evidence of age by any such
    12  person for the purchase of a bicycle with electric assist  any  document
    13  other  than:  (a)  a valid driver's license or non-driver identification
    14  card issued by the commissioner of motor vehicles, the  federal  govern-
    15  ment,  any  United  States  territory,  commonwealth  or possession, the
    16  District of Columbia, a state government within the United States  or  a
    17  provincial government of the dominion of Canada; or (b) a valid passport
    18  issued  by  the United States government or any other country; or (c) an
    19  identification card issued by the armed forces of the United States.
    20    3. Upon the sale of a bicycle with electric assist, the  seller  shall
    21  submit  to  the  commissioner,  in  a form and manner as provided for by
    22  regulations promulgated by the  commissioner,  a  copy  of  the  written
    23  evidence of age provided by the purchaser.
    24    §  3. Subdivision 2 of section 1242 of the vehicle and traffic law, as
    25  added by section 8 of part XX of chapter 58 of  the  laws  of  2020,  is
    26  amended to read as follows:
    27    2. No person less than [sixteen] eighteen years of age shall operate a
    28  bicycle  with  electric assist. The failure of any person to comply with
    29  the provisions of this subdivision  shall  not  constitute  contributory
    30  negligence or assumption of risk, and shall not in any way bar, preclude
    31  or  foreclose  an  action for personal injury or wrongful death by or on
    32  behalf of such person, nor in any way diminish  or  reduce  the  damages
    33  recoverable in any such action.
    34    §  4.  The  article heading of article 34-D of the vehicle and traffic
    35  law, as added by section 10 of part XX of chapter  58  of  the  laws  of
    36  2020, is amended to read as follows:
    37                REGISTRATION AND OPERATION OF ELECTRIC SCOOTERS
    38    § 5. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding two new sections
    39  1290 and 1291 to read as follows:
    40    §  1290.  Registration of certain electric scooters. 1. Every electric
    41  scooter which has an electric motor  that  does  not  cease  to  provide
    42  assistance  when  such bicycle or scooter reaches a speed of forty miles
    43  per hour shall be registered and issued a license plate by  the  depart-
    44  ment.  No  person  shall  operate such an electric scooter on the public
    45  highways unless such electric scooter shall have  a  distinctive  number
    46  assigned  to  it  by  the  commissioner and a number plate issued by the
    47  commissioner with a number corresponding to that of the  certificate  of
    48  registration.
    49    2.  Such  license  plates  shall be of such material, form, design and
    50  dimensions and contain or set forth such distinguishing number or  other
    51  identification  marks  as  the  commissioner  shall prescribe, provided,
    52  however, that there shall be at all times a marked contrast between  the
    53  color  of the number plates and that of the numerals or letters thereon,
    54  and provided further that no such electric  scooter  shall  display  the
    55  number  plates  of more than one state at a time, nor shall any plate be
    56  used other than those issued by the commissioner.
