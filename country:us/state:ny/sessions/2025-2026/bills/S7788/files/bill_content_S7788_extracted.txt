MAYER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to establishing reserve funds for costs relating to special education The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 3-a of section 3651 of the education law, as 2 added by chapter 588 of the laws of 1988, is amended and a new subdivi- 3 sion 1-c is added to read as follows: 4 1-c. Notwithstanding subdivision one of this section, any school 5 district may establish a reserve fund for payment of unanticipated costs 6 related to providing special education to students with disabilities, 7 provided that the amount in such fund shall not exceed four percent of 8 the district's current school year budget. Special education and 9 students with disabilities shall have the same meaning as in subdivision 10 one of section four thousand four hundred one of this chapter. Such 11 reserve fund shall be established only by a majority vote of the quali- 12 fied voters of the district. 13 3-a. Notwithstanding the provisions of subdivision three of this 14 section, any school district which establishes a reserve fund in accord- 15 ance with subdivision one-a or one-c of this section may make expendi- 16 tures from such fund for the purposes specified in such subdivision 17 without authorization of the voters. 18 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of July next succeeding 19 the date on which it shall have become a law.
