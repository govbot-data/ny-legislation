It is hereby 4 found that many of our citizens lack the opportunity to view, enjoy or 5 participate in living cultural and theatrical performances, musical 6 concerts, operas, dance and ballet recitals, art exhibits, cultural 7 exhibits, examples of fine architecture, and the performing and fine 8 arts generally, and that such lack is exacerbated by racial and social 9 inequities.
