        Introduced  by M. of A. LUPARDO -- read once and referred to the Commit-

        AN ACT to amend the agriculture and markets law, the public  health  law
          and the alcoholic beverage control law, in relation to the manufacture
          and sale of slushies made with liquor, wine, beer or cider

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivisions 14 and 15 of section 200  of  the  agriculture
     2  and  markets  law,  as  amended  by chapter 132 of the laws of 2020, are
     3  amended to read as follows:
     4    14. If it is ice cream or other frozen desserts,  including  slushies,
     5  made  with  liquor,  wine, beer or cider bearing or containing more than
     6  five percent of alcohol by volume.
     7    15. With respect to the manufacture and sale of  ice  cream  or  other
     8  frozen  desserts,  including  slushies,  made with liquor, wine, beer or
     9  cider bearing or containing more than one-half of one percent,  but  not
    10  more than five percent of alcohol by volume:
    11    (a)  No  person  shall  sell  at retail packages of ice cream or other
    12  frozen desserts, including slushies, made with  liquor,  wine,  beer  or
    13  cider for consumption off the premises where sold unless:
    14    (i) each package is a sealed package, as received from the manufactur-
    15  er or distributor;
    16    (ii)  each  sealed  package,  as  received  from  the  manufacturer or
    17  distributor, in a format to be established or approved  by  the  commis-
    18  sioner,  prominently  bears the following statements, provided that such
    19  statements may refer to either liquor, wine, beer or cider or a combina-
    20  tion thereof:
    21    (A) The sale of this product to individuals under the age  of  twenty-
    22  one years is prohibited.
    23    (B) This product is made with (insert liquor, wine, beer and/or cider)
    24  and contains alcohol up to five percent by volume.

    54    § 2. Section 1352-c of the public health law, as  amended  by  chapter
    55  132 of the laws of 2020, is amended to read as follows:

        A. 9368                             3
 
     1    §  1352-c.  Ice cream or other frozen desserts made with liquor, wine,
     2  beer or cider. No person shall sell at retail individual servings of ice
     3  cream or other frozen desserts, including slushies,  made  with  liquor,
     4  wine,  beer  or  cider  bearing  or containing more than one-half of one
     5  percent  but not more than five percent of alcohol by volume, unless the
     6  following statements are prominently displayed on the printed menu  (or,
     7  if  no  printed  menus are used, on the menu board or sign setting forth
     8  the bill of fare) immediately adjacent to the listing  of  the  item  or
     9  items of ice cream or other frozen desserts made with liquor, wine, beer
    10  or  cider  being  offered  provided  that  such  statements may refer to
    11  liquor, wine, beer or cider or a combination thereof:
    12    1. The sale of (insert liquor, wine, beer and/or cider) ice  cream  or
    13  other  frozen  desserts  to  individuals  under the age of twenty-one is
    14  prohibited.
    15    2. (Insert liquor, wine, beer and/or cider) ice cream or other  frozen
    16  desserts contain alcohol up to five percent by volume.
    17    3. Notice: (Insert liquor, wine, beer and/or cider) ice cream or other
    18  frozen  desserts  contain  alcohol  used as a flavoring and, as with any
    19  product that contains alcohol:
    20    (a) [women] persons who are pregnant should not consume alcohol during
    21  pregnancy because of the risk of birth defects, and
    22    (b) consumption of alcohol impairs your ability  to  drive  a  car  or
    23  operate machinery, and may cause health problems.
    24    § 3. Subdivision 1 of section 3 of the alcoholic beverage control law,
    25  as  amended  by  chapter  132 of the laws of 2020, is amended to read as
    26  follows:
    27    1. "Alcoholic beverage" or "beverage" mean and include alcohol,  spir-
    28  its,  liquor, wine, beer, cider and every liquid, solid, powder or crys-
    29  tal, patented or not, containing alcohol,  spirits,  wine  or  beer  and
    30  capable  of  being consumed by a human being, and any warehouse receipt,
    31  certificate, contract or other document pertaining thereto; except  that
    32  confectionery  containing  alcohol  as provided by subdivision twelve of
    33  section two hundred of the agriculture and markets law and ice cream and
    34  other frozen desserts, including slushies, made with liquor, wine,  beer
    35  or  cider  as  provided in subdivision fifteen of section two hundred of
    36  the agriculture and markets law shall not be regulated as an  "alcoholic
    37  beverage"  or  "beverage"  within  the meaning of this section where the
    38  sale, delivery or giving away is to a person aged  twenty-one  years  or
    39  older.  The  sale,  delivery or giving away of ice cream or other frozen
    40  desserts, including slushies, made with liquor, wine, beer or cider to a
    41  person under the age of twenty-one years may be  prosecuted  administra-
    42  tively and/or criminally in accordance with the provisions of this chap-
    43  ter.
    44    § 4. This act shall take effect immediately.
