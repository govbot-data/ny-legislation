DINOWITZ -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring memorization of multiplication tables as part of the basic education program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 819 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 819. Instruction relating to multiplication tables. The standard 4 course of study shall include the requirement that students enrolled in 5 public schools memorize multiplication tables to demonstrate competency 6 in efficiently multiplying numbers. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall apply to school 8 years commencing on the first of July next succeeding the date on which 9 it shall have become a law.
