ZELLNER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Elections AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to enabling constitution- ally-qualified electors to register to vote and to cast a ballot on the same day at a polling location The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 3 of section 8-302 of the election law is 2 amended by adding four new paragraphs (f), (g), (h), and (i) to read as 3 follows: 4 (f) "Conditional voter registration" means a properly executed affida- 5 vit in the form described in paragraph (e) of this subdivision at an 6 early voting or election day polling location and which may be deemed 7 effective after the board of elections processes the affidavit, deter- 8 mines the registrant's eligibility to register, and validates the regis- 9 trant's information, as specified in paragraph (h) of this subdivision. 10 (g) In addition to other methods of voter registration provided under 11 this chapter, an elector who is otherwise qualified to register to vote 12 and to cast a ballot under sections one and three of article two of the 13 New York state constitution may complete a conditional voter registra- 14 tion and cast an affidavit ballot at an early voting or election day 15 polling location at any time permissible under section five of article 16 two of the New York state constitution, other restrictions on the time 17 for registration in this chapter notwithstanding. 18 (h) (1) A conditional voter registration shall be deemed effective if 19 the board of elections is able to determine before or during the canvass 20 period for the election that the registrant is eligible to register to 21 vote and that the information provided by the registrant on the regis- 22 tration affidavit matches information contained in a database maintained 23 by the department of motor vehicles or the federal social security 24 administration. 25 (2) If the information provided by the registrant on the registration 26 affidavit cannot be verified based on information contained in a data- 27 base maintained by the department of motor vehicles or the federal EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
