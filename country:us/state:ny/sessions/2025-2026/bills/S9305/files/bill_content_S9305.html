S09305 Summary:

BILL NOS09305A
 
SAME ASSAME AS A10415-A
 
SPONSORMAY
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §92-a, Pub Serv L; amd Part H1 §4, Chap 62 of 2003
 
Restructures the New York telecommunications relay service center as a state-wide workforce; extends provisions of law authorizing the establishment of such workforce from June 30, 2027 until June 30, 2030.

        AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to restructuring the
          New York  telecommunications  relay  service  center  as  a  workforce
          employing  residents  of Onondaga county or adjoining counties; and to
          amend chapter 62 of the laws of 2003, amending the public service  law
          relating to establishing the New York telecommunications relay service
          center, in relation to extending certain provisions thereof
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 92-a of the public service law, as
     2  added by section 3 of part H1 of chapter 62 of  the  laws  of  2003,  is
     3  amended to read as follows:
     4    2.  The  commission  shall authorize the establishment of the New York
     5  telecommunications relay service [center] workforce.   In  developing  a
     6  request  for  proposals  to provide telecommunications relay service the
     7  commission shall include the following minimum provisions:
     8    (a) The [New York telecommunications relay  service  center  shall  be
     9  located  within  the municipality of Syracuse,] provider of telecommuni-
    10  cations relay service shall, in performing  services  under  a  contract
    11  awarded  pursuant to this subdivision, employ or engage a workforce, all
    12  of whom shall reside within Onondaga county or an adjoining county with-
    13  in the state of New York;
    14    (b) A minimum of eighty percent of all  calls  utilizing  telecommuni-
    15  cations  relay  service must be routed to and through the New York tele-
    16  communications relay service [center] workforce; and
    17    (c) A contract to provide telecommunications relay  service  shall  be
    18  renewable for up to five years, and the commission shall be empowered to
    19  promulgate  and adopt all regulations required to implement the terms of
    20  this subdivision.
