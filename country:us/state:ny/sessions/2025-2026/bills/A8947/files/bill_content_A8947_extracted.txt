Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as 2 the "youth & teen internet safety and social media literacy act". 3 § 2. Section 814 of the education law is REPEALED and a new section 4 814 is added to read as follows: 5 § 814. Courses of study in youth and teen internet safety and social 6 media literacy. 1. Any school district in the state may provide, to 7 pupils in grades kindergarten through twelve, instruction designed to 8 promote the proper and safe use of the internet, social media, and arti- 9 ficial intelligence. 10 2.
