and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Mary Ann Kubinski upon the occasion of her retirement after more than 50 years of distinguished service to the New York State Department of Labor on Wednesday, January 28, 2026; and WHEREAS, For over 50 years, Mary Ann Kubinski rendered faithful, conscientious, and valuable service to the New York State Department of Labor; and WHEREAS, In her capacities as an employment counselor and labor services representative, Mary Ann Kubinski served with loyalty, honor, and distinction; and WHEREAS, Mary Ann Kubinski entered the Department of Labor on May 4, 1970, and was first appointed to the position of employment counselor on September 9, 1975, serving in this role for 28 years before she initially retired in 2003; and WHEREAS, In 2006, Mary Ann Kubinski returned to the New York State Department of Labor as an hourly labor services representative, the esteemed role which she served valiantly for the past 20 years; and WHEREAS, In her official acts, Mary Ann Kubinski was governed by a keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and WHEREAS, With her throughout much of her career and full life had been her beloved husband, Ted, to whom she was proudly married for 50 years, as well as their son, Ted, and his wife, Tonya, all of whom feel privileged to be a part of her fruitful life and rejoice in her many achievements; and WHEREAS, During her tenure with the New York State Department of Labor, Mary Ann Kubinski earned the admiration, esteem, and affection of her colleagues; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Mary Ann Kubinski upon the occasion of her retirement after 50 years of distinguished service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Mary Ann Kubinski.
