Section 72-c of the general municipal law, as amended by 2 section 1 of subpart C of part C of chapter 97 of the laws of 2011, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 § 72-c. Expenses of members of the police department, the fire depart- 5 ment and other peace officers in attending police or firefighter train- 6 ing schools. The board or body of a county, city, town or village 7 authorized to appropriate and to raise money by taxation and to make 8 payments therefrom, is hereby authorized, in its discretion, to appro- 9 priate and to raise money by taxation and to make payments from such 10 moneys, for the annual expenses of the members of the police department 11 of such municipal corporation in attending a police training school, and 12 for the annual expenses of the members of the fire department of such 13 municipal corporation in attendance in a firefighter training school, as 14 provided by the regulations of the department, either within such munic- 15 ipal corporation or elsewhere within the state; and for the payment of 16 reasonable expenses of such members and other police officers [or], 17 peace officers or firefighters of the municipality while going to, 18 attending, and returning from any training school conducted by or under 19 the auspices of the federal bureau of investigation, or, in the case of 20 firefighters, under the auspices of the appropriate entity, whether 21 within or without the state.
