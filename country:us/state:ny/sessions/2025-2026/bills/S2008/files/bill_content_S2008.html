Amd §72-c, Gen Muni L
 
Authorizes municipalities to fund training of firefighters; allows a municipality to recoup the cost of sending a firefighter to training school from another municipality that hires that firefighter within three years of their training.

        AN ACT to amend the general municipal law, in relation to the  appropri-
          ation of funds for the training of firefighters
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 72-c of the general municipal law,  as  amended  by
     2  section  1  of subpart C of part C of chapter 97 of the laws of 2011, is
     3  amended to read as follows:
     4    § 72-c. Expenses of members of the police department, the fire depart-
     5  ment and other peace officers in attending police or firefighter  train-
     6  ing  schools.  The  board  or  body  of  a county, city, town or village
     7  authorized to appropriate and to raise money by  taxation  and  to  make
     8  payments  therefrom,  is hereby authorized, in its discretion, to appro-
     9  priate and to raise money by taxation and to  make  payments  from  such
    10  moneys,  for the annual expenses of the members of the police department
    11  of such municipal corporation in attending a police training school, and
    12  for the annual expenses of the members of the fire  department  of  such
    13  municipal corporation in attendance in a firefighter training school, as
    14  provided by the regulations of the department, either within such munic-
    15  ipal  corporation  or elsewhere within the state; and for the payment of
    16  reasonable expenses of such members  and  other  police  officers  [or],
    17  peace  officers  or  firefighters  of  the  municipality while going to,
    18  attending, and returning from any training school conducted by or  under
    19  the  auspices of the federal bureau of investigation, or, in the case of
    20  firefighters, under the auspices  of  the  appropriate  entity,  whether
    21  within  or without the state. Notwithstanding any inconsistent provision
    22  of any general, special or local law to the contrary, whenever a  member
    23  of  the  police  department  of  a  municipal corporation has attended a
    24  police training school, or a member of the fire department of a  munici-
    25  pal  corporation has attended a firefighter training school, the expense
