Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01501 Summary:BILL NOJ01501  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORGALLIVAN COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01501 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1501 BY: Senator GALLIVAN HONORING Noah Levan of Melody Magic upon the occasion of being recognized as the Clarence Chamber of Commerce's 2026 Emerging Entrepreneur WHEREAS, Individuals who give of their time and energies and serve the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and WHEREAS, Noah Levan has given not only of his time and energies but also of his competence, intelligence and leadership and consequently has been designated for special honor; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Noah Levan of Melody Magic upon the occasion of being recognized as the Clarence Chamber of Commerce's 2026 Emerging Entrepreneur at the 53rd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 19, 2026 at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, Through his company, Melody Magic, Noah Levan provides the community of Clarence the opportunity to realize their talents through piano lessons, DJ courses, art classes and publishing services; and WHEREAS, Noah Levan's vision of a creative sanctuary dedicated to the arts of storytelling, sound, and visual expression is brought to life in his business, Melody Magic, a multifaceted publishing house that occupies a unique niche where the written word meets the rhythmic beat and provides a home for aspiring creators of all ages and mediums; and WHEREAS, Melody Magic is named after the persistent magic that embodies Noah Levan's daughter, Melody, who carries this unique charm through her essence, creativity, and deep thoughtfulness for her surroundings; and WHEREAS, Aiming to engage with both children and adults in his hometown of Clarence, NY, Noah Levan is committed to giving back to the same community that influenced him at the former Clarence Youth Center and Clarence School District Arts Programs and the many role models and teachers who inspired him through art, music, poetry, and dance; and WHEREAS, Throughout the entire period of his community service, a period of constructive involvement, Noah Levan has stood constant in dignity, good grace and humor; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by an individual for the benefit of others which Noah Levan has displayed throughout his life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Noah Levan of Melody Magic upon the occasion of being recognized as the Clarence Chamber of Commerce's 2026 Emerging Entrepreneur; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Noah Levan.
