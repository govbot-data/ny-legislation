Notwithstand- 5 ing the provisions of any law to the contrary, if any authority shall 6 offer any electronic method of payment for tolls, fares, fees, rentals, 7 or other charges, including but not limited to a system called E-ZPass, 8 such authority shall not impose any periodic administrative or other 9 charge for the privilege of using such electronic method of payment for 10 such charges.
