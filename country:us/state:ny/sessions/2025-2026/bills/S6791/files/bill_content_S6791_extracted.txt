GRIFFO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to allowing snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles to be registered as farm vehicles The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 2222 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 15 to read as follows: 3 15. Registration of snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles to be used as 4 farm vehicles. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the 5 contrary, a snowmobile or all-terrain vehicle may be registered as a 6 motor vehicle in accordance with the provisions of subdivision thirteen 7 of section four hundred one of this chapter, where the owner of such 8 snowmobile or all-terrain vehicle has caused to be filed by mail or 9 otherwise, with the commissioner or with any agent of the commissioner, 10 an application for registration of such snowmobile or all-terrain vehi- 11 cle, addressed to the commissioner, and on a blank form to be furnished 12 by the commissioner for that purpose, containing the information 13 required by subdivision thirteen of section four hundred one of this 14 chapter. The commissioner or agent shall make such investigation, as the 15 commissioner or agent shall determine necessary, and if satisfied that 16 the snowmobile or all-terrain vehicle is to be operated exclusively as a 17 motor vehicle as provided by subdivision thirteen of section four 18 hundred one of this chapter shall, upon the payment of a fee of one 19 dollar, assign to such snowmobile or all-terrain vehicle a distinctive 20 number and issue and deliver to the applicant a set of number plates and 21 a certificate of registration in such form as the commissioner shall 22 prescribe, indicating the extent to which the snowmobile or all-terrain 23 vehicle registered may be operated on the public highways and such snow- 24 mobile or all-terrain vehicle may be operated only as so indicated. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. LBD02061-01-5 S. 6791 2 1 § 2. Notwithstanding its limited 4 use on highways, a snowmobile shall not be determined a motor vehicle 5 within the meaning of section one hundred twenty-five of this chapter or 6 any other successor statutes, except where a snowmobile is registered as 7 a farm vehicle pursuant to subdivision fifteen of section twenty-two 8 hundred twenty-two of this article. 9 § 3.
