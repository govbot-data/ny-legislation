Subdivision 3 of section 21-a of the workers' compensation 2 law, as amended by chapter 6 of the laws of 2007, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 3. An employer may cease making temporary payments of compensation and 5 prescribed medicine if such employer delivers within [five] sixteen days 6 after the last payment, to the injured employee and the board, a notice 7 of termination of temporary payments of compensation on a form 8 prescribed by the board. Such notice shall inform the injured employee 9 that the employer is ceasing temporary payment of compensation and 10 prescribed medicine. Upon the cessation of temporary payments of compen- 11 sation and prescribed medicine, all parties to any action pursuant to 12 this chapter shall retain all rights, defenses and obligations they 13 would otherwise have pursuant to this chapter without regard for the 14 temporary payment of compensation and prescribed medicine. 15 § 2.
