and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Bayla Lovens upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Port Jewish Center at its Annual Gala, "Fueling Our Future," on Saturday, June 14, 2025, at Harbor Links in Port Washington, New York; and WHEREAS, The Port Jewish Center is recognizing the dedicated leaders of its committees whose tireless efforts ensure the Center's spiritual, financial, and physical vitality; through their work, these committed congregants uphold the core tenets of Reform Judaism-pursuing justice, fostering community, caring for others, and repairing the world-thus safeguarding the Center's mission and lasting presence for generations to come; and WHEREAS, Bayla Lovens has been an integral member of the Port Jewish Center for over 37 years, and during that time, she has taken on a wide range of leadership roles, embodying the spirit of service and commitment to the community; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Bayla Lovens has served as Past President, Trustee, and lay leader of numerous Shabbat services, and now, as co-chair of the Religious School Committee and chair of the newly (re-)formed Worship Committee, she continues to make invaluable contributions to the Port Jewish Center; when the Center asks for help, Bayla responds without hesitation, offering her time, expertise, and passion to support the congregation's mission; and WHEREAS, As co-chair of the Religious School Committee, Bayla Lovens coordinates communication and collaboration between the rabbi, the religious school director, and parents, ensuring that the needs of religious school families are heard and met, while also strengthening the connection between these families and the senior members of the congregation, fostering unity across generations; and WHEREAS, Furthermore, as chair of the Worship Committee, Bayla Lovens convenes a group of congregants to work closely with the rabbi to provide feedback on the congregation's religious observances and holiday celebrations, while also generating ideas to engage a broader segment of the community in these practices; her leadership promotes a continuous improvement of religious programming, ensuring that it remains vibrant and meaningful to the entire congregation; and WHEREAS, Bayla Lovens' leadership, dedication, and passion for Port Jewish Center's mission have played a pivotal role in shaping the Center's religious programming and overall community engagement; through her tireless efforts, she ensures that the values of the Port Jewish Center are passed on to future generations; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the occurrence of such a dedicated, compassionate, and visionary individual, whose remarkable contributions over nearly four decades have left an indelible mark on the Port Jewish Center and its members; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Bayla Lovens upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Port Jewish Center, and to extend its sincere appreciation for her exceptional service and enduring contributions; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Bayla Lovens.
