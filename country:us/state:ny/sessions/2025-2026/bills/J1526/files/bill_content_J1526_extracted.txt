and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 77th Anniversary of the Clinton Figure Skating Club of Clinton, New York; and WHEREAS, The Clinton Figure Skating Club is a member of the United States Figure Skating Association and is one of the oldest figure skating clubs in the country; it also is one of the largest skating clubs on the East Coast; and WHEREAS, Since its founding in 1949, the Clinton Figure Skating Club has grown into one of the best skating clubs in New York State and its members feature some of the top figure skaters in the area; and WHEREAS, The Clinton Figure Skating Club is a volunteer organization comprised of many committees and operated largely by parents of young skaters and uses year-long fundraising efforts to provide ice rental, group coach payments, team competition costumes and entry fees, and all the means necessary to host its renowned annual ice show; and WHEREAS, A testament to Clinton Figure Skating Club's committed volunteers, supportive community, and dedication to the sport of hockey, the club was named the winner of the coveted national Kraft Hockeyville prize in 2018; and WHEREAS, In recognition of the club's storied past, the Clinton Historical Society will present a new exhibition titled "A Legacy on Ice: Clinton Figure Skating Club 1949-2025"; the new exhibition focuses on 75 years of the skating organization and was marked with a reception on Sunday, January 25, 2026, and remains open to the public through May 2026; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional organization, its numerous important contributions, and its vibrant role in improving the quality of life in its community, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the sparkling successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 77th Anniversary of the Clinton Figure Skating Club of Clinton, New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Clinton Figure Skating Club.
