MARTINEZ -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Higher Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring domestic violence coursework for requirements for certain professional licenses The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 7603 of the education law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 3-a to read as follows: 3 (3-a) Domestic violence coursework: The commissioner shall require 4 applicants for licensure as licensed psychologist to complete no less 5 than three credit hours of graduate-level course work related to domes- 6 tic violence. 7 § 2. Subdivision 2 of section 7607 of the education law, as added by 8 chapter 436 of the laws of 2018, is amended to read as follows: 9 2. During each triennial registration period, an applicant for regis- 10 tration as a psychologist shall complete a minimum of thirty-six hours 11 of acceptable learning activities, a minimum of three hours of which 12 shall be course work in the area of professional ethics, including the 13 laws, rules and regulations for practice in New York. The commissioner 14 shall require the completion of domestic violence-related continuing 15 education of not less than two hours as part of the mandatory continuing 16 education requirements for license renewal. Any psychologist whose first 17 registration date following the effective date of this section occurs 18 less than three years from such effective date, but on or after January 19 first, two thousand twenty-one, shall complete continuing education 20 hours on a prorated basis at the rate of one hour per month for the 21 period beginning January first, two thousand twenty-one up to the first 22 registration date thereafter. A psychologist who has not satisfied the 23 mandatory continuing education requirement shall not be issued a trien- 24 nial registration certificate by the department and shall not practice 25 unless and until a conditional registration is issued as provided for in 26 subdivision three of this section. Continuing education hours taken 27 during one triennium may not be transferred to the subsequent triennium.
