Subdivision 6 of section 401 of the vehicle and traffic 2 law is amended by adding a new paragraph b-2 to read as follows: 3 b-2. The provisions of this article with respect to the fees for a 4 gold star family license plate, shall be set at the same rate as 5 distinctive plates for gold star mothers issued under section four 6 hundred four-i of this title, and shall not incur any service charge. 7 § 2.
