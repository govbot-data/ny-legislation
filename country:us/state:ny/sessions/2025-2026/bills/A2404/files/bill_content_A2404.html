        Introduced  by M. of A. SMULLEN -- read once and referred to the Commit-
          tee on Local Governments
 
        AN ACT allowing for the creation of a joint police  department  for  the
          towns of Webb and Inlet
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. General. Notwithstanding the provisions of section 121-a of
     2  the general municipal law, or  any  law,  general  or  special,  to  the
     3  contrary, the town boards of the town of Webb, Herkimer County, and town
     4  of  Inlet,  Hamilton  County,  are hereby authorized, upon approval of a
     5  proposition duly submitted to the voters in such towns and upon a major-
     6  ity vote by the governing bodies of Herkimer and Hamilton  Counties,  to
     7  establish  a  joint  town  police department with jurisdiction over both
     8  towns pursuant to the provisions of this act.
     9    § 2. Town proposition and joint town laws. (a) The proposition  to  be
    10  submitted  to  the voters in such towns may be submitted at a general or
    11  special election of each town.
    12    (b) Upon the approval of a proposition by the electorate of each town,
    13  the town boards shall meet in joint session, at a time and place  to  be
    14  determined  by  agreement of such boards, and organize such joint police
    15  department and establish  rules  and  regulations  governing  the  same,
    16  including,  but  not  limited  to when such joint department shall begin
    17  operations. A copy of the resolution, referendum  vote,  and  all  local
    18  laws  relating  to  the  establishment  and  operation of a joint police
    19  department shall be provided to and filed with the town  clerks  of  the
    20  towns  of  Webb  and  Inlet; additionally, upon establishment of a joint
    21  department, notification  shall  be  provided  to  Hamilton  County  and
    22  Herkimer County, and the State of New York.
    23    (c)  Local  laws  establishing rules and regulations of a joint police
    24  department as authorized by this act shall, at a minimum:

     1    (i) Provide for the appointment of a chief of police  for  such  joint
     2  police department;
     3    (ii) Provide for funding, and, if necessary, apportionment between the
     4  towns,  required to operate the joint department through an annual joint
     5  resolution of the town boards;
     6    (iii) Provide for the titles  of  the  police  officers  and  civilian
     7  employees of the joint police department; and
     8    (iv)  Provide  for a means to coordinate all employment determinations
     9  for the joint department between the respective towns, counties and  any
    10  other entities necessary to the process.
    11    §  3. Property. The joint police department shall be empowered to hold
    12  title to property and shall take title to all real and personal property
    13  currently held by the towns of Webb and Inlet police departments.
    14    § 4. Obligations. The joint department shall be subject to all  appli-
    15  cable  contractual  obligations  entered into by the town of Webb police
    16  department and the town of Inlet police department.
    17    § 5. Discontinuing of joint police department. The town of Webb and/or
    18  the town of Inlet may  discontinue  their  participation  in  the  joint
    19  police  department  by means of a four-fifths vote of either of the town
    20  boards. In the event that either or both towns vote to  discontinue  the
    21  joint  police department, then the department shall be deemed dissolved,
    22  and the assets of the department shall be divided between  the  town  of
    23  Webb and the town of Inlet.
    24    § 6. This act shall take effect immediately.
