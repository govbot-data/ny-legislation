SMULLEN -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Local Governments AN ACT allowing for the creation of a joint police department for the towns of Webb and Inlet The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. General. Notwithstanding the provisions of section 121-a of 2 the general municipal law, or any law, general or special, to the 3 contrary, the town boards of the town of Webb, Herkimer County, and town 4 of Inlet, Hamilton County, are hereby authorized, upon approval of a 5 proposition duly submitted to the voters in such towns and upon a major- 6 ity vote by the governing bodies of Herkimer and Hamilton Counties, to 7 establish a joint town police department with jurisdiction over both 8 towns pursuant to the provisions of this act. 9 § 2. Town proposition and joint town laws. (a) The proposition to be 10 submitted to the voters in such towns may be submitted at a general or 11 special election of each town. 12 (b) Upon the approval of a proposition by the electorate of each town, 13 the town boards shall meet in joint session, at a time and place to be 14 determined by agreement of such boards, and organize such joint police 15 department and establish rules and regulations governing the same, 16 including, but not limited to when such joint department shall begin 17 operations. A copy of the resolution, referendum vote, and all local 18 laws relating to the establishment and operation of a joint police 19 department shall be provided to and filed with the town clerks of the 20 towns of Webb and Inlet; additionally, upon establishment of a joint 21 department, notification shall be provided to Hamilton County and 22 Herkimer County, and the State of New York. 23 (c) Local laws establishing rules and regulations of a joint police 24 department as authorized by this act shall, at a minimum: EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. Property. The joint police department shall be empowered to hold 12 title to property and shall take title to all real and personal property 13 currently held by the towns of Webb and Inlet police departments. 14 § 4. Obligations. The joint department shall be subject to all appli- 15 cable contractual obligations entered into by the town of Webb police 16 department and the town of Inlet police department. 17 § 5. Discontinuing of joint police department. The town of Webb and/or 18 the town of Inlet may discontinue their participation in the joint 19 police department by means of a four-fifths vote of either of the town 20 boards. In the event that either or both towns vote to discontinue the 21 joint police department, then the department shall be deemed dissolved, 22 and the assets of the department shall be divided between the town of 23 Webb and the town of Inlet. 24 § 6. This act shall take effect immediately. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
