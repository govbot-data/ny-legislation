LAVINE -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the civil rights law, in relation to the display of religious items on dwellings The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The civil rights law is amended by adding a new section 2 79-r to read as follows: 3 § 79-r. Display of religious items on dwellings. 1. Except as other- 4 wise provided in this section, a property owner shall not enforce or 5 adopt a restrictive covenant or any other restriction that prohibits one 6 or more religious items from being displayed or affixed on any entry 7 door or entry doorframe of a dwelling. 8 2. To the extent permitted by the New York State Constitution and the 9 United States Constitution, this section shall not prohibit the enforce- 10 ment or adoption of a restrictive covenant or other restriction prohib- 11 iting the display or affixing of a religious item on any entry door or 12 entry doorframe to a dwelling that: 13 (a) threatens the public health or safety; 14 (b) hinders the opening or closing of any entry door; 15 (c) violates any federal, state, or local law; 16 (d) contains graphics, language or any display that is obscene or 17 otherwise illegal; or 18 (e) individually or in combination with any other religious item 19 displayed or affixed on any entry door or doorframe, has a total size 20 greater than thirty-six by twelve square inches, provided it does not 21 exceed the size of the door. 22 3. (a) No governing document of an association shall limit or prohibit 23 the display of one or more religious items on the entry door or entry 24 doorframe of a member's separate interest.
