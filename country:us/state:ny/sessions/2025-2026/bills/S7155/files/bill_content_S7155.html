S07155 Summary:

BILL NOS07155
 
SAME ASSAME AS A01879
 
SPONSORCHAN
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd V & T L, generally
 
Directs the issuance of special number and distinctive license plates for military service members and members of veterans organizations without payment of service charges or renewal fees.

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to the issuance
          of special number plates
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivision 1-a of section 404 of the vehicle and traffic
     2  law, as amended by chapter 105 of the laws of 2017, is amended  to  read
     3  as follows:
     4    1-a.  The  commissioner  may  issue  special number and/or distinctive
     5  license plates to applicants who are members of  veterans  organizations
     6  or  active  duty  military  members  in  the same manner as other number
     7  plates are issued pursuant to this article. Such special  number  plates
     8  or  distinctive  plates shall be issued only upon (a) payment of [a one-
     9  time service charge of ten dollars in addition to] the regular registra-
    10  tion fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article, and (b)
    11  submission of proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that  the  appli-
    12  cant  is  presently  an  active  duty member or a member of the veterans
    13  organization for which such plate is requested. No additional  fees  for
    14  customization of such plates shall be required. Such membership shall be
    15  verified annually by the applicant. Application for such plates shall be
    16  made in accordance with regulations promulgated by the commissioner with
    17  respect  to the issuance of such plates. Provided, however, that nothing
    18  contained herein shall be deemed to supersede the provisions of  section
    19  four  hundred  four-d,  four  hundred  four-e, four hundred four-h, four
    20  hundred four-i, four hundred four-j, four hundred four-k,  four  hundred
    21  four-p, four hundred four-aa, or four hundred four-dd of this article.
    22    §  2.  Subdivision  6 of section 401 of the vehicle and traffic law is
    23  amended by adding a new paragraph c-1 to read as follows:
    24    c-1. The provisions of this subdivision with respect to the payment of
    25  registration renewal fees shall not apply to a motor  vehicle  registra-
