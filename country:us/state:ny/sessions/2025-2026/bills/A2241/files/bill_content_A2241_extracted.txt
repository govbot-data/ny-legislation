Subdivision (a) of section 1115 of the tax law is amended 2 by adding a new paragraph 47 to read as follows: 3 (47) Sales of any safe storage depository as defined in subdivision 4 three of section 265.45 of the penal law, firearm safety locks, trigger 5 locks or other items designed to ensure the safe handling and storage of 6 firearms. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
