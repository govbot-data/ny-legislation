        AN ACT to amend the insurance  law,  in  relation  to  authorizing  real
          estate value assurance insurance
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. Subsection (a) of section 1113  of  the  insurance  law  is
     2  amended by adding a new paragraph 36 to read as follows:
     3    (36)  "Real  estate value assurance insurance" means insurance against
     4  loss of real estate value specifically attributable to local rezoning or
     5  significant changes in land use within their neighborhood that  are  not
     6  related to broader economic downturns.

     8  ly  amended  by  chapters 369 and 569 of the laws of 2024, is amended to
