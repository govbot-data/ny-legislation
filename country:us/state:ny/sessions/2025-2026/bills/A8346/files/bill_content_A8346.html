A08346 Summary:

BILL NOA08346
 
SAME ASNo Same As
 
SPONSORBrown K
 
COSPNSR

Amd §3, Lien L
 
Expands mechanic's liens on real property to include certain legal work for attorneys and design work for engineers and architects which relates to permits and zoning approvals for such real property.
