The commissioner of transportation is authorized and 2 directed to conduct a study on the feasibility of removing all existing 3 3-cable guide rails on New York roadways and replacing such guide rails 4 with safer modern alternatives. Such study shall include, but not be 5 limited to: 6 a. The approximate location and mileage within the state where 3-cable 7 guide rails are still in use; 8 b. The estimated duration the removal would take if conducted in the 12 most cost-effective manner for both the state and affected localities; 13 and 14 d. The estimated impact on vehicle and motorist safety by removing and 15 replacing 3-cable guide rails with modern alternatives. 16 § 2.
