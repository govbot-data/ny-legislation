S03802 Summary:

BILL NOS03802

Amd Chap 15 of 1998
 
Relates to extensions of state and local deadlines following a state disaster emergency; adds school district to the list of municipality and district corporations; relates to the prerequisites, time and scope thereof.

        Introduced  by  Sen.  PARKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when

        AN ACT to amend chapter 15 of the laws of 1998 authorizing the extension
          of time for the collection of taxes, fines and payment of other moneys
          owed to government agencies and extending the deadline for  filing  of
          applications,  reports or other requirements to government agencies or
          programs as a result of state disaster emergencies, in relation to the
          prerequisites, time and scope thereof
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Chapter 15 of the laws of 1998 authorizing the extension of
     2  time for the collection of taxes, fines and payment of other moneys owed
     3  to government agencies and extending the deadline for filing of applica-
     4  tions,  reports or other requirements to government agencies or programs
     5  as a result of state disaster emergencies is amended to read as follows:
     6    Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the  contrary
     7  which  relates  to  the  collection  of real property taxes, fees, civil
     8  fines or other moneys owed to a municipal  or  district  corporation  or
     9  school district, to the imposition of penalties or interest therefor for
    10  a  late  payment,  or  to the filing of an application, report, or other
    11  document, form or other paper necessary for the processing of an  appli-
    12  cation  or other process to fulfill requirements to qualify for, or meet
    13  a condition of a government assistance program for  persons,  businesses
    14  or corporations, higher education loans, grants or financial assistance,
    15  licenses,  registrations or any other programs administered by a munici-
    16  pal or district corporation or school district, during a state  disaster
    17  emergency  as  defined  by section 20 of the executive law, the governor
    18  may, by executive order issued upon the request of the  chief  executive
    19  officer of a municipal or district corporation or school district in the
    20  affected  area,  extend the deadline for payment of any such moneys owed

    46  with  a  state  disaster  emergency  declared by the governor, the state
    47  shall provide aid to each affected municipal or district corporation  or
    48  school district, whichever is appropriate, to compensate it for the loss

    52  during the extension period or periods declared by the governor pursuant
    53  to section one of this act, which shall not exceed thirty days from  the
    54  date  that  the governor rescinded the executive order declaring a state
    55  disaster emergency, multiplied by an annualized  interest  rate  of  six
