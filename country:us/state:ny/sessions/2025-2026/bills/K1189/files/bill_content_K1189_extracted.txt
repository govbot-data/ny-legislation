and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the Broome County Regional Farmers Market; and WHEREAS, In 2016, the Broome County Regional Farmers Market was established through Cornell Cooperative Extension of Broome County as a regional hub for locally grown and produced food in the Greater Binghamton area; and WHEREAS, Now entering its 10th season, the Market connects residents with farmers, food-based businesses, and agricultural producers from across the region, strengthening local food systems and the regional agricultural economy; and WHEREAS, The Market participates in nutrition incentive programs, providing access to locally grown food for income-eligible shoppers throughout Broome County; many of the Market's food-based businesses are supported by Cornell Cooperative Extension's Incubator Kitchen which provides local food entrepreneurs with a licensed facility to produce the goods they contribute; and WHEREAS, Mindful of wastefulness, at the close of each market day, participating vendors donate unsold products to Binghamton Food Rescue, ensuring that fresh food reaches community members in need; and WHEREAS, Cornell Cooperative Extension of Broome County manages the Market's operations and connects this integral part of the community to broader food systems programming, including Grown in Broome and agricultural education initiatives; and WHEREAS, It is with immense pride that the hard-working and dedicated members of the Broome County Regional Farmers Market celebrate this milestone 10th Anniversary and anticipate continued success; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the Broome County Regional Farmers Market; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Broome County Regional Farmers Market.
