STECK -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the legislative law, in relation to the definition of outside earned income by members of the legislature The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph a of subdivision 2 of section 5-b of the legisla- 2 tive law, as amended by section 1 of part PP of chapter 55 of the laws 3 of 2025, is amended to read as follows: 4 a. For purposes of this section, the term "outside earned income" 5 shall mean wages, salaries, fees and other forms of compensation for 6 services actually rendered. The amount of income shall be determined by 7 taxable income. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
