AN ACT to authorize and direct the department of public service to
conduct a study on the deployment of energy interconnection processes
into the electrical grid
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Study. The department of public service is authorized and
2 directed to conduct a study on the regulatory barriers, if any, to the
3 rapid deployment of energy interconnection processes in the state that
4 shall include, but not be limited to:
5 (a) Potential changes to the interconnection process to rapidly bring
6 new renewable energy onto the electrical grid to meet the state's renew-
7 able energy goals set out under the climate leadership and community
8 protection act, as amended;
9 (b) The state's ability to comply with goals established in the
10 climate leadership and community protection act, as amended, with
11 current energy interconnection process timelines, including the effect
12 of recent reforms by the federally designated bulk system operator and
13 any additional anticipated reforms;
14 (c) The experience of other interconnection systems using "Connect and
15 Manage" or other alternative connection agreements in terms of the speed
16 of resource deployments and electrical grid reliability;
17 (d) The extent to which the studies and network upgrades currently
18 required for projects by the federally designated bulk system operator
19 go beyond the minimal set of studies and network upgrades needed to
20 safely operate the system with a new resource;

1 (e) Whether it is possible to identify more efficient system upgrades
2 in the context of New York's federally designated bulk system operator's
3 comprehensive system planning process rather than certain studies and
4 upgrades currently in the energy interconnection process;
5 (f) Improvements to the interconnection process for qualified energy
6 storage systems as defined by subdivision 1 of section 74 of the public
7 service law; and
8 (g) Whether any changes identified in this study at the transmission
9 level would also improve processes at the distribution level.
10 § 2. Report. No later than one year after the effective date of this
11 act, the department of public service shall submit to the governor, the
12 speaker of the assembly and the temporary president of the senate, a
13 report assessing its findings under the study conducted pursuant to
14 section one of this act, and any recommendations to rapidly bring new
15 renewable energy onto the electrical grid to meet the state's renewable
16 energy goals set out under the climate leadership and community
17 protection act, as amended.
18 § 3. Definitions. For the purposes of this act, the following terms
19 shall have the following meanings:
20 (a) "Energy interconnection process" means the process of integrating
21 new sources of renewable energy, as defined by section 66-p of the
22 public service law, into the electrical grid.
23 (b) "Electrical grid" means the network of electric plants and other
24 electric generating facilities and equipment, and electricity trans-
25 mission and distribution systems in the state.
26 (c) "Renewable energy" shall have the same meaning as defined by
27 section 66-p of the public service law.
28 § 4. This act shall take effect immediately.
