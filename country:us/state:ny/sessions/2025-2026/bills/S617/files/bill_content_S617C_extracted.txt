AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring
that all for-hire vehicles have New York state license plates in order
to receive a New York state safety inspection
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision (b) of section 301 of the vehicle and traffic
2 law is amended to read as follows:
3 (b) (1) The commissioner shall also require the inspection of any
4 motor vehicle duly registered under the laws of another state, a prov-
5 ince of Canada, a territory or a federal district to the extent to which
6 such state, province, territory or federal district requires the
7 inspection of motor vehicles duly registered under the laws of this
8 state; provided, however, that a motor vehicle used to carry passengers
9 for hire in for-hire vans and buses greater than nine passengers capaci-
10 ty have New York state issued license plates to receive a bi-annual New
11 York State DOT safety inspection for for-hire vehicle service under an
12 authorization issued by a municipality or the state shall not be
13 inspected in accordance with the provisions of this section unless such
14 motor vehicle used to carry passengers for hire in a for-hire vehicle is
15 duly registered under the laws of this state.
16 (2) For the purposes of this paragraph, the following terms shall have
17 the following meanings:

S. 617--C 2
1 (A) "for-hire vehicle" shall mean a motor vehicle carrying passengers
2 for hire in New York state, with a seating capacity of nine passengers
3 and above, not including the driver; and
4 (B) "seating capacity" shall include any plain view location which is
5 capable of accommodating a normal adult as part of an overall seat
6 configuration and design and is likely to be used as a seating position
7 while the vehicle is in motion.
8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
