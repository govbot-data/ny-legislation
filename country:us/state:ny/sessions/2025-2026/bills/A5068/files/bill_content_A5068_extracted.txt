SIMON, SAYEGH, REYES, TAYLOR, STERN, RAMOS, BENE- DETTO, MAGNARELLI, SEAWRIGHT, EACHUS -- read once and referred to the Committee on Election Law AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to restricting electio- neering on polling place property The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "vote in 2 peace act". 3 § 2. Subdivision 1 of section 8-104 of the election law, as amended by 4 chapter 232 of the laws of 2020, is amended to read as follows: 5 1. The American flag shall be kept displayed at each polling place 6 throughout the election. Facsimile ballots, voter information posting 7 and distance markers shall not be taken down, torn or defaced during the 8 election. While the polls are open no person shall do any electioneering 9 within the polling place, on the polling place property, or in any 10 public street, within a one hundred foot radial measured from the 11 entrances designated by the inspectors of election, to such polling 12 place or within such distance in any place in a public manner; and no 13 political banner, button, poster or placard shall be allowed in or upon 14 the polling place or within such one hundred foot radial. While the 15 polls are open no person shall consume any alcoholic beverages within 16 the polling place. While the polls are open no person shall make any 17 change, alteration or modification to any entrance to or exit from the 18 polling place unless such change, alteration or modification allows for 19 increased access for persons with disabilities or is necessary to main- 20 tain public safety due to the occurrence of an emergency. Any such 21 change, alteration or modification shall be clearly marked on signage 22 placed adjacent to the former entrance or exit. 23 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
