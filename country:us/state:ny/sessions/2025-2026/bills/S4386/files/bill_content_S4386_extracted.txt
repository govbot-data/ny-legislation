The executive law is amended by adding a new section 94-e 2 to read as follows: 3 § 94-e. Chief sustainability officer. 1. Purpose. The chief sustaina- 4 bility officer shall coordinate efforts across all agencies and other 5 state government entities to address climate change mitigation and 6 climate sustainability efforts in New York state. 7 2. Definitions. For the purposes of this section: 8 (a) "Officer" shall mean the chief sustainability officer appointed 9 pursuant to this section. 10 (b) "State agency" or "state agencies" shall mean any state depart- 11 ment, board, bureau, division, commission, committee, public authority, 12 public corporation, council, office or other governmental entity 13 performing a governmental or proprietary function of the state, except 14 the judiciary or the state legislature. 15 3.
