Paragraph (s) of subdivision 1 of section 1367 of the 2 racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, as amended by section 3 3 of part Y of chapter 59 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as 4 follows: 5 (s) "Prohibited sports event" means a [sport or athletic event in 6 which any New York college team participates regardless of where the 7 event takes place, or] high school sport or athletic event[. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 12 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
