Add §9, §1 of Chap 758 of 1992
 
Provides that the purposes, powers and authorized expenditures of the New York state archives partnership trust board may be exercised for the benefit of the New York state museum and the New York state library, in addition to the New York state archives.

        AN ACT to amend the New York state archives partnership  trust  act,  in
          relation  to authorizing the New York state archives partnership trust
          fund to be used for the benefit of the New York state museum  and  the
          New York state library

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 1 of chapter 758 of the laws of 1992,  constituting
     2  the  New York state archives partnership trust act, is amended by adding
     3  a new section 9 to read as follows:
     4    § 9. 1. Notwithstanding any provision of  law  to  the  contrary,  the
     5  purposes,   powers  and  authorized  expenditures  of  the  trust  board
     6  prescribed in this act may be exercised for the benefit of the New  York
     7  state museum and the New York state library, in addition to the New York
     8  state archives.
     9    2.  In  addition  to  the  powers  of the trust board as prescribed in
    10  section five of this act and expenditures authorized by section  six  of
    11  this  act,  the powers and authorized expenditures of the trust board as
    12  they relate to the New York state museum and the New York state  library
    13  shall include, but not be limited to:
    14    a. the acquisition, preservation, conservation, management, and acces-
    15  sibility  of  collections,  records,  objects,  artifacts, specimens and
    16  other materials of enduring historical, cultural, educational, scientif-
    17  ic or governmental value held by, or intended for, the  New  York  state
    18  museum or the New York state library;
    19    b.  the  support  of  research, exhibitions, interpretive initiatives,
    20  educational programming, publications, and public  programs  related  to
    21  the  collections, holdings, functions, and statutory missions of the New
    22  York state museum and the New York state library; and
    23    c. the  acceptance,  administration,  investment  and  expenditure  of
    24  gifts, grants, donations, bequests and other assistance, including those
    25  designated for specific purposes or projects.

     1    3.  The  director  of the New York state museum and the New York state
     2  librarian may, in collaboration with the director of the archives  part-
     3  nership trust and subject to the approval of the trust board, and pursu-
     4  ant  to a written agreement between the New York state archives partner-
     5  ship trust and such entities, carry out in their official capacities and
     6  consistent  with  the  provisions  of  this act, activities necessary to
     7  fulfill the purposes of the New York state  archives  partnership  trust
     8  fund  as they relate to the New York state museum and the New York state
     9  library, including but not limited to fundraising and  program  develop-
    10  ment.
    11    4.  Nothing  in  this section shall be construed to alter, diminish or
    12  transfer the statutory authority, governance or operational  control  of
    13  the New York state museum or the New York state library, or the authori-
    14  ty  of the board of regents or the commissioner of education, including,
    15  but not limited to, authority over personnel, appointments, and  employ-
    16  ment decisions, which shall remain with such entities.
    17    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
