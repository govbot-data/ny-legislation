Subdivision 3 of section 816 of the executive law is renum- 2 bered subdivision 4 and a new subdivision 3 is added to read as follows: 3 3. Any lands or buildings located within the forest preserve that are 4 listed in the national register of historic places or eligible for 5 inclusion in the national register of historic places shall be consid- 6 ered for an historical classification by the agency, in consultation 7 with the department of environmental conservation, in developing unit 8 management plans. 9 § 2.
