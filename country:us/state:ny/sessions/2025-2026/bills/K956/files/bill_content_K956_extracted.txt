this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Fillmore Central School Boys Soccer Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHSAA Class D Championship on November 16, 2025; and WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's colors of green and white, family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Eagles throughout their journey as they ended their season with an impressive overall record; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as soccer, which demands athletic prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Jarrett Vosburg and his expert coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and WHEREAS, Coach Jarrett Vosburg and all of the outstanding athletes on the Fillmore Central School Boys Soccer Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Fillmore Central School Boys Soccer Team: Avery Potter, James Hillman, Isaiah Sisson, Ezra Knapp, Connor McCumiskey, Brayden Walton, Levi Russell, Judah Russell, Jared Boon, Carson Sanasith, Braylon Hatch, Mason Findlay, Sam Cool, Byron France, Liam McCumiskey, Carsyn Ross, Isaac Wilcox, Jayce Ashton, Tyler Voss, and Zach Fraser; Head Coach Jarrett Vosburg; Assistant Coaches Mike Witkowski, James Mullen, Jordan Mullen, and Ezra Thomas; Athletic Director Jon Beardsley; Bookkeeper Darice Mullen; and Ball People Aaron Romance and Barrett Bowers; on their outstanding season and overall team record; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Fillmore Central School Boys Soccer Team and the dedicated coaching and athletic staff.
