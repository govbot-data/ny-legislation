Section 19.09 of the mental hygiene law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision (m) to read as follows: 3 (m)(1) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the 4 commissioner shall require all certified substance use disorder treat- 5 ment-related programs approved by the office to provide program partic- 6 ipants with a mental health evaluation conducted by a licensed profes- 7 sional whose scope of practice includes the diagnosis of mental health 8 disorders who shall, if appropriate, refer the participant for outpa- 9 tient mental health treatment.
