        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to  prohibiting
          parking, standing, or stopping within ten feet of a bus stop
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subparagraph l of paragraph 1 of subdivision (a) of section
     2  1202 of the vehicle and traffic law, as amended by chapter  206  of  the
     3  laws  of  2020,  is amended and a new subparagraph m is added to read as
     4  follows:
     5    l. In a space designated as provided  in  this  subparagraph  for  the
     6  exclusive purpose of charging an electric vehicle at an electric vehicle
     7  charging  station,  unless such vehicle is an electric vehicle connected
     8  to such charging station. Provided,  however,  that  an  electric  motor
     9  vehicle  disconnected  from the electric vehicle charging station for no
    10  more than thirty continuous minutes while stopped, standing or parked in
    11  such designated space shall not be in violation  of  this  subparagraph.
    12  Parking spaces designated for the exclusive purpose of charging an elec-
    13  tric  vehicle  at  an electric vehicle charging station shall be clearly
    14  identified, and such designation shall include  conspicuous  and  perma-
    15  nently  installed  above  grade signs which shall be positioned from the
    16  parking space surface at a height of not less than five  feet  nor  more
    17  than  seven  feet  and  which identify such space as an electric vehicle
    18  charging station, indicate that such space is only for electric  vehicle
    19  charging  and  include any day or time restrictions. For the purposes of
    20  this subparagraph, the term "electric vehicle" shall mean a motor  vehi-
    21  cle  that is propelled at least in part by an electric motor and associ-
    22  ated power electronics which provide acceleration torque  to  the  drive
    23  wheels  sometime  during  normal vehicle operation, and that draws elec-
    24  tricity from a battery or hydrogen fuel cell that is  capable  of  being
    25  recharged from an external source of electricity, such that the external
