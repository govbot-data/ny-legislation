AN ACT to amend the general business law and the executive law, in
relation to school security guards
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Paragraph d of subdivision 1 of section 89-n of the general
2 business law, as added by chapter 336 of the laws of 1992, is amended to
3 read as follows:
4 d. an eight hour annual in-service training course, provided that
5 where a security guard is employed or contracted by a school in this
6 state, such annual in-service training course shall require the
7 completion of school-specific training, including, but not limited to,
8 role and responsibility of school security guards, school-related laws
9 and liability, security awareness in the educational environment, youth
10 mediation/conflict resolution, disasters and emergencies, dynamics of
11 student behavior and cultural awareness, restorative practices, crisis
12 prevention, and the Dignity for All Students Act (DASA). Such school-
13 specific training shall be designed and enforced by the department, in
14 consultation with the department of education and the department of
15 criminal justice services, the division of homeland security, state
16 police and local law enforcement and shall be standardized and approved
17 statewide; and

19 section 6 of part Q of chapter 56 of the laws of 2009, is amended to
