Gray COMMEMORATING the 100th Anniversary of the Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proud to commemorate the 100th Anniversary of the Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue and to honor its past and present firefighting members for 100 years of fire protection to the citizens of Rensselaer Falls, New York, and its surrounding communities; and WHEREAS, Fire departments are an integral part of community protection services in every locality of our State; during the past 100 years, the courageous and devoted firefighters of the Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue have valiantly responded to all types of fire emergencies and have won the praise and respect of the community which they serve; and WHEREAS, The Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue, located in the Village of Rensselaer Falls, New York, proudly celebrates its 100th year of unwavering dedication and service to the community; and WHEREAS, For a century, the brave men and women of the Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue have stood as a beacon of courage, selflessness, and commitment, responding to emergencies, protecting lives, and safeguarding property; and WHEREAS, This esteemed department has consistently demonstrated professionalism, expertise, and a profound sense of civic duty, evolving with the times to provide essential fire suppression, rescue, and emergency medical services; and WHEREAS, The volunteers and members of the Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue have, for generations, contributed countless hours of training, sacrifice, and vigilance, often putting themselves in harm's way to ensure the safety and well-being of their neighbors; and WHEREAS, Their enduring legacy of community service, unwavering spirit, and dedication to public safety continues to inspire and strengthen the fabric of the Village of Rensselaer Falls; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body cannot express sufficient gratitude to those devoted individuals who recognize that the preservation of life and property is a sacred responsibility and who make that responsibility their own by serving as firefighters; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that we should give special recognition to those who work so assiduously for the betterment of their communities, and acknowledge publicly the heroic good works performed by the volunteer fire departments of this great Empire State, their officers and auxiliaries; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend and congratulate Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue upon the occasion of its 100th Anniversary, and to extend sincere appreciation for a century of dedicated service, heroic commitment, and enduring contributions to the safety and well-being of the community it so faithfully serves; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Rensselaer Falls Fire & Rescue.
