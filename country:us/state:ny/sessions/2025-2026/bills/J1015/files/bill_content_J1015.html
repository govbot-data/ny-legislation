Senate Resolution No. 1015
 
BY: Senator MAYER
 
        COMMENDING  the  Tuckahoe-Eastchester  Lions  Club
        upon the occasion of its 100th Anniversary  on  June
        25, 2025
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend  those  outstanding  organizations  whose  purposeful  endeavors
enhance  the  quality of life in their communities and beyond, and whose
commitment to service embodies the highest standards of excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the Tuckahoe-Eastchester Lions Club upon  the  occasion  of  its
100th  Anniversary  on  Wednesday,  June  25,  2025, to be celebrated on
Sunday, June 1, 2025 at Toscana on Main in Eastchester, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Tuckahoe-Eastchester Lions Club is part of Lions  Clubs
International,  one  of  the largest service organizations in the world,
and is deeply committed to meeting the unique needs of  the  communities
it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Founded  on  June  25,  1925,  by  a  group of citizens of
Tuckahoe, Eastchester, and Bronxville,  the  Tuckahoe-Eastchester  Lions
Club  has  a long and rich history of community service and philanthropy
in these founding communities and the surrounding area; and
 
  WHEREAS, Since its foundation, the Tuckahoe-Eastchester  Lions  Club
boasts  a  clear  mission to aid the blind and visually impaired, assist
youth, promote education, and fulfill community needs; these  goals  are
met  along  with putting into action the larger Lions Club International
organization's mission of fostering  a  spirit  of  understanding  among
peoples  of  the  world  and  encouraging  civic,  cultural,  and social
engagement within communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Tuckahoe-Eastchester Lions Club has  been  involved  in
various  community  projects  throughout its history, including donating
braille  books  to  local  libraries,  hosting   numerous   fundraisers,
organizing  clothing  and  toy  donation  drives, donating backpacks and
school supplies, providing scholarships, and  hosting  countless  events
for the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,   The  Tuckahoe-Eastchester  Lions  Club  is  dedicated  to
improving the lives  of  those  who  are  visually  impaired,  and  they
maintain  five  eyeglass  donation  mailboxes  throughout the community,
which have collected over 10,000 pairs of eyeglasses annually  that  are
regularly  distributed  to  those  who  lack  access  to  basic eye care
services; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  creative  community  projects   taken   on   by   the
Tuckahoe-Eastchester   Lions   Club   has   benefited   many   different
communities, groups,  and  organizations  in  the  area,  including  the
Lawrence  Hospital,  Big Brothers Big Sisters, the Eastchester Community
Action Partnership, the Westchester School, Guiding Eyes for the  Blind,
 
Vacation Camp for the Blind, and students in all grade levels within the
Tuckahoe, Eastchester, and Bronxville school districts; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Donations  are  a  vital  part of the Tuckahoe-Eastchester
Lions Club operations, and their donations have supported various  local
organizations  including  the  Bronxville-Eastchester-Tuckahoe Community
Fund, the Eastchester Rotary, Blue Path  Service  Dogs,  Tuckahoe  Youth
Association,  Tuckahoe  Senior Citizens, Lifting Up Westchester, and the
Lions International Disaster Relief Fund; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  its  history,  the  diverse  efforts  of   the
Tuckahoe-Eastchester  Lions  Club  demonstrates  a  strong dedication to
addressing a wide spectrum of community needs with a consistent focus on
youth development and overall community welfare; and
 
  WHEREAS,   Over   its   distinguished    100-year    history,    the
Tuckahoe-Eastchester  Lions  Club  has  truly  strived to live up to its
motto, "We Serve"; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Tuckahoe-Eastchester Lions  Club  is  in
direct  correlation to the efforts of its members, whose involvement is,
and always has been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an
unbridled  enthusiasm,  and  an uncompromising standard of excellence in
all endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves;
and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor   the   Tuckahoe-Eastchester  Lions  Club  upon  the  occasion  of
celebrating its 100th Anniversary, and to honor its  members,  past  and
present,  who  have labored so conscientiously to secure and improve the
quality of life in the community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Tuckahoe-Eastchester Lions Club.
