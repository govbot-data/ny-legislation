Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J01628 Summary:BILL NOJ01628  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORROLISON COSPNSRHINCHEY MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ01628 Text: Senate Resolution No. 1628 BY: Senator ROLISON HONORING Ron Hicks upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of distinguished public service WHEREAS, This Legislative Body seeks to acknowledge those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to enhance the stature of the State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Ron Hicks upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of distinguished public service; and WHEREAS, Ron Hicks has devoted more than three decades to public service in New York State and the Hudson Valley, beginning his career as a college intern for the Speaker of the New York State Assembly and culminating in his service as Assistant Dutchess County Executive, a position he has held since 2012; and WHEREAS, Throughout his career, Ron Hicks has demonstrated exceptional leadership, institutional knowledge, and a deep commitment to strengthening local government, advancing economic development, and improving the quality of life for residents across Dutchess County and the Mid-Hudson region; and WHEREAS, Ron Hicks has played a pivotal role in fostering workforce development and economic opportunity, including partnerships with Dutchess Community College that led to the creation of programs such as the Aviation Education Center and the Mechatronics Lab, helping to prepare the region's workforce for in-demand careers; and WHEREAS, Ron Hicks has previously served as District Director for the late Assemblywoman Eileen Hickey, Deputy County Clerk of Dutchess County, Regional Director for Governor George Pataki, Regional Director for Empire State Development, and in the Office of the Dutchess County Executive, bringing a wealth of experience and continuity to each role; and WHEREAS, In his official acts, Ron Hicks was governed by a keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and WHEREAS, Throughout his distinguished career, Ron Hicks has exemplified the highest standards of public service, professionalism, and dedication to the people of New York State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Ron Hicks upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of distinguished public service and extend its sincere appreciation and best wishes for continued success in all his future endeavors; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Ron Hicks.
