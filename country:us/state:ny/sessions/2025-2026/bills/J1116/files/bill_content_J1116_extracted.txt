and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Sarah Brown upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of a Liberty Medal, the highest honor bestowed upon an individual by the New York State Senate; and WHEREAS, The New York State Senate Liberty Medal was established by Resolution and is awarded to individuals who have merited special commendation for exceptional, heroic, or humanitarian acts on behalf of their fellow New Yorkers; and WHEREAS, On Friday, March 21, 2025, Sarah Brown was leaving work and driving to her daughter's daycare in Cheektowaga, a suburb of Buffalo, when she saw a crashed vehicle on the side of the road; a 60-year-old man experienced a medical emergency that caused him to crash, overturning his car on its side; and WHEREAS, The man was unresponsive and lying on the ground; bystanders were screaming, asking if anyone knew CPR; while many others drove past the accident, Sarah Brown did not; she is certified in CPR, and hearing those calls, she jumped out of her car and into action; and WHEREAS, After a few minutes of chest compressions, the man began to breathe again; he was taken to the hospital where his condition was stabilized; Sarah Brown's quick thinking and immediate, effective response saved the victim's life; and WHEREAS, Sarah Brown is a lifelong resident of Buffalo, New York, and a devoted paraprofessional with the Buffalo Public School District; known for her steady presence and compassionate nature, she plays a vital role in supporting students and creating a positive learning environment; and WHEREAS, Outside of education, Sarah Brown is also a professional fashion model; she brings confidence, poise, and creativity to the runway and the camera, representing strength, beauty, and authenticity in every shoot; and WHEREAS, Exemplifying her courage and deep commitment to the well-being of her community, Sarah Brown continues to serve with purpose, uplifting students, inspiring others through fashion, and walking boldly in her faith which is at the heart of everything she does; it guides her actions, fuels her compassion, and inspires her to be a source of light and encouragement to those around her; and WHEREAS, Due to Sarah Brown's noble and courageous actions, a precious life was saved, and a seemingly dire situation had positive results; this humble hero's intervention is a reminder of the profound impact which kindness and preparedness can have in our communities; and WHEREAS, Sarah Brown, in her spontaneous and heroic act, has so aptly demonstrated her character and compassion for the welfare of others, personifying, by virtue of her action, the collective concern of ordinary citizens across the community of New York State who voluntarily respond without thought of danger or reward when others need help; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Sarah Brown upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of a Liberty Medal, the highest honor bestowed upon an individual by the New York State Senate; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Sarah Brown.
