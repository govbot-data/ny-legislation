The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive plates for medical technologists. 1. Any medical 4 technologist shall, upon request, be issued a license plate bearing the 5 emblem and words "New York State" on the top of the license plate and 6 "Medical Technologist" on the bottom of the license plate. Application 7 for said license plate shall be filed with the commissioner in such form 8 and detail as the commissioner shall prescribe and shall be verified 9 annually by the applicant. 10 2. The distinctive plate authorized in subdivision one of this section 11 shall be issued upon proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that the 12 applicant is presently a medical technologist. 13 3. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 14 in the same manner as other number plates upon payment of the regular 15 registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article, 16 provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of fifteen 17 dollars shall be charged for such plate. 18 § 2. 1.
