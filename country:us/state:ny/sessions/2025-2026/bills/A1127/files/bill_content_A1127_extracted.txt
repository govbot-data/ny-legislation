SANTABARBARA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Local Governments AN ACT to amend the general municipal law, in relation to notice to the commander of a military station of certain municipal actions The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 239-m of the general municipal 2 law, as amended by chapter 459 of the laws of 1997, is amended to read 3 as follows: 4 2. Referral of proposed planning and zoning actions. In any city, town 5 or village which is located in a county which has a county planning 6 agency, or, in the absence of a county planning agency, which is located 7 within the jurisdiction of a regional planning council duly created 8 pursuant to the provisions of law, each referring body shall, before 9 taking final action on proposed actions included in subdivision three of 10 this section, refer the same to such county planning agency or regional 11 planning council. Such agency or council shall give notice of the 12 proposed action to the commander of any military station having a mili- 13 tary installation within three thousand feet of the property to be 14 affected by the proposed action. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect on the thirtieth day after it shall 16 have become a law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
