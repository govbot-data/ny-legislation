AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to establishing the offense
of vehicular homicide
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 125.13-a
2 to read as follows:
3 § 125.13-a Vehicular homicide.
4 A person is guilty of vehicular homicide when such person, while oper-
5 ating a motor vehicle under circumstances evincing a depraved indiffer-
6 ence to human life, intentionally engages in conduct that causes the
7 death of another person.
8 Vehicular homicide is a class B felony.
9 § 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after
10 it shall have become a law.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
