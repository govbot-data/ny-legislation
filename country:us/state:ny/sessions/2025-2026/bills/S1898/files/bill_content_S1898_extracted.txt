O'MARA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Civil Service and Pensions AN ACT in relation to authorizing Mark Barnhart, David Skinner, and James Pappas to take the competitive civil service examination and be placed on the eligible civil service list for employment as a full- time police officer for the Wayland police department The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding the provisions of section 58 of the civil 2 service law, or any other provision of law to the contrary, Mark Barn- 3 hart, David Skinner, and James Pappas shall be eligible to take the 4 competitive civil service examination for the position of full-time 5 police officer and shall, contingent upon such person's score on such 6 examination, be eligible to be placed on the eligible list for appoint- 7 ment as full-time police officers for the village of Wayland. 8 § 2. Mark Barnhart, David Skinner, and James Pappas shall otherwise be 9 subject to the merit and fitness provisions of the civil service law 10 applicable to full-time police officers. 11 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
