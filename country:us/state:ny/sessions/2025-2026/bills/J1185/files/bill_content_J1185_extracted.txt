Minor, Sister Sabrina L. Crockett, and Brother Robert B. Hensley on September 20, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend fraternal and civic organizations whose enduring commitment and service uplift communities and honor those whose leadership exemplifies excellence; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to honor Meridian Lodge No. 93 & Meridian Chapter No. 67 upon the occasion of hosting their "Meridian on the Bayou" celebration, to be held on Saturday, September 20, 2025; and WHEREAS, The "Meridian on the Bayou" event stands as a testament to the unity, cultural heritage, and community service embodied by the members of Meridian Lodge No. 93 and Meridian Chapter No. 67, who continue to enrich lives through charitable outreach, mentorship, and fellowship; and WHEREAS, This year's celebration will recognize three esteemed honorees whose distinguished contributions have left a lasting impact on their organizations and communities: Bro. David J. Minor, Sis. Sabrina L. Crockett, and Bro. Robert B. Hensley; and WHEREAS, Bro. David J. Minor is recognized for his unwavering leadership, integrity, and mentorship, fostering the growth and legacy of Meridian Lodge No. 93 and modeling the highest ideals of Freemasonry; and WHEREAS, Sis. Sabrina L. Crockett is honored for her tireless dedication to service, sisterhood, and civic engagement within Meridian Chapter No. 67 and beyond, championing initiatives that uplift and empower others; and WHEREAS, Bro. Robert B. Hensley is celebrated for his steadfast commitment to excellence and unity, his contributions to community enrichment, and his embodiment of the principles of brotherhood and service; and WHEREAS, It is fitting that this Legislative Body recognizes and celebrates the exceptional leadership of these honorees and the meaningful work of Meridian Lodge No. 93 and Meridian Chapter No. 67, who together exemplify the values of honor, service, and community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Meridian Lodge No. 93 & Meridian Chapter No. 67 upon the occasion of hosting their "Meridian on the Bayou" celebration, and to recognize Bro. David J. Minor, Sis. Sabrina L. Crockett, and Bro. Robert B. Hensley for their exemplary service and contributions; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Meridian Lodge No. 93, Meridian Chapter No. 67, and each of the honored individuals as a token of the esteem in which they are held.
