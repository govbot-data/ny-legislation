and WHEREAS, The National Action Network (NAN) will host its prestigious "Keepers of the Dream" Awards Dinner on Wednesday, April 2, 2025, celebrating individuals and organizations that have made significant contributions to civil rights, social justice, and the empowerment of marginalized communities; and WHEREAS, Founded by Reverend Al Sharpton, the National Action Network has been at the forefront of advocacy for racial justice, economic equity, and political empowerment, continuing the legacy of the Reverend Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.; and WHEREAS, The "Keepers of the Dream" Awards Dinner serves as a powerful tribute to those who exemplify the ideals of Dr. King through their unwavering dedication to human rights, leadership, and community advancement; and WHEREAS, This distinguished event convenes civil rights leaders, elected officials, community advocates, and changemakers from across the nation to celebrate the achievements of those who have worked tirelessly to advance justice and equality; and WHEREAS, The 2025 honorees will be recognized for their outstanding contributions in the fields of activism, government, media, business, and public service, reinforcing the enduring impact of Dr. King's vision in today's society; and WHEREAS, The National Action Network continues to be a guiding force in the ongoing struggle for equity, advocating for policy changes, social reforms, and grassroots mobilization to uplift historically underrepresented communities; and WHEREAS, The "Keepers of the Dream" Awards Dinner remains a cornerstone of the National Action Network's annual convention, inspiring individuals to recommit themselves to the fight for justice and equal opportunity for all; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize and honor the National Action Network upon the occasion of hosting its Annual "Keepers of the Dream" Awards Dinner, and to honor its continued commitment to civil rights and social change; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the National Action Network.
