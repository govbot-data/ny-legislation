CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Civil Service and Pensions AN ACT to amend the civil service law, in relation to reporting the race, ethnicity and salary ranges for women employed in service of the state The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraphs (c) and (d) of subdivision 2 of section 12 of 2 the civil service law, as added by chapter 460 of the laws of 2016, are 3 amended to read as follows: 4 (c) [The] the name of each office, agency, department and/or position 5 in the service of the state and the number of women employed in each 6 such office, agency, department and/or position; 7 (d) the race and ethnicity of each woman employed in each office, 8 agency, department and/or position in the service of the state pursuant 9 to paragraph (c) of this subdivision; 10 (e) the salary range for each racial and/or ethnic group of women in 11 each office, agency, department and/or position in the service of the 12 state pursuant to paragraph (d) of this subdivision; 13 (f) the president's recommendations and plans for increasing the 14 number of women in traditionally high paying jobs; and 15 [(d)] (g) any other information the president deems relevant. 16 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
