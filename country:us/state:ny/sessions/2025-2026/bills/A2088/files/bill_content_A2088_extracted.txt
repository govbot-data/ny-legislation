EICHENSTEIN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Election Law AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to the total number of voting machines required at polling sites in cities with populations of one million or more The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The election law is amended by adding a new section 7-202-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 7-202-a. Minimum number of voting machines required in cities with a 4 population of one million or more. In cities with a population of one 5 million or more the county board of elections shall provide a minimum of 6 one voting machine for every one thousand four hundred registered 7 voters. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
