BAILEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Insurance AN ACT to amend the insurance law, in relation to providing insurance coverage for cranial prostheses The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subsection (i) of section 3216 of the insurance law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph 40 to read as follows: 3 (40) (A) Every policy which provides coverage for prostheses shall 4 provide coverage for cranial prostheses for individuals experiencing 5 permanent or temporary medical hair loss provided: 6 (i) a licensed provider prescribes such cranial prosthesis for a 7 covered person's course of treatment for the diagnosed health condition, 8 chronic illness, or injury, including, but not limited to, alopecia 9 areata, alopecia medicamentosa, scarring alopecia, and lupus; 10 (ii) the policy provides coverage for a cranial prosthesis to a 11 covered person no more frequently than once every twelve months; and 12 (iii) the coverage is limited to seven hundred fifty dollars for each 13 instance of coverage, and shall be subject to any cost-sharing require- 14 ments that are otherwise applicable. 15 (B) For purposes of this paragraph, the term "cranial prosthesis" 16 means a wig or hairpiece. 17 § 2.
