and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Brenda Rigby Riehle upon the occasion of her retirement after 34 years of distinguished service; and WHEREAS, For the past 34 years, Brenda Rigby Riehle rendered faithful, conscientious and valuable service to Allegany County; and WHEREAS, Brenda Rigby Riehle began her service to Allegany County in May of 1992 as an Account Clerk Typist with the Department of Health, then, in 1994, she became a Senior Account Clerk Typist for the Clerk of the Board's Office, and in 1998 she was appointed Clerk of the Board; and WHEREAS, In 2019, Brenda Rigby Riehle was the Acting County Auditor and in 2020, she served as a Public Information Officer; and WHEREAS, Throughout her career, Brenda Rigby Riehle has served on a number of committees and boards such as the Ad Hoc Transportation Committee, the Allegany County Affordable Government Group, the Board of Ethics, and the Heroin Opioid Abuse Ad Hoc Committee; she also had a seat on the Inter-County Association of Western New York Resolution Committee; and WHEREAS, In her capacity as Clerk of the Allegany County Board of Legislators, Brenda Rigby Riehle served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and WHEREAS, Brenda Rigby Riehle has served the New York State Association of Clerks of County Legislative Boards as Program Committee Member from 2002-2004, Association Officer from 2004-2011, Treasurer from 2002-2009, 2nd Vice President from 2009-2011, 1st Vice President from 2011-2013, President from 2013-2015, and Association Officer from 2015-2016; and WHEREAS, In her official acts, Brenda Rigby Riehle was governed by a keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and WHEREAS, During her tenure with Allegany County, Brenda Rigby Riehle earned the admiration, esteem and affection of her colleagues; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Brenda Rigby Riehle upon the occasion of her retirement after 34 years of distinguished service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Brenda Rigby Riehle.
