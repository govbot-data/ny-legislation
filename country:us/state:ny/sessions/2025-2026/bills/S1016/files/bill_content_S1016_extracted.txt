In order to promote a spirit of patriotic and civic service and 21 obligation and to foster in the children of the state moral and intel- 22 lectual qualities which are essential in preparing to meet the obli- 23 gations of citizenship in peace or in war, the regents of The University 24 of the State of New York shall prescribe courses of instruction in 25 patriotism, citizenship, civic education and values, our shared history 26 of diversity, the role of religious tolerance in this country, and human 27 rights issues, with particular attention to the study of the inhumanity 28 of genocide, slavery (including the freedom trail and underground rail- 29 road), the Holocaust, and the mass starvation in Ireland from 1845 to 30 1850, to be maintained and followed in all the schools of the state.
