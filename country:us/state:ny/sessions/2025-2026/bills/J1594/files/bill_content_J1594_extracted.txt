the Eagle Scout Court of Honor ceremony will be held at St. Edward the Confessor Church in Syosset, New York, on Saturday, March 28, 2026; and WHEREAS, Abigail (Abby) Luk, of Whitestone, is a member of Scouting America Troop 205 in Syosset, New York; and WHEREAS, America has achieved her greatest heights of glory through the dedication, determination, and tireless efforts of the outstanding citizens of this Nation; and WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain her glory; and WHEREAS, Scouting America and its members are dedicated to the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in Scouting is the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout; and WHEREAS, Abigail Luk has been involved in Scouting for many years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals, and has attained all the necessary achievements, including completion of a community service project, which was successfully planned, developed, and executed; Abigail Luk officially became an Eagle Scout on May 13, 2025; and WHEREAS, Abigail Luk contributed her time and hard work to complete her project for the Variety Child Learning Center (VCLC) in Syosset; and WHEREAS, For her Eagle Scout project, Abigail Luk designed and created 11 sensory boards which provide various tactile and hands-on activities that help children with special needs by exposing them to various sounds, textures, and lights; and WHEREAS, The sensory boards also assist with developing fine motor skills and improve hand-eye coordination by reinforcing concepts such as cause and effect, problem-solving skills, and other basic skills; exploration of sensory boards, toys, and objects allows the children to stimulate their senses at their own pace; and WHEREAS, Abigail Luk's perseverance in rising through the ranks to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of her ability to master difficult tasks; her accomplishment will enable her to emerge as a leader, fully equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and WHEREAS, Abigail Luk's dedication to the well-being of the children of the Variety Child Learning Center is represented through her devotion to community and commitment to others; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to her family, her school, her community, her State, and her Nation; undoubtedly, her future will be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Abigail Luk on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Abigail Luk.
