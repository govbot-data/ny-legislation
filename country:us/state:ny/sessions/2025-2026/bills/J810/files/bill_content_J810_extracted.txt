Angelino HONORING Henry W. Bostelman upon the occasion of his retirement after 15 years of dedicated public service to the Afton Central School District Board of Education WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge the significant milestones in the distinguished careers of esteemed residents of this noble Empire State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Henry W. Bostelman upon the occasion of his retirement after 15 years of dedicated public service to the Afton Central School District Board of Education; and WHEREAS, A graduate of the Afton Central School District, Class of 1971, Henry W. Bostelman went on to shepherd the highly successful Bostelman Livestock Transportation Company; and WHEREAS, Henry W. Bostelman, a dedicated and invested community member and family man, was first elected to the Afton Central School District Board of Education in 2010; and WHEREAS, With a commitment to the people of his community and the Afton Central School District, Henry W. Bostelman served three terms on the Afton Central School District Board of Education with a great passion for this line of work; and WHEREAS, Henry W. Bostelman, while capably serving on the Afton Central School District Board of Education for three terms, had to make tough decisions with the goal to better his community and the Afton Central School District; and WHEREAS, Notably, under Henry W. Bostelman's exceptional leadership, the Afton Central School District Board of Education implemented a rigorous and challenging instructional program to support exceptional teaching and learning for all of its faculty and students; ensured a safe teaching and learning environment for all; passed multiple capital projects to dramatically improve its buildings and grounds; and responsibly managed the District's financial assets and resources; all this to provide the best possible opportunities for the community, faculty, staff, and students of the Afton Central School District; and WHEREAS, Henry W. Bostelman, upon his retirement from the Afton Central School District Board of Education, effective June 30, 2025 was, impressively, the longest serving member on the current Afton Central School District Board of Education; and WHEREAS, In his retirement from the Afton Central School District Board of Education, Henry W. Bostelman, a cattleman and farmer by trade, will now focus his unique energies and skills to his family; and WHEREAS, With Henry W. Bostelman throughout, has been his wife, Debbie, son, Matthew, and sister, Cindy, who feel privileged to be part of his life and rejoice in his many achievements; and WHEREAS, In his capacity as a member of the Afton Central School District Board of Education, Henry W. Bostelman served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and WHEREAS, In his official acts, Henry W. Bostelman was governed by a keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and WHEREAS, During his tenure serving on the Afton Central School District Board of Education, Henry W. Bostelman earned the admiration, esteem, and affection of his colleagues; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that when individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens of the great State of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Henry W. Bostelman upon the occasion of his retirement after 15 years of dedicated public service to the Afton Central School District Board of Education; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Henry W. Bostelman. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
