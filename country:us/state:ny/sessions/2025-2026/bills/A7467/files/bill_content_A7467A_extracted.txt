AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to including
stormwater in the definition of sewage for purposes of certain water,
sewer, and water and sewer authorities
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivisions 12 and 14 of section 1045-b of the public
2 authorities law, as added by chapter 513 of the laws of 1984, are
3 amended to read as follows:
4 12. "Sewage" shall mean the water-carried human or animal wastes from
5 residences, buildings, industrial establishments or other places,
6 together with such groundwater infiltration, stormwater, and surface
7 water as may be present. The admixture with sewage of industrial or
8 other waste also shall be considered "sewage" within the meaning of this
9 title.
10 14. "Sewerage system" shall mean the [sewage] system or systems for
11 the collection, conveyance, pumping, treatment neutralizing, storage and
12 disposal [system or systems] of sewage or stormwater in the possession
13 of, or under the jurisdiction and control of, the city or the water
14 board, including all additions, increases, enlargements, extensions or
15 improvements thereto.
16 § 2. Subdivisions 17, 18, 19, 20 and 21 of section 1045-b of the
17 public authorities law are renumbered subdivisions 18, 19, 20, 21 and 22
18 and a new subdivision 17 is added to read as follows:
