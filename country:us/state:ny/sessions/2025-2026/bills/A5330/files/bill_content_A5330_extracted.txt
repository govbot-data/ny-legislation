The public health law is amended by adding a new section 2 267-c to read as follows: 3 § 267-c. Uterine fibroids awareness and education program. 1. The 4 department shall conduct an ongoing public awareness and education 5 program under section two hundred seven of this article on the symptoms, 6 diagnosis and treatment of uterine fibroids and the elevated risk for 7 minority women. 8 2. The department shall produce and distribute educational materials 9 on the elevated risks of uterine fibroids for minority women, symptoms 10 of uterine fibroids and available treatments. Such materials shall be 11 made available to appropriate health care providers for distribution to 12 patients. The department shall also provide information on the symptoms, 13 diagnosis and treatment of uterine fibroids and the elevated risk for 14 minority women on the department's internet website. No provision of 15 this subdivision shall be deemed to prohibit the utilization and 16 distribution of educational materials relating thereto produced by any 17 public, private or governmental entity, in lieu of the department's 18 production of such materials. 19 3. The department shall periodically review available data on the 20 symptoms, diagnosis and treatment of uterine fibroids and the elevated 21 risk for minority women and update the information provided in its 22 educational materials and on its internet website, as appropriate. 23 4.
