CHANDLER-WATERMAN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to providing for the use of rooms within a public school at a reasonable rate The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 414 of the education law, as 2 amended by chapter 513 of the laws of 2005, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 2. The trustees or board of education shall determine the terms and 5 conditions for such use which may include reasonable rental at least in 6 an amount sufficient to cover all resulting expenses for the purposes of 7 paragraphs (a), (b), (c), (d), (e), (g), (i), (j) and (k) of subdivision 8 one of this section. Any such use, pursuant to paragraphs (a), (c), (d), 9 (h) and (j) of subdivision one of this section, shall not allow the 10 exclusion of any district child solely because said child is not attend- 11 ing a district school or not attending the district school which is 12 sponsoring such use or on which grounds the use is to occur. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
