COONEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to encouraging school districts to prioritize civic organizations which encourage community for students in allowing outside groups to use school facilities The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 414 of the education law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 3 to read as follows: 3 3. Local boards which grant access to school facilities by external 4 organizations shall give priority in the use of such facilities to any 5 youth group listed in title 36 of the United States code as a patriotic 6 society, including but not limited to Scouting America, formerly known 7 as Boy Scouts of America, and its affiliated New York groups and coun- 8 cils, and the Girl Scouts of the United States of America and its affil- 9 iated New York groups and councils, in order to facilitate access for 10 students to participate in activities provided by these groups at times 11 outside of the school day for the purpose of encouraging civic educa- 12 tion. 13 § 2.
