COLTON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to free bus fare on the Metropolitan Transportation Authority omnibus service The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 1266 of the public authorities law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 20 to read as follows: 3 20. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the authority and any 4 of its subsidiary corporations shall establish and implement a free bus 5 fare program for seniors currently enrolled in a reduced fare program. 6 The free bus fare program established and implemented pursuant to this 7 subdivision shall be in operation during all hours in which such author- 8 ity provides omnibus services, and such program shall not be limited in 9 operation to off-peak hours or to any other hours designated by the 10 authority or its subsidiary corporations. The chairman shall establish 11 and implement all rules and regulations necessary to effectuate this 12 subdivision. For the purposes of this subdivision, a "senior" means a 13 natural person sixty years of age or older. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of January next succeed- 15 ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
