AN ACT to amend the general city law, in relation to requiring addi-
tional notice prior to the discontinuance of drinking water fluorida-
tion
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The general city law is amended by adding a new section
2 18-e to read as follows:
3 § 18-e. Additional notification requirements for cities seeking to
4 cease fluoridation. No city that fluoridates a public water supply or
5 causes a public water supply to become fluoridated shall discontinue the
6 addition of a fluoride compound to such public water supply unless such
7 city has first complied with section eleven hundred-a of the public
8 health law. Such city shall publish a notice including the date of such
9 discontinuance in local newspapers and on the city's website and social
10 media pages at least ninety days before the date of such discontinuance.
11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
