A11471 Summary:

BILL NOA11471
 
SAME ASSAME AS S10552
 
SPONSORRules (Rivera)
 
COSPNSR

Authorizes the city of Buffalo to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands for the construction, operation, maintenance, and repair of a utility thermal energy network project.

                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          11471
 
                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                      May 20, 2026
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by  COMMITTEE ON RULES -- (at request of M. of A. Rivera) --
          read once and referred to the Committee on Cities
 
        AN ACT authorizing the city of Buffalo to alienate and  discontinue  the
          use of certain parklands
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section 1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the city of Buffalo,
     2  in the county of Erie, is hereby authorized, acting by and  through  its
     3  legislative  body,  and  upon such terms and conditions as determined by
     4  such body, to discontinue the use as parkland  and  alienate  the  lands
     5  described  in  section  three  of  this  act,  for  the  purpose  of the
     6  construction, operation, maintenance, and repair of  a  utility  thermal
     7  energy network project.
     8    § 2. The authorization contained in section one of this act shall only
     9  be  effective  on  the  condition  that the city of Buffalo dedicates an
    10  amount equal to or greater than the fair market value of  the  parklands
    11  being discontinued towards the acquisition of new parklands and/or capi-
    12  tal improvements to existing park and recreational facilities.
    13    § 3. The parklands authorized by section one of this act to be discon-
    14  tinued as parkland is described as follows:
    15    ALL  THAT  TRACT  OR  PARCEL  OF LAND, situate in the city of Buffalo,
    16  county of Erie and State of New York being more particularly bounded and
    17  described as follows:
    18    BEGINNING at a point at the intersection  of  the  southerly  line  of
    19  Seventh  Street  (66  feet  wide)  and  the westerly line of New Seventh
    20  Street (66 feet wide), said point also being the northeasterly corner of
    21  lands conveyed to the City of Buffalo as recorded  in  the  Erie  County
    22  Clerk's Office in Liber 11327 of Deeds at page 3650;
    23    THENCE N 34° 27' 26" W along the southwesterly line of Seventh Street,
    24  a  distance of 130.14 feet to the northeasterly corner of lands conveyed
    25  to  Harbor  Preservation  Housing  Development  Fund  Company,  Inc.  as
    26  recorded  in  the  Erie County Clerk's Office in Liber 11175 of Deeds at
    27  page 1302;
