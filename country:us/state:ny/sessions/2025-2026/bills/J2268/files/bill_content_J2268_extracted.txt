and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Sunshine Chapter No. 34, Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star, upon the occasion of hosting its Five Point Tea on Sunday, June 14, 2026, at the Prince Hall Masonic Temple in Brooklyn, New York, under the leadership of Worthy Matron Maxine White Brown; and WHEREAS, The dedicated sisters and brothers of Sunshine Chapter No. 34 are bound together by the five-pointed star and the timeless teachings drawn from the lives of Adah, Ruth, Esther, Martha, and Electa - figures of the biblical tradition whose stories of loyalty, constancy, faith, love, and fidelity speak across the centuries to the enduring values of the Order; and WHEREAS, The Five Point Tea is a cherished tradition within the Eastern Star family, a gathering that celebrates the five points of the star, brings together sisters, brothers, families, and friends in fellowship and joy, and reflects the warmth, grace, and communal spirit that have always defined the very best of the Order of the Eastern Star; and WHEREAS, Under the leadership of Worthy Matron Maxine White Brown, Sunshine Chapter No. 34 continues to uphold the highest traditions of the Order, fostering a spirit of sisterhood, mentorship, and community investment that reflects the finest ideals of the Prince Hall fraternal family; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor Sunshine Chapter No. 34, its Worthy Matron, its officers, and its members, whose faithful and dedicated service represents the living tradition of the Order of the Eastern Star at its finest; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Sunshine Chapter No. 34, Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star, upon the occasion of hosting its Five Point Tea on June 14, 2026, and to extend congratulations and deepest appreciation to Worthy Matron Maxine White Brown and the entire Chapter family; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Worthy Matron Maxine White Brown and the officers and members of Sunshine Chapter No. 34, Prince Hall Order of Eastern Star, State of New York.
