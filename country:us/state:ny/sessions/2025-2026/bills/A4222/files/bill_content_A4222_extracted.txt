CUNNINGHAM -- read once and referred to the Committee on Energy AN ACT to amend part AA of chapter 58 of the laws of 2016, relating to establishing a zero emissions vehicle and clean burning fuel vehicle rebate program, in relation to including used vehicles in the drive clean rebate program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The opening paragraph of paragraph b of subdivision 1 and 2 subdivision 3 of section 1 of part AA of chapter 58 of the laws of 2016, 3 relating to establishing a zero emissions vehicle and clean burning fuel 4 vehicle rebate program, are amended to read as follows: 5 "Eligible vehicle" means and includes a new or used motor vehicle 6 that: 7 3. The program created pursuant to this act shall include rebates for 8 eligible purchases, provided that a rebate for an eligible purchase of a 9 new motor vehicle shall not exceed two thousand dollars and an eligible 10 purchase of a used motor vehicle shall not exceed one thousand dollars. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall 12 have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amendment and/or 13 repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implementation of 14 this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and completed 15 on or before such effective date.
