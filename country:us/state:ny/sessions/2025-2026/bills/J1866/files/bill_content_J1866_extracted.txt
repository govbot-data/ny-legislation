Morton, Ed.D., the Prince Hall Grand Lodge of New York continues to exemplify a profound commitment to civic engagement, charitable works, and the cultivation of principled leadership grounded in faith and service; and WHEREAS, The esteemed appendant body of the Grand Lodge, Eureka Grand Chapter, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, under the devoted leadership of Grand Worthy Matron Marsha L. Mack and Grand Worthy Patron Grant M. Valentine, stands as a testament to the power of sisterhood, compassion, and charitable endeavor, faithfully advancing the principles of charity, truth, and kindness; and WHEREAS, Esther Day is a sacred and symbolic observance, inspired by the Biblical Queen Esther, whose courage, wisdom, and unwavering faith serve as a guiding light for women who lead with grace, strength, and purpose in the face of adversity; and WHEREAS, This distinguished celebration, held during Women's History Month, honors a legacy of virtuous and trailblazing women who, throughout generations, have labored faithfully within Prince Hall-affiliated bodies and beyond-quietly shaping communities, strengthening families, and advancing the sacred tenets of faith, charity, and truth; and WHEREAS, These women, walking in the spirit of Queen Esther, have served as pillars of light and moral strength as educators, caregivers, leaders, and advocates whose steadfast devotion and tireless service have left an indelible mark upon the communities they uplift; and WHEREAS, The women honored through this celebration-leaders in business, journalism, law, public service, and community advocacy-represent the living embodiment of excellence, continuing a proud lineage of courage, dignity, and purposeful service; and WHEREAS, The partnership between the Prince Hall Grand Lodge of New York and the Eureka Grand Chapter reflects a sacred and enduring bond, demonstrating that the collective strength of brothers and sisters united in purpose can transform communities and elevate humanity; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper that this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to recognize this sacred observance and to commend the enduring legacy and contributions of women who lead, serve, and inspire; now, therefore, be it PROCLAIMED, That this Legislative Body recognize and commend the Prince Hall Grand Lodge of New York and the Eureka Grand Chapter, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, upon the occasion of hosting Esther Day: A Women's History Month Celebration on March 30, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body extend its highest appreciation to the leadership, members, and honorees of this distinguished celebration, and offer best wishes for continued strength, unity, and faithful service; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Prince Hall Grand Lodge of New York and the Eureka Grand Chapter, Prince Hall Order of the Eastern Star, in recognition of this meaningful and historic occasion.
