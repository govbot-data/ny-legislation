Senate Resolution No. 43
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
        CONGRATULATING  the  Roslyn  High  School Marching
        Band upon the occasion of  winning  the  2024  Small
        School   Division   1  New  York  State  Field  Band
        Conference Championship on October 27, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  the
dedication and commitment of our young people who distinguish themselves
through excellence in musical competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Music  competition provides an opportunity for students to
exhibit their practical skills to an audience,  thereby  giving  them  a
sense of pride in their hard work and efforts; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Roslyn  High  School  Marching  Band  upon the occasion of
winning the 2024  Division  1  New  York  State  Field  Band  Conference
Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Roslyn High School's Marching Bulldogs placed first in the
Small School Division 1 category at the 2024 New York State  Field  Band
Conference  Championship  held at the JMA Wireless Dome in Syracuse, New
York on Sunday, October 27, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the able leadership of Band Director Frank Mauriello,
the Roslyn Marching Bulldogs earned a score  of  94.3  for  their  show,
titled, "Equilibrium," to capture this monumental victory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The New York State Field Band Conference Championship show
is the culmination of an eight-week  season  which  features  nearly  60
bands  in  34  regional competitions throughout the state; band members,
directors, staff members and boosters from each of the competing schools
spend countless hours preparing for this prestigious event; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Marching Bulldogs returned home on Monday, October  28,
2024, to a jubilant crowd gathered in front of the high school; Board of
Education President Meryl Waxman Ben-Levy, Superintendent Allison Brown,
and Principal Scott Andrews, along with various administrators, parents,
and  community  members, celebrated as the buses rolled into the circle;
students stepped off the buses beaming with joy, greeted by banners  and
pom-poms cheering them on; and
 
  WHEREAS,  No  marching  band  can succeed without outstanding talent
throughout its entire roster, and such was the case for the 2024  Roslyn
High School Marching Ensemble; and
 
  WHEREAS, It should be further noted that excellence in field band is
possible  only  through  vigorous  practice and discipline, habits which
will guide these young musicians to a lifetime of success in the musical
field or other career fields; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Roslyn High School Marching Band's  achievements  stand
as a proud testament to the leadership of Band Director Frank Mauriello,
 
the  hard  work  of  its  members,  and  the  dedication of its parents,
teachers, administration and community-at-large, illustrating the spirit
of excellence and achievement  which  is  the  district's  hallmark  and
tradition; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor the members of the Roslyn High School Marching Band who positively
represent their school, who are role models  to  their  classmates,  who
exemplify  the marching ensemble's goal of continued excellence, and who
have brought great pride to their community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the members of the Roslyn High School Marching Band and
Director Frank Mauriello.
