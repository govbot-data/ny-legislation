PERSAUD -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Social Services -- commit- tee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recom- mitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to finger imaging for SNAP benefit recipients The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 3 of section 95 of the social services law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows: 3 (d) No commissioner of social services shall require applicants for or 4 recipients of SNAP benefits to be subjected to finger imaging as a 5 requirement of the supplemental nutrition assistance program. 6 § 2. This act shall take effect September 1, 2026. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
