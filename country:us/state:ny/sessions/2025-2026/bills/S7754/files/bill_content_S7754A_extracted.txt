AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to finger imaging
for SNAP benefit recipients
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 3 of section 95 of the social services law is
2 amended by adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows:
3 (d) No commissioner of social services shall require applicants for or
4 recipients of SNAP benefits to be subjected to finger imaging as a
5 requirement of the supplemental nutrition assistance program.
6 § 2. This act shall take effect September 1, 2026.
