        AN ACT to amend the economic development law, in relation to creating an
          electric  school  bus  infrastructure  investment  pilot  program; and
          providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The  economic  development law is amended by adding a new
     2  article 6-B to read as follows:
     3                                  ARTICLE 6-B
     4                     ELECTRIC SCHOOL BUS INFRASTRUCTURE
     5                          INVESTMENT PILOT PROGRAM
     6  Section 189-aa. Electric  school  bus  infrastructure  investment  pilot
     7                    program.
     8          189-bb. Rules and regulations.
     9    § 189-aa. Electric school bus infrastructure investment pilot program.
    10  (a)  The department is authorized and directed to administer an electric
    11  school bus infrastructure investment  pilot  program.  Awards  for  such
    12  program shall be decided through the existing regional economic develop-
    13  ment  council  process,  with  two school districts within each economic
    14  development region being awarded grants  on  a  competitive  basis  each
    15  year.  Such  grant  money may be used to offset the cost of planning and
    16  building the clean energy micro-grids needed to effectuate  the  conver-
    17  sion  to electric buses, including, but not limited to, expenses such as
    18  engineering, land use, and other studies as may be necessary, as well as
    19  for other purposes deemed feasible and advisable by the department. Such
    20  micro-grid systems shall allow for the clean energy production of charg-
    21  ing infrastructure, by producing reduced long term  operating  expenses,

     1  and  reliable power generation to meet the state's goals and mandates in
     2  this area.
     3    (b)  The  New  York  energy  research  and development authority shall
     4  contribute forty million dollars' worth of its own resources, from funds
     5  collected from ratepayers for the purpose  of  supporting  clean  energy
     6  programs,  reducing  emissions  and  fighting  climate  change,  for the
     7  purposes of carrying out the provisions of this article.
     8    § 189-bb. Rules and regulations. The department, in  consultation  and
     9  cooperation with the New York energy research and development authority,
    10  is  hereby  authorized  and  directed to promulgate such rules and regu-
    11  lations as it deems necessary to fulfill the purposes of  this  article.
    12  Such  rules  and  regulations shall be promulgated within ninety days of
    13  the effective date of this article.
    14    § 2.  This act shall take effect April 1, 2026 and shall expire and be
    15  deemed repealed April 1,  2031.  Effective  immediately,  the  addition,
    16  amendment  and/or  repeal  of  any  rule or regulation necessary for the
    17  implementation of this act on its effective date are  authorized  to  be
    18  made and completed on or before such effective date.
