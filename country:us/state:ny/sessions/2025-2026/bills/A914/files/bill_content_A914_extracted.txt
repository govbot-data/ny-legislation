CLARK -- read once and referred to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to requiring the department of health to evaluate and make recommendations related to maternal health care and birthing best practices; to amend a chapter of the laws of 2024 requiring the department of health to convene a maternal health care and birthing standards workgroup, as proposed in legislative bills numbers S. 7702-A and A. 8207-A, in relation to the effectiveness thereof; and to repeal section 1 of such chapter The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1.
