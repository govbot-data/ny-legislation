KELLES, ROSENTHAL, SEAWRIGHT, SIMON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Energy AN ACT to amend the energy law, in relation to code requirements for heavy distribution centers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The energy law is amended by adding a new section 11-111 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 11-111. Special code requirements for heavy distribution centers. 1. 4 Definitions. As used in this section, the following terms shall have the 5 following meanings: 6 (a) "Electric vehicle charging station" shall mean a station that 7 delivers electricity from a source outside an electric vehicle into one 8 or more electric vehicles. An electric vehicle charging station may 9 include several charge points simultaneously connecting several electric 10 vehicles to the station and any related equipment needed to facilitate 11 charging plug-in electric vehicles. 12 (b) "Heavy distribution center" shall mean a facility that (i) 13 contains at least fifty thousand square feet of total floor space that 14 is used for warehouse activities; and (ii) has as its primary purpose 15 the temporary storage, sorting, and redistribution of goods to fulfill 16 e-commerce orders by receiving shipments of goods from one mode of 17 transport and redistributing those goods via a delivery vehicle. A 18 "heavy distribution center" shall not include any facility where goods 19 are manufactured or assembled on site and temporarily stored prior to 20 distribution, or any facility that has as its primary purpose the tempo- 21 rary storage of food products for wholesale distribution. 22 (c) "Last mile delivery" shall refer to the final portion of a 23 parcel's journey from a heavy distribution center to a consumer or end- 24 user.
