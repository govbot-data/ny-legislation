Amd §§413 & 488, Soc Serv L
 
Expands the persons responsible for reporting cases of suspected child abuse to include employees, volunteers, or agents of any corporate entity having an agreement with a municipality as a homeless shelter provider and the development of procedures related thereto.

     1    § 3. Section 413 of the social services law is amended by adding a new
     2  subdivision 7 to read as follows:
     3    7. Any person, institution or corporate agent having an agreement with
     4  a  municipality as a homeless shelter provider which employs persons who

    14  guard  the  life or health of the child; and establish, and implement on
    15  an ongoing basis, a training program for all current and  new  employees
    16  regarding  the  policies  and  procedures  established  pursuant to this
    17  subdivision.
    18    § 4. This act shall take effect immediately; provided,  however,  that
