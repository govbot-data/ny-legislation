this Legislative Body commends rare athletic achievements and pays special recognition to those who pursue such excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State; and WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both the moral and physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the Bolton Central School Boys Varsity Basketball Team and Coach Cody Kober upon the occasion of capturing the Class D State Championship; and WHEREAS, To the praise and applause of their excited fans, the Eagles defeated the Sackets Harbor High School Patriots in a 66-49 victory to capture the Championship; and WHEREAS, Family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically supported the Eagles throughout their journey as they ended their season with an impressive 26-1 overall record; and WHEREAS, In a sport such as Basketball, which demands athletic prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Cody Kober and his skilled coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the court; and WHEREAS, Coach Cody Kober and all of the outstanding athletes on the Bolton Central School Boys Varsity Basketball Team have clearly utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate the members of the Bolton Central School Boys Varsity Basketball Team: Lance Corey, Jaxon Egloff, Abram Figueroa, Liam Foy, Sam Foy, Jacob French, Will Hens, Jace Hubert, Andrew Morehouse, Brendon Pagan, Jack Pfau, Teddy Phelps, and John Roemer; Head Coach Cody Kober; and Assistant Coach Angel Figueroa on capturing the Class D State Championship, their outstanding season and overall team record 26-1; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the members of the Bolton Central School Boys Varsity Basketball Team, Head Coach Cody Kober, Assistant Coach Angel Figueroa, and Athletic Director Mark Andrejkovics.
