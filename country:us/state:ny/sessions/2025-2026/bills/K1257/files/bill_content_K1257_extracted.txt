and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and pay tribute to those young people within the State of New York who, by achieving outstanding success in athletic competition, have inspired and brought pride to their school and community; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Jaxon Egloff upon the occasion of his designation as the New York State Sportswriters Association's Class D Boys Basketball Player of the Year on April 22, 2026; and WHEREAS, A junior at Bolton Central High School, Jaxon Egloff has consistently showcased a high level of athletic performance in the sport of basketball, becoming just the second Section VII player to earn the prestigious accolade of Player of the Year; and WHEREAS, Jaxon Egloff averaged 17 points, 6.7 rebounds, 4.9 assists, 1.9 steals, and 1 block per game while leading the Bolton team to a 26-1 record and its first state championship; and WHEREAS, This outstanding young athlete, Jaxon Egloff, was named Most Valuable Player of the 2026 NYSPHSAA Class D boys basketball championships after starring in both final four victories against Bridgehampton and Sackets Harbor where he totaled 47 points while adding seven rebounds and three assists; and WHEREAS, Furthermore, Jaxon Egloff has surpassed the impressive threshold of 1,000 points in his career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Boys Basketball Team, showcasing his elite skillset and dedication to excellence in the sport; and WHEREAS, Such success in competitive sports can be achieved only through strenuous practice, a deep passion, and a sense of team spirit, nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic vision; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend Jaxon Egloff upon the occasion of his designation as the New York State Sportswriters Association's Class D Boys Basketball Player of the Year; and RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Jaxon Egloff.
