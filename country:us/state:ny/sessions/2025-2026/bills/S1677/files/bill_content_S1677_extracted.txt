The election law is amended by adding a new section 2 14-114-a to read as follows: 3 § 14-114-a. Recurring contributions. 1. Any candidate, political 4 campaign, political committee, party committee, not-for-profit entity, 5 or for-profit entity soliciting a recurring contribution to a political 6 campaign, political committee, or party committee shall receive the 7 affirmative consent of the contributor to make a recurring contribution 8 at the time of the initial contribution. 9 2. No solicitation of a contribution, whether electronically or by 10 paper, shall provide for recurring contributions as the default contrib- 11 ution. Passive action by the contributor, such as failing to uncheck a 12 pre-checked box authorizing a recurring contribution, shall not meet the 13 requirements of affirmative consent under this section. 14 3. All solicitations for contributions as described in subdivision one 15 of this section shall state that such contribution may be cancelled by 16 the contributor at any time. 17 4. Any candidate, political campaign, political committee, party 18 committee, not-for-profit entity, or for-profit entity accepting a 19 recurring contribution shall: 20 a. provide the contributor with a receipt for the initial contribution 21 and each recurring contribution that clearly and conspicuously discloses 22 all material terms of the contribution within twenty-four hours of the 23 initial contribution and each recurring contribution; EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
