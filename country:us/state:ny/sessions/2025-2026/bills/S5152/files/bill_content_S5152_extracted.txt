COMRIE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT appropriating certain monies for capital improvements to the Kew Gardens, Lefferts Boulevard bridge The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The sum of two million dollars ($2,000,000), or so much 2 thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated to the department of 3 transportation out of any moneys in the state treasury in the general 4 fund to the credit of the general fund, not otherwise appropriated, and 5 made immediately available, for the purpose of capital improvements to 6 the Kew Gardens, Lefferts Boulevard bridge, including but not limited 7 to, structural and full remediation of the outside wall. Such moneys 8 shall be payable on the audit and warrant of the comptroller on vouchers 9 certified or approved by the comptroller, in consultation with the 10 commissioner of transportation, in the manner prescribed by law. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
