LANZA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Elections AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to prohibiting the deliv- ery of an absentee ballot or an application therefore to an address associated with a candidate's campaign The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (d) of subdivision 2 of section 8-400 of the 2 election law, as amended by chapter 481 of the laws of 2023, is amended 3 to read as follows: 4 (d) The board of elections shall mail an absentee ballot to every 5 qualified voter otherwise eligible for such a ballot, who requests such 6 an absentee ballot from such board of elections in writing in a letter, 7 telefax indicating the address, phone number and the telefax number from 8 which the writing is sent or other written instrument, or an electronic 9 application submitted by the voter through the electronic absentee 10 ballot application transmittal system established by the state board of 11 elections, which is signed by the voter and received by the board of 12 elections not later than the tenth day before the election for which the 13 ballot is first requested and which states the address where the voter 14 is registered and the address to which the ballot is to be mailed; 15 provided, however, a military voter may request a military ballot or 16 voter registration application or an absentee ballot application in a 17 letter as provided in subdivision three of section 10-106 of this chap- 18 ter; [and] provided further, a special federal voter may request a 19 special federal ballot or voter registration application or an absentee 20 ballot application in a letter as provided in paragraph d of subdivision 21 one of section 11-202 of this chapter; and provided further, no ballot 22 application shall be mailed to an address associated with a candidate's 23 campaign.
