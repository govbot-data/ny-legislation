PERSAUD -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Budget and Revenue AN ACT to amend the tax law, in relation to exempting breast pumps and breast pump kits from sales and compensating use taxes The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph 46 of subdivision (a) of section 1115 of the tax 2 law, as added by section 1 of part MM of chapter 59 of the laws of 2021, 3 is amended to read as follows: 4 (46) Breast pumps, breast pump replacement parts [and], breast pump 5 collection and storage supplies, and breast pump kits to an individual 6 purchaser for home use. For purposes of this subdivision: 7 (A) "Breast pump" shall mean an electrically or manually controlled 8 pump device designed and marketed to be used to express milk from a 9 human breast during lactation, including the electrically or manually 10 controlled pump device and any battery, alternating current (AC) adapt- 11 er, or other power supply unit packaged and sold with the pump device at 12 the time of sale to power the pump device. 13 (B) "Breast pump replacement parts" shall mean items used in conjunc- 14 tion with a breast pump to collect milk expressed from a human breast 15 and shall include, but not be limited to: breast shields and breast 16 shield connectors; breast pump tubes and tubing adapters; breast pump 17 valves and membranes; backflow protectors and backflow protector adapt- 18 ers; and bottles and bottle caps specific to the operation of the breast 19 pump.
