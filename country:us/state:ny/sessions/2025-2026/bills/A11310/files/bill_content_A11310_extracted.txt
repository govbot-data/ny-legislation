Notwithstanding the provisions of section 58 of the civil 2 service law or any other provision of law to the contrary, Ivan Cruz 3 shall be eligible to take the competitive civil service examination for 4 the position of deputy sheriff and shall, contingent upon his score on 5 such examination, be eligible to be placed on the eligible list for 6 appointment as a full-time deputy sheriff for the Broome county sher- 7 iff's office. 8 § 2. Ivan Cruz shall otherwise be subject to the merit and fitness 9 provisions of the civil service law applicable to full-time deputy sher- 10 iffs. 11 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
