Subdivision 1 of section 3.17 of the parks, recreation and 2 historic preservation law, as amended by chapter 727 of the laws of 3 1978, is amended to read as follows: 4 1. No real prop- 10 erty shall be so acquired unless the title thereto is approved by the 11 attorney general.
