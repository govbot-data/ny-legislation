Gonzalez-Ro- jas) -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law and the public authorities law, in relation to authorizing a residential parking permit system in the city of New York; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1640-t to read as follows: 3 § 1640-t. Residential parking system in the city of New York. 1. 4 Notwithstanding the provisions of any law to the contrary, the city of 5 New York may, by adoption of a local law or ordinance, provide for a 6 residential parking permit system and fix and require the payment of 7 fees applicable to parking within the area of the city in which such 8 parking system is in effect in accordance with the provisions of this 9 section. 10 2. Such residential parking permit system may only be established 11 within the city of New York neighborhoods of Queens Community Board 12 three and Queens Community Board four. 13 3. Notwithstanding the foregoing, no permit shall be required on 14 streets where the adjacent properties are zoned for commercial or retail 15 use or in metered parking spaces. 16 4.
