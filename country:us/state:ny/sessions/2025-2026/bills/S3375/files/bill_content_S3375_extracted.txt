GRIFFO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Investigations and Govern- ment Operations AN ACT to amend the public officers law, in relation to the temporary appointment of heads of departments without the advice and consent of the senate The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public officers law is amended by adding a new section 2 33-b to read as follows: 3 § 33-b. Appointment of heads of departments without the advice and 4 consent of the senate. 1. No person shall serve as the head of a depart- 5 ment for more than ninety days without the advice and consent of the 6 senate when the legislature is in session. 7 2. When a vacancy occurs when the legislature is not in session, no 8 person shall serve as the head of a department without the advice and 9 consent of the senate for longer than thirty calendar days of the next 10 succeeding legislative session. 11 3. A person may only serve as the head of a department without the 12 advice and consent of the senate once, regardless of the amount of time 13 such person served as the head of a department. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
