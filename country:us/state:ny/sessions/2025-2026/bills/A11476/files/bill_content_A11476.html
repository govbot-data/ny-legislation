A11476 Summary:

BILL NOA11476
 
SAME ASSAME AS S10558
 
SPONSORRules (Shimsky)
 
COSPNSR
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §2, Chap 573 of 2025
 
Expands what qualifies as an authorized project for the "county of Westchester public works investment act" to include replacing certain bridges.
