Humphreys, Inc., working side-by-side for decades alongside their children and grandchildren, embodying the values of hard work, unity, and perseverance; and WHEREAS, Their dedication to community service is equally noteworthy, as active members of the New Hartford First United Methodist Church, where they served in numerous leadership and volunteer roles, and through their continued support of local charitable efforts; and WHEREAS, Blessed with a large and loving family, including four children, fourteen grandchildren, and seventeen great-grandchildren, Ralph and Lois Humphreys have created a lasting legacy grounded in faith, family, and shared values; and WHEREAS, Their lifetime of shared experiences, from raising a family and building businesses to traveling across all 50 states and around the world, reflects a deep and abiding commitment to one another and to living life to the fullest; and WHEREAS, The longevity of their marriage stands as a tribute to their strength of character and the deep affection they continue to share, offering an inspiring example to their community and to generations to come; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 72nd Wedding Anniversary of Ralph and Lois Humphreys, and to extend its heartfelt congratulations and best wishes for many more years of happiness together; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Ralph and Lois Humphreys.
