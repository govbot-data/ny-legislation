Senate Resolution No. 1831
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
        COMMENDING   Abby   Podwall   and   Keshin  Huang,
        co-founders of SPARKS, for their  dedicated  service
        to elementary students in their community
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions to  honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, The character and richness of life  in  this  noble  Empire
State  are  nourished  and  fulfilled  through  the concern and selfless
dedication of those young people who commit their energies  and  purpose
to the enrichment of others; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  takes pleasure in commending Abby
Podwall and Keshin Huang, co-founders of  SPARKS,  for  their  dedicated
service to elementary students in their community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  2024, Abby Podwall and Keshin Huang, students at Great
Neck North High School, founded SPARKS (STEM Programs And Resources  for
Kids  by  Students),  a  non-profit  program  which  inspires elementary
students through hands-on STEM (Science,  Technology,  Engineering,  and
Mathematics)   learning   experiences;  partnering  with  Adventures  in
Learning, the program offers engaging activities,  led  by  high  school
volunteers, to guide young learners; and
 
  WHEREAS,   The  programs  in  SPARKS  focus  on  hands-on  learning,
mentorship, and inspiring the next generation  of  STEM  leaders;  every
student  deserves  access  to  the  resources and opportunities that can
shape their future, and the program's volunteers are committed to making
that a reality; and
 
  WHEREAS, The activities are carefully tailored to the young audience
while  also  teaching  the  students   important   problem-solving   and
scientific thinking skills; some activities include exciting educational
experiments  which  captivate  the  children's  curiosity  and  foster a
passion for STEM subjects; and
 
  WHEREAS, Over the course of two years,  SPARKS  has  organized  more
than  40  STEM  projects for underserved elementary-aged students in the
Great Neck, New York, area; the organization has  reached  approximately
70  volunteers  and is beginning to open new chapters in New York State;
the program serves schools, community centers, and  youth  organizations
across the region; and
 
  WHEREAS, To fund the program, SPARKS relies on creative and engaging
fundraising  events;  Abby  Podwall and Keshin Huang are ambitious about
the future of SPARKS and hope to expand their program to more schools in
the district, reaching even more children and broadening  their  impact;
they believe that, with time, their efforts can inspire more students to
be passionate about STEM; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Although  SPARKS  is  still  in  its  early stages, it has
already made a meaningful impact on the local  community;  by  fostering
curiosity  and providing academic support, SPARKS helps children develop
their academic and personal potential; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  dedication  of  Abby Podwall, Keshin Huang, and their
team  of  volunteers  shows  that  high  school  students  can  make   a
significant  difference in their communities; programs like SPARKS prove
that a passion for  learning  can  inspire  others  and  create  lasting
change;  they  hope  that  SPARKS  lights  the way for these students to
explore their interests and discover a love for STEM; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that,  when
individuals  of  such  noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, it is appropriate to  publicly  proclaim  and  commend  those
individuals for the edification of others; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Abby Podwall and Keshin Huang, co-founders of SPARKS, for  their
dedicated  service  to elementary students in their community; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Abby Podwall and Keshin Huang.
