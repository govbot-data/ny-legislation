Pretlow) -- read once and referred to the Committee on Real Property Taxation AN ACT in relation to permitting the Westchester Muslim Center, Inc. to file an application for a real property tax exemption The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding the provisions of any other law, the asses- 2 sor of the city of Mount Vernon is hereby authorized to accept from 3 Westchester Muslim Center, Inc. an application for exemption from real 4 property taxes pursuant to section 420-a of the real property tax law 5 for the 1994 city assessment roll, for the parcel located at 22 Brook- 6 field Road in the city of Mount Vernon. If accepted, the application 7 shall be reviewed as if it had been received on or before the taxable 8 status date established for such rolls. If satisfied that Westchester 9 Muslim Center, Inc. would otherwise be entitled to such exemption if but 10 for a clerical error, the assessor, upon approval by the city council of 11 the city of Mount Vernon may grant exemption from taxation on such 12 assessment roll and make the appropriate correction of the subject roll. 13 If the exemption is granted and such entity, therefore, shall have paid 14 any tax with respect to the subject roll, the applicable governing body 15 or tax departments, in their sole discretion, shall provide for the 16 refund of the taxes paid and cancel taxes remaining unpaid. 17 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
