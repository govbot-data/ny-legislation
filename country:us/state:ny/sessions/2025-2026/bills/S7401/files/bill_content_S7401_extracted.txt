The election law is amended by adding a new section 6-127 2 to read as follows: 3 § 6-127. Designation by county committee. 1. Party designation of a 4 candidate for nomination at a primary election in counties, excluding 5 counties located within a city with a population of one million or more, 6 for any office to be filled by the voters of the entire county shall be 7 made by the county committee, if the party rules of such county so 8 provide. 9 2. Party designation of a candidate for nomination at a primary 10 election for any office whose electoral district is contained within a 11 county, excluding counties located within a city with a population of 12 one million or more, shall be made by the county committee members 13 representing the district associated with such office, if the party 14 rules of such county so provide. 15 3.
