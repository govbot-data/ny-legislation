Subdivision 1 of section 1194 of the vehicle and traffic 2 law is amended by adding a new paragraph (c) to read as follows: 3 (c) Oral fluid test. (1) Definition. For the purposes of this article, 4 "oral fluid test" means a standardized preliminary saliva analysis test 5 validated by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration for the 6 detection of cannabis. A field sobriety test is considered an oral fluid 7 test if it is administered in substantial compliance with the standards 8 prescribed by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration. 9 (2) Oral fluid test. Every person operating a motor vehicle which has 10 been involved in an accident or which is operated in violation of any of 11 the provisions of this chapter shall, at the request of a police offi- 12 cer, submit to an oral fluid test to be administered by the police offi- 13 cer.
