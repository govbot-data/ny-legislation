BROWN -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to certain charges for advanced metering infrastructure for water-works corporation services The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public service law is amended by adding a new section 2 89-q to read as follows: 3 § 89-q. Advanced metering infrastructure devices for water services. 4 It shall be the right of every customer of a water-works corporation, at 5 no penalty, fee or service charge to decline the permission of the 6 water-works corporation to replace an existing meter at such customer's 7 premises that is assigned to such customer's account. 8 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
