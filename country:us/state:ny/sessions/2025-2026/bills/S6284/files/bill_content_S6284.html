        AN ACT to amend the public health law and the social  services  law,  in
          relation  to  prohibiting  the establishment or operation of a shelter
          for asylum seekers on a property that operates as an  assisted  living
          residence, retirement home or nursing home
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may  be  cited  as
     2  the "Frank Tammaro Island Shores law".
     3    §  2.  Subdivision  2  of  section  4653-a of the public health law is
     4  amended by adding a new paragraph (e) to read as follows:
     5    (e) (i) No operator of an assisted living residence,  retirement  home
     6  or  nursing  home  shall  be  granted authority or approval to establish
     7  and/or operate a facility intending to house asylum seekers  after  such
     8  operator  has notified the appropriate regional office of the department
     9  that the operator intends to close and/or decertify the assisted  living
    10  residence, retirement home or nursing home.
    11    (ii) For the purposes of this paragraph, "asylum seeker" shall mean an
    12  individual  who  changes their country of usual residence to seek tempo-
    13  rary or permanent residence in another country, and who has applied  for
    14  asylum  under  8  U.S.  Code § 1158 or is eligible to apply; or who is a
    15  refugee as defined by section 101(a)(42) of the Immigration and  Nation-
    16  ality Act (INA).
    17    §  3. Section 43 of the social services law is amended by adding a new
    18  subdivision 12 to read as follows:
    19    12. (a) The commissioner shall not enter into  any  contract  for  the
    20  establishment  and/or  operation of a facility intending to house asylum
    21  seekers with an operator of an  assisted  living  residence,  retirement
