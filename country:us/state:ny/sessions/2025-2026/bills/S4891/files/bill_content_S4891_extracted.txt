Paragraph a of subdivision 4 of section 89-n of the general 2 business law, as amended by chapter 221 of the laws of 2003, is amended 3 to read as follows: 4 a. a correction officer of any state correctional facility or of any 5 penal correctional institution having the powers of a peace officer 6 pursuant to subdivision twenty-five of section 2.10 of the criminal 7 procedure law; 8 § 2.
