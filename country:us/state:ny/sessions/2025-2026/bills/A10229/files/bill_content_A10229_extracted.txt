CARROLL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Housing AN ACT to direct the division of housing and community renewal conduct a study on the demographics of tenants living in rent-stabilized apart- ments in Nassau, Rockland, and Westchester Counties, and in the City of Kingston; and provides for the repeal of such provisions upon expi- ration thereof The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. 1. The division of housing and community renewal shall 2 conduct a study on the demographics of tenants living in rent-stabilized 3 apartments in Nassau, Rockland, and Westchester Counties, and in the 4 City of Kingston. The report shall report the following demographics: 5 (a) income; 6 (b) race; 7 (c) ethnicity; 8 (d) the ages of the residents of the apartment; 9 (e) the gender or genders of the residents of the apartment; 10 (f) whether children are in the apartment, and, if so, the ages of the 11 children; and 12 (g) the length of tenancy within the apartment at the time of the 13 study. 14 2. Results of the study shall be reported by county or city, as appli- 15 cable. 16 3. Upon the conclusion of the study, the division of housing and 17 community renewal shall issue a report on the results of the study to 18 the governor, the speaker of the assembly, the temporary president of 19 the senate, and the chairs of the senate housing, construction and 20 community development committee and the assembly committee on housing. 21 § 2. This act shall take effect January 1, 2027, and shall expire and 22 be deemed repealed thirty days after the delivery of the report 23 described in section one of this act. The commissioner of the division 24 of housing and community renewal shall notify the legislative bill 25 drafting commission upon the occurrence of the delivery of the report EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
