AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to
enacting the "dollars for diesels act" relating to financial incen-
tives for the retirement of certain heavy- and medium-duty diesel-pow-
ered vehicles
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "dollars for diesels act".
3 § 2. The environmental conservation law is amended by adding a new
4 section 19-0333 to read as follows:
5 § 19-0333. Dollars for diesels program.
6 1. Definitions: When used in this section:
7 a. "Heavy-duty diesel-powered vehicle" means any vehicle powered by
8 diesel fuel having a gross weight of greater than twenty-six thousand
9 pounds.
10 b. "Medium-duty diesel-powered vehicle" means any vehicle powered by
11 diesel fuel having a gross weight of greater than fourteen thousand
12 pounds but no more than twenty-six thousand pounds.
13 2. The commissioner, in consultation with the New York state energy
14 research and development authority, shall establish and administer a
15 program to provide financial incentives to owners of heavy- and medium-
16 duty diesel-powered vehicles of a model year two thousand ten or older
17 to retire and replace such vehicles with new heavy- or medium-duty dies-
18 el-powered vehicles of model year two thousand twenty-four or newer that
19 meet or exceed the applicable emission standards.
20 3. For a vehicle to be eligible for the vehicle retirement incentive
21 under this section, such vehicle shall be a heavy-duty diesel-powered
