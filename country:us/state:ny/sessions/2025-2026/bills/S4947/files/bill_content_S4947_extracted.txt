HARCKHAM, WEBB -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the transportation law, in relation to requiring the addition of fog lines on all state highways and other public ways under the jurisdiction of the department of transportation when repaved The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 14 of the transportation law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 16-a to read as follows: 3 16-a. The commissioner shall ensure that fog lines are added to all 4 state highways and other public ways under the jurisdiction of the 5 department when such highway or public way is repaved. 6 § 2. This act shall take effect one year after it shall have become a 7 law. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
