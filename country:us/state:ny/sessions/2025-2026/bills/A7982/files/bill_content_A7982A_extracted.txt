AN ACT to amend the real property actions and proceedings law, in
relation to final judgments on a petition
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Short title. This act shall be known and may be cited as
2 the "small home owner and tenant fairness act".
3 § 2. Section 745 of the real property actions and proceedings law is
4 amended by adding a new subdivision 3 to read as follows:
5 3. In cases concerning dwellings with two units or less, the court
6 shall render a final judgment on a petition no later than six months
7 from the date upon which issue is joined. Further, in cases concerning
8 dwellings with three or four units, the court shall render a final judg-
9 ment on a petition no later than twelve months from the date upon which
10 the issue is joined. Provided, however, that the parties to the proceed-
11 ing may agree to waive the time requirement for final judgment as estab-
12 lished by this subdivision.
13 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
