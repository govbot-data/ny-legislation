The real property tax law is amended by adding a new 2 section 458-d to read as follows: 3 § 458-d. Exemption for National Guard members and reservists. 1. As 4 used in this section: 5 (a) "Qualified owner" means an active or retired member of the 6 National Guard or Reserves.
