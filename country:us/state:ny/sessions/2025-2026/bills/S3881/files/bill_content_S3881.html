BILL NOS03881
 
SAME ASSAME AS A05529
 
SPONSORMYRIE

Rpld & add Art 25 Title 3 §§2560 - 2563, Pub Health L
 
Relates to the Chisholm Chance Act; creates centers of pregnant and birthing persons and childcare services in Kings county and Bronx county to coordinate community-based organizations and community health workers to combat the pregnant and birthing persons health crisis.

        AN  ACT  to amend the public health law, in relation to the pregnant and
          birthing persons health crisis and establishing two centers  of  preg-
          nant  and birthing persons and childcare services; and to repeal title
          3 of article 25 of such law relating to the control of midwifery
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Chisholm
     2  Chance Act".
     3    § 2. Title 3 of article 25 of the public health law is REPEALED and  a
     4  new title 3 is added to read as follows:
     5                                  TITLE III
     6                             CHISHOLM CHANCE ACT
     7  Section 2560. Legislative findings.
     8          2561. State university of New York center of pregnant and birth-
     9                ing persons and childcare services.
    10          2562. Montefiore medical center of pregnant and birthing persons
    11                and childcare services.
    12          2563. Appropriations.
    13    § 2560. Legislative  findings.  1.  The  legislature  hereby finds and
    14  declares that severe pregnant and birthing person morbidity  constitutes
    15  a  public  health  crisis  that poses a serious threat to the health and
    16  quality of life of many pregnant and birthing persons and children.  New
    17  York  is among the bottom third of states for the number of pregnant and
    18  birthing persons dying, with pregnant  and  birthing  persons  of  color
    19  dying at a rate three and one-half times more than other non-pregnant or
    20  birthing  persons  who  could bear children. The highest rates of severe
    21  pregnant and birthing persons morbidity occur in two counties, Kings and

     1  Bronx county, with the highest  rate  occurring  in  north  and  central
     2  Brooklyn.
     3    2. The legislature further finds and declares that centers of pregnant
     4  and birthing persons and childcare services should be established at the
     5  state  university  of  New York downstate medical center in Kings county
     6  and at Montefiore medical center in Bronx county to address  the  severe
     7  pregnant and birthing persons morbidity crisis.
     8    §  2561.  State university of New York center of pregnant and birthing
     9  persons and childcare services.  1. There is hereby  established  within
    10  the  state  university  of New York downstate medical center a center of
    11  pregnant  and  birthing  persons  and  childcare  services,  hereinafter
    12  referred  to  in this section as "the center". The center shall be dedi-
    13  cated to the care of pregnant and  birthing  persons  and  children  and
    14  shall  offer  services, including but not limited to: (a) prenatal care;
    15  (b) prenatal and childbirth classes; (c) labor and  delivery  care;  (d)
    16  high risk pregnancy care; (e) inpatient and outpatient lactation consul-
    17  tation  and  support; (f) heart health and heart disease prevention; (g)
    18  breast health and mammography; (h) gynecologic surgery; (i) imaging; (j)
    19  lab services; and (k) postpartum care.
    20    2. The center shall provide clinical experiences for students enrolled
    21  in the state university of New York downstate health  sciences  center's
    22  school of health professions midwifery program.
    23    3.  The center shall also collaborate with the state university of New
    24  York downstate health sciences center for the conduction of research  to
    25  inform  and  support  health  care  professionals providing pregnant and
    26  birthing persons and childcare services at  the  center.  Such  research
    27  shall prioritize pregnant and birthing persons and children of color and
    28  shall  include, but not be limited to: (a) research to better understand
    29  and address  the  increasing  rates  of  life-threatening  complications
    30  around  pregnancy;  (b) identifying biomarkers for risk of morbidity and
    31  mortality during and after pregnancy; (c) racial and ethnic  disparities
    32  in rates of pregnancy-related death; and (d) treatments that will reduce
    33  the  rates of life-threatening complications during pregnancy, labor and
    34  delivery, and the postpartum period.
    35    4. The center shall coordinate with local social services agencies and
    36  specially trained community-based organizations to identify and  address
    37  gaps  in  systems  of  care  and coordinate outreach efforts to find and
    38  engage high-need pregnant and birthing persons  and  children  in  Kings
    39  county.

    41  childcare  services.   1. There is hereby established within the Montef-

    43  care services, hereinafter referred to in this section as "the  center".

    47  and  delivery  care;  (d)  high  risk  pregnancy care; (e) inpatient and

    49  heart disease prevention; (g) breast health and mammography; (h) gyneco-
