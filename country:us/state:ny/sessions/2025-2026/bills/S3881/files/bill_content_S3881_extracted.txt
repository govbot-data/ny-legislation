The legislature hereby finds and 14 declares that severe pregnant and birthing person morbidity constitutes 15 a public health crisis that poses a serious threat to the health and 16 quality of life of many pregnant and birthing persons and children. New 17 York is among the bottom third of states for the number of pregnant and 18 birthing persons dying, with pregnant and birthing persons of color 19 dying at a rate three and one-half times more than other non-pregnant or 20 birthing persons who could bear children.
