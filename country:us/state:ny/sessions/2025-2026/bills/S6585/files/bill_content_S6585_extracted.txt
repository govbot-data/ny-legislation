CANZONERI-FITZPATRICK -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT making an appropriation to the Island Park School District to offset a loss of tax revenue The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The sum of seven million dollars ($7,000,000), or so much 2 thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated to the Island Park 3 School District out of any moneys in the state treasury in the general 4 fund to the credit of the local assistance account, not otherwise appro- 5 priated, and one million dollars of which shall be made immediately 6 available, and one million dollars shall be made available each year on 7 July first for six subsequent years, for the purpose of stabilizing the 8 Island Park School District and offsetting a loss of tax revenue. Such 9 moneys shall be payable on the audit and warrant of the comptroller to 10 such school district as prescribed by law. 11 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
