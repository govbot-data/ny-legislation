FISCAL NOTE.--Pursuant to Legislative Law, Section 50: This bill would allow the beneficiary of justice Jerry Garguilo to elect to receive a lump sum equal to the value of the pension reserve as if justice Jerry Garguilo retired on the date of their death. If elected, the pension reserve will be paid in lieu of any other death benefits and offset thereby. This bill will not increase the state of New York's annual contrib- utions. There will be an immediate past service cost of $196,000 borne by the state of New York unified court system as a one-time payment. This cost assumes that payment will be made on March 1, 2027.
