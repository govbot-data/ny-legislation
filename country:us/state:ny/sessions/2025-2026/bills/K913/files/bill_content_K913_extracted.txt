and WHEREAS, Paul Stephen II has given not only his time and energies but also his competence, intelligence and leadership and consequently has been designated for special honor; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Paul Stephen II of Clarence Creative Kitchen upon the occasion of being recognized as the Clarence Chamber of Commerce's 2026 Innovator of the Year at the 53rd Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March 19, 2026 at Samuel's Grande Manor in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, Paul Stephen II has been called upon to contribute his time and talents to countless civic and charitable endeavors and has always given himself unstintingly; and WHEREAS, Born and raised in Clarence, New York, Paul Stephen II now lives in Williamsville, New York; and WHEREAS, Paul Stephen II earned a BA in International Affairs and East Asian Studies from George Washington University in 2012, then he earned his MS in Chinese-English Translation from New York University in 2015; he is a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) and Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS); and WHEREAS, Possessing a strong sense of justice, Paul Stephen II lived in New York City, Singapore, and Beijing while working as a consultant on diverse engagements including anti-money laundering, anti-bribery and corruption, sanctions monitoring, and pre-litigation investigations before returning to Clarence to join the family business; and WHEREAS, Paul Stephen II is the founder of Clarence Creative Kitchen (CCK), an innovative shared commercial flex kitchen and the first of its kind in Clarence, New York; and WHEREAS, Under Paul Stephen II's supervision, CCK provides a fully certified state-of-the-art kitchen facility for food startups of every type, and now it includes more than 50 members, making it the fastest-growing food service facility in Western New York; and WHEREAS, CCK's primary mission is to make the food industry accessible to small business owners through innovation and affordability, and in doing so to add to the unique spirit of community-oriented entrepreneurship that defines Clarence; members include food trucks, caterers, bakers, meal prep services, and food manufacturers; and WHEREAS, Paul Stephen II also serves as a Director at Stephen Development, a Clarence-based family-owned real estate development and property management firm; and WHEREAS, In his role at Stephen Development, Paul Stephen II primarily oversees new development projects and new business initiatives, such as CCK and the Main Stage coworking space, as well as commercial leasing and sales; and WHEREAS, Throughout the entire period of his community service, a period of constructive involvement, Paul Stephen II has stood constant in dignity, good grace and humor; and WHEREAS, With him throughout have been his wife, Diana, their children, Scarlett and Everett who feel privileged to be a part of his life and rejoice in his achievements; and WHEREAS, Rare indeed is the impressive dedication shown by an individual for the benefit of others which Paul Stephen II has displayed throughout his life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Paul Stephen II of Clarence Creative Kitchen upon the occasion of being recognized as the Clarence Chamber of Commerce's 2026 Innovator of the Year; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Paul Stephen II.
