Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the amounts appropriated herein may be sub-allocated or transferred between other agencies, including the state education department, city university of New York, state university of New York, and New York state council on the arts. Such funds shall be apportioned pursuant to a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees with the amount to be received by each, or the methodology for allocating such appropriation.
