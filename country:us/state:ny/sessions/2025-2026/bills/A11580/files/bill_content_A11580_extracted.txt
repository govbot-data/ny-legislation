Moreno) -- read once and referred to the Committee on Energy AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to penalties for the failure of gas and electric corporations to file or correct an annual report The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 66 of the public service law, as 2 amended by chapter 784 of the laws of 1923, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 6. Require every person and corporation under its supervision and it 5 shall be the duty of every such person and corporation to file with the 6 commission an annual report, verified by the oath of the president, 7 vice-president, treasurer, secretary, general manager, or receiver, if 8 any, thereof, or by the person required to file the same. The verifica- 9 tion shall be made by said official holding office at the time of the 10 filing of said report, and if not made upon the knowledge of the person 11 verifying the same shall set forth the sources of [his] such person's 12 information and the grounds of [his] the person's belief as to any 13 matters not stated to be verified upon [his] the person's knowledge. The 14 report shall show in detail (a) the amount of its authorized capital 15 stock and the amount thereof issued and outstanding; (b) the amount of 16 its authorized bonded indebtedness and the amount of its bonds and other 17 forms of evidence of indebtedness issued and outstanding; (c) its 18 receipts and expenditures during the preceding year; (d) the amount paid 19 as dividends upon its stock and as interest upon its bonds; (e) the 20 names of its officers and the aggregate amount paid as salaries to them 21 and the amount paid as wages to its employees; (f) the location of its 22 plant or plants and system, with a full description of its property and 23 franchises, stating in detail how each franchise stated to be owned was 24 acquired; and (g) such other facts pertaining to the operation and main- 25 tenance of the plant and system, and the affairs of such person or 26 corporation as may be required by the commission. Such reports shall be 27 in the form, cover the period and be filed at the time prescribed by the EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
