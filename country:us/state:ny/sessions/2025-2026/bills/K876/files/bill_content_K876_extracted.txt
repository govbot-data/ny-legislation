Conrad CONGRATULATING Chief James Stauffiger upon the occasion of his retirement after 31 years of distinguished service to the Town of Tonawanda Police Department WHEREAS, This Legislative Body seeks to acknowledge those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to enhance the stature of the State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Chief James Stauffiger, upon the occasion of his retirement on Thursday, February 19, 2026, after 31 years of distinguished service to the Town of Tonawanda Police Department; and WHEREAS, James Stauffiger served his country as a United States Marine before becoming a police officer, and he committed his entire adult life of service to his country and community; and WHEREAS, First hired as a police officer for the Town of Tonawanda, New York, on February 2, 1995, James Stauffiger rose through the ranks, becoming Chief on October 7, 2019; during his tenure as Chief, he led the Department through the COVID-19 pandemic and police reform efforts mandated by New York State; and WHEREAS, Working collaboratively with the community, the Town Board, and the officers of the Department, James Stauffiger helped the Town of Tonawanda develop and implement its Police Reform and Reinvention Collaborative Plan in 2021, strengthening the relationship with the community and reinforcing a commitment to transparency, accountability, and progressive policing practices; and WHEREAS, To the many law enforcement personnel who have served alongside him and those he has mentored and protected throughout his career, the dedicated service of James Stauffiger reflects the very best of what it means to be a public servant; he has earned the admiration, esteem, and affection of his colleagues; and WHEREAS, While a proud member of the Town of Tonawanda Police Department, James Stauffiger served his community with loyalty, honor, and distinction; in his official acts, he was governed by a keen sense of duty, assisting others in need and providing essential services to members of his community; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Chief James Stauffiger upon the occasion of his retirement after 31 years of distinguished service to the Town of Tonawanda Police Department; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Chief James Stauffiger.
