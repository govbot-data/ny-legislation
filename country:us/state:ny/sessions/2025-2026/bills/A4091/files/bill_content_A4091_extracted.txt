Section 305 of the education law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 63 to read as follows: 3 63. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, and 4 subject to an appropriation, the department shall contract with an 5 outside entity who has expertise in the prevention of anti-Semitism, 6 Islamophobia bias and discrimination based on religion, race, sexual 7 orientation or gender identity or expression to establish an anti-Semi- 8 tism, Islamophobia bias and discrimination prevention and education 9 program overseen by such department.
