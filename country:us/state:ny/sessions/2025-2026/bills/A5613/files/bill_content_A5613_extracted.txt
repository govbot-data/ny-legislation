The executive law is amended by adding a new section 837-y 2 to read as follows: 3 § 837-y. Operation SNUG program. 1. There is hereby established within 4 the division an operation SNUG program. The operation SNUG program shall 5 provide grant awards to local governments and/or community-based organ- 6 izations who provide programs and services including but not limited to, 7 educational, youth justice, gang prevention, social work and street 8 outreach that aim to reduce, prevent or respond to gun violence.
