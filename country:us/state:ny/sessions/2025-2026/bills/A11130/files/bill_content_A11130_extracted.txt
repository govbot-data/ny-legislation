BROWN -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to dedicating a portion of the state highway system to William E. Peterson The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 345-e to 2 read as follows: 3 § 345-e. Portion of the state highway system to be dedicated to 4 William E. Peterson. All that portion of the state highway system 5 constituting the northbound lanes of state route 231 between Long Island 6 Avenue and Lake Avenue in the town of Babylon, county of Suffolk, shall 7 be dedicated to William E. Peterson. 8 § 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal- 9 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system 10 as dedicated pursuant to section one of this act, provided that such 11 signing shall state "Highway Dedicated to William E Peterson". However, 12 to avoid confusion and to limit any possible disruption of commerce, the 13 dedication called for pursuant to section one of this act shall be one 14 of ceremonial nature and the official name of such highway shall not be 15 changed as a result of this act. 16 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
