GONZALEZ -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to the opera- tion of autonomous taxicabs in a city having a population of one million or more The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 397-d to read as follows: 3 § 397-d. Autonomous taxicabs. 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law 4 to the contrary, no autonomous vehicle may be used for taxi, livery or 5 transportation network company vehicle services in any city with a popu- 6 lation of one million or more without first being duly licensed by the 7 New York city taxi and limousine commission as a taxicab as defined in 8 section one hundred forty-eight-a of this chapter and section 19-502 of 9 the administrative code of the city of New York. 10 2. No autonomous vehicle shall be licensed by the New York city taxi 11 and limousine commission to provide transportation for hire or prear- 12 ranged trips, unless and until the New York city taxi and limousine 13 commission establishes a license for the use of an autonomous vehicle 14 pursuant to subdivision three of this section. The New York city taxi 15 and limousine commission shall not license any autonomous vehicle to 16 provide transportation for hire or prearranged trips unless that vehicle 17 is a taxicab as defined in section one hundred forty-eight-a of this 18 chapter and section 19-502 of the administrative code of the city of New 19 York. 20 3. Before licensing an autonomous vehicle as a taxicab, the New York 21 city taxi and limousine commission shall establish a license for use of 22 an autonomous vehicle as a taxicab and shall promulgate rules regarding 23 such license and the operation of autonomous taxicabs.
