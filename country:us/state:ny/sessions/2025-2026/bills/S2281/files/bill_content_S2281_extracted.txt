WEBB -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions AN ACT to amend the New York state urban development corporation act, in relation to the provision of grant funding for broadband services in non-competitive locations The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 16-gg of section 1 of chapter 174 2 of the laws of 1968, constituting the New York state urban development 3 corporation act, as added by section 2 of part MMM of chapter 58 of the 4 laws of 2022, is amended by adding a new paragraph (i) to read as 5 follows: 6 (i) "Non-competitive location" means a broadband-serviceable location, 7 as determined by the division, that has access to only one wired or 8 fixed wireless broadband service provider that offers high-speed inter- 9 net service, as such term is defined in section 224-c of the public 10 service law. 11 § 2. ConnectAll deployment program. The ConnectAll deployment program is 16 hereby established to provide grant funding to construct infrastructure 17 necessary to provide broadband services to unserved and underserved 18 locations in the state, and to the extent that appropriations are avail- 19 able, to non-competitive locations in the state.
