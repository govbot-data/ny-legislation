Section 27-0107 of the environmental conservation law is 2 amended by adding a new subdivision 1-a to read as follows: 3 1-a. Local enforcement. Any municipal agency having authority over 4 local sanitization or waste management may install surveillance cameras 5 and review such camera footage to help identify individuals who have 6 engaged in illegal dumping. 7 § 2.
