BUTTENSCHON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Small Business AN ACT to amend the state administrative procedure act and the economic development law, in relation to requiring the division for small-busi- ness to publish a small business compliance guide The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivisions 1 and 2 of section 202-e of the state adminis- 2 trative procedure act, subdivision 1 as amended by chapter 253 of the 3 laws of 2005 and subdivision 2 as added by chapter 730 of the laws of 4 2004, are amended to read as follows: 5 1. Not less than once each year, every agency shall submit to the 6 secretary of state for publication in the state register a list of all 7 guidance documents on which the agency currently relies, and provide 8 information on where and how regulated parties and members of the public 9 may inspect and obtain copies of any such document; provided, however, 10 that the department of environmental conservation shall be exempt from 11 the requirements of this subdivision. Guidance published according to 12 section one hundred thirty-eight-a of the economic development law shall 13 be published in the state register. Unless otherwise provided for by 14 law, an agency may make such documents available as provided in the 15 freedom of information law, and may charge fees pursuant to such law for 16 copies of any such document. 17 2. The secretary of state may exempt an agency from compliance with 18 the requirements of subdivision one of this section upon a determination 19 that the agency has published on its website the full text of all guid- 20 ance documents on which it currently relies except for guidance docu- 21 ments published according to section one hundred thirty-eight-a of the 22 economic development law. The secretary of state shall publish a notice 23 of such determination identifying the website in the state register. 24 § 2. The economic development law is amended by adding a new section 25 138-a to read as follows: 26 § 138-a. Small business compliance guide. 1.
