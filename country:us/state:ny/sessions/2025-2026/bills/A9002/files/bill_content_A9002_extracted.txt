The social services law is amended by adding a new section 2 368-g to read as follows: 3 § 368-g. Exemptions from the recipient restriction program. 1. 4 Notwithstanding any law, rule or regulation to the contrary, any indi- 5 vidual who is receiving medical assistance under this title from a Medi- 6 caid fee-for-service or Medicaid managed care provider and who is iden- 7 tified as part of the recipient restriction program set forth in 18 8 NYCRR 360-6.4 shall be fully exempt from all recipient restriction 9 program coverage and payment restrictions with respect to any covered 10 substance use disorder and mental health treatment services and all FDA 11 approved medications for the treatment of substance use disorders, HIV, 12 hepatitis C and mental health conditions. The department shall promulgate rules and regulations to implement 21 the provisions of this section. 22 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
