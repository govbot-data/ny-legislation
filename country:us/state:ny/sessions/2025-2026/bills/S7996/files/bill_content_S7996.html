Amd §1279-i, Pub Auth L
 
Requires the metropolitan transportation authority submit to the legislature and publish on its shared internet website an annual open data plan; requires the MTA to publish all customer surveys and complaint data, incident report data, police incident and arrest data collected by the authority or any of its affiliates and subsidiaries no later than January 1, 2026.

        AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in  relation  to  open  data
          reporting by the metropolitan transportation authority
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Subdivision 4 of section 1279-i of the  public  authorities
     2  law,  as added by chapter 482 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as
     3  follows:
     4    4. The authority and its subsidiaries and affiliates shall make  their
     5  publishable  MTA data available on its website and the open data website
     6  as follows:
     7    (a) The authority shall create a catalogue  of  publishable  MTA  data
     8  within  one  hundred  eighty days of the effective date of this section.
     9  Such catalogue shall be updated as necessary, but at a minimum annually;
    10    (b) The metropolitan transportation authority shall within one hundred
    11  eighty days of the effective date of this section, submit to the  legis-
    12  lature  and publish on its shared internet website a schedule for making
    13  its publishable MTA data publicly available. Such schedule shall provide
    14  for updating the data catalogue as appropriate; [and]
    15    (c) The metropolitan transportation authority shall  create  schedules
    16  for publishing all publishable MTA data within three years of the effec-
    17  tive date of this section[.];
    18    (d)  The  metropolitan  transportation  authority  shall submit to the
    19  legislature and publish on its shared internet website  an  annual  open
    20  data  plan  that  includes,  but is not limited to, a listing of the new
    21  datasets published, a listing of updated datasets, and information about
    22  public engagements sessions, number of full time staff dedicated to  the
    23  open  data initiative, and progress made in publishing datasets pursuant
    24  to the catalogue required by this section;

     1    (e)  The  metropolitan  transportation  authority  shall  publish  all
     2  customer  survey and complaint data collected by the authority or any of
     3  its affiliates and subsidiaries as required by  this  section  no  later
     4  than  January  first,  two  thousand twenty-six. Such published datasets
     5  shall  include,  but not be limited to, any tabular summary, quantifica-
     6  tion or table of the responses to a survey or complaint data, the number
     7  of participants of any such survey, a listing of all  survey  questions,
     8  the  number  of complaints or commendations received each month, and the
     9  type of complaint or  commendation.  Data  regarding  customer  surveys,
    10  customer  satisfaction,  cleanliness, complaints, or commendations shall
    11  include the operating agency, line, station, stop or other location  the
    12  customer  responses  or complaints are referring to. Such datasets shall
    13  also include, but not be limited to, any demographic data  submitted  by
    14  customers or complainants;
    15    (f)  The metropolitan transportation authority shall publish all inci-
    16  dent report data collected by the authority or any of its affiliates and
    17  subsidiaries as required by this section no later  than  January  first,
    18  two  thousand  twenty-six.  Data  regarding  incidents shall include the
    19  operating agency, line, station,  stop,  or  other  location  and  shall
    20  include  incident  details as reported to or by the bus command and rail
    21  control centers; and
    22    (g) The metropolitan transportation authority shall publish all police
    23  incident and arrest data collected by the authority or any of its affil-
    24  iates and subsidiaries as required by this section no later than January
    25  first, two thousand twenty-six.  Data  regarding  police  incidents  and
    26  arrests shall include the operating agency, line, station, stop or other
    27  location.
    28    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
