Amd §§1367-a & 1367, RWB L
 
Prohibits mobile sports wagering operators from enabling or otherwise permitting authorized sports bettors to deposit or withdraw funds to or from their account through gift certificates, open loop gift certificates, or e-wallets.

        Introduced  by M. of A. STECK -- read once and referred to the Committee
          on Racing and Wagering
 
        AN ACT to amend the racing, pari-mutuel wagering and  breeding  law,  in
          relation  to  prohibiting  use  of  gift  certificates, open loop gift
          certificates, and e-wallets for mobile sports wagering

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Paragraph  (b)  of subdivision 5 of section 1367-a of the
     2  racing, pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, as added by section 4  of
     3  part Y of chapter 59 of the laws of 2021, is amended to read as follows:
     4    (b)  (i)  Authorized  sports bettors may deposit and withdraw funds to
     5  and from their account on a  mobile  sports  wagering  operator  through
     6  electronically  recognized payment methods, including but not limited to
     7  credit cards and debit cards, or via any other  means  approved  by  the
     8  commission; provided however, that:
     9    (1)  in  the  case  of  credit  card  payments, each authorized sports
    10  bettor's account per operator shall be limited to a credit card spending
    11  amount of two thousand five hundred  dollars  per  year;  [and  provided
    12  further, that this]
    13    (2)  the  limitation  under  clause one of this subparagraph shall not
    14  apply to other payment methods or to debit cards; and
    15    (ii) No operator shall be authorized to provide a line  of  credit  to
    16  any authorized sports bettor.
    17    (3)  mobile  sports  wagering  operators shall not enable or otherwise
    18  permit authorized sports bettors to deposit or withdraw funds to or from
    19  their account through gift certificates or open loop gift  certificates,
    20  as  such  terms are defined by section three hundred ninety-six-i of the
    21  general business law, or e-wallets.
    22    § 2. Paragraphs (bb) and (cc) of subdivision 1 of section 1367 of  the
    23  racing,  pari-mutuel wagering and breeding law, as added by section 3 of
