and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 90th Anniversary of the Whitesboro Central School District to be celebrated on May 11, 2026; and WHEREAS, On May 11, 1936, the Whitesboro Central School District was officially established through the centralization of several smaller local school districts, marking a transformative moment in the history of public education in the Mohawk Valley; and WHEREAS, The formation of the Whitesboro Central School District enabled the development of a unified educational system, including the construction of a modern high school facility, which provided expanded academic programming and opportunities for students across the region; and WHEREAS, For nine decades, the Whitesboro Central School District has remained steadfast in its mission to educate, inspire, and prepare students for success, serving families across the Towns of Whitestown, Marcy, Deerfield, Schuyler, and Trenton; and WHEREAS, Countless families have received a prized education throughout the distinguished history of Whitesboro Central School District, which has demonstrated a commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement, fostering generations of students who have gone on to contribute meaningfully to their communities, the State of New York, and beyond; and WHEREAS, The Whitesboro Central School District stands as a testament to the power of collaboration and shared purpose, reflecting the values and resilience of the Mohawk Valley and its people; and WHEREAS, The district will commemorate this significant milestone with a 90th Anniversary celebration to be held on May 11, 2026, bringing together students, alumni, educators, and community members to honor its proud legacy and enduring contributions; and WHEREAS, As part of this commemoration, the district will establish a 25-year time capsule to preserve the stories, achievements, and spirit of the community, to be opened by a future generation in the year 2051, symbolizing a lasting connection between past, present, and future; and WHEREAS, The 90th Anniversary of the Whitesboro Central School District's centralization provides a meaningful opportunity to reflect upon its rich history, celebrate its achievements, and look forward to a future of continued growth and success; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 90th Anniversary of the Whitesboro Central School District, recognizing 90 years of learning and legacy; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Whitesboro Central School District.
