BROWN, ANGELINO, GIGLIO, MAHER, MOLITOR, MORI- NELLO, YEGER -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transporta- tion AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law and the highway law, in relation to requiring slow-moving traffic, trucks, buses, and other certain vehicles use the right two lanes of a multilane roadway The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 1128 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision (e) to read as follows: 3 (e) Upon a roadway which is divided into three or more lanes to be 4 used by traffic moving in a particular direction, slow-moving traffic, 5 trucks, buses, and types of vehicles specified by the commissioner shall 6 only use the two right most lanes except in preparation for making a 7 left turn, in preparation for using a left exit, or where required by a 8 traffic-control device. 9 § 2. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 333 to read as 10 follows: 11 § 333. Marking of certain lanes. Each roadway which is divided into 12 three or more lanes to be used exclusively by traffic moving in a 13 particular direction shall have each lane other than the two right most 14 lanes marked with "NO TRUCKS OR BUSES". 15 § 3. This act shall take effect on the first of January next succeed- 16 ing the date on which it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, 17 the addition, amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation neces- 18 sary for the implementation of this act on its effective date are 19 authorized to be made and completed on or before such effective date.
