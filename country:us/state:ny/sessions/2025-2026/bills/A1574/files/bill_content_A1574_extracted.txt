The private housing finance law is amended by adding a new 2 section 35-b to read as follows: 3 § 35-b. Moratorium on voluntary dissolution. Notwithstanding any other 4 provision of law, any urban rental company created pursuant to this 5 article and otherwise eligible to voluntarily dissolve pursuant to 6 section thirty-five of this article shall be prohibited from such 7 dissolution. 8 § 2. 1.
