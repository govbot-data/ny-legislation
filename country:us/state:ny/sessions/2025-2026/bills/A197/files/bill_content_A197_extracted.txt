There is hereby created in 8 the executive department, a commission to be known as the "Brooklyn 9 health care commission", hereafter referred to as the "commission", 10 which shall be charged with examining the system of general hospitals, 11 nursing homes, ambulatory and primary care facilities, and medical 12 school facilities in Kings county and recommending changes to that 13 system. 14 2.
