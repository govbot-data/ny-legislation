Supreme court library at Erie. The law library for the county 6 officials of the county of Erie, as now constituted and all of the books 7 therein, shall be the law library of the supreme court of the state of 8 New York in the fourteenth judicial district and shall be in the care 9 and custody and under the control of the justices of the supreme court 10 of the state of New York in the fourteenth judicial district or a major- 11 ity of them, not designated as justices of the appellate division, who 12 shall be the trustees thereof. The trustees shall have power to receive 13 by gift, devise or bequest any property given or conveyed for the 14 purpose of a law library and to hold and manage and dispose of the same, 15 and may make rules and regulations for the management and protection of 16 said library and prescribe penalties for the violation thereof. They may 17 sue for and recover such penalties and may maintain actions for injury 18 to said library. They may also designate from among their number a 19 library committee or committees, the members of which shall have the 20 power to administer the rules and regulations prescribed by the said 21 trustees. The trustees may employ and appoint such persons as they may 22 deem necessary for the proper care, management and maintenance of said 23 library and fix their salaries. The library and the equipment shall also 24 be available for the use of the surrogate, family and criminal court 25 judges, district attorney and other county officials of the county of 26 Erie and the judges of the civil court of the city of Buffalo presiding 27 in such county. The librarian and the assistant librarian, shall, in 28 addition to the duties of librarian and assistant librarian, perform any 29 and all duties as may be required of or imposed upon them by said trus- 30 tees. The trustees may also procure proper furniture for the said 31 library, purchase books therefor and defray all the expenses incidental 32 to its care and management. They shall yearly ascertain the amount 33 necessary for the aforesaid purposes and certify it to the board of 34 estimate who shall provide for raising and paying the same. 35 § 7. It is the intended object of this act to erect, in the fourth 36 judicial department, the fourteenth judicial district, comprised of the 37 county of Erie and to provide for the employees thereof selection, 38 designation, nomination and election in the general election to be held 39 in November, 2026 of justices of the eighth and fourteenth judicial 40 districts consistent with the foregoing provisions of this act. Any 41 other consistent provision of law, rule or regulation which by its terms 42 or in its application aids in the accomplishment of such object shall be 43 given full force and effect. Any provision which is inconsistent with 44 the accomplishment of such object shall be denied force and effect to 45 the extent that it may be applicable thereto. 46 § 8.
