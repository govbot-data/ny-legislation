BORRELLO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring curriculum or instruction in Italian history and civic impact in public schools The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 819 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 819. Italian history and civic impact curriculum or instruction. 1. 4 The commissioner shall establish Italian history and civic impact 5 curriculum or instruction for all public school districts in the state. 6 The contents of such curriculum or instruction shall be age appropriate 7 and developed according to the needs and abilities of students at 8 successive grade levels in order to provide robust Italian history and 9 civic impact instruction. Such contents shall include, but shall not be 10 limited to, instruction in the following: 11 a. Italian history, including the history of Italian immigration to 12 the United States; 13 b. The movements and policies that impacted the Italian community in 14 the United States; 15 c. The contributions made by the Italian community in government, the 16 arts, humanities, science, and the economic, cultural, social, and poli- 17 tical development of the United States; and 18 d. The structures and historical events that have limited or harmed 19 the Italian community, especially pertaining to the history of Italian 20 immigration to the United States. 21 2. The board of education or trustees of every school district shall 22 provide appropriate training and curriculum materials for teachers who 23 provide such instruction.
