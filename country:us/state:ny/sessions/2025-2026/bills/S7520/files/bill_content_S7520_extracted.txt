CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to prohibiting time limits or curfews on fares provided to students The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The public authorities law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1266-n to read as follows: 3 § 1266-n. Student fare policy. Notwithstanding any provision of law, 4 rule or regulation to the contrary, the authority shall not impose a 5 time limit or curfew on the use of a fare provided as part of a student 6 fare program. 7 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
