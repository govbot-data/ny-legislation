Unauthorized sale of cemetery markers and flag holders. 9 Except for a veterans' organization, those businesses which normally 10 carry on such trade, or are authorized by veterans' organizations to do 11 so, it shall be unlawful for any person, firm, association or corpo- 12 ration to engage in the buying or selling of metal cemetery markers and 13 flag holders bearing the insignia of any veterans' organization placed 14 upon the graves of veterans.
