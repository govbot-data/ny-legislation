Bailey CONGRATULATING Daniel J. Quinlan upon the occasion of his retirement after 35 years of distinguished service as Superintendent of the Village of Geneseo Department of Public Works WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and honor those distinguished citizens of this great Empire State who have devoted themselves to faithfully serving the best interests of their community and the needs of its residents; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Daniel J. Quinlan upon the occasion of his retirement after 35 years of distinguished service as Superintendent of the Village of Geneseo Department of Public Works to officially take place on June 30, 2026; and WHEREAS, Born on March 19, 1966, in Rochester, New York, Daniel J. Quinlan attended Avon Central School and has been a constant in the Geneseo community; he began his illustrious 35-year career when he was hired at the Department of Public Works on January 14, 1991; and WHEREAS, Throughout his career, Daniel J. Quinlan served the Village of Geneseo Department of Public Works (DPW) with intelligence and caring dedication, continuously striving to effectively fulfill the duties of his position; he has also served as a fireman for many years with the East Avon Fire Department; and WHEREAS, Sincerely loved and greatly respected by all those with whom he worked, Daniel J. Quinlan will be remembered for his honesty, integrity, keen sense of duty and ability to show a unique grasp of human problems in his official acts; and WHEREAS, Supporting him throughout have been his wife, Christine; their two children, Ashley and Zachary; and his late mother and father Kevin and Marcia Quinlan, all of whom feel privileged to be a part of his life and rejoice in his achievements; and WHEREAS, In completing his service as Superintendent, Daniel J. Quinlan leaves behind a legacy to the Village of Geneseo Department of Public Works and to the residents of the communities it serves; and WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that those who enhance the quality of life in their community and have shown a long and sustained commitment to the maintenance of high standards in their profession, certainly have earned the recognition and applause of all the citizens of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations congratulate Daniel J. Quinlan upon the occasion of his retirement after 35 years of distinguished service as Superintendent of the Village of Geneseo Department of Public Works; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Daniel J. Quinlan. Go to top Sitemap Request Information Directions and Maps
