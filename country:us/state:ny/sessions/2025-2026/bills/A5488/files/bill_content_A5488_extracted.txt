JACKSON -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Environmental Conservation AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to prohibiting the manufacture, sale, and use of wire grill brushes The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Article 37 of the environmental conservation law is amended 2 by adding a new title 13 to read as follows: 3 TITLE 13 4 WIRE GRILL BRUSHES 5 Section 37-1301. Definitions. 6 37-1303. Prohibition on manufacture, sale, and use of wire grill 7 brushes. 8 37-1305. Preemption. 9 37-1307. Rules and regulations. 10 § 37-1301. Definitions. 11 For purposes of this title, "wire grill brush" shall mean a wire-bris- 12 tled brush used to clean a grill. 13 § 37-1303. Prohibition on manufacture, sale, and use of wire grill 14 brushes. 15 On and after January first, two thousand twenty-six, no person shall 16 manufacture, distribute, sell or offer for sale, or use in the state any 17 wire grill brush. 18 § 37-1305. Preemption. 19 Jurisdiction in all matters pertaining to wire grill brushes which are 20 regulated pursuant to the provisions of this title shall be vested 21 exclusively in the state. 22 § 37-1307. Rules and regulations. 23 The department is authorized to promulgate such rules and regulations 24 as it shall deem necessary to implement the provisions of this title. 25 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
