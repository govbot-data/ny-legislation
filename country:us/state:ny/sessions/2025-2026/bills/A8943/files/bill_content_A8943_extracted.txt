SAYEGH -- read once and referred to the Committee on Codes -- recommitted to the Committee on Codes in accordance with Assembly Rule 3, sec. 2 -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the penal law and the criminal procedure law, in relation to prohibiting state and federal law enforcement acting with- in New York from covering their faces or otherwise concealing their identity, except in certain circumstances The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 190.91 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 190.91 Peace officer identity concealment; prohibited. 4 1. A law enforcement officer, which shall include any person desig- 5 nated as a peace officer under article two of the criminal procedure 6 law, shall not wear any mask or personal disguise while interacting with 7 the public in the performance of their duties, except for medical grade 8 masks which are surgical or N95 respirators designed to prevent the 9 transmission of airborne diseases and masks designed to protect against 10 exposure to smoke when responding to fires or during a state of emergen- 11 cy related to wildfires. 12 2. The provisions of subdivision one of this section shall not apply 13 to special weapons and tactics (SWAT) team officers utilizing such 14 equipment as necessary to protect such officers' faces from physical 15 harm while performing SWAT responsibilities. 16 3. All law enforcement officers shall include such officer's name, 17 badge number, or both on such officer's uniforms. 18 4. For the purposes of this section, "law enforcement officer" means 19 any officer of a local, state, or federal law enforcement agency, or any 20 person acting on behalf of a local, state, or federal law enforcement 21 agency.
