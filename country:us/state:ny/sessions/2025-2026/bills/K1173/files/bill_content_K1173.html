Assembly Resolution No. 1173
 
BY: M. of A. Barclay
 
        CONGRATULATING   the  Oswego  Sub  Shop  upon  the
        occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2025
        New York State Family-Owned  Business  of  the  Year
        Award  by  the  New  York Small Business Development
        Center
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
commend  those  distinguished  businesses  within  the State of New York
whose success and longevity strengthen their communities and serve as an
example of entrepreneurial excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate the Oswego Sub Shop upon the occasion of its designation as
recipient of the 2025 New York State Family-Owned Business of  the  Year
Award by the New York Small Business Development Center; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  prestigious  honor,  conferred  in  August  of 2025,
recognizes  the  Oswego  Sub  Shop's  outstanding  achievements   as   a
family-owned  business,  as  well as its enduring commitment to quality,
service, and community engagement; and
 
  WHEREAS, Established in 1969 by the Greene family,  the  Oswego  Sub
Shop  has  remained  a cornerstone of the Oswego community for 57 years,
guided by its founding principle of serving quality food by  using  high
quality, fresh ingredients; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Through  decades  of  operation,  the  Oswego Sub Shop has
demonstrated remarkable resilience, thriving despite fierce  competition
from  pizza  establishments,  fast-food  chains,  and  national sub shop
franchises; and
 
  WHEREAS, Today, the  business  employs  more  than  40  individuals,
contributing significantly to the local economy and providing meaningful
employment opportunities within the Oswego area; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  35  years,  Bill  Greene  Sr.  led  the  business and
established its solid reputation until 2002 when Bill Greene Jr. assumed
leadership  of  the  business,  continuing  the  legacy  of   excellence
established by his father, who remains a familiar and welcoming presence
behind the counter; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Oswego  Sub  Shop has continually evolved to meet the
needs of its customers, including a 2008 expansion and renovation  which
introduced  an  in-house bakery and enhanced operations, allowing for an
expanded menu featuring 60 varieties of subs, fresh  salads,  and  baked
goods; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Beyond  its  commercial  success,  the Oswego Sub Shop has
demonstrated an unwavering commitment to the community, sponsoring youth
sports teams and supporting numerous local events, thereby enriching the
lives of residents throughout the region; and
 
  WHEREAS, The establishment has also received  national  recognition,
including  praise  from NBC weatherman and SUNY Oswego alumnus Al Roker,
who, as a former regular customer, has declared the Oswego Sub  Shop  to
have "the best subs in America," an accolade further commemorated by the
naming of a signature sandwich, "The Roker," in his honor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  May  of  2025, the Oswego Sub Shop was named the North
Central  Small  Business  Development  Center  at   Onondaga   Community
College's   Small   Business  of  the  Year,  further  underscoring  its
excellence and impact; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper to recognize  businesses  such  as
the  Oswego  Sub  Shop,  whose dedication to quality, family values, and
community service exemplifies the very best of New  York  State's  small
business community; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the Oswego Sub Shop upon the occasion of its designation as
recipient of the 2025 New York State Family-Owned Business of  the  Year
Award  by  the  New  York  Small  Business Development Center; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Oswego Sub Shop.
