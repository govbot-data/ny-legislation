STIRPE -- read once and referred to the Committee on Economic Development AN ACT to amend the cannabis law, in relation to microbusiness licenses The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 73 of the cannabis law is amended by adding two new 2 subdivisions 4 and 5 to read as follows: 3 4. Licensees under this section may, upon approval by the office, 4 change their license type from microbusiness to adult use cannabis 5 cultivation. A licensee under this section who changes their license 6 type to adult use cannabis cultivation shall be required to relinquish 7 control, if established, over any property obtained for the purposes of 8 conducting adult use retail sales in accordance with this section. The 9 office may allow such licensee to maintain an interest in the real prop- 10 erty if the licensee demonstrates, to the satisfaction of the office, 11 that such real property will not be utilized by the licensee for the 12 purposes of adult use retail sales. Such proof may be supplied by the 13 licensee in the form of an attestation, affirmation or other oath to the 14 same effect. 15 5. The office shall promulgate regulations allowing a microbusiness 16 licensee under this section to be licensed and operate without opening a 17 retail storefront; provided, however, that nothing in this subdivision 18 shall authorize a licensee under this section to provide direct to 19 consumer sales without obtaining a retail storefront and approval from 20 the office to commence sales from such storefront. 21 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
