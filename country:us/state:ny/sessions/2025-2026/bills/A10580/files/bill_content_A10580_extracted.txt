TAPIA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Energy AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to requiring the power authority of the state of New York to build at least one elec- tric level three fast charging station for every five thousand New York state residents by December thirty-first, two thousand twenty- seven The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 9-a of section 1005 of the public authorities 2 law, as added by chapter 86 of the laws of 2025, is amended to read as 3 follows: 4 9-a. [As deemed feasible and advisable by the trustees, to] To design, 5 finance, develop, construct, install, lease, operate and maintain elec- 6 tric vehicle charging stations throughout the state for use by the 7 public. Such electric vehicle charging stations shall include at least 8 one space that is large enough to accommodate wheelchair accessible 9 electric vehicles, including but not limited to, vans and minivans. On 10 or before December thirty-first, two thousand twenty-seven, the authori- 11 ty shall construct, and maintain thereafter, at least one level three 12 fast charging station for every five thousand New York state residents. 13 The distribution of such level three fast charging stations shall be 14 spread throughout the state at the county level, so that each county 15 shall receive one level three charging station for every five thousand 16 New York state residents located therein. Siting of charging stations 17 shall be prioritized in high traffic areas including, but not limited 18 to, highway exits, transit hubs, municipal or town centers, or any other 19 widely accessible location the authority deems appropriate. The 20 construction and development of such electric vehicle charging stations 21 shall comply with the provisions of article eight of the labor law, 22 including the payment of a wage of not less than the prevailing wage in 23 the locality where such electric vehicle charging station is located. 24 The authority shall annually post on their website a report on those 25 activities undertaken pursuant to this subdivision, including but not EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
