STAVISKY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Higher Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to authorizing medical assistants to administer vaccinations The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 6527 of the education law is amended by adding a 2 new subdivision 12 to read as follows: 3 12. Notwithstanding any other law, rule, or regulation to the contra- 4 ry, physicians, nurse practitioners, and physician assistants shall be 5 authorized to delegate the task of drawing up and administering vaccina- 6 tions to medical assistants in an office setting, provided that: 7 (a) such vaccinations are recommended by the federal advisory commit- 8 tee for immunization practices and the centers for disease control and 9 prevention; and 10 (b) such medical assistants receive appropriate training and adequate 11 supervision in administering immunizations pursuant to regulations 12 determined by the commissioner. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
