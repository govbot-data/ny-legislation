for the purposes of this paragraph, there may be a finding that a 40 substantially consistent high level of care has been rendered where 41 there have been violations of applicable rules and regulations, that (1) 42 did not threaten to directly affect the health, safety or welfare of any 43 patient or resident, and (2) were promptly corrected and not recurrent; In the case of appli- 48 cations for adult homes, enriched housing programs, or residences for 49 adults, the department shall post on its webpage, and with notice to the 50 long term care ombudsman program, an opportunity for public comment, and 51 shall accept comments by electronic or regular mail. 52 § 4.
