RA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations CONCURRENT RESOLUTION OF THE SENATE AND ASSEMBLY proposing an amendment to section 6 of article 3 of the constitution, in relation to salary increases for members of the legislature 1 Section 1. Resolved (if the Senate concur), That section 6 of article 2 3 of the constitution be amended to read as follows: 3 § 6. (a) Each member of the legislature shall receive for [his or her] 4 such member's services a like annual salary, to be fixed by law. [He or 5 she] Each member shall also be reimbursed for [his or her] such member's 6 actual traveling expenses in going to and returning from the place in 7 which the legislature meets, not more than once each week while the 8 legislature is in session. Senators, when the senate alone is convened 9 in extraordinary session, or when serving as members of the court for 10 the trial of impeachments, and such members of the assembly, not exceed- 11 ing nine in number, as shall be appointed managers of an impeachment, 12 shall receive an additional per diem allowance, to be fixed by law. Any 13 member, while serving as an officer of [his or her] such member's house 14 or in any other special capacity therein or directly connected therewith 15 not hereinbefore in this section specified, may also be paid and 16 receive, in addition, any allowance which may be fixed by law for the 17 particular and additional services appertaining to or entailed by such 18 office or special capacity. Neither the salary of any member nor any 19 other allowance so fixed may be increased or diminished during, and with 20 respect to, the term for which [he or she] a member shall have been 21 elected, nor shall [he or she] any member be paid or receive any other 22 extra compensation. The provisions of this section and laws enacted in 23 compliance therewith shall govern and be exclusively controlling, 24 according to their terms. Members shall continue to receive such salary 25 and additional allowance as heretofore fixed and provided in this 26 section, until changed by law pursuant to this section. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. Upon approval by the 7 people, the amendment to increase legislative member's salaries shall 8 take effect immediately. The ballots to be furnished for the use of 9 voters upon submission shall be in the form prescribed by the election 10 law, and shall set forth the current salary of the members of the legis- 11 lature, the proposed increased per annum salary and shall explicitly ask 12 "Shall the increase in salary be approved?". 13 § 2. Resolved (if the Senate concur), That the foregoing amendment be 14 referred to the first regular legislative session convening after the 15 next succeeding general election of members of the assembly, and, in 16 conformity with section 1 of article 19 of the constitution, be 17 published for 3 months previous to the time of such election.
