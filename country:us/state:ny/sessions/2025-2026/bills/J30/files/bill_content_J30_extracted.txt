and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim January 9, 2025, as Law Enforcement Appreciation Day in the State of New York, in conjunction with the observance of National Law Enforcement Appreciation Day; and WHEREAS, Throughout the State of New York, various law enforcement advocacy organizations will be observing this well-deserved day of appreciation of our law enforcement members; and WHEREAS, It is increasingly being recognized that the jobs which these professionals face in their lines of duty are both dangerous and difficult; and WHEREAS, The Day of National Law Enforcement Appreciation is a time to recognize and reflect on the sacrifice which so many of our friends, family, and fellow New Yorkers make on a daily basis to keep us safe in our homes, places of work, and on the streets; and WHEREAS, Recognizing the importance of the work that law enforcement conducts on a daily basis whether it be tackling petty crime or taking down foreign terror operations to keep New Yorkers safe, these professionals are among the best our State has to offer, being highly skilled, disciplined, and well trained to execute their duties with courage and bravery; and WHEREAS, It is increasingly dangerous to perform these jobs in a world where law enforcement are targeted for the uniforms they wear and the duties they perform in the line of service; and WHEREAS, By focusing on the importance of those who serve in law enforcement, we would hope to bring attention to the difficulties their jobs present and the bravery so many exude in their everyday lives; we may also highlight that too often New York law enforcement professionals from across our great State are not appreciated and give them a moment to know that New York stands behind them; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim January 9, 2025, as Law Enforcement Appreciation Day in the State of New York, and to applaud those who have dedicated their lives to keeping New Yorkers safe from criminals, terrorists, and those who would otherwise do harm to them; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to The Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of New York.
