Senate Resolution No. 1867
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
        COMMENDING  Eta  Nu Sigma Chapter, Sigma Gamma Rho
        Sorority, Inc. upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  the
        Waterpalooza Wet Clinic - SWIM 1922 on May 30, 2026
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
celebrate those organizations whose  tireless  dedication  to  community
health,  public  safety,  and the empowerment of underserved communities
reflects the highest ideals of service and civic responsibility; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Eta Nu Sigma Chapter, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc.,  upon  the
occasion of hosting the Waterpalooza Wet Clinic as part of the SWIM 1922
initiative,  to  be held on Friday, May 30, 2026, at the Brooklyn School
for Social Justice; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority,  Inc.  was  founded  in  1922  at
Butler  University  in  Indianapolis,  and  has  grown into a nationally
renowned organization whose motto, Greater  Service,  Greater  Progress,
animates  its  members'  commitment  to uplifting communities across the
country and throughout the African diaspora; and
 
  WHEREAS, SWIM 1922 was created to address the  stark  and  troubling
reality  that  approximately  10  people  drown  every day in the United
States, and that 70 percent of African American children and 60  percent
of  Hispanic  children  do  not  know how to swim, with African American
children three times more  likely  to  drown  than  their  white  peers;
through  its partnership with USA Swimming, SWIM 1922 works to dismantle
these  disparities  by  providing  swimming  instruction,  water  safety
education,  and access to aquatic opportunities in communities that have
historically been denied them; and
 
  WHEREAS, The SWIM 1922 initiative is inspired by the  tragic  events
of  1922, when more than 100 African Americans drowned while swimming in
segregated public pools across the country, a sobering reminder  of  the
enduring  consequences  of  racial  injustice  and unequal access, and a
powerful call to action that continues to guide the work of Sigma  Gamma
Rho and its chapters to this day; and
 
  WHEREAS, Eta Nu Sigma Chapter, the Brooklyn Alumnae Chapter of Sigma
Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc., has been a steadfast champion of the SWIM 1922
initiative in our community, hosting annual clinics and events that have
brought swimming lessons, water safety demonstrations, CPR training, and
aquatic  career  education  to hundreds of young people across Brooklyn;
their dedication to ensuring that swim safety is a right for all and not
a privilege for some has made a lasting and  measurable  impact  on  the
lives of the children, families, and communities they serve; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Waterpalooza  Wet  Clinic  represents  the  Chapter's
continued commitment to this vital mission, bringing  together  sorority
members,  community  partners,  and  young  participants in a joyful and
empowering celebration of water  safety,  health,  and  inclusion;  this
 
event  exemplifies  the spirit of Greater Service, Greater Progress that
has defined Sigma Gamma Rho for over a century; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor and commend Eta Nu Sigma Chapter, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority,  Inc.,
upon  the  occasion  of hosting the Waterpalooza Wet Clinic - SWIM 1922,
and to recognize its  extraordinary  and  ongoing  commitment  to  water
safety,  community  health,  and  the  empowerment  of  young  people in
Brooklyn and across our state; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this   Legislative   Body   extends   its   deepest
appreciation  to  the  officers,  members,  and partners of Eta Nu Sigma
Chapter for their tireless dedication to ensuring that  every  child  in
our  community  has the opportunity to learn to swim safely, and for the
countless lives they have touched through this important work; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to Eta Nu Sigma Chapter, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc., as
a token of this Legislative Body's esteem and highest regard.
