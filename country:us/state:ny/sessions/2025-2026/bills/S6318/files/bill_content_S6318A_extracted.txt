AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to requiring instruction
in arts and music education to be incorporated into curriculums for
all public school students
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 819 to
2 read as follows:
3 § 819. Instruction in arts and music education. 1. The board of
4 regents shall establish and maintain standards for arts and music educa-
5 tion, which may include dance, music, theater, media arts, visual arts,
6 and studio arts, to be incorporated into the curriculum for students in
7 all public schools across the state. Rules and regulations related to
8 elementary and secondary education school programs shall be consistent
9 with the requirements of this subdivision.
10 2. Each school district shall ensure that instruction in arts and
11 music education, consistent with the standards established by the
12 regents pursuant to subdivision one of this section, is provided by
13 appropriately certified teachers, to the extent possible, in each such
14 school district. All students shall have equitable access to such
15 instruction, which shall be incorporated into the regular course of
16 study and consistent with state graduation requirements.
