MYRIE, GONZALEZ, GOUNARDES, RIVERA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance -- recommitted to the Committee on Finance in accordance with Senate Rule 6, sec. 8 -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the social services law and the state finance law, in relation to establishing a SNAP and cash assistance fraud victims compensation fund The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The social services law is amended by adding a new section 2 95-b to read as follows: 3 § 95-b. SNAP and cash assistance fraud victims compensation program. 4 1. The office of temporary and disability assistance shall establish and 5 maintain a SNAP and cash assistance fraud victims compensation program 6 to compensate persons who receive benefits from the supplemental nutri- 7 tion assistance program (SNAP) or cash assistance program who have had 8 such benefits stolen or who have been defrauded of such benefits. 9 2. The SNAP and cash assistance fraud victims compensation program 10 shall have an application process which shall be provided in English and 11 languages other than English, including but not limited to the ten most 12 commonly spoken languages, aside from English, in the state. 13 3. The inspector general and the attorney general shall work with the 14 commissioner of temporary and disability assistance to determine whether 15 a person who receives benefits under the SNAP or cash assistance program 16 was defrauded of such benefits or if such benefits were otherwise 17 stolen. 18 § 2.
