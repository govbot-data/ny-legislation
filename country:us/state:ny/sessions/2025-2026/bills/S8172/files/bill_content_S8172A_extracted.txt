AN ACT to amend the correction law, in relation to prohibiting the
obscuring of body-worn or stationary cameras in correctional insti-
tutions
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The correction law is amended by adding a new section 127
2 to read as follows:
3 § 127. Obscuring body-worn or stationary cameras. No employee of the
4 department or a correctional facility shall willfully obstruct, block or
5 otherwise obscure a body-worn or stationary camera from recording activ-
6 ities within a correctional facility. A person who violates the
7 provisions of this section shall be guilty of an unclassified misdemea-
8 nor.
9 § 2. This act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
10 have become a law.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
[ ] is old law to be omitted.
