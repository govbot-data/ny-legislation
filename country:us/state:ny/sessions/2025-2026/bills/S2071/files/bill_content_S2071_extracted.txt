No. 554 2025-2026 Regular Sessions IN SENATE January 15, 2025 ___________ Introduced by Sens. SCARCELLA-SPANTON, OBERACKER, WEBB -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Veterans, Homeland Security and Military Affairs -- recommitted to the Committee on Veterans, Homeland Security and Military Affairs in accordance with Senate Rule 6, sec. 8 -- reported favorably from said committee, ordered to first report, amended on first report, ordered to a second report and ordered reprinted, retaining its place in the order of second report AN ACT to amend the labor law, in relation to requiring the department of labor to create and maintain a list of available careers and job listings in the state for veterans The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The labor law is amended by adding a new section 45 to read 2 as follows: 3 § 45. Career and job opportunities for veterans. 1. For the purposes 4 of this section, "veteran" shall have the same meaning as such term is 5 defined in section thirty-two of the veterans' services law. 6 2. The commissioner shall create and maintain a list of available 7 careers and job listings in the state of New York for veterans. The 8 commissioner shall provide such list to the department of veterans' 9 services and shall update such list as necessary to ensure such career 10 and job listings are current. Such list shall be conspicuously placed 11 on the department of veterans' services website. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
