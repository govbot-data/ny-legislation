EACHUS -- read once and referred to the Committee on Consumer Affairs and Protection AN ACT to amend a chapter of the laws of 2024 amending the general busi- ness law relating to requiring charging cords for micromobility devices, mopeds, and bicycles with electric assist to have a red tag attached, as proposed in legislative bills numbers S. 7760-A and A. 8102-A, in relation to charging cords on limited use motorcycles The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 399-nnn of the general business law, as added by a 2 chapter of the laws of 2024 amending the general business law relating 3 to requiring charging cords for micromobility devices, mopeds, and bicy- 4 cles with electric assist to have a red tag attached, as proposed in 5 legislative bills numbers S. 7760-A and A. 8102-A, is renumbered section 6 495-b and amended to read as follows: 7 § 495-b. Tags on charging cords of micromobility devices, [mopeds] 8 limited use motorcycles, and bicycles with electric assist. 1. No 9 person, firm, partnership, association, or corporation shall manufac- 10 ture, distribute, assemble, recondition, sell or offer for sale at 11 retail any micromobility device, [moped] limited use motorcycles as 12 defined in section one hundred twenty-one-b of the vehicle and traffic 13 law, or bicycle with electric assist as defined in section one hundred 14 two-c of the vehicle and traffic law unless there is a red tag affixed 15 to the charging cord of such micromobility device, [moped] limited use 16 motorcycles as defined in section one hundred twenty-one-b of the vehi- 17 cle and traffic law, or bicycle with electric assist which clearly and 18 conspicuously states "UNPLUG WHEN NOT IN USE". Such tag shall include 19 additional consumer safety information regarding safe usage of the prod- 20 uct and its charging equipment and shall be affixed to the cord near the 21 end which is plugged into the electricity source.
