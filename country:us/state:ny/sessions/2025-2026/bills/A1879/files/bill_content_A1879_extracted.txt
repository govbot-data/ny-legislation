Subdivision 1-a of section 404 of the vehicle and traffic 2 law, as amended by chapter 105 of the laws of 2017, is amended to read 3 as follows: 4 1-a. The commissioner may issue special number and/or distinctive 5 license plates to applicants who are members of veterans organizations 6 or active duty military members in the same manner as other number 7 plates are issued pursuant to this article. Such special number plates 8 or distinctive plates shall be issued only upon (a) payment of [a one- 9 time service charge of ten dollars in addition to] the regular registra- 10 tion fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article, and (b) 11 submission of proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that the appli- 12 cant is presently an active duty member or a member of the veterans 13 organization for which such plate is requested. No additional fees for 14 customization of such plates shall be required. Such membership shall be 15 verified annually by the applicant. Application for such plates shall be 16 made in accordance with regulations promulgated by the commissioner with 17 respect to the issuance of such plates. Provided, however, that nothing 18 contained herein shall be deemed to supersede the provisions of section 19 four hundred four-d, four hundred four-e, four hundred four-h, four 20 hundred four-i, four hundred four-j, four hundred four-k, four hundred 21 four-p, four hundred four-aa, or four hundred four-dd of this article.
