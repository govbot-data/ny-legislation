AN ACT to amend the highway law, in relation to dedicating a portion of
the state highway system in the town of Lewisboro, county of Westches-
ter, to Captain Nathaniel Reynolds
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. The highway law is amended by adding a new section 344-s to
2 read as follows:
3 § 344-s. Portion of the state highway system to be dedicated to
4 Captain Nathaniel Reynolds. All that portion of the state highway system
5 constituting state route thirty-five, beginning at its intersection with
6 state route one hundred twenty-one and continuing in a generally
7 northeasterly direction to its intersection with North Salem road, in
8 the town of Lewisboro, county of Westchester, shall be dedicated to
9 Captain Nathaniel Reynolds.
10 § 2. The commissioner of transportation shall provide for the instal-
11 lation and maintenance of adequate signing of the state highway system
12 as dedicated pursuant to section one of this act, provided that such
13 signing shall state "Highway Dedicated to Captain Nathaniel Reynolds".
14 However, to avoid confusion and to limit any possible disruption of
15 commerce, the dedication called for pursuant to section one of this act
16 shall be one of ceremonial nature and the official name of such highway
17 shall not be changed as a result of this act.
18 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
