TEDISCO, ORTT -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring that license plates be inspected during safety inspections and waiving fees for the issuance of certain license plates The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision (c) of section 301 of the vehicle and traffic 2 law is amended by adding a new paragraph 4 to read as follows: 3 (4) A safety inspection shall also include a visual inspection of the 4 vehicle's license plates. Whenever a license plate shall have become so 5 delaminated, or otherwise damaged or worn as to become illegible, such 6 license plate shall not meet the proper and safe condition requirements 7 of subdivision (a) of section three hundred four of this article. A 8 license plate shall be deemed to be illegible when at least one of the 9 license plate's numbers or letters cannot be read because: (a) more than 10 one-third of a number or letter lacks color due to delamination or other 11 damage, including but not limited to fading; or (b) the license plate is 12 missing one or more pieces and such piece or pieces includes more than 13 one-third of a number or letter. The inclusion of the visual inspection 14 of a vehicle's license plates in the safety inspection pursuant to the 15 provisions of this paragraph shall not result in an increase in the 16 safety inspection fee allowed to be charged. 17 § 2. Subdivision 3 of section 401 of the vehicle and traffic law is 18 amended by adding a new paragraph e to read as follows: 19 e. The commissioner shall waive the payment of fees required by para- 20 graph a of this subdivision if the number license plates applied for are 21 required due to a change in license plate design, or if replacement 22 license plates are required as the result of a failed inspection of the 23 license plates as described in paragraph four of subdivision (c) of 24 section three hundred one of this chapter.
