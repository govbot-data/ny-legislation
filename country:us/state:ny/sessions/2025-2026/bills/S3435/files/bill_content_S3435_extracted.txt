HELMING, ASHBY, BORRELLO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Finance AN ACT to amend the executive law, in relation to establishing the fire station construction grant program; to amend the state finance law, in relation to establishing the fire station construction fund; and making an appropriation therefor The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The executive law is amended by adding a new section 720 to 2 read as follows: 3 § 720. Fire station construction grant program. 1. There is hereby 4 established within the division of homeland security and emergency 5 services a fire station construction grant program to be administered by 6 the commissioner for the purpose of awarding grants to fire departments 7 to assist in defraying the costs of eligible fire station projects. The 8 commissioner is authorized, within amounts from any source appropriated 9 or otherwise provided for such purpose, to make grants for such purpose. 10 2. Project grants shall be awarded on a competitive basis through a 11 request for proposal process which shall set forth the standards for the 12 selection process, the required project proposal format, costs eligible 13 for funding, project implementation requirements, reporting require- 14 ments, and such other provisions as the commissioner may deem necessary 15 or beneficial in implementing the program. The governing body of a fire 16 department seeking to apply for a grant shall submit an application on 17 behalf of the fire department to the commissioner. All such applications 18 shall have been reviewed, finalized and approved by the governing board 19 of the fire department prior to submission to the commissioner. Each 20 such application shall: 21 (a) demonstrate a specific need grant funds; 22 (b) demonstrate that resources are or shall be available to provide 23 for maximum utilization of the project if the grant is approved; EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 99-ss to 38 read as follows: 39 § 99-ss. Fire station construction fund. 1. There is hereby estab- 40 lished in the joint custody of the state comptroller and the commission- 41 er of the division of homeland security and emergency services a special 42 fund to be known as the "fire station construction fund". 43 2. Moneys of the fire station construction fund shall be available to 44 the commissioner of the division of homeland security and emergency 45 services for the administration and purposes of the fire station 46 construction grant program established by section seven hundred twenty 47 of the executive law. 48 3. The fund shall consist of all moneys appropriated for its purpose 49 and all other moneys credited or transferred thereto from any other fund 50 or source pursuant to law. 51 4. Moneys in the fire station construction fund shall be kept sepa- 52 rately from and shall not be commingled with any other moneys in the 53 joint or sole custody of the state comptroller or the commissioner of 54 the division of homeland security and emergency services. 55 5. Moneys in the fire station construction fund shall be payable from 56 the fund on the audit and warrant of the state comptroller on vouchers S. 3435 3 1 approved and certified by the commissioner of the division of homeland 2 security and emergency services. 3 § 3. The sum of ten million dollars ($10,000,000), or so much thereof 4 as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated to the fire station 5 construction fund out of any moneys in the state treasury in the general 6 fund to the credit of the state purposes account, not otherwise appro- 7 priated, and made immediately available, for the purpose of carrying out 8 the provisions of this act. 9 § 4.
