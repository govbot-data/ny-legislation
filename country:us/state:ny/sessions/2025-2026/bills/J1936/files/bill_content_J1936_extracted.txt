and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 50th Anniversary of Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services, an organization which has demonstrated unwavering dedication to expanding access to safe, affordable, and sustainable housing; and WHEREAS, Established on September 28, 1976, Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services (INHS) was formed through the collaborative efforts of local leadership, financial institutions, and residents, inspired by a national model of community reinvestment that emphasized partnership and neighborhood revitalization; and WHEREAS, Since its founding, Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services has played a vital role in strengthening communities by developing, preserving, and restoring affordable housing, while offering comprehensive services including homeownership support, rental opportunities, housing rehabilitation, and financial education; and WHEREAS, Over the past five decades, the organization has undertaken numerous impactful projects, contributing to neighborhood stability and growth, including residential developments and community-centered initiatives that have provided lasting benefit to individuals and families throughout the region; and WHEREAS, Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services has distinguished itself through a deeply rooted commitment to people-centered community development, ensuring that its work reflects responsiveness to local needs and promotes dignity, stability, and opportunity for residents; and WHEREAS, The organization has served as a model of excellence within a national network of housing organizations, earning recognition for its innovation, effectiveness, and leadership in advancing affordable housing solutions; and WHEREAS, In recent years, Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services has expanded its reach beyond its original geographic focus to serve multiple counties, thereby broadening its impact and addressing housing challenges across a wider region of New York State; and WHEREAS, Demonstrating forward-thinking leadership, the organization has also embraced environmentally sustainable building practices, incorporating advanced energy-efficient standards and green technologies to reduce costs for residents while promoting environmental stewardship; and WHEREAS, Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services has provided critical financial assistance and housing support to hundreds of individuals and families, including first-time homebuyers and low-income residents, thereby fostering economic stability and strengthening communities; and WHEREAS, Throughout its history, the organization has remained steadfast in its founding principles of equity, inclusivity, and neighborhood-focused service, continuing to adapt these values to meet the evolving needs of the communities it serves; and WHEREAS, The success of Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services is a testament to the vision and dedication of its founders, staff, partners, and community members, whose collective efforts have made a lasting and meaningful difference over the past 50 years; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 50th Anniversary of Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services, and to honor its enduring contributions to affordable housing and community development in the State of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Ithaca Neighborhood Housing Services.
