For the purposes of this section, "covered 18 state authorities" shall mean state authorities that are determined by 19 the office of language access to provide direct public-facing services 20 that have an impact on a significant amount of residents of New York. 21 Covered state authorities shall translate vital documents, including 22 essential public documents such as forms and instructions provided to or 23 completed by program beneficiaries or participants.
