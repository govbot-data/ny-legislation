GOUNARDES -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Civil Service and Pensions AN ACT to amend the retirement and social security law and the adminis- trative code of the city of New York, in relation to disability retirement benefits for certain participants in World Trade Center rescue, recovery, or cleanup operations The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph 4 of subdivision d of section 605 of the retire- 2 ment and social security law, as added by chapter 252 of the laws of 3 2019, is amended to read as follows: 4 4. Notwithstanding any other law, rule or regulation to the contrary, 5 any member who had an active membership in the New York state and local 6 employees' retirement system [or], the New York state teachers' retire- 7 ment system or the New York city teachers' retirement system, when such 8 member participated in World Trade Center rescue, recovery, or cleanup 9 operations, as such participation is defined in section two of this 10 chapter, who incurred a qualifying World Trade Center condition, as 11 defined in section two of this chapter, that is determined to have been 12 incurred in the performance and discharge of duty and is the natural and 13 proximate result of an accident not caused by such member's own willful 14 negligence, shall be paid a performance of duty disability retirement 15 allowance equal to three-quarters of final average salary. The payment 16 of such pension shall be subject to the provisions of section sixty-four 17 of this chapter. 18 § 2.
