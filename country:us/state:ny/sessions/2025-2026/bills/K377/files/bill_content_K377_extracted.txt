Gray HONORING Firefighter Ed Mereand upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Sackets Harbor Fire Company WHEREAS, Citizens across our State and Nation are inspired by and indebted to our noble volunteer firefighters who exhibit courage and bravery every day in the course of their duties; and WHEREAS, Volunteer firefighters exemplify the power of human compassion and the strength of the American spirit through actions of the most heroic magnitude; their sacrifices and selfless dedication merit tribute and recognition by all citizens of this great country; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Firefighter Ed Mereand upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Sackets Harbor Fire Company; and WHEREAS, Firefighter Ed Mereand has dedicated an extraordinary 50 years of distinguished service to the fire service, demonstrating unwavering commitment and selflessness in protecting the lives and property of his community; and WHEREAS, Firefighter Ed Mereand has further demonstrated his exceptional leadership and dedication by serving on the Board of Directors for more than 20 years, providing invaluable guidance and strategic direction to the organization; and WHEREAS, Firefighter Ed Mereand has held the esteemed position of Chairman of the Board of Directors, exemplifying his ability to lead with vision, integrity, and a profound sense of responsibility; and WHEREAS, Firefighter Ed Mereand's remarkable tenure reflects a deep-seated passion for service, a tireless work ethic, and an unparalleled commitment to the betterment of the fire service and the community it serves; and WHEREAS, Firefighter Ed Mereand has consistently demonstrated a level of commitment, dedication, integrity, and professionalism which truly exemplifies excellence in the volunteer service; and WHEREAS, Firefighters unwaveringly serve our great State with pride, valor, and integrity; these respected volunteers are often the first to respond to an emergency, whether the emergency is a fire, transportation accident, natural disaster, act of terrorism, medical crisis, or spill of hazardous materials; and WHEREAS, Firefighter Ed Mereand's contributions have left an indelible mark on the firefighting organization and community, and this Legislative Body celebrates his extraordinary achievements and extends its heartfelt appreciation for his selfless dedication; and WHEREAS, It takes a special dedication, a strong desire to help others, and a tireless sense of community to forsake precious time with family and friends to respond to the signal that a neighbor is in need; Ed Mereand is such a firefighter; throughout his years of devoted service, this volunteer heroically performed, above and beyond the call of duty, those responsibilities which define the task of fire protection; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Firefighter Ed Mereand, upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated service to the Sackets Harbor Fire Company, and express its deepest gratitude and highest commendation; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Firefighter Ed Mereand.
