GLICK, LEVENBERG -- read once and referred to the Committee on Environmental Conservation AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation to the definition of tidal wetlands The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Paragraph (a) of subdivision 1 of section 25-0103 of the 2 environmental conservation law, as added by chapter 790 of the laws of 3 1973, is amended to read as follows: 4 (a) those areas which border on or lie beneath tidal waters of the 5 marine and coastal district, as described in section 13-0103 of this 6 chapter, such as, but not limited to, banks, bogs, salt marsh, swamps, 7 meadows, flats or other low lands subject to tidal action, including 8 those areas now or formerly connected to tidal waters; 9 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
