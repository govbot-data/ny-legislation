JACKSON, TAPIA, DAVILA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Agriculture AN ACT to amend the agriculture and markets law, in relation to prohib- iting the sale of foods and beverages containing chlormequat The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The agriculture and markets law is amended by adding a new 2 section 214-p to read as follows: 3 § 214-p. Sale of foods and beverages containing chlormequat prohibit- 4 ed. 1. For the purposes of this article, the term "chlormequat" shall 5 mean 2-chloro-N,N,N-trimethylethan-1-aminium. 6 2. No person, firm, corporation, partnership, association or any other 7 business association shall sell, offer to sell, or display for sale at 8 retail any food or beverage for human consumption which contains 9 chlormequat. 10 3. It shall be an affirmative defense to a violation of this section 11 that the person did not know and could not reasonably have known that 12 the food or beverage contained chlormequat. 13 § 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after 14 it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amend- 15 ment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implemen- 16 tation of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and 17 completed on or before such date.
