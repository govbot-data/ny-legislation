        AN ACT to amend the alcoholic  beverage  control  law,  in  relation  to
          making  certain  liquor  license  fees  payable  in  installments; and
          providing for the repeal of certain  provisions  upon  the  expiration
          thereof

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Section 67  of  the  alcoholic  beverage  control  law,  as
     2  amended  by  chapter  523  of  the  laws  of 2023, is amended to read as
     3  follows:
     4    § 67. License fees, duration  of  licenses;  fee  for  part  of  year.
     5  [Effective  April  first,  nineteen  hundred  eighty-three, licenses] 1.
     6  Licenses issued pursuant to sections sixty-one, sixty-two[,] and  sixty-
     7  three[,  sixty-four, sixty-four-a, sixty-four-b, sixty-four-c and sixty-
     8  four-e] of this article shall be effective  for  three  years  at  three
     9  times that annual fee, except that, in implementing the purposes of this
    10  [section] subdivision, the liquor authority shall schedule the commence-
    11  ment  dates,  duration  and  expiration  dates thereof to provide for an
    12  equal cycle of license renewals issued under each such  section  through
    13  the  course  of  the  fiscal  year.  [Effective December first, nineteen
    14  hundred ninety-eight, licenses]
    15    2. Licenses  issued  pursuant  to  sections  sixty-four,  sixty-four-a
    16  [and], sixty-four-b, sixty-four-c, sixty-four-d and sixty-four-e of this
    17  article  shall  be  effective for two years at two times that annual fee
    18  established in such sections, except that, in implementing the  purposes
    19  of  this  [section] subdivision, the liquor authority shall schedule the
    20  commencement dates, duration and expiration dates thereof to provide for
    21  an equal cycle of  license  renewals  issued  under  each  such  section
    22  through  the  course  of the fiscal year. Notwithstanding the foregoing,
    23  [commencing  on  December  first,  nineteen  hundred  ninety-eight   and

     1  concluding  on July thirty-first, two thousand two,] a licensee issued a
