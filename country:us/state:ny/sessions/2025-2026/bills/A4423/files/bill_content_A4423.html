A04423 Summary:

BILL NOA04423B
 
SAME ASSAME AS S03791-A
 
SPONSORDilan
 
COSPNSRJacobson, Weprin, Walker, Hooks
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Amd §§2018-a & 2018-b, Ed L; amd §§84-a, 175-b & 213-b, Town L
 
Clarifies that individuals have the ability to vote by absentee ballot in certain elections if they are not currently incarcerated for a felony conviction.
