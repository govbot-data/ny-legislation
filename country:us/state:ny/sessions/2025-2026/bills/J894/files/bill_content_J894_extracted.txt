Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J00894 Summary:BILL NOJ00894  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORGRIFFO COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ00894 Text: Senate Resolution No. 894 BY: Senator GRIFFO HONORING Joe Ferlo upon the occasion of his induction into the Rome Arts Hall of Fame on May 4, 2025 WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor and pay tribute to those individuals whose commitment to promoting the richness and diversity of the performing, visual, and literary arts, has contributed to the strength, vitality, and cultural enrichment of their communities and the great State of New York; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Joe Ferlo upon the occasion of his induction into the Rome Arts Hall of Fame on Sunday, May 4, 2025, at the Capitol Theatre in Rome, New York; and WHEREAS, Established in 2005, The Rome Arts Hall of Fame aligns with their mission to honor individuals who have a strong connection to the Rome community and a significant involvement in the performing, visual, or literary arts; and WHEREAS, Joe Ferlo is a graduate of Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts, and a prominent voice in the jazz, blues, and classical guitar scene; and WHEREAS, Beginning to play guitar at the young age of 10, Joe Ferlo is now associated with several music groups throughout the Rome area; and WHEREAS, This honorable inductee, Joe Ferlo, is also an admired adjunct professor at Colgate University where he proudly teaches jazz guitar; and WHEREAS, New Yorkers who are devoted to the preservation and furtherance of the arts while resultantly strengthening the cultural fabric of this great State are deserving of the highest praise and recognition; Joe Ferlo is one such individual; and WHEREAS, The arts are the building blocks of civilization, are central to human expression, and inspire the imagination; educational institutions, individuals, and organizations which further such worthy purposes richly merit the recognition and appreciation of this Legislative Body and the people of the State of New York; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor Joe Ferlo upon the occasion of his induction into the Rome Arts Hall of Fame on May 4, 2025; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Joe Ferlo.
