McDONALD, PAULIN, GLICK, GRIFFIN, COLTON, SHIM- SKY, McMAHON, DINOWITZ, ROSENTHAL, SIMON, REYES, CUNNINGHAM, VALDEZ -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations -- recommitted to the Committee on Governmental Operations in accordance with Assembly Rule 3, sec. 2 -- committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to said committee AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation to the purchase of firearms, ammunition, and firearm accessories The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 2 139-n to read as follows: 3 § 139-n. Statement in bids for the purchase of firearms, ammunition, 4 and firearm accessories. 1. (a) Every bid hereafter made to the state or 5 any public department or agency thereof, where competitive bidding is 6 required by statute, rule or regulation, for the purchase of firearms, 7 ammunition, or firearm accessories, shall contain the following state- 8 ment subscribed by the bidder and affirmed by such bidder as true under 9 the penalty of perjury: 10 "By submission of this bid, each bidder and each person signing on 11 behalf of any bidder certifies, and in the case of a joint bid each 12 party thereto certifies as to its own organization, under penalty of 13 perjury, that the bidder: 14 (i) is duly licensed pursuant to applicable federal, state, and local 15 law to sell or transfer firearms, ammunition, or firearm accessories, 16 where such licensure is required; 17 (ii) is in compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local 18 laws, rules, and regulations relating to the sale, transfer, storage, 19 and disposition of firearms, ammunition, and firearm accessories; EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted. Any bid hereafter made to the state or any public department, agen- 41 cy or official thereof, by a corporate bidder for the purchase of 42 firearms, ammunition, or firearm accessories, where such bid contains 43 the statement required by subdivision one of this section, shall be 44 deemed to have been authorized by the board of directors of such bidder, 45 and such authorization shall be deemed to include the signing and 46 submission of such bid and the inclusion therein of such statement as 47 the act and deed of the corporation. 48 § 2.
