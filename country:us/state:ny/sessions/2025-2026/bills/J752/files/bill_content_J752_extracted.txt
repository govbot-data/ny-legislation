and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to honor The Bonello Foundation, Inc., upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Youth Summit 2025, a gathering devoted to inspiring, educating, and empowering young people in the heart of Brooklyn, New York, to be held April 25-26, 2025; and WHEREAS, Founded by Stacey Bonello, The Bonello Foundation, Inc., has earned a reputation for excellence in community service, offering mentorship, outreach, and resources that uplift families and individuals facing social and economic challenges; and WHEREAS, The Annual Youth Summit serves as a cornerstone of the Foundation's mission, offering interactive workshops, motivational speakers, and development opportunities that help equip young people with the tools, confidence, and support they need to succeed; and WHEREAS, In addition to the Youth Summit, The Bonello Foundation, Inc., has demonstrated a profound commitment to community enrichment through year-round initiatives such as toy drives, holiday dinners, clothing giveaways, and health fairs, providing direct assistance and hope to residents across the Borough; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body recognizes that the future of our State is vested in the promise of our youth, and organizations such as The Bonello Foundation, Inc., play a vital role in guiding, nurturing, and empowering the next generation of leaders; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor The Bonello Foundation, Inc., upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Youth Summit 2025, and to commend the organization for its unwavering dedication to youth empowerment, civic engagement, and community service; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Stacey Bonello, Founder and CEO of The Bonello Foundation, Inc.
