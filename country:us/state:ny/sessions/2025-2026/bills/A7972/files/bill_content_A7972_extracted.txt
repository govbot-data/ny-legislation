Section 50-b of the civil rights law, as amended by chapter 2 643 of the laws of 1999 and subdivision 1 as amended by chapter 155 of 3 the laws of 2022, is amended to read as follows: 4 § 50-b. Right of privacy; victims of sex offenses or offenses involv- 5 ing the transmission of the human immunodeficiency virus. 1.
