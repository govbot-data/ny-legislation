TAPIA -- read once and referred to the Committee on Alcoholism and Drug Abuse AN ACT to amend the mental hygiene law, in relation to establishing a wound care kit and xylazine test strip distribution program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The mental hygiene law is amended by adding a new section 2 19.47 to read as follows: 3 § 19.47 Wound care kit and xylazine test strip distribution program. 4 1. The commissioner shall establish a program whereby individuals with 5 a substance use disorder or who are otherwise at risk of drug use may 6 receive, free of charge, wound care kits specifically designed for the 7 treatment of injection-related wounds and skin and soft tissue 8 infections, and xylazine test strips to detect the presence of xylazine. 9 The following facilities may apply to distribute such wound care kits 10 and xylazine test strips: 11 (a) hospital emergency rooms; 12 (b) addiction treatment facilities; 13 (c) facilities for substance use disorder programs; 14 (d) homeless shelters; 15 (e) urgent care centers; 16 (f) mobile health units; 17 (g) public health offices; 18 (h) safe syringe programs; and 19 (i) any other setting, as determined by the department, which is 20 routinely accessed by individuals with a substance use disorder or who 21 are otherwise at risk of drug use, and who are in need of treatment for 22 injection-related wounds and skin and soft tissue infections, or who 23 would benefit from the use of xylazine test strips. 24 2. Facilities distributing wound care kits and xylazine test strips 25 pursuant to subdivision one of this section shall not record any EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
