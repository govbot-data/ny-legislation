A07410 Summary:

BILL NOA07410
 
SAME ASNo Same As
 
SPONSORRosenthal
 
COSPNSRReyes, Shimsky, Epstein, Raga, Gallagher, Glick, Steck, Burroughs, Lasher
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add §275-a, Ed L
 
Prohibits an individual with a vested interest or government agency from having a cause of action against a library staff member who refuses to remove such library materials whether such library materials belongs to a school or public library.

A07410 Text:




 
                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          7410
 
                               2025-2026 Regular Sessions
 
                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                     March 25, 2025
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by  M.  of  A.  ROSENTHAL  --  read once and referred to the
          Committee on Libraries and Education Technology
 
        AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to prohibiting a cause of
          action for refusing a request to remove library materials
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 275-a
     2  to read as follows:
     3    § 275-a. Refusal of a request to remove library materials. 1. For  the
     4  purposes  of  this section, the following terms shall have the following
     5  meanings:
     6    (a) "Individual with a vested interest"  means  any  resident  who  is
     7  served by the school or public library or resides in a county or munici-
     8  pality where the school or public library is situated.
     9    (b)  "Library  material" means any material including, but not limited
    10  to, nonfiction and fiction books; magazines; reference books; supplemen-
    11  tary titles; multimedia and digital material; software and instructional
    12  material and other material not required as part of  classroom  instruc-
    13  tion,  belonging to, on loan to, or otherwise in the custody of a school
    14  or public library.
    15    (c) "Library staff member" means a  school  or  public  library  media
    16  specialist, librarian, any certificated or non-certificated staff member
    17  assigned  to  duties  in  a  school or public library, or any individual
    18  conducting or assisting with the functions of a school or public library
    19  media specialist or school or public librarian.
    20    2. Where an individual with a vested  interest  or  government  agency
    21  requests  that  library  material  be  removed  from  a school or public
    22  library and a library staff member fails to remove such library  materi-
    23  al,  nothing  in  this section shall be construed as creating a cause of
    24  action against such library staff member  for  failing  to  remove  such
    25  library material from a school or public library.
    26    § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
 
         EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
                              [ ] is old law to be omitted.
