CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to requiring insurers participating in the NY state of health marketplace to offer at least one dental plan for individuals age 65 and older The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subparagraph (ii) of paragraph (a) of subdivision 1 of 2 section 268-d of the public health law, as added by section 2 of part T 3 of chapter 57 of the laws of 2019, is amended to read as follows: 4 (ii) offers at least one qualified health plan and/or other or addi- 5 tional health plans authorized for sale by the department of financial 6 services or the department in each of the silver and gold levels as 7 required by state law and offers at least one qualified dental plan or 8 dental plan approved for sale by the department of financial services 9 and/or the department for individuals age sixty-five and older, 10 provided, however, that the Marketplace may require additional benefit 11 levels to be offered by all insurers participating in the Marketplace; 12 § 2. This act shall take effect on the first of January next succeed- 13 ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
