(a) The grid modernization commission shall, in consultation with 23 the department of public service, conduct a study of research, develop- 24 ment, and demonstrations of electric grid modernization that address the 25 principal challenges identified in the New York Independent System Oper- 26 ator's 2021-2040 System & Resource Outlook and any subsequent twenty- 27 year comprehensive reliability plans.
