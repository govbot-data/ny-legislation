Section 139 of the public service law is amended by adding 2 a new subdivision 2-a to read as follows: 3 2-a. If a major electric transmission facility siting permit is 4 proposed for a site which is grassland bird breeding habitat or grass- 5 land bird wintering habitat within a department of environmental conser- 6 vation designated grassland bird conservation center, ORES shall require 7 a mitigation ratio of three acres of mitigation for every acre of occu- 8 pied grassland bird breeding habitat and one acre of mitigation for 9 every acre of grassland bird wintering habitat determined to be taken. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately, provided, however, that 11 the amendments to section 139 of the public service law made by section 12 one of this act shall not affect the repeal of such section and shall be 13 deemed repealed therewith.
