and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, this Legislative Body is proud to commemorate the 3rd Annual Marine Park Alliance Garden of Giving Gala, to be held on Wednesday, May 20, 2026, at the Marine Park Golf House in Brooklyn, New York; and WHEREAS, The Marine Park Alliance has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to preserving, enhancing, and promoting Marine Park, one of Brooklyn's largest and most treasured public green spaces, ensuring that residents and visitors alike may continue to enjoy its environmental, recreational, educational, and cultural benefits; and WHEREAS, Through environmental stewardship initiatives, community programming, volunteer engagement, beautification efforts, and public advocacy, the Marine Park Alliance has played an essential role in protecting natural habitats, promoting sustainability, and strengthening community pride and civic participation throughout Brooklyn; and WHEREAS, The Garden of Giving Gala serves as an important occasion to celebrate community partnership, recognize civic and philanthropic leadership, and raise critical support for the ongoing preservation and improvement of Marine Park and its surrounding environment; and WHEREAS, The 2026 Gala will honor distinguished leaders whose service and contributions reflect a deep commitment to public service, community development, and civic engagement; and WHEREAS, The Marine Park Alliance's continued efforts exemplify the importance of public-private collaboration in maintaining and enhancing urban green spaces, promoting environmental education, and fostering a greater sense of connection between residents and their communities; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body recognizes the invaluable contributions of the Marine Park Alliance's leadership, volunteers, supporters, and community partners, whose collective dedication has helped ensure that Marine Park remains a vibrant and welcoming resource for generations to come; and WHEREAS, The observance of the Garden of Giving Gala provides an opportunity to reaffirm the importance of environmental stewardship, community collaboration, and civic engagement in sustaining the public spaces that enrich the lives of all New Yorkers; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 3rd Annual Marine Park Alliance Garden of Giving Gala on May 20, 2026, and to recognize the organization's enduring dedication to environmental stewardship, public service, and community enhancement; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the leadership of the Marine Park Alliance as an expression of the esteem and appreciation of this Legislative Body for its continued service to the people of the State of New York.
