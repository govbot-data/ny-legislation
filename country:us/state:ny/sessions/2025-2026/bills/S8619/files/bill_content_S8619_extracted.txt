HELMING -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the legislative law, in relation to requiring fiscal notes for bills imposing mandated benefits under insurance plans and under the Medicaid fee-for-service program The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The legislative law is amended by adding a new section 50-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 50-a. Fiscal note in insurance mandate bills. 1. A bill which enacts 4 or amends any provision of law that imposes a new mandated insurance 5 benefit or service, or that expands an existing mandated insurance bene- 6 fit or service, shall contain an actuarial fiscal note stating the esti- 7 mated annual cost to the insurance premium on policyholders affected by 8 such enactment or amendment and the source of such actuarial estimate. 9 2. This section shall apply to all bills that will impact insurance 10 plans of the state of New York or of any of its political subdivisions 11 and also to all bills that will impact any insurance plan offered by an 12 insurer doing business in this state as an authorized insurer. For the 13 purposes of this subdivision, "doing business in this state as an 14 authorized insurer" shall have the same meaning as "doing an insurance 15 business" as defined in section eleven hundred one of the insurance law. 16 3. The fiscal note shall contain an actuarial analysis of the poten- 17 tial insurance premium impact of imposing such new mandated insurance 18 benefit or service or expanding such existing mandated insurance benefit 19 or service, as well as an analysis of its potential impact on all 20 segments of the insurance market. 21 4. An omission of such fiscal note shall invalidate such bill. 22 § 2.
