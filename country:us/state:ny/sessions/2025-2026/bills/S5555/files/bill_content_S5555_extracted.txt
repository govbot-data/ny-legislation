Section 240.20 of the penal law is amended to read as 2 follows: 3 § 240.20 Disorderly conduct. 4 A person is guilty of disorderly conduct when, with intent to cause 5 public inconvenience, annoyance or alarm, or recklessly creating a risk 6 thereof such person: 7 1.
