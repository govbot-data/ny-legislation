MATTERA -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Energy and Telecommuni- cations AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to decisions regard- ing the siting of major utility transmission facilities The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 120 of the public service law is amended by adding 2 a new subdivision 6 to read as follows: 3 6. "Public interest" when considering the siting of fuel transmission 4 lines, includes, but is not limited to, the consideration of energy 5 affordability, reliability, and the safety and environmental impact of 6 other methods of transportation, including the transportation of liqui- 7 fied natural gas using commercial vehicles. 8 § 2. Paragraph (h) of subdivision 1 of section 126 of the public 9 service law, as amended by chapter 521 of the laws of 2015, is amended 10 and a new paragraph (i) is added to read as follows: 11 (h) that the facility will serve the public interest, convenience, and 12 necessity, provided, however, that a determination of necessity made by 13 the power authority of the state of New York pursuant to section ten 14 hundred five of the public authorities law for a major utility trans- 15 mission facility for which an application has been filed prior to July 16 first, nineteen hundred seventy-eight pursuant to section one hundred 17 twenty-two of this [chapter] article, shall be conclusive on the commis- 18 sion[.]; 19 (i) in the case of a gas transmission line that the alternative method 20 of using commercial vehicles for the purpose of transporting liquified 21 natural gas poses a hazard to public safety. 22 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
