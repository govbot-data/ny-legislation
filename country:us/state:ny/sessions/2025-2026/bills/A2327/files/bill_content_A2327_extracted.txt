Section 4903 of the insurance law is amended by adding two 2 new subsections (j) and (k) to read as follows: 3 (j) Each health care plan and utilization review agent shall annually, 4 in such form as the superintendent shall require, report information to 5 the department regarding step therapy override requests and determi- 6 nations.
