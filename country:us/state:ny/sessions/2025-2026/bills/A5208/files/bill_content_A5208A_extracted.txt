AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to requiring general
hospitals to have a registered professional nurse as a sitting and
voting member of the governing entity responsible for developing the
general hospital's strategic plan, structure, systems, policies and
programs
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 2801-a of the public health law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision 18 to read as follows:
3 18. All general hospitals shall have a registered professional nurse,
4 holding a current unencumbered license from New York state, as a sitting
5 and voting member of the governing entity responsible for developing the
6 general hospital's strategic plan, structure, systems, policies and
7 programs. Such board position may be in addition to the maximum number
8 of seats allowed for the governing entity responsible for developing the
9 general hospital's strategic plan, structure, systems, policies and
10 programs.
11 § 2. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after
12 it shall have become a law. Effective immediately, the addition, amend-
13 ment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implemen-
14 tation of this act on its effective date are authorized to be made and
15 completed on or before such effective date.
