AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to reimbursement for
grants for the hiring of teachers, arts and music teacher grants and
nonpublic school safety equipment grants
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 3037 of the education law is
2 amended by adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows:
3 (d) Notwithstanding any inconsistent provision of law, any reimburse-
4 ment payable pursuant to this section shall be paid within twelve months
5 of the department's receipt of a complete application for such grant.
6 § 2. The education law is amended by adding two new sections 3037-a
7 and 3037-b to read as follows:
8 § 3037-a. Arts and music teacher grants for nonpublic schools.
9 Notwithstanding any inconsistent provision of law, any reimbursement
10 payable to a nonpublic school for arts and music teacher grants adminis-
11 tered by the commissioner shall be paid within twelve months of the
12 department's receipt of a complete application for such grant.
13 § 3037-b. Nonpublic school safety equipment grants for nonpublic
14 schools. Notwithstanding any inconsistent provision of law, any
15 reimbursement payable to a nonpublic school for nonpublic school safety
16 equipment grants administered by the commissioner shall be paid within
17 twelve months of the department's receipt of a complete application for
18 such grant.
19 § 3. This act shall take effect April 1, 2027.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
