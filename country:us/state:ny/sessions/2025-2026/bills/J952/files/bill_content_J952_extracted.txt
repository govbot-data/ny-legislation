and WHEREAS, The Port Washington Police Department, through unwavering commitment to public safety, professionalism, and excellence in duty, continues to exemplify the highest standards of law enforcement; and WHEREAS, On Monday, May 12, 2025, the Port Washington Police Department will host its Annual Recognition Night to honor members of the Department whose exemplary service and courageous actions reflect great credit upon themselves, their department, and the community they serve; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the following officers who have been selected to receive the Medal of Commendation, awarded to members who, in the line of police duty, distinguish themselves by acts of courage involving significant personal hazard in protecting or saving human life, or for the performance of a highly creditable and unusual police-related task: Police Officer, Kristin Kujan, Police Officer Kyle Lewis, Police Officer William McNally, and Police Officer Christopher Mele; and WHEREAS, The Meritorious Police Service Award is conferred upon those members who distinguish themselves through achievements or service beyond normal expectations, often under unusual or hazardous circumstances, bringing incidents to successful conclusions; the following members are recognized with this high honor: Lieutenant Nicholas Dello-Iacono, Sergeant John Steadman, Detective Jaime Arias, Detective Michele Cetta, Detective Charles Kouvel, and Detective Donald Wilson; and WHEREAS, The Lifesaving Award is bestowed upon members who, in the performance of duty, render distinguished medical first aid to a person in immediate jeopardy, contributing directly to the person's survival and enabling transportation to a medical facility; the following members are deserving of this prestigious recognition: Sergeant Robert Pape, Police Officer Jimmy Ambroise, Police Officer Zeshan Babar, Police Officer Jordan Caruso, Police Officer Christian Castillo, Police Officer Richard Cuomo (receiving two Lifesaving Awards), Police Officer Kyle DiLeo, Police Officer Kyle Lewis, Police Officer Patrick McKeever, Police Officer William McNally, and Police Officer Lionel Puton; and WHEREAS, These honors reflect the Port Washington Police Department's ongoing dedication to protecting and serving the public with valor, efficiency, and integrity, often placing themselves in harm's way in the preservation of life and safety; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Port Washington Police Department upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Recognition Night on May 12, 2025, and to pay tribute to the above-named honorees for their bravery, meritorious service, and lifesaving actions; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the aforementioned honorees.
