SEPTIMO, JACOBSON, STIRPE, STECK -- read once and referred to the Committee on Health AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to requiring health- care facilities to maintain a fifty percent operating threshold of certain reusable healthcare protective textiles in their inventory The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 2803 of the public health law is amended by adding 2 a new subdivision 15 to read as follows: 3 15.
