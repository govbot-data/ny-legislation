Clause 16 of subparagraph (i) of the opening paragraph of 2 section 1210 of the tax law, as amended by chapter 237 of the laws of 3 2023, is amended to read as follows: 4 (16) the county of Orleans is hereby further authorized and empowered 5 to adopt and amend local laws, ordinances or resolutions imposing such 6 taxes at a rate which is one percent additional to the three percent 7 rate authorized above in this paragraph for such county for the period 8 beginning June first, nineteen hundred ninety-three, and ending November 9 thirtieth, two thousand [twenty-five] twenty-seven; 10 § 2.
