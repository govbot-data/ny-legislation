STIRPE -- read once and referred to the Committee on Labor AN ACT to amend the workers' compensation law, in relation to providing an insurance premium reduction for landscape businesses whose employ- ees complete a safety course for the landscape industry The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The workers' compensation law is amended by adding a new 2 section 138 to read as follows: 3 § 138. Occupational safety and health administration course; premium 4 reduction. 1. For purposes of this section, the term "landscape busi- 5 ness" shall mean a business whose primary concern involves the modifica- 6 tion of the visible features of an area of land, and shall include, but 7 not be limited to, lawn care, gardening and the removal or pruning of 8 trees. 9 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, any 10 insurer doing business within this state who offers workers' compen- 11 sation insurance to landscape businesses doing business within this 12 state shall provide a ten percent reduction in its premiums for such 13 insurance to any landscape business that meets the requirements set 14 forth in subdivision three of this section. 15 3. Any person, firm, partnership, limited liability company, corpo- 16 ration or any other business entity shall receive a ten percent 17 reduction in its workers' compensation premium for any year in which 18 fifty percent or more of the employees of such business successfully 19 complete the ten hour construction safety course for the landscaping 20 industry approved by the occupational safety and health administration. 21 Such premium reduction shall be available on an annual basis. Applica- 22 tion for such premium reduction shall be made in a manner and with a 23 standardized form to be developed cooperatively between the commission- EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
