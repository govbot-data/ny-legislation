The executive law is amended by adding a new section 172-g 2 to read as follows: 3 § 172-g. Disclosure of annual employment report for certain non-chari- 4 table, non-profit entities. 1. The reports shall be submitted 15 on a form prescribed by the department of audit and control, and shall 16 be available for public inspection and copying pursuant to section 17 eighty-seven of the public officers law.
