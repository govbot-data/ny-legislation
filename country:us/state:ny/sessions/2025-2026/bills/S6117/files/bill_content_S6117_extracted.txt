The public health law is amended by adding a new article 2 2-C to read as follows: 3 ARTICLE 2-C 4 STRATEGIC STATE STOCKPILE 5 Section 297. State stockpile. 6 § 297. State stockpile. 1. The commissioner shall maintain a stockpile 7 or stockpiles of drugs, vaccines and other biological products, medical 8 devices and other supplies in such numbers, types and amounts as deter- 9 mined by the governor. The commissioner shall conduct an annual review 10 of the contents of such stockpile, including, but not limited to, non- 11 pharmaceutical supplies, and make necessary additions or modifications 12 to the contents of such stockpile based on such review. 13 2.
