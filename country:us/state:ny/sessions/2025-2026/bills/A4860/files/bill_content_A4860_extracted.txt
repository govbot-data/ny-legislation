Section 367-a of the social services law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 13 to read as follows: 3 13. (a) Any inconsistent provision of this chapter or other law 4 notwithstanding, on and after the effective date of this subdivision, 5 all payments with respect to medical assistance shall be capitated 6 payments. 7 (b) The commissioner may develop and assign patients rating categories 8 based on risk for the purposes of developing reimbursement methodologies 9 and payments under this subdivision. 10 § 2.
