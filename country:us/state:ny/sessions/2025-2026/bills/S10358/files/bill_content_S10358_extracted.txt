Title 6 of the education law is amended by adding a new 2 article 107 to read as follows: 3 ARTICLE 107 4 RESEARCH LITERACY ENHANCEMENT ACT 5 Section 5300. Short title. 6 5301. Definition. 7 5302. Research literacy enhancement program. 8 5303. Research literacy enhancement fund. 9 § 5300. Short title. This title shall be known and may be cited as 10 the "research literacy enhancement act". 11 § 5301. Definition. As used in this title, "program" shall mean the 12 research literacy enhancement program created pursuant to section five 13 thousand three hundred two of this article. 14 § 5302. Research literacy enhancement program. 1. The commissioner 15 shall establish within the department a research literacy enhancement 16 program through which the department shall make grants to academic 17 institutions within the state currently conducting or having an interest 18 in conducting basic and clinical, social, translational, technological, 19 and behavioral research on literacy. 20 2. All research funds shall be awarded on the basis of the research 21 priorities established for the program and the merit of the proposed 22 research, as determined by an open, competitive peer review process that 23 ensures objectivity, consistency, and high quality. All investigators, 24 regardless of affiliation, shall have equal access and opportunity to 25 compete for program funds. 26 § 5303. Research literacy enhancement fund.
