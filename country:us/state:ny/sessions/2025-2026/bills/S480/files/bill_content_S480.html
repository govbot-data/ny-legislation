        AN  ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to eliminating
          additional fees for special license plates issued to veterans
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  Subdivision 1-a of section 404 of the vehicle and traffic
     2  law, as amended by chapter 105 of the laws of 2017, is amended  to  read
     3  as follows:
     4    1-a.  The  commissioner  may issue special number plates to applicants
     5  who are members of veterans organizations in the same  manner  as  other
     6  number  plates  are issued pursuant to this article. Such special number
     7  plates shall be issued [only] upon [(a)] payment of [a one-time  service
     8  charge  of  ten  dollars  in  addition  to] the regular registration fee
     9  prescribed by section four  hundred  one  of  this  article,  and  [(b)]
    10  submission  of  proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that the appli-
    11  cant is presently a member of the veterans organization for  which  such
    12  plate  is  requested.  Such membership shall be verified annually by the
    13  applicant. Application for such plates shall be made in accordance  with
    14  regulations promulgated by the commissioner with respect to the issuance
    15  of  such  plates. Provided, however, that nothing contained herein shall
    16  be deemed to supersede the provisions of section  four  hundred  four-d,
    17  four  hundred  four-e,  four  hundred  four-h, four hundred four-i, four
    18  hundred four-j, four hundred four-k, four hundred four-p,  four  hundred
    19  four-aa, or four hundred four-dd of this article.
    20    § 2. Subdivision 3 of section 404-h of the vehicle and traffic law, as
