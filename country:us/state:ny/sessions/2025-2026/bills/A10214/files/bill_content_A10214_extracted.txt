Any method used to receive electronic 40 bids and offers shall comply with article three of the state technology 41 law, and any rules and regulations promulgated and guidelines developed 42 thereunder and, at a minimum, must (a) document the time and date of 43 receipt of each bid and offer received electronically; (b) authenticate 44 the identity of the sender; (c) ensure the security of the information 45 transmitted; and (d) ensure the confidentiality of the bid or offer 46 until the time and date established for the opening of bids or offers. 47 The timely submission of an electronic bid or offer in compliance with 48 instructions provided for such submission in the advertisement for bids 49 or offers and/or the specifications shall be the responsibility solely 50 of each bidder or offerer or prospective bidder or offerer.
