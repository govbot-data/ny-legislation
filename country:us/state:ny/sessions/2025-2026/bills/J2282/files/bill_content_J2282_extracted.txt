Bill No.: Summary   Actions   Committee&nbspVotes   Floor&nbspVotes   Memo   Text   LFIN   Chamber&nbspVideo/Transcript J02282 Summary:BILL NOJ02282  SAME ASNo Same As SPONSORMURRAY COSPNSR MLTSPNSR  Go to topJ02282 Text: Senate Resolution No. 2282 BY: Senator MURRAY MEMORIALIZING Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim September 13-19, 2026, as Truck Driver Appreciation Week in the State of New York WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize official weeks that are set aside to highlight the vital role and value of the men and women who deliver the essential goods and resources needed by all citizens of New York State; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim September 13-19, 2026, as Truck Driver Appreciation Week in the State of New York; and WHEREAS, Truck drivers play a vital and indispensable role in the economy of the State of New York and the United States by ensuring the safe and timely transportation of goods, materials, food, medicine, fuel, and other essential products; and WHEREAS, The trucking industry in New York supports over 300,000 jobs statewide and pays more than $22.5 billion annually in wages, demonstrating the industry's substantial economic impact throughout the State; and WHEREAS, The trucking industry is the primary means of transporting goods in New York State, supporting commerce, manufacturing, agriculture, construction, retail operations, healthcare systems, and countless other sectors essential to daily life; and WHEREAS, Professional truck drivers work tirelessly under demanding conditions and often spend long periods away from their families in order to maintain the supply chains upon which businesses, hospitals, schools, and communities depend; and WHEREAS, Truck drivers demonstrated extraordinary dedication and resilience during times of emergency and economic uncertainty, including during the COVID-19 pandemic and other crises, ensuring that essential supplies reached communities throughout the State and Nation; and WHEREAS, Truck Driver Appreciation Week provides an opportunity to recognize and honor the professionalism, sacrifice, commitment to safety, and hard work of the men and women who keep America's highways and supply chains moving; and WHEREAS, It is fitting and proper to acknowledge the contributions of truck drivers and to express gratitude for their service to the people and businesses of New York State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim the week of September 13-19, 2026, as Truck Driver Appreciation Week in the State of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to The Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of New York, and to such other persons and organizations as may be deemed appropriate.
