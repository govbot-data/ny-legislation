TAGUE, DeSTEFANO, MANKTELOW, WOERNER, HAWLEY, PALMESANO, JENSEN, DiPIETRO, LEMONDES, MILLER, MIKULIN, GIBBS, GALLA- HAN -- read once and referred to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to permitting schools located within the state to purchase certain New York milk The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The education law is amended by adding a new section 915-a 2 to read as follows: 3 § 915-a. Milk options. 1. As used in this section, the following terms 4 shall have the following meanings: 5 (a) "New York milk" shall mean raw milk produced by the milking of 6 cows physically located within the geographic boundaries of the state, 7 transported to a dairy processing facility located within the geographic 8 boundaries of the state and processed as fluid milk into containers 9 intended for distribution to consumers. 10 (b) "Reduced fat New York milk" shall mean New York milk containing 11 two percent milk fat. The term includes New York milk that has been 12 flavored. 13 (c) "Whole New York milk" shall mean New York milk containing at least 14 three percent milk fat. The term includes New York milk that has been 15 flavored. 16 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law or regulation to the 17 contrary, a school board or other governing entity of a school located 18 in the state may elect to utilize funds from state or local sources to 19 obtain whole New York milk or reduced fat New York milk to provide or 20 sell at such school. 21 3. The commissioner of education shall notify the superintendent or 22 chief administrator of each school in the state of the provisions of 23 this section.
