A07434 Summary:

BILL NOA07434
 
SAME ASSAME AS S00829
 
SPONSORHawley
 
COSPNSRMcDonough, DeStefano, Brown K
 
MLTSPNSR
 
Add §§120.65, 120.66, 125.28 & 125.29, Pen L; add §1221-a, V & T L
 
Establishes the crimes of vehicular assault and vehicular manslaughter in an active work zone and intrusion into an active work zone.

        AN  ACT  to  amend  the  penal  law  and the vehicle and traffic law, in
          relation to safety in active work zones
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section  1.  The  penal  law  is  amended  by adding four new sections
     2  120.65, 120.66, 125.28 and 125.29 to read as follows:
     3  § 120.65 Vehicular assault in an active work zone in the second degree.
     4    1. A person is guilty of vehicular assault in an active work  zone  in
     5  the second degree when, with criminal negligence, such person:
     6    a. enters or intrudes into an active work zone while operating a motor
     7  vehicle; and
     8    b.  such  entry or intrusion causes serious physical injury to another
     9  person.
    10    2. For purposes of this section the term "active work zone" shall mean
    11  the physical area on a public highway,  street  or  private  road  where
    12  construction, maintenance or utility work is being conducted, which area
    13  is  properly designated to be an active work zone by signs, cones and/or
    14  other traffic control devices and where workers are physically present.
    15    Vehicular assault in an active work zone in the  second  degree  is  a
    16  class E felony.
    17  § 120.66 Vehicular assault in an active work zone in the first degree.
    18    1.  A  person is guilty of vehicular assault in an active work zone in
    19  the first degree when such person recklessly:
    20    a. enters or intrudes into an active work zone while operating a motor
    21  vehicle; and
    22    b. such entry or intrusion causes serious physical injury  to  another
    23  person.
    24    2. For purposes of this section the term "active work zone" shall mean
    25  the  physical  area  on  a  public highway, street or private road where

     1  construction, maintenance or utility work is being conducted, which area

     4    Vehicular  assault  in  an  active  work zone in the first degree is a

     8    1. A person is guilty of vehicular manslaughter in an active work zone

    10    (a)  enters  or  intrudes  into  an active work zone while operating a

    12    (b) such entry or intrusion causes the death of another person.
    13    2. For purposes of this section the term "active work zone" shall mean

    23  in the first degree when such person recklessly:
    24    (a) enters or intrudes into an active  work  zone  while  operating  a
    25  motor vehicle; and
    26    (b) such entry or intrusion causes the death of another person.
    27    2. For purposes of this section the term "active work zone" shall mean
    28  the  physical  area  on  a  public highway, street or private road where
    29  construction, maintenance or utility work is being conducted, which area
    30  is properly designated to be an active work zone by signs, cones  and/or
    31  other traffic control devices and where workers are physically present.
    32    Vehicular manslaughter in an active work zone in the first degree is a
    33  class B felony.
    34    §  2.  The  vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new section
    35  1221-a to read as follows:
    36    § 1221-a. Intrusion into an active work zone. 1. No driver of a  vehi-
    37  cle,  operator of a bicycle or pedestrian shall enter or intrude into an
    38  active work zone, except upon direction from a flag person, police offi-
    39  cer or other designated person in charge of traffic control or direction
    40  from a traffic control device regulating entry therein. For purposes  of
    41  this  section,  the term "active work zone" shall mean the physical area
    42  on a public highway, street or private road where construction,  mainte-
    43  nance  or utility work is being conducted, which area is properly desig-
    44  nated to be an active work zone by signs,  cones  and/or  other  traffic
    45  control devices and where workers are physically present.
    46    2.  A  violation of subdivision one of this section shall constitute a
    47  class B misdemeanor punishable by a fine of not less  than  two  hundred
    48  fifty  dollars  nor  more  than  five hundred dollars, or by a period of
    49  imprisonment not to exceed three months, or by both such fine and impri-
    50  sonment.
    51    § 3. This act shall take effect on the first of November next succeed-
    52  ing the date on which it shall have become a law.
