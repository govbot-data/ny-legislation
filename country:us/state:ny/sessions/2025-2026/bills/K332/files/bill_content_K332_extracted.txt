Lavine HONORING the Nassau County Police Department upon the occasion of its 100th Anniversary of dedicated service to the people of Nassau County and the State of New York WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and commend those institutions and individuals who have demonstrated a long-standing commitment to protecting and serving the residents of New York State with honor, professionalism, and distinction; and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its proud traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor the Nassau County Police Department, upon the occasion of its 100th Anniversary in 2025, celebrating a century of law enforcement excellence and public service; and WHEREAS, Officially established in 1925, the Nassau County Police Department (NCPD) was created to provide centralized and professional police protection to the rapidly growing communities of Nassau County; since then, it has grown into one of the largest and most respected police departments in the Nation; and WHEREAS, Over the past century, the men and women of the Nassau County Police Department have demonstrated unwavering courage, commitment, and sacrifice, responding to the evolving needs of the community with skill and integrity; and WHEREAS, From combating organized crime and narcotics to leading innovations in traffic safety, emergency response, community policing, and technological advancements in law enforcement, the NCPD has continually adapted to meet the challenges of modern policing; and WHEREAS, The Department has also been a leader in fostering positive relationships with the diverse communities it serves, working tirelessly to build trust, promote transparency, and ensure the safety and well-being of all Nassau County residents; and WHEREAS, The legacy of the Nassau County Police Department is built upon the dedication of its officers, past and present, who have served with honor, some of whom have made the ultimate sacrifice in the line of duty; and WHEREAS, As the Department commemorates its 100th Anniversary in 2025, it stands as a proud symbol of public service, protection, and partnership, deserving of the highest praise and recognition; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to honor the Nassau County Police Department, upon the occasion of its 100th Anniversary, recognizing a century of outstanding service, sacrifice, and commitment to the people of Nassau County and the State of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the Commissioner of the Nassau County Police Department and to appropriate law enforcement and civic organizations in recognition of this historic milestone.
