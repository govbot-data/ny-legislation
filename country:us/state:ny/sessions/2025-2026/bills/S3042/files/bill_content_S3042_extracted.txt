The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 2 163-e to read as follows: 3 § 163-e. Active intelligent speed assistance system for state-owned 4 vehicles. 1. For purposes of this section, the term "active intelligent 5 speed assistance system" means a vehicle system that uses, at minimum, 6 the GPS location of the vehicle compared with a database of posted speed 7 limits, to determine the speed limit, and prevents throttle input once a 8 vehicle exceeds a specified speed threshold related to the speed limit. 9 An active intelligent speed assistance system shall be capable of being 10 temporarily disabled by the driver of the vehicle. 11 2. Each state agency shall prepare an active intelligent speed assist- 12 ance system plan for the equipment of all state agency vehicles with 13 active intelligent speed assistance systems. Such plans shall 19 include interim targets for achieving the equipment of all state agency 20 vehicles with active intelligent speed assistance systems. 21 3.
