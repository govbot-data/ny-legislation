        AN ACT in relation to the alienation of certain parklands by the Village
          of Southampton in the Town of Southampton in the County of Suffolk
 
          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:

     1    Section  1.  Subject  to  the  provisions  of this act, the village of
     2  Southampton, located in the town  of  Southampton,  county  of  Suffolk,
     3  acting  by and through its governing body and upon such terms and condi-
     4  tions as determined by such body, is hereby authorized to discontinue as
     5  parklands and alienate the lands described in section three of this  act
     6  for the purpose of constructing a new wastewater treatment facility.

     8  be effective only upon the condition that  the  village  of  Southampton
     9  dedicates  the  lands described in section four of this act as parkland,
    10  provided that such lands have never been used for public open  space  or
    11  municipal park purposes.
