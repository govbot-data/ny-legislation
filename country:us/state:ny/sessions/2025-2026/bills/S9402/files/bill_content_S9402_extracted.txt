PALUMBO -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to establishing bicycle with electric assist safety courses, requiring purchasers of bicycles with electric assist to provide a bicycle with electric assist safety certificate and requiring operators of bicycles with electric assist to carry identification when operating such bicycles The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 1244 to read as follows: 3 § 1244. Bicycle with electric assist safety course. 1. The commission- 4 er shall initiate and put into effect a comprehensive educational 5 program designed to advance bicycle with electric assist safety, which 6 provides for the training of all purchasers of bicycles with electric 7 assist in the safe operation of such bicycles. 8 2. "Bicycle with electric assist safety certificate" shall mean a 9 certificate issued by the commissioner evidencing that the holder there- 10 of has successfully completed a course of instruction in bicycle with 11 electric assist safety as provided in this section. The commissioner 12 shall put into effect a plan for the training of all purchasers, and in 13 the case of purchases by minors, the parent or guardian of such minor 14 purchasers, of bicycles with electric assist and the issuance of bicycle 15 with electric assist safety certificates. For the purposes of this 16 section, the term "bicycle with electric assist safety" shall include 17 instruction in the laws relating to and safe operation of bicycles with 18 electric assist. 19 3. "Bicycle with electric assist safety certificate" shall also mean a 20 certificate issued by the commissioner evidencing that the holder there- 21 of has successfully completed a course of instruction in the laws relat- 22 ing to and safe operation of bicycles with electric assist. The commis- 23 sioner is authorized to collect a fee not to exceed ten dollars for the 24 issuance of such certificate. The commissioner is authorized, at the commissioner's discretion, 20 to develop a method for approving internet-based bicycle with electric 21 assist safety courses as an appropriate and effective method for the 22 administration and completion of training in the laws relating to and 23 safe operation of bicycles with electric assist for the purposes of 24 awarding a bicycle with electric assist safety certificate pursuant to 25 this section. All approved internet-based bicycle with electric assist 26 safety courses shall employ methods to ensure attendance of the student 27 throughout the course. 28 § 2. Section 1242 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding 29 two new subdivisions 14 and 15 to read as follows: 30 14. No person, firm, association or corporation engaged in the busi- 31 ness of selling or leasing bicycles with electric assist shall sell or 32 lease any bicycle with electric assist unless the purchaser of such 33 bicycle provides a bicycle with electric assist safety certificate 34 issued pursuant to section twelve hundred forty-four of this article. If 35 the purchaser of a bicycle with electric assist is a minor, or if a 36 bicycle with electric assist is being purchased for a minor, then both 37 the minor and the parent or guardian of such minor shall each provide a 38 bicycle with electric assist safety certificate certifying that both the 39 minor and the parent or guardian of such minor have completed a bicycle 40 with electric assist safety course as required pursuant to section 41 twelve hundred forty-four of this article. 42 15. Every operator of a bicycle with electric assist shall carry iden- 43 tification with them when operating such bicycle with electric assist. 44 For the purposes of this subdivision, "identification" shall mean a 45 drivers' license, state-issued identification card, or, where the opera- 46 tor is a minor, a school-issued identification card. 47 § 3.
