and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Women Empowerment Ministry upon the occasion of hosting its 10th Annual Luncheon on Thursday, July 9, 2026, under the theme "Commitment, Dedication, and Outstanding Support to Our Communities"; and WHEREAS, For ten years, the Women Empowerment Ministry has gathered the women of Brooklyn and the surrounding communities in a spirit of fellowship, mutual support, and shared commitment to health and wellness, providing a free, accessible forum in which women can receive vital information on the health and legal issues that most directly affect their lives and the lives of their families; and WHEREAS, The 10th Annual Luncheon will bring together community members, health advocates, and sponsors to address critical topics, including health and legal issues affecting seniors, Medicaid access, mental health, high blood pressure, diabetes, and gut health; in communities where health disparities remain a persistent and devastating reality, events such as this one represent an act of care and advocacy that saves lives; and WHEREAS, The 10th Annual Luncheon will feature the wisdom and inspiration of guest speakers, Dr. Tamia Bethea-Williams, Doctor of Psychology and Counseling and Doctor of Metaphysical Science, who has a career as a certified motivational speaker, and John C. Maxwell, Leadership Coach, Life Mastery Consultant, and Rapid Transformation Therapist; these speakers have been devoted to empowering individuals to define their purpose and step into their highest potential; and WHEREAS, Under the leadership of Winnifred Grant and the dedicated committee of organizers who have sustained this ministry across ten years of free community service, the Women Empowerment Ministry has embodied the very theme it has always carried: commitment, dedication, and outstanding support to the communities it serves; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor the Women Empowerment Ministry and its leadership, sponsors, and guest speakers and the women of Brooklyn who gather each year to invest in their own health, knowledge, and well-being; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commend the Women Empowerment Ministry, upon the occasion of hosting its 10th Annual Luncheon on July 9, 2026, and to extend heartfelt congratulations for ten years of commitment, dedication, and outstanding support to the communities of Brooklyn; and be it further RESOLVED, That copies of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Winnifred Grant and the leadership, organizers, and members of the Women Empowerment Ministry in recognition of their decade of devoted service to the health and empowerment of Brooklyn's women and seniors.
