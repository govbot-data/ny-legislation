        Introduced  by  M.  of  A.  ROSENTHAL  --  read once and referred to the

        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law and  the  election  law,  in
          relation  to  notification of the national bone marrow program; and to
          repeal certain provisions of the  vehicle  and  traffic  law  relating
          thereto

          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is  amended  by  adding  a  new
     2  section 101-d to read as follows:
     3    §  101-d.  Bone marrow donor contact information.  The national marrow
     4  donor program contact information including the mailing address, contact
     5  telephone number and website address.
     6    § 2. Subdivision 2 of section 490 of the vehicle and  traffic  law  is
     7  amended by adding a new paragraph (c) to read as follows:
     8    (c)  In  addition  to the provisions of paragraphs (a) and (b) of this
     9  subdivision, the commissioner shall ensure that space is provided on the
    10  application to advise applicants of the existence of the national marrow
    11  donor program and to provide, at a minimum, the contact information  for
    12  this program and a description of its purpose.
    13    §  3.  Subdivision  1 of section 502 of the vehicle and traffic law is
    14  REPEALED and a new subdivision 1 is added to read as follows:
    15    1. Application for license. Application for a driver's  license  shall
    16  be  made to the commissioner. The fee prescribed by law may be submitted
    17  with such application. The applicant shall furnish such proof of identi-
    18  ty, age, and fitness as may  be  required  by  the  commissioner.    The
    19  commissioner  may  also  provide  that  the  application procedure shall
    20  include the taking of a photo  image  or  images  of  the  applicant  in
    21  accordance with rules and regulations prescribed by the commissioner. In
    22  addition, the commissioner also shall require that the applicant provide
    23  such  applicant's  social security number and shall provide space on the

    23  license that such applicant is a veteran  of  the  United  States  armed
    24  forces.  Additionally,  the  commissioner  shall  ensure  that  space is
    25  provided on the application to advise applicants of the existence of the
    26  national marrow donor program and to provide, at a minimum, the  contact
    27  information for this program and a description of its purpose.
    28    In  addition,  an applicant for a commercial driver's license who will
    29  operate a commercial motor vehicle in interstate commerce shall  certify
    30  that such applicant meets the requirements to operate a commercial motor
    31  vehicle,  as  set forth in public law 99-570, title XII, and title 49 of
    32  the code of federal regulations, and all regulations promulgated by  the
    33  United  States secretary of transportation under the hazardous materials
    34  transportation act. In addition, an applicant for a commercial  driver's
    35  license shall submit a medical certificate at such intervals as required
    36  by  the  federal  motor  carrier safety improvement act of 1999 and Part

    38  medical  certification  and  in a manner prescribed by the commissioner.
    39  For purposes of this section  and  sections  five  hundred  three,  five

    41  certificate" and "medical certification" shall mean a form substantially

    47    § 4. Section 508 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a
    48  new subdivision 7 to read as follows:
    49    7. The commissioner is directed to develop the license application and
    50  renewal  forms  and  the  directions  accompanying these forms to advise
    51  applicants of the existence of the national marrow donor program and  to
    52  provide,  at  a  minimum, the contact information for this program and a
    53  description of its purpose, and to further  amend  the  website  of  the
    54  department  to provide national marrow donor program contact information
    55  and a description of its purpose.
