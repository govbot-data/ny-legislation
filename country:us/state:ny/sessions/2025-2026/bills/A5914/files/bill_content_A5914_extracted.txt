This act shall be known and may be cited as the "3000 by 2 '30 act". 3 § 2. The education law is amended by adding a new section 679-k to 4 read as follows: 5 § 679-k. New York state "three thousand by thirty" social worker loan 6 forgiveness incentive program. 1. Eligibility. To be eligible for an 7 award pursuant to this section, applicants shall: 8 (a) have graduated and obtained a degree from an approved New York 9 state college or university; 10 (b) have an outstanding student loan debt from obtaining such degree; 11 (c) be employed as a licensed master social worker or licensed clin- 12 ical social worker in New York state in accordance with article one 13 hundred fifty-four of this chapter; 14 (d) apply for this program within two years of college graduation; 15 (e) comply with subdivision five of section six hundred sixty-one of 16 this part; and 17 (f) make a commitment to practice within the state for the next five 18 years. 19 2. Awards. The corporation shall grant such awards within amounts 20 appropriated for such purposes and based on the availability of funds. 21 § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
