COONEY -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Education AN ACT to amend part C of chapter 56 of the laws of 2020 directing the commissioner of education to appoint a monitor for the Rochester city school district, establishing the powers and duties of such monitor and certain other officers and relating to the apportionment of aid to such school district, in relation to the powers and duties of the monitor The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 5 of section 7 of part C of chapter 56 of the 2 laws of 2020 directing the commissioner of education to appoint a moni- 3 tor for the Rochester city school district, establishing the powers and 4 duties of such monitor and certain other officers and relating to the 5 apportionment of aid to such school district, is amended to read as 6 follows: 7 5. The monitor shall assist in resolving any disputes and conflicts, 8 including but not limited to, those between the superintendent and the 9 board of education and among the members of the board of education. The 10 monitor may recommend the removal of any member of the board of educa- 11 tion to the commissioner. 12 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
