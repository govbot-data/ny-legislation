Section 11-104 of the energy law is amended by adding a new 2 subdivision 9 to read as follows: 3 9. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph (b) of subdivision one 4 of section 11-103 of this article, the code shall not prohibit building 5 systems or equipment used for the combustion of fossil fuels including, 6 but not limited to, natural gas, propane, and fuel oil, in any new 7 building construction statewide, nor shall such systems be prohibited in 8 any existing building construction statewide, including renovations and 9 replacement of existing systems. 10 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
