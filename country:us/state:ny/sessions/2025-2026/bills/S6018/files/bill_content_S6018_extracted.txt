Hamilton Niagara Power Plant The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law, rule or regulation to 2 the contrary, the New York state power authority is authorized and 3 directed to rename the Robert Moses Niagara Power Plant located in the 4 town of Lewiston, county of Niagara, to the Shirley J.
