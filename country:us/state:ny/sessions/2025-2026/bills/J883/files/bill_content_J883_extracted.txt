and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its traditions, this Legislative Body is proud to commemorate the 69th Precinct Community Council's 9th Annual Fundraiser Benefit on Thursday, May 1, 2025; and WHEREAS, The 69th Precinct Community Council, based in Canarsie, Brooklyn, serves as a vital liaison between local residents and the New York Police Department (NYPD), working to enhance transparency, collaboration, and accountability while addressing issues that affect public safety and quality of life; and WHEREAS, The Annual Fundraiser Benefit is a celebration of partnership, recognizing the shared efforts of community members, local leaders, and law enforcement officers to create a safer, more connected, and resilient neighborhood; and WHEREAS, The proceeds from this event support ongoing community outreach, youth programming, public forums, and crime prevention initiatives, all of which are critical to fostering mutual understanding and inclusive progress; and WHEREAS, The 69th Precinct Community Council has demonstrated exemplary leadership in bringing together residents from all walks of life to collaborate on meaningful solutions, advocate for equity, and promote a shared sense of responsibility for the well-being of Canarsie; and WHEREAS, Events, such as the Annual Fundraiser Benefit, affirm the importance of community-police partnerships in building trust, promoting dialogue, and creating an environment where all individuals feel safe, respected, and valued; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 69th Precinct Community Council's 9th Annual Fundraiser Benefit and applaud its enduring efforts to uplift, protect, and unify the Canarsie community; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to the 69th Precinct Community Council in recognition of this important occasion.
