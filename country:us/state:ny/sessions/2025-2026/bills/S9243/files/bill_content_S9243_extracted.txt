CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Children and Families AN ACT in relation to establishing the interagency council on kinship care The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the office of 2 the aging is directed and authorized to establish the interagency coun- 3 cil on kinship care to investigate, evaluate and make recommendations 4 concerning the unique issues of kinship caregivers. 5 § 2. (a) Such council shall include, but not be limited to, represen- 6 tatives from the office of children and family services, the department 7 of health, the office of temporary and disability services, the office 8 of court administration, the office of mental health, the department of 9 education, the office of addiction services and supports, the New York 10 state homes and community renewal and any not-for-profit groups deemed 11 necessary, including but not limited to, the New York state caregiver 12 and respite coalition. 13 (b) The members of the council shall receive no compensation for their 14 services, but shall be allowed their actual and necessary expenses 15 incurred in the performance of their duties under this act. The council 16 may employ and at pleasure remove such personnel as it may deem neces- 17 sary for the performance of its functions and fix their compensation 18 within the amounts made available by appropriation therefor. 19 § 3. For the accomplishment of its purposes, the council may meet and 20 hold public and/or private hearings within or without the state, and 21 shall have all the powers of a legislative committee pursuant to the 22 legislative law. The council is authorized and empowered to undertake 23 any studies, inquiries, surveys or analyses it may deem relevant through 24 its own personnel or in cooperation with or by agreement with any other 25 public or private agency. 26 § 4. The council shall make recommendations and a report of its find- 27 ings. The council shall submit such recommendations and report, includ- 28 ing any recommendations for legislative action as it may deem necessary EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
