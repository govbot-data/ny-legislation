AN ACT to amend the civil practice law and rules and the administrative
code of the city of New York, in relation to updating the fee
collected by county clerks for deposit into the cultural education
account
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Subparagraph b of paragraph 4 of subdivision (a) and
2 subparagraph b of paragraph 11 of subdivision (b) of section 8021 of the
3 civil practice law and rules, subparagraph b of paragraph 4 of subdivi-
4 sion (a) as amended by section 2 and subparagraph b of paragraph 11 of
5 subdivision (b) as amended by section 3 of part B of chapter 83 of the
6 laws of 2002, are amended to read as follows:
7 b. For recording, entering, indexing and endorsing a certificate on
8 any instrument, an additional fee of five dollars to be paid monthly by
9 county clerks to the commissioner of education, after deducting twenty-
10 five cents, for deposit into the New York state local government records
11 management improvement fund and an additional [fifteen] thirty dollars,
12 after deducting [seventy-five cents] one dollar, for deposit to the
13 cultural education account.
14 b. For recording any instrument required by statute to be recorded, an
15 additional fee of five dollars to be paid monthly by county clerks to
16 the commissioner of education, after deducting twenty-five cents, for
17 deposit into the New York state local government records management
18 improvement fund and an additional [fifteen] thirty dollars, after
19 deducting [seventy-five cents] one dollar, for deposit to the cultural
20 education account.
EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
