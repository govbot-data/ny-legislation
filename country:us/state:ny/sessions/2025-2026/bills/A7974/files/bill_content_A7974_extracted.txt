CARROLL -- read once and referred to the Committee on Governmental Operations AN ACT to amend the executive law and the general business law, in relation to directing the division of homeland security and emergency services to develop rules and regulations relating to truck rental security The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section 709 of the executive law is 2 amended by adding a new paragraph (w) to read as follows: 3 (w) Promulgate rules and regulations within twelve months of the 4 effective date of this paragraph, in consultation with the department of 5 transportation and the department of state, to ensure that: 6 (i) any person or organization, or any subsidiary or affiliate, 7 including a franchise, in the business of renting trucks, vans or trail- 8 ers to the public from locations in this state, be registered with the 9 division of homeland security and emergency services; 10 (ii) each such person or organization maintains accurate records of 11 any person renting a truck, van or trailer for at least two years; and 12 (iii) any person or organization, or any subsidiary or affiliate, 13 including a franchise, in the business of renting trucks, vans or trail- 14 ers to the public require a valid driver's license and at least one 15 other form of identification to rent trucks, vans or trailers and 16 require that meaningful assurances are made by the rental entity to 17 verify the identity of any person renting a truck, van or trailer; 18 (iv) any person renting a truck, van or trailer may be checked against 19 state or federal criminal databases for the purposes of screening for 20 possible terrorists; and 21 (v) the division shall develop and promote best practices for the 22 truck rental industry in this state, and work to educate persons and EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
