CLEARE -- read twice and ordered printed, and when printed to be committed to the Committee on Crime Victims, Crime and Correction AN ACT to amend the correction law, in relation to the minimum standards for the construction or improvement of correctional facilities and the care, custody, correction, treatment, supervision, discipline, and other correctional programs for all persons confined in such facili- ties The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Subdivision 6 of section 45 of the correction law, as 2 amended by section 7 of part Q of chapter 56 of the laws of 2009, is 3 amended to read as follows: 4 6. Promulgate rules and regulations in coordination with local jail 5 administrators, county chief elected or appointed official and county 6 sheriffs establishing minimum standards for the review of the 7 construction or improvement of correctional facilities and the care, 8 custody, correction, treatment, supervision, discipline, and other 9 correctional programs for all persons confined in correctional facili- 10 ties. Such rules and regulations shall allow counties the flexibility in 11 establishing minimum staffing ratios and such rules and regulations 12 shall be forwarded to the governor, the temporary president of the 13 senate and the speaker of the assembly no later than January first, 14 nineteen hundred seventy-six and annually thereafter. 15 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
