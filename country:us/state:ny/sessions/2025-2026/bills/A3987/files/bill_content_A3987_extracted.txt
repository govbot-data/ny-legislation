SHIMSKY -- read once and referred to the Commit- tee on Codes -- reported and referred to the Committee on Codes -- reported and referred to the Committee on Rules -- Rules Committee discharged, bill amended, ordered reprinted as amended and recommitted to the Committee on Rules AN ACT to amend the criminal procedure law, in relation to designating security officers for the village court of the village of Pleasant- ville as peace officers The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. Section 2.10 of the criminal procedure law is amended by 2 adding a new subdivision 89 to read as follows: 3 89. Employees of the village court of the village of Pleasantville 4 serving as security officers at such village court. These officers 5 shall only have the powers listed in paragraphs (b), (h), and (i) of 6 subdivision one of section 2.20 of this article; provided, however, that 7 nothing in this subdivision shall be deemed to authorize such officer to 8 carry, possess, repair or dispose of a firearm unless the appropriate 9 license therefor has been issued pursuant to section 400.00 of the penal 10 law; provided, however, that if such employee is not currently a police 11 or peace officer pursuant to section 1.20 of this title or 2.10 of this 12 article, such employee must successfully complete the training required 13 under section 2.30 of this article. 14 § 2. This act shall take effect immediately. EXPLANATION--Matter in italics (underscored) is new; matter in brackets [ ] is old law to be omitted.
