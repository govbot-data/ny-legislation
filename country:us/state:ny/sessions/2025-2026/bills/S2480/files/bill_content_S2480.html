Directs the New York state energy and research development authority to establish a pilot program to install a microgrid in Glenwood Houses; requires the authority to annually report on the results of such program and any recommendations for expansion.

S02480 Text:




 
                STATE OF NEW YORK
        ________________________________________________________________________
 
                                          2480
 
                               2025-2026 Regular Sessions
 
                    IN SENATE
 
                                    January 21, 2025
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by  Sen.  PARKER -- read twice and ordered printed, and when
          printed to be committed to the Committee on  Energy  and  Telecommuni-
          cations
 
        AN  ACT  in relation to directing the New York state energy research and
          development authority to establish a pilot program to install a micro-
          grid in Glenwood Houses

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  1. No later than one hundred eighty days after the effec-
     2  tive date of this act, the New York state energy research  and  develop-
     3  ment  authority shall establish, within amounts appropriated therefor, a
     4  pilot program to install a microgrid in Glenwood Houses in Kings county.
     5  Such microgrid shall be used for local energy  production  and  distrib-
     6  ution  which  can  function  independently if disconnected from the main
     7  electricity grid or a supplement for  peaks  in  demand.  The  microgrid
     8  shall  be  powered by renewable energy sources including but not limited
     9  to solar panels, hydrogen or wind, and energy efficient generators which
    10  shall be combined with energy storage units such as batteries  with  low
    11  environmental impacts.
    12    2. The authority shall provide an annual report on or before the first
    13  day  of  January  to the governor, the temporary president of the senate
    14  and the speaker of the assembly, on the pilot program and any  recommen-
    15  dations for expansion of such pilot program.
    16    §  2.  This  act shall take effect on the thirtieth day after it shall
    17  have become a law.
