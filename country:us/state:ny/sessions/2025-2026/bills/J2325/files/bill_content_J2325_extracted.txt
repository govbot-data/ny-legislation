and WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and in full accord with its longstanding traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to commemorate the 70th Anniversary of King Pin Lanes of Rome, New York, to be celebrated in 2026; and WHEREAS, For seven decades, King Pin Lanes has stood as a cherished gathering place in the City of Rome, providing generations of residents with opportunities for recreation, fellowship, competition, and family entertainment; and WHEREAS, Located on East Dominick Street in Rome, New York, King Pin Lanes has become a fixture of the Rome community, welcoming countless families, friends, students, coworkers, and visitors through its doors while fostering lasting memories and meaningful community connections; and WHEREAS, Throughout its distinguished history, King Pin Lanes has adapted to meet the changing needs of the community while remaining committed to the values of hospitality, family-centered entertainment, and exceptional service; and WHEREAS, In addition to serving as a premier bowling center, King Pin Lanes has hosted leagues, youth programs, tournaments, fundraisers, birthday celebrations, reunions, corporate gatherings, and numerous charitable and community events, strengthening the social fabric of the region; and WHEREAS, As a locally recognized small business, King Pin Lanes has contributed significantly to the economic development of the City of Rome by creating employment opportunities, supporting local organizations, and attracting visitors from throughout Central New York; and WHEREAS, The enduring success of King Pin Lanes is a testament to the vision, dedication, and hard work of its ownership and staff, whose commitment to excellence has enabled the business to thrive across generations; and WHEREAS, It is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body acknowledges this exceptional business and its contributions to the local and State economies, fully confident that it will continue to enjoy the sparkling successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to commemorate the 70th Anniversary of King Pin Lanes of Rome, New York, and to honor its many years of outstanding service to the people of the City of Rome, Oneida County, and the State of New York; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to King Pin Lanes.
