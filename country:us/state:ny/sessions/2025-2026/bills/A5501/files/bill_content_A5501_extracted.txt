The executive law is amended by adding a new section 214-j 2 to read as follows: 3 § 214-j. Training. 1. The superintendent shall establish policies and 4 procedures requiring that state police basic training and pre-employment 5 course curricula include training in the areas of cultural competency, 6 including training pertaining to appropriate sensitivity to issues of 7 race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, age and disabili- 8 ties. Such training shall comprise not less than five percent of the 9 basic training and pre-employment course training curricula. Cultural 10 competency training shall be provided by an accredited educator and/or 11 accreditation body. 12 2. The accreditation body shall provide information pertaining to such 13 policies and procedures to municipal law enforcement agencies and 14 departments, fire departments, the department of corrections and commu- 15 nity supervision and the department of health for implementation of such 16 curricula by such agencies and departments. 17 3. The superintendent of the state police shall promulgate rules and 18 regulations necessary to effectuate the provisions of this section.
