and WHEREAS, Athletic competition enhances the moral and physical development of the young people of this State, preparing them for the future by instilling in them the value of teamwork, encouraging a standard of healthy living, imparting a desire for success, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Jadynn Egloff upon the occasion of reaching 1,000 points in her career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Girls Varsity Basketball Team; and WHEREAS, Jadynn Egloff is an outstanding scholar-athlete who excels in many aspects in her community; from the court to the classroom, she brings joy and hard work to all she does; and WHEREAS, As a student at Bolton Central High School, Jadynn Egloff has made a profound impact on many of her peers, teammates, coaches, teachers, and community; this exceptional athlete has been a major contributing factor to the success of the Bolton Central High School Girls Varsity Basketball Team and continues to bring her leadership skills and positivity to the program; and WHEREAS, During a game against Lake Placid on January 17, 2026, Jadynn Egloff scored her 1,000th career point, contributing to the team's 63-24 victory; and WHEREAS, Jadynn Egloff has been rightfully praised for her many achievements, and it is her strong work ethic, kind heart, and willingness to always help and encourage others that makes her an inspiration and significant part of her community; and WHEREAS, Poised eagerly with enthusiasm and determined purpose, this exemplary athlete now stands honorably, with just pride in her accomplishments and in eager anticipation of future challenges, understanding and appreciating the caring counsel and support provided by her coaches, teachers, and parents; and WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to commend and pay tribute to those young people who, by achieving outstanding success in athletic competition, have inspired and brought pride to our great Empire State; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to congratulate Jadynn Egloff upon the occasion of reaching 1,000 points in her career as a member of the Bolton Central High School Girls Varsity Basketball Team; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Jadynn Egloff.
