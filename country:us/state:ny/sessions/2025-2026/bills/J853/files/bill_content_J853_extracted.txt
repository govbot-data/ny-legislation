the Eagle Court of Honor ceremony will be held on Sunday, May 4, 2025; and WHEREAS, Michael Domingo is a member of Scouts BSA Troop 298; and WHEREAS, America has achieved its greatest heights of glory through the dedication, determination and tireless efforts of the outstanding citizens of this Nation; and WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain its glory; and WHEREAS, Scouts BSA and its members are dedicated to the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in Scouting, earned by approximately five percent of all Scouts, is the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout; and WHEREAS, This outstanding young man has been involved in Scouting for many years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all the necessary achievements, including completion of a community service project, which was successfully planned, developed, and executed; and WHEREAS, For his project, Michael Domingo directed the design and construction of the Wall of Heroes Memorial at Memorial Park in New Hyde Park, New York; and WHEREAS, With a desire to recognize and honor those heroic individuals who have paid the ultimate sacrifice for our Nation, Michael Domingo compiled information on 57 service members from New Hyde Park who lost their lives defending our freedom during World War I, World War II, the Korean War, and the Vietnam War; and WHEREAS, Michael Domingo directed and collaborated with local contractors and fellow scouts to beautify the area and construct three brick walls with bronze plaques attached honoring the 57 fallen heroes he researched; and WHEREAS, His dedication to the safety and well-being of the citizens of Nassau County, New York, is represented through his project and demonstrates his devotion to both community and commitment to others; and WHEREAS, As he goes forward in his journey, Michael Domingo plans on continuing the ideals and principles of Scouting America, and he will undoubtedly be met with more success in his future; and WHEREAS, Michael Domingo's perseverance in rising through the ranks to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of his ability to master difficult tasks; his accomplishment will enable him to emerge as a leader, fully equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school, his community, his State and his Nation, and no doubt his future will be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in recognition of the significance of this meritorious achievement, to congratulate and honor Michael Domingo on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout; and be it further RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be transmitted to Michael Domingo.
