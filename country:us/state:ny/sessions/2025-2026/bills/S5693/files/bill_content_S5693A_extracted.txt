AN ACT to amend the public health law, in relation to hospital compli-
ance with federal hospital price transparency law, rules and regu-
lations
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 2805 of the public health law is amended by adding
2 a new subdivision 3 to read as follows:
3 3. The commissioner shall report quarterly on hospital compliance
4 with federal hospital price transparency law, rules and regulations.
5 Such report shall be conspicuously posted on the department's website.
6 § 2. Hospitals not in compliance with federal price transparency law,
7 rules and regulations shall be subject to penalties under section 12 of
8 the public health law.
9 § 3. This act shall take effect on the one hundred eightieth day after
10 it shall have become a law.
