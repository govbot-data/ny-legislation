Career major in electric vehicle maintenance and repair. 1. 4 For purposes of this section: 5 (a) "career major" shall mean a number of courses in a career educa- 6 tion program which together provide a student with greater technical 7 skill and a strong academic core and are primarily designed for those 8 students who are not initially college bound and shall provide them with 9 alternatives to immediate entrance into a four-year university or 10 college after high school graduation.
