AN ACT to amend the mental hygiene law, in relation to establishing a
statewide residential families committee
The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
bly, do enact as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 13.05 of the mental hygiene law is amended by
2 adding a new subdivision (h) to read as follows:
3 (h) 1. In addition to any other committees established by the develop-
4 mental disabilities advisory council as deemed necessary by the chair
5 under paragraph one of subdivision (c) of this section, there shall be
6 established a statewide residential families committee whose members
7 shall be persons with developmental disabilities residing in community
8 residences, intermediate care facilities, or other residential facili-
9 ties for the care and treatment of persons with developmental disabili-
10 ties or family members or guardians of persons with developmental disa-
11 bilities residing in such residences or facilities for the care and
12 treatment of persons with developmental disabilities.
13 2. The commissioner shall provide for an appropriate and timely appli-
14 cation process to facilitate selection of members. No member of the
15 residential families committee shall be an owner, operator, person with
16 ownership interest in or board member of an entity operating a community
17 residence, intermediate care facility, or other residential facility for
18 the care and treatment of persons with developmental disabilities or be
19 employed by an entity operating a community residence, intermediate care
20 facility, or other residential facility for the care and treatment of
21 persons with developmental disabilities.
22 3. The statewide residential families committee shall meet quarterly.
