RA, COLTON -- read once and referred to the Committee on Transportation AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to "I Support Music Education" distinctive plates; and to amend the state finance law, in relation to establishing the "I support music education" fund The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem- bly, do enact as follows: 1 Section 1. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding a new 2 section 404-ff to read as follows: 3 § 404-ff. Distinctive "I Support Music Education" license plates. 1. 4 Any person residing in this state shall, upon request, be issued a 5 distinctive license plate bearing the phrase "I Support Music Educa- 6 tion". Application for said license plate shall be filed with the 7 commissioner in such form and detail as the commissioner shall 8 prescribe. 9 2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued 10 in the same manner as other number plates upon payment of the regular 11 registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article; 12 provided, however, that an additional annual service charge of twenty- 13 five dollars shall be charged for such plates. 14 3. Each twenty-five dollars received as the annual service charge 15 under this section shall be deposited to the credit of the "I support 16 music education" fund established pursuant to section ninety-five-j of 17 the state finance law and shall be used for music education programs 18 undertaken pursuant to section ninety-five-l of the state finance law. 19 § 2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 95-l to 20 read as follows: 21 § 95-l. "I support music education" fund. 1. There is hereby estab- 22 lished in the joint custody of the commissioner of taxation and finance 23 and the comptroller a fund to be known as the "I support music educa- 24 tion" fund.
