                               2025-2026 Regular Sessions
 
                   IN ASSEMBLY
 
                                     January 9, 2025
                                       ___________
 
        Introduced  by  M.  of  A.  RA,  COLTON -- read once and referred to the
          Committee on Transportation
 
        AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to  "I  Support
          Music  Education"  distinctive  plates; and to amend the state finance
          law, in relation to establishing the "I support music education" fund

          The People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and  Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section  1.  The  vehicle  and  traffic law is amended by adding a new
     2  section 404-ff to read as follows:
     3    § 404-ff. Distinctive "I Support Music Education" license  plates.  1.
     4  Any  person  residing  in  this  state  shall, upon request, be issued a
     5  distinctive license plate bearing the phrase  "I  Support  Music  Educa-
     6  tion".  Application  for  said  license  plate  shall  be filed with the
     7  commissioner  in  such  form  and  detail  as  the  commissioner   shall
     8  prescribe.
     9    2. A distinctive plate issued pursuant to this section shall be issued
    10  in  the  same  manner as other number plates upon payment of the regular
    11  registration fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article;
    12  provided, however, that an additional annual service charge  of  twenty-
    13  five dollars shall be charged for such plates.
    14    3.  Each  twenty-five  dollars  received  as the annual service charge
    15  under this section shall be deposited to the credit of  the  "I  support
    16  music  education"  fund established pursuant to section ninety-five-j of
    17  the state finance law and shall be used  for  music  education  programs
    18  undertaken pursuant to section ninety-five-l of the state finance law.
    19    §  2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 95-l to
    20  read as follows:
    21    § 95-l. "I support music education" fund. 1. There  is  hereby  estab-
    22  lished  in the joint custody of the commissioner of taxation and finance
    23  and the comptroller a fund to be known as the "I  support  music  educa-
    24  tion" fund.

     1    2.  Such  fund  shall  consist  of  all  revenues received pursuant to
     2  section four hundred four-ff of the vehicle and  traffic  law,  and  all
     3  other  moneys  appropriated,  credited  or  transferred thereto from any
     4  other fund or source pursuant to law.  Nothing contained in this section
     5  shall prevent the state from receiving grants, gifts or bequests for the
     6  purposes of the fund as defined in this section and depositing them into
     7  the fund according to law.
     8    2-a. On or before the first day of February each year, the comptroller
     9  shall certify to the governor, temporary president of the senate, speak-
    10  er  of  the assembly, chair of the senate finance committee and chair of
    11  the assembly ways and means committee, the amount of money deposited  in
    12  the  "I support music education" fund during the preceding calendar year
    13  as the result of revenue derived pursuant to section four hundred  four-
    14  ff of the vehicle and traffic law and from grants, gifts and bequests.
    15    3.  Monies  of  the  fund  shall  be expended only for music education
    16  projects approved by the department of education in New York state.
    17    4. Monies shall be payable from the fund on the audit and  warrant  of
    18  the  comptroller  on vouchers approved and certified by the commissioner
    19  of education.
    20    § 3. 1. A distinctive plate established pursuant to section 404-ff  of
    21  the  vehicle and traffic law shall only be designed, produced and issued
    22  upon the delivery to the department of motor vehicles of a  surety  bond
    23  in  the  amount  of  six  thousand dollars, which shall be executed by a
    24  surety company authorized by the department  of  financial  services  to
    25  transact  business in this state. Provided, however, that if the commis-
    26  sioner of motor vehicles shall have  received  prior  to  plate  design,
    27  production and issuance at least two hundred orders for such distinctive
    28  plate  together  with the additional annual service charge applicable to
    29  each such order, which shall be  non-refundable,  no  such  surety  bond
    30  shall  be  required.  All  service  charges  collected  pursuant to this
    31  section shall be deposited pursuant to the provisions of section  404-oo
    32  of  the vehicle and traffic law to the credit of the department of motor
    33  vehicles distinctive plate development fund established by section  95-l
    34  of  the  state finance law and shall be used for the design, production,
    35  advertising and distribution of distinctive license plates in accordance
    36  with such section 95-l.
    37    2. If, upon the expiration of two years following the date upon  which
    38  distinctive  plates  in  the  series  are  first  available for sale two
    39  hundred or more sets of such plates are sold, a bond delivered  pursuant
    40  to this section shall be discontinued. If fewer than two hundred sets of
    41  such  plates  are  sold  by  such time, the department of motor vehicles
    42  shall be entitled to recover against the bond in an amount proportionate
    43  to such shortfall.
    44    § 4. This act shall take effect immediately; provided,  however,  that
    45  sections  one  and  two of this act shall take effect on the one hundred
    46  eightieth day after it shall have become a law.  Effective  immediately,
    47  the  addition,  amendment and/or repeal of any rule or regulation neces-
    48  sary for the implementation of  this  act  on  its  effective  date  are
    49  authorized to be made and completed on or before such effective date.
